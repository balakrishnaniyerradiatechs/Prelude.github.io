Installing GUI Icons
If you want to use GUI, you must install the image files for the GUI icons. To install the GUI icons:
1
Go to the Customer Web Site (www.wdncustomer.activant.com).
2
Enter your Sign In Name and Password and click Submit.
3
On the top navigation bar, click Support.
4
On the left navigation bar, click File Center.
5
Click SBClient_Icons.
6
Click SBClient Icons.exe. You may also download the file and run it later. The Welcome screen appears.
7
Click Next. The License Agreement screen appears.
8
Choose I accept the agreement and click Next. The Ready to Install screen appears.
9
Click Install. The Complete screen appears.
10
Click Finish. The Load GUI Icons screen appears.
11
Choose which version of SBClient you use and click Start. After the icons are copied, the Complete popup appears.
12
Click OK.
