Checking the Disk Capacity of Your System
Drop down to TCL
At the “>” sign type : !df –k
The below screen will appear.
There are two critical areas to check:
/ud
/tmp
These are located under the “Mounted on” field.
Use the “%Used” field to check the capacity. If the capacity is 80% or above, call Activant. If you leave this unattended, you run the risk of running out of disk space and possibly crashing the system.
Prelude will advise you on the proper procedures to make more space available.
