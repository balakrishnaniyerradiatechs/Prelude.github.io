VSI-Fax Support Policy
Activant will support VSI-Fax in the following ways:
•
VSI-Fax support is provided at no additional charge if you have an annual VSI-Fax support agreement through Activant.
•
Support of the Prelude programs that interface with VSI-Fax is no additional charge and is governed by the Technical Services Agreemen and your monthly support charges.
•
Any change, set-up or work for and with your user forms billed at time and materials. That includes changes to forms solely for converting them to VSI-Fax.
•
Any issues that deal with communications are billed at time and materials.
•
Time that is spent by Activant personnel researching issues is billed at time and materials unless the result of the research indicates a problem with either VSI-Fax or its Prelude interface. If the issue is with VSI-Fax or Prelude, then the policy is covered in the first two items listed.
Customers must adopt this VSI-Fax support policy. VSI-Fax support goes far beyond whether the VSI-Fax software is working correctly or whether the Prelude interfaces to VSI-Fax are working correctly.
