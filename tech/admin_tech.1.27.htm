Logging In
To log in to Prelude:
1
Double-click on the SBClient icon. The SBClient screen appears.
2
Highlight the session you want to open and click Start a Session. The AIX system login prompt appears.
3
Enter the your AIX system login and press ENTER.
4
Enter your AIX system password and press ENTER. The SB+ login prompt appears.
5
Enter your SB+ login and press ENTER.
6
Enter your SB+ password and press ENTER.
7
If the Terminal ID prompt appears, choose one of the following:
•
TU.WYSE60 - character.
•
TU.WYSE60.GUI - GUI.
8
The main Prelude screen appears.
