Installing SBClient
To install SBClient:
1
Insert the SBClient CD.
2
Double-click on setup.exe. The progress bar appears, followed by the Welcome screen.
3
Click Next. The User Information screen appears.
4
Enter your Name and the name of your Company and click Next. The Choose Destination Directory screen appears.
5
Click Next. The Select Program Folder screen appears.
6
Click Next. The Installation Information screen appears.
7
Click Next. A progress bar appears, followed by a popup.
8
Click Yes to create a shortcut to SB Client on your desktop. Another popup appears.
9
Click Yes to view the Readme file or click No to skip the Readme file. The SB client installation is complete.
10
Click OK.
