Using RAS2 Dataswitch (Penril) Replacement
RAS2 uses the same dial-out software, Winport, that is used for the modem bank (RAS1 or Edgeserver). RAS2 allows dialing out using phone numbers that have commas in the dialing string which RAS1 ignores since it uses digital lines. It is also a backup for RAS1.
Note: add a 9 before the phone number when using RAS2 modems
Setup steps:
1
Click START then RUN
2
Enter: C:\program files\lansource\winport95\license.exe
3
Click OK
4
In the window for WINport License enter: AAE012239-gggsrk
5
Click OK
6
Shutdown and reboot your PC.
7
Double-click on the two red connectors on the task bar
8
Click SETUP
9
Click SHOW
10
Click the icon with the multiple diskettes
11
Click EXIT
12
Access to RAS2:
13
Since we have setup Winport to SHOW all the modems, you will see a status of all the modems when you start Sbclient. The modems for RAS2 will be the ones that begin with AAE in the list. Select one of these that is ready and you will be connected to RAS2.
