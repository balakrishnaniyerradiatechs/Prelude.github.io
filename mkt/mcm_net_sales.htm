Campaign Lists (Net Sales) screen
The Campaign Lists screen shows each campaign list assigned to a campaign and the net sales for each list.
Menu Path: Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Campaign Maintenance > F6-Lists > F5-Net Sales
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
List Name
The campaign lists on the campaign. Refer to Creating Campaign Lists.
Description
The description of the campaign.
Ln#
The line number on the screen.
Quantity
The number of customers or prospects in the list that you estimate will respond to the campaign.
Net Sales/List
The sales dollars generated from customers and prospects on this list.
Totals (not labelled)
The total Quantity and Net Sales for all campaign lists.
Cost Per Response
Based on Campaign Cost in the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen and the Estimated Resp Number, the cost for each sale as a result of the campaign.
Campaign Gross Profit
The gross profit of the campaign, based on Campaign Cost in the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen and the cost of the items sold as a result of the campaign.
F2-Save
Closes the screen.
