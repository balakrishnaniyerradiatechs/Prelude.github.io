Working with Campaign Lists
Before you create a campaign, you must create list(s) of customers who should receive the campaign literature. You can create the list of customers by using selection criteria (refer to Building a Campaign List). You can then edit the lists by Merging Campaign Lists, Deleting a Campaign List, or Adding a Campaign List to an Existing Campaign.
