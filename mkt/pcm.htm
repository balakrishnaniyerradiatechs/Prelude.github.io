Potential Code Maintenance screen
The Potential Code Maintenance screen allows you create codes that indicate how likely a contact is to purchase items from a product line or major group (refer to Product Line Objectives screen or Major Group Objectives screen) or prospect or customer is to purchase items from your company (refer to Setting up Prospect Accounts).
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > Potential Codes
