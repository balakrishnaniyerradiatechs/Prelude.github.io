Copying a Broadcast Email or Fax
If you want to email or fax a message that is similar to an existing message, you can copy the original message.
To copy a broadcast email or fax:
1
Choose one of the following:
•
To copy an email, choose Marketing > Broadcast/Export Functions > Broadcast Email to open the Send Broadcast Email screen.
•
To copy a fax, choose Marketing > Broadcast/Export Functions > Broadcast Fax to open the Send broadcast fax screen.
2
From the Action Bar, choose Copy. The Copy Email/Fax screen appears.
3
In Copy from, enter the name of the email or fax you want to copy or press F3 to search.
4
In Copy to, press ENTER to get a new number and enter a subject for the new email or fax.
5
Press F2-Save.
