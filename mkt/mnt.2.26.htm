Moving Contacts
In the Contact Move screen, you can move a contact from an old customer to a new customer. For example, John Smith is the contact at customer 100101 and changes employment to customer 100505. You can move the contact John Smith from 100101 to 100505.
 
You cannot move contact information if the contact has open sales order or quotes.
If you need to move a contact from one customer or prospect to another, you can move that contactâ€™s information, including activity history, form letter/literature history, and notes for the original contact. For the contact you copied from, Prohibit Sales and Obsolete are set to Y so that sales cannot be entered for that contact. To move a contact:
1
Choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Processes > Copy Contact To New Customer. The Contact Move screen appears.
2
For From Customer, enter the number of the customer you want to copy the contact from or press F3 to search.
3
For Contact, enter the number of the contact that you want to copy or press F3 to search.
4
In To Customer, enter the number of the customer you want to copy the contact to or press F3 to search.
5
Press F2-Save.
