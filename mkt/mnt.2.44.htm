Selecting Competitor Codes
Competitor codes allow you to track information about a competitor and their prices. This allows you to view the offerings your customers are receiving from the competitors. Competitor codes are used in:
•
Prospect Maintenance to enter notes for each type of competitor (refer to Entering Prospects).
•
Competitor Price Maintenance to track your competitors’ prices.
To enter a competitor code:
1
Select Add. Modules > Marketing > Maintenance > Competitor Codes. The Competitor Code Maintenance screen appears.
2
For Competitor Code, enter a new code (up to 4 alphanumeric characters) or an existing code (e.g.,01) or press F3 to search for an existing code.
3
For Description, enter or edit a brief description (e.g.,Fastners, Inc) of the code.
4
Press F2-Save.
