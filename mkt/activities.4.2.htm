Entering a Marketing Activity
You can enter marketing activities in Marketing Activity Entry or Marketing Activity Contact Entry. The process is very similar for both, with the following exceptions in Marketing Activity Contact Entry:
•
You must specify a valid contact for the activity.
•
You cannot change that contact.
To enter a marketing activity:
1
Choose one of the following:
•
Marketing > Marketing Activity Entry to access the Marketing Activity Entry screen.
•
Marketing > Marketing Activity Contact Entry to access the Marketing Activity Contact Entry screen.
•
Marketing > Prospect Maintenance > F9-Activity to access the Marketing Activity Entry screen. Skip to step 4.
You can also access these screens by using the Marketing Activity Inquiry or Marketing Activity Contact Inquiry.
2
For Prospect, enter the customer or prospect number or press F3 to search. If this activity was generated from another activity, the Prospect is already entered.
3
If you are using mail-to addresses for contacts, indicate which addresses you want to use in Mail-To.
4
For Transaction#, choose one of the following:
•
To access an existing transaction, enter the transaction number or press F3 to search.
•
If this activity was created from another, use the existing transaction number.
•
To get a new transaction number, press ENTER over the word NEW. A menu appears that asks you to identify the type of activity. For more information on how to use these menus, see your company procedures or System Administrator.
5
To choose a Contact for the activity, enter P for the primary contact or the number of the contact or press F3 to search. If you are using Marketing Activity Contact Entry, you must select a contact.
If you are using Marketing Activity Entry, you can change the contact if necessary.
6
If this marketing activity has to do with a specific product, product line, product group, or document, you can enter the necessary information in Product, PLine, Group, Doc Type, and Doc#. Otherwise, you can leave these fields blank.
•
If your system is set up to default related information based on a maintenance or entry screen, the product number, product line, major group, document type, and/or document number may already be entered. Change the data as necessary.
•
If your system is not set up to default related information or you did not start from a related maintenance or entry screen, enter the related product number, product line, major group, document type, and/or document number as necessary.
About Defaulted Information
You can choose to default information related to a marketing activity based on the last maintenance or entry screen you were in. For example, if you are in Sales Order Entry and go directly to Marketing Activity Entry or Marketing Activity Contact Entry, Doc Type defaults to S (sales order) and the sales order number appears in Doc#. Also, if you are in Product Maintenance and enter a marketing activity, Prod#, PLine, and Group default based on the product’s settings. For more information on setting up these defaults, refer to Setting Up Activity Defaults.
7
To schedule a related activity or phone call, enter the following next to To Do and/or Phone:
•
Date - enter the date on which the activity should be completed.
•
Time - enter the time at which the activity should be completed. Time defaults to AM. You can enter P or PM to enter the time as PM. If you enter military time, it is converted to regular time.
•
Action Summary - enter a brief description of the activity or press F3 to search for standard Activity codes (refer to Creating Activity Codes for more information).
•
P - enter the priority of the activity (e.g., A, B C, 1,2 3).
If this activity occurs regularly, you can also schedule a recurring activity to automatically generate. Refer to Scheduling a Recurring Activity
8
To record information about the activity, enter the following under Open:
•
Date - enter the date on which the activity occurred.
•
Time - enter the time at which the activity occurred. Time defaults to AM. You can enter P or PM to enter the time as PM. If you enter military time, it is converted to regular time.
•
Action Summary - enter a brief description of the activity or press F3 to search for standard activity codes (refer to Creating Activity Codes for more information).
•
P - enter the priority of the activity (e.g., A, B C, 1,2 3).
If your company uses activity defaults (refer to Setting Up Activity Defaults), the scheduled activities may default.
9
While you are still in the activity, you can perform additional functions by selecting one of the following:
•
F9-Notes. Allows you to enter a more detailed explanation of the activity.
•
F6-Send Lit. Allows you to send catalogs or literature to the customer. Refer to Sending Literature.
•
F7-Form Letter. Allows you to send an existing form letter to the customer. Refer to Sending a Form Letter.
•
Action Bar > Recurring. Allows you to schedule this activity as a recurring activity. Refer to Scheduling a Recurring Activity for more information.
10
When you are finished entering the activity, press F2-Save. A dialog box with the following message appears:
The Status Of This Activity Is...
11
Choose one of the following:
•
Closed. Completes the activity. Any future scheduled activities will go to the appropriate queues for further action. Refer to Viewing Open Marketing Activities.
•
Open. Leaves the activity open so you can come back later and enter more information about the activity.
•
Return. Brings you back to the activity so you can enter more information.
Notification of Marketing Activities
If any email addresses or user IDs are entered in the customer/prospect’s Marketing Notification List (Prospect or Customer Maintenance > Action Bar > Addt’l Options > Marketing Notification List), the Action Summary information is sent to each email address in the list when the activity is completed.
