Campaign List Report
The Campaign List Report shows response and cost statistics for a campaign. For more information on campaigns, refer to Assigning Products and Lists to a Campaign.
Menu Path: Marketing > Reports > Campaign List Report
Selection Criteria
User the following criteria to limit the information on the report:
•
Campaign Number. The number of the campaign for which you want to view statistics.
•
Pages. The number of pages in the campaign literature.
•
Mail Date. The date on which the campaign literature was mailed.
•
Beginning Date. The date on or before which the campaign began.
•
Ending Date. The date on or after which the campaign ended.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Ln#
The line number. Increments by one for each campaign list.
List Name
The name of the list for the campaign.
Description
The description of the campaign list.
Quantity
The number of customers/prospects in the campaign.
Estimated Resp
%
The percent of customers/prospects that were estimated to respond to the campaign.
Number
The number of customers/prospects that were estimated to respond to the campaign.
Actual Resp
%
The percent of customers/prospects that responded to the campaign.
Number
The number of customers/prospects that responded to the campaign.
Net Sales/List
The total sales of products on the campaign to the customers/products on the campaign list.
Cost Per Response
The cost of the campaign divided by the number of responses.
Campaign Gross Profit
The gross profit of the campaign based on the cost of the campaign and the cost of the products sold.
