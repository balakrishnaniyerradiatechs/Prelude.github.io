Maintaining Potential Codes
Potential codes indicate how likely a contact is to purchase items from a product line or major group (refer to Product Line Objectives Information screen or Major Group Objectives Information screen) or prospect or customer is to purchase items from your company (refer to Entering Prospects). You can then use the potential codes to build campaign or broadcast lists (refer to Working with Campaign Lists and Selecting a Broadcast List).
 
You can also view a list of existing potential codes. To view the list, choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Listings > Potential Codes.
To enter a potential code:
1
Choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Maintenance > Potential Codes. The Potential Code Maintenance screen appears.
2
For Potential Code, enter a new code (up to 2 alphanumeric characters) or an existing code or press F3 to search for an existing code (e.g., A, B).
3
For Description, enter or edit a brief description of the code (e.g., High, Low).
4
Press F2-Save.
