Form Letter screen
The Form Letter screen allows you to indicate that you want to send a form letter to a customer, prospect, or contact while entering a marketing activity. Refer to Entering a Marketing Activity for more information. After you save the marketing activity, you can print the form letter immediately or by batch (refer to Batch Printing Form Letters) depending on your system settings (refer to Setting Marketing Option Parameters).
Menu Path: Marketing > Marketing Activity Entry > F7-Form Letter
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
List Name
The name of the form letter to send (refer to Maintaining Form Letters).
F2-Save
Saves the form letter choice.
