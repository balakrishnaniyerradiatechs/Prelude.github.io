Contact Management Detail Inquiry
The Contact Management Detail Inquiry lists each time a customer and/or contact purchased a product within the time period displayed on the Contact Management Statistics Inquiry screen. This data is based on the view that appears in Contact Management Statistics Inquiry.
Menu Path: Marketing > Inquiries > Contact Management Statistics Inquiry > F5-Detail > F5-Detail
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The number and description of the customer who purchased the product.
Contact
The number and name of the contact who purchased the product for the customer.
Product
The product number and description for which you want to see related sales orders.
Order#
The sales order number that the product was sold.
Invoice#
The invoice number that the product was invoiced.
Ord-Date
The date on which the product was ordered.
Inv-Date
The date on which the product was invoiced.
Sell
The selling warehouse.
Ship
The shipping warehouse.
Qty-Ord
The quantity of the product that was ordered.
Qty-Shp
The quantity of the product that was shipped.
Net Price
The net price that the customer paid for the product.
F2-Save
Closes the screen.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen.
F9-Goto Line
Identifies a reference number on the screen that you want to jump to. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), or T (top).
