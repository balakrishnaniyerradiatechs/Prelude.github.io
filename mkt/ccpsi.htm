Customer Contact Performance Summary Inquiry
The Customer Contact Performance Summary Inquiry displays sales and profit information for a customer and contact for the current and previous year. For more information on contacts, refer to Setting up Customer Contacts.
Viewing Customer Contact Performance
To view the Customer Contact Performance Summary Inquiry:
1
Choose Marketing > Inquiries > Contact Performance Summary Inquiry. The Customer Contact Performance Summary Inquiry screen appears.
2
For Customer Number, enter the number of the customer or prospect for which you want to see sales and profit history.
3
For Contact Number, enter the number of the contact for which you want to see sales and profit history.
Sales and profit information for the customer and contact appear.
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Start Date
The date on which the contact was created.
Ytd Sales
The contact’s total sales for the current year.
Ytd Returns
The contact’s total returns for the current year.
Ytd Gr Profit
The contact’s total gross profit for the current year.
Ytd GP %
The contact’s gross profit percent for the current year.
Invoice Count
The contact’s total number of invoices for the current year.
Avg Sales/Invoice
The contact’s averages sales per invoice for the current year.
Avg G/P/Invoice
The contact’s average gross profit per invoice for the current year.
Prev. Yr Sales
The contact’s total sales for the previous year.
Prev. Yr Returns
The contact’s total returns for the previous year.
Prev. Yr G/P
The contact’s total gross profit for the previous year.
Prev. Yr GP%
The contact’s gross profit percent for the previous year.
Prev. Invoice Count
The contact’s total number of invoices for the previous year.
Prev. Yr Avg Sales
The contact’s averages sales per invoice for the previous year.
Prev. Yr Avg G/P
The contact’s average gross profit per invoice for the previous year.
OK
Closes the screen.
