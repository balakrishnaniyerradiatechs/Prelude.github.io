Campaign Lists screen
When you create a campaign (refer to Assigning Products and Lists to a Campaign), you can use the Campaign Lists screen to add lists of customers to the campaign.
Menu Path: Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Campaign Maintenance > F6-Lists
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
List Name
The campaign lists that you want to add to the campaign. Refer to Creating Campaign Lists.
Description
The description of the campaign list.
Estimated Resp
Quantity
The number of customers or prospects included in the campaign list.
%
The percent of the customers or prospects in Quantity that you estimate will respond.
Number
The number of customers or prospects you expect to respond based on Quantity and %.
Actual Resp
%
The actual percent of customer and prospects who responded to the campaign. Based on actual sales.
Number
The number of customers and prospects who responded to the campaign. Based on actual sales.
Totals (not labelled)
Estimated and actual response data for all campaign lists.
Cost Per Response
Based on Campaign Cost in the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen and the Actual Resp Number, the estimated and actual cost for each sale as a result of the campaign.
Campaign Gross Profit
The gross profit of the campaign, based on Campaign Cost in the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen and the cost of the items sold as a result of the campaign (gross profit - campaign cost).
F2-Save
Saves the campaign list data.
F5-Net Sales
Opens the Campaign Lists (Net Sales) screen, which shows sales dollars for the campaign.
F6-Report
Opens the Campaign List Report screen.
