Customers/Prospects on a Campaign List
The campaign list inquiry lists all of the campaign lists in Prelude and the number of customers and/or prospects in the list. For more information on campaign lists, refer to Building a Campaign List.
Viewing Customers/Prospects on Campaign Lists
To view customer/prospects on a campaign list:
1
Select Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Print Prospect Listing. The Campaign Prospect List screen appears.
2
Enter the name of the campaign list that you want to view in Campaign List and press ENTER.
3
At the Output To prompt, select an output method.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Prospect/
Description
Line one - The prospect or customer number.
Line two - The prospect or customer description.
City
The city in which the customer or prospect is located.
State
The state in which the customer or prospect is located.
Zip
The ZIP code in which the customer or prospect is located.
