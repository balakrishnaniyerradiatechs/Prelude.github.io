Merging Contacts
If a contact leaves the customer or prospect (e.g., another individual is taking the place of the previous contact), you can move that contactâ€™s information and history to another contact at the customer or prospect and merge their data. The history that is merged includes activity history, form letter/literature history, sales, statistics, product line objectives, major group objectives, and notes. If open sales orders or quotes exist, they are updated with the To Contact number.
To merge contacts:
1
Choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Processes > Merge Contacts Within Customer. The Contact Merge Process screen appears.
2
For Customer Number, enter the number of the customer for which you want to merge contacts or press F3 to search.
3
For From Contact, enter the number of the contact that you want to move data from or press F3 to search.
4
For To Contact, enter the number of the contact that you want to merge with the contact in From Contact or press F3 to search.
5
Press F2-Save.
