Contact Diary Inquiry
The Contact Diary Inquiry lists marketing activities and tracked transactions (refer to Setting Marketing Option Parameters for more information on tracking transactions). You can view activities and transactions for a customer or prospect or for a customer or prospect and contact.
Viewing Contact Diary Activities
To view the Contact Diary Inquiry:
1
Select Marketing > Contact Diary System > Contact Diary Inquiry to open the Contact Diary Inquiry screen.
2
For Customer, enter the number of the customer or prospect for which you want to see marketing activities or press F3 to search.
3
For Contact, enter the number of the contact for which you want to see marketing activities or press F3 to search.
4
For Sort, choose one of the following:
•
Entry Date.
•
User Id.
•
Contact.
•
Product.
•
Contact Method.
•
Action Summary.
•
Document Type.
5
For Filters, choose one of the following:
•
No.
•
Yes. Choose any of the following selection criteria:
-
Document Type.
-
Product.
-
Product Line.
-
Major Group.
-
Document Number.
-
Customer P/O Number.
-
Invoice Number.
-
Activity Summary.
-
User Id.
-

Marketing activities and transactions.
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Document ID
The document number associated with the activity or transaction (e.g., sales order number, activity transaction number).
Cd
The type of activity or transaction:
•
S - Sales order.
•
Q - Quote.
•
W - Work order.
•
M - Marketing activity.
•
D - Contact diary entry.
•
R - Sample request.
•
T - To do (scheduled activity).
Trn
The transaction number for the activity.
Date
The date on which the activity or transaction occurred.
Who
The user ID of the person who created the transaction or activity.
Contact
The contact identified on the activity or transaction, if any.
Activity Summary
A brief description of the activity or transaction. For activities, the activity summary appears. For a transaction, the document number, amount, or campaign priority codes may appear.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry.
F5-Review
Shows detail for highlighted activity or transaction.
F6-New Activity
Opens the Marketing Activity Contact Entry screen. Refer to Entering a Marketing Activity for more information.
F7-CMSI
Opens the Contact Management Statistics Inquiry, which displays product information for customer and contact.
F8-Flip
Switches the inquiry between showing activities and transactions for a customer or prospect and a customer or prospect and a contact.
F9-Log
Opens the log inquiry for the highlighted transaction.
