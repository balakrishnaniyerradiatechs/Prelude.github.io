Broadcast Faxing
The Broadcast Fax allows you to send one letter to a group of customers. You can either create the letter within the broadcast fax function, or you can attach an existing form letter (refer to Maintaining Form Letters).
To send a broadcast fax:
1
Select Marketing > Broadcast/Export Functions > Broadcast Fax to open the Send broadcast fax screen.
2
For Msg#, choose one of the following:
•
To edit a fax, enter the number of the fax or press F3 to search.
•
To create a fax, press ENTER to get a new number.
3
For File, enter the name of the file from which you want to extract fax numbers or merged fields:
•
CUSTOMER
•
VEND
•
PROSPECT
•
PROS.CONT
4
For Cover?, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Include a cover page in addition to the body of the fax.
•
N - Do not include a cover page. Only the body of the fax is sent.
5
Edit From, Name, and Email address if necessary. Defaults are based on your user ID.
6
To attach a file, in Attach, enter the full path to the document you want to attach. The file should be in the /tmp on the Prelude server (e.g., \/tmp/attach.pdf). You must have full permissions for a file to attach it.
7
For To, enter the fax of the recipients using one of the following methods:
•
Type Fax, Contact Name (if necessary), and Company Name.
•
Enter a campaign. Refer to Building a Campaign List.
•
Enter a select list name from a saved TCL list.
•
Enter the name of a list created using the list selector. Refer to Selecting a Broadcast List.
•
Press F6-Manual Entry to choose customers, vendors, prospects or contacts to email. The Manually enter customers/vendors/etc. screen appears.
-
For File, enter the name of the file you want to use: CUSTOMER, VEND, PROSPECT, or PROS.CONT.
-
For Number, enter the numbers of the customer, vendor, prospect, or contact that you want to fax.
-
Press F2-Save to return to the Send Broadcast fax screen.
If the fax preferences for the customer, vendor, prospect, or contact indicate that they do not want to receive faxes, that customer, vendor, prospect, or contact is not faxed even if they are in the list.
8
In Subject, enter the subject line of the fax.
9
To send a coverpage, press F7-Coverpage Notes, enter the text for the coverpage, and press F2-Save.
10
For Body of Fax, choose one of the following:
•
Type the text of the fax.
•
To use a form letter, press F5-Load Form Ltr and choose a form letter. Refer to Maintaining Form Letters.
You must use a merged field to insert data from a file into a fax (e.g., a contact’s name from the PROS.CONT file). To use a merged field, first determine the dictionary name of the field you want to use. The field name of the contact name in PROS.CONT is CONT.DESC. In the body of the fax, include the field name, surrounded by carets (e.g, ^CONT.DESC^).
11
Press F2-Save twice. A dialog box with the following message appears:
Send email/fax now?
12
Choose from the following:
•
Yes. Saves and sends the fax now.
•
No. Saves the fax without sending it.
