Broadcast Emailing and Faxing
You can email or fax letters to a list of customers and/or prospects based on a list you create when you send the email or fax or on a list you created previously (refer to Selecting a Broadcast List). You can also export a previously created list. For example, you can export the list to Excel (refer to Exporting a Broadcast List). You can generate the letter when you email or fax it, or you can use a form letter (refer to Maintaining Form Letters).
