Product Detail screen
The Product Detail screen allows you to assign products to a campaign. You can also provide special pricing for the products on the campaign. To include all products in a campaign, leave this screen blank.
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > Potential Codes > F7-Product
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Typ
Indicates whether you want to enter a product or product prefix:
•
R - Product prefix.
•
P - Product number.
Product
The product or product prefix to be associated with the campaign.
Description
The description of the product.
Qty Discount
Indicates if you want to provide special pricing for the campaign:
•
N - Use regular pricing.
•
Y - Use special pricing.
Brk
If Qty Discount is Y, indicates if you want to provide quantity breaks for the campaign:
•
Y - Use quantity breaks.
•
N - Use a specific price.
Price
If Qty Discount is Y and Brk is N, the selling price for this product on this campaign.
Page
The page number where the product is located in the catalog.
Sq Inch
The square inches of the advertisement for this product in the catalog.
Pict
Indicates if a picture is included in the catalog:
•
Y - A picture is included.
•
N - A picture is not included.
Highlight
Indicates if the product is highlighted in the catalog:
•
Y - The product is highlighted.
•
N - The product is not highlighted.
F2-Save
Saves the product information.
F9-Goto Line
Identify a reference number on the screen that you want to jump to. Valid codes are product number or prefix, page number, line number, B (bottom), or T (top).
Action Bar
Sort By
Resort the list by product, page, or vendor.
