Overview
The Marketing module in Activant Prelude provides a wide range of features and options to help you track interactions with current and prospective customers. The module allows you to:
•
Record and schedule activities and transactions. Refer to Working With Marketing Activities.
•
Create promotional campaigns and choose which customers, prospects, and products to include. Refer to Using Campaigns.
•
Email and fax multiple customers, prospects, or contacts at once. Refer to Broadcast Emailing and Faxing.
•
Define how you want to use the Marketing module. Refer to Marketing Maintenance.
