View Maintenance screen
The View Maintenance screen allows you to group product lines and major groups into categories that a customer and/or contact regularly purchases from. For example, you carry 10,000 items, but a customer only regularly purchases 3,000 of the items and the customer has two contacts, one of which regularly purchases 2,000 items and the other regularly purchases 1,000 items. If you want to see sales for the customer and/or contacts for only those items, you can create a view for the set of products the customer buys and the subsets of products that each of the contacts buys. You can establish a default view in customer maintenance for the 3,000 products the customer purchases and default views for each of the contacts that represents the products they typically purchase. Then you can use Contact Management Statistics Inquiry to view sales for only the products in the views you created.
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > View Maintenance
