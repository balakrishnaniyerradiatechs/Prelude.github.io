Salesman Performance Planning screen
The Salesman Performance Planning screen allows you to enter and edit the sales representative’s base salary, expenses, projected sales, and gross profit percentage.
Menu: Marketing > Maintenance > Sales Representatives > F5-Perform
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Base Forward
The sales representative’s annual salary.
Base (monthly)
The sales representative’s monthly salary.
Expenses (monthly)
The sales representative’s expected monthly expenses.
Projection (monthly)
The sales representative’s expected monthly sales.
GP % (monthly)
The sales representative’s expected monthly gross profit percentage.
F2-Save
Saves the base and projected information.
