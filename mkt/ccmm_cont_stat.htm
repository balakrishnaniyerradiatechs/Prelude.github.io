Contact Statistics screen
In the Contact Statistics screen, you can view the contact’s total sales, returns, profit, and freight.
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > Customer Contacts > F9-Stats
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer number and description for the contact.
Life Sales
The total sales for this contact.
Contact
The contact number.
Last Sale Date
The date on which the contact last placed a sales order.
Last Quote Date
The date on which a quote was last entered for the contact.
Month
The month for which totals are shown.
Sales
The contact’s total sales for the month. Total shows the total for the year.
Returns
The contact’s total returns for the month.
Profit
The contact’s total profit for the month.
Freight
The contact’s total freight for the month.
OK
Closes the screen.
