Establishing Views
Views help you group product lines and major groups into categories that a customer and/or contact regularly purchases from. For example, you carry 10,000 items, but a customer only regularly purchases 3,000 of the items. The customer has two contacts: one who regularly purchases 2,000 items and one who regularly purchases 1,000 items. If you want to see sales for the customer and/or contacts for only those items, you can create a view for the set of products the customer buys and the subsets of products that each of the contacts buys. You can establish a default view in customer maintenance for the 3,000 products the customer purchases and default views for each of the contacts that represents the products they typically purchase. Then you can use Contact Management Statistics Inquiry to view sales for only the products in the views you created.
To create a view:
1
Choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Maintenance > View Maintenance. The View Maintenance screen appears.
2
For View Code, enter a new code (up to 12 alphanumeric characters) or and existing code or press F3 to search for an existing code.
3
For Description, enter or edit a brief description of the view.
4
For Description, enter the name of a new group of products or press ENTER on an existing group to edit the group of products. The View Definition Maintenance screen appears.
5
To add a major group to the view, enter the code for the major group in Major Groups.
6
Repeat step 5 until you add all the necessary major groups.
7
To add a product line to the view, enter EQ (equals) in the unlabeled field.
8
In Product Lines, enter the code for the product line you want to add or press F3 to search.
9
Repeat steps 7-8 for each product line you want to add.
10
To remove a product line from the view, enter NE (not equal) in the unlabeled field.
11
In Product Lines, enter the code for the product line you want to remove or press F3 to search.
12
Repeat steps 10-11 for each product line you want to remove.
13
When you are finished entering product lines and major groups, press F2-Save.
14
Repeat steps 4-13 for each group of products you want to add to the view.
15
Press F2-Save.
