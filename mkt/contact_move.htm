Contact Move screen
The Contact Move screen allows you to copy a contact from one customer or prospect to another, including addresses, e-mail, and phone information. A new contact with the next sequential number is created for the customer or prospect. The activity history, form/literature history, and notes remain with the original contact and will not move to the new contact. For the contact you copied from, Prohibit Sales and Obsolete are set to Y so that sales cannot be entered for that contact.
This process does not move historical data or delete the original contact record; it only copies the information from one customer to another.
Menu Path: Marketing > Processes > Copy Contact To New Customer
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
From Customer
The customer or prospect number for the contact that you want to copy.
Contact
The number of the contact you want to copy.
To Customer
The new customer number to copy the contact information to.
F2-Save
Moves the data and opens the Customer Contact Management Maintenance screen.
