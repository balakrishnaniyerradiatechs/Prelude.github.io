Contact Merge Process screen
The Contact Merge Process screen allows you to combine the history of two contacts. The history that is merged includes activity history, form letter/literature history, sales, statistics, product line objectives, major group objectives, order history, quote history, order pad, and notes. If open sales orders or quotes exist, they are updated with the To Contact number.
Menu Path: Marketing > Processes > Merge Contacts Within Customer
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer or prospect number for the contact numbers to be merged.
From Contact
The contact number to be used for the merge process.
To Contact
The contact number to be updated with the From Contact history.
F2-Save
Combines the data.
