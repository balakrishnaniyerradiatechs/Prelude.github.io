Setting up Customer Contacts
A contact is each person that you may do business with at the customer or a prospect’s company. Each customer or prospect can have multiple contacts. Then you can track marketing activities, statistics, sales orders, and quotes by contact rather than just by company.
The following screens are included in Customer Contact Management Maintenance:
•
Customer Contact Management Maintenance screen.
•
Contact Additional Information screen.
•
Customer Contact Keywords screen.
•
ANI Numbers screen.
•
Mailing Address screen.
•
Contact Statistics screen.
•
Product Line Objectives Information screen.
•
Major Group Objectives Information screen.
•
Web User Maintenance screen.
