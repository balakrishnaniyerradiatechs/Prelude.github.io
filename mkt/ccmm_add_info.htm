Contact Additional Information screen
In the Contact Additional Information screen, you can indicate how the contact prefers to be reached.
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > Customer Contacts > F5-Add Info
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Default View
Determines which view the contact uses. If blank, the view defined in Customer Maintenance is used. For more information on views, refer to Establishing Product Views.
Prohibit Sales
Indicates if this contact can place orders or receive quotes:
•
N - This contact can place orders and receive quotes.
•
Y - This contact cannot place orders or receive quotes.
Obsolete
Indicates if this contact is obsolete:
•
N - This contact is not obsolete.
•
Y - This contact is obsolete.
Date of Birth
The contact’s date of birth.
Universal Link
The number that links this contact with other contacts. You can link a contact with other contacts from different companies.
Fax?
Indicates if the contact wants to receive broadcast faxes (refer to Broadcast Faxing):
•
No - Contact does not want to receive broadcast faxes.
•
Yes - Contact wants to receive broadcast faxes.
E-Mail?
Indicates if the contact wants to receive broadcast emails (refer to Broadcast Emailing):
•
No - Contact does not want broadcast emails.
•
Yes - Contact wants to receive broadcast emails.
Call?
Indicates if the contact wants to receive phone calls:
•
No - Contact does not want to receive phone calls.
•
Yes - Contact wants to receive phone calls.
Mail?
Indicates if the contact wants to receive mail:
•
No - Contact does not want to receive mail.
•
Yes - Contact wants to receive mail.
Custom Fields (unlabeled)
Any custom fields you define. Refer to Establishing Custom Fields.
F2-Save
Saves the data.
F5-Keywords
Allows you to enter additional keywords for searching. Refer to Customer Contact Keywords screen.
F6-ANI Numbers
Allows you to enter Automatic Number Identification (ANI) numbers for the contact. Refer to ANI Numbers screen for more information.
