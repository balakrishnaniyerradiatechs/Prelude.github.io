Deleting a Campaign List
If you have campaign lists that you want to delete, you can delete them. To delete campaign lists:
1
Select Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Delete Campaign Lists. The Campaign List Deletion screen appears.
2
To delete the list, enter Y in Ok To Delete? and press ENTER.
