Adding Campaign Lists to a Campaign
Once you create a campaign, you can add existing campaign lists to the campaign.
To add a list:
1
On the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen, press F6-Lists to open the Campaign Lists screen appears.
2
For List Name, enter the name of the campaign list you want to add to the campaign (refer to Creating Campaign Lists for information on setting up campaign lists). Quantity defaults to the number of customers and/or prospects on the list
3
For Estimated Responses:
•
If necessary, change Quantity. The number of customers/prospects on the list defaults.
•
For %, enter the percentage of the customers and/or prospects that you expect to purchase items as a result of this campaign. Number automatically updates.
As customers buy items on the campaign, the % and Number for Actual Response are updated with the number of customers who purchased items. To view the total sales dollars for the campaign, press F5-Net Sales.
Also, the Cost Per Response and Campaign Gross Profit recalculate based on the Campaign Cost and number of sales orders for the campaign.
4
Repeat steps 2-3 for each list you want to add.
5
Press F2-Save.
