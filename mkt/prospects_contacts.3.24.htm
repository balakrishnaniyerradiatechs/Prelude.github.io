Copying a Contact to Another Customer
In the Contact Move screen, you can copy a contact from an old customer to a new customer. For example, John Smith is the contact at customer 100101 and changes employment to customer 100505. You can copy the contact John Smith from 100101 to 100505. This process does not move historical data or delete the original contact record; it only copies the information from one customer to another.
You cannot move contact information if the contact has open sales order or quotes.
For the contact you copied from, Prohibit Sales and Obsolete are set to Y so that sales cannot be entered for that contact.
To copy a contact:
1
Select Add. Modules > Marketing > Processes > Copy Contact To New Customer. The Contact Move screen appears.
2
For From Customer, enter the number of the customer you want to copy the contact from.
3
For Contact, enter the number of the contact that you want to copy.
4
In To Customer, enter the number of the customer you want to copy the contact to.
5
Press F2-Save.
