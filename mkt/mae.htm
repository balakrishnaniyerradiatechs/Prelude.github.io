Marketing Activity Entry screen
The Marketing Activity Entry screen allows you to edit and schedule marketing activities for a customer or prospect. You can also specify a contact for the customer or prospect. From a marketing activity, you can send form letters or catalogs or schedule a related marketing activity.
For more information, refer to Entering a Marketing Activity.
Menu Path: Marketing > Marketing Activity Entry

F2-Save
Saves the marketing activity.
F4-Del
Deletes the transaction.
F5-History
Displays history of the transaction, if any exists.
F6-Send Lit
Opens the Catalog Literature Information screen which allows you to select literature to send to the customer, prospect, or contact. For more information on literature, refer to Setting Up Catalogs/Literature Codes.
F7-Form Letter
Opens the Form Letter screen which allows you to select a form letter to send to the customer, prospect, or contact. For more information on form letters, refer to Maintaining Form Letters.
F9-Notes
Allows you to enter notes for the marketing activity.
Action Bar
BrowseNext

BrowseLast
Goes to the next customer or prospect based on a search list, if any.
Recurring
Opens the Recurring Marketing Events screen, which allows you to set a schedule for marketing activities the occur regularly. Refer to Scheduling a Recurring Activity.
