Sending a Form Letter
When you enter a marketing activity, you can indicate that a form letter should be sent to the customer or prospect (for more information on form letters, refer to Maintaining Form Letters).
 
Based on your Marketing Option Parameters (refer toChoosing Marketing Options), you can either print the form letter after saving the activity or you can print form letters by batch (refer to Batch Printing Form Letters). If Printer Form Letters Batch is set to:
•
N - form letters print after you save the activity.
•
Y - form letters are printed only by batch.
•
A - when you select a form letter to print, you choose whether to batch print the letter or print it when you save the activity.
To send a form letter:
1
Start entering a marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity).
2
Press F7-Form Letter. The Form Letter screen appears.
3
In Form Letter, enter the code for the form letter you want to send or press F3 to search and press F2-Save.
4
Choose one of the following on the dialog box that appears:
•
Print - Send the letter to your default printer.
•
Fax - Fax the letter to the customer, prospect, or contact.
•
Email - Email the letter to the email address for the customer, prospect, or contact.
•
Return - Go back to the Form Letter screen.
If Print Form Letters Batch in Marketing Option Parameters is set to A, the following message appears:
Do You Wish To Print In Batch Mode Later Or Print Immediately After Completing This Activity?
5
Choose one of the following:
•
Batch - Batch generate the form letter. Refer to Batch Printing Form Letters.
•
Immediately - The form letter generates when you save the activity.
6
Repeat steps 2-5 for each letter you want to send.
