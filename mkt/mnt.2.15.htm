Establishing Custom Fields
You can create custom fields to use in Prospect Maintenance (refer to the Prospect Additional Info Screen) and Customer Contact Management Maintenance (refer to the Contact Additional Information screen) to track information specific to your company. You can add up to 4 fields in Prospect Maintenance and 2 fields in Customer Contact Management Maintenance.
To establish custom fields:
1
Select Marketing > Maintenance > Custom Field Descriptions to open the Prospect Mnt Custom Field Descriptions screen.
2
Under Prospect, enter the field name for the data you want to track in Prospect Maintenance in User1 Defined Field, User2 Defined Field, User 3 Defined Field, and/or User4 Defined Field.
3
Under Contact, enter the field name for the data you want to track in Customer Contact Management Maintenance in User1 Defined Field and/or User2 Defined Field.
4
Press F2-Save.
