Creating a Diary Entry
The contact diary allows you to create an abbreviated marketing activity. You cannot send catalog literature or form letters from a contact diary entry.
To enter a marketing activity in the contact diary:
1
Select Marketing > Contact Diary System > Contact Diary Entry to open the Contact Diary Entry screen.
2
For Prospect Number, enter the customer or prospect number or press F3 to search.
3
If you are using mail-to addresses for contacts, indicate which addresses you want to use in Mail-To.
4
For Contact Type, choose the type of contact (e.g., Engineer, Manager, Marketing).
5
For Contact Method, enter the code that describes how the prospect or customer was contacted or press F3 to search. Choose one of the following:
•
TH - Telephone.
•
MT - Meeting.
•
ML - Mail.
•
FAX - Fax.
•
EM - Email.
6
If you want to use a Contact, enter the number of the contact or press F3 to search. For more information on contacts, refer to Setting up Customer Contacts.
7
If your company uses a Diary Code to more specifically identify the type of contact, enter the code or press F3 to search. Refer to Identifying Diary Codes.
8
Change the Entry Date, Time, and User ID, if necessary.
9
If you want to schedule a follow up activity based on this contact diary entry, enter the Follow Up Date and Time that the activity should appear in the Marketing Activity Contact Inquiry or Marketing Activity Inquiry.
10
Enter the User ID of the person who should receive the activity (defaults to your user ID).
11
Enter a Description of the marketing activity.
12
For Document Type, Document Number, and Product Number, choose one of the following:
•
If your system is set up to default related information based on a maintenance or entry screen, the product number, document type, and/or document number may already be entered. Change the data as necessary.
•
If your system is not set up to default related information or you did not start from a related maintenance or entry screen, enter the related product number, document type, and/or document number as necessary.
About Defaulted Information
You can set up Prelude to default information related to a marketing activity based on the last maintenance or entry screen you were in. For example, if you are in Sales Order Entry and go directly to Marketing Activity Entry or Marketing Activity Contact Entry, Doc Type defaults to S (sales order) and the sales order number appears in Doc#. Also, if you are in Product Maintenance and enter a marketing activity, Prod#, PLine, and Group default based on the product’s settings. For more information on setting up these defaults, refer to Setting Up Activity Defaults.
13
Enter a Serial Number, if necessary.
14
To enter notes about the activity, press F7-Notes, enter the notes, and press F2-Save.
15
When you are finished entering information about the marketing activity, press F2-Save.
