Merging Campaign Lists
If you have two lists that you want to combine, you can merge them. To merge campaign lists:
1
Select Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Merge Campaign Lists to open the Campaign List Merge screen.
2
Enter the name of the list you want to merge into in First List Name.
3
In Second List Name, enter the name of the list you want to merge with the First List Name. The list you enter in Second List Name also remains as a separate list.
4
Press ENTER.
