CMSI Product Detail screen
The Product Detail screen displays sales by quarter for each product in a view (refer to Contact Management Statistics Inquiry and Establishing Product Views).
Menu Path: Marketing > Inquiries > Contact Management Statistics Inquiry > F5-Detail
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer for whom you are viewing sales.
Contact
The contact for whom you are viewing sales. This only displays if the view is on the contact level.
Products...
The categories established for this view type.
Description
The description of the product.
YearTD
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the current year.
This Quarter
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the current quarter.
Last Quarter
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the previous quarter.
2nd Previous Quarter
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the 2nd previous quarter.
3rd Previous Quarter
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the 3rd previous quarter.
4th Previous Quarter
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the 4th previous quarter.
LstYTD
The customer or contact’s sales for this product category in the previous year to date.
F2-Save
Closes the screen.
F5-Detail
Opens the Contact Management Detail Inquiry to show each order on which the product was sold.
F9-Goto Line
Identify a reference number on the screen that you want to jump to. Valid codes are product number, page number, line number, B (bottom), or T (top).

User Fields
