Activity Defaults Maintenance screen
When you create a marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity), you can set up defaults for scheduled activities based on the activities that you select. For example, if you called the contact and successfully sold products, you might choose Phone Call, then Successful. If an activity default is set up for that type of activity, a scheduled To Do and Phone marketing activity may automatically default. The default might be to schedule a Phone marketing activity in two weeks after the customer received the product to verify that they are happy with the product. The To Do default activity may be to follow up in a month to see if the customer needs any additional products.
You can base these defaults on the settings that came with Prelude or you can use the Menu Definitions tool to create your own defaults. Before using the Menu Definition tool, see your System Administrator.
