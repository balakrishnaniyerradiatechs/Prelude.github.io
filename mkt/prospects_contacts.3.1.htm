Setting up Prospects and Contacts
You can set up a potential customer as a prospect account and then use the account to enter work orders, quotes, and marketing activities for the prospect. If the prospect becomes a customer, you can convert the account to a valid customer account so that you can enter sales orders. Every valid customer is a prospect and can be accessed in Prospect Maintenance, but every prospect is not necessarily a customer.
You can also set up a contact record for each person that you do business with at a customer or prospect location. You can then track marketing activities and view statistics by contact.
