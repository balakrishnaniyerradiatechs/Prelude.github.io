Batch Printing Form Letters
If you chose to batch print form letter(s) when entering an activity (refer to Sending a Form Letter) or if all of your form letters are printed by batch, you must batch print the letters when you are ready to print, fax, or email them. To batch print a form letter:
1
Select Marketing > Processes > Form Letter Batch Print. The Form Letter Batch Print screen appears.
2
For Form Letter Number, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the code(s) for the types of form letters you want to print.
•
To print all form letters, leave Form Letter Number blank.
3
For Customer, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the number for each customer for which you want to print form letters.
•
To print form letters for all customers, leave Customer blank.
4
For Print Type, choose one of the following:
•
P - Only generate form letters that were flagged to print.
•
E - Only generate form letters that were flagged to email.
•
F - Only generate form letters that were flagged to fax.
•
Leave blank to generate all form letters.
5
For Printer Form Queue, enter the number of the printer you want to print the form letters to.
6
Press F2-Save and choose Execute. A dialog box with the following message appears:
Do You Wish To Print Mailing Labels For the Form Letters Just Printed?
7
Choose one of the following:
•
Reprompt - Keep the dialog box open.
•
No - Do not print labels.
•
Yes - Print labels. On the Print screen that appears, select a printer and stationery and press F2-Save.
A dialog box with the following message appears:
Printing Completed. Do You Wish To Process?
8
Choose one of the following:
•
Yes - Indicates that the form letters printed correctly and do not need to be reprinted.
•
No - Indicates that the form lettered did not print correctly. Repeat this process to regenerate the letters.
