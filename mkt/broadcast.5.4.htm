Broadcast Emailing
You can email a group of prospects and/or customers using a letter you write when you email it or you can use a form letter (refer to Maintaining Form Letters). To send a broadcast email:
1
Select Marketing > Broadcast/Export Functions > Broadcast Email. The Send Broadcast Email screen appears.
2
For Msg#, choose one of the following:
•
To edit an email, enter the name of the email or press F3 to search.
•
To create an email, press ENTER to get a new number.
3
For Blind Flag, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Recipients only see their email address.
•
N - Recipients see email address of all other recipients.
4
Edit the From email address if necessary. Defaults to the email address associated with your user ID.
5
In To, enter the email addresses of the recipients using one of the following methods:
•
Type each email address.
•
Enter a campaign list name. Refer to Building a Campaign List.
•
Enter a select list name from a saved TCL list.
•
Enter the name of a list created using the list selector. Refer to Selecting a Broadcast List.
•
Press F6-Enter Keys Manually to choose who to email. The Manually enter customers/vendors/etc. screen appears. For File, enter the name of the file to use: CUSTOMER, VEND, PROSPECT, or PROS.CONT. For Number, enter the numbers of the customer, vendor, prospect, or contact you want to email. Press F2-Save.
 
If the email preferences for the customer, vendor, prospect, or contact indicate that they do not want to receive email, that customer, vendor, prospect, or contact is not emailed even if they are in the email list.
6
For File, enter the name of the file from which you want to extract email address or merged fields:
•
CUSTOMER.
•
VEND.
•
PROSPECT.
•
PROS.CONT.
7
In Subject, enter the subject line of the email.
8
If you want to attach a file, in Attach, enter the full path to the document to attach (e.g., \/tmp/attach.pdf). The file should be in the /tmp on the Prelude server. You must have full permissions to a file to attach it.
9
For Body of Message, choose one of the following:
•
Type the text of the email.
•
To use a form letter, press F5-Load Form Letter and choose a letter. For more information, refer to Maintaining Form Letters.
 
To insert data from a file into an email, use a merged field (e.g., a contact’s name from the PROS.CONT file). To use a merged field, first determine the dictionary name of the field you want to use. The field name of the contact name in PROS.CONT is CONT.DESC. In the body of the email, include the field name, surrounded by carets (e.g, ^CONT.DESC^).
10
Press F2-Save twice. A dialog box with the following message appears:
Send email/fax now?
11
Choose Yes to save and send the email now or No save the email without sending it.
