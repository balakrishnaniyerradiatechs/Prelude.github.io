Adding Products
When you create a campaign, you can add products that will be promoted on the campaign. When a customer or prospect on a campaign list purchases one of these products, the campaign’s statistics are updated. If the campaign includes all products, do not enter any products in this screen.
To add products to a campaign:
1
On the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen, press F7-Products to open the Product Detail screen appears.
2
For Typ, choose one of the following:
•
P - To enter a product.
•
R - To enter a product prefix. Only available if your company uses product prefixes.
3
Enter the product number or product prefix in Product. The product description appears.
4
In Qty Discount, indicate if you want to use regular or discounted pricing:
•
N - Use regular pricing.
•
Y - Use discounted pricing for the campaign.
5
If you entered Y for Qty Disc, choose one of the following for Brk:
•
N - Do not give quantity breaks. Skip to step 9.
•
Y - Give quantity breaks. The Qty Break Entry screen appears.
6
Enter the table name, number, or code of the sum and reprice group you want to use in SRP Group.
7
Enter the number of the quantity break table you want to use in Brk Table or press F3.
8
Press F2-Save and skip to step 10.
9
If you entered Y for Qty Disc and N for Brk, enter the discounted campaign price in Price.
10
If necessary, provide the following information about the product’s placement in the campaign literature:
•
Page - The page number on which the product is featured.
•
Sq Inch - The number of square inches the product description and/or picture takes up in the campaign literature.
•
Pict - Enter Y if the literature has a picture of the product. Enter N if the literature does not have a picture of the product.
•
Highlight - Enter Y if the product is highlighted in the literature. Enter N if the product is not highlighted.
11
Repeat steps 2-10 for each product you want to add to the campaign.
12
Press F2-Save.
