Assigning Products and Lists to a Campaign
After you create campaign lists, you can create a campaign and specify products and lists for the campaign.
To create a campaign:
1
Select Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Maintenance to open the Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen.
2
For Campaign Number, choose one of the following:
•
To edit a campaign, enter the campaign number.
•
To create a campaign, enter a new campaign number.
3
In Description, enter a brief description of the campaign.
4
Enter the number of Pages in the campaign literature.
5
For Mail Date, enter the date on which the literature will be sent.
6
For Beginning Date, enter the date on which the campaign starts. If you use campaign pricing, that pricing goes into effect on this date.
7
For Ending Date, enter the date on which the campaign ends. If you use campaign pricing, that pricing expires on this date.
8
For Campaign Cost, enter the cost of the campaign (e.g., time spent working on the campaign, printing costs). This cost is used to calculate cost per response and campaign GP (refer to Adding Campaign Lists to a Campaign).
9
From here, choose one of the following:
•
F5-Comments. To enter comments about the campaign.
•
F6-Lists. To add campaign lists to the campaign. Refer to Adding Campaign Lists to a Campaign.
•
F7-Product. To add products to the campaign. Refer to Adding Products.
•
F8-Product Listing. To view the products on the campaign.
•
F2-Save. If you are finished entering information on the campaign.
