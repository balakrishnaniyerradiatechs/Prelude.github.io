Prospect Maintenance screen
In the Prospect Maintenance screen, you can enter and edit information about prospects and customers. The information on this screen is identical to Prospect Inquiry.
Menu Path: Marketing > Prospect Maintenance
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer or Prospect (not labeled)
Indicates if the record is for a customer or a prospect:
•
C - Customer.
•
P - Prospect.
Prospect
The prospect or customer number.
Mail-To
If you are using mail-to addresses, the number of the mail-to address for the prospect.
Description
The prospect’s company name.
Desc (To Sort By)
The description to use for sorting. For example, if the prospect’s company name is The Supply Company but you want to sort by Supply, enter The Supply Company in Description and Supply Company in Desc (To Sort By).
Address One
The first line of the customer or prospect’s address.
Address Two
The second line of the customer or prospect’s address.
Address Three
The third line of the customer or prospect’s address.
Country
The country in which the customer or prospect is based.
GeoCode
If you are using GeoCode maintenance, the code that represents the city, state, and ZIP code in which the customer or prospect is based.
City
The city in which the customer or prospect is based.
State
The state in which the customer or prospect is based.
Zip
The ZIP code in which the customer or prospect is based.
County
The county in which the customer or prospect is based.
E-Mail Address
The e-mail address of the main contact at the customer or prospect.
Phone
The phone number for the main contact at the customer or prospect.
Ext.
The phone extension for the main contact at the customer or prospect.
Fax
The fax number of the primary contact at the customer or prospect.
Primary Contact
The primary contact number for the account. If it is a new customer, the Customer Contact Management Maintenance screen opens. For more information on entering contacts, refer to Setting up Customer Contacts.
Salesrep #1
The primary salesrep for the account. Refer to Maintaining Sales Representatives.
Salesrep #2
The secondary salesrep for the account. Refer to Maintaining Sales Representatives.
F2-Save
Saves the customer or prospect’s information.
F4-Del
Deletes the record.
F5-Add Info
Opens the Prospect Additional Info Screen screen, which allows you to enter or edit warehouse, territory, SICs, potential codes, and source code information.
F6-Contacts
Opens the Customer Contact Management Maintenance screen, which allows you to create contacts for a customer or prospect.
F7-Next
Moves to the next prospect or customer on the search list, if you previously searched for prospects or customers.
F8-Last
Moves to the previous prospect or customer on the search list, if you previously searched for prospects or customers.
F9-Activity
Displays the Marketing Activity Entry screen which allows you to enter marketing activities for this customer or prospect. Refer to Entering a Marketing Activity.
Action Bar
User Fields
See your System Administrator for more information.
Notes
Allows you to enter notes about the prospect or customer. These notes do not print anywhere.
Add’l Options > Competitor Information
Opens the Competitor Information screen which allows you to enter competitor codes and notes. For more information, refer to Competitor Information screen.
Add’l Options > Sales Potential Information
Opens the Sales Potential screen which allows you to enter sales objectives by major group.
Add’l Options > Activity History
Opens the Prospect Activity History Inquiry.
Add’l Options > Product Line Objectives
Opens the Product Line Objectives screen to list product line objectives for each of the customer or prospect’s contacts.
Add’l Options > Major Group Objectives
Opens the Major Group Objectives screen which lists major objectives for each of the customer or prospect’s contacts.
Add’l Options > Form Letter and Literature History
Opens the Form & Literature Activity History screen which lists form letters and marketing literature sent to the customer or prospect.
Add’l Options > Marketing Notification List
Opens the Marketing Notification List screen, which allow you to enter user IDs of each person you want to notify when a marketing activity is entered for a customer or prospect.
Add’l Options > Verify Address
Allows you to verify that the prospect’s address is correct. Works with the optional Mailroom server. For more information, see your System Administrator.
