Marketing Activity Contact Entry screen
The Marketing Activity Contact Entry screen allows you to edit and schedule marketing activities for a customer/prospect and contact. From a marketing activity, you can send form letters or catalogs or schedule a related marketing activity.
For more information, refer to Entering a Marketing Activity.
Menu Path: Marketing > Marketing Activity Contact Entry

F6-Send Lit
Opens the Catalog Literature Information screen so you can select literature to send to the customer, prospect, or contact. For more information on literature, refer to Setting Up Catalogs/Literature Codes.
F7-Form Letter
Opens the Form Letter screen so you can select a form letter to send to the customer, prospect, or contact. For more information on form letters, refer to Maintaining Form Letters.

Allows you to enter notes for the marketing activity.
Action Bar
Contact Inquiry

Recurring
Opens the Recurring Marketing Events screen, which allows you to set a schedule for marketing activities that occur regularly. For more information, refer to Scheduling a Recurring Activity.
