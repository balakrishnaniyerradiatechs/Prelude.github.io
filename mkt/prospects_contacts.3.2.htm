Setting up Prospect Accounts
You can set up potential customers as prospects in Prospect Maintenance and then use the account to enter work orders, quotes, and marketing activities for the prospect. If the prospect becomes a customer, you can convert the account to a valid customer account so that you can enter sales orders.
Every valid customer is a prospect and can be accessed in Prospect Maintenance, but every Prospect is not necessarily a customer.
The following screens are included in Prospect Maintenance:
•
Prospect Maintenance screen.
•
Prospect Additional Info Screen.
•
Prospect Keywords screen.
•
Competitor Information screen.
•
Sales Potential screen.
•
Product Line Objectives screen.
•
Major Group Objectives screen.
•
Form & Literature Activity History screen.
•
Marketing Notification List screen.
You can view information on prospects and customers in Marketing > Inquiries > Prospect Maintenance Inquiry.
