Maintaining Sales Representatives
Sales representatives are used in Customer and Prospect Maintenance to identify who from your company is assigned to the customer or prospect.
 
You can also view a list of existing sales representatives. To view the list, choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Listings > Sales Representatives.
