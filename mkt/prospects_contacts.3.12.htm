Updating Prospects from a Campaign Saved List
If you want to update a group of customers or prospects based on a campaign list (refer to Building a Campaign List), you can use the Automated Prospect Maintenance screen. You can use the functionality of this list even if you are not using campaigns. To update prospects based on a campaign list:
1
Select Marketing > Prospect Maintenance Via Saved List. The Automated Prospect Maintenance screen appears.
2
In List Name, enter the name of the campaign list for which you want to edit customers and/or prospects.
3
In Prospect Number, choose one of the following:
•
To start with a customer and/or prospect, enter the customer or prospect’s number or press F3 to search.
•
To edit all customers and/or prospects in the list, leave Prospect Number blank.
4
Press F2-Save. The Prospect Maintenance screen appears.
5
Edit the customer or prospect as necessary. Refer to Setting up Prospect Accounts.
Each time you press F2-Save on the Prospect Maintenance screen, the next customer or prospect on the list automatically appears on the screen.
