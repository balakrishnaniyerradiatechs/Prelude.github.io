Working With Marketing Activities
A marketing activity is a process that allows you to record events for a customer or prospect that you want to track. Marketing activities can include site visits, phone calls, product demonstrations, form letters, and other literature. You can track marketing activities three ways:
•
By customer or prospect.
•
By customer or prospect and contact.
•
By contact diary.
Your company should select one consistent method to enter marketing contacts.
 
Many fields on the Marketing module reference a prospect number. This number can also be a customer number.
Entering Marketing Activities without Contacts
Allows you to enter activities for a customer or prospect. You may also specify a contact at the customer or prospect. You can change the contact person on the activity as needed. Use Marketing Activity Entry to record this type of marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity).
Entering Marketing Activities with Contacts
Allows you to enter activities for a customer or prospect. You must specify a contact from the contacts file at the customer or prospect. You cannot change the contact person for the activity. Use Marketing Activity Contact Entry to record this type of marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity). If you want to record activities with or without contacts and be able to change the contact, use Marketing Activity Entry and specify the contact when needed.
Using the Contact Diary
Allows you to enter activities for a customer or prospect. You may also specify a contact at the customer or prospect. However, you cannot send form letters or other literature as you can in Marketing Activity Entry or Marketing Activity Contact Entry. Use Contact Diary Entry to record types of activities that are not automatically created by any of the marketing activities. Transactions that may be included are:
•
Sales orders.
•
Quotes.
•
Product shipment.
•
Campaign literature.
•
Work order completions.
•
Marketing activities.
•
Sample requests.
If you enter marketing activities using Marketing Activity Entry or Marketing Activity Contact Entry, you can still use the Contact Diary Inquiry and Contact Diary Report to view all transactions for a customer or prospect.
