Broadcast Emailing and Faxing
You can email or fax letters to a list of customers and/or prospects based on a list. You can either generate the list when you send the email or fax, or you can use a previously created list. When you send the letter, you can either generate the letter when you email or fax it, or you can use a previously created form letter (refer to Maintaining Form Letters).
