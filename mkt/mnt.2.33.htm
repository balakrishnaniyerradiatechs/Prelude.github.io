Entering Titles
Titles are used to identify job responsibilities for contacts (for more information on contacts, refer to Maintaining Contacts). You can then use the titles in reports or to build campaign or broadcast lists (refer to Working with Campaign Lists and Selecting a Broadcast List).
 
You can also view a list of existing titles. To view the list, choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Listings > Contact Listings.
To enter a title:
1
Choose Add. Modules > Marketing > Maintenance > Contact Titles. The Contact Title Maintenance screen appears.
2
For Title, enter a new code (up to 2 alphanumeric characters) or and existing code or press F3 to search for an existing code (e.g., 01, 02).
3
For Description, enter or edit a brief description of the title (e.g., President, Manager).
4
Press F2-Save.
