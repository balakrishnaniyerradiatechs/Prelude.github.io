Sending Literature
When you enter a marketing activity, you can indicate that you want to send literature to the customer or prospect and/or contact. For more information about setting up literature, refer to Setting Up Catalog Literature.
To send literature:
1
Start entering a marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity).
2
Press F6-Send Lit. The Catalog Literature Information screen appears.
3
To select literature to send, enter the Code for the literature or press F3 to search.
4
Repeat step 3 for each piece of literature that you want to send.
5
When you are finished entering the activity, press F2-Save.
When you are ready to send the literature, run the Send Literature Report to see which pieces of literature you need to send.
