Scheduling a Recurring Activity
If you enter a marketing activity that occurs regularly, you can set a schedule for the activity to automatically appear on the Marketing Activity Inquiry or the Marketing Activity Contact Inquiry. For example, if you want to call a customer or prospect on the first of every month, you can set a recurring activity to appear on the first day of every month for that customer.
To schedule a recurring activity:
1
While you are in a marketing activity (refer to Entering a Marketing Activity), select Recurring from the Action Bar to open the Recurring Marketing Events screen.
2
To set up a recurring event, enter Y for Recurring.
3
Choose a recurring Method and define the recurring pattern:
•
DOM - Day of month. For Day Of Month, enter the date of each month on which you want this marketing activity to be automatically generated. For example, for the marketing activity to generate automatically on the 15th of every month, enter 15 for Day Of Month.
•
DOW - Day of week. Select the day of the week on which you want the marketing activity to automatically generate. To select a day, enter Y for Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and/or Sunday. For example, for the marketing activity to generate automatically each Monday, enter Y for Monday.
•
NOD - Number of days. For Number Of Days, enter the number of days between marketing activities. For example, for the marketing activity to generate automatically every other day, enter 2 for Number Of Days.
4
Press F2-Save to schedule the recurring activity.
