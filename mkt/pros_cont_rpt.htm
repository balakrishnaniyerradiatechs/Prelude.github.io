Prospect Contact Report
The Prospect Contact Report lists contacts for customers and prospects.
Menu Path: Marketing > Reports > Prospect Contact Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division Number. The highest possible grouping in your company.
•
Region Number. A subset of Division - if Division is used.
•
Warehouse Number. The warehouse for the customer or prospect.
•
Department Number. A subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used.
•
Sales Rep. The sales representative code for the customer or prospect.
•
Prospect. The customer or prospect number on the activity or transaction.
•
Sic Code. The SIC for the customer or prospect. For more information, refer to Using SICs.
•
Zip. The ZIP code of the contact.
•
Title. The contact’s title. Refer to Assigning Job Titles to Contacts.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Prospect
Line one - The number of the customer or prospect.
Line two - The name of the customer or prospect.
Mailto
The number of the mail-to address on the activity, if you are using mail-to addresses.
Contact
The name of the contact.
Address
Line one - The first line of the contact’s address.
Line two - The second line of the contact’s address.
Line three - The third line of the contact’s address.
Line four - The city, state, and ZIP of the contact’s address.
Title
The contact’s title.
Phone/Fax
Line one - The contact’s phone number.
Line two - The contact’s fax number.
Ext.
The contact’s phone extension.
