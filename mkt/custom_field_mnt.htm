Prospect Mnt Custom Field Descriptions screen
The Prospect Mnt Custom Field Descriptions screen allows you to create custom fields to use in the Prospect Additional Info Screen (in Prospect Maintenance) and the Contact Additional Information screen (in Customer Contact Management Maintenance) to track information specific to your company. You can add up to 4 fields in Prospect Maintenance and 2 fields in Customer Contact Management Maintenance.
For more information, refer to Establishing Custom Fields.
Menu Path: Marketing > Maintenance > Custom Field Descriptions
