Contact Management Statistics Inquiry
The Contact Management Statistics Inquiry displays sales information for a customer. You can also view the information by contact. The sales history that appears is based on the default view for the customer and contact. A view is group of product lines or major groups. By establishing a view for a customer or contact, you can limit the information that appears to only the product lines or major groups that the customer or contact typically buys from. For more information on views, refer to Establishing Product Views.
If the contact does not have a default view, the view for the customer is used. If the customer does not have a default view, no information appears.
Viewing Contact Management Statistics
To view the Contact Management Statistics Inquiry:
1
Choose Marketing > Inquiries > Contact Management Statistics Inquiry. The Contact Management Statistics Inquiry screen appears.
2
For Customer Number, enter the number of the customer or prospect for which you want to see sales history or press F3 to search. Sales for the customer appears in the customerâ€™s default view.
3
To see sales history by contact, highlight the contact you want to see in Cont and press F6-Toggle View.
4
To change the view, press F8-Change View and select the view you want to use.
