Creating a Campaign
After you create campaign lists, you can create a campaign and specify products and lists for the campaign. To create a campaign:
1
Select Marketing > Campaign System > Campaign Lists > Campaign Maintenance. The Marketing Campaign Maintenance screen appears.
2
For Campaign Number, choose one of the following:
•
To edit a campaign, enter the campaign number or press F3.
•
To create a campaign, enter a new campaign number.
3
In Description, enter a brief description of the campaign.
4
For Pages, enter the number of pages in the campaign literature.
5
For Mail Date, enter the date on which the literature will be sent.
6
For Beginning Date, enter the date on which the campaign starts. If you use campaign pricing, that pricing goes into effect on this date.
7
For Ending Date, enter the date on which the campaign ends. If you use campaign pricing, that pricing expires on this date.
8
For Campaign Cost, enter the cost of the campaign (e.g., time spent working on the campaign, printing costs). This cost is used to calculate cost per response and campaign GP (refer to Adding Campaign Lists to a Campaign).
9
Choose one of the following:
•
If you are finished entering information for this campaign, press F2-Save.
•
To enter comments about the campaign, press F5-Comments, enter your notes, and press F2-Save.
•
To add campaign lists to the campaign, press F6-Lists and refer to Adding Campaign Lists to a Campaign.
•
To add products to the campaign, press F7-Product and refer to Adding Products.
•
To view the products that are on the campaign, press F8-Product Listing.
