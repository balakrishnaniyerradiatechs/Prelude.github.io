Easy Lookup
Redirects you to the default page of the VIA website (Shopping Activities > Easy Lookup).
Quick Order Entry
Instead of seeing all products in a list, use Quick Order Entry to enter the desired product number without first searching for the product (Shopping Activities > Quick Order Entry).
Express Order Entry
During regular order entry, each product entry is verified. Express Order Entry allows you to enter the product or description without verification to quickly place items in your cart. Products are verified after you enter all items. Only valid products stay on the sales order (Shopping Activities > Express Order Entry).
Historical Orders
View and duplicate previous orders (Inquiries > Historical Orders).
