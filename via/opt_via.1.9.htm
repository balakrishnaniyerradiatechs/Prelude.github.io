Advertising Banner Maintenance
The Advertising Banner allows you to display types of advertising schemes to enhance your company’s business. Advertising items can be a product(s), a shopping list, or a link to another web site.
•
Banner Name: The name of the banner to be displayed.
•
Banner Image: The name of the banner image.
•
Type: The banner type: P - product, L - list of products, U - URL link to another web site.
•
Product/List/Url: The description of the banner type: P - product, L - the list name, U - URL address.
•
Banner Overtext: The text to display as the mouse hovers over the banner.
