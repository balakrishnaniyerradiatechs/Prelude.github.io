Removing or Restoring Customer Usage
If you have lost a customer and want to remove the usage recorded for a product, vendor, or product line so that forecasting numbers no longer reflect the customer’s usage, you can remove the customer’s usage from your forecast analysis. Once the customer’s usage is removed, you can restore it if you regain the customer’s business.
To remove or restore historical customer usage information:
1
Select Purchasing > Forecasting > Customer Usage Adjustments > Customer Usage Adjustment Procedure to open the Remove/Restore Customer Usage Procedure screen.
2
Enter the Customer for whom you want to remove/restore product usage.
3
Enter the Warehouse affected by the usage adjustment.
4
Enter the Product number to limit the usage adjustment to one product.
5
Enter the Primary Vendor that you want to remove/restore the selected customer’s usage for.
6
Enter the Product Line that you want to remove/restore the selected customer’s usage for.
7
For Remove/Restore Usage?, choose one of the following:
•
R - Remove the historical usage for the selected customers.
•
T - Restore previously removed usage.
8
For Adjust Override Usage?, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Back out the calculated customer usage values from any override usage as well as system calculated usage.
•
N - Do not back out calculated customer usage values.
9
Select Execute to adjust the customer usage.
