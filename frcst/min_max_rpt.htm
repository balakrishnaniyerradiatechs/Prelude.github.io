Minimum / Maximum Report
The Minimum / Maximum report includes products flagged as min/max items and displays the min/max settings along with the last 12 months of usage. The report also shows the forecast quantity and indicates if the usage is sporadic. Refer to the Example of Sporadic Usage.
Minimum and Maximum values for a product/warehouse are established in Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Forecasting Subsystem > Minimum / Maximum Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used. (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where the material is stocked.
•
Department. A subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product numbers to include on the report.
•
Product Type. The product types to include on the report. Choose one or more of the following:
-
F - Finished.
-
R - Raw.
-
B - Both finished and raw.
-
E - Expense.
-
U - Used.
-
N - Rental.
•
Stocking Status Code. The product status codes to include on the report. Choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Non-stock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
Primary Vendor. The primary vendors to include on the report.
•
Product Line. The product lines to include in the report. Product lines are used to group products together for sales functions.
•
Minimum. The min/max products to exclude that do not have a minimum value greater than or equal to this value.
•
Maximum. The min/max products to exclude that do not have a maximum value greater than or equal to this value.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number and description.
Cls
The inventory classification. Refer to “Classifying Products” in the Inventory documentation.
Frcst
The forecast quantity for this item.
Sp
Sporadic usage:
•
Y - The product has sporadic usage. Refer to the Example of Sporadic Usage.
•
N - The product does not have sporadic usage.
Min
The minimum quantity to stock for this item.
Max
The maximum quantity to stock for this item.
Thaw Dte
The thaw date. The date the frozen controls will be removed.
S
The product’s stocking status:
•
S - Stock.
•
N - Non stock.
•
C - Consigned.
Months
The quantity sold in each of the corresponding past 12 months.
