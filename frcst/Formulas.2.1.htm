Forecasting Formulas and Trends
Although the Forecasting module provides existing forecast formulas, you can use Forecast Formula Maintenance to modify or create formulas more applicable to your inventory requirements. You can then assign the formulas to your products at different levels in the system. The first level, Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance, overrides the second level, Vendor Line Maintenance, and so forth. The following hierarchy determines which level is used first:
•
1st - Product/warehouse level (Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance).
•
2nd - Vendor level (Vendor Line Maintenance).
•
3rd - Product line level (Product Line Maintenance).
•
4th - Company level (Purchase Parameters Maintenance).
