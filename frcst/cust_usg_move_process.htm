Moving Customer Usage
If an existing customer is assigned to a different warehouse within your company, you may want to move the usage recorded for a product, vendor, or product line to the new warehouse. You can move the customer’s usage to a new warehouse to correctly forecast usage for both warehouses.
To move a customer’s usage to a new warehouse:
1
Select Purchasing > Forecasting > Customer Usage Adjustments > Move Customer Usage Procedure to open the Move Customer Usage to New Whse Procedure screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number for whom you want to move product usage.
3
For From Warehouse Number, enter the warehouse that the customer was originally assigned to.
4
For To Warehouse Number, enter the new warehouse where the customer’s sales will be assigned to.
5
Enter the Product number to limit the usage adjustment to one product.
6
Enter the Primary Vendor that you want to move the selected customer’s usage for, if you only want to move usage for one vendor.
7
Enter the Product Line that you want to move the selected customer’s usage for, if you only want to move usage for one product line.
8
For Adjust Override Usage?, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Back out the calculated customer usage values from any override usage as well as system calculated usage.
•
N - Do not back out calculated customer usage values.
9
Select Execute to move the customer’s usage to the new warehouse.
Usage Audit Inquiry
The CMV code in the Usage Audit Inquiry (Inventory > Inquiries > Transaction Audit Inquiries > Usage Audit Inquiry) indicates that product usage was adjusted in the Move Customer Usage Procedure.
