Generating a DRP Purchase Order
After you run and review the Distribution Requirements Planning Report, you can use the same selection criteria to automatically create a purchase order for the recommended early buy quantities. The generated purchase order appears in the Buyer Queue Inquiry under Recommended Purchase Orders.
To generate a DRP purchase order:
1
Choose Purchasing > Forecasting Subsystem > Distribution Requirements Planning > DRP Purchase Order Generation Process to open the DRP Purchase Order Generation screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse that will receive the merchandise.
3
Enter the primary Vendor for which you want to generate the purchase order.
4
Enter the Buyer to limit the purchase order to products assigned to a buyer.
5
For Consolidate w/Warehouses, enter any additional warehouses to accumulate totals with the primary Warehouse Number defined. Enter ALL to get a complete corporate view of all warehouses. The Corporate view allows you to evaluate purchasing requirements for all warehouses in the company that use the DRP purchase method for a product. Leave blank to only view the forecast for the warehouse that you entered in Warehouse Number.
6
Enter a Purchase Method to limit the purchase order to one method (e.g., EOQ, DRP, CLS, AWU).
7
Enter a Product Line to limit the order to a product line.
8
Enter a beginning and ending Product to limit the order to a set of products.
9
Enter a Class to limit the purchase order to only products of a selected class (1-13). Products are classified based on the total value of a product that moves through inventory each month. Refer to “Classifying Products” in the Inventory Module.
10
For Override Lead Time Horizon, enter the number of months to use to determine how far ahead to generate a purchase order.
11
Once you choose to Execute, the recommended purchase order is sent to the Buyer Queue Inquiry along with the other purchase orders recommended by Nightly Processing. You can review and edit the purchase order before transmitting it to the vendor.
