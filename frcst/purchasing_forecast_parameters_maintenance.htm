Establishing Trends and Safety Levels
You can define trends or safety levels for DRP products at the product line/warehouse level in Purchasing Forecast Parameters Maintenance (e.g., warehouses have different purchasing requirements). These values override the values in Product Line Maintenance.
Purchasing Forecast Parameters Maintenance
To enter trends and safety allowances at the product line/warehouse level:
1
Select Purchasing > Forecasting Subsystem > Purchasing Forecast Parameters Maintenance to access the Purchasing Forecast Parameters Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to establish purchasing forecast parameters.
3
Enter the Product Line for which you want to establish purchasing forecast parameters or press F3 to search for existing product lines.
4
For each Month listed, enter the Trend%, which is a factor between
–100.00% and 100.00% to adjust the demand for each month. Remove the trend factor once the trend levels off.
Trend quantity is calculated by multiplying Forecast by the Trend Factor. The calculated trend quantity is added to or subtracted from the forecasted demand to provide an adjusted forecast.
The Trend % at this level can be overridden by the Trend % in Product Line Maintenance.
You can set a Trend % Maximum in Purchasing Controls > Purchase Parameters it you do not want the system to calculate a trend factor above a certain percent.
The Trend % Maximum in Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add Info overrides the Trend % Maximum in Purchase Parameters.
5
For each Class, enter the #Months/Safety (number of months of safety stock). Based on the class of the item, you can establish different amounts of safety stock. This information appears on the ”Distribution Requirements Planning Report” on page 4-8 and ”Distribution Requirements Evaluation Inquiry” on page 4-2.
6
Press F2-Save to save the purchasing forecast parameter information.
 
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Warehouse
The warehouse number for establishing purchasing forecast parameters.
Product Line
The product line for establishing purchasing forecast parameters.
Month
The month of the year.
Trend %
For each Month listed, the Trend%, is a factor between
–100.00% and 100.00% for adjusting the demand for each month.
Trend quantity is calculated by multiplying Forecast by the Trend Factor. The calculated trend quantity is added to or subtracted from the forecasted demand to provide an adjusted forecast. This value overrides the Trend % in Product Line Maintenance.
Class
Available product classifications.
#Months/Safety
The number of months of safety stock. Based on the class of the item, you can establish different amounts of safety stock. This information appears on the ”Distribution Requirements Planning Report” on page 4-8 and ”Distribution Requirements Evaluation Inquiry” on page 4-2. For DRP items, the surplus threshold also includes forecasts during the leadtime of the product. For example, if a product has a 60 day lead time and a 1 month safety quantity of 30, Surplus Threshold is calculated using a quantity of 90.
F2-Save
Saves the purchasing forecast parameter information.
