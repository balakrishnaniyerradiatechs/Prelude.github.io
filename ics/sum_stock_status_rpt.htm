Summary Stock Status Report
The Summary Stock Status report displays summaries of on-hand, on-order, and month-to-date information for a product or group of products. You can use this report to get a snapshot of current stocking levels, future commitments, or sales history for a group of products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Summary Stock Status
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) include. Leave blank to view all warehouses.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Product. The product number(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products that fall within the above criteria.
•
Primary Vendor. The vendor to limit the report to only products with this primary vendor. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Stocking Status. The stock status code to limit the items to print on the report. Leave blank to include all status codes or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
Month Code. Enter the code for the month to display (e.g., 01 for January, 02 for February). Leave blank to display the current month.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Pline
The product line assigned to the product.
Product
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Whse
The warehouse location for the product information.
Min/Max
The minimum and maximum re-order values if using the min/max purchasing method.
On-Hand
The current on-hand quantity of the product in this warehouse.
Mtd-Sls
The quantity of the product invoiced on sales orders in the selected month.
Mtd-T/R
The quantity of the product invoiced on transfers in the selected month.
Mtd-W/O
The quantity of the product invoiced on work orders in the selected month.
On-Ord
The quantity of the product on-order.
Commtd
The quantity of the product committed to sales orders, work orders, and transfers.
