Setting Up Serialized Products
You can set up products to require serial numbers when they are received and/or shipped, depending on whether you want to track serial numbers in inventory or serial numbers sold.
To set up a product as a serialized item:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product to set up as a serialized item.
3
Press F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information Screen.
4
For Serial# Flag, enter Y to require a serial number when this item is shipped. You can then use Detail and Track to determine when and how serial numbers are required.
5
For Detail, indicate if the serial number is tracked in detail. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Serial numbers are tracked in detail, which means that you must enter a serial number any time the item is increased or decreased (e.g. received, shipped, transferred). When the product is sold, the pick ticket prints SER# in front of the serial number for each item to pick. If you choose this option, Track is automatically set to Y to track each serial number.
•
N - Serial numbers are required only when the product ships. This option only applies if you want to track serial numbers sold but not serial numbers in inventory. If you choose this option, you must choose one of the following for Track:
-
Y - Serial numbers are required only when shipped. When you receive, adjust, or transfer the product, you can enter a serial number but it is not required.
-
N - Serial numbers are only required when shipped.
6
For Equipment Flag, indicate if the item is tracked as a piece of equipment:
•
Y - Track the product as a piece of equipment (normally the setting for a serialized item). If the product is entered as a trade-in using a new serial number, an equipment record is created in Equipment Maintenance for the new serial number when the order is invoiced.
•
N - Do not track the serialized item as a piece of equipment.
7
Press F2-Save to save the serialized information.
When you enter serial numbers in Stock Receipts Entry (refer to Receiving Stock), the serial numbers are carried over to the linked document (sales order or stock transfer).
