Printing Transfer Pick Tickets
To print transfer pick tickets:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Pick Ticket Printing.
2
For From Warehouse, enter the warehouse that is shipping the transfer.
3
Enter a Transfer Number to print or leave blank to print all transfer pick tickets for this warehouse with available quantities.
4
Enter the Printer Form Queue to indicate the printer location.
5
Once you choose to Execute, the following prompt appears:
Load Transfer Ticket Forms in Printer & Align Visually
6
Choose from the following:
•
Print Transfer Tickets: Prints the transfer tickets and creates the releases.
•
Mask Print Test: Runs a test on a pick ticket to make sure the paper is aligned (if using pre-printed forms).
•
Abort Printing: Cancels the print process.
7
After you select Print Transfer Tickets, the Print Confirmation prompt appears:
Transfer Pick Ticket Printing Complete...
Last Release NUmber -> 10000171-1
Choose from the following:
•
Continue: If all transfer tickets printed successfully.
•
Reprint All: If none of the transfer tickets printed successfully.
•
Single Reprint: If you want to reprint one or more transfer tickets individually.
Pick tickets may not print for the following reasons:
•
The transfer is on hold. Refer to Transfer Branch Hold under Establishing Transfer Options.
•
The transfer was left in the suspended status. Refer to Entering Stock Transfers.
•
No quantity is available on the transfer.
•
The pick date is in the future.
•
The transfer pick ticket already printed. You must re-print the pick ticket (refer to Re-Printing Transfer Pick Tickets).
