Universal Product Merge Entry screen
The Universal Product Merge Entry screen allows you to merge product, pricing, and usage information from one product to another. If you merge multiple products into one product, the product line, price group, and usage information is taken from the first product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Universal Product Replacement & Merge > Universal Product Merge Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Old Product Number
The original product number to merge into the New Product Number.
New Product Number
The new or existing product number to merge the Old Product Number information into.
F2-Save
Saves the merge information. The merge does not occur until you run the Universal Product Replacement/Merge Update.
F2-Del
Deletes the merge information.
