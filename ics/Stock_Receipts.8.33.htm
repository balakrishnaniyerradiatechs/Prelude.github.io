Assigning License Plates When Receiving Stock
Once you activate the license plate option and set up product numbers to use lots (refer to Setting Up License Plate Products), you can receive the license plate items in Stock Receipts Entry. As you complete each receiver, you can print the labels and attach them to the received shipment immediately.
If necessary, you can split or combine license plates after they are assigned. Refer to Splitting a License Plate and Combining License Plates.
To receive license plate items in Stock Receipts Entry:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts Entry. Refer to Receiving Stock for a full explanation on Stock Receipts Entry.
2
Select to receive the products by Line Item. As you receive each item, the License Plate Information screen appears to allow you to verify the label information.
3
For #/Lbls, enter the number of labels that you want to print for this received quantity. Each label is a unique license plate that identifies a product and quantity.
4
For Qty/Label, enter the quantity that goes with each label. The Qty/Label multiplied by the #/Lbls must equal the quantity received.
5
For Country of Origin and Default Bin Location, accept the defaults from Product Maintenance or change as necessary.
6
Enter the Expiration Date, if it is required (determined by the Lot Exp Date Flag in the Lot Information Screen in Product Maintenance).
7
If the Price/Cost Required flag is set to Y in Product Maintenance, enter the lot price and commission cost for each license plate.
Lot Description Information
Press F5-Description to open the Lot Description Maintenance screen You can enter the manufacturer number, manufacturer lot number and manufactured date, if the information is available. This information is available in the Lot Inquiry screen.
8
Press F2-Save to save the license plate information and continue receiving the stock.
License plate numbers generate and labels print for the received items. If labels for the warehouse print continuously (refer to Setting Up License Plate Products) the labels print immediately. If not, you can use License Plate Standalone Entry to print the labels manually. Refer to Printing License Plate Labels.
