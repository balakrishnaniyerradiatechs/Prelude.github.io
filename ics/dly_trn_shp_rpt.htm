Daily Stock Transfer Shipping Report
The Daily Stock Transfer Shipping report displays transfers and products shipped from a warehouse for a day.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movements Reports > Daily Stock Transfer Reports > Daily Stock Transfer Shipping Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the transfers.
•
Shipment Date. The date the transfers shipped.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Transfer#
The transfer release number that shipped.
Ent/Ship
Line 1: The date the transfer was entered.
Line 2: The date the corresponding product on the transfer shipped.
Additional Lines: The ship date for each of the corresponding products.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number that shipped.
Line 2: The lot number or serial number if applicable.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product if applicable.
TWhse
Line 1: The number of the warehouse that received the transfer.
Line 2: The receiving warehouse description.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped.
U/M
The unit of measure as entered on the transfer.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Ext-Cost
Line 1: The extended cost of the total quantity shipped (Qty-Shp x Cost).
Line 2: The linked sales order or work order number.
