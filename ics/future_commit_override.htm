Future Commitment Override screen
The Future Commitment Override screen allows you to override any future commitments for the inventory on the transfer. If the product being transferred is committed to a backorder, you can use this to override the commitment so that when this transfer arrives, the inventory can be allocated to another order.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry > Stock Transfer Header > Action Bar > Fut Commit Ov
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Commitment Override
Indicates if you want to override any orders that the products on this transfer are committed to:
•
Y - If the products on this transfer are committed to backorders, this overrides the commitment so that the products can be available for other orders when they are received.
•
N - Any future commitments for the products remain applicable.
User Identification
The user ID of the person entering the transfer.
F2-Save
Saves the future commitment information.
