Moving Product Usage
If you start buying a regular stock item from a new vendor (but different part number), or if usage is incorrectly recorded for a product, you may want to move usage from one product to another. Depending on why you are moving usage, you can either retain or clear the usage in the old part number. The old part number still remains in the system as a valid part number.
If you use Supercedures Maintenance to replace an obsolete product (refer to Supercedure Products), usage automatically moves from the old product to the new one.
Inventory Parameters
If Capture Weekly Usage is set to Y in Inventory Parameters, the weekly usage is also copied/cleared during this process. Refer to Tracking Average Weekly Usage.
To move product usage to a new product number:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Move Product Usage to open the Move Product Usage screen.
2
Enter the Product to move the usage from (the old part number).
3
For To Product, enter the new product number.
4
For (A) Add Usage or (C) Add Usage and Clear, indicate if usage should remain for the original product. Choose from the following:
•
A - Add usage to the To Product. The usage also remains in the original product number.
•
C - Add usage to the To Product and clear usage from the original product number.
5
Press F2-Save to move the product usage.
The Usage Audit Inquiry displays the MOV code for both product numbers: The To Product shows a positive quantity for each sales order, work order, or transfer that contains usage moved from the original product. The original Product shows a negative quantity for each sales order, work order, or transfer that has usage moved to the new part number.
