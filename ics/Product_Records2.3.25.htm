Product Notes
You can enter notes associated with a product and then identify the document types where you want the notes to appear.
To enter notes for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Product Notes to open the Product Notes screen.
5
For Notes, enter any notes associated with the product.
6
For Doc, choose the document where you want these notes to appear:
•
B - Both sales orders and quotes.
•
F - Both purchase orders and vendor request for quotes.
•
G - Global text (all documents listed here).
•
P - Purchase orders.
•
Q - Quotes.
•
R - Putaway report.
•
S - Sales orders.
•
T - Transfers.
•
V - Vendor request for quotes
•
W - Work order pick ticket.
7
For Type, choose the comment code for the product notes:
•
C - Prints on all sales-related documents.
•
CN - Does not print on external documents.
•
CB - Same as C but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CP - Prints on pick ticket.
•
CPB - Same as CP but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CI - Prints on the invoice.
•
CIB- Same as CI but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CPO - Prints on sales documents and purchase orders.
•
CIPO - Prints on linked purchase orders and invoices.
•
CNPO - Same as CPO but does not print.
•
CPPO - Prints on the purchase order and pick ticket but not on the invoice.
8
If you want to add another set of notes for another document, repeat steps 5-7.
9
Press F2-Save to save the notes entered for the product.
