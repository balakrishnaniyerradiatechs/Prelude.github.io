Assigning Products to Vehicles
Once you configure the automobile makes (refer to Defining Automobile Makes), you can then assign products to each model/make/year combination. This information allows you to search for a product by make/model/year.
To assign products to each automobile make/model/year:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Make/Model/Year Maintenance to open the Product Make/Model/Year Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the automobile Make set up in Automotive Make Maintenance.
3
Enter the Model associated with this automobile make.
4
Enter the model Year.
5
Under Product, enter any stock product numbers that are compatible with this vehicle.
6
Press F2-Save to save the product information.
Repeat steps 2-6 to assign products to each make/model/year combination. This information allows you to search for a product by make, model, or year.
