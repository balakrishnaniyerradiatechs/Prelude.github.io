Monthly Inventory Linked Adjustment Report
The Monthly Inventory Linked Adjustment Report lists, for a fiscal period, products on purchase orders that were linked to sales orders, for which the quantity on the sales order was changed after the product was received. You can choose to list the detail information for each product and order number, or to list the total value of the linked products per warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Monthly Inventory Linked Adjustment Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse Number. The warehouse where the inventory was received.
•
Fiscal Period. The fiscal period in which the original order was entered.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the product was received.
Document#
The document number on which the linked quantity was adjusted. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
P - Purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
Ent-Date
The date the order was entered.
C/V/W
The customer, vendor, or warehouse number depending on the document type.
Product Number
The product number for which the quantity was adjusted.
Qty
The quantity that was originally ordered.
Ext Value
The extended value of the quantity of the linked quantity.
