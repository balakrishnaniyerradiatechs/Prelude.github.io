Printing Vendor Claims
If you batch print vendor claims, you must use Vendor Claims Printing to email, fax, or print vendor claims to the vendor. A printed claim can also provide paperwork to ship the material back to the vendor.
To print, email, or fax a vendor claim:
1
Select Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claim Printing to open the Vendor Claims Printing screen.
2
Enter the Vendor Claim Number or leave blank to print all available vendor claims. Press F3 to search for claims by vendor number.
3
For Warehouse, enter a warehouse number or leave blank to include all warehouses.
4
Enter the Claim Type to limit the claims to print or leave blank to include all claims. Available claim types are:
•
01- Regular.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory.
•
04 - Non inventory expense.
5
Enter the Printer Form Queue to assign a printer.
6
Once you press ENTER, the following prompt appears:
Load Vendor Claim Forms in Printer & Align Visually
7
Choose from the following:
•
Print Vendor Claims - Prints, emails, or faxes the vendor claims.
•
Mask Print Test - Prints a test claim to make sure the printer is aligned.
•
Abort Printing - Cancels the printing.
Once the claims print, the following prompt displays:
Vendor Claim Printing Complete...
8
Choose from the following:
•
Process - Marks the vendor claims as printed so that they do not print again.
•
Reprint All - Reprints all the vendor claims if they did not print properly.
•
Single Reprint - Reprints just one vendor claim if only one requires reprinting.
•
Abort - Cancels the process so that the claims are not marked as printed.
Once you choose Process, the following prompt displays:
Are You Sure You Want To Process?
9
Select from the following:
•
Reprompt - Allows you to go back to step 7 to reprint the claims or cancel the process.
•
Yes - Marks the vendor claims as printed so that they do print again.
•
No - Does not mark the claims as printed so that they can be selected again for printing.
Reprint a Vendor Claim
To reprint a vendor claim before it is updated, select Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claims Reprinting.
To reprint a vendor claim after has been updated select Inventory > Vendor Claims > Updated Vendor Claims Reprinting. Use the (-1) extension which indicates that was updated.
