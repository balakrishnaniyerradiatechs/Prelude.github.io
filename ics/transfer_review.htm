Transfer Review screen
The Transfer Review screen displays available and backordered quantities, and cost information for a stock transfer.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry > Action Bar > Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer Number
The transfer number.
Shipment Date
The date the transfer shipped.
From
The shipping warehouse number and name.
To
The receiving warehouse number and name.
Product
The product numbers on the transfer.
Description
The product description.
F
Indicates if the transfer is a future:
•
F - A future transfer.
•
Blank - Not a future transfer.
Quantity
The quantity of the item requested by the receiving branch.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
Qty-Pck
The quantity available to pick on the transfer.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered.
S
The supply code which appears if the product is on backorder. The code varies depending on the product’s replenishment path and product settings. Possible supply codes include:
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
R - Emergency transfer
•
W - Work order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped.
Linkage S
The document number of the source of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - The product was purchased on this purchase order number at the shipping warehouse.
•
T - The product was ordered on this transfer number at the shipping warehouse.
Linkage D
The document number for the destination of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this sales order number.
•
T - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this transfer number.
•
W - When the product arrives, it will be allocated to this work order.
Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the transfer.
OK
Closes the review screen.
