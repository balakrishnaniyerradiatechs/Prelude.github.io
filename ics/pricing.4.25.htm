Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices
If the vendor gives discounts based on the total quantity, dollars, or weight of a product, you can set up matrices to compute the discount for each cost break level. You can enter a matrix for current prices or future prices.
To enter a company vendor cost discount matrix for either current or future pricing:
1
From the Company Vendor Cost Maintenance screen (refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost) select F5-Discount Matrix to open the Current Discount Matrix screen, or F6-Future Discount Matrix to open the Future Discount Matrix screen. The remaining procedures are the same for both current and future matrices.
2
For Discount Type, indicate what the discount is based on. Choose from the following:
•
# - The discount is based on the total weight of the item purchased.
•
$ - The discount is based on the total dollar amount of the item purchased.
•
U - The discount is based on the number of units purchased.
3
For Discount Method, indicate how you will enter the cost for each weight, dollar, or quantity break amount. Choose from the following:
•
$ (Cost) - To enter a cost for each break level. The Vendor Cost Discounts screen appears.
-
For Break Amount, depending on which Discount Type you chose, enter the minimum quantity, dollar amount, or weight you must buy to get the corresponding Break Cost.
-
Enter the Break Cost you will get when you purchase each corresponding Break Amount.
•
* (Discount percent) - To enter the discount off of cost that you will receive for each break amount purchased. The Vendor Discount Table screen appears.
-
For Break Amount, depending on which Discount Type you chose, enter the minimum quantity, dollar amount, or weight you must buy to get the corresponding Discount %.
-
Enter the Discount % off of cost you will get when you purchase the corresponding Break Amount. The discount comes off of the Cost in the Company Vendor Cost Maintenance screen.
4
Press F2-Save twice to save the discount matrix.
