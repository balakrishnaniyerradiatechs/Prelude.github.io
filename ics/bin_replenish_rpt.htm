Bin Replenishment Report
The Bin Replenishment Report displays products with a pick quantity that is greater than the primary bin quantity. The bulk bin location and quantity display also. You may want to run this report regularly to ensure adequate stock in primary bins.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Bin Replenishment
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) containing products whose primary bin needs replenishing.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Area
The warehouse area assigned to the product. Refer to Defining Warehouse Areas.
Product Number
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Bin Location
The product’s primary bin location.
Qty-Pck
The quantity of the product on open sales orders, work orders, and transfers in pick status (the pick ticket printed but the shipment is not yet confirmed).
Qty-Req
The quantity required to fill the open orders.
Bulk Bin Loc
The bulk bin locations where the product also exists.
On-Hand
The quantities on-hand in the product’s bulk bin locations.
