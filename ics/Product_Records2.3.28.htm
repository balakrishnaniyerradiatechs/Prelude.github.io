Core Products
A core product is the re-usable part of a product that a customer returns either for credit or for a discount against a new item. Refer to Pending Cores for more information on cores.
If you set up a product as a Core Product in the Additional Product Maintenance Information Screen, you should also set up a product to exchange for the core product when it is returned.
To set up the exchange product for a core product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product to set up as an exchange product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Core Product to open the Core Product Entry screen.
5
For Core Product, enter the number of the core product which is the re-usable part of the product.
6
Press F2-Save to save the core product information for this exchange product number.
The next time you press ENTER in the Core Product field in the Additional Product Maintenance Information Screen for the core product, the exchange product displays.
