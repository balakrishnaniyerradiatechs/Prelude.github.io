Inventory Inquiry Information screen
The Inventory Inquiry Information screen displays additional purchasing and packaging information for a product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry > F7-Add Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Purchasing Cost
The replacement cost for the item.
Stock/Buy Ratio
The relationship between the stocking quantity and the buying quantity.
For example, if you stock the quantity as each, but purchase the item in cases of 20, the stock to buy ratio is 20. When Nightly Processing recommends a P/O, the recommended quantity is divided by this factor. When the product is received, the quantity is multiplied by this factor to update the inventory balance.
Selling Package Quantity
The default selling package quantity. If you enter an order for less than this amount, a warning message displays and allows you to round up to the next selling package quantity.
Quantity Per Case
The quantity that normally comes in a case.
Quantity Per Pallet
The quantity that normally comes in a pallet.
Replenish Method
Defines the replenishment method for the product:
•
P - Purchase. Nightly Processing recommends a purchase order. If the product is a stock product, all products in the vendor line are checked to see if vendor line targets can be met.
•
D - Direct. Nightly Processing recommends a purchase order in the central warehouse.
•
T - Transfer. Nightly Processing recommends a transfer.
•
M - Manufacture. Nightly Processing recommends a work order.
•
O - Outplant. Nightly Processing recommends an outplant work order.
Formula Method
Identifies the purchase method for this product:
•
EOQ - Economic Order Quantity.
•
CLS - Class (always used for Replenishment Method T).
•
AWU - Average Weekly Usage. Enter the number of weeks to calculate usage. If left blank, the number of weeks from Inventory Parameters are used. Refer to Tracking Average Weekly Usage.
•
DRP - Distribution Requirements Planning; for long lead time items.
•
DS - Days Supply.
Buyer/Planner
The number for the buyer assigned to the product.
Country of Origin
The country where the item was purchased or manufactured.
Product Line
The product line for this product. Product lines group similar products together for sales analysis to update the general ledger. Refer to Maintaining Product Lines.
Primary Vendor
The primary vendor for the product.
Vendor Line
The vendor line for this product. Vendor lines group products within a vendor to optimize purchasing discounts. Vendor lines are established in Vendor Line Maintenance in the Purchasing module.
Vendor Part Number
The vendor part number, if one exists.
Last Update Date
The last date the Purchasing Cost (replacement cost) was updated.
Damaged Onhand
The quantity of the product on hand that is in damaged inventory. Refer to Adjusting Inventory Quantities for information on damaged inventory.
Warranty Onhand
The quantity of the product that is in warranty inventory. Refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory.
Scrapped Onhand
The quantity of the product that is scrapped inventory. Refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory.
Vend Cons Onhand
The quantity of vendor consigned inventory currently on-hand. This quantity is updated when a type 10 consigned purchase order (or the Vend Consign flag is set to Y in the purchase order header) is received.
Last Physical
The date that this product was counted in a physical inventory. Refer to Physical Inventory.
OK
Closes the screen.
F5-Usage
Opens the Product Usage Inquiry screen to allow you to view usage for the product.
