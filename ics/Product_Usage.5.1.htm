Product Usage
Incorrect inventory levels can cause your company to carry costly overstock or risk lost sales due to backorders. To maintain profitable inventory balances, Prelude provides a series of checks to determine when and where to record product usage. You can record usage for each product in each warehouse over a period of time to help determine future purchase quantities. You can also calculate weekly usage for seasonal products that generate high usage for only a few months of the year.
