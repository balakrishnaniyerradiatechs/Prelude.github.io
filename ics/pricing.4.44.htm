Running the Price Tape Pre-Update Report
After you load the price tape (refer to Loading a Price Tape), run the Price Tape Update Report to verify price and cost information before you update the product prices.
To run the price tape pre-update report:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Tape Update > Price Tape Pre-Update Report.
2
For Number of Products to Print, enter the number of products to include on the report. Enter a zero to include all products. The Price Tape Pre-Update selection screen appears.
3
For Diskette Name Equal To, enter the full name from the price tape setup (refer to Setting Up a Price Tape).
4
For And Vendor Number Equal To, enter the vendor number from the price tape setup.
5
Press Accept to generate the report.
