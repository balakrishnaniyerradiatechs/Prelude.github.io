Building Price/Cost Lists
To update prices and costs by list, you must first create a list of products.
To build a list of products:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Cost Changes by Lists > Build Lists.
2
Choose one of the following options to group the products to update:
•
By Vendor.
•
By Product Line.
•
By Bin Location.
•
By Product Number Range.
•
By Other Criteria.
These procedures are similar whether creating a list by product line, by bin location, by product number, or by select statement.
3
Select By Vendor to open the Price / Cost Build List By Vendor screen.
4
Enter a Warehouse, or enter ALL to update prices for all warehouses at the product level.
5
For List Name, enter a descriptive name to assign to the list (e.g., KLEIN or any vendor name). Do not use spaces in the name.
6
For Inventory/Catalog, choose from the following:
•
I - Creates the list from the active inventory file.
•
C - Creates the list from the catalog file.
7
For Vendor, enter the vendor number(s) to include in this list.
8
Press F2-Create List to create the list. The number of items selected for the list displays.
To view the list, refer to Displaying Price/Cost Lists or run the Price/Cost Pre-Save Report to view the current price cost information. When you are ready to start updating prices based on your list, refer to Updating Prices/Costs.
