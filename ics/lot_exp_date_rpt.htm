Lot Shelf Life Expiration Report
The Lot Shelf Life Expiration report displays lot products for which the shelf life has expired or is about to expire. This information helps determine which products to sell before they expire and which products to remove. The expiration date for a lot product is entered when the product is received (refer to Receiving Lot Number Products).
Expiration Dates
When you set up a lot product in Product Maintenance, the Lot Exp Date Flag in the Lot Information Screen determines if an expiration date is required when the product is received. If so, you can either require the expiration date or the date of manufacture. If you use the date of manufacture, the Shelf Life Days in Product Maintenance are added to the date of manufacture to determine the expiration date.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Lot Shelf Life Expiration Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse with the expiring shelf life lot numbers.
•
Product. The product number(s) with the expiring shelf life lot numbers. Leave blank to view all products.
•
Shelf Life Days Remaining. The number of days until the lot expires.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Lot
The lot number assigned to the product.
Exp Date
The date the shelf life for the lot product expired or will expire.
Onhand
The quantity of the product/lot number on-hand.
