Establishing Product Price Groups
Product price groups combine products for special pricing in customer price matrices. You can set up the price group codes in Product Price Group Maintenance and then enter the codes in Product Maintenance and/or Product Warehouse Maintenance to group products. Price Matrix Maintenance in the Order Processing module uses the price groups to provide discounts or special pricing.
Product Price Group Codes Listings
To view a listing of product price codes, select Inventory > Listings > Miscellaneous Listings > Product Price Group Codes.
To set up product price groups:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Price Group Codes to open the Product Price Group Maintenance screen.
2
For Prod Price Group, enter the product price group code (1 to 6 characters) to use for special pricing.
3
Enter a Description of the product price group to display when the code is used.
4
Press F2-Save to save the new product price group information.
To assign a product price group code to a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product then press F7-Pricing to open the Product Pricing Screen.
3
For Product Prc Group, enter the code established in Product Price Group Maintenance.
4
Press F2-Save to save the product price group information. Press F2-Save again on the Product Maintenance screen.
To assign a product price group to a product warehouse record:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance to open the Product Warehouse Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product and the Warehouse.
3
For Price Group, enter a code from Product Price Group Maintenance.
4
Press F2 - Save to save the price group information for this product.
