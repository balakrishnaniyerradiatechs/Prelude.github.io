Entering Bulk Bin Moves
You can use a bulk bin move to move all of the contents from one bin to another, but you cannot change quantities or primary bin assignments. If you need to adjust quantities or primary bin assignments while changing bins, use Bin Individual Move Entry > F5-Load Bin (refer to Entering Individual Bin Moves).
To move all the contents of one bin into another bin:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Bin Bulk Move Entry to open the Bin Bulk Move Entry screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse where products are moving.
3
For Move Number, enter an existing move number or press ENTER to let the system assign a new number.
4
For From Bin, enter the bin that the products are moving from.
5
For To Bin, enter the bin that the products are moving to.
6
Press F2-Save to complete the bin move.
