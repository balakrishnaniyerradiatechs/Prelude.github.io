Creating a Product
A product contains information common to all warehouses and vendors. You can create the product at the company level in Product Maintenance and then establish pricing, usage, purchasing parameters, and other information specific to each warehouse and each vendor.

Product changes, sorted by product number. You can choose to show changes made in Product Maintenance, Product Warehouse Maintenance, or Product Vendor Maintenance.
