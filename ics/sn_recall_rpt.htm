Serial Number Recall Notification Report
The Serial Number Recall Notification report displays serial numbers and associated customer and sales order information for a serialized product. This report can help identify customers who purchased a recalled product so that you can arrange an exchange or return.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Serial Number Recall Notification Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Product. The serialized product number.
•
Serial Number Range. The beginning and ending serial numbers to view or leave blank to view all serial numbers for the selected product.
•
Sold?. Indicates if the report includes sold items. Choose from the following:
-
Y - List products sold (default).
-
N - List products not sold.
•
Warehouse. Only accessible if Sold? is set to N. The resulting report displays the warehouse number, the serial number, and a receipt date.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Serial Numbers
The serial numbers assigned to the product.
Order#
The sales order number on which the associated product/serial number was sold to the customer.
Inv#
The invoice number on which the customer was billed for the serialized product.
Inv-Date
The date the customer was invoiced.
Cust#
The number for the customer who bought the serialized product.
Description
The customer description.
Telephone #
The customer’s telephone number.
Contact
The customer’s contact name (from the Customer Maintenance screen).
