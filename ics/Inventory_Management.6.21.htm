Sorting Bins by Warehouse Location
The Bin Location Sort Sequence Procedure allows you to sort bins in a logical sequence in your warehouse to streamline order picking (if you use the Warehousing module). When a pick ticket prints, the products appear in the sequence you define.
To sort bins by location:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Bin Location Sort Sequence Codes Update Procedure to open the Bin Location Sort Sequence Codes Update screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse.
3
In Beginning Bin, enter the first bin to pick.
4
In Ending Bin, enter the last bin to pick.
5
Enter the Sequence Order: Choose from the following:
•
A - Ascending.
•
D - Descending.
6
Enter the Beginning Sequence Number to assign to bins. For example, if you enter 1, the Beginning Bin that you defined becomes number 1, or the first bin in this bin location range. This number displays under Sort Sequence in Bin Location Maintenance (refer to Defining Bins).
7
Press F2-Begin Update to sort bins in the defined picking order and to assign sort sequence codes to each bin in the bin location range.
