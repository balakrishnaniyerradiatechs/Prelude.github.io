Upsell Products
An upsell product is a more expensive product or list of products that you may want to offer when a customer asks for an item. When you enter the original product on a sales order, a dialog box appears to inform you that the product has up-sell products to offer. You can review the products and replace the original product if necessary. Refer to “Entering Upsell Products” in the Order Processing documentation for more information.
To assign an upsell product to a part number:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product for which you want to assign upsell products.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Upsells to open the Upsell Product Maintenance screen.
5
Enter the Product Number to offer as an upsell product when the original part number is entered on a sales order.
6
Enter a Y for Script, to open the Notes Entry screen.
7
Enter any notes regarding the upsell product that you want to appear when the salesperson enters the original product on a sales order. This could be a sales pitch to help encourage the customer to buy the upsell product.
8
Press F2-Save to save the upsell product information.
