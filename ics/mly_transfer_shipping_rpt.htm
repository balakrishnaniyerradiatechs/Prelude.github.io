Monthly Stock Transfer Shipping Report
The Monthly Stock Transfer Shipping report displays all the products transferred from a warehouse for a month or fiscal period.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movements Reports > Monthly Stock Transfer Reports > Monthly Stock Transfer Shipping Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the transfers.
•
Period. The fiscal period or month in which the transfers shipped.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Transfer#
The transfer release number that shipped.
Shp Date
The date the transfer shipped.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number that shipped.
Line 2: The lot number or serial number if applicable.
Description
 
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
TWhse
Line 1: The receiving warehouse number.
Line 2: The receiving warehouse description.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped.
U/M
The unit of measure as entered on the transfer.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Ext-Cost
Line 1: The extended cost of the total quantity shipped (Qty-Shp x Cost).
Line 2: The linked sales order or work order number.
