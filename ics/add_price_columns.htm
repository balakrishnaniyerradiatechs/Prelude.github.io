Add. Price Columns Screen
If you need more price columns in addition to the 5 columns already available, you can use the Add. Price Columns screen to add up to 999 extra price columns. You may want to use this to reduce the chance of employees referencing price columns that are not defined, causing price exceptions. These price columns can be referenced in Customer Maintenance, Product Maintenance, Product Warehouse Maintenance, and Price Matrix Maintenance. The automatic price update programs do not update any more than 5 columns, but, you if the prices are calculated as a percent or a multiple of another column, they will be updated accordingly.
Inventory Parameters
The Add. Price Columns screen is only accessible if Maximum Price Columns in Inventory Parameters is greater than 5.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F7-Price/Cost > Action Bar > Add. Prices
