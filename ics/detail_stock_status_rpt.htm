Detail Stock Status Report
The Detail Stock Status report displays the detail on-hand, committed, and on-order information for a product or group of products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Detail Stock Status
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include. Leave blank to view all warehouses.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Primary Vendor. The vendor to limit the report to only products with this primary vendor assigned. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products that meet the above criteria.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number that is low in stock.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Whse
The warehouse containing the product.
Rep-Cost
The replacement cost of the product.
On-Hand
The current on-hand quantity of the product in this warehouse.
Commt
The quantity of the product currently committed to each of the corresponding sales orders, work orders, and transfers.
Doc#
The document number containing the committed quantity.
Type
The order type of the corresponding document:
•
S/O - Sales order.
•
W/O - Work order.
•
TRN - Transfer.
On-Ord
The quantity of the product on-order.
Doc#
The document number containing the on-order quantity.
Type
The order type of the document that contains the on-order quantity:
•
P/O - Purchase order.
•
TRN - Transfer.
