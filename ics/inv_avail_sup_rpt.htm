Inventory Available For Superceded Product Report
The Inventory Available For Superceded Product report list superceded products that have available inventory. Refer to Supercedure Products for information on superceded products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Inventory Available for Superceded Product
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where the superceded products exist.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The superceded product number.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse where the superceded product exists.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Avail
The quantity of the product that is available.
