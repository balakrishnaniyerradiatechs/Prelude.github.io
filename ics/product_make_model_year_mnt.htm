Product Make/Model/Year Maintenance screen
The Product Make/Model/Year Maintenance screen allows you to assign products to each automobile model/make/year combination. This information allows you to search for a product by make/model/year. Refer to Maintaining Automotive Information for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Make/Model/Year Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Make
The automobile make. Refer to Defining Automobile Makes.
Model
The model number associated with this automobile make.
Year
The automobile model year.
Product
The stock product numbers that are compatible with this automobile make/model/year.
Description
The product description.
F2-Finish
Saves the automobile product information.
