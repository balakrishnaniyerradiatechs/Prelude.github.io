Releasing Pick Tickets
Whenever you receive products into your warehouse or increase the quantity of any item, the products automatically allocate to open backorders. However, no tickets print until you release pick tickets. This extra step allows you to receive or adjust a large quantity of items before printing tickets, so that a separate ticket does not print for every item on a sales order, work order, or transfer that has more than one filled backorder.
To fill open backorders, you must release pick tickets after:
•
Receiving stock (refer to Receiving Stock).
•
Receiving transfers (refer to Receiving Stock Transfers).
•
Manually allocating inventory (refer to Allocating Inventory Manually).
•
Entering a credit memo for stock products.
•
Adjusting inventory that increases the quantity of an item (refer to Adjusting Inventory).
To release pick tickets:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Release Pick Tickets.
2
For Warehouse, enter the warehouse for which you want to release pick tickets or leave blank to print pick tickets for all warehouses.
3
For Sales Orders?, enter Y to print pick tickets for sales orders.
4
For Printer For Sales Orders, enter the printer number for sales order pick tickets.
5
For Work Orders?, enter Y to print pick tickets for work orders.
6
For Printer For Work Orders, enter the printer number for work order pick tickets.
7
For Transfers?, enter Y to print pick tickets for transfers.
8
For Printer For Transfers, enter the printer number for transfer pick tickets.
9
Select Execute to print pick tickets to the designated printers.
Form Queue Maintenance
Use Form Queue Maintenance (Systems Menu > Maintenance > Miscellaneous Controls > Printers > Form Queue Maintenance) to set up form queues and to identify the warehouses that may print to the form queues when you release pick tickets.
