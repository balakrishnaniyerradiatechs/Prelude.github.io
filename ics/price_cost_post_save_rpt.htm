Price/Cost Post-Save Report
The Price/Cost Post-Save report displays the updated price/cost information.
Running the Price/Cost Post-Save Report
To print or view updated price/cost information:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Cost Changes by Lists > Price/Cost Post-Save Report to open the Price/Cost Post-Save Report screen.
2
For Product or Warehouse, choose how to generate the report:
•
P - By product prices and costs.
•
W - By warehouse prices and costs (for products with unique warehouse prices).
3
Enter the Warehouse associated with the list.
4
For Save List Name, enter the name of the list to view. Refer to Building Price/Cost Lists.
5
For Future or Current, indicate which price/cost columns to view:
•
F - Future prices.
•
C - Current prices.
6
If you entered F for Future or Current, enter the Future Effective Date that the future prices take effect.
7
Indicate which price/cost fields to display on the report:
•
Enter Y for each price/cost column to include on the report.
•
Enter N for each price/cost column to exclude from the report.
You can view the results on the screen or print the information to a printer.
Column Descriptions

Description

The new replacement cost for the product.
Std Cost
The new standard cost for the product.
Prc Grp
The price group code for the product. Refer to Establishing Product Price Groups.
