OTW Purchase Order Inquiry screen
The OTW Purchase Order Inquiry screen allows you to view detail information for On-The-Water purchase orders in transit to your warehouse.
Refer to Receiving On-The-Water Merchandise for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry > On The Water (under Sources) > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O#
The on-the-water purchase order number created to receive the product from an overseas vendor.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exists in the header of the on-the water purchase order.
Vend#
The number for the vendor that shipped the material.
Description
The vendor description.
P/O Type
Indicates the type of OTW purchase order that contains the product:
•
OTW-Stock. The material is coming into stock.
•
OTW-Direct. The material is shipping directly to a customer.
P/O Date
The date the OTW purchase order was created.
Qty-Ord
The quantity of the product ordered on the OTW purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the OTW purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity of the product not yet received on the purchase order.
Qty-Stk
The quantity of the product to go into stock when it is received.
Delivery
The date entered as the Expected Date when the OTW purchase order was created.
Promised
The delivery date promised by the vendor when the original purchase order was created.
Vendor Ord#
The Reference # entered when the OTW purchase order was created. You can use this number in Import Cost Inquiry to inquire on import costs associated with a shipment.
Ship#
The number for the ship that contains the merchandise if you receive OTW products using the shipping bin method.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review P/O
Opens the Purchase Order Display screen to allow you to view the entire purchase order.
F7-Log
Opens the Purchase Order Log Inquiry screen to allow you to view every transaction that affected the purchase order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a page number, reference number, or line number to go directly to.
