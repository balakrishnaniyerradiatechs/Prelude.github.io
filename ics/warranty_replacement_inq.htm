Warranty Replacement Inquiry
The Warranty Replacement Inquiry displays open (suspended) warranty replacement entries for a vendor.
Viewing Warranty Replacements
To view open or suspended warranty replacement entries:
1
Select Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Warranty Replacement Inquiry to open the Warranty Replacement Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Vendor for which you want to view open warranty replacements.
Open warranty replacement entries for the vendor display.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The number for the vendor for which you wish to view open replacement entries.
Wty Rep#
The warranty replacement number entered in Warranty Replacement Entry. Refer to Entering Warranty Replacements.
Rpl Date
The date the warranty replacement was received.
Vendor Invoice#
The vendor’s invoice number for the warranty replacement.
Replaced
Units
The number of units returned on the vendor claim that are being replaced by the vendor.
Dollars
The dollar amount of the items being replaced.
Replacing
Units
The number of units the vendor sent to replace the items returned on the vendor claim.
Dollars
The dollar amount of the units the vendor sent to replace the items returned on the vendor claim.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review W/R
Opens the Warranty Replacements Inquiry screen to display the original entry information. You can view the replaced products on the Warranty Replaced Review screen and the replacing products on the Warranty Replacing Review screen.
F6-Enter W/R
Allows you to enter a warranty entry number if several pages of warranty replacements are listed.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
