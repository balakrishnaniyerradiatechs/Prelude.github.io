Freezing Purchasing Controls
To freeze purchasing controls for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance to open the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product and the Warehouse number.
3
For Frozen Controls, enter Y so that the productâ€™s purchasing parameters are not automatically recalculated.
4
In the next (unlabeled) field, enter the thaw date which is the date that you want to the frozen controls removed. End Of Month Calendar close will start factoring in monthly usage after this date to calculate purchasing parameters for the product.
5
Press F2-Save.
