Linked Merchandise Received & Not Allocated Report
The Linked Merchandise Received & Not Allocated report displays received products that are not yet allocated to linked sales orders, work orders, or transfers. The report also displays the reason why the item is not allocated.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Linked Merchandise Received Not Allocated
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Selling. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The received product number linked to a sales order, work order, or quote.
Document
The document number that the product is linked to. The prefix indicates the order type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
T - Transfer.
Ln#
The line number of the product on the sales order, work order, or transfer.
Landed Cost
The cost of the material at the time of receipt.
Qty-Ord
The quantity ordered on the sales order, work order, or transfer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the sales order, work order, or transfer.
Qty-Rec
The quantity of the product received.
Time Date
The time and date the material was received.
Message
The reason why the item is not yet allocated to the order (e.g., if the order is on credit hold or if the original order is awaiting invoicing).
