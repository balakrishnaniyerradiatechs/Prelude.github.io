Receiving Lot Products Sold In Packs
The Lot Pack Confirmation screen appears when you receive a product if the productâ€™s Lot Pack flag is set to Y in Product Maintenance (Refer to Setting Up a Lot Product).
To receive lot pack products:
1
For Lot #, enter the lot number from the package or enter N to assign a new lot number.
2
Enter the Bin Number, if applicable.
3
For LP Qty, accept the default lot pack quantity or enter a different quantity.
4
For Desc, accept the default description or enter a different description.
5
For CO, accept the default country of origin or change it to the appropriate country.
6
For #LP, accept the default number of lot packs per pallet or enter a different quantity.
7
For Price, accept the default price or enter a new price.
8
For Comm Cost, enter the default commission cost or enter a new cost.
9
Enter the Quantity to receive for this lot number.
10
Repeat steps 1-9 for each lot number and quantity to receive for this product. The total quantities of all the lot numbers must equal the quantity received in the Detail Information screen. If not, you must re-enter the lot quantities or default the quantity received to the total of the lot quantities.
11
Press F2-Save to save the lot pack information and to receive the next item on the purchase order.
