End of Month & End of Year Procedures
Recording and tracking sales and product information is vital to controlling inventory levels. The End of Month Calendar Close calculates usage and forecast information and rebuilds customer, vendor, and product statistics. The End of Year Procedure rolls the data for each year into the previous year buckets so that the information can be tracked over several years.
