Stock Transfer Sources screen
The Stock Transfer Sources Screen allows you to view incoming transfers for a product that have not yet been received.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry > Transfers (under Sources) > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer#
The transfer number containing the product.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that product notes exist for this item on this transfer.
From
The shipping warehouse.
Description
The description of the shipping warehouse.
Ent-Date
The date the transfer was entered.
Qty-Opn
The quantity on the transfer not yet received.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the transfer.
F
Indicates if the transfer is a future:
•
F - A future transfer.
•
Blank - Not a future transfer.
Qty-Pck
The quantity picked on the transfer.
Qty-Bo
The quantity backordered on the transfer.
Qty-Shp
The quantity already shipped on the transfer.
Shp-Date
The date the transfer shipped from the shipping warehouse.
Linkage S
The document number of the source of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - The product was purchased on this purchase order at the shipping warehouse.
•
T - The product was ordered on this transfer at the shipping warehouse.
Linkage D
The document number for the destination of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this sales order number.
•
T - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this transfer number.
•
W - When the product arrives, it will be allocated to this work order.
Status
The status of the transfer:
•
PRINTED - The transfer pick ticket printed but the transfer is not yet shipped.
•
ON-ORDER - The transfer is entered and/or backordered, but not yet printed.
•
IN-TRANSIT - The transfer is on its way to the receiving warehouse. The release was entered in Transfer Shipments Entry for the From warehouse but is not yet received at the To warehouse.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen and brings you back to the Inventory Inquiry Screen.
F5-Review Transfer
Opens the Stock Transfer Display screen to display detail information regarding the transfer.
F7-Log
Opens the Transfer Log Inquiry to list all transactions for the highlighted transfer.
F9-Goto line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
