Low Stock Status Report
The Low Stock Status report displays products with an available inventory that is below the minimum quantity or safety allowance for the product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Low Stock Status
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include. Leave blank to view all warehouses.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Product. The product number(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products that fall within the selected product type criteria.
•
Status Code. The stock status code to limit the items on the report. Leave blank to include all status codes or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product with low stock.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Min/Max
Line 1: The minimum amount to keep in stock if using the min/max purchasing method.
Line 2: The maximum amount to keep in stock if using the min/max purchasing method.
Sfy/Qty
Line 1: The safety allowance quantity to keep in stock if using safety allowance to calculate purchase quantities.
Line 2: The recommended quantity to buy if using safety allowance to calculate purchase quantities.
On-Hand
The on-hand quantity of the product in this warehouse.
Qpk
The quantity of the product committed to open sales orders in pick status (the pick ticket printed but the shipment is not yet confirmed).
Qpst
The quantity of the product committed to open outgoing transfers in pick status (the pick ticket printed but the shipment is not yet confirmed).
Qpr
The quantity of the product not yet received on open purchase orders.
Qprt
The quantity of the product requested but not yet received on open transfers.
Available
The quantity available for sale.
