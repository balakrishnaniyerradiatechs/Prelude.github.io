Batch Transfer Receipts Entry
You can receive stock for multiple transfers on one batch number.
To receive stock transfers by batch:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Batch Transfer Receipts > Batch Transfer Receipts Entry to open the Batch Number Entry screen.
2
Enter the Batch Number as designated by your company. This number is for reference only and is not validated.
3
Press ENTER. The Batch Transfer Receipts Entry screen appears.
4
Enter the Transfer Release# from the pick ticket (e.g., 10000297-1) to list the open items on the transfer.
5
Enter the Receipt Date. The following prompt displays:
Do You Want to Receive Transfer By...
6
Choose from the following:
•
Line Item: Receives one line item at a time. You can use this option if only a few items or partial quantities arrive. Highlight the item to receive and press ENTER to open the Transfer Receipts Detail screen. Continue with step 7.
•
Exception: Automatically enters the shipped Quantity into the Received quantity on every item. You can use this method if most of the order was received in full, but you need to change only a few items or quantities. Highlight the item to change and press ENTER to open the Transfer Receipts Detail screen. Continue with step 7.
•
Complete: Receives the entire transfer and completes the stock receipt. This option can be a very dangerous since it receives all items in full and does not allow changes. If the transfer is complete, you can also use Exception, which receives everything but gives you chance to review the transfer.
•
Abort: Exits the receiver without updating any information.
7
If you chose Line Item or Exception in step 6, the Transfer Receipts Detail screen displays.
8
For Rec Qty, enter the quantity received.

Receiving to Bins
If the receiving warehouse uses bins, the Bin Locator Information screen appears after you enter the Rec Qty. You can enter one or more bins for the material, but the total of the bin quantities must equal the amount of the Rec Qty.
9
Press F2-Save to complete the receipt for the line item.
10
After receiving the correct quantity for each line item, press F2-Save to complete the transfer receipt and to update inventory.
After you receive multiple transfers in a batch, run the Batch Transfer Receipts Report to verify the received information. If necessary, you can go back into Batch Transfer Receipts Entry to adjust any quantities. Once the quantities are correct, run the Batch Transfer Receipts Update to update inventory.
