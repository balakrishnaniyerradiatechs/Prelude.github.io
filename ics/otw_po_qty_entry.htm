OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen
The OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen provides a worksheet to allow you to enter or change purchase order quantities when you receive an on-the-water purchase order.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > On the Water Maintenance > F7-Chg P/O Qty
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O#
The open OTW purchase order numbers containing the item to change.
P/O Date
The date each purchase order was entered.
Open Quantity
The quantity not yet shipped from the vendor on the open purchase orders.
Worksheet Quantity
The new open quantity that displays when you re-access this screen after changing the quantity.
Remain Qty
The quantity remaining on the purchase order.
New Quantity
The new quantity to go on the OTW purchase order.
Totals
The total quantities for each column.
F2-Save
Saves the quantity information.
