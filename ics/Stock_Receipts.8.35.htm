Combining License Plates
You can combine multiple license plates into one license plate and create a new license plate number containing the combined quantities. The original license plates show a zero on-hand balance.
Example: If you received 10 of a line item and accidentally created 5 labels instead of just 1 label for the entire quantity, you may need to combine the 5 license plates.
To combine license plates:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > License Plate > License Plate Combine Entry to open the License Plate Combine Entry screen.
2
For License Plate, enter the license plate numbers that you want to combine. Press F3 to look up license plate numbers by product. The Product, Lot, and Quantity for each license plate display.
3
Press F2-Save to combine all of the listed license plate numbers. A new label prints with the new license plate number.
Lot Inquiry
Lot Inquiry displays information on lot/license plate numbers and quantities.
Transaction Audit Inquiry
The Transaction Audit Inquiry displays an LCM code for the product number to reference that the license plate was combined.
