Clearing and Resetting Physical Inventory Tags
After you enter tag quantities, verify counts, run the necessary reports, and update inventory, you must clear and reset the inventory tag numbers before you run the next physical inventory.
To clear and reset physical inventory tags:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Tag Procedure > Physical Tag Clear Procedure to open the Physical Tag Update And Clear screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse.
3
Press ENTER. The following message displays:
Warning! This Procedure Will Clear out all
Physical Inventory Tag Records and Reset
The Tag Range Counters! Do you wish to Continue?
4
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Clears the physical inventory tag records and resets the tag counters.
•
No - Cancels the clear and reset procedure.
Do not run the Physical Tag Clear Procedure until after your physical inventory is complete or all the tag and recount information will be lost.
