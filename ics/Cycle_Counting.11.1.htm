Cycle Counting
Cycle counting is defined as counting part of your inventory each day and counting the items in each bin several times per year. This method of taking physical inventory, rather than an annual physical inventory, continuously maintains an accurate on-hand count for each product in your warehouse.
You can set up tolerance limits to determine which products to recount when the new count differs from the on-hand quantity. If you enter a count that is outside of the tolerance limit, the product goes into the second level queue for a recount.
You can cycle count with radio frequency devices or manual count sheets. You do not have to purchase the Warehousing module to use cycle counting, but you must set the appropriate maintenance flags and use bins to track the inventory.
