Copying All Products to a New Warehouse
If you have a new warehouse and you want a quick and efficient way to set up the products in the new warehouse, you can copy all products from an existing warehouse to the new one. If a product already exists in the new warehouse, it is not overlaid. This feature only works with products in the inventory file, not the catalog file.
To copy all products to a new warehouse:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Copy Warehouse Utility to access the Copy Warehouse Utility screen.
2
Enter the Copy From Whse, which is the existing warehouse that contains the products.
3
For Copy To Whse, enter the warehouses where you want the new products created. Enter ALL to copy the product information to all warehouses.
4
Press F2-Save to copy all products in the From warehouse to the To warehouses listed. If a product already exist in a To warehouse, it is skipped and not overlaid with the new product information.
