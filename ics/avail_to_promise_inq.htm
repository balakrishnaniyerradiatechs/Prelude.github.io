Available to Promise Inquiry
The Available to Promise Inquiry displays the future availability of a product by defined time periods. You can view detail information for each document that has a quantity on order or committed.
Checking Availability by Time Period
To view future time period availability for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Availability Inquiries > Available to Promise Inquiry to open the Available to Promise Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product to view.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
4
For ATP Days, enter the number of days to use to calculate availability for each time period (the default is 7 days). Five of these periods display, starting with todayâ€™s date.
The calculated inventory status of the item displays for the future time periods.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number. The product description displays to the right.
Warehouse
The warehouse for which you want to inquire on availability for the product. The warehouse description displays.
ATP Days
The number of days to use to calculate availability for each time period (the default is 7). Five of these periods display, starting with todayâ€™s date.
Thru
The time periods of availability. The date on each period is the ending date of the period.
Beginning Inv
The estimated quantity available on the first day of each time period, calculated from all open transactions.
On P/O
The quantities on purchase orders for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from estimated delivery dates or future purchase orders.
On Transfer
The quantities on transfers for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from estimated delivery dates or future transfers.
On Work Order
The quantities on work orders for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from estimated delivery dates or from future work orders.
On Consignment
The quantities on consignment orders for each time period.
On the Water
The quantities committed to on-the-water purchase orders for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from estimated delivery dates or future on-the-water purchase orders.
To Sales Order
The quantities committed to sales orders for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from the Required Date in the Sales Order Header screen.
To Transfer
The quantities committed to outgoing transfers for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from the Pick Ticket Date in the transfer header screen.
To Work Order
The quantities committed to work orders for each time period. Future quantities are calculated from the Required Date in the Work Order Header screen.
To Vend Claim
The quantities allocated to vendor claims for each time period.
Ending Inv
The ending inventory for each time period, taking into consideration all of the incoming and committed quantities.
Non-Nettable
If a quantity exists, you can press F5-Review to view any products that are non-nettable material not available for sale. The product exists in a non-nettable bin.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry.
F5-Review
Allows you to view the orders that contain the item. If a quantity exists in any of the time periods, place the cursor on the quantity and press F5-Review for a detailed listing of orders.
