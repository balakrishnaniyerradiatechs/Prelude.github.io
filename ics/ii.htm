Inventory Inquiry
The Inventory Inquiry screen displays detailed information on the inventory status and usage of a product for a warehouse. You can review open transactions to identify where an item is committed to an order or in transit to a warehouse. Information on kits, bills of material, alternates, substitutes, accessories, and complementary products is also available.
Checking Inventory Status
To view the status and availability of a product:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry to open the Inventory Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product to view.
3
Enter the Warehouse for the product. The on-hand, on-order, and committed quantity information displays.
4
To view open transactions containing the product, move the cursor to the field in front of the transaction type that you want to view under Sources or Commitments and press F5-Review.
Source refers to quantities coming into the warehouse; Commitment refers to quantities committed to orders going out of the warehouse. For example:
•
Source: If a quantity exists under Open next to Purchase Orders, you can view all purchase orders containing the item. Place the cursor on the blank field to the left of Purchase Orders and press F5-Review to display a list of all open purchase orders. From here you can view the detail information on each purchase order.
•
Commitments: If a quantity exists under Open next to Sales Orders, you can view all sales orders containing the item, that have not yet been released for invoicing. Place the cursor on the blank field to the left of Sales Orders and press F5-Review to display a list of all open sales orders, including the quantity picked. From here you can view the detail information on each sales order.
Refer to the Field and Function Key Descriptions table for further explanation of the information and inquiries available from this screen.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description, if applicable.
Weight
The weight of the product, if a Weight exists in the Product Maintenance Screen.
U/M
The stocking unit of measure of the product from the Product Maintenance Screen. Refer to Establishing a Unit of Measure.
Warehouse
The warehouse number for which you want to view the inventory status of the product. The description of the warehouse displays.
Bin
The primary bin location for the product in this warehouse record if you use bins.
Class
The Inventory Class from the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen for this product/warehouse record. Refer to Classifying Products.
Forecast
The calculated Forecast quantity of the product from the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
Op/Min
The order point or minimum of the product as calculated in the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen. If the available stock falls below this quantity, the system recommends replenishment.
Lp/Max
The line point or maximum quantity. The maximum quantity to keep in stock displays if a value is established in the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen. Otherwise, the line point quantity displays for the product.
Ldt
The average lead time from the Product Vendor Maintenance Screen. The average number of days required to replenish the product from the day it is ordered to the day it is received.
Status
The stocking status of the product:
•
S - Stock.
•
N - Nonstock.
•
C - Consignment.
Surplus
The surplus quantity of the product. If using the EOQ purchase method, this is any quantity available over the EOQ plus the line point. If using the min/max purchase method, this is the quantity over the maximum recommended quantity to keep in stock.
Onhand
The quantity currently on-hand of the product in this warehouse, regardless of availability.
Sources: The documents containing inventory coming into the warehouse.
Open
The quantities on the corresponding document types that are on-order but not yet received.
Purchase Orders
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Purchase Order Sources screen to view detail information for each purchase order containing the product.
Transfers
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Stock Transfer Sources screen to view detail information for each incoming stock transfer containing the product.
Work Orders
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Work Order Sources screen to view detail information for each work order in process for the bill of material.
Consignments
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Consignment Sources screen to view detail information for each consignment sales orders (type 07 & 08) that contain a product. The products are on loan to the customer and will go back into on-hand inventory when the customer is invoiced.
On The Water
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the OTW Purchase Order Inquiry screen to view detail information for each on-the-water purchase order containing the product. Refer to Receiving On-The-Water Merchandise for more information.
Commitments: The documents containing quantities committed to orders.
Open
The quantities of the product on the corresponding transaction types that are committed to sales orders, work orders, or transfers.
Pick
The quantities of the product on the corresponding transaction types that are pickable. If a sufficient quantity exists in inventory to cover all open transactions, this quantity is the same as the Open quantity. If a product is backordered, only the quantity available to pick displays.
Sales Orders
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Sales Order Commitments screen to view detail information for each open sales order containing the product. These are sales orders that have not yet been released for invoicing.
Transfers
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Stock Transfer Commitments screen to view detail information for each outgoing stock transfer containing the product.
Work Orders
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Work Order Commitments screen to view detail information for each work for which the product is committed to as a component.
Vendor Claims
If a quantity exists under Open, press F5-Review to open the Vendor Claim Commitments screen to view detail information for each vendor claim containing the product. Refer to Creating Vendor Claims.
Non-Nettable
If a quantity exists under Pick press F5-Review to open the Product Bin Inquiry to view bin information on the non-nettable product. Non-nettable means that the material is not available for work orders, transfers, sales orders, or vendor claims. The material is located in a bin defined as non-nettable. Refer to Setting Up Bins.
Available
The quantity of the item available to sell.
Cross Reference & Related Product Information
(unlabeled field at the bottom left corner of the screen)
Some products may display one or more of the following cross reference and related product information codes. This information also displays on the Line Item Detail screen on a sales order or quote.
•
ALT - Alternate products exist for this product. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Alternates to view the alternate product numbers. Refer to Alternate Products for information on setting up alternate product numbers.
•
SUB - Substitute products exist for this product. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Substitutes to view the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen. Refer to Substitute Products for information on setting up substitute products.
•
ACC - Accessory products can potentially be sold with this product. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Accessories to view the list of recommended accessories to sell with this product. Refer to Accessory Products for information on setting up accessories.
•
COMP - Complement information exists for this product. Complements provide the sales person with helpful information when selling the product. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Complements to view complements entered for this product. Refer to Complements for information on setting up product complements.
•
DESC - An additional extended product description exists for the product. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Additional Description to view the extended description set up for the product. Refer to Additional Product Descriptions for information on setting up an additional description.
•
KIT - This product is a kit parent item. The number displayed is the number of kits you can make with the available components. Select F6-Whse Avail > F5 Kit Available to view the kits available in each warehouse. Then select F5-Component Availability to view the Product Kit Availability Inquiry, which shows the availability of each component. Refer to Kit Parent Products for more information.
•
BOM - This product is a bill of material. The number displayed is the number of BOMs you can make with available components. Select F6- Whse Avail > F6-BOM Available to view availability of this BOM in each warehouse. Then select F5-Components to view the availability of each component on the BOM.
•
EQV - Equivalent products exists for product. Equivalent products are part numbers that do not exist in the system, but may in the future. Once the equivalent product becomes a valid product in the system, it becomes a substitute rather than an equivalent. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Substitutes > F7-Equivalents to view the Equivalents screen. Refer to Substitute Products for more information.
•
RSVD - The quantity in parentheses is reserved for a customer. Select Action Bar > Inquiries > Reserved Inventory to view the customer for whom it is reserved. Refer to Reserving Inventory for a Customer.
F2-Finish
Closes the inventory inquiry.
F5-Review
Displays detail information for any of the transactions listed under Sources or Commitments. For example: Place the cursor in the field in front of Sales Orders and press F5 to bring up a list of all open sales orders containing this item.
F6-Whse Avail
Opens the Product Warehouse Availability Inquiry to display available quantities and forecast information for the product in all viewable warehouses setup for this warehouse.
F7-Add Info
Opens the Inventory Inquiry Information screen to display additional purchasing and packing information for the product.
F8-Future
Opens the Future Availability Inquiry to display future available quantities of the item. The future available quantity is calculated by taking the onhand quantity, subtracting the quantities committed to orders, and adding the quantities in-transit to the warehouse. You can review detail information for each document containing the item.
F9-ATP
Opens the Available to Promise Inquiry to display the future availability by time period. You can view detail information for each document that has a quantity on order or committed.
Action Bar
Inquiries
Provides access to the following inquiry information:
•
Alternates - Opens the Product Alternates Inquiry screen to allow you to view alternate products that exist for the product. Refer to Alternate Products for information on setting up alternate products.
•
Substitutes - Opens the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen to allow you to view information on substitutes for this product. Refer to Substitute Products for information on setting up substitute products.
•
Accessories - Opens the Accessory Inquiry screen to allow you to view the list of recommended accessories to sell with this product. Refer to Accessory Products for information on setting up accessories
•
Complements - Opens the Product Complement Inquiry screen to allow you to view complentary information entered for this product. Refer to Complements for information on setting up product complements.
•
Additional Description - Opens the Additional Description screen to allow you to view any additional lines of description that exists for the product. Refer to Additional Product Descriptions for information on setting up an additional product description.
•
Lot Information - Opens the Lot Inquiry to allow you to view detailed lot number information for the product.
•
Core Product Info - Opens the Core Product Information screen to allow you to view core cost information. Refer to Core Products and Pending Cores for information on setting up core products.
•
Product Notes - Opens the Product Notes screen to allow you to view any product notes linked to comment codes. Refer to Product Notes for information on entering product notes.
•
Notes - Internal notes entered for the product.
•
Reserved Inventory - Corp View - Opens the Reserved Inventory - Corporate View screen (only accessible if RSVD displays at the bottom left of the screen) to display the quantity reserved for each customer for each warehouse (refer to Reserving Inventory for a Customer).
•
TPCx Price Availability Request - If you are set up to use TPCx (Trading Partner Connect), you can check price and availability for the product from other trading partners.
•
Kit Component Availability - Opens the Kit Component Availability Inquiry screen to allow you to view the on-order, backorder, available, and delivery date information for the components in a kit.
