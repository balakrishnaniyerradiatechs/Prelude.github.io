Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen
The Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen allows you to establish hold areas within your warehouse to stage material for 02 - Ship Complete and 03 -Tag and Hold order types. When a hold area is assigned to a sales order in Hold Area Assignment, a message displays on the pick ticket to inform the picker of the designated hold area. When backorders come in, the hold area prints on the pick ticket to indicate where the rest of the order is located.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Warehouse Hold Areas
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Warehouse
The number for the warehouse where you want to set up the hold area.
Hold Area Number
The code to indicate where hold orders are assigned.
Description
The description of the hold area.
Description Two
The second line of description of the hold area.
F2-Save
Saves the hold area information.
F4-Del
Deletes the hold area information.
