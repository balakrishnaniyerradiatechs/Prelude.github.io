Keywords
Product keywords are search words you can use to access an item in addition to the product number, description, or other fields identified in Keyword Maintenance. You can enter multiple keywords for each product number.
To enter product keywords:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Keywords to open the Additional Keywords screen.
5
Enter any Keywords to use for all product searches.
6
Press F2-Save to save the keyword information.
