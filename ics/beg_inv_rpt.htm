Year Beginning Inventory Value Report
The Year Beginning Inventory Value report shows the total value of inventory at the beginning of a fiscal year. The selection criteria allows you to limit the report by warehouse, product, product type, or product line.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Inventory Valuation Reports > Year Beginning Inventory Value
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product numbers.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Only Onhand not equal to 0. Determines if you want to view only products with an on-hand quantity. Choose from the following:
-
Y - Only products with an on-hand quantity or negative on-hand quantity.
-
N - All products, including products with an on-hand balance of zero.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Description Two
The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Beg-Inv
The quantity of the product in stock at the beginning of the current fiscal year.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure.
Beg-cost
The unit cost of the product at the beginning of the current fiscal year.
Beg-Value
The extended value of the total quantity of the product at the beginning of the year (Beg-Inv x Beg-cost).
