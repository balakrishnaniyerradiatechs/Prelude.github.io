Future Availability Inquiry
The Future Availability Inquiry displays the future availability of a product. You can view detail information for each document with a quantity on order or committed.
Viewing Future Product Availability
To view future availability for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Availability Inquiries > Future Availability Inquiry to open the Future Availability Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product to view.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
The committed and in-transit information for the product displays.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number. The product description displays.
Warehouse
The warehouse for which you want to check availability for the product. The warehouse description displays.
Lead Time Days
The average number of days it takes to receive the product from the vendor or warehouse transfer.
Onhand Quantity
The quantity currently on-hand in the warehouse.
Document#
The number of the document containing the product.
Type
The type of document:
•
PO - Purchase order.
•
SO - Sales order.
•
WO - Work order (coming in).
•
WC - Work order committed (going out).
•
TS - A transfer shipping out of the warehouse.
•
TR - A transfer in-transit to the warehouse.
•
VC - Vendor claim.
Date
The date the document was entered.
Customer/Vendor/Whse
The source of the document:
•
The customer number if the document is a sales order.
•
The vendor number if the document is a purchase order or vendor claim.
•
The warehouse number if the document number is a transfer or work order.
Quantity
The quantity on the document.
Future Amount
The quantity available after the purchase orders and transfers are received and the quantities committed to sales orders, work orders, and transfers are shipped.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen.
F5-Review
Allows you to view detail information on the highlighted document number.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, reference number, or page number to go directly to.
