Saving Current Onhand Quantities
To make sure that the inventory in the warehouse is accurate before you save the on-hand quantities, you must:
•
Receive all items on purchase orders and transfers that physically arrived in the warehouse.
•
Update and invoice all sales orders, transfers, and work orders that shipped out of the warehouse.
•
Create the inventory count lists (refer to Building the Physical Inventory Lists). If you print the count sheets before you save on- hand quantities, the on-hand quantities will not display on the count sheets.
Once you save the on-hand quantities, you cannot enter any transactions that could change a product’s on-hand balance:
To save the current on-hand quantities:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Radio Frequency Procedure > Save Current Onhand Quantities to open the Save Current Onhand Quantities screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse number associated with the list.
3
Enter the Physical ListName of the inventory count list or leave blank to save on-hand quantities for all lists created for the warehouse.
4
Press ENTER. The following message displays:
Do You Wish To Continue With Physical Inventory Quantity Onhand Save Procedure For Warehouse 20 And Listname 2010101?
•
No - Returns to the screen. Onhand quantities are not saved.
•
Yes - Saves the on-hand quantities.
After you save on-hand quantities, you can enter the new counts (refer to Entering Counts on RF Devices).
