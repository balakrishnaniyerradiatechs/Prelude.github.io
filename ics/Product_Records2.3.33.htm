Setting Up the Calling Product
To set up the calling product that pulls the surcharge onto the order:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product to set up as the calling product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Surcharge Product to open the Surcharge Products screen.
5
Enter the Surcharge Product to add when the calling product is entered on an order. For information creating a surcharge product, refer to Setting Up the Surcharge Product.
6
For Srchg Amt, enter the dollar amount to add to the order when the surcharge product is invoiced. If you entered a Multiplier for the surcharge product, you can leave this field blank (refer to Setting Up the Surcharge Product).
7
For Qty, enter the minimum quantity of the calling product that the customer must buy to prevent being billed the surcharge.
For example:
•
If you enter 10 for Qty, any sales order that contains a quantity of 10 or higher of this product has no surcharge. If you enter a quantity of less than 10 on a sales order, a dialog box displays to let you know that a surcharge will be added. You can choose to accept or remove the surcharge.
•
If you leave Qty blank, all orders with this calling product automatically add a surcharge.
8
Press F2-Save to save the surcharge product information.
