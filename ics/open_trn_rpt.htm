Open Stock Transfer Report
The Open Stock Transfer report displays entered and shipped transfers that are not yet received at the receiving warehouse. You can select to view transfers going out or transfers coming into a warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movement Reports > Open Stock Transfer Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse for which you want to view open transfers. Either the shipping or the receiving warehouse.
•
Entry Date. The date the transfer was entered. Leave blank to view all open transfers for the warehouse.
•
Shipping/Receiving Whse S/R. Determines whether you want to view transfers shipped from the selected warehouse or transfers in-transit to the selected warehouse:
-
S - To view open transfers shipping from the warehouse.
-
R - To view open transfers coming into the warehouse.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Transfer#
The open transfer number if the order was entered but not shipped.
The open transfer release number if the order shipped.
To (if you selected S for shipping whse)
From (if you selected R for receiving whse)
To: The warehouse to receive the material.
From: The warehouse shipping the material.
Ent-Date
Line 1: The date the transfer was entered.
Line 2: The status of the transfer:
•
ON-ORDER - The transfer was entered but is not yet printed or shipped.
•
PRINTED - The transfer printed but is not yet shipped.
•
IN-TRANS - The transfer shipped and is in transit to the receiving warehouse.
Product Number
The products on the corresponding transfer.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
S
The source of the product request, if applicable:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity remaining on the transfer (quantity entered - quantity shipped).
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
Qty-Pck
Line 1:The quantity allocated to the transfer.
Line 2: The sales order or work order number linked to the product, if applicable.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered.
Cost/Ext
Line 1: The unit cost of the product.
Line 2: The extended cost of the ordered quantity.
