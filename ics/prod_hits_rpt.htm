Product Hits Report
The Product Hits report displays the number of times a product was requested. For example, if a customer buys 10 of a product on one order, 1 hit is recorded for the product. If part of the quantity backorders and is invoiced separately, the total number of hits calculated for the product is still 1.
Product hits are updated on:
•
Sales orders when the order is invoiced (first invoicing only).
•
Work orders when the work order is issued (if using the warehousing module or a fixed bill of material) or at the time of completion (if not using the warehousing module). First issue only.
•
Transfers when the transfer is shipped (first shipment only).
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Sales Status Reports > Product Hits Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Vendor. The vendor(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
How many months?. The number of months to include. The report begins with the most recent complete month. For example, if today is December 15 and you enter 6 months, the report will include all product hits in the months from June through November.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Sales
The number of product hits from sales orders for each of the corresponding months. The number of hits is the number of times a product is requested, regardless of the quantity ordered or the number of releases required to ship the full quantity. Sales order hits are updated when the order is first invoiced.
Transfers
The number of product hits from transfers for the corresponding months. The number of hits is the number of times a product is requested, regardless of the quantity ordered or the number of releases required to ship the full quantity. Transfers hits are updated in when the transfer is first shipped.
Work Orders
The number of product hits from work orders for the corresponding months. The number of hits is the number of times a product is requested for a work order, regardless of the quantity ordered or the number of issues required to complete the request. Work order hits are updated in Work Order Issues Entry (if using the warehousing module or a fixed bill of material) or at the time of Work Order Completions (if not using the warehousing module). First issue only.
Total Hits
The total number of hits for the product on sales orders, transfers, or work orders.
Month columns
The months when the product hits occurred.
