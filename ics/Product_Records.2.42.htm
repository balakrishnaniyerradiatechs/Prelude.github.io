Maintaining Product Purchasing Parameters
Product Purchasing Parameters define purchasing methods and ordering requirements to determine how Nightly Processing replenishes each product. The purchasing parameters defined at this level override any parameter settings at the company and warehouse level.
The following screens are available in Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance:
•
Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
•
Status Inquiry Screen.
•
Product Alert Information Screen.
•
Product Usage Maintenance Screen.
Purchase Parameters Listing
To obtain a listing of purchasing parameters for a product, select Inventory > Listings > Product Listings > Purchase Parameters.
