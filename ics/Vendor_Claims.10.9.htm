Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory
Once a product is in warranty inventory (refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory), you can assign it to a vendor claim to allocate it for return to the vendor.
Nonstock or “*” part numbers are not allowed on vendor claims without a warranty tag.
To enter a vendor claim for warranty inventory:
1
Follow steps 1-12 in Entering a Vendor Claim for Regular Inventory.
2
On a blank line, press F7- Wty Tag# to open the Warranty Tag Number screen.
3
Enter the warranty tag number for the material to return. If you do not have the tag number, press F3 to search for tag numbers by vendor, product, order number, or date. The product and quantity on the warranty tag number display on the Vendor Claim Entry screen. Any return information and warranty tag comments also display.
If the vendor’s RMA number on the warranty tag is different than the RMA number on the vendor claim, you must choose which number to use.
4
Complete the vendor claim starting with step 17 in Entering a Vendor Claim for Regular Inventory.
