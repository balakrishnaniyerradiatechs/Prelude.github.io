Stock Transfer Discrepancy Report
The Stock Transfer Discrepancy report displays items on open transfers that have a quantity shown as shipped by the shipping warehouse but were not received at the receiving warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movement Reports > Stock Transfer Discrepancy Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) containing the transfer discrepancies.
•
Shipping/Receiving Whse S/R. Determines how to sort the transfers:
-
S - Sort open transfers by shipping warehouse.
-
R - Sort open transfers by receiving warehouse.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Trans#/
To (if you selected S to sort by shipping whse)
From (if you selected R to sort by receiving whse)
Line 1: The open transfer release number.
Line 2: The shipping/receiving warehouse:
•
The warehouse to receive the material (if you selected S to sort by shipping whse).
•
The warehouse that shipped the material (if you selected R to sort by receiving whse).
Date-Shp
The date the transfer shipped.
Product Number
The product with the discrepancy.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
S
The source of the product request, if applicable:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity that shows as shipped but is not received at the receiving warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
Cost/Ext
Line 1: The unit cost.
Line 2: The extended cost of the total discrepancy quantity (Qty-Opn x Cost).
