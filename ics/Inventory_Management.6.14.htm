Adjusting Inventory Quantities
To account for discrepancies in on-hand balances, you can make manual adjustments to inventory. Inventory adjustment reason codes (refer to Establishing Inventory Adjustment Reason Codes), must be set up before you can adjust inventory.
Counter Range Setup
Before you can adjust inventory, you must set up the number range and beginning number in Counter Range Setup > Inventory Adjustment Numbers.
To enter adjust inventory balances:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Inventory Adjustments Entry to open the Inventory Adjustment Entry screen.
2
For I/A Number, press ENTER to assign the next available inventory adjustment transaction number.
3
For Entry Date, either accept the default of today’s date or enter the appropriate date for the transaction. The fiscal period displays to the right.
4
Enter the Warehouse where the quantity is being adjusted.
Option Parameters
Force User Location in Inv Adj in Additional Option Parameters > F6-ICS determines if the location assigned to your user ID defaults as the Warehouse number.
5
For Adj.Type, enter the type of inventory to adjust. Choose from the following:
•
INV - Regular on-hand inventory. Enter a positive or a negative quantity to adjust inventory. Average cost is updated when inventory is increased.
•
DMG - Damaged inventory. Enter a positive quantity to increase the damaged inventory amount for the product and decrease the on-hand inventory of the product. Enter a negative quantity to decrease the damaged inventory amount and increase the on-hand amount.
•
SCR - Scrapped/lost inventory. Enter a positive quantity to increase the scrapped inventory for the product and decrease the on-hand inventory of the product. Enter a negative quantity to decrease the scrapped inventory amount and increase the on-hand amount.
•
WTY - Warranty inventory. Enter a positive quantity to move the item from the on-hand quantity into warranty inventory. Refer to Creating Warranty Tags in Inventory Adjustment Entry for information on warranty inventory adjustments.
Inventory Adjustment Types
You can set the default adjustment type and determine which adjustment types are valid in Inventory Controls > Inventory Adjustment Entry Types.
6
Enter the Product Number to adjust. The Inventory Adjustment Line Detail screen appears.
7
Enter the Quantity to add or subtract from inventory. Refer to step 5 to determine whether to enter a positive or a negative quantity.
8
If you are adding a quantity to inventory, enter the Cost.
9
For Reason, enter the inventory adjustment reason code.
You must use the correct reason code to post to the correct general ledger account (refer to Establishing Inventory Adjustment Reason Codes).
10
Once everything in the detail screen is correct press F2-Save.
11
Repeat steps 6 through 10 to add products. Press F2-Save when complete.
Notes
To enter notes regarding each inventory adjustment, select the Notes option from the Action Bar. Enter notes for each line number or for the entire adjustment.
Lot Products
If you are adjusting lot products, either the Lot Number Information screen or the Lot Pack Confirmation screen appears. Refer to Setting Up a Lot Product.
