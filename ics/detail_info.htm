Detail Information screen
The Detail Information screen allows you to enter the quantity and cost of each product received.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > Stock Receipts Entry > Line item
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number from the purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity not yet received.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the purchase order.
Whse
The warehouse receiving the material.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received in this shipment.
Accept
Indicates if everything in the detail information screen is correct:
•
Y - The information in the detail information screen is correct. Returns to the Stock Receipt Entry screen.
•
N - The information is not correct. You can change any of the fields.
Qty-B/O
The quantity not yet received.
Del-Date
The expected delivery date as entered on the purchase order.
Gross
The cost of the product before any discount. Only accessible if Stock Rec Cost Prompt is set to Y in Inventory Parameters.
Disc%
The percent discount off of the Gross amount to calculate cost for this product.
Net Cost
The cost of the product after the discount. Gross x Disc%.
Src
 
The source from which the cost was derived:
•
BKL - Blanket purchase order.
•
CBQ - Cost quantity break.
•
OV - Manual override.
•
PDQ - Quantity break discounts.
•
PH - Product replacement cost.
•
PHD - Product direct ship cost.
•
PRC - Product replacement cost.
•
PV - Product vendor unit cost.
•
PVC - Product vendor unit cost.
•
PVD - Product vendor direct ship cost.
•
PW - Product warehouse replacement cost.
•
PWD - Product warehouse direct ship cost.
•
VLD - Vendor line discount.
•
VW - Company vendor unit cost.
•
VWC - Company vendor unit cost
•
VWD - Company vendor direct ship cost.
•
WRC - Product warehouse replacement cost.
Promised
The date the product was scheduled to arrive in the warehouse. This date is used to calculate vendor performance statistics. If this field is left blank, Del-Date is used to calculate vendor performance statistics.
Vendor Product#
The vendor’s part number, if applicable.
F2-Save
Saves the received information and closes the screen.
F5-Reject Claim
Opens the Line Item Quality Control screen to allow you to reject the product if it is defective or shipped in error. Refer to Rejecting Stock for more information.
F6-Bin Loc
Opens the Bin Location Information screen which shows the bin number and the quantity going into the bin. This function key is only available if the receiving warehouse uses multiple bins (Multiple Bin Locations set to Y in Warehouse Maintenance).
F7-Notes
Opens the Line Item Notes screen to allow you to enter notes regarding the receipt of this product.
