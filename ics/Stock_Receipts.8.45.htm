Changing Quantities in On The Water Maintenance
You can change quantities in an OTW purchase order to reflect the quantity shipped by the vendor. If an item exists on several purchase orders to the same vendor, you may want to choose which purchase order to use or to change quantities on several purchase orders at one time. The OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen provides a worksheet to allow you to view and adjust quantities.
Separate Lines on Worksheet
Create Separate OTWM Lines? in Purchasing Parameters determines if separate lines should be created for each purchase order added to the OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen:
•
Y - Create a separate line for each purchase order.
•
N - Do not create a separate line for each purchase order.
To change shipped quantities for on-the-water purchase orders:
1
In On The Water Maintenance, use the Reference # to open the original OTW purchase order number (refer to Creating an On-The-Water Purchase Order).
If either Direct Ship or Link are set to Y, you cannot change quantities.
2
Move the cursor to Qty for the item to change and choose one of the following:
•
To change the quantity on just one purchase order without viewing others, enter the quantity for the item under Qty and press ENTER. Continue with the order as usual.
•
To view all purchase orders containing the item, press F7-Chg P/O Qty to open the OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen.
This screen serves as a worksheet to allow you to change quantities on any of the purchase orders listed.
3
Enter the New Quantity as necessary for each purchase order. If you increase the quantity, the quantity on the original purchase order decreases by the same amount. If you decrease the quantity, the quantity on the original purchase order increases by the same amount.
If the total of the New Quantity is greater than the total of purchase order quantities available for the product, you must increase the quantity on the OTW purchase order to equal the new quantity.
4
Press F2-Save. The Qty field on the OTW Receipts Maintenance screen reflects the total quantity of all purchase orders used.
If you need to adjust more quantities, press F7-Chg P/O Qty again. The OTW P/O Quantity Entry screen displays the Worksheet Qty with the updated quantities. You can adjust quantities as necessary.
