Purchase Order Sources screen
The Purchase Order Sources screen displays date and quantity information for purchase orders in transit to the warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry > Purchase Orders (under Sources) > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O#
The purchase order containing the item that is in-transit.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that product notes exist for this item on this purchase order.
Vendor
The vendor assigned to the purchase order.
Description.
The vendor description.
P/O Type
The purchase order type:
•
01 - Regular.
•
02 - Direct ship.
•
03 - Will call.
•
04 - Mutli branch.
•
05 - Special.
•
07 - Capital assets.
•
08 - Non inventory expense.
•
09 - Future purchase order.
•
10 - Consigned.
•
12 - On the water - stock.
•
13 - OTW - direct.
P/O Date
The purchase order date.
Qty-Ord
The quantity on order.
U/M
The unit measure of the item on this purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity not yet received on this purchase order.
Qty-Stk
The quantity to go into stock based on the stocking unit of measure. For example, if the quantity received was 10 and the stocking unit of measure is DZ (dozen), the quantity received is 10 and the quantity in stock is 120. Refer to Establishing a Unit of Measure for more information on unit of measure.
Delivery
The date the line item is expected to be delivered. This date defaults from the header but can be overridden at the line item level.
Del Date Flg
Indicates the source of the Delivery date:
•
C - The date originally confirmed by vendor.
•
E - The date confirmed by vendor after expediting.
•
O - The delivery date was overridden.
•
Blank - The date assigned when the purchase order was entered.
Promised
The promised date of the item from the purchase order header.
Linkage
Indicates if the item is linked to an order. The prefix indicates the order type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T- Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Vendor Order
The vendor’s order number, if applicable.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen and returns to the Inventory Inquiry screen.
F5-Review P/O
Opens the Purchase Order Display screen to allow you to view detail information on the highlighted purchase order.
F7-Log
Opens the Purchase Order Log Inquiry screen to allow you to view all transactions that affected this purchase order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
