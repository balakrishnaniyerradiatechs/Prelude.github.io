Receiving Stock For a Seal Number
To receive stock by seal number:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts By Seal to open the Batch Stock Receipts by Seal screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
For Seal #, enter the seal number from the shipping notice.
3
Press ENTER. Stock is automatically received for all items in the advanced shipping notice received (ASN.RECEIVE) transmission, for the purchase orders listed for the license plates within the seal number.
Use the License Plate Batch Edit Report to view the products received in the seal.
Use the License Plate Receiving Report to view the information in the ASN.RECEIVE file.
4
After you verify the received product information, you can update the batch (refer to Updating Batch Stock Receipts) to put the license plate items into inventory.
