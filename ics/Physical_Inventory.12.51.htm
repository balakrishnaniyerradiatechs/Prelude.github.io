Creating an Inventory List by Product Number
To build a physical inventory list by product:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Radio Frequency Procedure > Build Physical Inventory Lists > By Product(s) to open the Physical Inventory List By Product screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for the inventory list.
3
For Physical ListName, enter a name to identify the count list. You will use this name to enter counts, update inventory, and run inventory reports.
4
For Starting Product and Ending Product, enter a range of products to include in the count lists. Only part numbers within this exact range will be included.
For example, if you enter product numbers AK 1112 and AP 9999; product numbers AK 1111, and AR 1111 are not included.
5
Press F2-Create List. The following prompt appears:
## Items Selected. Press Return.
6
Press ENTER to create the lists for the selected bins.
To view the list, refer to Displaying Physical Inventory Lists.
