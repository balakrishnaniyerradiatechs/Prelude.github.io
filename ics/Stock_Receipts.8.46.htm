OTW Receiving Using Shipping Bins
To use the shipping bin method to receive on-the-water merchandise:
1
Set up the maintenance requirements for overseas vendors and ship bins (refer to Requirements to Receive into Ship Bins).
2
Enter a regular purchase order to the overseas vendor.
3
When the vendor notifies you that the material shipped, receive the products (refer to Receiving Stock for OTW Shipping Bins) into a ship bin. The ship bin designates the material as On-The Water.
4
When the material physically arrives at you warehouse, move the products from the ship bin into a regular bin to update inventory and the general ledger (refer to Entering Individual Bin Moves).
The importing fees associated with the shipment (e.g., consolidation fees, ocean freight, brokerage fees, duty, marine insurance) are rolled into the average cost of the material, even if you pay these fees separately from the vendor’s invoice.
