Related Product Information screen
The related product screen has two functions:
•
Selling Related Products With a Product. If you have one or more products that should always be sold with another product, you can set them up as related products. Each time you enter the original product on an order, the related products may appear on the order depending on how you set it up. You can delete or edit the related products/quantities as necessary.
•
Updating Usage for Another Product. If you have a part number for which similar products can be used to fill an order, you may want usage for all of the similar products to update just one product number. When the similar product is sold, the usage product is updated with usage. For more information, refer to Product Usage.
Refer to Related Products for information on setting up related products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Related Products
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Usage Product#
The product number whose usage you want to update each time the original part number is entered on a sales order, work order, or transfer. Usage for the original part number will not be updated. When you move product usage, usage for the last 12 months is moved. If you remove this product number, the usage goes back to the original part number. This field is independent of the remaining fields.
Description
The description of the Usage Product#.
Related Product#
The product to automatically add each time the original product is entered on a sales order, work order, or transfer.
Description
The description of the Related Product#.
Type
Indicates how to add the related product when the original product is entered on a sales order. Choose from the following:
•
D - Default. The related product automatically appears on the sales order. Only one related product is allowed. Multiplier and Price are not accessible.
•
O - Optional. A list of all available related products display. The salesperson can select the related products and quantities to add to the order.
•
R - Required. The related products automatically appear on the sales order. You can add one or more related products and determine how many of each product to add to the order when the original product is entered on a sales order.
Multiplier
Determines the quantity of the related product to add to the order when the original product is entered on an order. For example, if the multiplier is 2 and you enter a quantity of 3 of the original product on a sales order; 6 of the related product will be added to the order.
Price
The price for the related product when it is sold with the original product.
F2-Save
Saves the related product information.
