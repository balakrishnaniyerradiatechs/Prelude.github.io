Product Characteristics
Product characteristics define special ordering requirements for a product. When you sell an item containing product characteristics, you are required to enter the characteristic information necessary to fill the order. These characteristics are then stored for the next time this customer buys this product.
You can set up product characteristics codes in Product Characteristics Codes Maintenance and then add them to a product in Product Maintenance. Refer to the following instructions on setting up product characteristics:
