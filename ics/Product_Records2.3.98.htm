Setting Up Rationing Comments
You can enter standard ration comments in Ration Comment Maintenance to display when a customer exceeds their ration quantity in a cycle. These comments print for all customers and all customer ration types when a ration quantity is exceeded.
To enter comments for exceeded customer rations:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Rationing > Ration General Comment Maintenance to open the Ration Comment Maintenance screen.
2
Enter any Comments to display when customers exceed their ration quantity for the ration cycle. Refer to Setting Up Customer Ration Types.
3
Press F2-Save to save the ration comments.
