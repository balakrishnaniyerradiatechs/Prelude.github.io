Receiving License Plates by Seal
If you receive shipping notices from your vendor through an EDI (electronic data interface), you can receive the shipped material into ASN.RECEIVE (advance shipping notice receipts) inventory when you are notified by the vendor that the merchandise shipped. A seal number identifies all the purchase orders listed for a license plate. When you receive the seal in Batch Stock Receipts by Seal, the license plate products do not appear in inventory until you update the batch (refer to Updating Batch Stock Receipts).
