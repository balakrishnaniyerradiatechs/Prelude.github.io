Vendor On-Time Receipts/Quality Report
The Vendor On-Time Receipts/Quality Report allows you to view the shipping performance for a vendor, including late and rejected shipments. The report groups the shipments by on-time or by the number of days late, (e.g., 1-5 days late, 6-10 days late, 11+ days late).
If a purchase order is received after the promised date (or delivery date if no promised date exists), the order is considered late, which affects on-time percentage. If the receiver is rejected, the quality percentage is affected.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Vendor On-Time Receipts/Quality Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Period. The fiscal period or the month to include on the report. Leave blank if you want to select the report by Receipt Date Range.
•
Receipt Date Range. If the Fiscal Period is left blank, enter the beginning and ending product receipt date range to include in the report.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Vendor. The vendor for which you want to view shipping performance. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Cutoff Day One. The first cutoff date for late shipments. For example, enter 5 to display a column for shipments that are 1-5 days late.
•
Cutoff Day Two. The second cutoff date for late shipments. For example, enter 10 to display a column for shipments that are 6-10 days late.
•
Detail or Summary. Determines whether to print a detail report or a summary report. Choose from the following:
-
D - Displays purchase order and product information for items that shipped late or incorrectly.
-
S - Only displays the number of late or rejected shipments for each vendor.
•
Suppress Warehouse Breaks. Determines whether to print a separate page for each warehouse:
-
Y - Do not break between warehouses.
-
N - Print a separate page for each warehouse.
•
Include P/O Types. The purchase order types to include in the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
On Time
The number of shipments shipped on-time for the vendor and time period.
Late 1-5
The number of shipments considered late according to the first cutoff date.
Late 6-10
The number of shipments considered late according to the 2nd cutoff date.
Late 11+
The number of shipments received after the 2nd cutoff date.
Total Recvd
The total count of purchase orders received for the vendor and the time period.
%On Time
The percentage of shipments for this vendor received on-time (On Time divided by Total Recvd).
Total #Accept
The total count of purchase orders received that had no rejections.
Reject After Stk Receipt
The number of purchase orders containing items rejected in Late Rejections Entry. Refer to Rejecting Inventory After it is Received.
Total Quality%
The percentage of shipments that shipped correctly (Total #Accept divided by Total Recvd).
If you chose the Detail option, the following columns also display for late items
PO#
The receiver number containing the late items.
Product #
The product number that shipped late.
Qty Ord
The quantity originally ordered on the purchase order.
Qty Rec
The quantity received late on this receiver.
Rec Date
The date the product was received.
Promised
The date the product was promised (or delivery date if no promised date exists on the purchase order header).
