Usage Summary Screen
The Usage Summary screen displays the total usage of sales orders, work orders, and transfers for the last three years. You can also view forecast information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Usage Maintenance > F6-Summary
Statistics Sequence
Reverse Statistics Sequence (Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info) determines the order to display statistics screens:
•
Y - Usage and statistics screens appear in reverse order (December to January) for each calendar year.
•
N - Usage and statistics screens appear in regular order (January - December).
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number and description.

The forecast quantity of the item from the previous year.
P2Usage
The usage of the product for the year before last, including sales orders, work orders, and transfers.
