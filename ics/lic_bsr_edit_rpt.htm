License Plate Batch Edit Report
The License Plate Batch Edit Report displays the purchase orders, part numbers, and license plate numbers received on a seal number (refer to Receiving Stock For a Seal Number). After you verify the information on this report, you must update the batch to move the items into inventory. Refer to Updating Batch Stock Receipts.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts > License Plate Batch Edit Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Batch Number. The bill of lading number from the advance shipping notice.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Only Lines with Qty Rec’d?. Indicate if you only want to display lines that have a received quantity:
-
Y - Show only lines with received quantities.
-
N - Show all lines in the batch.
•
Sort By Vend PN or Stock PN?. Determine how products are sorted:
-
Y - Sort items on each receipt by product number.
-
V - Sort items on each receipt by vendor product number.
•
Only Prod Still in Dock Bin?. Determine if you want to only include products received in the ASN (advance shipping notice) dock bin:
-
Y - Only include items with a quantity received into the ASN bin.
-
N - Include all items with a received quantity.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Batch Number
The bill of lading number assigned to the purchase order from the advance shipping notice.
Seal#
The seal number assigned to the purchase order from the advanced shipping notice.
Po#
The purchase order numbers included in the batch.
Vend#/Name/ License#
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Line 3: The license plate number.
Product/Description/
Serial#
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description, if applicable.
Line 4: The serial number, if applicable.
whse
The warehouse where the material was received.
Qty-Ord
The original quantity ordered on the purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity still open before this receiver was entered.
Qty-Rec
Line 1: The quantity received in this batch.
Line 2: The bin number, if applicable.
Line 3: The bin quantity, if applicable.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered. The quantity open after this receiver was entered.
Gross Price
The gross price of the item before any discounts.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the purchase order.
Disc%
The discount received from the vendor for this item.
Extension
The extended dollar amount of the quantity received.
