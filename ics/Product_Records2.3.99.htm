Entering Catalog Page Numbers
You can enter the catalog page number one product at a time in the Catalog Information Screen, or you can enter multiple products and page numbers at one time in Catalog Page Utility Maintenance. The Product Maintenance Audit Reports are updated with the product changes.
To enter a mass update of catalog page numbers:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Catalog Page Utility Maintenance to open the Catalog Page Utility Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Catalog# and the Catalog Page to update.
3
For Product, enter all products that appear on the Catalog Page.
4
Press F2-Save.
