Replacing and Merging Products
The Universal Product Replacement and Merge procedures allow you to:
•
Change a product number for all occurrences of the product within the system.
•
Merge product information (e.g., usage, pricing) from one product number to another.
Only standard files and fields that contain the product number are updated. A modification is required if custom fields must be updated also.
To replace and merge products, refer to the following sections:
•
Replace a Product Universally.
•
Merge Products.
•
Universal Product Replacement Preview Report.
•

•
