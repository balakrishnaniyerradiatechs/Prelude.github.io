Competitor Price Inquiry
The Competitor Price Inquiry allows you to compare your product number and price to a competitor’s product number and price. This information is set up in Competitor Price Maintenance. Refer to Maintaining Competitor Prices & Part Numbers.
Viewing a Competitor’s Prices
To view a competitor’s product number and price:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Miscellaneous Inquiries > Competitor Price Inquiry to open the Competitor Price Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product for which you want to display competitor information.
3
Enter the Code Number for the competitor as set up in Competitor Code Maintenance (refer to Setting Up Competitor Codes).
The competitor product information displays.
You can also view this inquiry in the Sales Order Entry and Quote Order Entry in the Line Item Detail screen (Action > Inquiries > Competitor Pricing).
 
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
Your part number.
Code Number
The competitor code number established in Competitor Code Maintenance (refer to Setting Up Competitor Codes).
Competitor Product
The competitor’s part number which is an alternate keyword for your part number.
Description
The description of the competitor’s product.
Product Match
Indicates how close of a match the competitor’s product is to your product:
•
E - Exact match. The products are exactly the same
•
P - Private label match. The products are the same but have different manufacturer labels.
•
S - Similar match. The products are similar but not exactly the same.
Retail Price
The competitor’s retail price for the product.
Good Price
The competitor’s best price for the product.
Unit of Measure
The unit of measure for the product.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
