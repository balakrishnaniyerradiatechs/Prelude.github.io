Transfer Shipments Entry screen
The Transfer Shipments Entry screen allows you to confirm shipped quantities on a transfer and to update inventory for the shipping warehouse.
Refer to Shipping Stock Transfers for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Shipments Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer Release#
The transfer release number from the pick ticket.
Shipment Date
The date the material left the warehouse.
From
The ship-from or shipping warehouse.
To
The ship-to or receiving warehouse.
Product
The part number shipped.
Description
The product description.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for this line item.
Linkage D
The document number for the destination of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this sales order number.
•
T - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this transfer number.
•
W - When the product arrives, it will be allocated to this work order.
Linkage S
The document number of the source of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - The product was purchased on this purchase order number at the shipping warehouse.
•
T - The product was ordered on this transfer number at the shipping warehouse.
Quantity
The quantity the shipping warehouse shows as available to ship.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the transfer.
Qty-Shp
The verified shipped quantity.
F2-Save
Saves the information on the Transfer Shipments Entry screen.
F4-Del
Deletes the transfer shipment entry.
F5-Header
Opens the Stock Transfer Header screen to allow you to enter or edit shipping information.
F7-Wty Tag#
Only accessible in the Transfer Entry screen.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, reference number, or page number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Allows you to view cost and source information.
Review
Opens the Transfer Review screen to display pick, backorder, and cost information for the line items on the transfer.
Notes
Allows you to enter header or line item notes for the transfer.
