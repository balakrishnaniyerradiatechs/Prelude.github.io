Printing a Bill Of Lading
If your carrier requires a bill of lading to ship a transfer, you can batch print it manually in Bill of Lading Printing.
To print a bill of lading:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Bill of Lading Printing.
2
For From Warehouse Number, enter the shipping warehouse.
3
For To Warehouse Number, enter the receiving warehouse.
4
For Transfer Number, enter the transfer release numbers to include on the bill of lading.
5
For Printer Form Queue, enter the printer number.
6
Once you choose Execute. The following prompt appears:
Load Bill Of Lading Forms In Printer & Align Visually
7
Choose from the following:
•
Print Bill of Lading: Prints the bills of lading.
•
Mask Print Test: Tests the alignment of the bill of lading forms on the printer (not necessary if using a laser printer). After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Bill of Lading.
•
Abort Printing: Cancels the print job.
8
After you select Print Bill Of Lading, the following prompt appears:
Bill of Lading Printing Complete
Choose one of the following:
•
Process: Updates the Transfer Log Inquiry if all bills of lading printed successfully.
•
Reprint All: Reprints all bills of lading if none printed successfully.
•
Single Reprint: Allows you to reprint one or more bills of lading individually.
•
Abort: Cancels the print process.
