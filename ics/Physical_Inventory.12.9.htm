Creating an Inventory List by Range
To build a physical inventory list using a combination of bin location range, product line range, product number range, and/or vendor number range:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Sheet Procedure > Build Physical Inventory Lists > By Range to open the Physical Inventory List By Range screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for the inventory list.
3
For Physical ListName, enter a name to identify the count sheet. You will use this name to print count sheets, enter counts, update inventory, and run inventory reports.
4
For Beginning Bin and Ending Bin, enter a range of primary bins to include on the count sheet or leave blank to include all bin locations.
5
For Beginning Pline and Ending Pline, enter a range of product lines to include on the count sheet or leave blank to include all product lines.
6
For Beginning Vendor and Ending Vendor, enter a range of primary vendors to include on the count sheet or leave blank to include all vendors.
7
For Starting Product and Ending Product, enter an exact range of products to include on the count sheet or leave blank to include all products.
8
Press F2-Create List. The following prompt appears:
## Items Selected. Press Return.
9
Press ENTER to create the lists for the selected product lines.
To view the list, refer to Displaying Physical Inventory Lists.
