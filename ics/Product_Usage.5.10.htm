Tracking Average Weekly Usage
If you stock products that have an extremely high sales volume for just a few months of the year, you can use the AWU (average weekly usage) formula to calculate recommended purchase quantities.
To use the AWU formula to calculate weekly usage you must:
1
Determine which products and warehouses to set up for weekly usage calculations. Refer to Establishing the Weekly Usage Calculation Hierarchy.
2
Calculate new recommended purchase quantities. Refer to Calculating Weekly Usage.
3
Update weekly usage calculations by running Nightly Processing.
4
Review and update product classifications in the End of Month Calendar Close. For more information on product classifications, refer to Classifying Products.
