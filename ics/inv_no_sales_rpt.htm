Onhand With No Sales Report
The Onhand With No Sales report displays products that have a quantity on-hand but no sales for the period of time that you define. This report helps to identify dead stock (refer to Classifying Products for information on dead stock).
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Sales Status Reports > Onhand w/No Sales
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Stocking Status Code: The stock status codes to include on the report. Leave blank to include all stock status codes or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
# Of Months For Analysis. The number of months of usage to evaluate (no more than 36 months). If a product has fewer than this number of months usage, it will not appear on the report.
•
Sort Option. Determines how to sort the report. Choose from the following:
-
P - Sort by product.
-
V - Sort by vendor.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Primary Vendor. The vendor(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Line 3: The model number, if a product has a make/model/year defined.
On-Hand
The quantity of the product in on-hand inventory.
Qpk
The quantity of the product committed to open sales orders.
Qprst
The quantity of the product committed, but not yet shipped, on outgoing transfers.
Qpr
The quantity of the product remaining on open purchase orders.
Qprt
The quantity of the product not yet received on incoming transfers.
Qoh$Value
The total extended value of the quantity on-hand (On-Hand x Average cost).
#Months
The number of months since the last sale. The ** indicates that the product has not been sold since it was created.
Date-Est
The date the product was established for this warehouse.
Bin:
The bin number if bins are assigned to products.
