Surcharge Products screen
The Surcharge Products screen allows you to assign a surcharge to add to an invoice every time this item (the calling product) is sold. Refer to Surcharge Products for information on setting up the surcharge product and the calling product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Surcharge Product
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Surcharge Product
The surcharge product number to add any time this product (the calling product) is entered on a sales order or quote.
Description
The description of the surcharge product.
Description 2
The second line of description of the surcharge product, if applicable.
Srchg Amt
The dollar amount to add to the order when the surcharge product is invoiced. If you entered a Multiplier for the surcharge product, you can leave this field blank (refer to Setting Up the Surcharge Product).
Qty
The minimum quantity of the calling product that the customer must buy to prevent from being billed the surcharge.
For example:
•
If you enter 10 for Qty, any sales order that contains a quantity of 10 or higher of this product has no surcharge. If you enter a quantity of less than 10 on a sales order, a dialog box displays to let you know that a surcharge will be added. You can choose to accept or remove the surcharge.
•
If you leave Qty blank, all orders with this calling product automatically add a surcharge.
F2-Save
Saves the surcharge product information.
