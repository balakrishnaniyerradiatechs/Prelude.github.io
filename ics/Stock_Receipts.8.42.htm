OTW Receiving Using a Purchase Order
To use the purchase order method to receive on-the-water merchandise:
1
Set the maintenance requirements for OTW receiving (refer to Requirements for OTW Receiving).
2
Enter a regular purchase order to the overseas vendor.
3
When the overseas vendor notifies you that material is in transit, create an OTW purchase order to hold the shipped material in an On The Water bucket until you receive it into your warehouse. The new OTW purchase order can contain products from one or more purchase orders shipped at one time (refer to Creating an On-The-Water Purchase Order).
4
When the material arrives, receive the OTW purchase order (refer to Receiving Stock) to move products from the On The Water bucket into inventory. The importing fees associated with the shipment (e.g., consolidation fees, ocean freight, brokerage fees, duty, marine insurance) are rolled into the average cost of each product, even if you pay these fees separately from the vendor’s invoice.
