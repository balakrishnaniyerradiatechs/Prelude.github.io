Rebuilding Product Classifications
To rebuild product classifications manually:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Rebuild Product Classifications to open the Product Classification Rebuild Procedure screen.
2
For Enter Calendar Month-End Closing Date, enter the last month-end date. For example, if today is November 10, enter 10/31/05.
3
For Is This The First Execution For This Month, choose from the following:
•
Y - If this is the first time you are running the rebuild this month.
•
N - If you already rebuilt classifications this month. The previously built calculations are overridden.
The product classifications are rebuilt.
Inventory Month End Calendar Close
To rebuild product classifications as part of the end of month procedures, refer to End of Month Calendar Close.
