Entering MSDS Numbers
When material data safety sheets (MSDS) are received from manufacturers, you must enter the MSDS information into the system before you can assign MSDS numbers to products.
MSDS Numbers Listing
To obtain a list of MSDS numbers set up in Material Safety Date Sheet Maintenance, select Inventory > Listings > Miscellaneous Listing > MSDS Numbers.
To enter MSDS information:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > MSDS Numbers to open the Material Safety Data Sheet Maintenance screen.
2
For MSDS Number, enter the material safety data sheet number from the manufacturer.
3
For Description One, enter a description of the MSDS.
4
For Description Two, enter a second more detailed description of the MSDS, if necessary.
5
Enter the Last Revision Date, which is the last time the MSDS was revised. Customers who purchased the product with the MSDS before this date will receive a new MSDS with orders after this date. The revision date prints on the pick ticket for reference.
6
If applicable, enter the Revision Number for the MSDS. This number prints on the pick ticket for reference.
7
Enter the Bin where the MSDS number is stored. The bin number prints on the pick ticket for reference.
8
For Hazardous Matl, indicate if the MSDS is for hazardous material. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The MSDS refers to hazardous material.
•
N - The MSDS does not refer to hazardous material.
9
For Flammable Matl, indicate if the MSDS is for flammable material. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The MSDS refers to flammable material.
•
N - The MSDS does not refer to flammable material.
10
For Combustible Matl, indicate if the MSDS is for combustible material. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The MSDS refers to combustible material.
•
N - The MSDS does not refer to combustible material.
11
For Vendor Name, enter the name of the vendor/manufacturer who originally produced the material.
12
For MSDS Sheet Imaged, enter the path to the MSDS image scanned into the system. You must enter individual images for each MSDS page. For example, if the MSDS is 3 pages, you must scan each page of the MSDS separately so each page is stored as its own image. You can enter an unlimited number of image paths. The MSDS images print on the pick ticket.
Licensing Note: MSDS images are only available if you have licensed the Imaging module and the latest version of Optio through Activant.
13
Press F2-Save to save the MSDS information.
Refer to Assigning MSDS Numbers to Products for information on entering MSDS numbers in Product Maintenance.
