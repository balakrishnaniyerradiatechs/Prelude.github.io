Product Family Codes
Product family codes allow you to group products for purchasing purposes. When one product in the group needs replenishment, you may want to view all the products in the group to determine what else needs to be ordered to meet any minimum ordering requirements.
Related Report
Description
Family Replenishment Report (Purchasing module).
Lists all products in a family when one of the products reaches its order point
To set up product family codes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Product Family Codes to open the Product Family Code Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Family Code, which can be up to 20 alpha-numeric characters.
3
Enter a Description of the family code.
4
Press F2-Save to save the family code information.
To assign a family code to a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Press F5 > Add Info > F5 Add Info to open the Additional Product Maintenance Information Screen.
4
Enter the family code in Family Name.
5
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the family code information.
