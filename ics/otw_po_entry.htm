OTW P/O# Entry screen
The OTW P/O# Entry screen allows you to enter open purchase order numbers from an overseas vendor to create a new OTW purchase order number.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > On the Water Maintenance > F6-Enter P/Os
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O#
The open purchase order number to include on the OTW purchase order.
P/O Date
The date the purchase order was entered.
Amount
The dollar amount of the purchase order.
F2-Save
Saves the purchase order information.
