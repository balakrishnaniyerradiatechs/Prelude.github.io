Rejecting Stock
If an item arrives in your warehouse that is damaged, shipped in error, over-shipped, or for any other reason must be returned to the vendor, you can reject the item in Stock Receipts Entry. You must first set up Rejection Claim Reason Codes to identify the reasons for return.
