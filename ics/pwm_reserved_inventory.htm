Reserved Inventory Screen
The Reserved Inventory Screen allows you to reserve a quantity of a product for a customer. The reserved quantity reduces the available quantity of the product and cannot be allocated to another customer. Refer to Reserving Inventory for a Customer.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Optns > Reserved Inventory
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
RCust#
The number of the customer for whom the inventory is reserved.
Description
The customer name.
Quantity
The quantity of inventory to commit to this customer.
F2-Save
Saves the reserved inventory information.
