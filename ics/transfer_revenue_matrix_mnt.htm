Transfer Revenue Matrix Maintenance screen
The Transfer Revenue Matrix creates a markup matrix to allow you to earn a profit on transfers shipped to other branches. You can define markups at either the warehouse or the warehouse/product line level for any from/to warehouse/product line combination. The calculated markup cost does not display in Transfer Entry, Transfer Shipments Entry, or in Transfer Receipts Entry.
Refer to Generating Revenue on a Stock Transfer for more information.
General Ledger Table Maintenance
Two General Ledger table accounts handle revenue markup. These accounts post to the shipping warehouse when the transfer is received. The difference between the two accounts is the markup profit for shipping to other warehouses.
•
Transfer Cost Of Goods: Debited in the shipping location when a Transfer Revenue Matrix exists between warehouses and a product is transferred between the two locations.
•
Transfer Revenue: Credited in the shipping location when a Transfer Revenue Matrix exists between warehouses and a product is transferred between the two locations.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Transfer Revenue Matrix
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
From Warehouse
The number for the warehouse shipping the material and charging the markup cost.
To Warehouse
The number for the warehouse receiving the material and paying the markup charge.
Product Line
The product line that the markup applies to if you want to limit the markup to a product line. Leave blank to use the markup for all products transferred between the warehouses.
Basis Field
The cost basis to calculate the markup cost:
•
R - Replacement cost.
•
S - Standard cost.
•
A - Average cost.
•
V - Average cost from the To Warehouse.
•
Blank -The difference between the basis cost and the accounting cost on the transaction.
Markup Rate
The rate to calculate markup. For example, enter 4 to charge a 4% markup when transferring stock for each item shipped for the warehouse/product line combination.
Post to COGS
Determines where the transfer revenue is posted:
•
Y - Post to inventory.
•
N - Post to the Expense Account number entered below.
Expense Account
Div
The division number for this account. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number for this account, if applicable. A region is a subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
Loc
The location or warehouse for this account.
Dep
The department number for this account. A department is a subset of warehouse, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center number for this account. A cost center is a subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account#
The general ledger account number to post to.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
F2-Save
Saves the markup calculation information.
F4-Del
Deletes the markup calculation information.
