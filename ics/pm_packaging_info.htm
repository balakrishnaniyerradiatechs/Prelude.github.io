Packaging Information Screen
The Packaging Information screen allows you to enter quantity, weight, and cube information for transactions that contain package quantities of the product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Package Information
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quantity
The quantity included for each of the pack types listed.
Weight
The weight for each of the pack types listed. This field is updated when a product is received in stock receipts entry. You can set Weight Required in Inventory Parameters > F5-Add info to Y to require a weight when a product is received.
Cube
The volume for each of the pack types listed.
Single Pack
A single unit of the product.
Inner Pack
The break-down of a case pack. For example; you normally ship a product in quantities of 24, but you can break it down to an inner pack quantity of 6.
Case Pack
A case of the product.
Pallet Pack
A pallet of the product.
F2-Save
Saves the packaging information.
