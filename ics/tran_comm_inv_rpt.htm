Transfer Committed Inventory Report
The Transfer Committed Inventory report is used with Transfer Bin Allocation in the Warehousing module. You can print the report before you allocate the quantities to bins so that you can view the total quantity and weight of products committed to transfers.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movements Reports > Transfer Committed Inventory Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
From Warehouse. The warehouse shipping the material.
•
To Warehouse. The warehouse receiving the material.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
To Warehouse
The warehouse that will receive the material.
# Lines
The number of lines entered on stock transfers.
Pck-Qty
The total quantity of products on stock transfers that are available to pick.
Weight
The total weight of the products entered on the stock transfers.
