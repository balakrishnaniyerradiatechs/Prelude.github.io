Accessory Products
Accessory products are products that you can sell to go with another product. When you sell an item that has accessory products, you can view the list of accessories and select the ones you want to add to the order. Once accessory products are assigned to a product, any time you enter the product on a transaction or in an inquiry, ACC displays to let you know that accessories are available. If accessory groups are set up (refer to Accessory Groups), you can assign an accessory group so that an entire list of products display any time the original product is entered on a sales order.
Product Accessory Inquiry
To view a list of accessory products that exist for a product, select Inventory > Inquiries > Product Inquiries > Product Accessory Inquiry.
To enter accessory items for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product for setting up accessory products.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Accessories to open the Accessory Information screen.
5
For Accessory Product#, enter a valid part number that is an accessory for the product.
6
For Script, indicate if you want to enter a promotional message to display when the product is sold:
•
Y - Opens the General Notes Entry screen to allow you to enter Product Script. This could be a sales pitch that you want the sales person to give to help add accessory items to the order.
•
N - No script is included for this product.
7
For Accessory Group, enter an accessory group code from Accessory Group Maintenance. When you enter the product on an order, all products in the accessory group display to allow you to select which items to add to the order.
Once you enter an Accessory Group, you can press F5-Group to open the Accessory Group Maintenance screen. Refer to Accessory Groups.
8
Press F2-Save to save the accessory product information.
