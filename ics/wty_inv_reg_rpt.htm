Wty Inventory Register Report
The Warranty Inventory Register report displays the last transaction status code and related document information for every item in warranty inventory. Once the vendor claim for the warranty tag is reconciled with the vendor credit memo or invoice, it no longer appears on this report.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Vendor Claim Reports > Wty Inventory Register Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Current Whse. The warehouse with the open warranty tags.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Tag Type. Determines if the material was returned to the vendor or scrapped. Leave blank to include both or choose from the following:
-
R - Returned to the vendor.
-
S - Scrapped.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Tag Number
The warranty tag number assigned to the product. Refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory.
T
The tag type. Indicates if the product will be returned to the vendor or scrapped:
•
R - Returned.
•
S - Scrapped.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Doc Number
The document number associated with the tag. The numerical prefix indicates how the product was put into warranty inventory:
•
R - The stock receiver number if the product was rejected in receiving. Refer to Creating Warranty Tags in Stock Receipts Entry.
•
A - The adjustment number if the product was adjusted into warranty inventory. Refer to Creating Warranty Tags in Inventory Adjustment Entry.
•
T - The transfer number if the warranty item was received on a type 03 Warranty transfer from another warehouse for return to the vendor.
•
S - The credit memo number if the tag was created when the customer was issued credit for the returned product. Refer to Creating Warranty Tags in Credit Memo Entry.
Doc Date
The date the warranty tag was created.
Status
The status code that represents the last transaction that occurred for the warranty tag:
•
AUPD - The credit memo to the customer was invoiced.
•
CLM - A vendor claim was entered for the product.
•
IAE - Inventory was adjusted to move the product from regular inventory to warranty inventory.
•
R - The product was rejected in receiving.
•
SOE - A credit memo was entered for the customer.
•
TE -The product was entered on a stock transfer.
•
TR - The product was received on a stock transfer.
•
TRR - Inventory was adjusted to move the product from warranty inventory to regular inventory.
•
TS - The product was shipped on a stock transfer.
•
VCP - The vendor claim was updated.
•
WTR - A warranty replacement was entered for the product.
Cost
The cost of the product when the warranty tag was created.
Quantity
The quantity on this tag.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Extension
The total extended cost of the quantity in warranty inventory (Cost x Quantity).
