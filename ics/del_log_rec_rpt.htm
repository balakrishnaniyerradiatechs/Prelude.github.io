Delivery Log Receipts Report
The Delivery Log Receipts Report shows estimated delivery information for purchase orders or transfers (refer to Scheduling Deliveries).
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Delivery Log Receipts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Estimate Date Range. The beginning and ending estimated receipts dates.
•
Status. The status of the delivery:
-
A - Arrived.
-
C - Cancelled.
-
L - Late.
-
R - Received.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include.
•
Carrier. The shipping methods to include.
•
Contact. The purchasing contacts to include.
•
Document Type. The document types to include.
-
P - Purchase order.
-
T - Transfer.
-
M - Miscellaneous delivery.
•
Document Number. The purchase order or transfer numbers to include.
•
Appointment number. The appointment numbers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Est-date
The estimated receipt date.
Time
The estimated receipt time.
Product
The product number
Description
The product description.
Bin Number
The bin number assigned to the product.
Onhand
The quantity of the product on-hand in the warehouse.
Quantity
The quantity of the product on-order.
Cube
The cubic space required for the product.
Plt
The pallet quantity
Case
The case quantity of the product.
