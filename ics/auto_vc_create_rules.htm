Generating Vendor Claims from Open Warranty Tags
You can generate vendor claims for products in warranty inventory based on the return rules established in Vendor Maintenance (refer to Setting Flags for Vendor Claims & Warranty Inventory). The vendor claims automatically transmit to the buyer queue for review. Refer to “Vendor Claims” in the Buyer Queue Inquiry in the Purchasing documentation for more information.
The buyer can review, edit, and transmit the claims to the vendors when necessary. The claims remain in the queue until they are updated to remove the quantity from inventory. Refer to Updating Vendor Claims.
To generate vendor claims from open warranty tags:
1
Select Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claims Creation From Tags - Rules Based.
2
Enter the Warehouse or leave blank to include all warehouses.
3
Enter the Vendor or leave blank to include all vendors.
Vendor claims automatically generate and transmit to the buyer queue for review.
