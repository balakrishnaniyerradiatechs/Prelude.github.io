Updating Physical Inventory Counts
After you enter and verify the physical inventory counts, you can update inventory with the new on-hand balances. The on-hand quantity is adjusted by the difference between the saved on-hand quantity and the physical count. If no count was entered, inventory is not adjusted.
To update the physical inventory counts into on-hand balances:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Sheet Procedure > Physical Update Procedure to open the Physical Inventory Update Procedure screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
Enter the Warehouse number to update.
3
Enter the Physical ListName to update. Leave blank to update all lists for the warehouse.
4
For Physical Post Date, enter the date that you want to record as the day the inventory counts are updated.
5
Press ENTER. The following message displays:
Warning! This Procedure Will Update Onhand And Shrinkage Values For The Inventory Counts Entered For Each Product. Do You Wish To Continue With Physical Inventory Update Procedure For Warehouse ## and All Physical Lists
6
Choose from the following:
•
No - Cancel the update.
•
Yes - Update the on-hand quantities. A general ledger entry is created for the value of each adjustment.
