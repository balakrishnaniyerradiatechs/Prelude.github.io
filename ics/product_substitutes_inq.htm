Product Substitutes Inquiry screen
The Product Substitutes Inquiry screen displays price and availability information for substitute products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Product Inquiries > Product Substitute Inquiry
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number for which you want to list substitutes.
Warehouse
The warehouse number.
Available
The available quantity of the Product Number.
Substitute Product
Substitute products that exist for the Product Number.
Sugg Retail
The suggested retail price of the substitute item.
Cust Price
The customer’s price, if you are in a sales order or quote.
Available
The quantity of the Substitute Product# available.
Surplus
The surplus quantity of the substitute product available.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen.
F5-Accept Substitute
If you are in a sales order, work order, or transfer when viewing the Product Substitutes Inquiry, you can use this key to place the substitute product on the order.
F6-Whse Avail
Opens the Product Warehouse Availability Inquiry screen to display available quantities in other warehouses.
F7-Equivalents
Opens the Equivalents screen to allow you to view equivalent items that exist for the product. Equivalents are products that can be used as a substitute product, that are not valid products in the system. If the equivalent later becomes a valid product, the equivalent shows as a substitute item the next time you open the Substitutes screen.
