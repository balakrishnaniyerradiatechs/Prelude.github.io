Company Vendor Cost Inquiry
The Company Vendor Cost Inquiry displays company vendor replacement cost information for a product/vendor combination.
Viewing Company Vendor Cost
To view company vendor replacement cost information:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Maintenance Inquiries > Product Inquiries > Company Vendor Cost Inquiry to open the Company Vendor Cost Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Enter the Vendor for which you want to display replacement cost.
Company vendor replacement cost information displays.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Weight
The weight of the product from this vendor.
Unit Cost
The product/vendor level replacement cost that applies only to purchases from this vendor.
This cost overrides the cost at the product level in the Product Pricing Screen and the product warehouse level in the Warehouse Pricing Screen.
Depending on how the Replacement Cost Update flag is set in Inventory Parameters, you can also change replacement cost in Purchase Order Entry and/or Stock Receipts Entry.
New Unit Cost
The future unit cost for the item, if one exists.
Effective Date
(unlabeled field)
The date the New Unit Cost becomes effective.
Direct Ship Cost
The direct ship cost for direct ship purchase orders.
Last-Upd
The last date this company vendor replacement cost was updated.
F5-Discount Matrix
Displays the current discount matrix for quantity vendor cost discounts. Refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices.
F6-Future Discount Matrix
Displays the future discount matrix for quantity vendor costs discounts. Refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices.
