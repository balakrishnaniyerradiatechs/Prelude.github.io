Assigning Lot Numbers When Receiving Stock
When you receive a lot product in Stock Receipts Entry, you must choose to receive by Line Item to enter lot number information. Depending on how the Lot Pack flag is set in Product Maintenance (refer to Setting Up a Lot Product), one of the following screens appears:
•
Lot Pack Confirmation screen. Refer to Receiving Lot Products Sold In Packs.
•
Lot Number Information screen. Refer to Receiving Lot Products Not Sold in Packs.
