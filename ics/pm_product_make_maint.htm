Product Make Maintenance Screen
The Product Make Maintenance screen allows you to enter make, model and year information to define which vehicles are compatible with the product. Refer to Maintaining Automotive Information for instructions on vehicle/product searches.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Configurator Information
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Make
The make of the car for which this product is valid.
Model
The car model that corresponds to the Make.
Year
The year for the make and model.
F2-Save
Saves the product make information.
