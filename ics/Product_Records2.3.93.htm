Setting Up Parker Distributor Numbers
Parker Distributor Maintenance is used to set up distributor numbers assigned by Parker Hannifin.
Distributor Number by Warehouse
You can enter a distributor number for an individual warehouse in Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > Parker Reporting Information.
To set up Parker distributor numbers:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Parker Reporting Codes > Parker Distributor Maintenance to open the Parker Distributor Maintenance screen.
2
For Parker Dist#, enter the number to represent your distributor.
3
For Description, enter the name of the distributor.
4
Enter the address information for the distributor.
5
Press F2-Save to save the Parker distributor information.
