Transfer Inquiry
The Transfer Inquiry displays open (not received) transfers for a warehouse. To view received transfers, refer to the Transfer History Inquiry.
Viewing Open Transfers
To view information on open transfers:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Transfer Inquiries > Transfer Inquiry to open the Transfer Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse number.
Open transfers for the warehouse display.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Warehouse
The warehouse number. The warehouse description displays to the right.
Transfer#
The transfer or transfer release number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist in either the header or the line item detail for the transfer.
From
The shipping warehouse.
To
The receiving warehouse.
W/B
The user ID of the person who entered the transfer.
Type
The transfer type:
•
01 - Regular order.
•
02 - Non-inventory.
•
03 - Warranty inventory.
•
04 - Crib replenishment.
•
05 - Ship line item complete.
Refer to Defining Transfer Types for complete descriptions of transfer types.
Ship Via
The code that indicates how the transfer shipped.
Ent-Date
The date the stock transfer was entered.
Shp-Date
The date the transfer shipped, if applicable.
Status
The status of the transfer:
•
PRINTED - The transfer pick ticket printed but the transfer has not yet shipped.
•
ON-ORDER - The transfer was entered and/or backordered but not printed.
•
IN-TRANSIT - The transfer is on its way to the receiving warehouse. The release was entered in Transfer Shipments Entry for the From warehouse, but is not yet received at the To warehouse.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Stock Transfer Display screen to allow you to view detail product and quantity information for the highlighted transfer.
F6-Enter T/R
Allows you to enter a transfer number to go directly to.
F8-Log
Opens the Transfer Log Inquiry to allow you to view all transactions for the highlighted transfer.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Allows you to view the previous page if multiple pages of transfers are listed.
sF6-Next Group
Allows you to view the next page if multiple pages of transfers are listed.
Action Bar
Output
Allows you to view, print, email, or fax the usage information.
