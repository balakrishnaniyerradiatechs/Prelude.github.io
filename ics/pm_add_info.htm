Additional Information Screen
The Additional Information screen within Product Maintenance allows access to flag settings, codes, and other information for the master product.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Lot Number Flag
Determines the following:
•
Y - The product requires standard lot control. The Lot Information Screen opens to allow you to enter lot, license plate, and reel information. When you receive the item, you must enter a lot number.
•
S - The product requires seed lot control. The Lot Information Screen opens to allow you to enter lot information. When you receive the item, after you enter the lot information, an additional screen appears to allow you to enter seed lot information (e.g., germination, hard seed, purity, crop).
•
N - The product is not a lot, license plate, seed, or reel item.
For more information, refer to Receiving Lot Number Products, Receiving License Plate Products, or Allocating Optimal Reel Quantities.
Available for Sale
Indicates if the item is available for sale:
•
Y - Available for sale.
•
N - Not available for sale (e.g., a product that you may purchase for in-house use, but do not sell to customers).
Serial# Flag
Indicates if this product is tracked by serial number:
•
Y - Tracked by serial number.
•
N - Not tracked by serial number.
Refer to Entering and Tracking Serial Numbers.
Detail
If you entered Y in Serial# Flag, you must indicate if detail serial number tracking is required for this product:
•
Y - Serial numbers are tracked in detail which means that you must enter a serial number any time the item is increased or decreased (e.g. received, shipped, transferred). When the product is sold, the pick ticket prints SER# in front of the serial number for each item to pick. If you choose this option, Track is automatically set to Y to track each serial number.
•
N - Serial numbers are required only when the product ships. This option only applies if you want to track serial numbers sold but not serial numbers in inventory.
Refer to Entering and Tracking Serial Numbers.
Track
If you entered N for Detail, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Serial numbers are required only when shipped. When you receive, adjust, or transfer the product, you can enter a serial number but it is not required.
•
N - Serial numbers are only required when shipped.
Option Group
The equipment option group established in the Equipment Option Group Maintenance screen. If you purchase equipment that comes with optional features, you can set up option codes to identify each feature. You can use option groups to identify the type of equipment (e.g., tractor, backhoe) that each option applies to. When you purchase the piece of equipment, you can add the available option codes. The codes are added as comments on the purchase order. When you receive the purchase order, you are prompted to confirm the equipment options and can change them as necessary. The option information is stored in the product serial number record. Refer to Using Equipment Option Codes.
Sum & Reprice Flag
Determines if quantity break levels apply to a group of items:
•
Y - Sum & reprice groups apply for this item. When the product is sold, the system totals the quantity sold of all items in the same Sum & Reprice group. The price quantity break that applies to the total quantity of the group is used for each item on the sales order.
•
N - Sum & reprice groups do not apply to this item.
Refer to “Sum and Reprice” in the Order Processing documentation.
Grp
If the Sum & Reprice Flag is set to Y, enter the Sum & Reprice group to which you want to assign the product.
Tbl
The quantity discount table code to use when products are grouped. If this field is blank, the product’s price break table is used. Refer to Setting Up Discount Break Tables.
Backorder Flag
Indicates if the product can go on backorder when stock runs out:
•
Y - The product can backorder.
•
N - The product cannot backorder.
Surcharge Flag
Indicates if this is a surcharge that is assigned to a calling product that requires a surcharge:
•
Y - The product is a surcharge: It is automatically added to a sales order whenever a calling product is entered on an order (refer to Surcharge Products for information on entering surcharge and a calling products). The Product Surcharge Add Info Maintenance screen opens to allow you to enter surcharge information.
•
N - The product is not a surcharge product.
A surcharge product is similar to a service item in that inventory is not affected but a surcharge can be backordered
Cost Only
Indicates if the item is a cost-only product with no selling price:
•
Y - The item is a cost only product and is not included in gross margin exception reports or zero price/cost exception reports. The zero cost and gross profit warnings are suppressed.
•
N - The product is not a cost-only product.
Price Tape
Indicates if this product can have its price and cost updated by a vendor -supplied price tape:
•
Y - The product can be updated by a price tape, if applicable.
•
N - The product cannot be updated by a price tape.
Refer to Updating Prices/Costs Using a Price Tape.
Nonreturnable Flag
Determines if the product can be returned:
•
Y - The product should not be returned. The message “This Product is Non-Returnable! Do you Wish to Continue?” displays when the product is entered on a credit memo, RGA, or as a negative entry on a sales order, but you are still allowed to enter the item.
•
D - Disallow returns. The message “This product is set to Disallow Returns” displays when the product is entered on a credit memo, RGA, or as a negative entry on a sales order and the product is not allowed to be entered.
•
N - The product can be returned. No message displays when the product is entered on a credit memo or RGA.
Vendor Returnable
Determines if the vendor allows you to return this product directly from stock:
•
Y - You can return this product directly from stock.
•
N - You cannot return this product directly from stock. If you enter the item in Vendor Claim Entry, a warning message displays. Refer to Vendor Claims & Warranty Replacements.
Warranty inventory and warranty returns are not affected by this flag.
Availability Type
Determines how the product is available for sale:
•
Y - Always available (e.g., catalogs). Pick the full quantity ordered on the sales order regardless of the available quantity. The description displays as IN STOCK.
•
N - Only pick the available quantity of the product in stock and backorder the remainder.
•
B - Always backorder the product. You must manually allocate the product (refer to Allocating Inventory Manually). This type does not involve picking and shipping.
For example, you can enter an order for a customer to project how may of an item they expect to purchase over a period of time. The description displays as BACKORDER.
•
D - Always backorder the product and default to a D supply code if no available inventory exists for the product. The description displays as DROP SHIP.
•
T - Never transfer. Manual transfers, Nightly Processing, and manual linkage are disallowed.
•
X - Never allow direct ships.
Equipment Flag
If this is a rental product, this flag determines how the product is tracked:
•
Y - The product is tracked as rental equipment and an asset record can be created (refer to the Rental Asset Information Screen). If the product is entered as a trade-in using a new serial number, an equipment record is created in Equipment Maintenance for the new serial number when the order is invoiced. Refer to Entering and Tracking Serial Numbers.
•
N - The product is not tracked as a piece of equipment.
Shelf Life Days
The number of days until the product expires. Leave blank if the product has unlimited shelf life. If the item is a lot product (Lot Number Flag set to Y), the default expiration date is the current system date plus the shelf life days. Refer to Receiving Lot Number Products.
Product Tax Group
The tax group code used to group products together for sales tax overrides. The Sales Tax Matrix groups products as taxable or non-taxable.
Prod Security Access
Allows you to control which products users can view. You must set Product Security Access to Y in Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add Info to use this feature.
You can create security codes in Security Access Code Maintenance (Systems menu > Miscellaneous Controls) and then assign the codes to a user in User Identification Maintenance > F6-Add Info > F5-Cust Sec. Each user only has access to products with a matching security code.
No Auto B/O Cancel
Determines if backorders are automatically cancelled:
•
Y - If the product goes on backorder, the backordered quantity is not cancelled.
•
N - If the product goes on backorder, the backordered quantity is automatically cancelled. You can override this setting on a sales order (Backorder Flag).
Qual Insp Required
Enter Y if the product requires inspection by a quality control inspector. Depending on how the Quality Inspection- AQl flag is set in Inventory Parameters, one of the following screens opens:
•
If you use the Quality Inspection Subsystem in the Order Processing module (Inventory Parameters > Quality Inspection - AQL flag set to Y), the Inspection Procedures screen opens to allow you to enter the type of inspection procedure required for the product. Refer to the Quality Inspection Subsystem documentation for more information.
•
If you use Quality Control Inspections for receiving in the Inventory module (Inventory Parameters > Quality Inspection - AQL flag set to N), the Inspection Level screen opens to allow you to enter the inspection level for this product. Refer to Requiring Quality Control Inspections in Receiving.
When you receive this item, the quality control inspector is notified to inspect the product. You can override this setting in Qual Insp Required in the Product Warehouse Maintenance Screen. Qual Insp Required overrides ISO Qualified in Vendor Maintenance.
Always Hold Order
Determines if an order containing this product is always placed on Administrative Order Hold and/or Administrative Quote Hold:
•
Y - If you use Admin Order Hold and/or Admin Quote hold, any sales orders or quotes containing this product automatically go on Administrative Order Hold and/or Administrative Quote Hold.
•
N - Sales orders or quotes containing this product are not automatically put on hold.
Refer to “Administrative Order Inquiries” in the Order Processing documentation for information on activating Administrative Order Hold and Administrative Quote hold and releasing orders from hold.
Recv Certification Req
Determines if the product requires a receiving certificate:
•
Y - A receiving certificate is required (informational only - for custom reporting).
•
N - A receiving certificate is not required.
Common Carrier Flag
Indicates if orders containing this product must have a Ship Via code that has the Common Carrier flag set to Y in Ship Via Maintenance:
•
Y - Orders containing this product must have a Ship Via code that represents a common carrier.
•
N - Orders containing this product do not have to have a Ship Via code that represents a common carrier.
Primary Seq Number
The primary sequence number that indicates the position of this product in the sort order on user-defined reports.
Second Seq Number
The secondary sequence number that indicates the position of this product in the sort order on user-defined reports.
Warranty Tag Type Def
The default warranty type to use for warranty returns in Inventory Adjustment Entry, Sales Order Entry, or Quote Order Entry. You can leave this blank to default to the Warranty Tag Type Default setting in Vendor Maintenance:
•
R - Return. If the item is usually returned to the vendor for credit or replacement when it is under warranty.
•
S - Scrapped. If the item is usually thrown away and you receive credit or a replacement if it is under warranty.
Condition Code
Determines if you can choose the condition of the product when you purchase it:
•
Y - The Condition Code Days Entry screen opens when this product is entered on a purchase order. You can enter information regarding the product condition (e.g., new, used, refurbished) and warranty information.
•
N - The product’s condition is not required.
W/O Mfg Production
Determines if work order usage is included in EOQ calculations for this product:
•
Y - Work order usage is not included.
•
N - Work order usage is included.
You can override this setting in the Product Warehouse Maintenance Screen.
Contact Prc/Delivery
Determines if a message appears to remind you to contact the vendor for price and delivery information when you enter this product on sales orders, quotes, or purchase orders:
•
Y - A message reminds you to ask the vendor for price and delivery information.
•
N - No message appears.
Bypass LowLevel GP Chk
Determines if the product is included in the manager gross profit % check for Admin Order Hold and Admin Quote Hold:
•
B - Bypass both the manager low and high-level gross profit tests.
•
H - Bypass the manager high-level gross profit test
•
Y - Bypass the manager low-level gross profit check
•
N or blank - Include the product in the gross profit check.
F2-Save
Saves the product information.
F5-Add Info
Opens the Additional Product Maintenance Information Screen to allow you to access and edit additional code and product information.
F6-MSDS/Permits
Opens the MSDS/Restricted Permits Information Screen to allow you to enter Material Safety Data Sheet Information for the product. Refer to Using Material Safety Data Sheets.
F7-Labels
Opens the Product Labels Information Screen to allow you to enter label printing information for the product.
F8-Freight
Opens the Freight Information Screen to allow you to enter freight class information for a product.
Action Bar
Incomp Ship Via
Opens the Incompatible Ship Vias Screen to allow you to enter incompatible shipping methods for this product number.
Sell Pk Dim
Opens the Selling Package Dimensions Screen to allow you to enter packing dimensions for the product to use when:
•
Length is required for base products.
•
The product is not compatible with a ship via code.
