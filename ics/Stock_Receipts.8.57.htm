Scheduling Deliveries
If you set up appointments for deliveries, you can use Delivery Log Entry to schedule and view delivery appointments for purchase orders and stock transfers.
Related Reports
Description
Delivery Log Appointment Report
Delivery Appointments entered in Delivery Log Entry
Delivery Log Receipts Report
Estimated delivery/receipt information for purchase orders or transfers.
To enter or view a delivery appointment:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts > Delivery Log Entry to open the Delivery Log Entry screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you would like to view or schedule delivery appointments.
3
Enter the Date for which you would like to view or schedule appointments.
4
Enter the Sort sequence of the delivery schedule. Choose from the following:
•
Received date/time.
•
Vendor name.
•
Appointment number.
•
Carrier.
5
For Filters, indicate if you want to use additional selection criteria to filter delivery appointments:
•
N - Do not use additional filters.
•
Y - Opens the Delivery Log Inquiry Filter Options screen to allow you to filter the appointments by date, vendor, carrier, contact, phone number, document type, document number, appointment number, or status.
6
Press ENTER. Scheduled deliveries for the date display.
7
Choose from the following:
•
F5-Review. Allows you to review detail information for a delivery appointment.
•
F6-New Entry. Opens the Delivery Log Entry screen to allow you to schedule a new Appointment. Continue with step 8.
8
For Document Type, choose from the following:
•
P - Purchase Order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
M - Miscellaneous delivery.
9
For Document #, enter the purchase order numbers or transfer numbers to schedule for delivery. If applicable, the associated Vendor displays.
10
Enter the Estimated Date and the Estimated Time for delivery.
11
Enter the Number/Cartons and/or the Number/Pallets to be delivered.
12
Enter the Carrier for the delivery. Use F3 to search for available carriers.
13
If applicable, enter the Contact and Phone number.
14
If necessary, enter a Priority sequence for the delivery. This field is informational only.
15
Enter the Status of the delivery. Choose from the following:
•
A - Arrived.
•
C - Cancelled.
•
L - Late.
•
R - Received.
16
If applicable, enter a Call Date, which is the date you should call to notify of a delivery.
The system assigns the next available Appointment number.
17
If applicable, enter any Comments regarding the delivery.
18
Press F2-Save to save the delivery information.
