Physical Inventory Linked Entry screen
The Physical Inventory Linked Entry screen allows you to adjust inventory for linked and non-stock items counted during the physical inventory. If the counted quantity is less than the quantity linked to orders, you must determine which orders to link to the available product quantity.
Menu Path: Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Linked Procedure > Physical Linked Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Document #
The document number that references the linkage adjustment.
Entered Date
The adjustment date to post to the general ledger.
Product
The product number to adjust.
Warehouse
The number for the warehouse that contains the linked product.
Onhand
The quantity of the product counted during the physical inventory.
Linked Qty
The quantity of the product linked to sales orders, work orders, and transfers.
Linked Value
The total value of the linked product. Onhand x Linked Qty.
Document#
The document number for the linked product. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Qty Opn
The quantity required for the corresponding document.
Qty Pck
The quantity available to pick for this order, according to the system.
Linked Qty
The quantity linked to the document.
Unit Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the linked document.
Ext Value
The extended value of the linked quantity (Linked Qty x Unit Cost).
Qty To Stk
Any amount above the linked quantity that is to go into stock.
Total
The total of the linked quantities, total extended value, and the total quantity to go to stock after the counts are adjusted.
General Ledger Impact
Inventory - Stock
The value updated to the general ledger for stock inventory, if you changed the Linked Qty.
Inventory - Indirect
The value updated to the general ledger for non-stock inventory, if you changed the Linked Qty.
Inventory - Physical
The value updated to the general ledger for your physical inventory count, if you changed the Linked Qty.
F2-Save
Saves the adjusted linked information.
