Selling Related Products With a Product
To set up related products to always be sold with another product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product that has related products.
3
Select F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Related Product to open the Related Product Information screen.
5
For Related Product#, enter the product that you want to add each time this product is entered on a sales order, work order, or transfer.
6
For Type, determine how to add the related product when the original product is entered on a sales order. Choose from the following:
•
D - Default. The related product automatically appears on the sales order. Only one related product is allowed. Multiplier and Price are not accessible.
•
O - Optional. A list of all available related products display. The salesperson can select the related products and quantities to add to the order.
•
R - Required. The related products automatically appear on the sales order. You can add one or more related products and determine how many of each product to add to the order when the original product is entered on a sales order.
7
Enter the Multiplier for the quantity of the related product to add to the order when the original product is entered on an order. For example, if the multiplier is 2 and you enter a quantity of 3 of the original product on a sales order; 6 of the related product will be added to the order.
8
Enter the Price that you want to use for the related product when it is sold with the original product.
9
Press F2-Save three times to save the related product information.
