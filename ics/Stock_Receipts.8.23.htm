Receiving Carousel Non-Nettable Products
A carousel is a mechanical device that moves rows of bins around so that the picker can remain in one spot while the carousel rotates. Carousels are commonly used in warehouses that have a large number of bins with small parts. When carousel products arrive in your warehouse, they may not be ready for sale until the products are sorted into their proper bins. You may still want to receive the products so that Accounts Payable can pay the vendor and so that the products show in on-hand inventory.
Warehouse Maintenance Flags
To activate carousel bins for a warehouse you must set the following flags in Warehouse Maintenance:
•
Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add Info > Carousel Interface = Y.
•
Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Additional Options > Bin Locator Information Multiple Bin Locations = N.
•
Warehouse Maintenance > F7-RTS Maint > F5-Warehousing > Warehousing Flag = N.
The Prelude carousel interface allows you to receive the products into non-nettable inventory and then move the items to a nettable bin when they become available and are ready for picking. A non-nettable product is part of on-hand inventory but is not available to sell until it is moved into a nettable bin.
To move inventory from non-nettable to available inventory:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Nonnettable File Update Entry to open the Nonnet File Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product to update.
3
Enter the Warehouse. All documents that received the product into a carousel non-nettable bin display.
4
Press F4-Del. The following prompt displays:
Are You Sure You Wish to Delete?
5
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Moves the quantity of the product from non-nettable to nettable status.
•
No - Leaves the product in non-nettable status.
