Entering Stock Transfers
Transfer Entry is the first step in transferring stock from one warehouse to another. The transfer designates the shipping warehouse and the receiving warehouse. Depending on company policy, you can enter the transfer from any warehouse.
You can also use Transfer Entry to create the transfers recommended by Nightly Processing. Refer to the Purchasing documentation for information on Nightly Processing and recommended stock transfers in the Buyer Queue Inquiry.
