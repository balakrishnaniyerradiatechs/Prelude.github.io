Entering a Future Transfer
If you want a stock transfer to ship sometime in the future, you can enter a future pick ticket print date. Nightly Processing evaluates the quantities on the pick ticket for future availability calculations to determine when to recommend a purchase or transfer.
To enter a future pick ticket date for a transfer:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry.
2
Follow the steps for Entering Stock Transfers.
3
When the Stock Transfer Header screen opens, enter the future Pick Ticket Date to indicate when you want the pick ticket to print.
4
Complete the transfer as usual. The pick ticket will not print until the indicated date.
The Future Availability Inquiry displays the transactions and future requirements for a product and calculates the future availability.
The Available to Promise Inquiry displays the future availability of a product including future transfers.
