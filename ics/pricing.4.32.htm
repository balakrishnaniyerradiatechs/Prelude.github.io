Updating Prices and Costs
The following methods of Price/Cost Update Procedures allow you to update groups of future and current prices and costs without having to enter each product in Product Maintenance or Product Warehouse Maintenance:
•
Updating Prices/Costs by List
Builds a list of products so you can update prices/costs at the product or warehouse level.
•
Updating Price/Cost Using a Percent Markup
Calculates new prices by percentage markup or a percentage of a price. Entire product lines, vendors, or price groups are updated at once.
•
Updating Prices/Costs Using a Price Tape
Updates prices/costs when you load a price tape, floppy, C.D., or spreadsheet. You can update products based on either the vendor’s part number or your part number.
Inventory Parameters
Disp Save Prev Pricing in Inventory Parameters determines if the “Save Previous Price?” prompt displays when you change a price. Choose from the following:
•
Y - You can choose to save the previous price when you change a price.
•
N - Previous Prices are not saved.
•
U - Previous Prices are automatically saved.
