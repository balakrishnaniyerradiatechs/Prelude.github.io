Monthly Stock Receipts Report
The Monthly Stock Receipts Report lists all of the merchandise received for a fiscal period. The report also displays product cost, extended cost, import costs, bin locations, and serial number information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Monthly Stock Receipts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Period. The fiscal period when the stock was received.
•
Receiver. The receiver number to limit the report to just one receiver. Leave blank to display all receivers for the month.
•
P/O Type. The purchase order type to limit the report to just one type. Leave blank to display receivers for all purchase order types for the month.
•
Vendor. The vendor number to limit the report to just one vendor. Leave blank to display receivers for all for all vendors for the month.
•
Totals Only?. Indicates if you want to print the detail product information or only the warehouse totals:
•
Y - Print warehouse and grand totals only.
•
N - Print detail stock receipt information for the month.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Received
The date the product was received.
P.O. #
The receiver number.
Vendor
The vendor number from the purchase order.
Product Number /
Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Additional lines: Serial and lot numbers.
BIN: The bin number for the received item.
QTY: The quantity put into each bin.
Qty-Rec
The total quantity of the product received on this receiver number. The sum of all the bin quantities.
Qty-Stk
The quantity to go into stock based on the stocking unit of measure. For example, if the quantity received was 10 and the stocking unit of measure is DZ (dozen), the quantity received is 10 and the quantity in stock is 120. Refer to Establishing a Unit of Measure for more information on unit of measure.
Net-Price
The net price of the item after discounts.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the purchase order.
Extension
The extended price of the item (Qty-Stk x Net-Price).
Duty
The duty amount charged for the item, if you use import cost factors.
Freight
The inbound freight charge for the item. Includes ocean freight, inland freight, and any import freight charges.
Brkrge
The brokerage charge for the item, if you use import cost factors
