Printing Shipment Contents on SSCC Labels
To set up your warehouse to print the contents of a transfer shipment on SSCC labels:
1
Select Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > Transfers to open the Transfer Information screen.
2
For Contents Tracking, enter Y to require SSCC detail contents’ tracking labels to print when shipping transfers in Transfer Serial Ship Container Entry. If you ship a transfer in Regular Transfer Shipment Entry, you are automatically brought into the Serial Shipping Container Inquiry screen so you can enter the detail contents of the shipment.
3
For Ucc Number, enter the UCC manufacturer number for the warehouse to use in the SSCC identification number, if it is different than the UCC Mfg Number defined at the company level.
UCC Number
The UCC Mfg Number is the Uniform Code Council manufacturer number assigned to your registered organization by the Uniform Code Council. This code is part of the SSCC identification number.
4
For Pc Label Pgm, enter the name of your custom program that prints SSCC labels in Transfer Serial Container Entry. The labels will include a detail listing of the contents of the shipment.
5
For Pc Label Execution, choose from the following to determine when the labels print:
•
E - Labels print after each transfer SSCC entry.
•
F - Labels print after all SSCCs for a transfer release number are entered.
6
Press F2-Save twice to save the SSCC label information.
