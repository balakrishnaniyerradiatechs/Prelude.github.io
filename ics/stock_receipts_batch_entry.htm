Stock Receipts Batch Entry screen
The Stock Receipts Batch Entry screen allows you to receive multiple purchase orders that are assigned to a batch number. After you receive stock for the batch, you can run the Batch Edit report (refer to Running the Batch Stock Receipts Edit Report) to confirm products and quantities before you update inventory.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The purchase order number to receive.
Receipt Date
The day the material entered the warehouse.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Received By
The user ID of the person who entered the receiver.
Product Number
The products on the purchase order.
Description
The description of each product.
Whse
The warehouse to receive the product.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist on the purchase order for this product.
Linkage
The document that the product is linked to. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Quantity
The quantity of the product open on the purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the purchase order.
Received
The quantity of the product received.
Weight
The total weight of the items on the purchase order. If the purchase order has been partially received, then this is the total weight of the items remaining on the purchase order.
Units
The total number of units on the purchase order. If the purchase order has been partially received, then this is the total number of units remaining on the purchase order.
P/O Amount
The total amount of the items on the purchase order. If the purchase order has been partially received, then this is the total amount remaining on the purchase order.
Recv Amount
The running total of the amount received on this receiver. The dollar amount increases as each item is received.
F2-Save
Saves the received information. Inventory is not updated until you update the batch. Refer to Updating Batch Stock Receipts.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen to allow you to view or edit the header information.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a page number, part number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays shipping and receiving information for the received product.
Review
Opens the Purchase Order Review screen to display the detail information for each line on the purchase order.
Recost
Allows you to enter a different cost for a line item if the cost changed after the purchase order was entered.
Sort
Allows you to resort the items on the purchase order:
•
D - Sort by product description.
•
P - Sort by product number.
•
V - Sort by vendor product number.
Buyer Hold
Allows you to put the stock receiver on Buyer Hold so that the receiver cannot be reconciled with the vendor invoice until the buyer reviews and releases it in the Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry. You can enter notes to the buyer to explain why you are putting the receiver on hold.
Notes
Allows you to enter line item or header notes regarding the receiver.
