Temporary Transfer Conversion screen
The Temporary Transfer Conversion screen allows you to convert a temporary transfer, generated by Nightly Processing, into a valid transfer. You can edit the quantities and part numbers as necessary. The recommended transfers appear on the Recommended Stock Transfer Report in the Purchasing Module.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry > F5-Convert Temp Transfer
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Temporary Transfer#
The temporary transfer number from the Recommended Stock Transfer Report generated by Nightly Processing.
From Warehouse
The shipping warehouse.
To Warehouse
The receiving warehouse.
Exclude Backorders?
Indicates if backorders from the temporary transfer are excluded when the transfer is converted:
•
Y - Exclude backorders on new transfer. Only convert items that are available.
•
N - Include backorders on new transfer.
Convert Transfer
Convert the temporary transfer into a transfer.
