Entering Weight When Receiving Stock
If you track product weight, you can require a weight for all received products upon receipt. If a product does not have a weight, you can add it in Stock Receipts Entry.
Require Weight in Stock Receipts Entry
Set Weight Required (Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info) to Y to require weight in Stock Receipts Entry for any product that does not already contain a weight in Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Package Information.
To enter weight when receiving stock:
1
After you receive all the products (refer to Receiving Stock for complete instructions on receiving), press F2-Save.
If weight is required and a product does not have a weight, the Stock Receipt Weight Entry screen appears. Products that do not contain a weight display under Product Number.
2
Enter the Weight for each product. You must enter a weight for all products before you can exit the receiver.
3
Press F2-Save to complete the receiver and to update inventory.
The weight is now stored for the product (Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Package Information). This weight is used the next time you receive this item.
