Bin Quantity Discrepancy Report
The Bin Quantity Discrepancy Report displays products with an on-hand quantity that differs from the total of the quantities in all of the product’s bins. You should run this report regularly (e.g., once a week) to ensure bin accuracy.
 
Contact Activant Prelude Customer Support to correct any discrepancies that occur.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Bin Quantity Discrepancy
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) containing the bin discrepancies.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product with a bin count discrepancy.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Onhand
The on-hand quantity of the product.
Bin List
The bin locations set up for the product.
Onhand
The on-hand quantity in each of the corresponding bins.
Reference#
Indicates how the product quantity was put into each of the corresponding bins:
•
REC - Received on a purchase order or transfer.
•
ADJ - Adjusted in inventory.
•
AUPD - Invoiced a sales order.
•
BMV - Bin move.
Date
The date the corresponding on-hand quantity was put into the bin.
Bin Disc
The difference between the product’s on-hand quantity and the total on-hand quantity in all of the product’s assigned bin locations.
