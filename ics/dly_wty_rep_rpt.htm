Daily Warranty Replacements Report
The Daily Warranty Replacements Report displays the warranty replacement entries for a date or a range of dates. You can view the products that were returned to the vendor and the replacement products received from the vendor.
Menu Path: Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Daily Warranty Replacements Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where the warranty replacements were entered.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Replacement Date Range. The beginning and ending date range for the warranty replacement entries. Leave the default of today’s date to view only warranty replacement entries for today.
•
Vendor. The vendor that replaced the warranty inventory. Leave blank to include all vendors.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Wty Rep#
The warranty replacement number assigned to the replacement product. Refer to Entering Warranty Replacements.
Vendor
The number for the vendor that replaced the returned product.
Replaced
The date the warranty replacement was received from the vendor.
Product Number /
Description
Line 1: The product number that was returned to the vendor on a vendor claim (replaced product).
Line 2: The replaced product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the replaced product.
Line 4: The replacing product received from the vendor and entered in Warranty Replacements Entry to replace the returned product.
Line 5: The replacing product description.
Line 6: The second line of description for the replacing product.
Qty-Rpld
The quantity replaced (returned to the vendor).
Qty-Rplg
The replacing quantity (received from the vendor).
Net-Cost
The net cost of the product.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Rpld Ext
The extended cost of the replaced quantity (Qty-Rpld x Net-Cost).
Rplg Ext
The extended cost of the replacing quantity (Qty-Rplg x Net-Cost).
