Product Surcharge Add Info Maintenance screen
The Product Surcharge Additional Information Maintenance screen allows you to determine how to invoice a surcharge product. Refer to Surcharge Products for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F5 - Add Info > Surcharge Flag = Y
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice Equal Quantities
Determines how the pick and backorder quantities for the surcharge product are charged relative to the calling product. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The pick and backorder quantities of the surcharge product are always equal to the pick and backorder quantities of the calling product. For example, if 5 pieces of the calling product are ordered but only 3 are shipped, then only 3 of the surcharge product are charged and the other 2 remain on backorder.
•
N - The entire quantity of the surcharge is invoiced on the initial invoice regardless of the quantity of the calling product that shipped.
•
E - The entire quantity of the surcharge is invoiced when the calling product is shipped, but the surcharge may not necessarily correspond with the quantity of the calling item. For example, if you have 10 of the calling product and 2 of the surcharge product; when 1 of the calling products is invoiced, the entire surcharge is invoiced.
Separate Surcharge Line Item
Determines how the surcharge appears on the invoice:
•
Y - The surcharge prints as a separate line item on the invoice and is included in the merchandise total.
•
N - The surcharge is included in the freight, tax, or handling field depending on how the Type of Surcharge Indicator field is set.
Consolidate Surcharge Line Items
If Separate Surcharge Line Item is set to Y, indicate how you want multiple surcharge line items listed:
•
Y - All occurrences of a surcharge product are consolidated as a single line at the bottom of the invoice.
•
N - Each occurrence of the surcharge product is printed on the invoice as it occurs on the pick ticket.
Type Of Surcharge Indicator
If Separate Surcharge Line Item is set to N, determine the type of surcharge and where it prints on the invoice:
•
T - The surcharge is for tax and is included in the tax on the invoice.
•
F - The surcharge is for freight and is included in the freight on the invoice.
•
H - The surcharge is for handling and is included in the handling charges on the invoice.
Multiplier
The value to multiply by the cost of the surcharge product to calculate the selling price. For example, if the cost of the surcharge is $10 and the Multiplier is 1.6, the selling price of the surcharge is $16.
Exclude Surcharge On Pick Tickets
Determines if surcharge items print on pick tickets:
•
Y - The surcharge item does not print on the pick ticket.
•
N - The surcharge item prints on the pick ticket.
F2-Save
Saves the surcharge product information.
