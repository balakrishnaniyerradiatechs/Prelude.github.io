Transfer Entry screen
The Transfer Entry screen allows you to request products to ship from one warehouse to another. Refer to Entering Stock Transfers for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer Number
The new or existing number of the transfer.
From
The ship-from or shipping warehouse.
To
The ship-to or receiving warehouse.
Product
The part number to ship.
Description
The product description.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for the product.
Linkage D
The document number for the destination of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this sales order number.
•
T - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this transfer number.
•
W - When the product arrives, it will be allocated to this work order.
Linkage S
The document number of the source of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - The product was purchased on this purchase order number at the shipping warehouse.
•
T - The product was ordered on this transfer number at the shipping warehouse.
Quantity
The quantity to ship.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item.
F2-Save
Saves the information on the Transfer Entry screen.
F4-Del
Deletes the stock transfer.
F5-Header
Opens the Stock Transfer Header screen. You can view or edit the shipping information.
F6-Link
Opens the Transfer Linkage screen to allow you to link a backordered product from another transfer, sales order, or work order, to this transfer.
F7-Wty Tag#
Allows you to enter a warranty tag number (refer to Transferring Warranty Inventory).
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, reference number, or page number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays cost and supply information for the highlighted product.
Review
Opens the Transfer Review screen to display pick, backorder, and cost information for the line items on the transfer.
Notes
Allows you to enter header or line items notes for the transfer.
After entering a Transfer Number and before entering a From warehouse:
Dup Transfer
Opens the Transfer Duplication screen to allow you to duplicate an open transfer. Refer to Duplicating an Open Transfer.
Dup Tran Hist
Opens the Transfer History Duplication screen to allow you to duplicate a received transfer. Refer to Duplicating a Received Transfer.
F5-Convert Temp Transfer
Opens the Temporary Transfer Conversion screen to allow you to convert a temporary transfer, generated by Nightly Processing, into a valid transfer.
