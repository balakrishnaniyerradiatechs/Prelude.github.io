Verifying Products With no Count
Before you update the physical count into on-hand inventory, make sure all products have a count in the count field. Enter a zero for items with no quantity, otherwise, the existing on-hand quantity for the product is not updated.
To verify that all zero quantity products on a list have a zero count in the count field:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Radio Frequency Procedure > Process Products Not Counted.
2
Enter the Warehouse.
3
Enter the Physical List Name to verify.
4
Press ENTER.
A zero count is entered for all items on the list with no count entry.
