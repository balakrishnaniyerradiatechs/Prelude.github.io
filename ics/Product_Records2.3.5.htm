Freezing and Thawing Product Purchasing Controls
Nightly Processing uses the information in a product’s purchasing parameters to determine when the product should be purchased. End-of-month processing re-evaluates the purchase parameters for each product in each warehouse to determine if they should be adjusted to accommodate product usage. In some cases, you may want to manually override the recommended purchase quantities and then freeze purchasing controls for a product, so that the purchasing parameters calculated by end-of-month processing are ignored.
For example: When you create a new product, you may set minimum and maximum stocking quantities that Nightly Processing uses to recommend a replenishment quantity. Since there is no usage, end-of-month processing adjusts the minimum and maximum levels to reflect zero usage. If you freeze the purchasing controls for the product, end-of-month processing will not adjust the product’s parameters.
You can also enter the date to remove the frozen controls, which is when usage begins to factor into the replenishment quantity recommended by Nightly Processing. If you want to thaw products before the set date, you can either thaw just one product at a time or remove frozen controls for all products up to the date you indicate.
