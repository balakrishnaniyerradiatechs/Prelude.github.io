Universal Product Replacement Preview Report
The Universal Product Replacement Preview Report allows you to verify replaced and merged product information before you update the products. Refer to Update Replaced/Merged Products.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Universal Product Replacement and Merge > Universal Product Replacement/Merge Preview Report
Column Descriptions
Column
Description
Old Product Number
The old product number that will be replaced or merged into a new product number when the products are updated.
Description
The description of the Old Product Number.
Pline#
The product line of the Old Product Number.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
New Product Number
The new product created or merged.
M/R
Indicates whether the products will be merged or replaced:
•
M - The old product number will be merged into the new product number.
•
R - The old product number will be replaced by the new product number.
