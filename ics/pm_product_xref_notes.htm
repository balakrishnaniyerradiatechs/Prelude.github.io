Product Xref/Notes Screen
The Product Xref/Notes screen allows you to enter cross-reference and other related information for a product. For each of the topics, one of the following displays:
•
(*) asterisk - Indicates that product cross-reference information already exists for the corresponding topic.
•
N - Indicates that no product cross-reference information exists for the corresponding topic.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Alternates
Enter Y to open the Alternates Information screen to add or edit alternate part numbers. Alternates are additional product numbers, such as a competitor’s part number or a UPC that you can use to access this product. Refer to Alternate Products for more information on setting up alternate products.
Substitutes
Enter Y to open the Substitutes screen to add or edit product substitute information. Substitutes are products you can use to replace a product. When you enter this product on a sales order, work order, or transfer, you may be notified that substitutes exist. You can also set up automatic product substitution for customers who accept substitutes. Refer to Substitute Products for more information on setting up substitute products.
Accessories
Enter Y to open the Accessory Information screen to enter or edit product accessory information. Accessories are products you can sell to use with this product. When you enter the product on a sales order, you are notified that accessories exist. You can view the accessories and add them to the order. Refer toAccessory Products for more information on setting up accessory products and groups.
Related Product
Enter Y to open the Related Product Information screen to enter or edit related product information. A related product is a product that should always be sold with this product. When you enter this product on a sales order, the related products are automatically added to the order but can be deleted. Refer to Related Products for more information.
Complements
Enter Y to open the Complementary Information screen to enter or edit product complements. A complement is useful information that can help a salesperson to sell the product. When you enter this product on a sales order, the complements display. Refer to Complements for more information on setting up complements.
Add’l Description
Enter Y to open the Additional Description screen to enter additional descriptions or information for the product in addition to the standard two lines of description. When you enter this product on a sales order, the additional description may display (if the Disp Add Desc in SOE/QOE is set to Y in Order Processing Parameters). Refer to Additional Product Descriptions for more information on setting up an additional description for a product.
Keywords
Enter Y to open the Additional Keywords screen to enter or edit keywords for the product. You can use a keyword to access a product by something other than the product number, description, or product line. Refer to Keywords for more information on setting up keywords.
Kit Parent
Enter Y to open the Kit Maintenance screen to enter or edit standard component products for an item if the product is a kit parent. Refer to Kit Parent Products for more information on setting up kits.
PStructure
Enter Y to open the PStructure Definition screen to define pstructure length and segment definitions. A pstructure is a product number made up of an infinite combination of kit component items. Refer to PStructures.
Product Notes
Enter Y to open the Product Notes screen to enter or edit notes regarding the product. Refer to Product Notes for more information on setting up product notes.
Commission
Enter Y to open the Commissions screen to view or adjust the commission percent for the product. Normal commission is defined by sales representative or by a Commission Matrix. Refer to Commissions.
Catalog Product Line
Enter Y to open the Catalog Product Lines screen to enter additional product lines so that the product will appear in multiple sections of a catalog. Refer to Catalog Product Lines.
Core Product
Enter Y to open the Core Product Entry screen. If this product is an exchange product for a core product, enter the corresponding core product number. Refer to Core Products and Pending Cores for more information on cores.
Fill With Product
Enter Y to open the Fill With Product screen to enter a fill-with product for this selling product. The fill-with product is a generic product that fills order requirements for this selling part number. Refer to Fill-With Products for more information.
Supercedures
Enter Y to open the Supercedures Information screen to view supercedure information set up in the Supercedure Maintenance screen. A superceded product is a product that is replaced by a new product. The superceded product becomes obsolete and usage is transferred to the new, superceding product. Refer to Supercedure Products for more information.
Surcharge Product
Enter Y to open the Surcharge Products screen to add or edit a surcharge to include when this product is entered on a sales order. A surcharge is a charge for a service, special handling, pallet charges, packing supplies, or any other extras not tracked as inventory.
Refer to Surcharge Products for more information on setting up surcharge and calling products.
Characteristics
Enter Y to open the Product Characteristics screen to add or edit product characteristic codes for the product. Product characteristics identify special buying or selling requirements. Refer to Product Characteristics for more information.
Upsells
Enter Y to open the Upsell Product Maintenance screen to enter or edit products to offer for upselling. Upsell products appear in Sales Order Entry to encourage the sales-person to try to sell a more expensive product. Refer to Upsell Products for more information.
F2-Save
Saves any new information in the Product Xref/Notes screen.
