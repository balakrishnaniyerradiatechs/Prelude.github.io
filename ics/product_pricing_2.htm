Price Column Quantity Breaks Screen
The Price Column Quantity Breaks screen allows you to give discounts to customers based on the quantity or dollar amount purchased. You can also choose whether the price columns are a set price, a percentage of a default price, or based on a quantity discount table.
Customer Pricing
If a customer’s prices are based on price columns, Price Code in Customer Maintenance determines which price column to use on sales orders and quotes.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F7- Pricing > F7-Col QtyBrk
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Column Qty Break Type
Determines how prices are calculated:
•
$ - Allows you to enter a price for each quantity level. You can enter an actual price or calculate the price as a percentage off of a basis.
•
* - Calculates prices as a percentage off of a default price.
•
T - Uses a quantity discount table from Discount Break Table Maintenance. Refer to Setting Up Discount Break Tables.
Quantity Discount Table (unlabeled)
If you chose T for the Column Qty Break Type, enter the table number established in Discount Break Table Maintenance.
Break Level Type
Indicates how break levels are determined.
If you entered $ for Column Qty Break Type, the Price Column Quantity Break Screen opens to allow you to enter break levels:
•
$ - By dollar amount.
•
M - By unit of measure.
•
U - By quantity.
If you entered * for Column Qty Break Type, you can enter the quantity and the percent to determine the break levels:
•
$ - By dollar amount.
•
M - By unit of measure.
•
U - By quantity.
F2-Save
Saves the price column quantity break information.
