Warranty Replacements Detail screen
The Warranty Replacement Detail screen allows you to edit the replaced quantities on a warranty replacement entry.
Menu Path: Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Warranty Replacement Entry > F5-Replaced Products > Detail Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number from the vendor claim.
Qty-Opn
The quantity sent to the vendor on the vendor claim.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the vendor claim.
A
Indicates if everything on the screen is correct:
•
Y - The information on the screen is correct.
•
N - The information on the screen is not correct.
Qty-Rpld
The quantity expected from the vendor to replace the quantity returned.
Gross
The gross cost of the product.
Vendor Product#
The vendor’s product number, if applicable.
F2-Save
Saves the warranty replacement information.
