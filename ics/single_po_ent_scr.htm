On The Water P/O Entry screen
The On The Water P/O Entry screen allows you to list enter a single purchase order from which you want to add products to your on-the-water purchase order. For any product listed that has a Remain Qty, you can enter a quantity under Wksht Qty to include on the on-the-water shipment.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > On the Water Maintenance > F6-Enter P/Os > Single Purchase Order
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The purchase order number from which you want to add products to the on-the-water purchase order.
Product Number
The products originally entered on the purchase order.
Description
The product description.
Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the purchase order.
Open Quantity
The quantity of each product originally entered on the purchase order.
Remain Qty
The quantity of the product still open on the purchase order.
Wksht Qty
The quantity that you want to add to the on-the-water purchase order.
F2-Save
Closes the screen and adds each Wksht Qty to the on-the-water purchase order.
