Receiving a Transfer
To receive transferred stock:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Receipts Entry to open the Transfer Receipts Entry screen.
2
For Transfer Release #, enter the transfer number from the pick ticket. All open lines on the transfer display.
3
Enter the Receipt Date. The following prompt displays:
Do You Want to Receive Transfer By...
4
Choose from the following:
•
Line Item: Receives one line item at a time. You can use this option if only a few items or partial quantities arrive. Highlight the item to receive and press ENTER to open the Transfer Receipts Detail screen. Continue with step 5.
•
Exception: Automatically enters the shipped Quantity into the Received quantity on every item. You can use this method if most of the order was received in full, but you need to change only a few items or quantities. Highlight the item to change and press ENTER to open the Transfer Receipts Detail screen. Continue with step 5.
•
Complete: Receives the entire transfer and completes the stock receipt. This option can be dangerous since it receives all items in full and does not allow changes. If the transfer is complete, you can also use Exception, which receives everything but gives you chance to review the transfer.
•
Abort: Exits the receiver without updating any information.
5
If you chose Line Item or Exception in step 4, the Transfer Receipts Detail screen displays.
6
For Rec Qty, enter the quantity received.

Receiving to Bins
If the receiving warehouse uses bins, the Bin Locator Information screen appears after you enter the Rec Qty. You can enter one or more bins for the material, but the total of the bin quantities must equal the Rec Qty.
7
Press F2-Save to complete the receipt for the line item.
8
After entering the correct received quantity for each line item, press F2-Save to complete the transfer receipt and to update inventory.
After the stock is received, you can allocate the material to open backorders (refer to Allocating Inventory) and then print pick tickets to ship the merchandise to the customer (refer to Releasing Pick Tickets).
The Auto Unallocate Flag in Inventory Parameters must be set to Y so that inventory is unallocated when the on-hand inventory is decreased by a negative transfer receiver.
