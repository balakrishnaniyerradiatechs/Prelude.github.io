N/S Product Merged Report & Process
The N/S Product Merged report lists products moved from the catalog file (NS.PRODUCT) to the inventory product file. Catalog products can be moved to the inventory file in one of 3 ways:
•
When you enter it on a sales order.
•
When you move it manually (Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Move).
•
During your physical inventory (refer to Merging Catalog Products into Inventory Count Lists).
The report displays a Y under the Move column for any product with a zero on-hand quantity. After the report runs, a dialog box appears to allow you to move the products flagged with a Y, back to the catalog file.
To move just one product to and from the catalog file, select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Move.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > N/S Product Merged Report & Process
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number moved from the catalog file to the product file.
Description
The product description.
Date
The date the product number was moved.
Time
The time the product number was moved.
User
The user that moved the product.
Doc#
The document number on used to move the product to the product file.
Move
Indicates if the product has a zero on-hand quantity and can be moved back to the catalog file:
•
Y -The product will be moved back to the catalog file if you choose to move flagged products after you run the report.
•
Blank - The product has an on-hand quantity and will not be moved back to the catalog file upon completion of the report.
