G/P Percent Limits screen
The G/P Limits screen allows you to enter upper and lower gross profit limits for a product line. Gross profit limits are used to warn the sales person if a product is outside of the acceptable gross profit range.
You can also set a low level gross profit percent which causes a sales order to go on Administrative Order hold if it is below this percent. This percent overrides the percent in Order Processing Parameters.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line > Gross Profit Test flag set to Y
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
G/P Lower Percent
The lowest gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you sell a product that generates a gross profit for less than this percent, you are warned that it falls below the acceptable gross profit percent.
G/P Upper Percent
The highest gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you sell a product that generates a gross profit for more than this percent, you are warned that it is above the acceptable Gross Profit percent.
Mgr Low Level G/P
%
The minimum acceptable gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you enter an item on a sales order with a gross profit less than this percent, the order goes on Administrative Order Hold. This value overrides Mgr Low Level Minimum GP% in the Administrative Order Hold Parameters screen (Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters > Administrative Order Hold = Y).
Mgr High Level G/P %
The maximum acceptable gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you enter an item on a sales order with a gross profit less than this percent, the order goes on Administrative Order Hold. This value overrides Mgr High Level GP% in the Administrative Order Hold Parameters screen (Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters > Administrative Order Hold = Y).
F2-Save
Saves the gross profit limit information.
