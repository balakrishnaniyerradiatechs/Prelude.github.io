Product Bin Inquiry
The Product Bin Inquiry displays product bin and availability information.
Viewing Product Bin Information
To view bin information for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Miscellaneous Inquiries > Product Bin Inquiry to open the Product Bin Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product. The product description displays.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
The bin location and availability information for the product displays.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of the product description, if available.
Warehouse
The warehouse number. The warehouse description displays.
Bin
The primary bin for the product.
Available
The quantity of the product that is available to sell (the on-hand quantity less the quantity committed to sales orders, work orders, transfers, and vendor claims).
Bin List
The list of bins where the product currently exists. A product may exist in more than one bin at a time.
Onhand
The quantity on-hand in each bin.
Committed
The quantity committed to sales orders, work orders, and transfers. This information is used with the Warehousing module.
Available
The quantity in each bin that is available for sale. This information is used with the Warehousing module.
Nonnet
Indicates if this bin contains products that are available for sale:
•
Asterisk (*) - The bin is non-nettable meaning the material is not available to sell.
•
Null - The material is available to sell.
Docs
The document types containing the product if a quantity is committed (Warehousing module):
•
S - Sales orders.
•
W - Work orders.
•
T - Transfers.
F5-Orders
Opens the Sales Order Commitments screen for the highlighted bin location to allow you to view detail information on sales orders containing the product (Warehousing module).
F6-Transfers
Opens the Stock Transfer Commitments screen for the highlighted bin to allow you to view detail information on transfers containing the product. Used with the Warehousing module.
F7-Work Orders
Opens the Work Order Commitments screen for the highlighted bin to allow you to view detail information on work orders containing the product. Used with the Warehousing module.
F8-Repl
Displays bin replenishment information for the highlighted bin. Used with the Warehousing module.
F9-Vnd Clm
Opens the Vendor Claim Display screen if this product exist on a vendor claim.
