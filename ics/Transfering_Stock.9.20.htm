Receiving Stock Transfers
When a stock transfer enters the receiving warehouse, you must receive the transfer to update the product quantities into inventory.
Refer to the following sections to receive a stock transfer:
•
Receiving a Transfer.
•
Receiving Transfers by Batch.
Two-Step Transfer
To eliminate this step by automatically updating inventory at the receiving branch during Transfer Shipments Entry, refer to the Two Step Transfer Process under Establishing Transfer Options.
Related Reports
Description
Transfer Putaway Document Report
Products and putaway locations for material received on transfers.
 
Stock Transfer Receiving Report
A list to check in items transferred from another warehouse. You can use this as a putaway document after a transfer is received.
Stock Transfer Discrepancy Report
Shipped items that are zero received or not received. Transfers no longer appear on the report once all the items are received.
Stock Transfer History Discrepancy Report
Received transfers that contain a discrepancy between the quantity open and the quantity received.
 
Stock Transfer Log Report
All activity for a transfer.
 
Daily Stock Transfer Receiving Report
All material received in a warehouse on the day indicated.
 
Monthly Stock Transfer Receiving Report
All material received in a warehouse in the month indicated.
