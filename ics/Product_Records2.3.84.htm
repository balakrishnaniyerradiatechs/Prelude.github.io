Hazardous Material Class
Hazardous material classes identify products that require hazardous material information to print on a bill of lading when it is shipped. These classes are set up by the Office of Hazardous Materials Safety within the U.S. Department of Transportation. After you create the hazardous material classes, you can assign them to products in Product Maintenance.
Hazardous Material Class Listing
To view a list of hazardous material codes, select Inventory > Listings > Miscellaneous Listings > Hazardous Material Class.
To set up hazardous material classes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Freight > Hazardous Material Class to open the Hazardous Material Class Maintenance screen.
2
For Haz Material Class, enter the hazardous material class as published by the office of Hazardous Materials Safety.
3
Enter the Description of the hazardous material class.
4
For Hazmat Codes, enter all the hazardous material codes that may apply to this hazardous material class. These codes also determine the packing requirements for the product.
5
For Manifest Charge, indicate if hazardous charges are applicable if you use PSS for shipping. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Hazardous charges apply for PSS.
•
N - Hazardous charges do not apply for PSS.
6
Press F2-Save to save the hazardous material information.
To assign a hazardous material class to a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product that requires the hazardous material class.
3
Press F5-Add Info > F8-Freight to open the Freight Information Screen.
4
For Haz Material Class, enter the hazardous material class from Hazard Material Class Maintenance.
5
For Haz Material Code, enter the hazardous material code from this Haz Material Class that applies to this product.
6
Press F2-Save.
