Product Bin Onhand Report
The Product Bin Onhand report displays bin and on-hand quantity information for a product or list of products. You can use this report to view all bins where a product may exist.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Product Bin Onhand Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include.
•
Product. The product(s) for which you want to view bin information.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Bin Location
The bin for the product.
Bin Oh
The on-hand quantity of the product in the bin.
Bin Cmt
The quantity of the product committed to sales orders, work orders, transfers, and vendor claims.
Bin Avl
The quantity of the product available.
