Receiving Stock for OTW Shipping Bins
When you are notified by the overseas vendor that products are in-transit:
1
Use Stock Receipts Entry (refer to Receiving Stock) to receive the purchase order. After you enter the header information, the Ship Number screen appears.
2
Enter the Ship Number, which is the bin set up for on-the-water receipts. This ship number defaults as the bin number for each line on the purchase order.
3
Press F2-Save. The Stock Receipts Entry screen appears.
4
Receive the items on the purchase order according to the vendor’s shipping notice.
5
After entering all the shipped items, press F2-Save to:
•
Calculate and post the estimated importing fees to the general ledger. The import fees are rolled into average cost and included in the accrual postings.
•
Move the products to the ship bin to await the arrival of the merchandise. Inventory Inquiry shows the product as on-hand but not available for sale.
6
Once the merchandise arrives in the warehouse, use Bin Individual Move entry to move the products from the ship bin into their proper bins (refer to Moving Products to New Bins).
