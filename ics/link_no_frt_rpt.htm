Linked Merchandise Received With Zero Freight Report
The Linked Merchandise Received With Zero Freight report displays received products that were linked to sales orders, transfers, and work orders, but no freight was billed to the customer. If the Total Freight field on the sales order, transfer, or work order was left as zero, the product and order number display on the report. This information can help to follow-up on orders that require additional freight billing.
Refer to Allocating Freight to Linked Sales Orders for information on receiving linked sales orders.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Linked Merchandise Received Zero Freight Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Selling. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Department . A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Beginning and Ending Receipt Date Range. The beginning and ending receipt dates to include in the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number on the linked sales order, transfer, or work order.
Link Hist
The linked document number with zero freight. The prefix indicates the order type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
T - Transfer.
Cost
The cost of the item received on the purchase order.
Qty-Ord
The quantity linked to the sales order, work order, or transfer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the purchase order.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received.
