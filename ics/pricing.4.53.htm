Tracking Competitor Prices
To compare a competitor’s prices to your prices, you can set up competitor part numbers and prices to cross reference to your part numbers. Each competitor part number displays in the Alternates Information screen for your corresponding part number (refer to Alternate Products). You can use the Competitor Price Inquiry to compare a competitor’s price to your price.
