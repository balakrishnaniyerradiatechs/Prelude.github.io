Allocating Inventory Manually
If you don’t automatically allocate received material to backorders (refer to Allocating Inventory Automatically), you must manually fill backorders. The Allocation Procedure reviews all products with an increase in available balance since the last time products were manually allocated. It allocates backordered material to customers based on their priority code (refer to Allocating Inventory). An exception report prints to allow you to view open backorders.
After you manually allocate inventory, you must release the pick tickets for the backordered material. Refer to Releasing Pick Tickets.
Related Reports
Description
Linked Merchandise Received & Not Allocated Report
Received products linked to sales orders, work orders, or transfers, that are not yet allocated. Includes the reason why the item is not allocated.
Inventory Available for Backorders Report
Products that have a quantity available to fill open backorders.
To manually allocate received material:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Allocation Procedure to open the Allocation Procedure screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to allocate inventory or leave blank to allocate for all warehouses.
3
Enter a Product if you only want to allocate material for one product or leave blank to allocate inventory for all products.
4
For Full Analysis Run, choose from the following:
•
Y - Allocate all open document line items.
•
N - Allocate only items with activity since the last allocation.
5
Press ENTER. The following prompt appears:
Do you wish to run Allocation by Age?
6
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Allocate the oldest order first.
•
No - Do not allocate inventory. You must enter each order to allocate the material.
Once you allocate inventory, you can release pick tickets for the filled backorders. Refer to Releasing Pick Tickets.
If the reserve inventory of an item is allocated, the sales order or transfer goes on Branch Hold for management review.
Credit Hold
If Credit Hold/Unallocate Inv is set to Y In Accounts Receivable Parameters, received inventory does not allocate to sales orders on credit hold. When the sales order is released from credit hold, inventory allocates based on the available quantity of each item at that time.
