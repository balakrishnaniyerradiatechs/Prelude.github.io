Zone Maintenance
You must enter a Bin Count Frequency for each zone to determine the number of times per year to count bins in the zone. Zones group bins for cycle counting. This information is used by Cycle Count Monthly Processing to determine which bins to count each day.
To enter the frequency of bin counts:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Bin Files > Zone Maintenance to open the Zone Maintenance screen. For a full explanation of all the fields in Zone Maintenance, refer to Defining Zones.
2
For Bin Count Frequency, indicate the number of times to count the bins in this zone annually.
3
Press F2-Save to save the bin count information.
