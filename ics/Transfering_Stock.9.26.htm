Generating Revenue on a Stock Transfer
The Transfer Revenue Matrix creates a markup matrix to allow you to earn a profit on transfers shipped to other branches. You can define markups at either the warehouse or the warehouse/product line level for any from/to warehouse/product line combination. The calculated markup cost does not display in Transfer Entry, Transfer Shipments Entry, or in Transfer Receipts Entry.
General Ledger Table Maintenance
Two General Ledger table accounts handle revenue markup. These accounts post to the shipping warehouse when the transfer is received. The difference between the two accounts is the markup profit for shipping to other warehouses.
•
Transfer Cost Of Goods: Debited in the shipping location when a Transfer Revenue Matrix exists between warehouses and a product is transferred between the two locations.
•
Transfer Revenue: Credited in the shipping location when a Transfer Revenue Matrix exists between warehouses and a product is transferred between the two locations.
To enter a markup rate on stock transfers:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Misc. Code Files > Transfer Revenue Matrix to open the Transfer Revenue Matrix Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the From Warehouse, which is the shipping warehouse charging the transfer markup.
3
Enter the To Warehouse, which is the warehouse receiving the merchandise and paying the transfer markup.
4
For Product Line, choose from the following:
•
Enter a product line if you want to limit the markup to one product line.
•
Leave the field blank to apply the markup to all products transferred for this warehouse combination.
5
For Basis Field, enter the basis for the markup cost:
•
R - Replacement cost.
•
S - Standard cost.
•
A - Average cost.
•
V - Average cost from the To Warehouse.
•
Blank -The difference between the basis cost and the accounting cost on the transaction.
6
Enter the Markup Rate to calculate the markup revenue. For example, enter 4 to add 4% to the cost for each item shipped for the warehouse/product line combination.
7
For Post To COGS, determine where the transfer revenue is posted:
•
Y - Post to inventory.
•
N - Post to the Expense Account number entered below.
8
If you entered N for Post To COGS, enter the general ledger Expense Account prefix information to post the revenue to.
9
Press F2-Save to save the transfer markup information.
You can exclude a product line from a markup by entering it in Product Line and then setting Markup Rate to zero.
