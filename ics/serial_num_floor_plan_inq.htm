Serial Number Floor Plan Inquiry
The Serial Number Floor Plan Inquiry displays serial numbers used with A/P floor planning. An A/P floor plan refers to products that you do not have to pay for until sold or until a until a negotiated date.
Viewing Serial Number Floor Plan Information
To view floor plan information for a product/serial number combination:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Product/Serial Number Inquiries > Serial Number Floor Plan Inquiry to open the Serial Number Floor Plan Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product. The product description displays.
3
Enter the Serial Number associated with the product.
The floor plan information for this product/serial number displays.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number.
Serial Number
The serial number that is floor planned.
Warehouse
The warehouse where the product/serial number exists.
Floor Plan
Indicates if this product/serial number has a floor plan set up:
•
Y - A floor plan exists for the product. A floor plan means that you do not have to pay for the product until a negotiated date or when it is sold.
•
N - No floor plan exists for the product.
Receiver Number
The stock receiver number on which the product/serial number was received.
Vendor
The vendor that the product/serial number was ordered from.
Vendor
The vendor that was paid for the product/serial number.
Vendor Invoice#
The vendor invoice number for the product/serial number.
Invoice Date
The date of the vendor invoice.
Due Date
The due date of the vendor invoice.
Extension
The extended amount of the vendor invoice.
Amt. Paid
The amount paid on the vendor invoice.
Status
The status of the item:
•
O - Open. The item is not yet shipped or paid.
•
S - Shipped. The item is partially or fully shipped.
•
P - Paid. The item is fully paid.
Date Shipped
The date the item shipped to the customer.
Sold Invoice#
The customer invoice number if the item was sold.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
F6-Check History
Opens the Check History Inquiry screen to allow you to view the check number, check date, and the check amount.
