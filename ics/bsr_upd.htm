Updating Batch Stock Receipts
After you verify the received quantities and part numbers on the Stock Receipts Edit Report, run the Batch Stock Receipts Update Procedure to update inventory with the quantities received in Batch Stock Receipts Entry.
To update inventory:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts Update Procedure to open the Batch Stock Receipts Update Procedure screen.
2
Enter a Purchase Order Number to update or leave blank to include all purchase orders in the following Batch Number.
3
Enter the Batch Number to update (not required if a Purchase Order Number is entered).
4
Enter the Warehouse for the batch you are updating.
5
Press ENTER.
The onhand quantities are updated based on the above criteria.
If received material is allocated automatically, you can release pick tickets after you enter one or more batches to fill open backorders (refer to Releasing Pick Tickets). Refer to Allocating Inventory for information on allocating inventory either manually or automatically.
