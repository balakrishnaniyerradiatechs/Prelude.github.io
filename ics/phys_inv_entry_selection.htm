Physical Inventory Entry Selection screen
The Physical Inventory Entry Selection screen allows you to enter header information for a count sheet.
Menu Path: Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Sheet Procedure > Physical Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Warehouse
The warehouse for which you want to enter inventory counts.
Physical ListName
The ListName from the count sheet. If you find a product that is not on a count sheet, enter M to enter a Manual Physical Inventory ListName.
Count Take Initials
The initials of the employee that counted the stock.
Starting Product
The beginning product number counted.
Ending Product
The ending product number.
