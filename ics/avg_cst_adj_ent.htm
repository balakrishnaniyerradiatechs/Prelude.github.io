Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry screen
The Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry screen allows you to adjust cost for vendor consigned inventory. Once the vendor invoice is reconciled, you can no longer adjust the cost. This type of cost adjustment does not update the Transaction Audit Inquiry.
Menu Path: Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Cost Adjustment Entry -Vendor Consigned
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Adjustment Number
The inventory adjustment number assigned by the system.
Entry Date
The date inventory was adjusted.
Product
The vendor consigned product number whose inventory is adjusted.
Vendor
The vendor you consigned the inventory from.
Warehouse
The warehouse where the consigned inventory exists.
Quantity On Hand
The quantity of the product in on-hand inventory.
Current Cost
The existing cost of the product in your system.
Revised Cost
The new cost of the product.
Cost Adj Reason
The cost adjustment reason code.
F2-Save
The cost adjustment reason code to explain why the cost is adjusted. Refer to Establishing Cost Adjustment Reason Codes.
