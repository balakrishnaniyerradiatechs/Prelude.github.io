Major Group Codes
Major groups allow you to combine a large number of products or product lines to calculate product classifications and discounts within each warehouse (refer to Classifying Products). You can assign the Major Group codes to product lines in Product Line Maintenance.
To set up major group codes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Major Group Codes to open the Major Group Maintenance screen.
2
For Major Group, enter a code to represent a group of products.
3
Enter a Description that identifies the products in the major group.
4
For Product Group, enter the code for the product group that the major group belongs to. Refer to Product Group Codes. You can use this code to group products in Commission Matrix Maintenance.
5
Press F2-Save to save the major group information.
General Ledger Account Overrides
Press F5-G/L Accounts if you want to enter overrides to the general ledger accounts for products in this major group. These accounts are used if no G/L account is set up at the product line level.
To assign major groups to product lines:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line to open the Product Line Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product Line number.
3
Enter the major group code in Major Group.
4
Press F2-Save to save the major group information.
