Allocating Freight to Linked Sales Orders
If a sales order is linked to a purchase order that incurs a freight charge, you can allocate all or part of the freight charge to the customer.
Freight Allocations
Option Parameters > Purchasing Parameters > Allocate Linked Frt To S/O determines if the freight charge is allocated to a linked sales order when a purchase order is received:
•
Y - Freight is allocated proportionally to Inbound Freight for any linked sales orders.
If the calculated freight amount is lower than the Minimum LInked Frt Amt in Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add Info, the system uses the Minimum LInked Frt Amt from Warehouse Maintenance for Inbound Freight.
•
I - Same as Y, but it also includes the freight cost paid expenses as part of the G/L posting for the receiver.
•
N - Freight for received material is not automatically passed on to linked sales orders.
For more information on allocating freight, refer to “Allocating Freight” in the Purchasing documentation.
To allocate freight to a linked sales order:
1
After you receive all the line items on the purchase order and press F2-Save (refer to Receiving Stock), the Freight Closeout screen appears.
2
Verify or change the Freight Amount.
3
Press F2-Save.
4
The Freight to Sales Order screen appears if the purchase order includes a linked sales order (and if Allocate Linked Frt To S/O in Purchasing Parameters is set to Y or I).
5
For Total Freight, accept or enter total amount of the freight charge.
6
For Percent Of Freight To S/O, enter the percentage of the total freight to allocate to the linked received items, based on the freight allocation method (e.g., dollars, weight, units, or volume).
7
Press F2-Save to allocate the freight and complete the stock receiver. When the linked sales order is invoiced, the freight charge is automatically billed to the customer.

Linked Merchandise Received With Zero Freight Report
Allows you to follow-up on orders that require additional freight billing. The report displays all receivers, including linked products, that have the Total Freight amount set to zero in the Freight To S/O screen in Stock Receipts Entry.
