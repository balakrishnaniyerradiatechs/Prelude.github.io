Line Item Quality Control screen
The Line Item Quality Control screen allows you to enter rejection and warranty tag information when you reject an item in Stock Receipts Entry. The warranty tag is used to reference the item on a vendor claim.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > Stock Receipts Entry > Detail Information Screen > F5-Reject Claim.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Rejection Claim Reason
The Rejection Claim Reason code that indicates why the product is rejected. Refer to Setting Up Rejection Claim Reason Codes for information on rejection codes.
Rejection Claim Quantity
The quantity to return or scrap.
 
Rejection Warranty Tag#
The warranty tag number used to track the product in the system as it goes through Vendor Claim Entry, Vendor Claim Update Processing, and Warranty Replacement Entry. Refer to Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory.
Rejection Claim Notes
An explanation of why the material is being returned.
 
F2-Save
Saves the rejection information.
