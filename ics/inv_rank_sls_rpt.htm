Inventory Ranking By Sales Report
The Inventory Ranking By Sales report displays all inventory in a warehouse, ranked by the total cost of the product sold year-to-date. The product with the largest inventory amount sold is ranked first.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Sales Status Reports > Inventory Ranking By Sales
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Rank
The ranking of the product by Ytd-Value (the total amount sold year-to-date).
YTD-Sales
The quantity of the product sold year-to-date.
Avg Cost
The average cost of the product.
YTDValue
The cost of the total quantity of the product sold year-to-date (Ytd-Sales x Avg Cost).
