Product Line Maintenance Screen
Product lines group similar product together for selling (e.g., sales analysis, discounts, price matrices, product campaigns). They also allow you to establish gross profit limits, freight allocation methods, and handling percentages to assign to a group of products. Product lines only determine how you sell a product to your customers and do not affect vendor or purchasing information. Every product established in Product Maintenance or Quick Product Maintenance must have a product line assigned.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Line
The alphanumeric product line code to assign to product numbers.
Description
The product line description that displays whenever the product line is entered.
Major Group
The major group for this product line established in Major Group Code Maintenance (refer to Major Group Codes.) Major groups allow you to group a large number of products for sales analysis reports. You can use major groups to calculate product classifications within each warehouse. Refer to Classifying Products).
Freight Allocation
Determines how the cost of freight is allocated when stock is received:
•
$ Allocate by dollars.
•
# Allocate by pounds.
•
U Allocate by units.
•
V Allocate on volume.
•
No allocation.
Refer to the section on freight in the purchasing documentation for further explanation of freight charges and allocation methods.
Catalog Sort Option
Determines how products within this product line are sorted in catalogs:
•
1 - Vend by Vend Prod#. By vendor by vendor/product number.
•
2 - Retail by Vend By Vend Prod#. Retail by vendor, by vendor product number.
Handling Percent
The percentage of the total sale used to calculate handling charges.
Duty Percent
The percentage of the product cost to use to calculate duty charges when the product is received from an overseas vendor.
Brokerage Percent
The percentage of the product cost to use to calculate brokerage charges when the product is received from an overseas vendor.
Freight Class
The freight class for the product line. Refer to Freight Class for information on setting up freight classes. The customer is billed according to the freight class.
Gross Profit Test?
Determines if upper and lower gross profit limits regulate the product line. Gross profit limits alert the salesperson if a product in the product line is outside of this range on a sales order. You can also set up a low level gross profit percent to determine if the product should go on Administrative Order Hold:
•
Yes - Gross profit limits are established for this product. The G/P Percent Limits screen appears to allow you to enter the maximum gross profit percentage that the product can have on a sales order and not go on Administrative Order Hold. Refer to the “Administrative Order Inquiry” in the Order Processing documentation for more information.
•
No - Gross profit limits are not set for this product line.
Round Prices?
Determines if prices are rounded for products in the product line:
•
Yes - The Price Decimals Rounding screen displays to allow you to enter the appropriate rounding for each price.
•
No - The product line defaults to the system option for decimal rounding.
Rental
Determines if the product line is used for rental products:
•
Yes - Used for rental products.
•
No - Not used for rental products.
F2-Save
Saves the product line information.
F4-Del
Deletes the product line.
F5-Add Info
Opens the Trend Factors Screen, which is used with the Forecasting module to establish forecasting formulas.
F6-Season Index
Opens the Index Maintenance Screen to allow you to enter a seasonality index between 0.00 and 12.00 for each month. This index averages annual usage for each product in Sales Forecast Maintenance.
F7-G/L Accounts
Opens the Account Number Entry screen to allow you to enter account numbers for general ledger postings.
Action Bar
Additional Options > Parker Information
Opens the Parker Information Screen to allow you to enter Parker division codes and parker product codes for a product line. Refer to Parker Reporting Codes.
