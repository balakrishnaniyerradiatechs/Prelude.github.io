Entering Prices/Costs for a Product
The Product Pricing screen in Product Maintenance allows you to establish multiple price and cost levels for an individual product. From within the Product Pricing screen, you can expand the price/cost possibilities by accessing additional screens to establish price column quantity breaks, cost quantity breaks, and quantity breaks using break tables from Discount Break Table Maintenance.
Refer to Implementing the Price/Cost Hierarchy for more information on price/cost levels.
The following screens allow you to set up prices and costs at the product level:
•
Product Pricing Screen.
•
Price Column Quantity Breaks Screen.
•
Price Column Quantity Break Screen.
•
Quantity Break Table Screen.
•
Quantity Break By Dollar Amount Screen.
•
Cost Quantity Break Table Screen.
•
Product Future Pricing Screen.
•
Previous Prices Screen.
