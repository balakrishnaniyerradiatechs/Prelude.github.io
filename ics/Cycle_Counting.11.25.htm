Entering 2nd Level RF Cycle Counts
After you run the Cycle Count Second Level Report (refer to the Cycle Count Second Level Report) you can count the products in the second level queue and enter the recounted quantities.
To enter the second level counts:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Cycle Counting Procedure > Radio Frequency Cycle Count Entry (2nd Level) to open the Cycle Count Entry screen.
2
Enter the number for the Warehouse.
3
For # Products, enter the number of products to display at one time. If you leave this blank, all products available for cycle counting for the warehouse number display.
4
Enter your Counter ID.
5
Press F3 the key to bring up the first Product Number on the count sheet.
If Default P/N in 2nd Level Entry in Warehouse Maintenance is set to Y, the product number is defaulted in the Product Number field.
6
Press ENTER. The following information appears at the bottom of the screen:
•
The Bin where the product exists.
•
An asterisk (*) displays under P if there is a discrepancy outside the defined tolerance limits.
•
C remains blank until the second level count is entered.
•
Qty, which is the quantity entered for the first level count.
7
Press ENTER. The next Cycle Count Entry screen displays.
The System quantity (first level count), and the amount of the discrepancy (Disc) display.
8
Enter the second level Count. If the count is different than the System quantity, the following prompt displays:
Is The Quantity Entered of ##
The Correct Count Quantity?
9
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Accepts the count.
•
No - Allows you to change the count.
10
Repeat steps 6-9 for the remaining items in the bin.
11
After you enter counts for all the items in the bin, press F2-Upd. The following prompt displays:
An Inventory Adjustment for a Quantity
Of ## and a Value of ## Will Now Take
Place! Do You Wish To Continue?
12
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Adjusts inventory. The product number is updated with a transaction audit code of ADJ in the Transaction Audit Inquiry.
•
No - Allows you to adjust the counted quantities.
13
Once you choose Yes, continue entering the rest of the items on the second level report.
14
After you enter all of the bins, the Bin number is left blank. If necessary, you can add any part numbers that were not on the report.
15
Press F2-Upd after you enter all the recounts.
