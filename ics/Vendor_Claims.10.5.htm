Creating Warranty Tags in Inventory Adjustment Entry
If you have a product in your warehouse that you want to return to the vendor and you do not want it to show as available, you can adjust inventory to put the product into warranty inventory until it is returned. Inventory Adjustment Entry assigns a warranty tag to track the product as it goes through Vendor Claim Entry, Vendor Claim Update Processing, and Warranty Replacement Entry.
To create a warranty tag by adjusting inventory:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Inventory Adjustment Entry to open the Inventory Adjustment Entry screen.
2
For I/A Number, press NEW to assign a new number.
3
The Entry Date defaults to today’s date. Change if neccessary.
4
Enter the Warehouse for the adjustment.
5
For Adj. Type, enter WTY for warranty inventory.
6
Enter the Product Number to put into warranty inventory. The Inventory Adjustment Line Detail screen appears.
7
Enter a positive Quantity to move the material from regular inventory into warranty inventory.
The Warranty Tag Information screen appears.
8
For Returned/Scrapped, indicate if the material will be sent back to the vendor or thrown out.
•
R - The material will be returned to the vendor.
•
S - The material will be scrapped.
9
For Vendor RMA #, enter the vendor’s return material authorization (RMA) number, if one is available.
10
For Tag Number, enter N to generate a new tag number or enter your own tag number.
11
For Tag Quantity, enter the defective quantity. You can enter more than one tag number per line item, but the total in the Tag Quantity column must equal the Quantity entered on the Inventory Adjustment Line Detail screen.
Vendor Return Rules
To view any notes regarding return policies for this vendor, press F5-Vendor Return Policy. The vendor return rules are established in Vendor Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F6 > Warranty > F5-Rules.
12
Press F2-Save to save the tag information and return to the Inventory Adjustment Line Detail screen.
13
For Reason, enter the code to represent why you are adjusting inventory (Refer to Establishing Inventory Adjustment Reason Codes).
14
Once everything in the detail screen is correct, press F2 to return to the Inventory Adjustment Entry screen.
15
Repeat steps 6 through 14 to adjust additional warranty items.
16
Press F2-Save when complete.
Notes
To enter notes regarding each inventory adjustment, select Notes from the Action Bar. You can enter notes for each line number or for the entire adjustment.
You can now return the defective items to the vendor on a vendor claim. Refer to Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory.
Warranty Tag Bins
If you use bins to track inventory, you can manually enter a warranty tag product in the warranty tag bin using the Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance screen (Inventory > Vendor Claims > Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance). The inventory in this bin does not affect on-hand inventory, but is only for reference in the Warranty Tag Inquiries.
