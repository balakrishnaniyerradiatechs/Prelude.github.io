Product Vendor Maintenance Screen
If you buy a product from more than one vendor, you can use the Product Vendor Maintenance screen to create a separate record for each vendor/product/warehouse combination.
The Product Vendor Information Listing (Inventory > Listings > Product Listings > Vendor Information) lists the information set up in Product Vendor Maintenance
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Vendor Maintenance
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description

The product number to assign to this vendor.

The warehouse number to assign to this vendor.
Vendor
The vendor number to assign to the product in this warehouse.

The vendor line for this product. Vendor lines group products for purchasing, discounting, and analyzing purposes.
Vendor lines are established in Vendor Line Maintenance in the Purchasing module.
Vendor Product
The part number used by the vendor. This number prints on purchase orders and is used for vendor price tape updates. Refer to Updating Prices/Costs Using a Price Tape.
Package Quantity
The package quantity for this product when you purchase it from this vendor. If you enter a purchase order for multiples less than this quantity, you are warned but still allowed to enter the quantity. Calendar Month End rounds to package quantities to calculate the Recommended Qty in the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
Min Buy Qty
The minimum purchase quantity allowed for this product for this vendor. Recommended purchase quantities are rounded to this amount.
A warning displays if you enter less than this amount on a purchase order. You can choose to round to the minimum buy quantity or to keep the quantity entered.

The weight of the product from this vendor. Used to calculate requirements to recommend purchase orders.
Unit Of Measure
The unit of measure of the product.

Recent Lead Time Exception
The number days of a leadtime exception, if an unusual leadtime occurred for this product.
If you do not want to use unusual leadtimes to calculate Average Lead Time (on this screen), you can set a value in Lead Time Exception Days in Purchasing Parameters Maintenance for this warehouse. Any lead time that is more than this number of days is excluded in the average lead time calculation.

The cubic size of the product. This value displays in the Recommended Purchase Order Inquiry to help determine the amount of material required to fill a shipping container. It is also used to determine bin allocation if you use the Warehousing Module.
Does Vendor Barcode
Determines if the vendor supplies the barcode for this product.
â€¢

Last Lead Time
The number of days leadtime calculated from the last time the product was ordered until the last time it was received from this vendor. If the product is flagged on the purchase order as an emergency or as having unusual usage, this field is not updated when the product is received. You can override this value by pressing F7-Override Lead Times.
Next-Last Lead Time
The leadtime calculated the time before the last time the product was received from this vendor. You can override this value by pressing F7-Override Lead Times.
Average Lead Time
The average leadtime calculated for the product, based on the date the product was received. Uses the Number of Lead Times established in Purchasing Parameters Maintenance (Systems Menu) for this warehouse to determine how many past lead times to use in the calculation (if Primary Vendor for this product/vendor is set to Y).
If you do not want unusual lead times to affect the calculation, you can set a maximum value in Lead Time Exception Days in Purchasing Parameters Maintenance for this warehouse. Any lead time that is more than this number of days is excluded in the lead time calculation. The most recent exception appears in Recent Lead Time Exception on this screen.
Worst Lead Time
The longest lead time calculated for the product.
Last Purchase Date
The last date the product was entered on a purchase order. Exclude emergencies and unusual usage.
Last Purchase Qty
The quantity of the product on the last receiver.
Last Purchase Cost
The cost of the product on the last receiver.

Opens the Override Lead Times screen to allow you to override the system calculated values for Last Lead Time, and Next-Last Lead Time for this product/vendor/warehouse combination. The lead time is used to project the receipt date for a product.
