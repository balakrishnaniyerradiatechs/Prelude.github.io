Copying a Product to Create a New Product
The Copy feature in Product Maintenance allows you to copy all the information from one product to create a new product without having to re-enter most of the detail information. This feature is helpful if you have products that are very similar except for color, size, or other repetitive characteristics. Once a the new product is created, you can use Product Maintenance to adjust any detail information.
Inventory Parameters
Copy Whse In Prod Copy? in Inventory Parameters determines if only the product header is copied or if the product is copied into all warehouses that stock the original product.
•
Y - The new product is created in all warehouses that stock the original product.
•
N - The new product is only created in Product Maintenance but not for any warehouses. You can manually add the product to each warehouse in Product Warehouse Maintenance.
To copy a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to access the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Before you enter a product number, select Copy from the Action Bar to open the Product Maintenance Copy screen.
3
Enter the From Product Number, which is the original product whose information you are copying.
4
Enter the To Product Number, which is the new product you are creating.
5
Change the Description and/or the Description Two for the new product number as necessary.
6
Press F2-Save to add the new product to your inventory.
