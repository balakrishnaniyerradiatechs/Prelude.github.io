Transferring Stock
To enter a stock transfer:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry to open the Transfer Entry screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
For Transfer Number, choose from the following:
•
To create a new transfer, press ENTER.
•
To edit an existing transfer, enter the transfer number or press F3 to search for transfer numbers by warehouse.
3
Enter the From warehouse number to indicate the shipping warehouse.
4
Enter the To warehouse number to indicate the receiving warehouse.
The Stock Transfer Header screen appears.
Ship-To Address
If you want to change the ship-to address to someplace other than the warehouse location, press F7-Ship-To on the header screen to open the Manual Ship-To Entry screen. You can create an address in this screen or use a code from Transfer Ship-To Maintenance. Refer to Maintaining Ship-To Addresses for Stock Transfers.
5
Enter the Transfer Type (refer to Defining Transfer Types for a full explanation on transfer types). Choose from the following:
•
01 - Regular.
•
02 - Non inventory.
•
03 - Warranty inventory.
•
04 - Crib replenishment.
•
05 - Ship line item complete.
6
For Written By, enter your user ID. The default is the user ID of the person logged onto this session.
7
Enter the Ship Via to designate the method of transfer. The default comes from the Ship Via set up in Warehouse Maintenance for the receiving warehouse.
8
For Entry Date, accept the default of today’s date or change as necessary.
9
For Pick Ticket Date, accept the default of today’s date or change it to print at a later date. Refer to Entering a Future Transfer.
10
For Unusual Usage Flag, choose from the following:
•
N - The transfer of the material is not unusual, therefore usage is updated.
•
Y - The transfer of the material is unusual, so usage is not updated for the products included on the transfer. Refer to Recording Product Usage for information on transfer usage.
11
Enter any Instructions to print on pick tickets.
12
Press F2-Save to complete the header screen and return to the Transfer Entry screen.
13
For Product, choose from the following:
•
Enter the exact product number. The Transfer Entry Detail screen appears.
•
Press F7-Wty Tag to enter a Type 03 warranty transfer. Refer to Transferring Warranty Inventory for instructions on warranty transfers.
•
Enter part of the product number, description, or other keyword, then press ENTER to search for the part number. The Product Tagging Entry screen appears to allow you to enter quantities for the part numbers to include in the transfer.
Product Tagging
The Product Tagging flag in Inventory Parameters determines how you can look up and enter part numbers when searching by keyword:
•
N - Product tagging is not activated. You must enter 1 item at a time on the order, then enter the quantity in the detail information screen.
•
Y - Activates the Product Tagging Entry screen for entry programs to allow you to enter quantities for multiple products to go directly on the order.
•
A - The same as Y, except that the Product Tagging Entry screen also displays for inquiries, but in the inquiry mode. The inquiry mode displays the available and on-order, but you can only select one product.at at time.
•
I - The Product Tagging Entry screen displays in the inquiry mode for both entry programs and inquiries. The inquiry mode displays the available and on-order, but you can only select one product.at at time.
Substitutes
If the item is backordered and substitutes are available, SUB displays on the bottom right side of the screen. Substitutes are added to a transfer depending on how the product is set up in Product Maintenance. Refer to Substitute Products
14
For Qty Opn, enter the quantity to transfer. The available quantity to pick displays in Pck Qty. The backordered quantity displays in B/O Qty.
Transfer Package Quantity
If you enter a quantity less than the Transfer Pkg Qty set in Product Maintenance for the item, it automatically rounds up to the next package quantity. A warning message appears if you override the package quantity but does not prevent you from overriding it.
15
For Ship Complete, choose from the following:
•
Y - Hold the transfer until the entire quantity ordered is available.
•
N - Ship the amount of the item currently available and ship backorders as they arrive.
16
Once everything in the detail screen is correct, press F2-Save.
17
Repeat steps 13-16 for each product on the transfer.
Comments
To add comment lines to the transfer, enter one of the following on a blank line:
•
C - Allows you to enter a comment that prints on all documents related to the transfer (e.g., pick ticket, packing list, bill of lading).
•
CPO - Allows you to enter a comment to print on a linked purchase order.
Link a Backorder
To manually link a backorder from another transfer, work order, or sales order; select F6-Link to open the Transfer Linkage screen. You can view all documents that contain a backorder for the highlighted product. You can select the document to link to the product.
You cannot link a sales order to a transfer if the user is still in the transfer.
18
Press F2-Save to complete the transfer. The following prompt appears:
Do You Wish To...
19
Choose from the following:
•
Print - Flags the transfer for pick ticket printing based on product availability and the Pick Ticket Date on the header. Refer to Printing Stock Transfers.
•
Suspend: Holds the ticket until you go back into Transfer Entry to edit or release the transfer.
•
Return: Returns to line item entry.
Transfers on Branch Manager Hold
Refer to Establishing Transfer Options for information on holding transfers that will reduce the quantity of an item to below its safety allowance.
