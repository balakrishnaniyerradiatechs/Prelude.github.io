Stock Receipts Exception Report
The Stock Receipts Exception Report displays discrepancies that occurred when stock was received (e.g, the product was not on the original purchase order, the received cost is outside your tolerance limits, the purchase order is received late).
Days Early/Late
The following flags in Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add info set the number of days to use to determine if a product should appear on this report:
•
Days Early: The number of days before the promise date that a product is considered as received early.
•
Days Late/Orig Or Exp Date: The number of days after the original promise or expedite date that a product is considered as received late.
•
Days Late/Latest Prom Date: The number of days after the latest promise date that a product is considered as received late.
 
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Stock Receipts Exception Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Vendor. The vendor number to limit the report to just one vendor. Leave blank to display receivers for all for all vendors for the selected date range.
•
Receipt Date Range. The beginning and ending receipt dates to include in the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Receiver#
The receiver number that contains the discrepancy.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Product
Line 1: The product number and description.
Line 2: The reason for the exception:
•
Not on original purchase order.
•
Received after the original promise date. Includes the number of days late.
•
Received after the latest promise date. Includes the number of days late.
•
Received before the original promise date. Includes the number of days early.
•
Over-received. Includes the percentage over-received.
•
Damaged. Includes damaged quantity and warranty tag number.
Qty-Ord
The quantity originally ordered on the purchase order.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received on this receiver.
Grs Cost
The gross unit cost of the item which is the cost before any discounts.
Rec-Date
The date the item was received.
Orig-Del
The original delivery date if the buyer received a confirmed or expedited ship date from the vendor.
Due Date
The due date calculated from the purchase order date plus average lead-time for this product for this vendor to this warehouse (stored in Product Vendor Maintenance).
