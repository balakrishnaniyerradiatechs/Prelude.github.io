Establishing the Barcode Label Printing Hierarchy
Depending on how you set up your vendors, products, and warehouses you can determine when and how labels print in Stock Receipts Entry. If a vendor provides barcodes, you do not have to print labels for that vendor. When you set up products or vendors to print labels, the hierarchy to determine when barcode labels print is:
•
1st level - Product Warehouse Maintenance
•
2nd level - Product Maintenance
•
3rd level - Product Vendor Maintenance
•
4th level - Vendor Maintenance
Continuous Printing
Stk Rec Label (Warehouse Maintenance > F6-Printing > F5-Cont Labels Setup) determines if barcode labels print continuously in Stock Receipts Entry. If you enter Y to print labels continuously, you must indicate when labels print: Choose from the following:
•
L - Print stock receipts labels as each line item is received.
•
U - Print stock receipts labels after saving the receiver or updating the batch.
Flags in the following programs determine how barcode labels print:
Vendor Maintenance > F5-Add Info
For Does Vendor Barcode, indicate if the vendor prints barcodes labels on products. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The vendor barcodes products. Barcode labels do not print when stock is received.
•
N - The vendor does not barcode products. Barcodes print on labels and on the Purchase Order Receiving Report when stock is received.
•
L - The vendor does not barcode products. Barcodes only print on the Purchase Order Receiving Report when stock is received.
Product Vendor Maintenance
For Does Vendor Barcode, indicate if labels print for this vendor/product combination. The Print Barcode Label flag in both Product Maintenance and Product Warehouse Maintenance determine if this field is checked at all. Choose from the following.
•
Y - The vendor barcodes products. Barcode labels do not print when stock is received.
•
N - The vendor does not barcode products. Barcodes print on labels and on the Purchase Order Receiving Report when stock is received.
•
L - The vendor does not barcode products. Barcodes only print on the Purchase Order Receiving Report when stock is received.
Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F7-Labels
For Print Barcode Label, determine if the setting in Product Vendor Maintenance is used. Choose from the following:
•
V - Use the flag at the product vendor level to determine if barcode labels print. If the product vendor flag is blank, the vendor flag is checked.
•
Y - Print barcode labels regardless of any vendor setting.
•
N - Do not print barcodes for the product regardless of any vendor flag.
•
R - Print barcodes only on reports and documents. Custom programming is required. No labels print.
Product Warehouse Maintenance
For Print Barcode Label, choose from the following:
•
V - Use the flag at the product/vendor level to determine if barcode labels print. If the product vendor flag is blank, the vendor flag is checked.
•
Y - Print barcode labels regardless of any vendor setting.
•
N - Do not print barcodes for the product regardless of vendor flag.
•
R - Print barcodes only on reports and documents. Custom programming is required. No labels print.
