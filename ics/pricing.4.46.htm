Updating the Price Tape Data
After you review and adjust the price tape information (refer to Correcting Price Tape Updates), you can update your products with the new data.
To update price tape data:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Tape Update > Price Tape Update to open the Price Tape Update Routine screen.
2
For Name on Diskette, enter the name of the file to load. This is the same name you used in Setting Up a Price Tape and in Loading a Price Tape. The Vendor and the data from the price tape automatically display. Change as necessary.
3
Press F2 - Save to start the update. If Disp Save Prev Pricing in Inventory Parameters is set to Y, the following prompt displays:
Do You Wish To Save Previous Prices?
4
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Saves the previous prices. You can view these in the Previous Prices screen in Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing > Action Bar > Previous Prices (refer to the Product Pricing Screen).
•
No - Does not save previous prices.
5
When the following prompt displays:
Choose from the following:
Do You Wish to Update ALL Warehouses or
Only the Warehouses Selected in the
Warehouse List?
•
All - Creates warehouse records for new products based on all the warehouses in Warehouse Maintenance.
•
Selected - Creates warehouse records only for the warehouses listed in Warehouses.
The new prices are updated in the system.
If an alternate product number is the same as the number in your price tape file, the alternate part number is not updated.
