Receiving Stock Without a Purchase Order
If material arrives in your warehouse that is not on a purchase order, you can create and receive the purchase order at the same time in Stock Receipts Entry.
To receive stock without a purchase order:
1
Follow the same steps as for Receiving Stock, except enter NEW for the P/O Number.
2
After you enter the Received By, the Purchase Order Header screen appears to allow you to enter information for this vendor and purchase order. Refer to “Entering Purchase Order Information” in the Purchasing documentation for information on the purchase order header.
3
Press F2-Save to open the Stock Receipts Entry screen.
4
Enter line items as necessary. When the Detail Information screen appears, enter the Qty-Opn and the Qty-Rec.
5
After you enter and receive all of the applicable line items, press F2-Save to save the receiver and update inventory.
