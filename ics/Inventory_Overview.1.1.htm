Inventory Overview
The Prelude Inventory module allows you to efficiently manage inventory in one or more warehouses to accommodate any product, pricing, purchasing, receiving, transfer, or usage requirements your company may have. Comprehensive reports and inquiries are available to help analyze product statistics and to maximize profitability.

Cycle Counting.
•
Physical Inventory.
•
End of Month & End of Year Procedures.
•
Inventory Reports.
