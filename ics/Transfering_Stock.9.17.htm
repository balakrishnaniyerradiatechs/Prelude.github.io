Transfer Shipments Maintenance
Transfer Shipments Maintenance allows you to print a packing list and verify shipped quantities before you update inventory. To use this feature, you must have continuous printing set up for packing lists.
To verify shipped quantities before updating inventory:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Shipments Maintenance to open the Transfer Shipments Entry screen.
2
Refer to steps 2 through 8 for Transfer Shipments Entry to edit shipped and backordered quantities as necessary.
3
When you are finished editing the shipped quantities, press F2-Save. The packing list prints (continuous printing must be set up), but inventory is not updated.
To update inventory for the shipment, you must use Transfer Shipments Entry to confirm the shipment.
