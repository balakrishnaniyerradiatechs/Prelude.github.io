Substitute Products
Substitutes are products you can use to replace a product that is not available. You can set flags to determine when and how substitute products are placed on sales orders, work orders, and transfers. Once substitutes are assigned to a product, any time you enter the product on a transaction or in an inquiry, SUB displays to let you know that substitutes are available.
Customer Maintenance
Accept Substitutes in Customer Maintenance (F7-Order > F5-Add Info) determines if the customer allows automatic substitution on sales orders:
•
Y - If a product is backordered, an available substitute (depending on the how the options are set in the Substitutes screen) is automatically placed on the order.
•
N - If a product is backordered, SUB displays on the Line Item Detail screen to inform you that the product has substitutes but the product is not automatically substituted. You can press F6-Subs to view available substitute products. A warning displays to let you know that the customer does not accept substitutes but you can override the setting to accept the substitute.
Product Substitutes Inquiry
To view substitutes set up for a product, select Inventory > Inquiries > Product Inquiries > Product Substitutes Inquiry.
To set up substitute part numbers for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Press F6-Xref/Notes to open the Product Xref/Notes Screen.
3
Enter Y next to Substitutes to open the Substitutes screen.
4
For Product Substitution Type, determine how the substitute product is added to the order. The first three options only apply to customers that allow substitutes (Accept Substitutes set to Y in Customer Maintenance):
•
A - Substitute Backordered Product. If you enter a backordered quantity of the product, the first substitute product with an available quantity is selected for the order. Pricing is based on the substitute (replacement) product.
•
P - Price on Substituted Product. Works the same as the previous but the pricing is based on the original product (the product that backordered).
•
B - Supersedes All Other Types. Automatically substitutes regardless of the availability of the original product. For example, the vendor provides a bonus pack of four instead of the usual pack of three. Once the bonus product runs out, the system defaults back to the original product. Pricing is based on the original (substituted) product.
•
S - Substitute Screen Notification - or none: When you enter the product on a sales order, work order, or transfer, the substitution is not automatic. You can press F6 to view and/or select a substitute product from the available list. Pricing is based on the substitute (replacement) product.
If Product Substitute Type is set to S:
Auto Substitute Display? in Additional Option Parameters determines if a message displays in Sales Order Entry asking if you want to display substitutes when the available quantity is less than the order quantity:
•
Y - A message appears asking if you want to display substitutes when the available quantity is less than the order quantity.
•
N - No message displays when the available quantity is less than the order quantity, but you can press F6 to view available substitutes.
5
For Auto Sub Prompt in SOE/WOE/TE?, choose from the following:
•
Y - When an item is backordered, the first available substitute item is automatically placed on the sales order or work order. No message displays regarding the substitution.
•
A - The same as Y but also includes transfers.
•
N - When an item is backordered, the Substitute Product? message displays to allow you to choose to use a substitute.
6
For Product Substitution Comment 1, enter the first line of the comment to add to the order when a product is substituted.
7
For Product Substitution Comment 2, enter the second line of the comment to add to the order when a product is substituted.
8
For Substitute Product#, enter valid product numbers to use as substitutes for the current product. If you enter multiple products, they are checked for availability in the order in which they are entered.
Equivalent Products
If you have a nonstock product that can be used as a substitute product, you can set it up as an equivalent product. To enter equivalent products, press F5-Equivalent to open the Equivalents screen.
If the equivalent later becomes a stock product, it shows on the Substitutes screen as a substitute item.
9
Press F2-Save to save the substitute information. You must press F2-Save twice to save the product information.
Shipping a Substitute Product
If a substitute product was shipped rather than the product shown on the picking ticket, you can enter the substitute product when you ship the order in Release Confirmation Entry.
If the product you enter is not a substitute product for the original product, you can choose to either create the product as a valid substitute or to return to the line item and enter another substitute product. The original product’s pricing is used.
Substitutes are not available for the following types of line items when you confirm a shipment:
•
Comment lines.
•
Kit parent items.
•
Serial numbers.
•
Lots.
•
Sales order linked to a purchase order.
•
Sales order linked to a transfer.
•
Sales order linked to a work order.
•
Rental order.
•
Job accounting order.
•
Core linked to core exchange.
•
Core exchange linked to core.
•
Surcharge item.
