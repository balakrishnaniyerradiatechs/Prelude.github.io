Warehouse Product Notes Screen
You can use the Warehouse Product Notes screen to enter notes about this product / warehouse and determine which documents display these notes.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Optns > Product Notes
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Notes
The notes to display when you enter the product number on a purchase order or run the Recommended Purchase Order Action Report.
Doc
The documents on which the notes appear:
•
A - Replenishment Action Report.
•
P - Purchase orders.
•
C - Both purchase orders and the Recommended Action Report.
•
T - Recommended Stock Transfer Report for the To warehouse.
•
B - Recommended Stock Transfer Report .
•
V - Vendor request for quotes.
•
F - Both vendor request for quotes and purchase orders.
Type
The comment type:
•
C - Prints on all sales-related documents.
•
CN - Does not print on external documents.
•
CB - Same as C but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CP - Prints on pick ticket.
•
CPB - Same as CP but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CI - Prints on invoice.
•
CIB - Same as CI but remains after the backorder cycle.
•
CPO - Prints on sales documents and purchase orders.
•
CIPO - Prints on linked purchase order and invoice.
•
CNPO - Same as CPO but does not print.
•
CPPO - Prints on purchase order and pick ticket but not the invoice.
F2-Save
Saves the warehouse product notes information.
