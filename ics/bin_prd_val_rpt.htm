Bin Product Valuation Report
The Bin Product Valuation report displays the extended average cost of each product in a bin and the total cost for the combined quantities in a bin.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Bin Product Valuation Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse containing the bins.
•
Bin Number Range. The beginning and ending bins to view. Leave blank to view all bins for the warehouse.
•
Bin Type. The bin type to include. Leave blank to include all bin types or choose from the following:
-
B - Bulk.
-
C - Crib.
-
D - Receiving dock.
-
I - Inspection.
-
K - Bulk un-packaged.
-
L - Work in process line stock.
-
O - On-the-water.
-
P - Pick.
-
R - Replenishment repackaging.
-
S - Staging.
-
T - Retail.
-
U - Unallocated.
-
W - Work in process.
•
Storage Type. Indicates how the products in this bin are stored. Leave blank to include all storage types or choose from the following:
-
P - Pallet racks.
-
R - Flow racks.
-
C - Carousel.
-
F - Floor stock.
-
S - Shelf.
-
M - Mobile.
-
W - Work center.
-
T - Truck.
-
O - Outplant.
-
G - Pre-stage.
•
Product List Sort (P/Null). Indicates how the products are sorted:
-
P - Sorts the products in each bin in alphanumeric order.
-
Blank - Sorts the products in the order they were added to the bin.
•
Sort By Zone.
-
Y - Sorts, break, and subtotal the products by zone.
-
N - Do not sort by zone.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Bin Location
The bin number.
Product Number
The products in the corresponding bin.
Description
The product description.
Bin OH
The quantity of the product in the bin.
Bin Cmt
The quantity of the product in the bin that is committed to sales orders, work orders, transfers, and vendor claims.
Bin Avl
The quantity of the product in the bin that is available.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure.
Avg Cost
The average unit cost for the product.
Ext Avg Cost
The cost for the total on-hand quantity of the product in the bin (Bin OH x Avg Cost).
