Adjusting Cost
When inventory cost discrepancies occur for a warehouse, you can manually adjust the cost in Cost Adjustment Entry. When you adjust the cost of a product in inventory, the cost that is changed depends on whether your company uses standard cost, average cost, or FIFO cost (G/L Cost flag in Company Maintenance). If you use FIFO cost, any cost adjustment affects the lot number and serial number layers of cost.
Before you can adjust inventory cost, you must set up reason codes to identify the reason for the adjustment. The Average Cost Adjustment Inquiry allows you to view all cost adjustments for a warehouse. Refer to the following sections for information on cost adjustments:
