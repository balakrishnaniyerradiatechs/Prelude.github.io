Product Maintenance
Each product in your warehouse must be established in your system so that you can enter it on sales orders, purchase orders, work orders, transfers and other transactions. You can set up a product and then use the same basic information to create the product in other warehouses while accommodating the unique characteristics of each warehouse and vendor. Every product record interacts with all Prelude modules so that data (e.g., usage, pricing, cross references) is accurately updated.
