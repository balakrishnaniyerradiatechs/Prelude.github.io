Requiring Quality Control Inspections in Receiving
If your warehouse requires an inspection for products received into your warehouse, you can use the Quality Control Inspection procedures to define inspection requirements. Maintenance flags determine which products require an inspection and who is authorized to release the items into inventory. You can also print receiving inspection labels and reports for verification.
When items that require inspections arrive in your warehouse, you can receive them as usual (refer to Receiving Stock). The received quantity is in on-hand inventory but is not available to sell until released by an inspection manager in the Quality Control Inspection Inquiry.
Quality Inspection Subsystem
Prelude also provides the Quality Inspection Subsystem which allows you to record feedback, incidents, corrective actions, and inspection results for a complete quality control system. Refer to the Quality Inspection Subsystem documentation for more information.
