Entering Serial Numbers for Products in Inventory
Serial Number Entry allows you to enter serial numbers for products with on-hand quantities that exist on open sales orders, purchase orders, work orders, and transfers.
You can enter a serial number for a product if it is:
•
In On-Order status on an open stock transfer.
•
In Entered status on an open work order.
•
On an open sales order, not yet confirmed for invoicing.
•
On an open purchase order.
To enter serial numbers:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Serial Number Entry to open the Serial Number Entry screen.
2
Enter the Product that requires the serial number. The warehouses that stock this item display along with the Onhand quantity for each warehouse.
3
Press F5-Serial Numbers to enter the serial numbers for the product for each warehouse that has an on-hand quantity. The number of serial numbers you enter must equal the Onhand quantity for the selected warehouse.
4
Press F2-Save to save the serial number information.
The Serial# Flag in Product Maintenance > F5 > Add Info is automatically set to Y for each product that has a serial number entered.
