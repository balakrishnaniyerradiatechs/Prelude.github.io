Setting up Equipment Option Groups
Option groups identify a type of equipment that may have optional features. Different types of equipment may have unique groups of options. For example, a lawn mower may have different optional features than a snowblower. You would want to group the options so that when you buy a lawn mower, you only have to choose from the features available for a lawn mower.
First you must identify the available options (refer to Setting up Equipment Option Codes), then you can assign the options to a group in Equipment Option Group Maintenance.
To assign equipment option codes to option groups:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Equipment Option Group Maintenance to open the Equipment Option Group Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Option Group to assign to a group of equipment option codes. This may be the type of equipment.
3
Enter the Description of the option group (e.g., tractor, lawn mower).
4
Enter the Option Codes that belong to this option group. Refer to Setting up Equipment Option Codes.
5
Press F2-Save to save the option group information.
Once option groups are established, you can assign the Option Group to a product in the Product Maintenance Additional Information Screen. When you purchase the product, you are prompted to add the available option codes that are part of this group. When you receive the product, you can confirm the equipment option codes that came with the piece of equipment. The option information for each product you receive is stored in the product serial number record. Refer to the Serial Number Inquiries.
