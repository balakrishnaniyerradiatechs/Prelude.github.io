Inventory YTD Value Change Report
The Inventory YTD Value Change report displays the total change in inventory value between the beginning of the year and now.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Inventory Valuation Reports > Inventory YTD Value Change
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Beg-Inv/
Beg-Cost
Line 1: The quantity on-hand at the beginning of the year.
Line 2: The cost of the product at the beginning of the year.
On-Hand/
Avg-Cost
Line 1: The current on-hand balance.
Line 2: The current average cost of the item.
Un-Chng/
Cost-Chng
Line 1: The change in the number of units on-hand between the beginning of the year and now.
Line 2: The change in cost of the product between the beginning of the year and now.
Beg-Value
The total extended value of the product at the beginning of the year (Beg-Inv x Beg Cost).
Cur-Value
The total extended value of the product currently on-hand (On-Hand x Avg-Cost).
Chng-Value
The total extended change in value of the product from the beginning of the year to now (Cur-Value - Beg-Value).
