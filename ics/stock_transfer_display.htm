Stock Transfer Display screen
The Stock Transfer Display screen displays detail product and quantity information for a stock transfer.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer Number
The stock transfer number or release number.
Shipment Date
The date the product was shipped in either Transfer Shipments Entry or Transfer Serial Shipping Container Entry.
From
The branch that shipped the material.
To
The branch that received the material.
Product
The product numbers on the transfer.
Description
The description of the product.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist in either the header or the line item detail of the transfer.
Linkage S
The document number of the source of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - The product was purchased on this purchase order at the shipping warehouse.
•
T - The product was ordered on this transfer at the shipping warehouse.
Linkage D
The document number for the destination of the product, if it is linked to another transaction. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this sales order number.
•
T - When the product arrives, it will be shipped out on this transfer number.
•
W - When the product arrives, it will be allocated to this work order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity not yet received at the receiving warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the transfer.
F
Indicates if the transfer is a future:
•
F - A future transfer. The pick ticket will print on the future date entered on the transfer.
•
Blank - Not a future transfer.
Qty-Pck
The quantity available to ship.
Qty-B/O
The quantity on backorder at the shipping warehouse.
S
The supply code that appears if the product is on backorder. The code varies depending on the product’s replenishment path and product settings. Possible supply codes include:
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
R - Emergency transfer.
•
W - Work order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped from the shipping warehouse.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Emg
Indicates if the transfer was entered as an emergency:
•
Y - The product was marked as an emergency when it was entered on the transfer (Emg in the Transfer Entry Detail screen).
•
N or Blank - The product was not entered as an emergency on the transfer.
Customer Product #
The customer’s part number for this product number, if applicable.
Cube
The cubic volume of the products already shipped on this transfer.
Weight
The total weight of the products already shipped on this transfer.
Cost
The total cost of the products already shipped on this transfer.
F2-Finish
Closes the display screen.
F5-Header
Displays the Stock Transfer Header screen.
Action Bar
Examine Line
Displays cost, source, and linkage information for the highlighted item.
Notes
Allows you to view any header or line item notes for the highlighted item.
SSCI
Opens the Serial Shipping Container Inquiry screen to allow you to view SSCI numbers and shipping information for the transfer.
