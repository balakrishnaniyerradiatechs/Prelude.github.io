Entering a Negative Receiver
If stock is over-received, you can enter a negative receiver to remove the stock from inventory. If the item is linked to a sales order, work order, or transfer, you must re-link the item to the purchase order.
To enter a negative receiver:
1
Follow the steps for Receiving Stock, except enter the original purchase order number in P/O Number. The Vendor Number from the purchase order displays.
2
Depending on whether any line items are open on the purchase order, one of the following occurs after you enter Received By:
•
If the purchase order is completely received, the Purchase Order Header screen appears to allow you to verify the vendor name and address information. Change as necessary and press F2-Save to open the Stock Receipts Entry screen.
•
If open items remain on the purchase order, the Stock Receipts Entry screen appears.
3
On a blank line, enter the over-received product number. The Detail Information screen appears.
4
For Qty-Opn and Qty-Rec, enter a negative value for the quantity received in error.
5
Press ENTER. The Receiver Credit Information screen appears.
6
Enter the Original Receiver# that contains the product with the quantity received in error.
7
Press ENTER. One of the following occurs:
•
If the item was linked to a sales order, work order, or transfer, the following message appears:
Choose Continue and complete the receiver. The quantity is removed from on-hand inventory and placed back on the purchase order. You can go back into Purchase Order Entry to re-link the item to the sales order.
This Original Receiver Line Item Was Linked To Document S-100093. You Will Need To
Re-link The Document When You Complete This Negative Receiver!
•
If the item is not linked, the following message appears:
Choose from the following:
Create As An Open Line Item On This P/O?
-
N - The quantity is removed from on-hand inventory and deleted from the purchase order.
-
Y - The quantity is removed from on-hand inventory and the item remains as an open line on the purchase order. Use this option if the vendor is still going to ship the material.
8
Repeat steps 3-7 to enter any additional negative quantities.
9
Press F2-Save to complete the receiver and update inventory.
The Auto Unallocate Flag in Inventory Parameters must be set to Y so that inventory is unallocated when the on-hand inventory is decreased by a negative receiver.
