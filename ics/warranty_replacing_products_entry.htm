Warranty Replacing Products Entry screen
The Warranty Replacing Products Entry screen allows you to enter the replacement products sent from the vendor to replace the items returned on a vendor claim.
Menu Path: Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Warranty Replacement Entry > F6-Replacing Products
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Entry#
The warranty replacement number assigned in Warranty Replacement Entry.
Vendor
The vendor that replaced the merchandise.
Product
The products replaced by the vendor for the vendor claim.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse to receive the replaced products.
Quantity
The quantity the vendor sent to replace the products.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the vendor claim.
Replaced Qty
The total number of units replaced by the vendor.
Replaced Dollars
The total value the products replaced by the vendor for the vendor claim.
Replacing Qty
The total number of units sent from the vendor to replace the items.
Replacing Dollars
The total value of the products sent from the vendor to replace the items.
F2-Save
Saves the replacing products information and returns to the Warranty Replacement Entry screen.
F5-Load From Replaced Entry
Allows you to copy the products from the Warranty Replaced Products Entry screen, which are the products that were returned to the vendor. You can change any quantities if necessary.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays the vendor product number.
Review
Displays detail information from the vendor claim.
