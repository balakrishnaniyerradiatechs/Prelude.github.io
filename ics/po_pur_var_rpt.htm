Purchased Price Variation Report
The Purchased Price Variation Report report displays products with a received cost that differs from the current average cost. You can enter a variance percent to limit the report to products with a significant cost variance.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Purchased Price Variation Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Beginning and Ending Received Date Range. The receipt date range to include in the cost variance calculations.
•
Variance Percent. The minimum cost variation percent to include. If the variance percentage (positive or negative) between the received cost and the actual cost for the product is greater than this amount, it is included on the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
Line 1: The product number with the cost variance.
Line 2: The product description.
Rec Qty
The quantity received within the selected dates.
PurAvgCst
The average receipt cost of all receipts of the product in the selected date range.
CurAvgCst
The average cost of the product from this vendor in this warehouse.
Var Amt
The difference between the PurAvgCst and the CurAvg/Cst.
Var %
The percentage difference between the PurAvgCst and the CurAvgCst.
Usage
The current monthly usage of the product.
