Override Lead Times screen
The Override Lead Times screen allows you to override the system- calculated lead times for a product/vendor/warehouse combination.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Vendor Maintenance > F7-Override Lead Times
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Last Lead Time
The last lead time of the product for this product/vendor warehouse.
Next-Last Lead Time
The lead time before the Last Lead Time for this product/vendor/warehouse.
Average Lead Time
The calculated average lead time for this product/vendor/warehouse.
F2-Save
Saves the override lead time information.
