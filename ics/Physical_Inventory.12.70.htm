Adjusting Inventory for a Linked Nonstock Item
To adjust inventory for linked and asterisk items:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Linked Procedure > Physical Linked Entry to open the Physical Inventory Linked Entry screen.
2
For Document #, press ENTER over NEW to create a new entry or enter an existing document number to edit.
3
For Entered Date, enter the date to post the adjustment.
4
Enter the Product to adjust.
5
Enter the Warehouse number for the linked item. The on-hand and linked document information displays.
6
Adjust the Linked Qty and the Qty To Stk for each for each document as necessary. If the Onhand quantity is less than the quantity linked to orders, you must determine which orders to link to the product quantity available. The linked quantity cannot be more than the counted quantity.
7
Once all the linked information is correct, press F2-Save to update the information. The following message displays.
Do You Wish To Continue With Update? General Ledger Impact Will Be Made!
8
Choose from the following:
•
No - Allows you to edit the linked inventory information.
•
Yes - Updates the linked inventory information to the general ledger and completes linkage entry.
