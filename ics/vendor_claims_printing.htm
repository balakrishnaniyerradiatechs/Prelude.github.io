Vendor Claims Printing screen
The Vendor Claims Printing screen allows you to batch email, fax, or print vendor claims to the vendor. A printed claim can also provide paperwork to ship the material back to the vendor.
Menu Path: Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claims Printing
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor Claim Number
The vendor claim number to print.
Warehouse
The warehouse locations to include. Leave blank to include all.
Claim Type
The type of vendor claims to include:
•
01- Regular.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory.
•
04 - Non inventory expense.
Printer Form Queue
The printer number where the claims are to print.
F5-Vendor Claim Reprint
Opens the Vendor Claim Reprint screen to allow you to reprint a vendor claim.
F6-Updated Vendor Claim Reprint
Opens the Updated Vendor Claim reprint screen to allow you to reprint a vendor claim that has already been updated. Refer to Updating Vendor Claims.
