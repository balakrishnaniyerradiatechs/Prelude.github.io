Clearing the RF Count File
After you enter product quantities, verify counts, run the necessary reports, and update inventory, you must clear and re-set the temporary RF inventory file before the next physical inventory.
To clear and re-set the RF inventory file:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Radio Frequency Procedure > Physical RF Clear Procedure to open the Physical RF Clear screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse containing the RF inventory file to clear.
3
Press ENTER. The following message displays:
Warning! This Procedure Will Clear out all
Physical Inventory RF Records! Do you wish to Continue?
4
Choose from the following:
•
No - Cancels the procedure.
•
Yes - Clears and re-sets the RF inventory count.
Do not run the Physical RF Clear Procedure until after your physical inventory is complete or all the count information will be lost.
