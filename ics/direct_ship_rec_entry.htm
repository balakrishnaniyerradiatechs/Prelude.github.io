Direct Ship Receipt Entry screen
The Direct Ship Receipt Entry screen allows you to receive a purchase order that was shipped directly to the customer.
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipts > Direct Ship Receipt Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The direct ship purchase order number.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Receipt Date
The date the vendor shipped the merchandise.
Received By
The user ID of the person entering the receiver.
Product Number
The product received by the customer.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse where the order was entered.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist on the purchase order.
Linkage
The direct ship sales order that is linked to the purchase order.
Quantity
The quantity entered on the order.
U/M
The unit of measure as entered on the sales order.
Received
The quantity received by the customer as entered in the detail information screen.
Weight
The total weight of the product that shipped.
Units
The total number of units that shipped.
P/O Amount
The total dollar amount entered on the purchase order.
Recv Amount
The total dollar amount of the quantity received by the customer.
F2-Save
Saves the direct ship receipt information.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen to allow you to view or edit information on the purchase order header.
Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
