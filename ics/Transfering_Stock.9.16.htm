Transfer Shipments Entry
Transfer Shipments Entry confirms the shipped quantities on a transfer and relieves the on-hand quantity at the shipping warehouse.
Two-Step Transfer
To update inventory at the receiving branch at the time of shipment entry; refer to the Two Step Transfer Process under Establishing Transfer Options.
To confirm a transfer shipment:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Shipments Entry to open the Transfer Shipments Entry screen.
2
Enter the Transfer Release# from the pick ticket, to list products on the transfer.
3
Enter the Shipment Date. The Stock Transfer Header screen appears.
4
For Picker, enter the user ID of the person that filled the transfer.
5
For Packer, enter the user ID of the person that packaged the order.
6
For Loader, enter the user ID of the person that loaded the material.
7
For Freight Amount, enter the amount freight to bill the receiving branch, if applicable.
8
Enter the Weight, Number Of Cartons, or the Waybill Number if necessary.
9
If necessary, you can change the following header information:
•
To enter the freight amount, select F5-Freight Detail to open the Transfer Freight Detail Info screen.
•
To enter or change a ship-to address, select F7-Ship-To to open the Manual Ship-To Entry screen.
10
Press F2 -Save to save the header information and return to the Transfer Shipments Entry screen.
11
To change any of the items or quantities, highlight the product number and press ENTER to open the Transfer Shipment Detail screen.
If you are shipping a warranty transfer, you cannot access the Transfer Shipment Detail screen. Refer to Transferring Warranty Inventory.
12
If necessary, change the Qty Opn, the Shp Qty, or the B/O Qty to reflect the amount shipped or to cancel any backorders.
13
Press F2-Save to go back to the Transfer Shipments Entry screen.
14
Once all the shipped quantities are correct, press F2-Save to complete the shipment. The following prompt appears:
Do You Wish To ...
15
Choose from the following:
•
Update: Completes the transaction and relieves inventory at the shipping location. Depending on how the Transfer Freight Prompt flag is set (refer to Establishing Transfer Options), the Freight Closeout screen may appear to allow you to enter the Freight Amount.
•
Suspend: Suspends the transaction. Inventory is not updated and the transfer cannot be received. You can access and update it at a later time.
•
Return: Allows you to return to the line items.
