Delivery Log Appointment Report
The Delivery Log Appointment Report lists delivery appointments scheduled in Delivery Log Entry (refer to Scheduling Deliveries).
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Delivery Log Appointment Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received the material.
•
Estimate Date Range. The beginning and ending estimated receipts dates.
•
Status. The status of the delivery:
-
A - Arrived.
-
C - Cancelled.
-
L - Late.
-
R - Received.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include.
•
Carrier. The shipping methods to include.
•
Contact. The purchasing contacts to include.
•
Document Type. The document types to include.
-
P - Purchase order.
-
T - Transfer.
-
M - Miscellaneous delivery.
•
Document Number. The purchase order or transfer numbers to include.
•
Appointment number. The appointment numbers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Est-date
The estimated receipt date.
Time
The estimated receipt time.
Appt
The appointment number assigned by the system in Delivery Log Entry
Carrier
The carrier that is delivering the material.
Contact
The contact for the delivery.
Phone
The contact’s phone number.
Vendor
The vendor associated with the delivery.
Ctn
The number of cartons included in the delivery.
Plt
The number of pallets included in the delivery.
Document#
The purchase order or transfer number included in the delivery.
