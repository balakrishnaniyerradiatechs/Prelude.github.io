Upsell Product Maintenance screen
The Upsell Product Maintenance screen allows you to enter upsell products to offer when a product is entered on a sales order. An upsell product is a more expensive product or list of products that you may want to offer when a customer asks for an item. When you enter the original product on a sales order, a dialog box appears to inform you that the product has upsell products to offer. You can review the products and replace the original product if necessary. Refer to Upsell Products for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Upsells
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Number
The upsell product number to offer when a salesperson enters a sales order for the original part number. The upsell product must be a valid product number.
Description
The description of the upsell product.
Script
Indicates if notes exist for the upsell product number. These notes display when the salesperson chooses to review an upsell product on a sales order:
•
* - Notes exist for the upsell product
•
N - No notes exist for the upsell product.
•
Y - Opens the General Notes Entry screen to allow you to enter notes for the upsell product.
F2-Save
Saves the upsell product information.
