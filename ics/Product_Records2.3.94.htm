Assigning Parker Codes to Product Lines
Once you create the Parker division, marketing, and product codes, you can assign them to product lines in Product Line Maintenance.
To assign Parker reporting codes to a product line:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line to open the Product Line Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product Line number.
3
Select Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Parker Information to open the Parker Information Screen.
4
Enter the Parker Division Code to use for the product line. Refer to Setting Up Parker Divisions.
5
Enter the Parker Marketing Code to use for the product line. Refer to Setting Up Parker Marketing Codes.
6
Enter the Parker Product Code to assign to this product line. Refer to Assigning Product Codes to Parker Divisions.
7
Press F2-Save to save the Parker information.
These Parker codes can be overridden by product in Product Maintenance.
