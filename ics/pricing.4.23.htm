Using Company Vendor Cost
If you want to maintain the replacement cost of an item for a product from a vendor for the whole company rather than for just one warehouse, you can access company vendor replacement cost directly in Company Vendor Cost Maintenance. Refer to Implementing the Price/Cost Hierarchy for information on how the system determines which cost to use.
Company Vendor cost works effectively when you have different costs from different vendors, but the costs are still the same for all warehouses.

Company Vendor Cost Listing
To view company vendor cost for a product and/or vendor, select Inventory > Listings > Company Vendor Cost Listing.
