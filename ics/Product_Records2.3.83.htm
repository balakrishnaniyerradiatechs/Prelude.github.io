Production Control Groups
If you use work orders to manufacture products, you can use Production Control Product Maintenance to enter a description and manufacturing instructions to assign to products in the same production group. These codes are assigned to products in the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen (Replenishment Mtd is set to M).
Production Control Product Group Listing
To view a list of product group descriptions and instructions, select Inventory > Listings > Miscellaneous Listings > Production Control Product Group Listing.
To set up descriptions and instructions for production groups:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Production Control Product Maintenance to open the Production Control Product Maintenance screen.
2
For Product Group, enter a code to represent a group of products.
3
Enter the Description of the production group.
4
Enter Instructions for the production group to print on the work order pick tickets and the Open Work Order Report.
5
Press F2-Save to save the production group information.
To assign a production group to a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Purch Params Maintenance to open the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product and the Warehouse.
3
For Replenishment Mtd, enter M to open the Production Control Parameters screen.
4
Enter the Production Group.
5
Press F2-Save.
