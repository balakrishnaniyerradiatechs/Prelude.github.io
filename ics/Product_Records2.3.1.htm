Copying Products
Once a product exists in Product Maintenance, you can copy the information to create a new product without having to re-enter all of the detail product information. You can also copy a product, or all products, from one warehouse to one or more other warehouses.
Refer to the following three methods for copying products:
•
Copying a Product to Create a New Product.
•
Copying a Product to a New Warehouse.
•
Copying All Products to a New Warehouse.
