Catalog Product Lines
If you want a product to appear in multiple sections of a catalog, you can assign multiple product lines to a product. These product lines are for catalog purposes only. For example, you may want sandpaper to show up under abrasives and under accessories in addition to displaying for its own product line.
To set up catalog product lines for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product that you want to appear in multiple sections of your catalog.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Catalog Product Line to open the Catalog Product Lines screen.
5
For Pline#, enter an additional product line number to assign to this product for the catalog. The product line description displays.
6
For Sort Seq, enter the sequence number for this product to determine where in the catalog, within the product line, the product should print.
7
Repeat steps 5 and 6 to assign additional product lines.
8
Press F2-Save to save the catalog product line information.
