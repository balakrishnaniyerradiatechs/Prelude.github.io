Entering Counts on RF Devices
After you build the inventory lists and save the on-hand quantities, you can count inventory using an RF gun.
To enter counts with an RF gun:
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Radio Frequency Procedure > Physical RF Entry to open the Physical RF Entry screen.
2
Scan the product barcode. The part number appears.
3
Enter the Bin for the product.
4
For Qty, enter the quantity of the product in this bin.
5
Press F2-Save to save the count information.
Continue to enter counts for each item in each bin, then run the Physical Inventory Radio Frequency Count Report to verify the counts.
