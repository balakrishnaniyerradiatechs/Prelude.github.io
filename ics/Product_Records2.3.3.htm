Copying a Product to a New Warehouse
The copy feature in Product Warehouse Maintenance allows you to duplicate a product for another warehouse without having to re-enter most of the detail information. This feature is helpful if you have a product in one warehouse and you want the same general information to exist for the product in other warehouse(s). Once the product is copied to the new warehouse, you can use Product Warehouse Maintenance to adjust any detail information. You can copy products in both the inventory and the catalog files.
To copy a product warehouse record:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance to access the Product Warehouse Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product and the Warehouse of the product to copy.
3
Select Copy from the Action Bar to open the Copy Warehouse Utility screen.
4
The Copy From Whse defaults to the current warehouse.
5
Enter the Copy To Whse, which is the warehouse to receive the new product information. You can enter one or more warehouses or enter ALL to copy the information to all warehouses.
6
Press F2-Save. The following prompt appears:
Copying Product 03275 from warehouse 10 to 20
7
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - If the From product resides in the inventory file or in the catalog file, the new product is created in the same file for the To warehouse(s).
•
No - The product is not copied to the new warehouses.
If a product’s purchasing parameters has Single Customer set to Y in the Purchase Parameters Additional Information Screen, when the product is copied to the new warehouse, Single Customer will be set to N in the new warehouse. The Y setting indicates that usage is dominated by one customer.
