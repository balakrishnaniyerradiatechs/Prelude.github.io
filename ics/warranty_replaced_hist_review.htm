Warranty Replaced History Review screen
The Warranty Replaced History Review screen allows you to review the detail information for products on vendor claims that were replaced by the vendor.
Menu Path: Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Warranty Replacement History Inquiry > F5-Review W/R > F5-Replaced Products
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Entry#
The warranty replacement number assigned in Warranty Replacement Entry.
Vendor Number
The vendor that replaced the merchandise.
Product Number
The products returned to the vendor on a vendor claim.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse that returned the products to the vendor.
Qty-Opn
The quantity of the product returned on the vendor claim.
Qty-Rpld
The quantity expected to be replaced by the vendor.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the vendor claim.
Cost
The unit cost of the product as entered on the vendor claim.
Extension
The total value of the product returned to the vendor (Qty-Opn x Cost).
Vendor Product#
The vendor’s product number, if applicable.
Tag Number
The warranty tag number if the product was put into warranty inventory using a tag number. Refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory.
Vendor RMA#
The vendor’s RMA number if one was entered on the vendor claim.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
