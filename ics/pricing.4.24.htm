Setting Up Company Vendor Cost
To maintain the cost for a product at the company/vendor level:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Company Vendor Cost Maintenance to open the Company Vendor Cost Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product for which you want to change the company vendor cost.
3
Enter the Vendor associated with the product.
4
Enter the Weight of the product.
5
Enter the Unit Cost, which is the product/vendor level replacement cost that applies only to purchases from this vendor. This cost overrides the cost at the product level (Product Pricing Screen) and the product warehouse level (Warehouse Pricing Screen). Depending on how the Replacement Cost Update flag is set in Inventory Parameters, you can also change replacement cost in Purchase Order Entry and/or Stock Receipts Entry.
6
To enter a future cost, enter a New Unit Cost and the effective date for the new cost in the next (unlabeled) field.
7
Enter the Direct Ship Cost if you want to define a separate cost for direct ship purchase orders from this vendor.
8
Press F2-Save to save the company vendor cost.
To enter vendor cost discounts based on total price, quantity, or weight, refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices.
Vendor Maint Audit in File Update Parameters determines if unit cost, replacement cost, and direct ship cost are tracked in the system.
