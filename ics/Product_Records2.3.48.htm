Replace a Product Universally
If a vendor changes a part number or if a part number was incorrectly created, you may want to update all occurrences of the part number in your system with the new number. If you use the Universal Product Replacement Entry to replace a part number, any time the old part number is entered on any transaction, the new part number automatically appears. The first and second line of description also display for the new part number. Inventory Inquiry displays the old part number as an alternate for the new part number.
To replace a product with a new number:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Universal Product Replacement & Merge > Universal Product Replacement Entry to open the Universal Product Replacement Entry screen.
2
Enter the Old Product, which is the number being replaced.
3
Enter the New Product, which is the new number to replace the old part number.
4
Press F2-Save to save the information. To complete the replacement, run the Universal Product Replacement/Merge Update (refer to Update Replaced/Merged Products).
