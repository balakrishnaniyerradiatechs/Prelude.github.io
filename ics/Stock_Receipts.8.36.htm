Printing License Plate Labels
If you do not continuously print license plate labels (refer to Setting Up License Plate Products), you can print labels individually in License Plate Standalone Label Entry.
To manually print license plate labels:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > License Plate > License Plate Standalone Label Entry to open the License Plate Standalone Label Entry screen.
2
Enter the License Plate# or press F3 to search by product number. The product information displays.
3
For Continue (Y/N), enter Y to print the license plate label.
