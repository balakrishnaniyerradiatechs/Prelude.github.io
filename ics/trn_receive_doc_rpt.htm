Stock Transfer Receiving Report
You can use the Stock Transfer Receiving report to receive transfers that arrive without a packing slip. The report lists products on transfers (including bins) and provides a space for you to write in the received quantities.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movement Reports > Stock Transfer Receiving Report
Generating the Stock Transfer Receiving Report
To create a transfer receiving report:
1
For Transfer#, enter the transfer release number to receive. Press F3 to search for transfers by vendor. You can enter multiple transfers on one receiving report.
2
For Sort By Bin/Product, indicate if you want to sort the report by bin or product:
-
B - Sorts by primary bin within each transfer. If a product has no bin number, it appears at the beginning of the list.
-
P - Sorts by product.
-
N - Do not sort,. List the products in the order they were entered on the transfer.
3
Press F2-Create to generate the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Transfer#
The transfer release number received.
Trn Date
The date the transfer was entered.
From
The warehouse that shipped the material.
Lne
The line number for the received product.
Product/Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Bins
The bin number.
Qty-Ship
The quantity shipped according to the shipping warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
HM
A asterisk (*) in this column indicates the product is classified as hazardous material. Refer to Hazardous Material Class.
Qty-Rec
A blank line to allow you to enter the verified received quantity.
