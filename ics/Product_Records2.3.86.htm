NMFC Codes
The National Motor Freight Classifications (NMFC) are used to compare transportation cost for commodities. Commodities are grouped into 18 classes (50-500) based on 4 transportation characteristics: Density, stowability, handling, and liability. Once you set up an NMFC, you can assign it to a product in Product Maintenance.
This information is used with the freight class of a product for billing purposes (refer to Freight Class for information on setting up the freight class).
To set up an NMFC code:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Freight > NMFC Code to open the NMFC Maintenance screen.
2
For NMFC Code, enter the National Motor Freight Classification that describes what material is and how easy it is to transport.
3
Enter a Description of the code.
4
Press F2-Save to save the NMFC information.
To assign an NMFC to individual products:
1
Press Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Select F5-Add Info > F8-Freight to open the Freight Information Screen.
4
Enter the NMFC Code NMFC Maintenance.
5
Press F2-Save three times to save the NMFC information.
