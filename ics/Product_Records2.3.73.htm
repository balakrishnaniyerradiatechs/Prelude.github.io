Classifying Products
Product classifications are based on the total value of a product that moves through inventory each month. The last 12 months of usage and the cost of each stock item calculate a usage value. Classifications range from 1-13; class 1 is assigned to items with the greatest turns and value and class 13 is assigned to items with no sales (dead stock). You can set purchase parameters to determine which products are assigned to each class. You can re-classify products manually or as part of the End of Month Calendar Close.
