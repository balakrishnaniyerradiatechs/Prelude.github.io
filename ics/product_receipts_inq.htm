Product Receipts Inquiry
The Product Receipts Inquiry displays the receivers for a product for a warehouse. You can also view the purchase order information for each receiver.
Viewing the Product Receipts Inquiry screen
To view the receipt history for an item:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Stock Receipt Inquiries > Product Receipts History to open the Product Receipts Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
Receivers for the product/warehouse combination display.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field / Function
Description
Product
The product number. The product description displays to the right.
Warehouse
The warehouse number. The warehouse description displays to the right.
P/O#
The receiver number that the item was received on.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exists for the receiver.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The vendor description.
Rec Date
The date the material was received into the warehouse.
Qty-Opn
The quantity on the purchase order before the receiver was entered.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the purchase order.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received on this receiver.
Stk-Qty
The quantity put into stock based on the stocking unit of measure. For example, if the quantity received was 10 and the stocking unit of measure is DZ (dozen); the quantity received is 10 and the quantity in stock is 120. Refer to Establishing a Unit of Measure for more information on unit of measure.
Net Cost
The cost for each unit of measure for the item.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Purchase Order History Display screen to allow you to review the purchase order.
F8 - PO Log
Opens the Purchase Order Log Inquiry to display purchase orders for the selected product.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Allows you to view the previous page if multiple pages of transfers are listed.
sF6-Next Group
Allows you to view the next page if multiple pages of transfers are listed.
