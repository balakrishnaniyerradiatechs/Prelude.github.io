Defining Automobile Makes
For each automobile manufacturer, you can set up every configuration of make, model, and year that you sell products for. You can then assign the appropriate make/model/ year information to each product to help search for a product by vehicle.
To create an automotive make:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance >Product Maintenance > Automotive Make Maintenance to open the Automotive Make Maintenance screen.
2
For Make, enter the automobile make or manufacturer name.
3
For Make Abbreviation, enter an abbreviation to use for this automobile manufacturer.
4
For Model, enter the car models available from the manufacturer.
5
For Abbr, enter an abbreviation to represent the model.
6
For BgYr, enter the first year this model was manufactured.
7
For EnYr, enter the last year this model was manufactured. If the car manufacturer skipped any years in producing this model, you must enter a new line for each date range associated with the model.
8
Press F2-Save to save the automobile make information.
