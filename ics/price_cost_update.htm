Price / Cost Update screen
The Price / Cost Update screen allows you to enter the new prices and costs for a product number. Only the fields you selected to update in the Price / Cost Entry screen display.
Menu Path: Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Cost Changes by Lists > Entry and Update > F2-Save
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Input Data
The type of data you selected to update (e.g., product or product warehouse, current prices or future prices).
Product Number
The first product number on the list to update.
Selling U/M
The selling unit of measure for the product.
Effective Date
If updating future prices, the date the prices go into effect.
List
The new list price for the product.
Price 1
The new column 1 price for the product.
Price 2
The new column 2 price for the product.
Price 3
The new column 3 price for the product.
Price 4
The new column 4 price for the product.
Price 5
The new column 5 price for the product.
Repl Cost
The new replacement cost for the product.
Std Cost
The new standard cost for the product.
Cost/Sales
The new cost of sales for the product.
Dir Shp Cst
The new direct ship cost for the item.
F2-Save
Saves the new prices and costs and allows you to save the previous prices.
