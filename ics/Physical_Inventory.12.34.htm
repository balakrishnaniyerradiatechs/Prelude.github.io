Entering Counts From Tags for Lot Products
If you enter a lot product in the Physical Count Sheet Entry screen (refer to Entering Physical Inventory Counts from Tags), the Physical Tag Lot Entry screen displays.
1
Enter the Lot Number for the product. You can create a new lot number if necessary.
2
Enter the Count associated with the lot number.
3
Continue to enter the Count for each Lot Number. The accumulated count of all the lot numbers for a product becomes the total count for the product.
4
Press F2-Finish after you enter all the counts. If the accumulated count for all the lot numbers does not equal the Count you entered for the product on the Physical Inventory Tag Entry screen, the following message displays:
Lot Onhand Quantities Do Not Equal Product Quantity. Do You Wish To Set Product Quantity To The Lot Total?
5
Choose from the following:
•
No - Allows you to adjust the counts for the lot numbers.
•
Yes - Automatically adjusts the Count on the Physical Inventory Lot Entry screen to equal the accumulated count of the lot numbers.
6
Once the total of the lot number counts equals the product count, you can continue with the next product number on the count sheet. Refer to Entering Physical Inventory Counts from Tags for a complete explanation on entering counts.
