Supercedures Information screen
The Supercedures Information screen displays product supercedure information set up in Supercedure Maintenance.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Supercedures
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Superceded
Indicates if the product is superceded by another product:
•
Y - The product is obsolete and is superceded by another product number.
•
Blank - The product is not superceded by another product number.
Superceding Product
The product number that supercedes (replaces) the part number (if Superceded is set to Y).
Supercedes Product
The product that was superceded (if the part number you are maintaining is the superceding product).
Superceded Date
The date the product was superceded.
F2-Save
Saves the supercedure information and closes the screen.
