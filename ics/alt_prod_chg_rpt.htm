UPC Number Change Report
The UPC Number Change report displays products that have a new or altered UPC (universal product code). You can use this information to re-barcode items with the new code. UPC codes are added or edited in Product Maintenance > F6-Xref > Alternate Products. Refer to Alternate Products
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > UPC Number Change Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Product Line Range. The product line range to include in the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Upc Change Date Range. The beginning and ending dates when the UPC was changed.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Pline#
The product line number assigned to the product.
Product
Line1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Chg Date
The date the UPC was changed.
Current UPC
The UPC after the change.
Previous UPC
The UPC before the change.
