Status Inquiry Screen
The Status Inquiry screen displays the inventory status, vendor information, and purchasing information for a product/warehouse combination.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance > F8-Status Inquiry
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Onhand
The current quantity on-hand in the warehouse, including allocated quantities.
Available
The current quantity available to sell in the warehouse.
Committed
The quantity committed to sales orders for the warehouse.
Onorder
The quantity on order for the warehouse.
Status Code
The status code of the product:

S - Stock. Recommended replenishment quantities are generated by Nightly Processing.

N - Nonstock. Recommended replenishment quantities only generate to fill backorders. Manual purchase orders must be linked to a sales order, work order or transfer.
â€¢
C - Consignment. The item is for consignment purposes only. Replacement cost updates the general ledger.
Primary Vendor
The primary vendor of the product as defined in Product Vendor Maintenance.
Vendor Line
The vendor line for the product as designated in Vendor Line Maintenance in the purchasing module.
Lead Time
The average number of days it takes to receive the product from the time the purchase order is entered (to the primary vendor).
Next Review
The next review date for this product calculated from the last time a recommended purchase order was converted using the review cycle.
Unit Cost
The unit cost of the item from the product/warehouse/vendor record.
Average Cost
The average cost of the item.
Last Physical
The last time a physical inventory was taken for this product.
Primary Bin Number
The primary bin number assigned to the product in Product Warehouse Maintenance.
Rank By Sales
The product ranking sales value for this product compared to all other products.
Rank By Gross Marg
The product ranking gross margin value for this product compared to all other products.
