Updating Usage for Another Product
To update usage for a product when another product is entered on a order:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product whose usage will update another productâ€™s usage.
3
Select F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Related Product to open the Related Product Information screen.
5
For Usage Product#, enter the product number whose usage you want to update each time the original product is entered on a sales order, work order, or transfer. When you move product usage, usage for the last 12 months is moved. If you remove the usage product number, the usage goes back to the original part number.
6
Press F2-Save three times to save the usage information.
Usage Audit Inquiry
When the Usage Product# is entered in the Usage Audit Inquiry, the Description column references the original part number.
The original product number displays a transaction code of PUI, indicating that usage was moved to another product.
