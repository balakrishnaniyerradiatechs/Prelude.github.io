Assigning Product Characteristic Codes to Products
Once product characteristic codes are set up in Product Characteristics Code Maintenance, you can assign the codes to a product. When you sell the product to a customer, you must enter the necessary characteristics to fill the order. The information is saved for the next time this product is sold to this customer.
To assign a product characteristic code to a product number:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Characteristics to open the Product Characteristics screen.
5
Enter the Code from Product Characteristic Code Maintenance to assign to this product number. The next time you sell this product, the Product Characteristics Information screen opens so that you can enter the information required to fill the order.
6
Press F2-Save to save the product characteristic information.
