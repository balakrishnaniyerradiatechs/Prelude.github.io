Setting Up Parker Marketing Codes
Once you set up Parker divisions, (refer to Setting Up Parker Divisions) you can enter Parker Marketing codes for each division to print on the Parker report.
To enter Parker marketing codes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Parker Reporting Codes > Parker Division/Marketing Maintenance to open the Parker Division/Marketing Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Parker Division Number (established in Parker Division Maintenance) to add the product codes to.
3
For Cde, enter the 2-digit Parker Marketing Codes to create for this division.
4
Enter a Description that identifies the type of market for each code.
5
Press F2-Save to save the Parker division/ market code information.
