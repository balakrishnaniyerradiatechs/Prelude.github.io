Customer Invoice Core Inquiry
The Customer Invoice Core Inquiry displays invoices for a customer that contain core charges.
Viewing Customer Core Invoices
To display the status of customer core invoices:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Core Inquiries > Customer Invoice Core Inquiry to view the Customer Invoice Core Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer. The Open Core Total of invoices for this customer display.
3
Choose an Invoice Status to limit the invoices to display:
•
Open Invoices.
•
Paid Invoices.
•
Both Open & Paid.
4
Choose a Display Sequence:
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Invoice Number.
•
By Invoice Date.
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Descending Due Date.
Core invoices for the customer appear.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer with the core charges.
Open Core Total
The total of open invoices for the customer.
Invoice Status
The status of the invoice:
•
Open Invoices.
•
Paid Invoices.
•
Both Open & Paid.
Display Sequence
The sort order for which you want to display the core charges:
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Invoice Number.
•
By Invoice Date.
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Descending Due Date.
Invoice#
The invoice generated for the core.
Notes
An asterisk (*) displays if notes were entered for the invoice.
Whse
The warehouse number for the invoice.
Inv Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due for payment.
Past
The number of days past due for the invoice.
IS
The invoice status:
•
B - Bankruptcy.
•
C - Collections.
•
D - Disputed.
•
L - Litigation.
•
R - Rebate.
•
W - Write Off.
Core Amount
The core charge on this invoice.
Invoice Amount
The total amount for the invoice.
Consolidated
Indicates if the invoice is consolidated:
•
Y - The invoice is consolidated. Consolidated Inv? must be set to Y in the Sales Order Header F5 > F5.
•
N - The invoice is not consolidated.
Balance Due
The remaining balance due on the invoice.
Status
The status of the invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
P - Paid.
Excluded
Indicates if the invoice is excluded from the customer’s credit limit:
•
Y - The invoice is excluded from the customer’s credit limit.
•
N - The invoice is included in the customer’s credit limit.
F5-Review Invoice
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry screen for the invoice selected.
F7-Cumulative
Displays the cumulative balance of the invoices on the screen.
F8-Credit Comments
Displays any credit comments entered for the selected invoice.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a page number, reference number, or line number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Images
Allows you to print images of related documents, if you use the imaging software.
Review Consol Hist
Displays the invoices entered in the Consolidated Invoice Printing screen. These invoices were paid off when the consolidated invoice was created.
