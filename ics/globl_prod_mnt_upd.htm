Update Products
Once you create a template of product changes (refer to Create an Update Template), you can update the products included on your list (refer to Create a Product List).
To update products globally:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Global Product Maintenance Menu > Global Product Update Process to access the Global Product Maintenance Update screen.
2
Enter the Save-List Name created in the Save-List Creation Process (refer to Create a Product List).
3
Enter the Template Name created in Template Product Maintenance Entry (refer to Create an Update Template).
4
Choose Execute. All products in the list are updated with the changes in the product template.
