Product Serial Inquiry
The Product Serial Inquiry displays the on-hand and available serial numbers for a product/warehouse combination.
Viewing Product Serial Numbers
To view serial number information for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Product/Serial Number Inquiries > Serial Number Inquiry to open the Product Serial Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product. Press F3 to search for products.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
The Onhand and Available quantity of the product display along with the listing of serial numbers entered for the product.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number. The description of the product displays.
Warehouse
The warehouse number.
Onhand
The quantity of the product on-hand for this warehouse.
Available
The quantity of the product available to sell.
Serial Numbers
The serial numbers for the products in the warehouse.
Code
Inventory status for the serialized product:
•
S - Scrapped.
•
D - Damaged.
•
W - Warranty
•
Blank - Regular inventory.
Floor Plan
Indicates if this product/serial number has a floor plan established:
•
Y - A floor plan exists for the product. A floor plan means that you do not have to pay for the product until a negotiated date or when it is sold
•
N - No floor plan exists for the product.
Bin
The bin location for the serialized item.
Rec Date
The receipt date for the item with the corresponding serial number.
Current Doc#
The sales order, transfer, or work order number if this serial number is on an open transaction.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Serial # History
Opens the Serial Number History Inquiry to display detailed information on all transactions regarding this serial number.
F6-Equipment Inq
Opens the Equipment Inquiry screen to display the equipment information for the equipment ID that corresponds with the serial number.
