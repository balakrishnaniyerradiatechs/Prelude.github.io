Receiving Direct Ship Purchase Orders
Since you do not receive direct ship purchase orders into your warehouse, Accounts Payable does not have a receiver to reconcile to the vendor invoice. Direct Ship Receipt Entry allows you to create a receiver for invoice reconciliation (refer to Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry in the A/P documentation). You may want to use this option if you want to bill the customer for a direct ship order before you receive the vendor invoice.
To receive a direct ship purchase order:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts > Direct Ship Receipt Entry to open the Direct Ship Receipt Entry screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
Enter the P/O Number to receive. The Vendor and all open merchandise for this purchase order appear.
3
For Receipt Date, enter the day the vendor shipped the material.
4
For Received By enter your user ID. The following prompt appears:
Do you Want to Receive Purchase Order By...
5
Choose from the following:
•
Line Item: Receives one line item at a time. You might use this option if only a few items or partial quantities arrive. Highlight the item to receive and press ENTER to open the Detail Information screen. Continue with step 6.
•
Exception: Automatically enters the ordered Quantity into the Received quantity on every item. You might use this option if most of the order arrived and you only need to change a few items or quantities. Highlight the item to change and press ENTER to open the Detail Information screen. Continue with step 6.
•
Complete: Receives the entire purchase order in full and closes the receiver. This option does not allow changes. If the purchase order is in fact complete, you may want to use the Exception option which defaults all of the open quantities on the purchase order as received, but allows you to review and change the quantities.
•
Abort: Exits the receiver without updating any information.
If you choose Line Item or Exception, the Detail Information screen displays to allow you to enter the received quantity for a line item.
6
For Qty-Rec, enter the quantity received by the customer.
7
Press F2-Save to receive the line item.
8
After you enter the correct received quantity for each line item, press F2-Save to complete the direct receipt.
Freight Allocation
Option Parameters > Purchasing Parameters > Allocate Linked Frt To S/O determines if the Freight to Sales Order screen appears in Stock Receipts Entry and Direct Stock Receipts Entry:
•
Y - Freight is allocated proportionally to Inbound Freight for any linked sales orders.
•
I - Same as Y, but it also Includes the freight cost paid expenses as part of the G/L posting for the receiver.
•
N - Freight for received material is not automatically passed on to linked sales orders.
