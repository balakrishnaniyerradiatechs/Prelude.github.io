Shipping Stock Transfers
After you physically ship a transfer out of a warehouse, you must confirm the quantities and relieve inventory for the shipping warehouse. The material is in In-Transit inventory until it is received at the receiving warehouse (refer to Receiving Stock Transfers).
You can use one of the following methods to ship transfers:
•
Transfer Shipments Entry (updates inventory immediately).
•
Transfer Shipments Maintenance (allows you to review shipped quantities before updating inventory).
If you require a bill of lading or a packing list to ship transfers, refer to the following instructions:
•
Printing a Bill Of Lading.
•
Printing a Transfer Packing List.
Tracking Transfer Fill Rate
Set Transfer Fill Rate in File Update Parameters to Y to track the percentage of transfer line items that are 100% filled before shipping.
