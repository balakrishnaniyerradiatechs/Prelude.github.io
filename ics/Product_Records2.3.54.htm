System Requirements for Serialized Items
The following system flags determine how serial numbers are used:
Additional Option Parameters
For Generate Sequential Serial Numbers, indicate if you want to generate sequential serial numbers when you receive products that require serial numbers. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Generate sequential serial numbers.
•
N - Do not generate sequential serial numbers. You must manually enter the serial numbers.
For Use Whse Over Serial Cost, indicate if you want to use warehouse average cost instead the average cost of a serial number to update the general ledger. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Use warehouse average cost to update the general ledger.
•
N - Use serial cost to update the general ledger.
For Use Serialized Cost with FIFO, determine if transactions for serialized items use the serial cost of the item for FIFO costing. With FIFO costing, FIFO layers are relieved based on the first-in first-out method and the cost is the layer being relieved. If you have serialized items, FIFO normally ignores the serial cost and updates by layer. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Use the serial cost of the item instead of the FIFO layers cost. The layer’s on-hand cost is relieved accordingly.
•
N - Use the FIFO layer cost of the item.
For Serial # DS POs Non-Detail, determine if you can enter serial numbers for serialized products when direct ship purchase orders are received (if the detail tracking flag within Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info is set to N). Choose from the following:
•
Y - You can enter serial numbers for serialized products when you receive a direct ship purchase order.
•
N - You cannot enter serial numbers for serialized products on direct ship purchase orders.
 
Inventory Parameters
For Dropship P/O Serial#, determine if serial numbers are prompted for on serialized items on direct ship purchase orders:
•
Y - Prompt for serial numbers.
•
N - Do not prompt for serial numbers.
For Phys. Inv. Include Serial#, determine if you can use serial numbers when you adjust inventory to reflect the physical inventory counts (tag method). Refer to Taking Physical Inventory Using Tags. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Use serial numbers to adjust inventory.
•
N - Do not use serial numbers to adjust inventory.
 
Inventory Controls > Starting Serial # Maintenance
For Prefix, enter the prefix to add to the serial number created when a work order is completed.
For Starting Number, enter the next available serial number for serialized items in Work Order Completions.
