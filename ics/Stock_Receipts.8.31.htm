Receiving License Plate Products
License plates provide an exclusive, complete inventory control system for lot-controlled items. You must assign a unique license plate number (in addition to the vendor’s lot number) to every package of an item received into the warehouse and maintain a separate on-hand balance for each lot. When you ship a license plate item, Serial Shipping Container Entry in the Order Processing module tracks the license plates and updates inventory accordingly. If you use license plates to control inventory, you do not have to do a physical inventory to verify on-hand balances.
