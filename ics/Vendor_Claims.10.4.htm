Creating Warranty Tags in Credit Memo Entry
When a customer returns an item, you can enter a credit memo to issue credit to the customer and to designate the item as warranty inventory. A warranty tag tracks the inventory as it goes through Vendor Claim Entry, Vendor Claim Update Processing, and Warranty Replacement Entry.
To create a warranty tag while issuing a credit memo:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > Credit Memo Entry. Refer to the Order Processing documentation for detailed information on entering a credit memo.
2
When you enter the Product, the Line Item Credit Detail screen appears.
3
Enter the Quantity being returned. The default is (-1), but you can change it as necessary.
4
Press ENTER. The Credit Information screen appears.
5
Enter the Credit Reason code that identifies why you are returning the product.
6
Depending on system settings, you may or may not have to enter the Original Inv#. Press F3 to search for valid invoices for this customer/product combination.
7
Press ENTER to return to the Line Item Credit Detail Screen.
8
For Whse, enter the warehouse that is receiving the returned inventory.
9
For QtyWty, enter a negative number for the quantity to put into warranty inventory. The Warranty Tag Information screen appears.
10
For Returned/Scrapped, indicate where the material will go. In either case you will receive a credit or a replacement from the vendor:
•
R - The material will be returned to the vendor.
•
S - The material will be scrapped.
11
For Vendor RMA #, enter the vendor’s return material authorization (RMA) number, if applicable.
12
For Tag Number, enter N for a new tag number or enter your own tag number.
13
For Tag Quantity, enter the defective quantity. You can enter more than one tag number per line item, but the total in the Tag Quantity column must equal the Quantity entered on the Line Item Credit Detail screen.
Vendor Return Rules
To view any notes regarding return policies for this vendor, press F5-Vendor Return Policy.
14
Press F2-Save to save the tag information and return to the Line Item Credit Detail screen.
15
If necessary, change the remaining fields regarding the customer’s credit amount. The pricing information comes from the original invoice, if you entered one. If you did not enter an original invoice, the net price is the customer’s regular price.
16
Press F2-Save to save the credit detail information.
17
After you enter all the line items and comments, press F2-Save to complete the credit memo.
You can now return the items to the vendor on a vendor claim. Refer to Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory.
Comments
To enter comments to print on the credit memo, use the CV warranty tag comment code. When the credit memo is invoiced, the CV comment lines become the warranty tag notes. You can view these notes in Warranty Tag Inquires > F5-Review Tag > F7-Wty Tag Notes.
Warranty Tag Bins
If you use bins to track inventory, you can manually enter a warranty tag product in the warranty tag bin using the Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance screen (Inventory > Vendor Claims > Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance). The inventory in this bin does not affect on-hand inventory, but is only for reference in the Warranty Tag Inquiries.
