Projected Stock Receipts Report
The Projected Stock Receipts Report allows you to predict when products will arrive in your warehouse. The projected date is based on the delivery date of each product on the purchase order. If you select more than one vendor, the projected date is sorted first by expected delivery date and then by vendor.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Receipts Reports > Projected Stock Receipts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Vendor. The vendor number to limit the report to just one vendor. Leave blank to project stock receipts for all for all vendors for the selected cutoff day.
•
Proj. Receipt Cutoff Date. The latest date for which you want to view expected receipts. The report is sorted by ascending date.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Rec Date
The date that the product is expected to arrive. Based on the delivery date from the purchase order.
Vendor
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Linkage
The document number that the product is linked to. The prefix indicates the order type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
T - Transfer.
P/O#
The purchase order number.
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Qty
The quantity not yet received.
Ext. Weight
The weight of the total open quantity of the product.
Ext. Volume
The volume of the total open quantity of the product.
Extension
The cost of the total open quantity of the product.
