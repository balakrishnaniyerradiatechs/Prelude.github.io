YTD Inventory Gross Profit Report
The YTD Inventory Gross Profit report displays year-to-date sales, cost, gross profit, and turnover information for each stocking product. You can use this report to evaluate the return on investment and turnover rates for product lines.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Sales Status Reports > YTD Inventory Gross Profit Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Pline#
The product line number.
Product Number / Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Avg Yr CFS
The cost of goods sold from stock year-to-date (average cost x quantity sold YTD).
Avg Inv$
The average value of the on-hand inventory (average cost x on-hand quantity).
Ytd Sales
The quantity of the product sold year-to-date.
Ytd Sales$
The total extended value of the quantity sold year-to-date.
Ytd Gp$
The gross profit amount generated from year-to-date sales of the product (Ytd Sales $ - Avg Yr CFS).
Ytd Sls%
The gross profit percent generated from year-to-date sales of the product (Ytd Gp$ / Ytd Sales $).
Ytd Cst%
Ytd Gp$ / Avg Yr CFS. The gross profit percent generated from cost of goods sold.
Turns
The number of times the product turned so far this year. Turnover is calculated using the cost of stock sold YTD and the average inventory investment for the product (Avg Yr CFS / Avg Inv$).
