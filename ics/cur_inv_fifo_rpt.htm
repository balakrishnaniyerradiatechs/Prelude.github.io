Current Inventory Value Report - FIFO
This report displays the received cost and document number for each FIFO layer of a product in inventory.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Inventory Valuation Reports > Current Inventory Value Report - FIFO
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Stocking Status: The stock status codes to include on the report. Leave blank to include all status codes or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
Only Onhand not equal to 0. Determines if you want to see only products with an on-hand quantity. Choose from the following:
-
Y - Display only products with an on-hand quantity or negative on-hand quantity.
-
N - Display all products, including products with an on-hand balance of zero.
•
Sort by (P)rodLine (V)endor. Determines how you want to sort the report. Choose from the following:
-
P - Sort by product line.
-
V - Sort by vendor.
•
Primary Vendor. The primary vendor(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
•
Exclude Serial# Products?. Determines if you want to exclude or include serialized products. Choose from the following:
-
Y - Exclude serialized products.
-
N - Include serialized products.
•
Period. The fiscal period for which you want to include FIFO layers updated from the month-end inventory balance.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure.
On-Hand
The quantity of the product in stock.
C/V/W
The customer, vendor, or warehouse number:
•
The customer number if the product was received from a customer return.
•
The vendor number if the product was received from a purchase order.
•
The warehouse number if the product was received from a transfer.
Date
The date the product was received.
Doc#
The document number:
•
The sales order number if the product was received from a customer return.
•
The purchase order number if the product was received from a purchase order.
•
The transfer receipt number if the product was received from a transfer.
Qty-Rec
The quantity that entered the warehouse on the corresponding document number.
Rec-Cost
The cost of the item when it was received.
Value
The sum of the extended value of all the FIFO layers of the product currently in stock.
Unit-Cost
The average of the FIFO costs.
