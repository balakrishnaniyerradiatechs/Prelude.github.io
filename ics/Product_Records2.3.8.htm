Thawing Purchasing Controls For All Products
If you want to remove frozen purchasing controls for all products, you can enter a date up to which all frozen controls for all warehouses are removed.
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Manual Frozen Control Thaw Process to open the Manual Frozen Control Thaw Process screen.
2
Enter a Thaw Date Less Than Or Equal To the date by which you want products to come off of frozen controls. For example: Enter 06/01/05 to thaw all products that have a thaw date set in purchasing parameters of 06/01/05 or earlier.
The following prompt displays:
This Process Will Remove All Overrides And Frozen Controls For All Products That Have A Frozen Control Thaw Date Equal To Or Earlier Than 06/01/05. Do You Wish To Continue?
3
Choose from the following:
•
No - Cancels the thaw override.
•
Yes - Removes frozen controls for all products with a thaw date before or on the date entered.
