Related Products
The related product feature has two functions. Refer to the following for information on:
•
Selling Related Products With a Product. If you have one or more products that should always be sold with another product, you can set them up as related products. Each time you enter the original product on an order, the related products may appear on the order depending on how you set them up. You can delete or edit the related products/quantities as necessary.
•
Updating Usage for Another Product. If you have a part number for which similar products can be used to fill an order, you may want usage for all of the similar products to update just one product number. When the similar product is sold, the usage product is updated with usage. For more information, refer to Product Usage.
