Duplicating a Received Transfer
To duplicate a received stock transfer:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Movements > Transfer Entry to open the Transfer Entry screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
For Transfer Number, press ENTER to assign a transfer number.
3
From the Action Bar, select Dup Tran History. The Transfer History Duplication screen displays.
4
For Duplicate Transfer #, enter the transfer release number to duplicate.
5
Enter the From Warehouse, which is the warehouse shipping the material. The default is the From Warehouse from the original transfer.
6
For To Whse, enter the receiving warehouse. The default is the To warehouse from the original transfer.
7
Press F2-Save. The products from the original transfer display in the Transfer Entry screen.
Complete or edit the transfer as usual. Refer to Entering Stock Transfers.
