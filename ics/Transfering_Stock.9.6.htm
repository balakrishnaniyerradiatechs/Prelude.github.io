Transferring Warranty Inventory
You must use a type 03 transfer type to move warranty inventory from one warehouse to another. The warranty product must already have a tag number assigned (refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory) that identifies the reason for return and the cost of the product.
Transfer Revenue
Warranty transfers are excluded from transfer revenue calculations. Refer to Generating Revenue on a Stock Transfer.
General Ledger Maintenance
The Inventory Warranty In Transit GL Table header identifies the account that holds the value of warranty inventory in transit.
To transfer warranty inventory:
1
Follow steps 1-4 for Entering Stock Transfers.
2
In the Stock Transfer Header screen, enter 03 for Transfer Type.
3
Continue with steps 6-12 for Entering Stock Transfers.
4
From the Transfer Entry screen, select F7-Wty Tag.
5
For Warranty Tag# choose from the following:
•
Enter the warranty tag assigned to the material (refer to Moving Products into Warranty Inventory).
•
Press F3 to list warranty tag numbers by vendor, customer, sales order, or product number. Highlight the correct tag number and press ENTER.
The products assigned to the warranty tag appear.
The line item information comes from the tag number and cannot be changed.
6
Repeat steps 4-5 for any additional warranty tag products to add to the transfer.
7
Press F2-Save to complete the transfer entry.
You must ship a 03-Warranty Inventory transfer complete since you cannot access the line item detail screen (refer to Shipping Stock Transfers).
When you receive a type 03-Warranty Inventory transfer, you can only access Qty Rec. You cannot add any part numbers or quantities (refer to Receiving Stock Transfers).
The Transfer Log Inquiry displays the TAG code to reference warranty tag products.
