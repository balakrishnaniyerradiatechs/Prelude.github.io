Current Inventory Value Report - Damaged
This report displays the total value of all damaged products in inventory. The cost used is the average cost of each product when it was returned, received, or adjusted into damaged inventory.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Inventory Valuation Reports > Current Inventory Value Report - Damaged
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all products.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Stocking Status: The stock status codes to include on the report. Leave blank to include all stock status codes or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
Sort by (P)rodLine (V)endor. Determines how you want to sort the report. Choose from the following:
-
P - Sort by product line.
-
V - Sort by vendor.
•
Primary Vendor. The primary vendor(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number in damaged inventory.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Cost
The average cost of the product when it was put into damaged inventory.
Damaged
The quantity of the product in damaged inventory.
Val@Cost
The extended value of the total quantity of the damaged inventory (Cost x Damaged).
