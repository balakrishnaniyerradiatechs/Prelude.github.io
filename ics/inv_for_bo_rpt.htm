Inventory Available for Backorders Report
The Inventory Available for Backorders report displays products that have a quantity available to fill open backorders. If you automatically allocate to open backorders (refer to Allocating Inventory), backorders are normally filled automatically. If you do not use automatic inventory allocation, you can use this report to monitor open backorders.
Inventory Option Parameters
Suppress Allocation Rpt in Inventory Parameters determines if the Inventory Available for Backorders Report prints after the inventory is allocated.
•
Y - Do not print the report.
•
N - Print the report.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Status Reports > Inventory Available for Backorders
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) containing products with quantities available to fill open backorders.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product with an available quantity.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
On-Hand
The quantity on-hand in the warehouse.
Avail
The quantity available to fill open backorders.
Documnt#
The sales order, work order, or transfer number containing the backorder. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
T - Transfer.
Cn/Whse/Name
The customer number and name if the corresponding document is a sales order or work order.
The warehouse number and name if the corresponding document is a transfer.
Pck-Date
The pick ticket date on the document.
Linkage
The purchase order, transfer, or work order number if the product was linked to an incoming order. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
P - Purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
S
The supply code depending on the product’s replenishment path and product settings. Possible supply codes include:
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
R - Emergency transfer.
•
W - Work order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered on the sales order, transfer, or work order.
