Printing Stock Transfers
If your warehouse is set up to continuously print transfer pick tickets, the pick tickets print upon completion of the transfer. Otherwise, you must print transfer pick tickets by batch. You can print just one transfer or all transfers for a warehouse. To print or reprint a transfer, refer to the following:
•
Printing Transfer Pick Tickets.
•
Re-Printing Transfer Pick Tickets.
Sorting Products for Transfer Pick Tickets
Bin Loc Sort - TRN in Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > Transfers, determines whether to sort the products on the transfer by bin location or by product number.
Consolidating Quantities on Transfer Pick Tickets
Transfer Consolidation in Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info determines if stock transfers with the same ship-to and ship-from warehouse are consolidated into one transfer during batch printing. Refer to Establishing Transfer Options.
