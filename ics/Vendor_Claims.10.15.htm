Entering Warranty Replacements
If a vendor replaces an item on a vendor claim, Warranty Replacement Entry reconciles the returned product with the replacement product. Once you receive the items into the warehouse, the on-hand quantities and the G/L postings are updated.
Make sure that your receiving personnel does not receive the replacement item in Stock Receipts Entry or the vendor claim and general ledger will not be updated correctly.
To enter a warranty replacement for a vendor claim:
1
Select Inventory > Warranty Replacements > Warranty Replacement Entry to open the Warranty Replacement Entry screen.
2
For Entry Number, press ENTER over NEW to assign a new warranty replacement number or enter a previous entry to edit.
3
Enter the Vendor, which must be the same as the vendor on the vendor claim.
4
Enter the Vendor Invoice#, if available.
5
For Received By, enter your user ID.
6
Enter the Replacement Date for reporting and general ledger posting date purposes. The default is today’s date.
7
For Vendor Claim Number, enter the receiver number for the returned item. The receiver number is the vendor claim number with the (-1) extension. Press F3 to search for open vendor claim receivers for this vendor.
8
To identify which products were replaced, select F5-Replaced Products to open the Warranty Replaced Products Entry screen.
The screen initially contains no line items.
9
Choose one of the following methods to enter products on the claim:
•
Enter each replaced product.
•
Press F5-Load From Original Claim to copy the products from the vendor claim.
10
To change any quantities, highlight the item to change and press ENTER to open the Warranty Replacements Detail screen. Change the quantity and press F2-Save to save the changes.
11
Once all the quantities are correct, press F2-Save to return to the Warranty Replacement Entry screen. Value - Replaced shows the dollar amount of the merchandise returned on the vendor claim.
12
To enter the replacement products sent from the vendor, select F6-Replacing Products to open the Warranty Replacing Products Entry screen. The screen initially contains no line items.
13
Choose from the following to enter the replacing products:
•
If the replacing products are different than the products on the vendor claim, enter the replacing products manually.
•
If the replacing products are the same as the products on the vendor claim, press F5-Load From Replaced Entry to copy the products from the vendor claim.
14
After you enter the replacing products, press F2-Save to return to the Warranty Replacement Entry screen. Value - Replacing shows the dollar amount of the replacement items sent from the vendor.
Discrepancy reflects any difference between the value of the replaced items to the value of the replacing items.
15
If necessary, change either the replaced (F5) or replacing (F6) items. Any remaining discrepancy posts to the Vendor Claim Cost Difference account.
16
Press F2-Save to save the entry. The following prompt appears:
Do you wish to...
17
Choose from the following:
•
Process - Adds the replacement products to available inventory and updates the vendor claim as replaced.
•
No update - Suspends the transaction. Inventory is not updated and the vendor claim is not reconciled. You can view suspended warranty entries in the Warranty Replacement Inquiry.
•
Return - Returns to the entry screen.
When you complete the warranty replacement, the system:
•
Removes replaced quantities from the vendor claim receiver. If the quantity on the receiver drops to zero, the line is deleted.
•
Deletes the receiver when all line items are deleted from the original receiver.
•
Updates the Vendor Claim Log inquiry for each product replaced on the original vendor claim.
•
Updates replacing products as an increase in available inventory. If new costs are used for the replacing products, the average cost of the products reflects the change.
•
Updates general ledger accounts as follows:
-
Reverses the original vendor claim accrual postings from the vendor claim update.
-
Credits the replaced products value to either the warranty inventory account (if any item is associated with a warranty tag) or the regular inventory account.
-
Debits the replacing products’ value to the associated regular inventory accounts.
-
Posts any discrepancy between the replaced products’ value and the replacing products’ value to the vendor claim cost difference account.
Related Report
Description
Daily Warranty Replacements Report
Warranty replacement entries, including the replaced and replacing products, for a day or date range.
 
The Warranty Replacement Inquiry displays all open (not updated) warranty replacement entries for a vendor.
The Warranty Replacements History Inquiry displays all processed warranty replacement transactions for a vendor.
The Warranty Tag Inquiries allows you to look-up warranty tag information.
The Transaction Audit Inquiry displays the WTR code to indicate that a product was placed into stock from a warranty replacement transaction.
The Vendor Claim Log Inquiry displays the WTR code when an item is updated with a warranty replacement.
