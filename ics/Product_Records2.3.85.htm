Freight Class
You can assign freight classes to products or product lines to help determine how much freight to bill a customer based on the weight of the shipped product. You can also use this with the NMFC (National Motor Freight Classification) that describes what the material is. Refer to NMFC Codes.
Freight Class Listing
To view a list of freight classes, select Inventory > Listings > Miscellaneous Listings > Freight Class Listing.
To set up freight classes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Control Files > Freight > Freight Class to open the Freight Class Maintenance screen.
2
Enter a Freight Class to assign to products. This class calculates the customerâ€™s freight charge based on the weight of the product.
3
Enter the Description of the freight class.
4
Press F2-Save to save the freight class information.
To assign freight classes to product lines:
1
Press Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line to open the Product Line Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product Line.
3
Enter the Freight Class established in Freight Class Maintenance.
4
Press F2-Save to save the freight class information.
This freight class can be overridden by the freight class in Product Maintenance.
To assign freight classes to individual products:
1
Press Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Select F5-Add Info > F8-Freight to open the Freight Information Screen.
4
Enter the Freight Class from Freight Class Maintenance.
5
Press F2-Save three times to save the freight class information.
This freight class overrides the freight class in Product Line Maintenance.
