Surcharge Products
A surcharge product is a charge for a service, special handling, pallet, packing supplies, or any other extra charges not tracked as inventory. The surcharge is usually added to an order as a result of a calling product that automatically places the product on the order. Surcharge products are similar to service products in that inventory is not relieved, but you can backorder a surcharge product.
You must set the appropriate flags in Product Maintenance for both the calling product and the surcharge product. Refer to the following instructions for surcharge products:

•
