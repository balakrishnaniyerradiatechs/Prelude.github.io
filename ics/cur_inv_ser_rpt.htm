Current Detail Serialized Value Report
This report displays the total value of serialized products in on-hand inventory. You can select to print the detail serial number information for each product for each vendor or to print only the total value of serialized inventory for each vendor.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Inventory Valuation Reports > Current Detail Serialized Value Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) to include on the report.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product. The product(s) to include on the report.
•
Product Type. The products type(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product types codes or choose from the following:
-
N - Rental item.
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Both finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense item.
-
U - Used item.
•
Stocking Status: The stock status codes to include on the report. Leave blank to include all status codes, or choose from the following:
-
S - Stock.
-
N - Nonstock.
-
C - Consigned.
•
Sort by (P)rodLine (V)endor. Determines how you want to sort the report. Choose from the following:
-
P - Sort by product line.
-
V - Sort by vendor.
•
Primary Vendor. The primary vendor(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all vendors.
•
Product Line. The product line(s) to include on the report. Leave blank to include all product lines.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The serialized product number.
Description
The description of the serialized product.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure.
Serial Numbers
The serial numbers of the product with on-hand inventory.
Cost
The cost of the product for each corresponding serial number. The total cost for the combined serial numbers for the product displays at the bottom of each section.
