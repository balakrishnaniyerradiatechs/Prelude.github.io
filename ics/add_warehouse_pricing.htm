Additional Warehouse Pricing Screen
The Additional Warehouse Pricing screen allows you to view or change first acquisition costs (the cost the first time a product was received) or landed cost (the cost including duty, brokerage fees or freight) for a product in a warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance > F7-Price/Cost > F9-Add Info
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
1st Acq Cost
The cost of the item the first time it was received (acquired) from the vendor.
Cy - 1st Acq Cost
The cost of the item the first time it was received (acquired) in the current year.
Last Landed Cost
The last landed cost. Landed cost includes any additional costs such as duty, brokerage fees, or freight.
Last Landed Date
The date of the last stock receipt.
Last Landed Po#
The purchase order number for the last receipt.
F2-Save.
Saves the warehouse quantity break pricing information and closes the screen.
