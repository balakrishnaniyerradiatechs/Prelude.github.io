Fill With Product screen
The Fill With Product screen allows you enter a generic product to fill any sales order requirements for the selling product. You may want to use this if you have a part number that can be filled with one or more generic brands of a similar item, but you don’t necessarily want the customer to know the brand they are receiving. You can only set up a fill-with product for a product with no on-hand quantity.
When you enter the selling product on a sales order, the following occurs:
•
The fill-with product number displays in the 2nd line of description (if the part number does not already have a 2nd line of description and if the Omit Fill With Update flag in Inventory Parameters is set to N).
•
The inventory of the fill-with product displays.
•
The price is pulled from the selling product number but the cost comes from the fill-with product.
•
The pick ticket displays the fill-with product number and the selling product’s description.
•
The invoice displays the selling product number and description.
•
Product usage is updated for both the selling product and the fill-with product.
Refer to Fill-With Products for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Fill With Product
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Fill With Product
The part number to use to fill an order when the selling product number is entered on a sales order.
Description
The description of the Fill With Product.
F2-Save
Saves the fill-with-product information.
F4-Del
Deletes the fill-with-product information.
