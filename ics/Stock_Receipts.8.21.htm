Entering UPCs as Alternate Part Numbers
To set up a UPC as an alternate product number:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Alternate Product Maintenance to access the Alternate Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product that requires a UPC code.
3
For Alternates, enter the UPC or enter N to let the system generate the next UPC number.
4
For UPC, enter Y to identify the alternate product number as a UPC.
If UPCs are required and this flag in not set to Y, you will be required to enter a UPC when you receive this product into your warehouse. Refer to Entering UPCs When Receiving Stock.
5
Press F2-Save to save the UPC Code.
Refer to Alternate Products for detailed information on setting up alternate part numbers.
