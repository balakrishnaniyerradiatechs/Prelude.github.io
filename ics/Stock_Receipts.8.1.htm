Stock Receipts
To keep accurate inventory records and maintain high levels of customer satisfaction, your receiving department must have an efficient system to track each item that enters your warehouse. With Prelude, you can receive stock, update inventory, and allocate material to backorders as quickly as possible.
Maintenance flags determine which information is necessary (e.g., bin locations, lot information, quality assurance, UPC codes, labels, import costs, serial numbers) to receive stock. When damaged or unwanted material arrives in your warehouse, you can immediately allocate the rejected product into a separate inventory and tag it for return to the vendor.

Related Reports
Description
Purchase Order Receiving Report
Allows you to receive products that arrive without a packing slip. Lists products on a P/O or multiple P/Os and provides a space for you to write in the received quantities.
Putaway Documents Report
Bin location and/or linked sales order information for received products. You can run this report after you receive one or more purchase orders to help your warehouse personnel determine where to put away stock.
Daily Stock Receipts Report
All the stock received for a day.
 
Monthly Stock Receipts Report
All the stock received for a month.
 
Stock Receipts Exception Report
Discrepancies that occurred when stock was received (e.g, not on the original purchase order or the received cost is outside the tolerance percentage limits).
Projected Stock Receipts Report
Allows you to predict when products will arrive in your warehouse. The projected date is based on the delivery date of each product on the purchase order (entry date + lead time).
Purchased Price Variation Report
Products with a received cost that differs from the current average cost.
Vendor On-Time Receipts/Quality Report
The shipping performance for a vendor, including late and rejected shipments. If a P/O is received after the promised date, the order is considered late which affects on-time percentage. If the receiver has a rejection, the quality percentage is affected.
