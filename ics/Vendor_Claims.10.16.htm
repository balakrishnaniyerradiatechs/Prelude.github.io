Entering a Vendor Show Claim
If you send products out of your warehouse for display purposes and expect to receive the products back, you can enter a vendor show claim to track the inventory. A vendor show claim is similar to a warranty replacement, except that when you update the show claim in Vendor Claim Update Processing, an open purchase order is created to receive the products from the vendor. You can receive the product in Stock Receipts Entry (refer toReceiving Stock) rather than Warranty Replacement Entry.
The advantage of show claims, rather than normal vendor claim/warranty replacements, is that the inventory shows as on-order so Nightly Processing does not recommend a purchase quantity.
To enter a vendor show claim:
1
Follow the procedures for Entering a Vendor Claim for Regular Inventory, except in the Vendor Claim Header screen, enter a Claim Type of 02 for Vendor Show.
2
Print the vendor claim, refer to Printing Vendor Claims.
3
Update the vendor claim to automatically create a purchase order and adjust inventory. Refer to Updating Vendor Claims.
4
In Stock Receipts Entry (refer to Receiving Stock), press F3 at the P/O Number prompt to search for the receiver number created in Vendor Update Processing.
When the stock is received, the receiver is applied against the vendor claim to reverse the quantities and general ledger postings. The received products are placed into physical inventory and the receiver is automatically deleted.
5
When the vendor invoice is reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry, the receiver is zero cost. Press F4-Del to delete the zero receiver to reverse the previous G/L posting.
