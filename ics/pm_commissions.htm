Commissions screen
The Commissions screen allows you to override the standard commission that your sales representatives receive for a product. Normally, commission amounts are defined by Sales Representative and/or by a commission matrix.
Refer to the “Commission Subsystem” documentation in the Order Processing Module for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Commissions
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Regular Sales: Orders shipped from your warehouse.
SlsRep #1 Comm Type
Indicates how commission is calculated for the Salesrep One for an order:
•
$ - A dollar amount per unit sold.
•
* - A percent of sales.
•
G - A percent of gross profit.
Commission $,%
The commission that SlsRep #1 will receive for this product:
•
The dollar amount if you chose $ for SlsRep #1 Comm Type.
•
The percent of sales if you chose * for SlsRep #1 Comm Type.
•
The percent of gross profit if you chose G for SlsRep #1 Comm Type.
SlsRep #2 Comm Type
Indicates how commission is calculated for the Salesrep Two on the order:
•

•
* - A percent of sales.
•
G - A percent of gross profit.
Commission $,%

The dollar amount if you chose $ for SlsRep #2 Comm Type.
•
The percent of sales if you chose * for SlsRep #2 Comm Type.
•
The percent of gross profit if you chose G for SlsRep #2 Comm Type.
