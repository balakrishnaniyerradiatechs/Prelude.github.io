Vendor Claim Entry screen
The Vendor Claim Entry Screen allows you to enter products, quantities, and return information on a vendor claim.
Refer to Creating Vendor Claims for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claim Entry
