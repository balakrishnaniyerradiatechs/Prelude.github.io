License Plate Shelf Life Expiration Report
The License Plate Shelf Life Expiration report displays license plate products whose shelf life has expired or is about to expire. This information helps determine which products to sell before they expire and which products to remove. The expiration date for a license plate product is entered when the product is received (refer to Receiving License Plate Products).
Expiration Dates
When you set up a lot/license plate product in Product Maintenance, the Lot Exp Date Flag in the Lot Information Screen determines if an expiration date is required in Stock Receipt Entry. If so, you can set it up to either require the expiration date or the date of manufacture. If you use the date of manufacture, the Shelf Life Days established in Product Maintenance are added to the date of manufacture to determine the expiration date.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > License Plate Shelf Life Expiration Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse with the expiring shelf-life license plate numbers.
•
Product. The product number(s) with the expiring shelf-life license plate numbers. Leave blank to view all products.
•
Shelf Life Days Remaining. The days left until the lot expires.
•
Bin List #. The bins to exclude from the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Lot Number
The lot assigned to the product.
Exp Date
The date the shelf-life expires for the product/lot number.
Onhand
The quantity of the product/lot number combination still on-hand.
Bin Lst
The bin number.
