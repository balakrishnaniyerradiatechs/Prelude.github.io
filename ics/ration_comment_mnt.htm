Ration Comment Maintenance screen
The Ration Comment Maintenance screen allows you to set up a comment to display on a sales order when a customer exceeds their ration quantity in a cycle. These comments print for all customers and all customer ration types when a ration quantity is exceeded.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Ration General Comment Maintenance
