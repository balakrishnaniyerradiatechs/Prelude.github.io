Running the Batch Stock Receipts Edit Report
The Batch Stock Receipts Edit Report lists items and quantities received on all purchase orders in a batch. This information helps to verify products and quantities before you update on-hand inventory. If your company uses RF devices to receive stock, you can use this report to verify receipts.
If the report shows any discrepancies from what was received, you can go back into Batch Stock Receipts Entry to adjust the quantities. If you change or delete any items, run the Stock Receipts Edit Report again before you update on-hand inventory (refer to Updating Batch Stock Receipts).
Menu Path: Inventory > Stock Receipt > Batch Stock Receipts > Batch Stock Receipts Edit Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Batch Number. The batch number(s) to review.
•
PO Number. The purchase orders to include on the report. Leave blank to include all the purchase orders in a batch.
•
Warehouse Number. The warehouse for the received material.
•
Only Lines With Qty. Rec’d?. The line items to include on the report:
-
Y - Only include lines with a received quantity.
-
V - Only include line items with an over or under received quantity.
-
Leave blank to include all line items on the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Po#
The purchase order numbers included in the batch.
Vendor/Name
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Product/Description/Serial#
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description, if applicable.
Line 4: The serial number, if applicable.
Whse
The receiving warehouse.
Qty-Ord
The original quantity ordered on the purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity open before this receiver was entered.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received in this batch.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered. The quantity open after this receiver was entered.
Gross Price
The gross price of the item before any discounts.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as entered on the purchase order.
Disc%
The discount received from the vendor for this item.
Extension
The extended dollar amount of the quantity received, including the discount percent.
