Assigning Product Codes to Parker Divisions
You can set up product codes for each Parker marketing division in Parker Division/Marketing/Product Maintenance
To set up Parker product codes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Parker Reporting Codes > Parker Division/Marketing/Product Maintenance to open the Parker Division/Marketing/Product Maintenance screen.
2
For Parker Division Number, enter the division number (established in Parker Division Maintenance).
3
For Parker Marketing Number, enter the 2 digit code established in Parker Division/Marketing Maintenance.
4
For Cde, enter a code that represents products for this marketing divisions.
5
Press F2-Save to save the Parker division/marketing/product information.
