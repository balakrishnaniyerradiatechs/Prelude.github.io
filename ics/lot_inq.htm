Lot Inquiry
The Lot Inquiry displays lot numbers for a product. You can view cost, on-hand and available quantities, bin location, manufacturer and expiration date information, along with other detail information regarding the lot.
Viewing Product/Lot Information
To view lot numbers assigned to products:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Miscellaneous Inquiries > Lot Inquiry to open the Lot Inquiry Screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
4
For Disp Sequence, enter the sequence in which you want to display the items. Choose from the following:
•
R - By received date.
•
E - By expiration date.
•
A - By ascending on-hand balance.
The lot numbers for the product display.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number. The product description displays to the right.
Warehouse
The number for the warehouse containing the product.
Disp Sequence
The sequence to display the items:
•
R - By received date.
•
E - By expiration date.
•
A - By ascending on-hand balance.
Stocking U/M
The stocking unit of measure for the product. Refer to Establishing a Unit of Measure.
Lot
The lot numbers for this product that have quantities on-hand.
Description
The description of the lot number.
Vendor
The vendor for the products in the lot.
Bin
The first bin where the products in the lot are located.
B
An asterisk (*) indicates that the lot exists in more than 1 bin.
C
An asterisk (*) indicates that comments exist for the lot.
Exp-Date
The expiration date of the lot if one was entered in Stock Receipts Entry. You can review products with expiration dates in the Lot Shelf Life Expiration Report.
Lot Cost
The cost assigned to the on-hand quantity within this lot.
On Hand
The quantity of the product on-hand in this lot. Includes committed quantities.
Avail
The quantity of the product available for sale.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Cmnts
Opens the Lot Comments screen to allow you to view comments entered in Product Warehouse Maintenance in the Lot Maintenance Screen.
F6-Bin Inq
Opens the Lot Bin Inquiry screen to allow you to view the bin assigned to this lot/license plate number.
F7-Addit Info
Opens the Lot Additional Info Inquiry screen to allow you to view detailed manufacturer, cost, and bin information for this lot.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
