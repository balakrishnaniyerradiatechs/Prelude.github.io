Price/Cost Pre-Save Report
The Price/Cost Pre-Save report is an optional report you can run to view current price and cost information before updating the data.
Running the Price/Cost Pre-Save Report
To view or print current price/cost information:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Cost Changes by Lists > Price/Cost Pre-Save Report to open the Price /Cost Pre-Save Report screen.
2
For Product Or Warehouse, choose from the following to indicate how to generate the report:
•
P - Generate a report by product prices and costs.
•
W - Generate a report by warehouse prices and costs (for products with unique warehouse prices).
3
Enter the Warehouse associated with the list.
4
For Save List Name, enter the list name to print. Refer to Building Price/Cost Lists.
5
For Future or Current, indicate which prices to include:
•
F - Future prices.
•
C - Current prices.
6
If you entered F for Future or Current, enter the Future Effective Date that the prices take effect.
7
In the remaining price and cost fields, indicate which price/cost fields to display on the report:
•
Enter Y for each price/cost column to include on the report.
•
Enter N for each price/cost fields to exclude from the report.
You can view the results on the screen or send the information to a printer.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
The product number to update.
Retail
The current or future retail price for the product.
Price 1
The current or future price column 1 for the product.
Price 2
The current or future price column 2 for the product.
Price 3
The current or future price column 3 for the product.
Price 4
The current or future price column 4 for the product.
Price 5
The current or future price column 5 for the product.
Repl Cost
The current or future replacement cost for the product.
Std Cost
The current or future standard cost for the product.
Prc Grp
The price group code for the product. Refer to Establishing Product Price Groups.
