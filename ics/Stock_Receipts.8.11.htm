Rejecting Inventory When it is Received
When a damaged item arrives in your warehouse, you can reject it upon receipt, so that it does update your inventory as available.
To reject an item in receiving:
1
Follow steps 1-4 in Receiving Stock.
2
When you get to step 5, choose Line Item.
3
Highlight the item to reject, and press ENTER to open the Detail Information screen.
4
Enter the full quantity received (including any quantity to go into stock) in Qty-Rec.
5
Select F5- Reject Claim to open the Line Item Quality Control screen.
6
Enter the Rejection Claim Reason code to indicate why you are rejecting the material. Refer to Setting Up Rejection Claim Reason Codes for information on rejection codes.
7
For Rejection Claim Quantity, enter the quantity to reject.
8
For Rejection Warranty Tag#, enter N to assign a system generated number or enter your own tag number. This tag number tracks the product for return to the vendor.
9
For Rejection Claim Notes, enter any comments to explain why you are returning the material. These notes appear in the Buyer Queue Inquiry.
10
Press F2 - Save to save the rejection claim information. Continue with the receiver as usual.
The receiver is placed on hold in the Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry (refer to Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry). The product is now in a separate warranty inventory so that it is not available to sell. The buyer can review the item and enter a vendor claim to return it to the vendor. Refer to Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory.
