Batch Transfer Receipts Update
After you receive a batch of stock transfers (refer to Batch Transfer Receipts Entry) and verify the quantities (refer to the Batch Transfer Receipts Report), you must run the Batch Transfer Receipts Update to update inventory and average cost for the received products.
To update inventory for the products received in Batch Transfer Receipts:
1
Select Stock Movements > Batch Transfer Receipts > Batch Transfer Receipts Update to open the Batch Transfer Receipts Update screen.
2
Enter the To Warehouse, which is the receiving warehouse.
3
Enter the Transfer Number to update or leave blank to update received material for all transfers.
4
Enter a Batch Number to update or leave blank to include all batches.
5
Press ENTER to update on-hand quantities.
After you receive the stock, you can allocate the material to open backorders (refer to Allocating Inventory), and then print pick tickets to ship the merchandise to the customer (refer to Releasing Pick Tickets).
