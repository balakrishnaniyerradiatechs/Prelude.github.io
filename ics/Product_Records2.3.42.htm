Reserving Inventory for a Customer
You can reserve inventory in a warehouse for a customer. The quantity still shows as available in the Inventory Inquiry screen and the Line Item Detail screen, but will display the RSVD code at the bottom of the screen to indicate that it is reserved.
If you enter a sales order for the product and there is not enough inventory to cover the reserved quantity plus the quantity on the sales order, a message displays to let you know that the line item is on Branch Hold.
Branch Manager
You must be set up as a Branch Manager to reserve inventory for your customer or to release an order on Branch Hold. Refer to the Branch Release Inquiry in the Order Processing documentation for more information on Branch Hold.
To reserve a quantity of a product for a customer:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Enter the Warehouse.
4
Select Action Bar > Addtl Optns > Reserved Inventory to open the Reserved Inventory Screen.
5
For RCust#, enter the number for the customer for whom you want to reserve inventory. The customer description displays.
6
Enter the Quantity to reserve for this customer.
7
Press F2-Save twice to save the reserved quantity information.
To find out who a product is reserved for, select one of the following:
•
Inventory > Inquiries > Availability Inquiries > Product Warehouse Availability Inquiry > Action Bar > Rsrv Inv
•
Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiry > Inquiries > Reserved Inventory-Corp view
