Company Vendor Cost Maintenance screen
The Company Vendor Cost Maintenance screen allows you to enter replacement cost for a product from a vendor for all warehouses instead of just one.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Company Vendor Cost Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number to update.
Description
The product description.
Vendor
The vendor number that you purchased the product from.
Weight
The weight of the product when purchased from this vendor.
Unit Cost
The replacement cost that applies when you purchase from this vendor.
New Unit Cost
The new or future unit cost for the item.
Effective Date
(unlabeled field)
The date the New Unit Cost is effective.
Direct Ship Cost
The direct ship cost if you want to define a separate cost for the product on direct ship purchase orders.
Last-Upd
The date the last time this cost was updated.
F2-Save
Saves the company vendor cost information.
F4-Del
Deletes the company cost information for this vendor.
F5-Discount Matrix
Allows you to enter a discount matrix for quantity discounts. Refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices.
F6-Future Discount Matrix
Allows you to enter a future discount matrix for quantity discounts. Refer to Setting Up Company Vendor Cost Discount Matrices.
