Using Product Prefixes
You can use product prefixes to identify the manufacturer or any other grouping for a line of products. When you use a prefix to set up part numbers in Product Maintenance, standard information (e.g., price columns, price tape information, product line, vendor number) defaults from the prefix to help expedite the entry of new products.
Inventory Parameters
Set Product# Prefix in Inventory Parameters to Y to require product number prefixes for new part numbers. You must also enter the Length, which is the number of digits for the product prefix.
To set up product prefixes:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Prefix Maintenance to access the Product Prefix Description Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product Prefix to create, which typically is an abbreviation of the vendor’s name. The length of the prefix depends on the value entered in the Length field in Inventory Parameters.
3
Enter the Description of the product prefix (e.g., the vendor’s name).
4
For Maximum Price Column, enter the maximum number of price columns to set up for items with this product prefix.
5
For Sugg. Retail Desc, enter the suggested retail price column description to default in the pricing screen in Product Maintenance when a new product is created with this prefix.
6
For Price 1 Desc. through Price 5 Desc., enter the price column descriptions to default in the pricing screen in Product Maintenance when a new product is created with this prefix.
7
Enter the Product Line to default in the Product Line field when a new product is created with this product prefix.
8
Enter the Vendor Number to default in the Vendor Number field when a new product for a warehouse is created with this product prefix.
9
Enter the Vendor Line to default in the Vendor Line field when a new product for a vendor is created with this product prefix.
10
For Price Tape Flag, enter the default setting for Price Tape in the Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info screen when a new product is created with this product prefix. Refer to Updating Prices/Costs Using a Price Tape for information on price tapes.
11
Press F2-Save to save the default information for the product prefix code.
Once product prefixes are established, the default information displays when you create new part numbers in Product Maintenance. You can change any of the default information for individual products as necessary.
Product Prefix Listing
For a listing of product prefixes and their default information, select Inventory > Listings > Product Listings > Product Prefix Listing.
