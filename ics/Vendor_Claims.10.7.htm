Creating Vendor Claims
A vendor claim identifies material to return to a vendor. Once the material leaves the warehouse, you can update the claim to relieve inventory and to create a record for accounts payable to receive the credit or replacement. You can enter a vendor claim for products in regular inventory or warranty inventory.

Non-Nettable Bins
If you use bins to identify stock locations, the VCRE Allow Nonnet Bin flag in Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info, determines if you can use non-nettable bins in Vendor Claim Entry. If the flag is set to Y, you are warned in Vendor Claim Entry that shipping from a non-nettable bin will commit the inventory twice until the vendor claim is posted.
