Previous Prices Screen
The Previous Prices screen in Product Maintenance allows you to view the prices/costs that were in place for a product before the last time prices were updated. Previous prices for a product are only saved if you chose to save the last set of prices.
Inventory Parameters
Disp Save Prev Pricing in Inventory Parameters determines if the “Save Previous Price?” prompt displays when you change a price in Product Maintenance. Choose from the following:
•
Y - You can choose to save the previous price when you change a price in Product Maintenance.
•
N - Previous prices are not saved.
•
U - Previous Prices are automatically saved.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing > Action Bar > Previous Prices
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Previous
The previous prices/costs for each of the columns.
Save Dte
The date the prices/costs were changed.
F2-Save
Saves the previous price/cost information.
