Reprinting Stock Receipt Barcode Labels
To reprint a barcode label for a line item received on a purchase order:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Stock Receipts Label Reprint to open the Stock Receipts Label Reprint screen.
2
For Purchase Order History #, enter the receiver number that originally generated the labels.
3
For P/O History Line Number, enter the line number for the product on the receiver for which you want to reprint labels.
4
Enter the Number Of Labels to print.
5
For Printer Form Queue, indicate where the label is to print.
6
Press F2-Save to print the labels.
