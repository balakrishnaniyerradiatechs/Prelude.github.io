Stock Transfer In-Transit Report
The Stock Transfer In-Transit Report report displays open stock transfers that shipped out of the From warehouse but are not yet received at the To warehouse. You can sort transfers either for the shipping or the receiving warehouse.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Stock Movement Reports > Open Transfer In-Transit Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped transfers not yet received at the receiving warehouse. Leave blank to include all warehouses.
•
Receiving Warehouse. The warehouse to receive the transfers from the shipping warehouse. Leave blank to include all warehouses.
•
Shipment Date. The date the transfer shipped. Leave blank to view all shipped transfers for the selected warehouse.
•
Shipping/Receiving Whse. Determines how to sort the transfers:
-
S - Sort open transfers by the shipping warehouse.
-
R - Sort open transfers by the receiving warehouse.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Trans#/
To if you selected S to sort by shipping whse)
From (if you selected R to sort by receiving whse)
Line 1: The open transfer release number.
Line 2: The shipping/receiving warehouse:
•
The warehouse that will receive the material (if you selected S to sort by shipping whse).
•
The warehouse that shipped the material (if you selected R to sort by receiving whse).
Date-Shp
The date the transfer shipped.
Product Number
The products on the corresponding transfer.
Description
Line 1: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of description for the product.
Lot#: The lot number if the product is part of a lot quantity.
Ser#: The serial number if the product is a serialized item.
S
The source of the product request, if applicable:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
Qty-Req
The quantity of the product originally requested on the transfer. If the product is linked to a sales order, work order, or transfer. The document number displays.
Qty-Opn
The quantity available to ship.
Qty-Shp
Line 1: The quantity shipped on this transfer release number.
Line 2: The document number if the product is linked to a sales order, work order, or transfer. The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
•
T - Transfer.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered on this release number.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
Cost/Ext
Line 1: The unit cost.
Line 2: The extended cost of the total shipped quantity (Qty-Shp x Cost).
