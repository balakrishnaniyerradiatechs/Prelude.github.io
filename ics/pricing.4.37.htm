Updating Prices/Costs
Once you verify the product prices and costs on the Pre-Save List /Cost Report (refer to Price/Cost Pre-Save Report) you can update the prices and costs.
To update prices:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Price Cost Changes by Lists > Price/Cost Entry & Update to open the Price / Cost Entry screen.
2
For Product or Warehouse, indicate whether you are updating prices and costs at the product level or the warehouse level:
•
P - Update prices/costs at the product level.
•
W - Update prices/costs at the warehouse level (for products with unique prices for each warehouse).
3
Enter the Warehouse from the list.
4
For Save List Name, enter the name of the list for the products you want to update. Refer to Building Price/Cost Lists.
5
Enter a Starting Product if you don’t want to update all the products on a list. For example, you may want to use a starting part number if you previously had to exit while updating prices. Leave blank to update all products.
6
For Future or Current, indicate which prices to update:
•
F - Future prices and costs.
•
C - Current prices and costs.
7
For Future Effective Date, if you choose F for Future or Current, enter the date that the prices/costs take effect.
8
For each of the update fields, enter one of the following:
•
Y - Update the field.
•
N - Do not update the field.
9
Press F2 - Save to open the Price / Cost Update screen.
10
Enter the new prices and costs for each item in the list. You can only update the fields specified in step 8.
11
Press F2-Save after you enter all of the prices. If your company tracks previous prices, the following prompt displays:
Do You Wish To Save Previous Prices?
•
Yes - Saves the previous prices and goes to the next item on the list to update. You can view previous prices in Product Maintenance or Product Warehouse Maintenance > F7-Pricing > Action Bar > Previous.
•
No - The previous prices are not saved. The next item on the list appears.
12
Repeat steps 10 and 11 for each item on the list. If at any time you must exit the screen, press Esc. You can come back and finish updating the list by entering the last part number in Starting Product.
If a price or cost is based on another price or cost level that is updated, the system automatically recomputes all levels of prices and costs. You can still manually recompute prices if necessary. Refer to Recomputing Prices/Costs Updated by List.
After all of the products are updated, you can view the new prices on the Price/Cost Post-Save Report.
