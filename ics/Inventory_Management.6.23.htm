Entering Individual Bin Moves
To move a product from one bin to another:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Bin Individual Move Entry to open the Bin Individual Move Entry screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse where the product is changing bins.
3
For Move Number, enter an existing move number or press ENTER to let the system assign a new number.
4
For Product Number, choose from the following:
•
To enter a comment regarding the bin move, enter C.
•
To load all products in a bin, press F5-Load Bin to open the Bin Move Default Bin Entry screen. Go to step 5.
•
To enter product numbers one at a time, enter the product to move. If the product is a lot number, you must also enter the lot number for the product. Go to step 10.
•
If you use license plates for lot products, enter the license plate number. The lot number associated with the license plate displays (refer to Setting Up License Plate Products). Go to step 10.
Moving all the Products in a Bin
The Bin Move Default Bin Entry screen allows you to move all the products from one bin to another, with the ability to change quantities or primary bin assignments for each product. If you just want to move products from one bin to another without changing quantities or primary bins, refer to Entering Bulk Bin Moves.
5
Enter the Warehouse number.
6
Enter the Bin that contains the products.
7
For Load Quantities, choose from the following:
•
Y - Defaults the current on-hand quantity for each product to the designated To Bin.
•
N - Allows you to manually enter each quantity to move to the To Bin when you return to the Bin Individual Move Entry screen.
8
For Default To Bin Type, choose from the following:
•
P - Automatically defaults the product’s assigned primary bin for the To Bin.
•
M - Allows you to manually enter a To Bin for each product. You can change products within the bin when you return to the Bin Individual Move Entry screen.
9
Press F2-Finish to return to the Bin Individual Move Entry screen. The products from the Bin indicated in step 6 display.
Depending on what you entered for Product Number, Load Quantities and Default To Bin Type, you may or may not be prompted for From Bin, Quantity and To Bin.
10
Enter the From Bin, which is the old bin number.
11
Enter the Quantity to move. If the quantity entered exceeds the quantity in the bin, a warning message appears and allows you to re-enter the quantity.
12
Enter the To Bin which is where the product is moving to. If the new bin is not the primary bin for the product, a warning message appears and allows you to re-enter the new bin location.
13
Press F2-Save to complete the bin move.
Inspection Bins
Inspection Bin Movement in User Identification Maintenance > F6-Add Info must be set to Y to move products into or out of inspections bins.
