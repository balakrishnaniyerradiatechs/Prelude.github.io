Transfer Log Inquiry
The Transfer Log Inquiry allows you to view every transaction that affected a transfer.
Related Reports
Description
Stock Transfer Log Report
All the transactions that occurred for a transfer.
 
Viewing Transfer Activity
To view transfer information:
1
Select Inventory > Inquiries > Transfer Inquiries > Transfer Log Inquiry to open the Transfer Log Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Stock Transfer Number.
Each transaction that occurred for the transfer number displays. Refer to Transfer Log Inquiry Codes for an explanation of the codes.
