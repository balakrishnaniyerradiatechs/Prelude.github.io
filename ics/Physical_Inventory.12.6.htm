Creating an Inventory List by Product Line
To build a physical inventory list by product line.
1
Select Inventory > Physical Inventory > Inventory Sheet Procedure > Build Physical Inventory Lists > By Product Line(s) to open the Physical Inventory List By Product Line screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for the inventory list.
3
For Physical ListName, enter a name to identify the count sheet. You will use this name to print count sheets, enter counts, update inventory, and run inventory reports.
4
For Pline, enter one or more product lines to include on the count sheets. Only products assigned to these product lines in the Product Maintenance Screen will print on the count sheets. Leave blank to include all product lines.
5
Press F2-Create List. The following prompt appears:
## Items Selected. Press Return.
6
Press ENTER to create the lists for the selected product lines.
To view the list, refer to Displaying Physical Inventory Lists.
