Setting Up the Surcharge Product
To set up the product that is the surcharge:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product to set up as a surcharge product.
3
If necessary, enter a Description and Description Two for the surcharge.
4
For Product Line, enter the product line set up for surcharge products. Refer to Maintaining Product Lines. You must set up separate product lines for service type items so that inventory is not relieved.
5
Press F5-Add Info to access the Additional Information Screen.
6
Enter Y in Surcharge Flag to open the Product Surcharge Add Info Maintenance screen.
7
For Invoice Equal Quantities, indicate how you want to handle the pick and backorder quantities for the surcharge product relative to the calling product. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The pick and backorder quantities of the surcharge product are always equal to the pick and backorder quantities of the calling product. For example, if 5 pieces of the calling product are ordered but only 3 are shipped, then only 3 of the surcharge product are charged and the other 2 remain on backorder.
•
N - The entire quantity of the surcharge is invoiced on the initial invoice regardless of how much of the calling product shipped.
•
E - The entire quantity of the surcharge is invoiced when the calling product is shipped, but the surcharge may not necessarily correspond with the quantity of the calling item. For example, you have 10 of the calling product and 2 of the surcharge product; when 1 of the calling products is invoiced, the entire surcharge is invoiced.
8
For Separate Surcharge Line Item, indicate how you want the surcharge to appear on the invoice. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The surcharge prints as a separate line item on the invoice and is included in the merchandise total.
•
N - The surcharge is included in the freight, tax, or handling field depending on how the Type of Surcharge Indicator field is set.
9
For Consolidate Surcharge Line Items (only applicable if the Separate Surcharge Line Item field is set to Y), choose from the following to determine how you want multiple surcharge line items listed:
•
Y - All occurrences of a surcharge product are consolidated as a single line at the bottom of the invoice.
•
N - Each occurrence of the surcharge product is printed on the invoice as it occurs on the pick ticket.
10
For Type of Surcharge Indicator (only applicable if the Separate Surcharge Line Item flag is set to N), choose from the following to determine the type of surcharge and where it prints on the invoice:
•
T - The surcharge is for tax and is included in the tax on the invoice.
•
F - The surcharge is for freight and is included in the freight on the invoice. The Ship Via on the sales order must have Freight Surcharge? set to Y.
•
H - The surcharge is for handling and is included in the handling charges on the invoice.
11
For Multiplier, define a value to multiply by the cost of the surcharge product to calculate the selling price. For example, if the cost of the surcharge is $10 and the Multiplier is 1.6, the selling price of the surcharge is $16.
12
For Exclude Surcharge On Pick Tickets, indicate if surcharge items print on pick tickets:
•
Y - The surcharge item does not print on the pick ticket.
•
N - The surcharge item prints on the pick ticket.
13
Press F2-Save to save the surcharge information screen.
14
Press F2-Save again to return to the Product Maintenance screen. The Service Item flag automatically changes to Y.
15
Press F2-Save again to save the surcharge information.
