List Products With No Primary Bins
The List Products Numbers With No Primary Bin report displays products that have no primary bin designated in Product Warehouse Maintenance. You can use this information to review, add, or change bin assignments.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Listing of Product Numbers With No Primary Bin
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse(s) where the products are located.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number with no primary bin assigned.
Description
The product description.
