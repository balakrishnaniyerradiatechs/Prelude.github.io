Recomputing Prices/Costs Updated by List
When you update prices/costs by list, the new values recalculate when you complete the Price/Cost Entry & Update (refer to Updating Prices/Costs). However, you may still want to run the Recompute Prices process to make sure that all prices/costs are updated. This process re-calculates prices and costs in the same manner as the F5-Recompute Prices option in Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing (refer to the Product Pricing Screen).
Auto Recalc Prices in Inventory Parameters determines if prices and costs automatically recompute.
To recompute prices/costs after updating prices by list:
1
Select Inventory > Processes > Price/Cost Update Procedure > Recompute Prices.
2
For (P)roduct or (W)hse File, select from the following:
•
P - Recompute prices at the product level.
•
W - Recompute prices at the warehouse level.
3
For (C)urrent or (F)uture Prices, select from the following:
•
C - Recompute prices in the current price field.
•
F - Recompute prices in the future price field.
4
For Warehouse, enter the warehouse number for which you want to recompute prices or leave blank to include all warehouses. If recomputing at the product level, leave this field blank.
5
For Number of Cycles, enter the number of cycles the system must complete to recalculate pricing.
For example: If Cost for Sales is calculated using a percentage of Replacement Cost, and Price Column 1 is calculated with a percentage above Cost for Sales, then you must use 2 cycles since you are calculating two fields. If calculating three fields, then you need three cycles.
