Bin Replenishment Report
The Bin Replenishment Report displays bin replenishment information for primary bins that have both an on-hand quantity in a primary bin and a quantity in an alternate bin. This information is used with the Warehousing module.
Menu Path: Inventory > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Bin Replenishment Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse with primary/alternate bin quantities.
•
Bin Number Range. The beginning and ending bin numbers to include in the report.
•
Primary Bin Quantity. The primary bin on-hand quantity to limit the report to all products with a bin on-hand quantity of less than this amount. The product must also have a quantity in an alternate bin to appear on this report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Primary Bin
The primary bin for the product.
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Onhand
The on-hand quantity of the product in the primary bin.
Alternate Bins
The alternate bins for the product.
Onhand
The on-hand quantity in the alternate bin.
