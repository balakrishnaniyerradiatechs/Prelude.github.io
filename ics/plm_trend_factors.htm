Trend Factors Screen
The Trend Factors screen allows you establish forecasting formulas by product line. Refer to the Forecasting documentation for information on trends.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line > F5-Add Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Forecast Formula
The forecast formula to calculate usage for non-seasonal products for this product line.
Forecast Formula - Sea
The forecast formula to calculate usage for seasonal products for this product line.
#/Mo. Safety For DRP
The number of months of safety stock you want to keep on-hand for DRP (Distribution Requirements Planning) products. The Distribution Requirements Planning Report and Distribution Requirements Inquiry are used for items with long lead times. Refer to the Forecasting documentation for information on DRP purchasing.
Month
The months of the year.
Trend%
The trend percent between -100 and 100, used to forecast product demand for each corresponding Month. Remove this value once the trend stops. Trend quantity is calculated by multiplying the Forecast by the Trend Factor. The calculated trend quantity is added to or subtracted from the forecasted demand to provide an adjusted forecast. The Trend % at this level can be overridden by the Trend % in Purchasing Forecast Parameters Maintenance.
F2-Save
Saves the trend factor information.
