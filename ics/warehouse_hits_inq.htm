Warehouse Hits Inquiry Screen
The Warehouse Hits Inquiry screen displays the number of times a product was:
•
Invoiced on a sales order (first invoicing only).
•
Issued or completed on a work order (first issue only).
•
Shipped on a transfer (first shipment only).
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Usage Maintenance > F8-Prod Hits
You can also access this screen by selecting Inventory > Inquiries > Product Inquiries > Product Hits Inquiry.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number and description.
Warehouse
The warehouse number.
Month
The months of the year.
S/O Hits
The number of times a sales order was invoiced for this product at this warehouse.
W/O Hits
The number of times a work order was issued or completed for this product at this warehouse.
T/R Hits
The number of times a transfer was shipped from this warehouse for this product.
