Fill-With Products
If you have a part number that can be filled with one or more generic brands of a similar item, but you don’t necessarily want the customer to know the brand they are receiving, you can set up the generic product as a fill-with product for the selling product. The generic product will fill any sales order requirements for the selling product. You can only set up a fill-with product for a product with no on-hand quantity.
When you enter the selling product on a sales order, the following occurs:
•
The fill-with product number displays in the 2nd line of description (if the part number does not already have a 2nd line of description and if the Omit Fill With Update flag in Inventory Parameters is set to N).
•
The inventory of the fill-with product displays
•
The price is pulled from the selling product number but the cost comes from the fill-with product.
•
The pick ticket displays the fill-with product number and the selling product’s description.
•
The invoice displays the selling product number and description.
•
Product usage is updated for both the selling product and the fill-with product.
Inventory Parameters Flags
Display Fill With Subs determines if the fill-with product displays substitutes on sales orders:
•
Y - Display the substitute items for the fill-with product.
•
N - Do not display substitute items for the fill-with product.
Omit Fill With Update determines if the fill-with product displays on the second line of description for the selling product:
•
Y - Omit the fill-with product on the second line of description.
•
N - Display the fill-with product on the second line of description when the selling product is entered.
Fill With Inquiry
To see the selling part number for a fill-with product number, select Inventory > Inquiries > Miscellaneous Inquiries > Fill With Inquiry.
To enter a fill-with product for a selling product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product for which you want to assign a fill-with product.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Fill With Product to open the Fill With Product screen.
5
Enter the Fill With Product to use to fill an order when the selling product number is entered in on a sales order.
6
Press F2-Save to save the product information.
