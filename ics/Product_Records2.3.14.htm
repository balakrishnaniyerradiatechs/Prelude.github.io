Accessory Groups
You can use accessory groups to identify a group of products that are accessories for a product or multiple products. You can assign the accessory group to a product in Product Maintenance (refer to Accessory Products), so that when you enter the product on a sales order, all the items included in the group display. You can then decide which products, if any, to add to the order.
To create an accessory group:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Accessory Group Maintenance to access the Accessory Group Maintenance screen.
You can also access this screen within Product Maintenance. Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Accessories.
2
Enter the Accessory Group name to assign to the group of products.
3
Enter a Description of the accessory group.
4
If necessary, enter a second line of description in Description 2.
5
For Product, enter all the products that belong in this group.
6
For Script, indicate if you want to enter a promotional message to display when a product in the group is sold:
•
Y - Opens the General Notes Entry screen to allow you to enter Product Script. This could be a sales pitch that you want the sales person to give to help add accessory items to the order.
•
N - No script is included for this product.
7
Press F2-Save to save the product information.
