Creating Warranty Tags in Stock Receipts Entry
If a product arrives in your warehouse that is damaged or incorrectly shipped, you can put it into warranty inventory when you receive it so that it is never available for sale. You can assign a warranty tag in Stock Receipts Entry that tracks the item as it goes through Vendor Claim Entry, Vendor Claim Update Processing, and Warranty Replacement Entry.
To create a warranty tag while receiving stock:
1
Select Inventory > Stock Receipts > Stock Receipts Entry to open the Stock Receipts Entry screen. Refer to Receiving Stock for detailed instructions on receiving merchandise.
2
When you get to step 5, select to receive by Line Item.
3
Highlight the item to reject, and press ENTER to open the Detail Information screen.
4
Enter the full quantity (including any quantity not being returned) in Qty-Rec.
5
Select F5-Reject Claim to open the Line Item Quality Control screen.
6
Enter the Rejection Claim Reason code to indicate why you are rejecting the material. Refer to Setting Up Rejection Claim Reason Codes for information on rejection codes.
7
For Rejection Claim Quantity, enter the quantity to reject.
8
For Rejection Warranty Tag#, enter NEW to assign a system-generated number or enter your own tag number.
9
For Rejection Claim Notes, enter any comments to explain why you are returning the material. These notes will appear for the buyer in the Buyer Queue Inquiry.
10
Press F2-Save to save the rejection claim information. Continue with the receiver as usual.
The receiver goes on hold in the Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry (refer to Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry). The buyer can review the item and enter a vendor claim to return it to the vendor. Refer to Entering a Vendor Claim For Warranty Inventory.
Warranty Tag Bins
If you use bins to track inventory, you can manually enter a warranty tag product in the warranty tag bin using the Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance screen (Inventory > Vendor Claims > Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance). The inventory in this bin does not affect on-hand inventory, but is only for reference in the Warranty Tag Inquiries.
