Product Notes screen
The Product Notes screen allows you to enter notes associated with a product and then identify the document types where you want the notes to appear.
Menu Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Product Notes
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Notes
Notes associated with the product.
Doc
The type of document that you want these notes to appear on:
•
B - Both sales orders and quotes.
•
F - Both purchase orders and vendor requests for quotes.
•
G - Global text (all documents).
•
P - Purchase orders.
•
Q - Quotes.
•
R - Putaway report.
•
S - Sales orders.
•
T - Transfers.
•
V - Vendor request for quotes.
•
W - Work order pick ticket.
Type
The comment code for the product notes:
•
C - Prints on all sales related documents.
•
CA - Print on acknowledgments only.
•
CN - Does not print on external documents.
•
CB - Same as C but remains after backorder cycle.
•
CP - Prints on pick ticket.
•
CPB - Same as CP but remains after backorder cycle.
•
CI - Prints on Invoice.
•
CIB- Same as CI but remains after the B/O cycle.
•
CPO - Prints on sales documents and purchase orders.
•
CIPO - Prints on linked purchase order and invoice.
•
CNPO - Same as CPO but does not print.
•
CPPO - Prints on purchase order and pick ticket but not on invoice.
F2-Save
Saves the product notes information.
F5-Notes Review
Displays the note information including the user ID of the person who entered the notes and the date they were entered.
