Maintaining Ship-To Addresses for Stock Transfers
You can enter a stock transfer to ship to an address other than the regular address for the To warehouse. If you will be using the address regularly, use Transfer Ship-To Maintenance to enter or edit multiple ship-to addresses for stock transfers.
Transfer Ship-To Inquiry
You can view the information set up in Transfer Ship-To Maintenance in the Transfer Ship-To Inquiry (Inventory > Inquiries > Transfer Ship-To Inquiry).
To enter or edit a transfer ship-to address:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Transfer Ship-To Maintenance to open the Transfer Ship-To Maintenance screen.
2
For Transfer Ship-To, enter a number or letter combination to represent this ship-to address.
3
Enter a Description of the address.
4
Enter the address information.
5
Press F2-Save to save the ship-to address.
The next time you enter a transfer (refer to Entering Stock Transfers) you can use this code in the Manual Ship-To Entry screen (Transfer Entry > F5-Header > F7- Ship-To).
