Moving Products to New Bins
To move products from one bin to another, you can either re-assign the bin number for a single product in the bin, or you can re-assign the bin number for all of the products contained in the bin.
Inventory Parameters
Multiple Bin Locations in Inventory Parameters must be set to Y to enable bin moves.
