Adjusting Vendor Consigned Inventory Cost
If your vendor consigned inventory was received at a different cost than regular inventory, you may need to adjust only the vendor consigned inventory. When you use Cost Adjustment Entry - Vendor Consigned, the system finds all layers of inventory for that warehouse/product/vendor combination to adjust cost. You can adjust cost for vendor consigned inventory up until the vendor invoice is reconciled. This type of cost adjustment does not update the Transaction Audit Inquiry.
To adjust cost for a vendor consigned product:
1
Select Inventory > Inventory Adjustments > Cost Adjustment Entry - Vendor Consigned to open the Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry screen.
2
For Adjustment Number, press ENTER to assign the next available cost adjustment transaction number.
3
For Entry Date, either accept the default of todayâ€™s date or enter a different date for the transaction. The fiscal period displays to the right.
4
Enter the vendor consigned Product for which you want to adjust cost. The product description displays. For serialized products, you must enter a serial number. For lot products, you must enter a lot number.
5
Enter the Vendor from whom the inventory is consigned.
6
Enter the Warehouse for the cost adjustment. The warehouse description, Quantity On Hand, and the Current Cost for the product display.
7
For Revised Cost, enter the new cost. An increase in cost debits the Inventory account and credits the Cost Adjustment account. A decrease in cost credits the Inventory account and debits the Cost Adjustment account.
8
For Cost Adj Reason, enter the cost adjustment reason code to explain why you are adjusting cost. Refer to Establishing Cost Adjustment Reason Codes.
9
Press F2-Save to save the adjusted cost.
