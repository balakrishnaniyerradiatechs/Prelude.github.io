Serial Number Entry screen
The Serial Number Entry Screen allows you to enter serial numbers for items on open purchase orders, sales orders, work orders, and transfers.
You can enter a serial number for a product if it is:
•
In On-Order status on an open stock transfer.
•
In Entered status on an open work order.
•
On an open sales order, not yet confirmed for invoicing.
•
On an open purchase order.
Refer to Entering Serial Numbers for Products in Inventory for more information.
Menu Path: Inventory > Processes > Serial Number Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product that requires the serial number.
Whse
The warehouses that stock this product.
Description
The warehouse descriptions.
Onhand
The on-hand quantity for each warehouse.
F2-Save
Saves the serial number information.
F5-Serial Numbers
Allows you to enter the serial numbers for the product for each warehouse. The number of serial numbers must equal the on-hand quantity for the corresponding warehouse.
