Receiving Transfers by Batch
Receiving transfers by batch can help to minimize possible receiving errors. You can receive multiple transfers under one batch number and then print the Batch Transfer Receipts report to verify products and quantities before updating inventory.
Use the following three steps to receive stock by batch:
•
Batch Transfer Receipts Entry.
•
Batch Transfer Receipts Report.
•
Batch Transfer Receipts Update.
