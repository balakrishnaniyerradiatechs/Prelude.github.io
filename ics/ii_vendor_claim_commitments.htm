Vendor Claim Commitments screen
The Vendor Claim Commitments screen allows you to view a list of vendor claims entered for a product but not yet updated (refer to Updating Vendor Claims).
Menu Path: Inventory > Inquiries > Inventory Inquiries > Vendor Claims (under Commitments) > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
V/C#
The vendor claim number.
Vendor
The number for the vendor to which you are returning the merchandise.
Description
The description of the vendor.
V/C Type
The vendor claim type:
•
01 - Regular.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory.
•
04 - Non-inventory expense.
V/C Date
The date the vendor claim was entered.
Qty-Opn
The quantity entered on the vendor claim.
U/M
The unit of measure for the item as entered on the vendor claim.
Qty-Rtn
The quantity of the item being returned to the vendor.
Del-Date
The delivery date from the Vendor Claim Detail Information screen, which is the date that you expect the vendor to receive the merchandise.
Del Date Flg
Indicates the source of the Delivery date:
•
C - The date originally confirmed by vendor.
•
E - The date confirmed by vendor after expediting.
•
O - The delivery date was overridden.
•
Blank - The date assigned when the vendor claim was entered.
Qty-Scrp
The quantity of the item that will be scrapped instead of returned.
Order Number
The vendor’s order number if one was entered in the Vendor Claim Additional Information screen (Vendor Claim Header screen > F5-Add Info).
F2-Finish
Closes the screen and returns to the Inventory Inquiry screen.
F5-Review V/C
Opens the Vendor Claim Display screen to display detailed information for the highlighted vendor claim number.
F7-Log
Opens the Vendor Claim Log Inquiry to allow you to view all transactions that occurred for the highlighted order.
F9-Goto
Allows you to enter a page number, reference number, or line number to go directly to.
