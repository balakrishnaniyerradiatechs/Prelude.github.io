Maintaining a Product by Warehouse
Once a product is established in Product Maintenance, you can create the product in one or more selling warehouses in Product Warehouse Maintenance. You can enter bin locations for each product and set up pricing, purchasing, and usage information unique to each warehouse.
The following screens are accessible in Product Warehouse Maintenance:
•
Product Warehouse Maintenance Screen.
•
Lot Maintenance Screen.
•

•

•

•
Product Whse Transfer Information Screen.
•
Product Usage Maintenance Screen.
•
Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance Screen.
•
Warehouse Pricing Screen.
•
Product Vendor Maintenance Screen.
