Vendor Claim Review screen
The Vendor Claim Review screen allows you to review detail information for each line item on a vendor claim.
Menu Path: Inventory > Vendor Claims > Vendor Claim Entry > Action Bar > Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Claim Number
The vendor claim number.
Vendor RMA#
The vendor’s return material authorization number.
Vendor Number
The number and description of the vendor to whom you are returning the merchandise.
Product Number
The product being returned on the vendor claim.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse that is returning the product.
RMA Number
The vendor’s return material authorization number from the warranty tag.
Delivery
The date the product is expected to arrive at the vendor location.
Qty-Ord
The quantity of the product to return.
Qty-Opn
The quantity of the product not yet returned.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Gross
The gross amount of the vendor’s cost for the product.
Disc%
The discount given by the vendor for this product.
Vendor Product
The vendor’s product number if one was entered in the Vendor Claim Detail Information screen.
OK
Closes the screen.
