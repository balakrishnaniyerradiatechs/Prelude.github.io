Additional Product Descriptions
If the standard 2 lines of description are not adequate for a product, you can enter additional lines of description to display when the product is entered on a sales order or quote.
Order Processing Parameter
Disp Add Desc in SOE/QOE must be set to Y so that the additional description will display when the product is entered on a sales order or a quote.
To add additional lines of description to a product number:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product that requires an additional product description.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Addâ€™l Description to open the Additional Description screen.
5
Enter the Description to display when the product is entered on a sales order or quote.
6
Press F2-Save to save the product description information.
