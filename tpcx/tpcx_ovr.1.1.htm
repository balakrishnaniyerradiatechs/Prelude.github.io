Overview
This document provides an overview of processes, maintenance routines, and entry screens available for Trading Partner Connect (TPCx) and Activant Prelude. TPCx is a separate system available from Activant Solutions Inc. that is integrated with Prelude for trading partners to establish relationships with each other for buying and selling. Prelude sends messages to the central hub, which sends data back to Prelude. Refer to documentation for Trading Partner Connect for detailed processes outside Prelude.
 
Licensing Note: You must license Trading Partner Connect through Activant to use this functionality. Please contact your Sales Representative for pricing or more information about this module.
