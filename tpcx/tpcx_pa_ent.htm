Buying Stock from the Hub
If a product is rationalized on TPCx, you can view price and availability for the item and automatically generate a P/O from Prelude. To purchase a part from a TPCx trading partner from a sales order:
1
Open the Line Item Detail screen to see if a product is rationalized.
2
Choose Action Bar > Inquiries > TPCx Price/Availability Request. The TPCx Price/Availability screen appears for the backordered amount.
 
As data is gathered, a message appears every few seconds to allow you to continue searching or to stop the search. If you see a vendor youâ€™d like to use, choose Stop. To keep searching, choose Continue.
3
Press F5-Review to see availability or F6-Message to see comments.
4
When you find a trading partner to purchase from, highlight that vendor and press F7-Purchase. A P/O is automatically generated according to your company and user settings.
