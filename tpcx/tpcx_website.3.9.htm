Rationalizing Items
To rationalize items:
1
Log onto the Trading Partner Connect web site and click Inventory. The Item Rationalization Create Request page appears.
2
In Select Manufacturer, find the manufacturer you want to rationalize.
3
Enter the vendorâ€™s number from Prelude in Enter Your ID for this Manufacturer.
4
Check Exclude Previously Rationalized Items.
5
For Select Item Type, choose one of the following based on how you set up your stock item and catalog item databases.
-
All Items - Both stock and catalog items are sent for rationalization. If the same part numbers are in both your stock and catalog item databases, do not choose this option. TPCx views them as duplicates and rejects the items for rationalization. For more information, refer to Managing Duplicates.
-
Inventory Only - only stock items are sent for rationalization.
-
Catalog (Nonstock) Only - only items from your catalog item database are sent in the rationalization request. If the same part numbers are in both your stock item and catalog item databases, send two requests for rationalization: once for Inventory Only and once for Catalog (Nonstock) Only.
6
Click Create to submit the request to the hub to rationalize your items. You should receive a response within 24 hours. Then the request is approved or cancelled on the Inventory screen under Review Request.
