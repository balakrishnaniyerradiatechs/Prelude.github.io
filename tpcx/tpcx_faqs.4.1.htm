Frequently Asked Questions
Why do I need to rationalize as many items as possible?
Items must be rationalized and posted as inactive items (dead stock) and be recognized by the Trading Partner Connect Marketplace and your alliance partners (trading partners). Items are submitted for rationalization one vendor at a time. The more vendor lines are rationalized, the more you can use Trading Partner Connect for a greater return on investment (ROI).
For more information on item rationalization, refer to Rationalizing Items.
Why should I form Alliance Partnerships with other distributors?
Your goal is to create as many B2B Alliance Relationships with your trading partners as possible. B2B Alliance gives you online access to your trading partnersâ€™ products. That can translate into hundreds of thousands of additional SKUs (stock keeping units) in your online inventory. Now when a customer calls to place an order, you can fill it immediately without ever having to pick up the phone, saving you valuable time and increasing your profitability and customer service ratings.
You can transact electronically with your partners without paying any VAN charges. For more information refer to B2B Alliance.
Someone requested an Alliance Relationship with me. What do I do?
This request appears in the Requests To Trade With Me section of your Home page at www.tpcx.com. To create a new Alliance Relationship:
1
Click New under Respond To. Contact information of your potential new alliance partner appears.
2
Decide internally whether to agree to this relationship. You may want to do a credit check and any other steps you take for new customers.
3
If you decide to accept an Alliance Relationship with this company, set up the company as both a Customer and a Vendor in Prelude and create or assign a pricing schedule for this company.
4
Return to the Home Page and click New under Respond To for that trading partner.
5
Click Accept and enter the customer and vendor codes in Customer Code and Vendor Code on the Relationships page.
6
Click Submit to create the new Alliance Relationship.
The request from this trading partner may appear in the Outstanding Requests section of your Home Page until they enter you as a Customer and Vendor in their system and finalize the relationship in the B2B Marketplace as you did.
For more information, refer to Using the TPCx Website.

How do I request a B2B Buyer Relationship with new suppliers who become members of Trading Partner Connect?
To request a B2B Buyer Relationship with new suppliers:
1
Click Trading Partner Directory under Relationships in the Company Profile tab.
2
Select the supplier from the directory and click Create Request. This request appears on your Home Page as an Outstanding Request until the supplier agrees to the relationship and set ups are complete. You must complete an Implementation Guide about this relationship and return it to Activant. The information gathered allows your Electronic Business Document Specialist to set up your Electronic Business Document. Items must rationalize at 90% or better for this vendor.
For more information, refer to Using the TPCx Website.

What if someone wants to buy the dead stock I have posted to the Trading Partner Connect Marketplace?
Any Trading Partner Connect member can see what you have posted as dead stock, but they must form an Alliance Partnership with you before they can purchase from you via Trading Partner Connect.
You can be emailed when an alliance partner initiates a GET PA (Pricing & Availability Lookup) on an item that you have posted as dead stock. If they only inquire about the item and do not send a purchase order, have your sales people be proactive and call to see if they are interested in purchasing
How can I encourage my Sales Department to be proactive in selling dead stock I have posted to the Trading Partner Connect Marketplace?
Use the Data Mining Report, which lists all trading partners, whether you have an Alliance Partnership with them or not, who have an active purchase requirement for the items you posted. This is your Potential Sales Report. Give a copy to your sales reps and have them call each distributor, letting them know what you have available for sale, at a discount off of cost, the item(s) for which they have an active purchase requirement.

How can I confirm what I have sent via Trading Partner Connect, and what I should have received via Trading Partner Connect?
From the www.tpcx.com web site, click Document Tracking. Much like e-mail , Trading Partner Connect provides an electronic bin for any incoming documents, and another for copies of documents you send to your trading partners. When you first enter a bin, a summary of its contents appear. You can then search the contents to find the document(s) you are looking for. More information refer to Using the TPCx Website.
