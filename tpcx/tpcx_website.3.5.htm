Relationships
Remember that developing and maintaining good trading partner relationships is key to successful implementation of Trading Partner Connect. Relationships is a sub-section of Company profile where you manage your trading relationships. It has four sections:
•
Create - search for either trading partners in the vertical markets you select or manufacturers with whom to trade. By default, all manufacturer lines you rationalized are chosen. You can search by company name or e-mail and restrict the search to distributors, manufacturers, or marketplace.
•
Trading Partner Directory - alphabetized list of all members of the Trading Partner Connect Network and the status of your relationship.
•
Existing - list of your trading partners, including links to view details, modify the trading partner information, and cancel the partnership.
•
Custom Documents Translation - ask Activant to set up an EDI document between you and another trading partner with some basic specifications you establish online.
