Setting Up Prelude
To use Prelude with TPCx, you must setup data in:
•
Company Maintenance.
•
Web User Maintenance.
•
Warehouse Maintenance.
•
Product Maintenance.
Customer Maintenance and Vendor Maintenance also show TPCx data.
