Document Tracking
Document Tracking has the subtopics:
•
Search - search for Trading Partner Connect documents. You can limit the search by document location, Trading Partner, transaction type, tracking numbers, ID number, and date.
•
Inbox - bundled document groups sent to you by your trading partners.
•
Outbox - documents you sent to your trading partners.
•
Document Box - individual documents sent to you by your trading partners.
•
Errorbox - documents you sent to your trading partners that failed to reach their recipients.
