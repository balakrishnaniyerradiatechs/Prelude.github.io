How Does TPCx Work?
Trading Partner Connect works with Prelude to provide a comprehensive buying and selling solution. Two extremely important aspects of Trading Partner Connect are Item Rationalization and Trading Partner Relationships. If properly administered, these two features can significantly increase your profits and vastly increase your access to both items and customers, without changing the way you label or track your inventory.The following figure shows the flow of a pricing and availability request through Prelude, the B2B Marketplace, and your trading partners’ systems.
1
Checks Trading Permissions – A pricing and availability request is sent from your system to the B2B Marketplace. When the request is received, Trading Partner Connect finds your trading partners.
2
Rationalizes Part Numbers – Once Trading Partner Connect finds your partners, it checks to see if they have that item rationalized, and if any member of Trading Partner Connect has it listed as dead stock.
3
Sends Requests to Trading Partners Using Their Part Numbers – Your request is sent to your trading partners using their part numbers.
4
Transmits Trading Partner Responses to You with Your Item Code – Each trading partner processes the request and sends it back to the B2B Marketplace. The hub then responds to Prelude with your part number.
