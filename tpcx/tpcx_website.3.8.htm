Item Rationalization
Remember that you can only trade rationalized items via Trading Partner Connect. If you do not rationalize your items, you cannot use Trading Partner Connect. The Item Rationalization topic has the subtopics:
•
Create Request - request to have a manufacturer’s items rationalized. You must choose from the list of manufacturers, provide the vendor number from Prelude, indicate if you are going to rationalize your item catalog, of all of your listings, and provide the e-mail address of the contact. Refer to Rationalizing Items.
•
Review Request - view the status of previous requests. Refer to Reviewing Rationalization Results
•
Rationalize Failed Requests - generate a list of your items that failed rationalization, compare them to items stored in the hub’s database, and then manually match your items to those in the database, rationalizing the item pair.
•
My Rationalized Lines - list of rationalized manufacturer lines in Trading Partner Connect.
