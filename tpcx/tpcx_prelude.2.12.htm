Defining TPCx Partners
When a customer and vendor are trading partners at the TPCx hub, the hub sends Prelude a message and Prelude automatically creates the standard inbound and outbound EDI transactions. To view a trading partner, choose E-Commerce > Flat Files > Maintenance > Partner Maintenance. The Flat File Partner Maintenance screen appears.
For all TPCx trading partners Partner Number and Partner Description default from the TPCx relationship management process. For all TPCx trading partners File Method is E and Transfer Type is TPCX. The remaining fields are automatically updated by TPCx.
 
The Transfer Type TPCX is automatically added to Transfer Type Maintenance when TPCx is setup on your system.
