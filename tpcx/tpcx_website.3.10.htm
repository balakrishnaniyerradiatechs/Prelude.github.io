Reviewing Rationalization Results
Once you rationalize your items, you receive an e-mail to notify you that the request can be reviewed. To review your requests, click Inventory, then Review Request. A screen similar to the following appears:
1
Click a manufacturer’s name in Manufacturer. The Review Request pages lists the number of items sent, rationalized, and available to update. The number of duplicate items and failed items also appear.
2
Click Email to email items to yourself. However, the report could be very large, depending on how many item were part of the request.
3
Review the items that:
•
Rationalized True (successful) to verify that the rationalization.
•
Rationalized False and determine if the data is invalid, missing, or is a special order item.
4
After reviewing the results of the rationalization request, choose either:
•
Cancel - do not update the rationalized items to the hub. You may need to update your date and resubmit the rationalization request.
•
Update -update rationalized items to the hub (except duplicates). The items rationalized are now recognized and can be traded via TPCx.
For example, if you submitted 100 items you submitted for rationalization and only 10 were successful, you could cancel this request and update and resubmit your data.
If you delete or change the product number or vendor of a rationalized item, Prelude notifies TPCx so that the database continues to match your data. Also, once a vendor is rationalized , when you add items from that vendor, Prelude sends a rationalization request to Trading Partner Connect.
