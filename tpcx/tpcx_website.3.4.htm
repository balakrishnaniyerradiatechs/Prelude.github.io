Company Profile
You must complete your Company Profile prior to using any of the Trading Partner Connect features. In this section, you record your company contact information as well as other options regarding your Trading Partner Connect system:
•
My Account - user ID and logon information.
•
My Documents - upload documents that trading partners need, such as tax exemptions, to the Trading Partner Connect hub, making them available to review. You can also view and delete existing documents.
•
Partner Profile - edit the information about your company provided to trading partners, including Company Name, Company E-mail, Company ID #, and identification code.
•
Billing Information - edit your billing information and address.
•
Shipping Information - edit your shipping information and address.
•
Contact Information - edit information for any contacts you set up. You can choose what alerts are sent to a contact, reset passwords, and add and remove contacts. This feature is only available if you are an administrator. It will not appear for those without administrator access.
•
Vertical Markets - select the vertical markets you wish to trade in.
•
Availability Options - set limits on your listed available quantities.
•
Notification Defaults - select how often the hub e-mails you reminders.
•
Document Support - establish which of your company contacts receive reports.
