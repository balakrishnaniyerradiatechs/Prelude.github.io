Creating Trading Partner Relationships
Effectively managed trading partner relationships are the key to successful implementation of Trading Partner Connect. If properly cultivated, those relationships can significantly increase your profits and help you eliminate dead stock, improve customer service, and create a new customer base.
Creating New Customers
Since Trading Partner Connect allows you to purchase items from your trading partners, it follows that your trading partners can purchase items from you. The more you purchase from your trading partners, the more likely they are to purchase items from you. By maintaining strong trading partner relationships, you can potentially become the vendor of choice for several different trading partners. In addition to creating a new customer base among your trading partners, the improved customer service you provide to your current customers provides the opportunity for referral customers.
Identifying Dead Stock
Once you upload the dead stock information to Trading Partner Connect, the information is immediately available to your trading partners as well as other Trading Partner Connect users with whom you do not have a trading relationship. If a Trading Partner Connect user with whom you do not have a current relationship wants to purchase your dead stock items, they must first establish a trading partner relationship with you.
Eliminating Dead Stock
Through the relationships you develop with your trading partners, you could eliminate your dead stock. The Trading Partner Connect system allows other distributors to view your stock cards for any rationalized item; they can then purchase the items they want. If you have dead stock that another distributor needs, they can purchase it from you, rather then directly from the manufacturer or their supplier. Dead stock searches reveal available inventory from all members of Trading Partner Connect, but distributors must establish trading relationships with each other to purchase from or sell to each other. Thus the more you maintain and enhance your trading partner relationships, the greater the potential to eliminate dead stock. For more information, refer to Uploading Dead Stock to the Hub.
Improving Customer Service
With Trading Partner Connect, you can streamline your customer relationships by virtually eliminating the time calling manufacturers and distributors to fill customer orders for material you don’t have available. Trading Partner Connect provides a Pricing & Availability option that you can access from Prelude to view your trading partners’ available stock and purchase those items. The time you previously spent calling other vendors for stock availability, faxing orders, and checking on order status can be streamlined into one shorter, easier process. The time you save in fulfilling your customer orders can quickly improve your customer service statistics.
By actively pursuing and maintaining Trading Partner relationships, you will have a larger base of trading partners with whom to trade. A large trading partner base gives you the opportunity to fulfill your customer orders more quickly and economically then your competitors, thus increasing the likeliness that your customers will turn to you first to fill their needs.
