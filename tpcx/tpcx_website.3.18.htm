Searching Online Help
Online help is provided on all screens of the web site. To access the search functionality of the online help on any screen, click Help at the top right.
The Welcome screen appears and provides the basic descriptions of the online help sections of Contents, Index, and Search.
Select the Contents tab at the top left to see sections you can search for information. For example, click on Company Profile shows a submenu of the Company Profile subtopic and the Relationships subtopic. Click the subtopic Company Profile and its subtopics appear. Click any link to view information about that link.
Select the Index tab to see keywords for topics in the online help.
Type a keyword and press ENTER to view topics with the keyword.
Select the Search tab to find all instances of a word or words. To search for two or more words, use the word “and” between them. For example, to search for the words “request” and “relationship” as in Requesting a Trading Partner Relationship, type “request and relationship”. A list of topics with one or both words appears on the left. Click a topic to view more information.
To close the online help, select File > Close.
