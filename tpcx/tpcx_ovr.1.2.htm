What is Trading Partner Connect?
Trading Partner Connect offers organizations a private Internet trading network designed to streamline the commerce process among their distributors, manufacturers/suppliers, and customers. Trading Partner Connect allows you to streamline your business practices by making transactions with other members of the trading network in real time. Instead of mailing printed forms or making phone calls, transactions through Trading Partner Connect are immediately transmitted to trading partners, allowing for faster responses. Trading Partner Connect consists of B2B Marketplace, B2B Alliance, B2B Buyer, and B2B Gateway.
B2B Marketplace
Trading Partner Connect offers members a private area where you can build trading relationships, rationalize items, and post dead stock. You also set up company profile information and specify options such as how to define availability to other trading partners.
It is vital that you use the B2B Marketplace (a.k.a., Trading Partner Connect web site) to rationalize items and build trading partner relationships. If you do not use the B2B Marketplace, you cannot fully take advantage of the unique TPCx features. The Marketplace can be found at www.tpcx.com. For more information, refer to Using the TPCx Website.
B2B Alliance
B2B Alliance is the relationship between distributors that allows you to source from and sell to other distributors directly from Prelude, without phone calls or faxes. The main tool for this is the Trading Partner Connect Pricing & Availability Inquiry in Prelude.
B2B Alliance provides you with online access to your trading partnersâ€™ product lines, which can translate into hundreds of thousands of additional SKUs in your online inventory. Now, when a customer calls to place an order, you can fill it immediately without picking up the phone, saving you valuable time and increasing your profitability and customer service ratings. It is distributor-to-distributor trading and pricing and availability information exchange made fast and accessible.
B2B Buyer
B2B Buyer is the relationship between distributors and their manufacturers and suppliers. It provides electronic purchasing and invoicing, and shows stock availability information between Trading Partner Connect distributors and their suppliers. The main tool for this is the Flat File setup in Prelude.
With B2B Buyer, you gain greater and more efficient access to important supplier information for product availability and pricing. For example, you can send and receive updates on purchase orders sent to and from your trading partners, which can eliminate the frustration of dealing with placing and receiving calls about the status of ship dates, order fulfillment, and order information.
In addition, B2B Buyer facilitates real-time procurement of products. The entire process of finding information about products and then ordering those products is automated, thus B2B Buyer can reduce your VAN charges by sending P/Os and receiving invoices electronically.
B2B Gateway
B2B Gateway allows communication with other Internet marketplaces and trade organizations. With your connection to Trading Partner Connect, you gain access to more customers via Internet marketplaces.
B2B Gateway provides the digital infrastructure required for distributors to participate in the global electronic marketplace. Through extensive tools and utilities, you can enable customer-specific product catalogs, post excess or dead inventory for auction, efficiently respond to RFQs, provide real-time pricing and availability, communicate key order and shipment information, and transfer funds electronically.
