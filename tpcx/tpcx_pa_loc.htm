Viewing Availability on the Hub
Customer Price & Availability and Inventory Inquiry both allow you to view price and availability for rationalized products on TPCx if the product is rationalized for TPCx.
To view the TPCx price and availability, choose Action Bar > Inquiries > TPCx Price/Availability Request. Enter a quantity you want and press ENTER to view the TPCx Price/Availability Results screen.
 
As data is gathered, a message appears every few seconds to allow you to continue searching or to stop the search. If you see a vendor you’d like to use, choose Stop. If you want to keep searching, choose Continue.
Field Descriptions
 
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number from the inquiry screen.
Requested
The requested quantity entered.
Partner
The trading partners setup on the TPCx hub available for purchasing.
Description
The description of the trading partner.
Quantity
The quantity available for purchasing from the trading partner.
Cost
The cost of the item. If trading partner is not found, the cost is $0.
Extension
The extension of the item (Quantity X Cost).
Status
The status of the trading partner connect request for price/availability:
•
RECEIVED - your message was successful.
•
MESSAGE - the item is dead stock or there is an error message.
•
TIMED OUT - TPCx times out.
Comment
If Status is MESSAGE, the following may appear:
•
SALE - the item was found with an existing trading partner.
•
NO TP REL - the item was found with a trading partner with whom you do not have a relationship.
•
MESSAGE - press F6-Message to view the message.
F5-Review
View availability from all warehouses for the trading partner selected.
F6-Message
View messages from TPCx about errors or additional information.
F7-Purchase
Not applicable from an inquiry.
F9-Refresh
Refreshes the results.
