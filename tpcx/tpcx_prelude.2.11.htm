Sharing Data with TPCx
To get the full benefit from TPCx, you must establish good relationships with your trading partners. To maintain those relationships, you must define your trading partners, share data with them, and update dead stock.
 
The TPCx menu has two options that you should use only when directed to by Activant:
•
Sending Your TPCx Spoke Version - sends a request to the TPCx hub for spoke version. TPCx must have your version of TPCx on the spoke (Prelude version number).
•
Synchronizing Your Vendor Data with the Hub - This process sends a message to the hub for the re-synch process for vendor rationalization. Rationalized vendors at the hub can be in synch with items marked as rationalized at the spoke system (Prelude). The re-rationalization request is sent to the hub. A response back from the hub is received and the vendors are rationalized again.
