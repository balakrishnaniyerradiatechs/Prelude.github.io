Selecting Dead Stock
To select dead stock to post to Trading Partner Connect, type M:TPCX.MENU and choose Dead Stock Identification. Then use the following selection criteria to find dead stock.
Selection Criteria
•
Customer (For Pricing) - the customer to use for pricing the dead stock. This customer defaults from the Dead Stock Customer in Company Maintenance.
•
Warehouse (For Pricing) - the warehouse to use for pricing the dead stock.
•
TPCx Vendor - the TPCx vendor.
•
Product Range - the beginning and/or ending product number to use.
•
Product Line - the product line to use.
•
Date Last Sold - the last date the item(s) were sold.
•
Date Last Purchased - the last date the item(s) were purchased.
•
Include Item Never Sold - indicate if you want to include items never sold.
•
Include Item Never Purchased - indicate if you want to include items never purchased.
•
Inventory Class - the maximum inventory class.
•
TPCx Rationalized Status - Indicate which products you want to view:
-
Y – view only products that are rationalized.
-
N – view only products that are not rationalized.
-
Null – include both rationalized and non-rationalized products.
•
TPCx Dead Item Status - Indicate if you want to include items that are considered TPCx dead stock.
Press F2-Execute to evaluate your items for dead stock. The TPCx Dead Stock Report/Update Screen appears.
