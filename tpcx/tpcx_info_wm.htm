Warehouse Maintenance
To allow price and availability of products in a warehouse to be available from Trading Partner Connect, your warehouses must be set up to work with TPCx. Choose System Parameter > Maintenance > Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > TPCx Information to view the TPCx Information screen.
Fields/Function Keys
 
Field/Function Key
Description
TPCx Warehouse
Indicates if this warehouse’s products are available to TPCx:
•
Y - the warehouse’s products are available (default).
•
N - the warehouse’s products are not available.
F2-Save
Saves the setting.
