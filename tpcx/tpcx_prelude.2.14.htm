Uploading Dead Stock to the Hub
Dead stock items must be rationalized in order to post, buy and/or sell. It is highly recommended that you rationalize all lines that you buy and sell; so that once the dead stock items have been determined, they can be posted easily to the hub. This updates dead stock in Prelude.
 
Items cannot be rationalized as dead stock individually. They must be rationalized based on the vendor code associated with the part numbers. For example, 25 part numbers for a vendor are determined to be dead stock items. These 25 items cannot be singled out for rationalization; you must request rationalization for the entire vendor line.
