Environmental Charge Code Maintenance (/ECCM)
Use this maintenance to create and maintain environmental codes and charges. These codes are assigned to rental products in Product Maintenance. Each environmental code must be a valid service product before being added.
Path: Rental > Maintenance > Environmental Charge Code Maintenance
•
Product Number: the service product number to be used as the environmental charge line item on a rental order.
•
Percent: the percent to calculate the environmental charge for products with this environmental charge code.
