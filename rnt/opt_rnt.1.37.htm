Rental Pre-Billing
Pre-billing allows you to bill rental orders at the beginning of the rental for the expected rental period (i.e., you bill the customer when they rent the item, not when they return it). Since the customer may return the item before the rental period expires, you can automatically create a credit line for an early return on a pre-billed rental order. Pre-billing may be used with periodic billing to pre-bill for the next rental period. In other words, you could use periodic billing to pre-bill the customer for the next monthâ€™s use. This process works with weekly or monthly rental period.
