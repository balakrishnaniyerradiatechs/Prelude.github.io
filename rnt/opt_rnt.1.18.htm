Assigning Codes to Products
To use damage waiver, environmental, liability, and/or fuel charges, you must assign codes to rental products in product maintenance.
Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action > Addtl Opts > Rental Information
•
Damage waivers - assign a Damage Waiver Product Number from Damage Waiver Code Maintenance to the product. You must also specify a Rental Retail Value to compare to the customer’s Physical Damage Coverage to determine if a damage waiver charge should be added to a rental order.
•
Environmental - assign an Environmental Charge P/N from Environmental Charge Code Maintenance to the product. (e.g., ENV)
•
Liability - assign a Liability Ins Charge P/N from Damage Waiver Code Maintenance to the product (e.g., LIAB). You must also specify a Rental Retail Value to compare to the customer’s Physical Damage Coverage to determine if a damage waiver charge should be added to a rental order.
•
Fuel - for each product, you must indicate if a fuel charge applies, the fuel charge product number, and fuel type:
-
Fuel Charge Applies: determines if fuel charges are applied to the rental order.
-
Y - apply fuel changes to the rental return.
-
N - do not apply fuel charges to the rental return.
-
T - apply fuel charges to the rental return based on the number of gallons in the tank Specify the size of the tank in Equipment Maintenance.
-
Fuel Charge Product Number: the service product number for fuel charges.
-
Fuel Type: the fuel type for this product from in Fuel Type Maintenance.
