Renting by Square Foot
Square Footage Factor in Product Maintenance allows you to calculate a rental rate based on a price per square foot, percent of list, or a total amount. The Rental Retail Value is used to calculate the extended list price for all items on the order. These values are part of the formula for % List, the percent value of the square footage price compared to the list price.
Product Maintenance (/PM)
Use Square Footage Factor to allow rental items to be calculated by the square foot in Product Maintenance.
Path: Inventory Maintenance Product Maintenance > Action > Addtl Optns > Rental Information
Enter a numeric amount in Square Footage Factor to be calculated when rented and a Rental Retail Value.
Rental Order Entry (/ROE)
The square footage factor for a rental item is calculated based on square footage price, the percent of list price (Square Footage Factor), or the lump sum total price in rental order entry (Rental Retail Value).
Path: Rental > Rental Order Entry > Action > Order Func > Reprice Order Options > Pricing by Square Foot
•
Price: the price for the rental item per square foot. This field is calculated by dividing Lump Sum Total by the total extended amount of the square footage for each of the line items that have a value in the Square Footage Factor in Product Maintenance. The total extended amount of square footage is calculated by multiplying the quantity times the value in Square Footage Factor. If this field is overwritten at time of entry, the % List and Lump Sum Total fields are recalculated. The new % List price is derived by the total extended amount of square footage using the new Sqft Price entered and dividing by the total extended list price comprised of the sum of the quantities for each line multiplied by the list price. The new Lump Sum Total is derived from the new Sqft Price entered and multiplying it by the total extended amount of square footage comprised of the sum of the quantities of each line multiplied by the Square Footage Factor setup in Product Maintenance.
•
% List: the percent of the square footage compared to the list price. This field is calculated by dividing Lump Sum Total by the total extended amount of all items on the order using the list price. If this field is overwritten at time of entry, the Sqft Price and Lump Sum Total fields are recalculated. The new Sqft Price is derived by the Lump Sum Total divided by the total extended amount of square footage comprised of the sum of the quantities of each line multiplied by the Square Footage Factor setup in Product Maintenance. The new Lump Sum Total is derived by multiplying the new % List value by the total extended list price comprised of the sum of the quantities for each line multiplied by the list price.
•
Total: the total value of the order. This field is calculated by adding the total extended amounts of all line items on the order. If this field is overwritten at time of entry, Sqft Price and % List are recalculated. The new Sqft Price is derived by the Lump Sum Total divided by the total extended amount of square footage comprised of the sum of the quantities of each line multiplied by the Square Footage Factor in Product Maintenance. The new % List is derived by dividing the Lump Sum Total by the total extended list price of the sum of the quantities for each line multiplied by the list price.
The Net Rate (Rental > Rental Order Entry > Line Item Rental Detail) in Line Item Rental Detail is calculated by multiplying the list price by the % of List. The difference in the Gross Rate and Net Rate is shown as a discount.
For Example:
•
Net Rate for Product 10524 is $41.84.
Calculation of Net Rate: .4651 (% of List in Pricing By Square Footage screen) X 89.95 (Rental Retail Value established for Product 10524 in Product Maintenance) = $89.95.

The following is logic determines the square footage values:
1
Calculate the extended list price for each item on the order with the square foot factor. The list price is the Rental Retail Value.
Calculation: Qty x Rental Retail Value: 50 X 89.95 = 4497.50; 75 X 71.26 = 5344.50
2
Total the extended list price total for all items on the order with square foot factor.
Calculation: 4497.50 + 5344.50 = 9842.00
3
Calculate the extended list price total for each item on the order without square foot factor.
Calculation: Qty x List Price: 75 X 9.29 = 696.75; 50 X 4.23 = 211.50
4
Total the extended list price total for all items on the order without square foot factor.
Calculation: 696.75 + 211.50 = 908.25
5
Add the extended list price total for all items on the order.
Calculation: 9842.00 + 908.25 = 10750.25
6
Calculate the total extended price/ft2 for items with a value in Square Footage Factor.
Calculation: Qty x square footage factor x ft2 price/box.
50 X (8 X 3.1250) = 1250
75 X (16 X 3.1250) = 3750
7
Capture the total extended ft2 for items with a Square Footage Factor.
Calculation: Qty x Square Footage Factor.
50 X 8 = 400
75 X 16 = 1200
400 + 1200 = 1600
8
total value of all line items on the order.
Calculation:
50 X 20 (rental/day) = 1000
75 X 20 (rental/day) = 1500
75 X 20 (rental/day) = 1500
50 X 20 (rental/day) = 1000
1000 + 1500 + 1500 + 1000 = 5000
9
Calculate the percent of square footage in list price.
Calculation: Lump Sum Total/ Extended list price for all items
5000 / 10750.25 = .4651 or 46.51%
10
Determine the price for rental item per square foot.
Calculation: Lump Sum Total / G (Extended square foot total for all items)
5000 / 1600 = $3.1250 sq. ft. price
11
Override Sq. Ft. Price with $3.200.
% of List recalculation: Extended ft2 price/ Extended list price total
% of List: 50 X (8 X 3.200) = 1280
75 X (16 X 3.200) = 3840
1280 + 3840 = 5120
5120 / 10750.25 = .4762 or 47.62%
Lump Sum Total recalculation: ft2. price X extended ft2 total
Lump Sum Total: 3.200 X 1600 = 5120
12
Override % of List with 48%.
Square Ft. Price recalculation: Lump Sum Total/ Extended square foot total
Square Ft. Price: 5160.12 / 1600 = 3.225 (value displayed for Square Ft. Price now)
Lump Sum Total recalculation: % of List X Extended list price total (E)
 Lump Sum Total: .48 X 10750.25 = 5160.12 (value displayed for Lump Sum now)
13
Override Lump Sum Total with 5200.00.
% of List recalculation: Lump Sum Total / Extended list price total (E)
% of List: 5200 / 10750.25 = .4837 or 48.37% (value displayed for % of List now)
Square Ft. Price recalculation: Lump Sum Total / Extended square foot total (G)
Square Ft. Price: 5200 / 1600 = 3.2500 (value displayed for Square Ft. Price now)
Net Rate = % of List x list price
