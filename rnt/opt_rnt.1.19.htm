Assigning Codes to Customers
To use damage waiver, environmental, and/or liability charges, you must assign codes to customers in Customer Maintenance.
Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > F5-Add Info > F6-Rental
•
Physical Damage Coverage: the amount of coverage a customer has, which is used to determine if a damage waiver or liability charge are added to rental orders.
•
Physical Damage Expiration Date: the date the physical damage coverage expires.
•
Environmental Charge: indicates if the customer should be assessed environmental charges (overrides Rental Parameters). Set this flag to ‘Y’ to default Environmental Charge in Rental Order Header to Y for this customer. Set this flag to N to default Environmental Charge in Rental Order Header to N for this customer. You can change this flag for the rental order.
•
Liability Insurance Limit: the insurance limit for this customer.
•
Liability Expiration Date: the date the customer's insurance expires.
