Damage Waiver and Liability Code Maintenance (/DWCM)
Use this maintenance to create and edit damage waiver and liability codes and charges. You can assign the codes to rental products in Product Maintenance. Each code must also be a valid service product before you add it.
Path: Rental > Maintenance > Damage Waiver Code Maintenance
•
Product Number: the service product number for the damage waiver charge line item on a rental order.
•
Percent: percent to calculate damage waiver charge for products with this code.
•
Pct Off: the percent to deduct from Percent if a liability charge is also assessed on an order. For example, if damage waiver is 10% and Pct Off is 2%, the damage waiver on the rental order is 8% if damage waiver and liability insurance charges were both added to the order. If only damage waiver charges are added to the rental order, Percent (10%) is used to calculate the damage waiver charges.
