Rental Sales Tax
in Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance, you can set a Rental Tax Rate % rate to use on rental orders instead of Tax Rate %. If you sell a rental item Sales Order Entry using Rental Unit Sold (Action > Prod Func), the Tax Rate % is used to calculate the sales tax because the rental item is being sold as regular inventory.
Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Sales Tax > Tax Jurisdictions (/TJM)
Maintenance Settings:Rental Sales Tax
