Order Log Inquiry (/OLI)
Path: Order Processing > Inquiries Sales Order Inquiries Sales Order Log Inquiry
The Order Log Inquiry has the following codes for Periodic Billing:
•
RPB - rental order invoiced through periodic billing. Reference is the billing date.
•
LDE (Line Delete) - charge (damage waiver, environmental, liability) deleted .
•
PBR (Periodic Billing Removed) – an item was removed from Periodic Billing
