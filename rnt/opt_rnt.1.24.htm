Entering Equipment Attachments (/EAE)
Path: Rental > Equipment Subsystem > Equipment Attachment Entry
This process is used to attach an item to a equipment Id number (EIN).
•
Equip. #: Press ENTER to assign the next transaction number, or enter a transaction to change.
•
Entry Date: Defaults to today’s date but can be changed.
•
Equip Id#: Enter the EIN (Equipment Id Number) or serial number that the items will be attached to.
•
Product#/Description: Enter the products to be associated with the EIN.
•
Quantity: Enter the quantity of the item.
•
F5 - Current Attachments: Displays any items that are already attached to this EIN.
