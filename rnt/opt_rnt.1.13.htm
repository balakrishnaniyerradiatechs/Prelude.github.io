Tracking Meter Readings
Prelude can track measurements for meter readings within the Rental module for products that require it. You can specify measurement requirement in Product Maintenance.
Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action > Addtl Opts > Rental Information
•
Measurements Required: Select H - Hour Meter, O - Odometer, W - Wear Charges.
Release Confirmation Entry (/RCE)
If the rental product requires hour meter readings, you can enter a single serial number and its meter readings. If hours are not required, you can enter multiple serial numbers.
Path: Rental > Release Confirmation
If the existing meter out is different from your entry, the following message appear. Choose Y to use your entry.
OUT Meter Reading Does Not Match The Current Meter Reading For This Equipment. Do You Wish To Update The Current Meter Reading.
After you enter additional information, a screen with date, times, and total rental amount appears. Enter an off rent date and time to stop the rental and calculate and roll up the number of days on rent and convert to months, weeks, and days. Adjust the dates and times or the rental periods or rental rates. When the information and order totals are correct, press F2 to save the order and choose Complete. The standard sales order completion screen allows you to add charges and adjust sales tax.
Rental Order Entry - Return (/ROE)
During rental return, you must enter meter in value equal to or greater than the meter out value. The meter out value is updated at invoicing with the meter in value.
Path: Rental > Rental Order Entry
Rental Exchange Entry (/REE)
After you enter the original and new product numbers, you can enter detail information in the Line Item Detail screen. Non-serialized products only require the exchange quantity, re-price flag, and reason code. All other fields are for serialized products.
Path: Rental > Processes > Rental Exchange Entry
•
Original Meter Out: defaults to the Meter Out reading from the rental order. Required if Measurements Required in Product Maintenance is H (hour meter) or O (odometer).
•
Original Meter In: the current meter reading for the unit being exchanged. Required if Measurements Required in Product Maintenance is H (hour meter) or O (odometer).
•
New Meter Out: the meter reading for the unit replacing the returned unit. Required if Measurements Required in Product Maintenance is H (hour meter) or O (odometer).
After you enter all the exchange line items, press F2-Save save and close the order. You can print, complete, or suspend the exchange or to return to the line item detail screen.
