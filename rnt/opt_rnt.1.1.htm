Rental Status Maintenance
The rental status of a serialized product is shown in Rental Status Maintenance. You can also create and maintain serialized and non-serialized rental products.
Path: Rental > Maintenance > Rental Status Maintenance (/RESM)
•
Product Number: the rental product number.
•
Serial Number: the serial number of the serialized rental item.
•
Rental Unit#: the rental unit number created at Inventory Type Conversion Entry.
•
Warehouse: the warehouse number of the order.
•
Order Number: the rental order number for the product and serial number.
•
Rental Status: the product’s rental status. Refer to the following table for codes.
