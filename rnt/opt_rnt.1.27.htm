Equipment Statistical Information Maintenance (/ESIM)
This program can be used to maintain statistical information for equipment.
•
EIN: Enter the EIN to perform maintenance on.
•
Product Number: Press return to start at the beginning of the list of attached products or enter the product.
•
Mfr Serial Number: Enter the serial number.
•
Receive Date: Enter the date the item was received.
•
Sold Date: If applicable, enter the date the item was sold.
•
Selling Price: Enter the selling price for the item.
•
Transfer to Rental Date: If applicable, enter the date the item was transferred to a rental item.
