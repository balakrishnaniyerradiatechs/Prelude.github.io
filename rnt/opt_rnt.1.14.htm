Rental Charges
Overview
Path: System Parameter > Maintenance > Option Parameters > Rental Parameters
Rental orders can have up to four special charges based on flags in Rental Parameters:
•
Damage Waiver - charge that the customer incurs for any necessary repairs for broken items. If a damage waiver is not charged and the rental equipment needs repair after the rental, the customer will pay all repair costs for the rental item. In Rental Order Entry, the total rental retail value of the rental items is compared to the amount of physical damage coverage for the customer. If the total retail value exceeds the amount of physical damage coverage, a damage waiver charge is added. Set the flag in Rental Parameters to Y check rental orders for environmental charges. You can override this flag in Customer Maintenance and Rental Order Entry.
•
Environmental - charge for environmental costs associated with the rental order. Set the flag in Rental Parameters to Y check rental orders for environmental charges. You can override this flag in Customer Maintenance and Rental Order Entry.
•
Liability - charge for liability insurance costs associated with the order. If a customer does not have liability insurance or the insurance is expired a liability insurance charge is added. Set the flag in Rental Parameters to Y check rental orders for environmental charges. You can override this flag in Rental Order Entry.
•
Fuel - charges that are automatically added based on rental return.
