G/L Postings for Selling Rental Items
Prelude allows you to sell a rental product as a regular inventory item either by selling directly from a rental order or transferring a USED product in Inventory Type Conversion and selling it. The cost is the higher of the book value of the item, which is the salvage value or the installation cost minus accumulated depreciation. In both methods, the disposal information in the fixed asset record is updated and the item’s equipment status changes to S (sold). The item post to the G/L either as a fixed asset or as regular inventory, depending on Sell Rent Prod as Inventory in Rental Parameters:
•
N - treat rental products as fixed assets (default). For example, if a rental product was sold for $1000, the postings are:
