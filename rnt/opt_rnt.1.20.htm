Assessing Charges in Rental Orders
Assessing Damage Waiver, Liability, and Environmental Charges
When you save the rental order header a screen with rental information appears.
Path: Rental > Rental Order Entry
Set the following flags to Y to evaluate the charge on the order or N to ignore the charge:
•
Damage Waiver?
•
Environmental Charge?
•
Liability Insurance Charge?
When you add a rental item that is be subject to damage waiver, liability, or environmental charges, the following message appears.
Damage And Theft Required. Do You Wish To Continue?
You can continue with the rental order knowing that rental charges may be applied to the rental order. When you complete the order, the charges are evaluated and added:
•
Damage waiver - the total rental retail value of all rental products is compared to the physical damage coverage for the customer. If the total exceeds the customer’s coverage or if the physical damage coverage has expired, a damage waiver charge is calculated as a percent of the rental amount based on the Damaged Waiver Code. If a liability insurance charge is also added, the damage waiver percent is reduced by the Pct Off for the Damage Waiver Code.
•
Environmental - calculated as a percent of the rental amount based on the percent of the Environmental Charge Code.
•
Liability - if the total order exceeds the customer’s liability limit or if the liability insurance is expired, a liability charge is added based on the Damage Waiver Code.
If any charges are added, a message notifies you of the charges.
Additional Charges of 16.00 Have Been Added To This Order!
