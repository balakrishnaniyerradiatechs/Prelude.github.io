Rental Sales Representatives
You can specify rental sales representatives in Customer Maintenance and Customer Ship-To Maintenance to use with rental orders instead of the regular sales rep. If you sell a rental item using Rental Unit Sold, the sales representatives do not automatically change from the rental to the regular sales rep. You must manually change the reps.
Customer Maintenance (/CM)
Enter rental sales representatives in the Rental Module screen in Customer Maintenance.
Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > F5-Add Info > F6-Rental
•
Salesrep #1: the primary sales rep for the customer’s rental orders.
•
Salesrep #2: the secondary sales rep for the customer’s rental orders.
Customer Ship-To Maintenance (/CSTM)
Enter rental sales representatives in the Rental Information screen in Customer Ship-To Maintenance to use with the ship-to on a rental order.
Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F5-Add Info > Action > Rent Info
•
Salesrep #1: the primary sales rep for the customer ship-to number’s rental orders.
•
Salesrep #2: the secondary sales rep for the customer ship-to number’s rental orders.
