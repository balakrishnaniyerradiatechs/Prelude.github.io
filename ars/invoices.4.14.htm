Customer Maintenance
Options in Customer Maintenance determine service charge calculations for each customer for overdue invoices.
To calculate the service charge percent for each customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance.
2
Enter the Customer number.
3
Press F6-A/R to open the A/R Information screen.
4
For Service Charge %, enter the service charge percent to charge this customer for past due invoices. If you do not want this customer to pay service charges, set this field to zero or leave it blank.
5
For Service Charge Days, enter the number of days overdue an invoice must be to incur a service charge. This value overrides Current Days Limit in Auto Service Charge Calculation (refer to Automatically Assessing Service Charges). Leave blank to use the value from the Auto Service Charge Entry screen.
6
Press F2-Save twice to save the service charge information.
Refer to “Customer Maintenance” in the Order Processing documentation for a more detailed explanation of each field in Customer Maintenance.
