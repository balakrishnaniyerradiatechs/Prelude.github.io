Editing Customer Invoice Rebates
Customer rebates are calculated according to the rebate program assigned to the customer and the date the customer paid the invoice (refer to Creating a Customer Rebate Program). If the customer paid an invoice after the date defined by their rebate program, you can use Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance to override the eligibility setting to allow them to receive the rebate anyway. You can also disallow the customer from receiving a rebate for a selected invoice.
To edit a customer rebate:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance to open the Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer whose rebate calculations you want to view or edit. If rebates were calculated on invoices paid by the customer, the following information displays:
•
Customer Rebate - The rebate program assigned to the customer.
•
Invoice# - The invoices eligible for rebates.
•
Due Date - The date the invoice must be paid to receive the rebate.
•
Days - The Eligible Days To Pay defined in Customer Rebate Maintenance. If the rebate program has an Eligibility Type of I, this is the number of days from the invoice date that the invoice must be paid to earn the rebate).
•
Invoice Amount - The total amount of the invoice including taxes, freight, and other charges.
•
Balance Due - The balance due on the invoice.
•
Applied - The amount paid (to date) against the invoice.
•
Eligible Amount - The amount of the invoice that is eligible for a rebate (excludes tax, freight, or other charges):
•
Elg %, the percent of the order that was paid in the eligibility period. If the entire amount of an invoice is paid within the eligibility period, then the eligibility percent is 100. If only part of the invoice is paid within the period, then only the percent eligible displays.
•
Period - The Credit Report Freq from Customer Rebate Maintenance that determines how often rebates are paid.
-
A - Annually.
-
Q - Quarterly.
-
M - Monthly.
-
S - Semi-monthly.
3
For Eligible, determine if the customer should receive the rebate amount calculated by their rebate program for this invoice:
•
Y - The customer will receive the rebate amount calculated for this invoice. If the customer paid the invoice by the date defined in their rebate program, this flag will be initially be set to Y.
•
N - The customer will not receive a rebate for this invoice. If the customer did not pay the invoice by the date defined in their rebate program, this flag will be initially be set to N.
Reviewing Invoices
•
F5-Review opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry to display the A/R information for an invoice.
•
F7-Cumm Bal opens the Cumulative Balance Inquiry to show open balances for the customer.
4
Press F2-Finish to close the screen and to save any changes.
