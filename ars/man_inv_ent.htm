Manual Invoice Entry screen
If you need to create customer invoice that does not affect inventory or sales, you can use the Manual Invoice Entry screen to manually enter the invoice. The manual invoice updates accounts receivable and the general ledger but no invoice or documentation prints and no sales data is updated (e.g., order history, sales analysis). Refer to Manually Entering an Invoice for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Manual Invoice Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice Number
The invoice number used to bill the customer:
•
Press ENTER over NEW for the next system generated number.
•
Enter your own invoice number.
Customer
The customer to bill.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number, if applicable.
Salesrep
The salesrep for this customer. The default is Salesrep One assigned to the customer in Customer Maintenance.
Invoice Date
The date of the invoice. This date is used to calculate the fiscal period and due date.
Payment Terms
The payment terms code that applies to this invoice (defaults from Customer Maintenance or Customer Ship-To Maintenance).
Discount Date
The date by which the customer must pay the invoice to receive the Discount Amount, if you enter one.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due. Defaults from the Payment Terms on the sales order.
Invoice Amount
The amount of the invoice.
Discount Amount
The discount the customer receives if they pay the invoice by the discount due date.
Invoice Status
The status of the invoice:
•
Blank - None.
•
Bankruptcy.
•
Collections.
•
Disputed. Include Disputed Invoices in Collections in Additional Option Parameters determines if disputed invoices are included in the Credit Collections Subsystem
•
Litigation.
•
Insufficient funds.
•
Rebate.
•
Write-off.
Repl. Equipment
Indicates if the invoice is for replacement equipment:
•
Y - The invoice is for replacement equipment.
•
N - The invoice is not for replacement equipment.
Ln#
The line number for the general ledger distribution amounts.
Div
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The warehouse location.
Dep
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The general ledger account that this invoice should update.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
Dist Amount
The amount to distribute to the corresponding account. You can enter multiple accounts and amounts, but the total distribution amount must equal the invoice amount.
F2-Save
Generates the manual invoice and updates the general ledger.
F5-Comments
Allows you to enter any information to explain the purpose of the manual invoice.
