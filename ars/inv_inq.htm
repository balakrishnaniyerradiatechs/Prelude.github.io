Invoice Detail Inquiry
The Invoice Detail Inquiry displays detail information for a customer invoice.
Viewing a Customer Invoice
To view detail information for an invoice:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Invoice Detail Inquiry to open the Invoice Detail Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Invoice # to view.
Detailed information for the invoices appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice #
The customer’s invoice number. An asterisk (*) appears in the next field if notes exists for the invoice. Press F6-Notes to view the notes.
Customer
The customer associated with the invoice.
Customer P/O#
The customer’s purchase order number.
Order#
The sales order number.
Sell
The selling warehouse.
Merchandise
The total amount of merchandise sold to the customer on this order.
Freight
The amount of freight charged to the customer.
Tax Amt
The amount of tax charged to the customer.
Other
Any other miscellaneous charges on this invoice.
Inv Date
The date this order was invoiced.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive the cash discount.
Disc Amount
The discount amount that the customer will receive if they pay their invoice by the discount date.
Disc Taken
The amount of the discount taken by the customer, if the invoice is already paid.
Ex
Indicates if the order is excluded from the credit limit test:
•
Y - The value of the order is excluded from the credit limit check calculations.
•
N - The order value is included in the credit limit check.
Refer to Excluding Sales Orders From the Credit Limit.
Cons Inv#
The consolidated invoice number, if the invoice was set up for consolidated invoice printing (Consolidate Inv? set to Y in the sales order header).
Stat
The payment status of the invoice:
•
Blank - None.
•
B - Bankruptcy.
•
C - Collections.
•
D - Disputed.
•
L - Litigation.
•
N - Insufficient funds.
•
P - An unpaid customer invoice has an open RGA.
•
R - Rebate.
•
W - Write-off.
Trn Date
The date that the customer paid the invoice.
Check Number
The check number on which the customer paid the invoice.
Check Amount
The total amount of the check used to pay this invoice.
Cash Amount
The amount of cash that was applied to this invoice. Refer to Receiving Customer Payments.
Misc Amount
The amount of cash that was entered as a miscellaneous amount to write off for an underpaid invoice. Refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance.
Chrgbck Amount
The amount of cash that was charged back to the customer. Refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
Balance Due
The amount the customer still owes on the invoice.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to list detail shipping and pricing information for an invoiced sales order.
F6-Notes
Displays any notes entered during Cash Receipts Entry regarding this invoice. You can also enter new notes.
F7-Pending RGA’s
Opens the Pending RGA Inquiry screen to list any RGAs that are pending against this invoice.
Action Bar
Multi Level Discounts
Opens the Multi Level Discounts screen to display the discounts earned if the invoice is paid on time. Multi-level discounts can be set up for a payment terms code in A/R Payment Terms Maintenance > F5-Multi-Disc Level.
Enter Release#
Allows you to enter a release number to access an invoice number for a customer.
