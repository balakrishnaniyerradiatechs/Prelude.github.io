Updating a Batch
Once the invoices are correctly matched, you must update the customerâ€™s accounts receivable information to the general ledger. If the batch does not balance, you will not be able to complete the update.
To update a batch:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Update Procedure. The High Volume Cash Update Procedure screen appears.
2
Enter the Batch Number to update. Leave blank to update all matched batches.
3
For Default Warehouse, enter the warehouse to post to if no warehouse is found for the invoice. This warehouse could be a corporate warehouse or a dummy warehouse used just to catch posting errors.
4
Press F2-Save. If all of the invoices balance, the matched invoices are marked as paid and the payments post to the G/L.
5
If the batch is out of balance, the following message appears:
Batch 000564 Is Out Of Balance! Process Aborted
6
Press Continue. You must edit the batch (refer to Manually Matching and Editing Invoices) before you can update it. Once the batch balances, you can update the batch.
