Adjusting Tax and Freight
If a customer under or over pays an invoice due to a discrepancy in taxes or freight, you can adjust the tax or freight to account for a partial payment.
Related Reports
Description
Monthly Sales Tax Adjustment Detail Report (Order Processing Module)
Lists tax adjustments in Cash Receipts Entry for a fiscal period.
To adjust for freight or tax discrepancies:
1
In the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen, choose A for M/C. The Credit Adjustment Entry screen appears.
2
For Inbound Freight, change or verify the freight cost to bring the product into your warehouse.
3
For Outbound Freight, change or verify the freight cost to ship the material to your customer.
4
For Taxable Amount, change or verify the value of the invoice to tax.
5
For NonTaxable Amount, change or verify the value of the invoice that should not be taxed.
6
For each tax jurisdiction (Jur), change or verify the following:
