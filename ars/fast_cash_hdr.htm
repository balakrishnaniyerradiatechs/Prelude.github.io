High Volume Cash Receipts Entry screen
If you receive a large volume of payments per month from customers via EDI, flat files, or spreadsheets, you can use High Volume Cash Receipts Entry to expedite the cash application process. You can compare your invoice number to the customerâ€™s order number, invoice number, release number, or customer P/O number. Refer to Entering a High Volume Cash Receipts Batch.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Batch Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Batch Number
The batch number to identify the group of checks.
Batch Amount
The amount of all the checks in the batch.
Deposit Date
The date the batch is deposited. This date determines the fiscal period for general ledger postings.
Entered Net
The amount of cash applied in the batch. This field is not updated until after cash is applied to invoices in the High Volume Cash Detail Entry screen.
Bank Code
The code for the bank where the cash is deposited.
Deposit Comments
Any comments regarding this batch.
F2-Save
Closes the High Volume Cash Receipts Entry screen.
F4-Del
Deletes cash receipts batch.
