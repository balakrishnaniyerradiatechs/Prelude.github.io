Manually Assessing or Editing a Service Charge
To manually generate a service charge invoice or to modify a previously generated service charge:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Manual Service Charge Entry to open the Manual Service Charge Entry screen.
2
For Invoice Number, choose from the following:
•
To create a new service charge invoice, type N to assign the next system generated service charge number.
•
To edit an existing service charge invoice, enter the service charge invoice number.The remaining fields default from the invoice.
3
Enter the Customer for whom you want to generate a service charge.
4
For Due Date, enter the date the invoice is due.
5
For Invoice Basis Amt, enter the base amount to use to calculate the service charge (e.g., total amount of overdue invoices). This amount is multiplied by the Service Charge % for the customer in Customer Maintenance to determine the service charge.
6
If necessary, change the Invoice Amount. This value is calculated according to the Accounts Receivable Option Parameters and the service charge settings for the Customer (refer to Setting Maintenance Options for Service Charges).
7
For Print Flag, determine when the invoice prints (or generates):
•
Y - Print the invoice during Service Charge Invoice Printing (refer to Printing Service Charge Invoices).
•
N - Do not print the invoice,
8
Press F2-Save to generate the service charge invoice.
Service Charge Listing
To list all service charge invoices generated through the Auto Service Charge Calculation or Manual Service Charge Maintenance, select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Service Charge Listing.
