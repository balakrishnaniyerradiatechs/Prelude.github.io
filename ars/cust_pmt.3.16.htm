Applying EDI Payments in High-Volume Cash Receipts
If you receive a large volume of payments per month from customers via EDI, a flat file, or a spreadsheet, you can use High Volume Cash Receipts to expedite cash application. When the bank sends electronic notification that a payment was made, you can use Prelude to search for open invoices by comparing the customer’s reference number (e.g., P/O number, release number, invoice number, or order number) to your invoice number.
Accounts Receivable Option Parameters for High Volume Cash Receipts
High Volume C/R Method in A/R defines the criteria required to find a customer invoice:
•
I - Invoice. Match on invoice number only.
•
R - Reference. Match on any reference number (e.g., invoice, order, customer P/O, release number).
