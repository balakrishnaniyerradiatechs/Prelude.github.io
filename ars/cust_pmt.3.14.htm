Refunding a Customer Account for Over-Payment
If a customer has funds remaining after you apply cash to their open invoices, and you want to issue a check to the customer rather than to keep the extra cash in their account, you can apply the extra amount to an invoice and then issue a credit. The check will then be processed through Accounts Payable.
Miscellaneous Vendor
To generate customer refunds, the vendor 00000M must be set up with A/P Payment terms MV (Miscellaneous Vendor). A separate check prints for each customer using vendor 00000M.
To over-apply cash to a customer invoice:
1
In the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen (refer to Manually Applying Cash to Individual Invoices) enter the full amount the customer paid in Cash Appl. If it is an overpayment, a warning message appears to let you know that it is an overpayment.
2
Press ENTER.
3
The Balance for the invoice becomes a negative number.
To generate the credit balance refund:
1
Go to Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Generate Credit Balance Refund to open the Generate Credit Balance Refund screen.
2
Enter the Bank Code or press F3 to search.
3
For Customer#, enter the customer number or a keyword or press F3 to search.
4
For Invoice#, enter the invoice number that you want to credit or press F3 to search.
5
Edit the Refund Amt if necessary. It will be a negative number.
6
Repeat steps 4-5 for all invoices to refund.
7
Press F2-Save to generate the refund. Refer to the Accounts Payable documentation for information on generating payments.
