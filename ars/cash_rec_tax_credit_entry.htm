Credit Adjustment Entry screen
If the customer underpays or overpays an invoice due to a discrepancy in freight or taxes, you can use the Credit Adjustment Entry screen to adjust the freight or tax amount to account for the partial payment (refer to Adjusting Tax and Freight). The Monthly Sales Tax Adjustment Detail Report in the Order Processing module lists tax adjustments for a fiscal period.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > F5-Enter Detail > enter A for M/C
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Inbound Freight
The amount charged to the customer for freight cost to bring the merchandise into your warehouse.
Outbound Freight
The amount charged to the customer for freight cost to ship the merchandise to the customer.
Taxable Amount
The amount of merchandise for which tax was calculated and charged to the customer.
Nontaxable Amount
The amount of merchandise that is tax exempt.
Jur
The tax jurisdiction code for the customer ship-to address. Refer to Tax Jurisdictions in the Order Processing module for more information on sales tax.
Description
The description of the tax jurisdiction code.
Tax?
Indicates if tax is charged to the customer for this tax jurisdiction code.
Rate
The tax rate used to calculate the tax amount for this tax jurisdiction code.
Tax Amount
The amount of tax charged for this tax jurisdiction code.
Total
The total amount of all tax jurisdiction codes.
F2-Save
Saves the tax and freight amount adjustment information.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to allow you to view detail information on the invoiced sales order.
