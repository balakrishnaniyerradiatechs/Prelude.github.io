Creating a Customer Rebate Program
If you offer rebates to customers for products purchased, you can define rebate percents and eligibility requirements in Customer Rebate Maintenance. If you do not want all products to calculate rebates, you can exclude products by part number, product price group, product line, or vendor.
To set up a rebate program:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Customer Rebates to open the Customer Rebate Maintenance screen.
2
For Customer Rebate, enter the rebate number that identifies this rebate program.
3
For Rebate Description, enter a description of the rebate program.
4
For Rebate Credit Percent, define the percent that determines the rebate amount due to the customer for each invoice.
Rebate Amount = (amount paid during the eligibility period) x (Rebate Credit Percent).
5
For Credit Report Freq, determine how often customer rebates are calculated:
•
A - Annually.
•
Q - Quarterly.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
This information is used when you run the rebate reports in the Order Processing module to determine which rebates to include. This Credit Report Freq must match the Rebate Period for the report.
6
For Rebate Percent, indicate the percent used to calculate customer rebates. This field is for information only and may be used for custom reports.
7
For Rebate Check Freq, determine how often customer rebates are paid:
•
A - Annually.
•
Q - Quarterly.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
8
For Eligibility Type, indicate how to calculate the date by which a customer must pay an invoice to be eligible for a rebate:
•
I - By invoice date. For Eligible Days to Pay, enter the number of days from the invoice date that the invoice must be paid to receive the rebate.
•
D - By due date. The customer must pay the invoice by the invoice due date to receive the rebate.
9
If you want to exclude products from the rebate program, choose one of the following for Exclusion Type.
•
L - Exclude by product line.
•
P - Exclude by product.
•
V - Exclude by vendor.
•
G - Exclude by product price group.
This code is used with the Exclusion List to determine which products are not part of rebate calculations.
10
For Exclusion List, enter the products, product lines, product price groups, or vendors to exclude from rebate calculations, depending on the Exclusion Type.
Rebate Tables
If you use rebate tables to define rebates based on purchase amounts, press F5-Tables to open the Rebate Table Maintenance screen. A table allows you to define rebate percents based on the total amount of products purchased. You can set up multiple rebate percents that the customer will earn based on the quantities purchased. You can view the results in the Rebate Table reports in the Order Processing Module.
11
Press F2-Save to save the rebate program information.
Once you set up a rebate, you can assign each rebate number and start date to one or more customers (refer to Assigning a Rebate Program to a Customer). The rebates are calculated when cash is applied to invoices, but you can review and edit the rebate in Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance (refer to Editing Customer Invoice Rebates).
