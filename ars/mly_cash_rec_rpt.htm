Monthly Cash Receipts Report
The Monthly Cash Receipts Report lists all cash receipt batch information for a fiscal period, including the amount applied, the discounts taken, charge back amounts and miscellaneous write-off amounts. You can choose to list each invoice in the batch or to only show the totals for each batch.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Cash Receipts Reports > Monthly Cash Receipts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Bank Code. The bank code entered when the cash receipts batch was created (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch).
•
Period. The fiscal period to include.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Date
The deposit date.
Customer
Line 1 - The customer number.
Line 2 - The customer description.
Check Number
The number of the check used to pay the invoice(s).
Check Amount
The total amount of the check.
Invoice#
The invoice numbers that were paid on the check.
Cash Applied
The cash applied from the check to the invoice. Refer to Applying Cash to Invoices.
Disc Appl
The discount applied to the invoice. Refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount.
Chargeback
The amount billed to the customer on a charge back invoice. Refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices.
Misc Appl
The amount written off to a miscellaneous G/L account. Refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance.
Miscellaneous G/L
The G/L account to which the miscellaneous amount was written off.
