Unearned Cash Discounts Report
The Unearned Cash Discounts report lists cash discounts that were allowed and taken even though cash was applied to the invoice after the discount due date. Discount amounts and due dates are determined by the Payment Terms assigned to each customer in Customer Maintenance. If you override the discount amount when you apply cash, you must enter an unearned discount reason code (refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount).
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Unearned Cash Discounts
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Deposit Date Range. The beginning and ending cash receipt deposit dates to include.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer that took the unearned discount.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice#
The invoice number with the unearned discount.
Inv Date
The date the customer was billed for the order.
Dsc Date
The date by which the customer should have paid the invoice to receive the calculated discount amount. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms for information on how discount amounts and due dates are calculated.
Unearned Disc Reason
The unearned discount reason code and description that identifies why the discount was allowed for the customer even though they did not pay the invoice on time. Refer to Creating Unearned Discount Reason Codes.
AllowAmt
The amount of the discount given to the customer even though the invoice was paid after the discount due date.
Invoice Amt
The total amount of the invoice.
Disc%
The percent discount taken by the customer.
(AllowAmt / Invoice Amt) x 100
Due Date
The date by which the invoice should have been paid to receive the cash discount.
CR Date
The date that cash was applied to the invoice in cash receipts entry.
User
The user ID of the person that applied cash to the invoice (refer to Receiving Customer Payments).
