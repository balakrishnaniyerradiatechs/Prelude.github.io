Manually Placing an Order on Credit Hold
To place just one sales order for a customer on Credit Hold, use the Manual Credit Hold Status Procedure.
To manually place a sales order on credit hold:
1
Select Order Processing > Processes > Manual Credit Hold Procedure to open the Manual Credit Hold Status Procedure screen.
