Manually Matching and Editing Invoices
If multiple matches were found for an invoice, or to edit any batch discrepancies found after running the Fast Cash Receipts Work File Report, use High Volume Cash Receipts Maintenance to manually edit a batch.
To edit a high volume cash receipt batch:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Maintenance to open the High Volume Cash Receipts Maintenance screen.
2
For Batch Number, enter the batch entry number to adjust. The remaining information from the batch should appear.
3
Press ENTER to open the High Volume Cash Detail Maintenance screen. The open invoices appear.
4
Enter the Transaction Number to edit.
5
Highlight a reference number and press F5-Get Invoice List to open the Invoice List screen. You can view invoices that matched multiple reference numbers and the Source used to find the match.
6
To view detail information of an invoice, press F5-Review Invoice to open the Invoice Detail Inquiry screen.
7
Identify the correct match and enter Y in OK? for that invoice. If no matching invoice exists, an on-account credit is created. Use Cash Receipts Entry to apply the cash.
8
Press F2-Save to close the Invoice List screen.
9
Repeat steps 4 -7 for each match you need to edit.
10
Press F2-Save.
