Assigning a Rebate Program to a Customer
Once you set up rebate programs (refer to Creating a Customer Rebate Program), you can assign the rebate number and start date to one or more customers.
To assign a rebate program to a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer number.
3
Press F7-Order to open the Order Information screen.
4
For Customer Rebate#, enter the rebate program created in Customer Rebate Maintenance (refer to Creating a Customer Rebate Program).
5
For Rebate Start Date, enter the date the rebate calculations begin for this customer.
6
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the rebate information.
The rebates are calculated when cash is applied, but you can review and edit the rebate in Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance (refer to Editing Customer Invoice Rebates).
