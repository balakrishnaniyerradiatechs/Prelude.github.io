Cash Receipts Detail Display screen
The Cash Receipts Detail Display screen lists invoices and payment amounts for a customer in a cash receipts batch.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Cash Receipts History Inquiry > F5-Review Detail
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Pst-Date
The date the invoice posted to the general ledger.
Customer
The customer that paid the invoices.
Check Number
The check number for the paid invoices.
Check Amount
The amount of the check entered in the cash receipts.
Comment
Any comments entered for the customer when the cash was applied in the batch. Cash Receipt Entry > Action Bar > Cash Rec Functions > Additional Comment.
Invoice
The invoices paid in the batch.
Disc Appl
The discount amount applied for each invoice in the batch.
Cash Applied
The cash applied to each invoice in the batch.
Misc Appl
The write-off amount applied to a miscellaneous general ledger account when cash was applied. Refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance.
Unearned Disc
The unearned discount amount taken by the customer for the invoice. Refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount.
Balance Due
The balance still due from the customer for the invoice.
F2-Finish
Closes the Cash Receipts History Inquiry screen.
F5-Review Invoice
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry to display detail payment information for the invoice.
