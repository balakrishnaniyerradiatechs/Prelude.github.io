Cash Receipts History Report
The Cash Receipts History Report displays the total amount of cash applied to a customer’s invoice, including discounts, charge backs and miscellaneous amounts.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Cash Receipts Reports > Cash Receipts History Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Post Date range. The beginning and ending cash receipt posting dates to include.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Check Number. The customer check numbers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
Line 1 - The customer number.
Line 2 - The customer description.
Batch
The cash application batch number.
Check Number
The customer’s check number.
Check Amount
The total amount of the check.
Pst-Date
The date the deposit was posted.
Invoice#
The invoice numbers that were paid on the check.
Cash Applied
The amount of cash applied against this invoice.
Disc Appl
The cash discount applied to the invoice. The customer may earn a cash discount if the invoice is paid by the invoice date. Refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount.
Chargeback
The amount billed to the customer on a charge back invoice. Refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices.
Misc Appl
The amount written off to a miscellaneous G/L account. Refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance.
Miscellaneous G/L
The G/L account to which the miscellaneous amount was written off.
