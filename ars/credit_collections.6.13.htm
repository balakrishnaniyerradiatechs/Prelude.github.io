Generating Scheduled Collection Letters
Credit Collections Letter Generation allows you to print, email, or fax credit collection letters to customers. Credit Collections Nightly Processing determines the customers whose next scheduled follow-up activity is a collection letter (refer to Running Credit Collections Nightly Processing) and which letter is sent to the customer (refer to Creating Credit Collections Activity Codes.
You may find it easier to run the Credit Collections Activity Report before generating collection letters to determine which group of customers you want to contact or which type of letters you want to send.
Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F5-Add Info > Collections Method defines how letters are sent to the customer:
•
B - Mail and fax.
•
E - Email.
•
F - Fax.
•
M - Mail.
•
S - Mail and email.
To generate credit collection letters:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Processes > Credit Collections Letter Generation to open the Credit Collections Letter Generation screen.
2
Enter the Credit Collections Type to include. Collection types define groups of customers for collection purposes. Refer to Defining Credit Collections Types.
3
Enter the Security Access Code, if applicable.
4
Enter the Printer Form Queue to indicate where the letters should print, if applicable.
5
Press ENTER. The following message appears:
Load Letter Head In Printer & Align Visually
6
Choose from the following:
•
Print Letter. Prints the letters.
•
Abort Printing. Cancels the print process. Returns to the menu.
7
Once you choose Print Letter, the following message appears:
Credit Collections Letter Printing Complete...
Choose from the following:
•
Process. If you want to update the credit collections activities so that the letters do not print again the next time you generate letters.
•
Reprint All. If the letters did not print correctly, saves the generated information to allow you to reprint the letters.
•
Abort.
8
Once you choose Process , the following message appears:
Are You Sure You Want to Process?
Choose from the following:
•
Yes. Updates the scheduled credit collections activities.
•
No. Does not update credit collections activities. The letters will print again the next time you run the Credit Collections Letter Generation.
9
Once you choose Yes, the scheduled activities are updated by Nightly Processing.
Credit Collections Letter on Demand
If you want to generate a letter that was not scheduled by Nightly Processing, use Credit Collections Letter on Demand. Refer to Generating Collection Letters on Demand.
