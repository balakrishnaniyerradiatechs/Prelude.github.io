Incomplete Cash Receipts Report
The Incomplete Cash Receipts report displays batches that were suspended in Cash Receipts Entry because the cash applied did not match the batch total (refer to Completing the Batch).
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Cash Receipts Reports > Incomplete Cash Receipts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Bank Code.The bank code from the cash receipts batch (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch).
•
Deposit Date Range. The beginning and ending cash receipt deposit dates to include.
•
Batch Number Range. The beginning and ending cash receipt batch numbers to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
CR Date
The date the cash receipts batch was entered.
Batch
The cash receipts batch number.
Dep Total
The total amount of the deposit entered when the batch was started (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch).
Cash Applied
The total amount of cash applied in the batch.
Disc Appl
The total amount of cash discounts applied in the batch.
Chargeback
The total of invoices charged back to the customer for underpaid invoices (refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices).
Misc Appl
The total amount miscellaneous charges written off in the batch (refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance).
Open Amt
The amount of the discrepancy between the batch total and the applied amount.
Dep Total - (Cash Applied -+ Disc Appl).
