Customer Performance Summary Inquiry
The Customer Performance Summary Inquiry displays customer performance information including the sales, returns, and gross profit amounts for the current and previous years.
Displaying Customer Performance Statistics
To view sales and performance information for a customer:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Customer Performance Summary Inquiry to open the Customer Performance Summary Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
Sales performance information for the customer appears.
