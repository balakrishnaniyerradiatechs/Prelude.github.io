Editing an A/R Invoice
You can use Accounts Receivable Maintenance to edit the due date, invoice status, or other information on an open invoice.
To edit an open accounts receivable invoice:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > A/R Invoice Maintenance to open the Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen.
You can also access this screen using Cash Receipts Entry > Action Bar > Invoice Func > Invoice Maintenance.
2
Enter the Invoice Number to edit (if you access this screen from the Cash Receipts screen, the invoice is already filled in). The Customer, Invoice Date, Due Date, and Discount Date information appears.
3
If necessary, edit the Due Date or the Discount Date.
4
If you want to change the Invoice Status, choose from the following:
•
None.
•
Bankruptcy.
•
Collections.
•
Disputed. Include Disputed Invoices in Collections in Additional Option Parameters determines if disputed invoices are included in the Credit Collections Subsystem
•
Litigation.
•
Insufficient funds.
•
Rebate.
•
Write-off.
5
For Repl. Equipment, choose from the following:
•
Y - The invoice is for replacement equipment for material returned on an RGA.
•
N - The invoice is not for replacement equipment.
Replacement Equipment Option Parameter
To generate a replacement order from an RGA, you must set Generate Replacement Equip S/O (Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters) to Y.
6
For Excluded, indicate if the invoice is excluded from the credit limit test. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Exclude this invoice from the normal customer open A/R balance. The invoice will be held in the excluded A/R balance so that it is not evaluated in the credit limit check for new sales orders. Refer to Excluding Sales Orders From the Credit Limit.
•
N - Include this invoice in the credit limit check calculations.
Excluded is only applicable if Exclude from Credit Limit (Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add Info) is set to Y.
7
If applicable, enter or edit the Customer P/O.
8
If applicable, enter or edit the Cust Release#.
9
Press F2-Save to save the edited invoice information.
