Field Definitions
A/R Credit Card Payment Entry screen
If a customer uses a credit card to pay off open accounts receivable invoices, you can use the A/R Credit Card Payment Entry screen to enter the credit card payment. Refer to Receiving Credit Card Payments for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > A/R Credit Card > A/R Credit Card Payment Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Batch Number
The system generated number that identifies the credit card batch.
Transaction Date
The date to post the credit card batch to the general ledger.
Bank Code
The code for the bank where the cash is deposited.
Customer
The customer who is making the payment.
A/R Balance
The customer’s open accounts receivable balance. Sales orders invoiced but not yet paid.
Credit Card Type
The code for the credit card company (e.g., M/C, Visa, AMEX) set up in Credit Bureau Maintenance (Order Processing module).
Credit Card Number
The customer’s credit card number. Defaults from the customer record if one was set up in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F7-Credit Card.
Expiration Date
The expiration date on the credit card. Defaults from the customer record if one was set up in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F7-Credit Card.
CVV Number
The 3-digit code on the back of the credit card used by some companies to prevent theft.
AVS Street Addr
If you use AVS (address verification service) enter the card user’s billing address. Once an AVS response is received, this information is discarded.
AVS Zip Code
The AVS zip code for billing verification.
Invoice List Option
Determines how to list the invoices:
•
A - Lists all open invoices for the customer.
•
S - Allows you to enter select invoices for the customer.
Invoices
The customer’s open invoice numbers.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Invoice Amount
The original amount of the invoice.
Balance
The amount still due on the invoice.
Disc Avail
The discount available to the customer if they pay their invoice on time or by the discount date (refer to Using A/R Payment Terms).
Cash Applied
The amount to charge to the customer’s credit card for the corresponding invoice.
Amount Applied
The total amount of all the invoices applied to the customer’s credit card.
F2-Save
Charges the Amount Applied to the customer’s credit card and allows you to continue with the next transaction.
