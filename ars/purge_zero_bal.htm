Purge Zero Balance A/R Records screen
After accounts receivable invoices are completely paid you can use the Purge Zero Balance A/R Records screen to purge the zero balance records.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Purge Zero Balance A/R Records
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
With Warehouse Equal To
The warehouse for the records to purge. Leave blank to purge records for all warehouses.
With Customer Equal To
The customer whose records you want to purge. Leave blank to include all customers in the selected warehouses.
With Zero Bal Date Less Than Or Equal To
The date to purge all zero balance invoices. Any paid invoices that contain a date less than or equal to this date will be purged.
