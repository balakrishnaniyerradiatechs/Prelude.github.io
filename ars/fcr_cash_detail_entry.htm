High Volume Cash Detail Entry screen
If you receive a large volume of payments per month from customers via EDI, you can use the High Volume Detail Entry screen to search for invoices to apply cash to.
Accounts Payable Option Parameters for High Volume Cash Receipts
High Volume C/R Method in A/R defines the criteria required to find a customer invoice:
•
I - You must enter the invoice number to find a match.
•
R - You can find a match by invoice, order, customer P/O, or release number.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Batch Entry

Reference Number
The reference number to identify the invoice. Depending on how High Volume C/R Method is set AP Option Parameters, choose one of the following:
•
Invoice number only (High Volume C/R Method = I). You must enter an invoice number. The gross Amount, Discount, and Net Amount appear.
•
Reference numbers (High Volume C/R Method = R). Enter an invoice, order, customer P/O, or release number. Enter the Amount and/or Discount of the invoice. If you only have a discount for the check, not for each invoice, enter the entire amount in the first Discount.
Amount
The amount of the customer’s check.
Discount
The cash discount that the customer took for this invoice.
Net Amount
The amount the customer is paying for this invoice (Amount - Discount).
Totals
The totals columns for Amount, Discount, and Net Amount.
