Defining Credit Collections Types
Credit collection types are used to group customers for collections (e.g., slow paying customers, good customers). After you create the credit collections types, you can assign them to customers in Customer Maintenance.
To create a credit collection type:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Maintenance > Credit Collections Type Maintenance to open the Credit Collections Type Maintenance screen.
2
For Credit Collections Type, enter the alphanumeric code to identify a group of customers.
3
Enter a Description for the group (e.g., slow paying customer, good customer).
4
Press F2-Save to save the credit collection type information.
