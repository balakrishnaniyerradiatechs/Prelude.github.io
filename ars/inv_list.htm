Invoice List screen
The Invoice list screen allows you to view invoices that matched more than one reference number in High Volume Cash Receipts Maintenance, so that you can select the correct invoice.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Maintenance > F5-Get Invoice List
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice#
The invoice number that was paid.
Cust#
The customer number.
Source
The source of the matching reference number:
•
CPO - Customer P/O number.
•
ORD - Order number.
•
REL - Release number.
•
INV - Invoice number.
•
GROS - Gross amount.
•
NET - Net amount.
Open Balance
The open balance on this invoice.
Due Date
The due date of the invoice.
OK?
Indicates if this invoice is the correct match:
•
Y - The invoice is the correct match.
•
N - The invoice is not the correct match
If no invoice number exists, an on-account credit is created. The invoice number is the Reference Number preceded by M.
F2-Save
Saves the invoice information.
F5-Review Invoice
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry screen to allow you to view invoice and payment information.
