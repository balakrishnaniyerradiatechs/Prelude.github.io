Overriding an Unearned Discount
When you apply cash to invoices in the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen (refer to Applying Cash to Invoices), the Disc Avail (discount available) is the discount the customer receives if they pay the invoice by the discount date. If the Post Date that you enter is past the customer’s discount date, the Disc Appl (discount applied) defaults to zero. If you want to give the customer the discount anyway and override the Disc Appl, the following message appears:
The Discount Entered is UNEARNED Because The Discount Date Has Expired Based On the Post Date.
1
Choose from the following:
•
Change Discount - Returns to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen to allow you to change the discount.
•
Allow Unearned Discount - Opens the Unearned Discount screen.
2
For Unearned Disc Code, enter the reason code to explain why you are overriding the discount (e.g., mail delay, good customer, cash receipts late). Refer to Creating Unearned Discount Reason Codes for information on setting up codes.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen.
4
Enter the Cash Applied to the invoice and continue with the cash application (refer to Manually Applying Cash to Individual Invoices).
