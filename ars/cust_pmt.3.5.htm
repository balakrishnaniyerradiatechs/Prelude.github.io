Manually Applying Cash to Individual Invoices
To select invoices to pay one at a time or to adjust discounts or handle payment discrepancies, you can manually apply cash to individual invoices.
To apply cash to individual invoices:
1
After you enter the batch and customer information (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch), move the cursor to the invoice to pay and press ENTER.
To search for an invoice number, press F3 on the Invoices field to open the Select Invoice for Pay screen. You can search for an invoice by date, balance due amount, purchase order, child account, or ship-to number.
2
For Disc Appl, change or accept the discount applied to the invoice and press ENTER. The Disc Avail (discount available) is the discount the customer receives if they pay the invoice by the discount date. If the Post Date that you enter is past the customer’s discount date, the Disc Appl (discount applied) defaults to zero. If you want to go ahead and give the customer the discount anyway, you can override the Disc Appl. Refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount.
Include Tax in Cash Discount
Incl Tax/Cash Disc in State Abbreviation Maintenance (Order Processing > Maintenance > Sales Tax > State Abbreviations) determines how the customer discount is calculated:
•
Y - Cash discounts are based on the merchandise total plus the sales tax.
•
N - Cash discounts are based on the merchandise total only.
The Cash Applied defaults to the amount of the invoice if the Amount Remaining on the check is enough to cover the invoice. Otherwise, Cash Applied defaults to Amount Remaining.
3
If the cash applied does not match the balance, select from the following:
•
If the customer underpaid an invoice, a warning message appears. Refer to Reconciling Partial Payments.
•
If the customer overpaid an invoice, refer to Reconciling Over Payments.
•
If the invoice is incorrect, refer to Editing an A/R Invoice.
4
Repeat steps 1-3 for each invoice covered by this check.
5
Press F2-Save to complete the cash application for this check. If the check amount does not match the amount applied, a warning message appears. Refer to Completing the Batch.
6
Repeat steps 1-5 for each check in the batch. When all checks are applied, refer to Completing the Batch.
Invoice Detail Inquiry
To examine an invoice (e.g., a discrepancy exists between what the customer is paying and the open balance), from the Action Bar select Invoice Func > Examine Invoice to open the Invoice Detail Inquiry. You can examine the detail information on the invoice including freight, tax, and any pending RGAs.
 
Accounts Receivable Maintenance
To edit invoice due dates, discount dates, or other A/R information, select Action Bar > Invoice Func > Invoice Maintenance to open the Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen. Refer to Editing an A/R Invoice.
 
Cumulative Balance Inquiry
To view a cumulative balance of open invoices, press F7-Cumm Bal to open the Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen. The individual balance for each invoice displays along with the cumulative A/R balance.
