Overdue Accounts Receivable Report
The Overdue Accounts Receivable report list overdue invoices for a customer. You can choose to list each invoice that is past due or to only show the total amount past due.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Overdue Accounts Receivable
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the order, regardless of the shipping warehouse.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Days Old >. The number of days past due an invoice must be to appear in this report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer’s account number.
Description
The customer description.
Phone
The customer’s phone number.
Contact
The name of the person responsible for paying the invoices. Identified in the Customer Maintenance screen.
LastPaid
The date that cash was last applied to this customer’s account.
AveDays
The average number of days it takes this customer to pay invoices. Refer toCalculating Average Days to Pay for more information on how this number is calculated for a customer.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number for this invoice.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
COD
If the invoice is COD, an asterisk (*) appears.
Due Date
The date on which the invoice was due.
Balance Due
The amount left to pay on the invoice.
D
If the invoice is disputed, a D appears. You can mark an invoice as disputed in Manual Invoice Entry (refer to Manually Entering an Invoice) or in Accounts Receivable Maintenance (refer to Editing an A/R Invoice).
