Credit Review Report
The Credit Review report displays credit comments for a customer. You can enter credit comments for a customer in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F6-Comments.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Credit Information Review
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The selling warehouse assigned to the customer.
•
Review Date <. All credit comments with a review date before this date.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer name.
Whse
The selling warehouse assigned to the customer.
Rev Date
The date to follow up with this customer regarding accounts receivable collections. The date defaults from Default Parameters Maintenance.
Credit Limit
The credit limit assigned to the customer in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R. When you enter a sales order for this customer, if the total value of the customer’s A/R balance and open sales orders (excluding disputed and long-term future orders) exceeds the credit limit, the order goes on credit hold.
A/R Balance
The total open accounts receivable balance due by the customer (i.e., sales orders billed but not yet paid).
Ord Balance
The total open sales order balance for the customer (i.e, sales orders placed but not yet invoiced).
Credit Comments
Credit comments entered for the customer in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F6-Comments.
