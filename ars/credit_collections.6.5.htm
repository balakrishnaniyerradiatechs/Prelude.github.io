Customer Maintenance
To set credit collection options for a customer:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Press F6-A/R to open the A/R information screen.
Security Access Codes
If you use security access codes to select customers in the collection subsystem:
•
Select Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > Action Bar > Security Access to open Security Access Maintenance screen.
•
For Cust Security Access, enter the customer security access code that limits who has access to this customer. Only users with this security access code set in User Identification Maintenance > F6-Add Info > F5-Cust Sec can view this customer.
•
Press F2-Save to return to the A/R Information screen.
4
Press F5-Add info to open the Additional A/R Information screen.
5
Enter the Credit Collections Type that defines the schedule of activities and letters for collections. Collection types are set up in Credit Collections Type Maintenance to group customers for collection. Leave this field blank if the customer is excluded from credit collection. Refer to Defining Credit Collections Types.
6
For Suppress Collections, indicate if the customer is excluded from the credit collection process:
•
N - Include the customer in credit collections.
•
Y - Do not include the customer in credit collections (usually a temporary status).
•
D - Exclude the customer from credit collections if their A/R balance is below the Credit Collections Min $ in the following field.
7
For Credit Collections Min $, enter the minimum open balance that determines if a customer is included in the Credit Collections process (if Suppress Collections is D). If the customer’s open balance is below this amount, Nightly Processing skips the customer for credit collections. This value overrides the Min Cred Coll Amt in Accounts Receivable Parameters. Leave blank to use the minimum amount from Accounts Receivable Parameters.
8
For Collections Method, indicate how letters are sent to the customer:
•
B - Mail (print) and fax.
•
E - Email.
•
F - Fax.
•
M - Mail.
•
S - Mail and email.
9
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the credit collection information.
Refer to “Customer Maintenance” in the Order Processing documentation for a detailed explanation of each field in Customer Maintenance.
