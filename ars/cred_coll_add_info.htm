Additional Info screen
The Additional Information screen in Credit Collections Schedule Entry allows you to enter additional follow-up activities to occur after the override activity that you entered in Next Action. You can enter the dates for each subsequent follow-up activity.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Credit Collections Schedule Entry > F5-Review > override the Next Action
