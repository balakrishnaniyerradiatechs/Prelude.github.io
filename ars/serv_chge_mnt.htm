Manual Service Charge Entry screen
If you want manually enter a service charge invoice or if you need to modify a previously generated service charge (refer to Automatically Assessing Service Charges), use Manual Service Charge Maintenance to enter or edit a service charge to a customer. Refer to Manually Assessing or Editing a Service Charge for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Manual Service Charge Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice Number
For Invoice Number, choose from the following:
•
To create a new service charge invoice, type N to assign the next system generated service charge number.
•
To edit an existing service charge invoice, enter the service charge invoice number.
Customer
The customer to bill for the service charge.
Due Date
The date the service charge invoice is due.
Invoice Basis Amt
The base amount to use to calculate the service charge (e.g., the total amount of overdue invoices). This amount is multiplied by the Service Charge % established for the customer in Customer Maintenance to determine the service charge.
Invoice Amount
The amount of the service charge invoice. The charge is calculated according to the Accounts Receivable Option Parameters and the service charge settings in the Customer’s account (refer to Setting Maintenance Options for Service Charges). You can override the calculated amount if necessary.
Print Flag
Determines if the invoice prints during Service Charge Invoice Printing:
•
Y - Print the invoice during Service Charge Invoice Printing.
•
N - Do not print the invoice,
Refer to Printing Service Charge Invoices.
F2-Save
Generates the service charge invoice.
F4-Del
Deletes the service charge invoice.
