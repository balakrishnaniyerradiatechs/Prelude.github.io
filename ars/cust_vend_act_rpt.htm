Customer/Vendor Activity Report
The Customer/Vendor Activity report lists
-
Open receivables for customers who are also vendors.
-
Invoices set up for payment for vendors who are also customers.
You can choose to list the total amount of open payables and receivables, or to list detail information for each customer invoice and vendor invoice.
To cross-reference a vendor to a customer, enter the vendor number in Customer Maintenance > F5-Add Info > Our Vendor Number. The customer number will then appear in Vendor Maintenance > F7-Contacts > Our Customer.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Customer/Vendor Activity Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include.
•
Detail/Summary. Indicates whether you want to list detail information for each open invoice or to only show the summary of each open balance.
-
D - Detail. List each invoice number, due date, and invoice amount for both the A/R balance and the A/P balance.
-
S - Summary. Display the for summary each A/R balance and A/P balance.
Column Descriptions - Summary
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/ Description
The customer number and description.
Vend#/Description
The vendor number and description. Displays below Cust#/ Description.
A/R
Inv #
The unpaid invoices for the customer.
Due Date
The due date of the unpaid invoices.
Inv Amt
The amount of the unpaid invoices.
A/P
Inv #
The invoice number of the vendor invoice set up for payment.
Due Date
The due date of the vendor invoice.
Inv Amt
The amount of the vendor invoice.
