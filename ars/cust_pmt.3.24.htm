Receiving Credit Card Payments
If a customer uses a credit card to pay off open accounts receivable invoices, you can use A/R Credit Card Payment Entry to accept the credit card payment. After the balance is paid off, you must close the batch to complete the transfer of money from the customer’s account to your account. These procedures work with the Protobase verification software.
