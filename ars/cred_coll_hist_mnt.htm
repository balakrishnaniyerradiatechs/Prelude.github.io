Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance screen
You can use the Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance screen to view notes entered in the Credit Collections Notes Maintenance screen. This screen also indicates when the customer’s account was reviewed in the credit collections queue (refer to Entering Scheduled Credit Collection Activities) or when credit collection letters were sent to the customer (refer to Generating Scheduled Collection Letters).
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Maintenance > Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer for whom notes were entered.
Notes
May display one or more of the following:
•
Historical notes entered for this customer in Credit Collections Notes Maintenance.
•
The date the customer was reviewed in the credit collections queue.
•
The date a credit collection letter was sent.
You can edit the notes but can not add another line.
Date
The date the notes were entered.
Type
The types of notes entered:
•
P - Permanent. The notes stay in the customer’s credit collections history indefinitely.
•
G - General. The notes have an expiration date.
•
T - Temporary. The notes are associated with a customer invoice. When the invoice is paid, the note is removed from the customer’s collection history.
F2-Save
Saves any changes and closes the screen.
