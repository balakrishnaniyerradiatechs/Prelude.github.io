Assigning Credit Managers to a Group of Customers
Customer Credit Manager Assignment allows you to change the existing credit manager assignment for an alphabetical range of customers to a new credit manager. You can override this assignment for a customer in Customer Maintenance.
To assign a credit manager to an alphabetical range of customers for a warehouse:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Customer Credit Manager Assignment to open the Customer Credit Manager Assignment screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to assign a credit manager ID.
3
For Beginning Customer Name and Ending Customer Name, enter the customer name range to include in this assignment. You can enter the whole name or just one letter. For example, enter A and D to include all customers whose names begin with A,B,C, and D.
4
For Old Credit Manager, enter the credit manager ID assigned to the customers. When the re-assignment is complete, only customers in the customer name range with this Credit Manager ID assigned in Customer Maintenance will have a new credit manager. To re-assign the credit manager for all customers in the name range, leave this field blank.
5
For New Credit Manager, enter the credit manager ID that you want to assign to customers in the name range that were previously assigned to the Old Credit Manager.
6
Press F2-Save to re-assign the credit manager for the selected customers. One of the following occurs:
•
If any credit managers were re-assigned, the screen closes.
•
If no customers met the selection criteria, the following message appears:
No Items Present. Press Return.
7
Press RETURN to return to the menu. You may need to verify your date and re-enter the information.
