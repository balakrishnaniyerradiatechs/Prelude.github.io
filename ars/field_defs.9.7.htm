Cash Receipts Chargeback By Customer P/O Number screen
If you issue a charge back and if Chargeback Inv by Cust P/O? is set to Y in Accounts Receivable Option Parameters, the Cash Receipts Chargeback By Customer P/O Number screen allows you to enter a charge back reason code for each customer purchase order number. Refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > F5-Enter Detail > enter C for M/C
