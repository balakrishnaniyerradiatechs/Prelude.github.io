Applying Extra Cash On-Account
If funds are left over after a customer pays an invoice, you can apply the cash to their account to use for future orders.
Related Report
Description
Customers With On Account Balances Report
Customers, by credit manager, that have extra cash applied to their account.
To apply the cash on account:
1
On the first blank line on the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen (refer to Applying Cash to Invoices), under Invoices, enter one of the following:
•
NEW to generate the next sequential invoice number.
•
An invoice number (e.g., if the check number is 1234, you could enter COA1234).
2
Press ENTER. The following dialog box appears:
Using this invoice number will create a
Cash on Account Entry
3
Choose Continue. The Cash On Account Invoice Information screen appears.
4
Accept or change the Warehouse Number. The default is the selling warehouse for the customer from Customer Maintenance.
5
Enter the Invoice Date.
6
Press ENTER to return to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen.
7
Enter the amount of cash to apply in Cash Appl to create a negative A/R balance for the customer.
If you apply cash to the account instead of paying open invoices, the customer may lose discounts on those invoices.
To continue applying cash to this batch, refer to Applying Cash to Invoices. To complete the batch, refer to Completing the Batch.
