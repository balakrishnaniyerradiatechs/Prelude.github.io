Company Maintenance
Credit check options at the company level determine if sales orders should go on credit hold. You can override these options for individual customers or sales orders.
To set the credit check options at the company level:
1
Select Company Maintenance > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Credit Check, determine if customer credit limits are checked as sales orders are entered.
•
H - Sales orders go on credit hold when a customer is over their credit limit or has past due invoices. Nightly Processing calculates the amount of open invoices to determine if the customer should go on credit hold the next time an order is entered.
•
N - Sales orders never go on credit hold.
3
Press F2-Save twice to save the credit check information.
