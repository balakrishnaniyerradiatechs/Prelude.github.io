Cash Receipts Entry screen
The Cash Receipts Entry screen allows you to enter the header information for a batch of cash receipts (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch). You can accumulate checks from one or more customers to create a batch total. The batch amount represents the total cash and checks included in a deposit to a bank.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Batch Number
The number to assign to a batch of checks.
Deposit Date
The date the customer’s payments post to the general ledger. The fiscal period displays to the right of the date.
Bank Code
The code that identifies the bank where the checks are deposited. Bank codes are set up in Bank Account Code Maintenance on the Systems menu.
Deposit Total
The total amount of all the cash and checks in the batch.
Batch Total Applied
The total amount of cash applied once the cash application is complete.
Deposit Comments
Any note or comments regarding the cash batch.
F2-Save
When you are finished applying cash to the batch and the Deposit Total matches the Batch Total Applied, the batch completes and posts to the general ledger.
F5-Enter Detail
Opens the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen to allow you to apply cash to customer’s open invoices.
F6-Status Display
Opens the Cash Receipts Status Display screen to allow you to view check numbers and amounts already applied for a customer.
