Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance
Once you enter credit collection notes for a customer in Credit Collections Notes Maintenance (refer to Entering Credit Collections Notes), you can view the notes in Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance. This screen also indicates when the customer’s account was reviewed in the credit collections queue (refer to Entering Scheduled Credit Collection Activities) or when credit collection letters were sent to the customer (refer to Generating Scheduled Collection Letters).
You can review, edit, or delete any existing notes, but you cannot add any notes.
To review, edit, or delete notes for a customer:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Maintenance > Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance to open the Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer whose notes you want to review. The following information may display:
•
Historical notes entered for this customer in Credit Collections Notes Maintenance.
•
The date the customer was reviewed in the credit collections queue.
•
The date a credit collection letter was sent.
3
If necessary, you can edit or delete any information under Notes, but you cannot add another line.
4
Press F2-Save to close the screen and save any changes.
