Entering/Viewing Credit Collection Notes
As you attempt to collect payment from customers for overdue invoices, you may want to record conversations or notes regarding the customer’s past due balances. Normally you would enter collection notes for a customer in Credit Collections Schedule Entry, but if the customer is not in the collection queue, you can enter notes in Credit Collections Notes Maintenance. Once entered, they appear in Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance and in the Credit Collections History Inquiry.
