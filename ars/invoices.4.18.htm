Generating Statements for Customers
A customer statement lists all open invoices for a customer and includes due dates and aging information for each invoice. Settings In Prelude, determine which customers receive a statement, minimum amounts that generate statements, and if past due invoices (balance forward) statements are included. If you send statements to your customers, refer to the following procedures:
•
Maintenance Options for Statements.
•
Printing Statements.
Month-End Customer A/R Aging Save
(General Ledger > Processes > End of Month Closing)
Saves all customer A/R aging balances. You can run the A/R reports for a period after this process is complete. Customer statements (excluding Balance Forward) use this information to print statements. Balance forward statement history updates when the original statement prints. Run this process only at month-end and only once per fiscal period. Refer to Maintenance Options for Statements.
