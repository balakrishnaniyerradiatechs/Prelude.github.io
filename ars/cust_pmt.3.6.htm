Automatically Applying Cash to Invoices
To apply cash to an open batch of customer invoices without going through each invoice, you can use one of several auto pay options to automatically apply cash to open invoices. Each auto pay option applies the discount amount in Disc Avail (unless the invoices is past its discount date) and pays as much of the invoice balance as possible.
1
To automatically apply cash to open invoices for a customer, press F6-Auto Pay and choose from the following options:
•
Pay From Invoice Down - Applies the available cash to each invoice going down, starting from the highlighted invoice.
•
Invoice Number Range - Opens the Invoice Number Range Entry screen so you can enter beginning and ending invoice numbers to apply cash to.
•
Consolidated Invoice - Opens the Consolidated Invoice Number screen. If you use consolidated invoicing, you can enter the Consolidated Invoice Number to pay matching invoices by ascending date (refer to “Consolidated Invoicing” in the Order Processing documentation).
•
Customer P/O Number - Opens the Customer P/O Number screen to allow you to pay invoices using the customer’s purchase order number.
2
After you use one of the auto pay options, choose one of the following to complete the application:
•
To edit the discount amount, refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount.
•
To account for an underpayment from the customer, refer to Reconciling Partial Payments.
•
If the customer overpaid an invoice refer to Reconciling Over Payments.
•
If additional checks exist for the batch, press F2-Save and continue to apply cash for each customer and check number.
•
If you are finished applying cash for this check, refer to Completing the Batch.
Invoice Detail Inquiry
To examine an invoice (e.g., a discrepancy exists between what the customer is paying and the open balance), from the Action Bar select Invoice Func > Examine Invoice to open the Invoice Detail Inquiry. You can examine the detail information on the invoice including freight, tax, and any pending RGAs.
 
Accounts Receivable Maintenance
To edit invoice due dates, discount dates, or other A/R information, select the Action Bar > Invoice Func > Invoice Maintenance to open the Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen. Refer to Editing an A/R Invoice.
 
Cumulative Balance Inquiry
To view a cumulative balance of open invoices, press F7-Cumm Bal to open the Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen. The individual balance for each invoice displays along with the cumulative A/R balance.
