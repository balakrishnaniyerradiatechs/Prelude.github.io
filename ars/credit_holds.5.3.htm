File Update Parameters
To keep a record of credit authorization transactions (e.g., sales orders release from credit hold, customers put on or taken off credit hold), set the following system options.
To determine if credit hold transaction information is retained:
1
Select Option Parameters > File Update Parameters to open the File Update Parameters Maintenance screen.
2
For Credit Authorization, determine if credit authorization history information is captured:
•
Y - Keep credit authorization information (orders released from credit hold). The data appears on the Credit Authorization Report (Order Entry module).
•
N - Do not keep credit authorization information.
3
For Credit Statistics, determine if credit status history information is captured:
•
Y - Keep credit status information. The Daily Credit Status Change Report lists all customers who have been put on or taken off auto credit hold or have had their credit code, credit limit, or terms code changed.
•
N - Do not keep credit status information.
4
For Credit Mgr. Release, determine if credit release information is captured for a credit manager:
•
Y - Keep credit manager release information. The Credit Manager Release Report lists information for orders released by a credit manager.
•
N - Do not keep credit manager release information.
5
Press F2-Save to save the credit information.
