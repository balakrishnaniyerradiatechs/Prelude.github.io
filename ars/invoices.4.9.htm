Printing Charge Back Invoices
To print charge back invoices created in Cash Receipts Entry (refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices), use Charge Back Invoice Printing. Each printed invoice displays the check number, charge back comments, the original invoice numbers included on the charge back invoice, and the total charge back amount.
To print charge back invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Charge Back Invoice Printing to open the Charge Back Print screen.Enter the charge back Invoice Number or leave blank to print all charge back invoices.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to print charge back invoices or leave blank to print charge back invoices for all warehouses.
3
For Printer Form Queue, enter the printer where statements should print.
4
For Invoice Message, enter any text that you want to appear at the bottom of each charge back invoice.
5
Press ENTER. The following message appears:
Load Invoice Forms in Printer & Align Visually
6
Choose one of the following:
•
Print Invoices - Prints the charge back invoices.
•
Mask Print Test - Tests the alignment of the charge back invoice forms in the printer. Not necessary for laser printers. After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Invoices.
•
Abort Printing - Cancels the process. No charge back invoices print.
