Misc Cash Receipts Entry screen
If the customer underpaid an invoice (refer to Reconciling Partial Payments) and you want to write-off the difference, you can post the unpaid amount to one or more general ledger write-off accounts. Refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance for more information.
Maximum Write-Off Limit
If you want to set a maximum amount that a user can write-off to the general ledger, enter the maximum amount he/she can write-off in User Id Maintenance > F5- Administrative > C/R Write-Off Limit.
 
Miscellaneous Cash Receipts G/L Numbers
You must set up general ledger account numbers in Accounts Receivable Controls > Misc Cash REceipts G/L Numbers to post cash receipts write-off amounts to.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > Enter M for M/C.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Misc Left to Distribute
The difference between the Invoice Amt and the Cash Applied that needs to be written off.
Ln#
The line number for the write-off account.
Div
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The warehouse location.
Dep
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account#
The general ledger account for a cash receipts write-off. These are set up in Misc. Cash Receipts G/L Numbers Maintenance on the Systems menu.
Description
The description of the general ledger write-off account.
Debit Amount
The amount to apply to the corresponding general ledger write-off account.
F2-Save
Saves the general ledger account information.
F4-Del
Deletes the general ledger account information.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
