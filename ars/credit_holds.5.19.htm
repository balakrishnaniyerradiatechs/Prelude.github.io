Excluding Sales Orders From the Credit Limit
A credit manager can exclude a sales order from a customer’s credit limit check. You may want to so this so that a pre-approved large order does not cause subsequent orders to go on credit hold.
If you have Exclude From Credit Limit (Order Processing Parameters) set to Y, the following message displays each time you release an order from credit hold:
Do You Wish To Exclude This Sales Order From This Customer’s Credit Limit Test?
Choose from the following:
•
No - The order is included in the customer’s open A/R balance for credit limit checks.
•
Yes - The order’s value is not included in the customer’s credit limit check so that it will not cause other sales orders to go on credit hold. This exclusion carries over to any invoice that results from shipments of this order.
 
You can identify excluded orders on the following screens:
•
Sales Order Inquiry (Order Processing Module): Y appears under E (excluded) to indicate that an order is excluded from the customer’s credit limit test.
•
Accounts Receivable Inquiry: The excluded sales order amount appears to the right of the Order Balance and the excluded invoice amount appears to the right of the A/R Balance.
•
Invoice Detail Inquiry: Y appears under Ex to indicate that the order is excluded from the credit limit test.
•
Accounts Receivable Maintenance (refer to Editing an A/R Invoice): A Y for Excluded indicates that the order is excluded from the credit limit test. You can change this flag as necessary.
