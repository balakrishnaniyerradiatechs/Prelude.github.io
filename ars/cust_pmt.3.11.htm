Writing off the Unpaid Balance
If the customer underpaid an invoice (refer to Reconciling Partial Payments) and you want to write-off the difference, you can post the unpaid amount to a general ledger account.
Maximum Write-Off Limit
If you want to set a maximum amount that a user can write-off to the general ledger, enter the maximum amount he/she can write-off in User Id Maintenance > F5- Administrative > C/R Write-Off Limit.
To write off an unpaid balance:
1
In the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen, choose M for M/C. The Misc Cash Receipts Entry screen appears.
2
Enter the appropriate Division, Region, Location, Department, and Cost Center as necessary.
3
For Account#, enter the general ledger account for a cash receipts write- off. Press F3 to list the G/L account numbers set up in Misc. Cash Receipts G/L Numbers (Accounts Receivable Controls on the Systems menu).
4
For Debit Amount, enter the amount to write-off to the corresponding general ledger account.
5
If necessary, enter additional account numbers to write-off additional amounts until the Misc Left to Distribute equals zero.
6
Press F2-Save to save the account information and to return to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen. The Balance for the invoice line should now be zero if no other discrepancies exist.
