Cash Receipts History Inquiry
The Cash Receipts History Inquiry lists the customers, check numbers, and payment amounts for all payments in a cash receipts batch (completed or suspended). You can view detail information for each invoice for each customer.
Viewing a Cash Receipt Batch
To view customers and payment amounts in a cash receipt batch:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Cash Receipts History Inquiry to open the Cash Receipts History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Batch Number.
Customers, check numbers, and payment amounts appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Batch Number
The number assigned to the cash receipts batch.
Deposit Date
The date the batch was deposited.
Bank Code
The code for the bank where the money was deposited.
Deposit Comments
Any comments regarding the deposit that were entered during Cash Receipts Entry (refer to Beginning a Cash Receipt Batch).
Deposit Total
The total amount of the deposit from the Cash Receipts Entry screen.
Batch Total Applied
The total amount of invoices that were paid in the batch.
User ID (unlabeled field)
The user ID of the employee that entered the cash receipts batch.
Customer
The customer whose invoices were paid in Cash Receipts Entry.
Description
The customer description.
Check Number
The customer’s check number.
Check Amount
The amount of the customer’s payment against this invoice.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Detail
Opens the Cash Receipts Detail Display screen to show detailed payment information for each invoice for a customer.
