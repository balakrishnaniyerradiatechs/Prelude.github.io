Charge Back Entry screen
You can use the Cash Receipts Charge back By Customer P/O Number screen to charge the customer back for the difference. Refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > F5-Enter Detail > enter C for M/C
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Amount Left To Distribute
The amount that needs to be charged back to the customer. The difference between the Cash Applied to the invoice and the Invoice Amt.
Reason
The charge back reason code to explain why you are charging the customer. Refer to Creating Charge Back Reason Codes for more information.
Description
The description of the charge back reason.
Debit Amount
The amount you are charging back to the customer.
F2-Save
Saves the charge back information and returns to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen.
