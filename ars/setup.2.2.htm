Using A/R Payment Terms
Before you can create a customer account in Customer Maintenance (refer the Order Processing documentation for detailed information on Customer Maintenance), you must create payment terms to define payment rules and discount dates and amounts for each customer. You can set up as many payment terms codes as necessary to accommodate any variation of due dates, payment dates, and payment methods.
