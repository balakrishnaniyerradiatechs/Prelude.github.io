Generating Customer Rebates
A rebate program defines products that should receive rebates, the percent rebate to give the customer, and the period in which the products should be bought to receive the rebate. You can set up one or more rebate programs to assign to customers. As sales orders are invoiced, each line item is evaluated according to the customer’s rebate program to determine if the item is eligible for a rebate. When cash is applied to the invoice, the rebate amount is calculated based on when the customer paid the invoice and the eligibility period. The information appears in the Customer Rebate Detail Report and the Customer Rebate Summary reports in the Order Processing Module. If you need to adjust the rebate amounts due to customers, use Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance.
