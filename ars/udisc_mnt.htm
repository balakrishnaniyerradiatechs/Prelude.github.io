Unearned Discount Reason Maintenance screen
The Unearned Discount Reason Maintenance screen is used to set up reason codes to enter in Cash Receipts Entry when you override a discount amount. If a customer pays an invoice past the due date, normally they will not receive the cash discount earned for paying on time. But you may have situations when you want to give the customer the discount anyway (e.g., good customer, the check was late due to slow mail, you are behind in applying cash). If you override Disc Appl, you must enter an unearned discount code to justify the discount (refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount).
Related Report
Description
Unearned Cash Discounts Report
Lists cash discounts that were allowed and taken even though cash was applied to the invoice after the discount due date.
 
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Unearned Discount Reason Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Unearned Disc Reason
The 1 or 2 digit alphanumeric code to use when overriding the discount amount in Cash Receipts Entry.
Description
The description of the unearned discount reason code that explains why you are overriding the discount.
F2-Save
Saves the unearned discount reason code.
F4-Del
Deletes the unearned discount reason code.
