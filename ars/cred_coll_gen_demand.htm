Credit Collections Letter On Demand screen
If you want to send a collection letter to a customer even though they are not scheduled for a letter by Credit Collections Nightly Processing, you can use the Credit Collections Letter On Demand screen to generate the letter independently from the scheduling process. Refer to Generating Collection Letters on Demand.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Processes > Credit Collections Letter on Demand
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer to receive the credit collection letter.
Letter
The activity code that determines the letter to include. Collection letters are set up in the Credit Collections Correspondence Definition screen (refer to Creating Credit Collections Activity Codes).
Invoices To Include
Indicates which invoices to include in the letter:
•
All overdue invoices.
•
All undisputed overdue invoices. You can mark an invoice as disputed in Manual Invoice Entry (refer to Manually Entering an Invoice) or in Accounts Receivable Maintenance (refer to Editing an A/R Invoice). Include Disputed Invoices in Collections in Additional Option Parameters determines if disputed invoices are included in the Credit Collections Subsystem.
•
Days overdue. The number of days past due in Days Overdue.
•
No Invoices. Exclude invoices from printing on the letter.
•
Select Invoices. Opens the Invoice List screen to allow you to enter the invoice numbers to include in the letter.
•
Complete list. Includes all positive balance invoices for the customer, even if they are not past due.
Days Overdue
If you selected Days Overdue for Invoices to Include, the number of days overdue an invoice must be to be included on the generated invoice list within the letter.
Document Option
Indicates how the letter is sent to the customer:
•
B - Mail and fax.
•
E - Email.
•
F - Fax.
•
M - Mail.
•
S - Mail and email.
Printer Form Queue
The printer where the documents should print if mailing the letter to the customer.
Postscript Message
Text to print at the bottom of the letter.
