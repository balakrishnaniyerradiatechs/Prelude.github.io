Printing Service Charge Invoices
After you generate service charges (refer to Automatically Assessing Service Charges and Manually Assessing or Editing a Service Charge), you can print, fax, or email the service charge invoices.
In Prelude, the term Printing can also refer to faxing or e-mailing, depending on customer settings.
To create service charge invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Service Charge Invoice Printing to open the Service Charge Invoice Printing screen.
2
For S/C Invoice Number, enter the service charge invoice or leave blank to print all service charge invoices.
3
Enter the Warehouse Number or leave blank for all warehouses.
4
If applicable, enter an Invoice Message to appear on all the service charge invoices.
5
Enter the Printer Form Queue where the service charge invoices should print.
Delay SC Post Until Collect? in Accounts Receivable Parameters determines when and how the service charge posts to the general ledger.
Reprint Service Charge Invoices
To reprint service charge invoices, select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Service Charge Invoice Reprinting.
