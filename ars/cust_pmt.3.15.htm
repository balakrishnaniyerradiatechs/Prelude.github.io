Completing the Batch
After you apply cash from all checks (refer to Applying Cash to Invoices), you can complete the batch.
To complete the batch:
1
Press F2-Save from the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen.
•
If the amounts balance, go to step 2.
•
If the Amount Applied does not equal the Check Amount when you press F2-Save on the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen (refer to Applying Cash to Invoices), the following dialog box appears:
The Detail does not balance to the Check Amount.
Choose one of the following:
-
Rtn to Detail Entry - Returns to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen to allow you to adjust the invoices.
-
Chge Chk Amt to Detail - Automatically changes the Check Amount to the Amount Applied.
-
Carry Over Bal - Applies the Amount Remaining and Check Amount to the next customer’s account. The Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen re-appears with the check information only. Enter the next Customer and apply the cash to their invoices.
2
When the amounts balance, the Cash Receipts Entry screen appears. The Batch Total Applied shows the amount of all the checks applied in this batch.
3
Verify that the Deposit Total matches the Batch Total Applied. If it does not match, press F6-Status Display to open the Cash Receipts Status Display screen to review the status of each check and to verify that the amounts are correct. If you try to post the batch without balancing the totals, the following message appears:
Batch Total Applied Does Not Balance To Deposit Total! Do You Wish To Return To Cash Receipts Entry To Resume Cash Applications Or Suspend The Current Batch?
•
Choose from the following:
-
Return - Returns to the Cash Receipts Entry screen. Either change the Deposit Total or select F5-Enter Detail to go back to the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen to edit the batch.
-
Suspend Batch - Suspends the batch so that you can access and update it later.
WARNING: Even if you suspend the batch, the general ledger has already been posted to and the invoices are marked as paid on the customer’s A/R aging. Run the Incomplete Cash Receipts Report weekly to view and complete the suspended batches.
4
Once the Deposit Total matches the Batch Total Applied, press F2-Save to complete the batch. The following message appears:
Would you like a deposit slip printed?
5
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Completes the batch and allows you to choose the method to print or transmit the deposit slip.
•
No - Completes the batch but does not print a deposit slip.
