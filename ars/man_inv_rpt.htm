A/R Manual Invoice Report
The A/R Manual Invoice Report lists all manually entered invoices for a date range. A manual invoice does not affect inventory or sales but updates accounts receivable and the general ledger (refer to Manually Entering an Invoice). You can choose to list detail information for each invoice for each customer or to only see a total amount of manual invoices entered within a time frame.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > A/R Manual Invoice Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Post Date Range. The beginning and ending dates that the manual invoices posted to the general ledger.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer for whom a manual invoice was entered.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice#
The manual invoice number.
Tm
The payment terms assigned to the invoice. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms for information on payment terms.
Rep1
The primary sales representative assigned to the invoice.
Inv Date
The date the manual invoice was entered.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive the cash discount.
RE
Indicates if the invoice is for replacement equipment:
•
Y - The invoice is for replacement equipment.
•
N - The invoice is not for replacement equipment.
Inv. Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
Dist. Prefix
The distribution prefix numbers depending on how you set up your general ledger (e.g., division, region, location, department, cost center).
Account#
The general ledger account number updated by this invoice.
Dist Amt
The amount distributed to the corresponding general ledger account.
