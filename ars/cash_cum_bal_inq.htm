Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen
The Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen lists unpaid invoices for the customer. You can view the amount of each open invoice and the cumulative A/R balance.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > F7-Cumm Bal
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoices
The open invoices for the customer.
Balance
The unpaid balance for each invoice.
Cumulative
The cumulative balance which includes the amount of the corresponding invoice and previous invoices.
OK
Closes the cumulative balance inquiry screen.
