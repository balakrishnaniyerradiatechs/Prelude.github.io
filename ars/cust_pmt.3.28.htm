Recording Cash Deposited from Cash Sales
When you deposit the cash received from cash sales in the bank, you can use Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance to update bank reconciliation records.
Related Report
Description
Cash Deposit Report
Lists cash deposits made in Cash Deposit Maintenance.
To record cash sales deposits:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Cash Deposit Maintenance to open the Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance screen.
2
For Type, select Cash Sale Deposit.
3
For Deposit Number, accept the default of the next system generated number or enter your own number.
4
For Bank Code, select the code for the bank where the cash is being deposited.
5
Identify the Warehouse where the cash was received.
6
For Deposit Date, enter the date the cash is being deposited.
7
Enter the Deposit Amount.
8
Press F2-Save to save the deposit information and to update the general ledger.
