Aged A/R User Defined Dates Report
The Aged A/R User Defined Dates report lists open customer accounts receivable information for the date range that you choose.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > A/R Aging Reports > Aged A/R User-Def Dates
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer Range. The beginning and ending customer numbers to include. Leave blank to include all customers.
•
Ship Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the material.
•
Sort by Warehouse?.
-
Y - Sort by selling warehouse.
-
N - Do not sort by warehouse.
•
First Aging Date. The first date to use to view outstanding invoices. You can age the invoices in periods rather than the standard 1-30, 31-61, and so on.
•
Second Aging Date. The second date to view outstanding invoices. You can age the invoices in periods rather than the standard 1-30, 31-61, and so on.
•
Third Aging Date. The third date to view outstanding invoices. You can age the invoices in periods rather than the standard 1-30, 31-61, and so on.
•
Report As Of Date. The cutoff date for the report (does not have to be a month-end date).
•
Age by Due Date or Invoice Date.
-
D - Age by due date.
-
I - Age by invoice date.
•
Detail Or Summary?:
-
D - List each invoice for each customer.
-
S - Only list total amounts due for each customer.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
The customer with outstanding invoices.
Customer Name
The customer description
Inv Number
The invoice number with an outstanding balance.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Outstanding
Invoice Amount
The total outstanding invoice amount.
Applied Cash
The amount of cash applied against the invoice.
On Or After (third aging date)
The outstanding balance due as of the third aging date entered in the selection criteria.
On Or After (second aging date)
The outstanding balance due as of the second aging date entered in the selection criteria.
On Or After (first aging date)
The outstanding balance due as of the first aging date entered in the selection criteria.
Before (first aging date)
All outstanding invoices before the first aging date.
