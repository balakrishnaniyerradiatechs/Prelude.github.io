Generating Collection Letters on Demand
If you want to send a collection letter to a customer, even though they are not past due on payments and a letter is not scheduled by Credit Card Nightly Processing, you can generate the letter independently from the scheduling process.
To generate a credit collection letter:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Processes > Credit Collections Letter on Demand to open the Credit Collections Letter On Demand screen.
2
Enter the Customer for whom you want to send a credit collection letter.
3
For Letter, enter the activity code that determines the letter to include. Collection letters are set up in the Credit Collections Correspondence Definition screen. Refer to Creating Credit Collections Activity Codes.
4
For Invoices To Include, indicate which invoices to include in the letter. Choose from the following:
•
All overdue invoices.
•
All undisputed overdue invoices Refer to the Include Disputed Invoices in Collections flag in Additional Option Parameters.
•
Days overdue. Enter the number of Days Overdue that an invoice must be to appear in the generated invoice list within the letter.
•
No Invoices. Exclude an invoice list from printing on the letter.
•
Select Invoices. Opens the Invoice List screen to allow you to enter the invoice numbers to include in the letter.
•
Complete list. Includes all positive balance invoices for the customer, including invoices that are current.
5
For Days Overdue, if you selected Days Overdue for Invoices to Include, enter the number of days overdue an invoice must be to appear on the generated invoice list within the letter.
6
For Document Option, indicate how the letter is sent to the customer:
•
B - Mail (print) and fax.
•
E - Email.
•
F - Fax.
•
M - Mail.
•
S - Mail and email.
7
Enter the Printer Form Queue where the documents should print if mailing the letter to the customer.
8
For Postscript Message, enter up to three lines of text to print at the bottom of the letter.
9
Press ENTER. The following message appears:
Load Letter Head In Printer & Align Visually
10
Choose from the following:
•
Print Letter. Prints the letters.
•
Abort Printing. Cancels the print process.
Once you choose Print Letter, the following message appears:
Credit Collections Letter Printing Complete...
11
Choose from the following:
•
Process. If you want to update the credit collections activities so that the letters do not print again the next time you generate letters.
•
Reprint All. If the letters did not print correctly, saves the generated information to allow you to reprint the letters.
•
Abort.
Once you choose Process , the following message appears:
Are You Sure You Want to Process?
12
Choose from the following:
•
Yes. Updates the scheduled credit collections activities.
•
No. Does not update credit collections activities. The letters will print again the next time you run the Credit Collections Letter Generation.
Once you choose Yes, the notes will appear in Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance and the Credit Collections History Inquiry. You can see who printed the letter, the letter type, and the date the letter was printed.
