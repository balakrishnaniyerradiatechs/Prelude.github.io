Manually Entering an Invoice
To bill a customer for services or goods that do not affect inventory, enter a manual invoice. The manual invoice updates accounts receivable and the general ledger but does not print an invoice or documentation or update any other records (e.g., order history, sales analysis).
If you do require printed documentation for invoices, you can enter a regular sales order and enter M or MN product numbers or service charge items to bill customers for special charges without affecting inventory and sales.
Related Report
Description
A/R Manual Invoice Report
Detail Information for all manual invoices within a date range.
To manually create an invoice:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Manual Invoice Entry to open the Manual Invoice Entry screen.
If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
For Invoice Number, choose from the following:
•
Press ENTER over NEW for the next system generated number.
•
Enter your own invoice number.
3
Enter the Customer number or keyword, or press F3 to search.
4
If applicable, enter the Customer P/O.
5
For Salesrep, enter the number for the sales representative who will receive credit for this sale. The default is Salesrep One assigned to the customer in Customer Maintenance.
6
Enter the Invoice Date. This date calculates the fiscal period and due date.
7
Enter the Payment Terms that apply to this invoice. Payment terms are necessary to calculate the Discount Date and Due Date (refer to Using A/R Payment Terms for more information).
8
Accept or change the Discount Date, which is the date the invoice must be paid to receive the cash discount.
9
Accept or change the Due Date, which is the date the invoice is due if the discount is not taken.
10
Enter the total Invoice Amount.
11
Accept or change the Discount Amount, which is the discount the customer gets for paying by the discount due date defined in their payment terms.
12
To indicate a different status of an invoice, choose one of the following codes for Invoice Status:
•
None.
•
Bankruptcy.
•
Collections.
•
Disputed. Include Disputed Invoices in Collections in Additional Option Parameters determines if disputed invoices are included in the Credit Collections Subsystem.
•
Litigation.
•
Insufficient funds.
•
Rebate.
•
Write-off.
13
For Repl. Equipment, choose from the following:
•
Y - The invoice is for replacement equipment for material returned on an RGA.
•
N - The invoice is not for replacement equipment.
Replacement Equipment Option Parameter
To generate a replacement order from an RGA, you must set Generate Replacement Equip S/O (Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters) to Y.
14
Enter the Division, Region, Location, Department Cost Center as required by your company.
15
Enter the general ledger Account that this invoice should update.
16
For Dist Amount, enter the amount to distribute to the corresponding Account. You can enter multiple accounts and amounts, but the total distribution amount must equal the invoice amount.
Comments
Press F5-Comments to enter any information to explain the purpose of the manual invoice.
17
Press F2-Save to generate the manual invoice and to update the general ledger.
To view manual invoice information, see the Manual A/R Inquiry.
