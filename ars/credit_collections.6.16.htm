Entering Credit Collections Notes
To enter credit collection notes for a customer:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Maintenance > Credit Collections Notes Maintenance to open the Credit Collections Notes Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer for whom you want to record notes.
3
For Note Type, choose one of the following types of notes:
•
P - Permanent. The notes stay in the customer’s credit collections history indefinitely.
•
G - General. The notes have an expiration date. You must use this option to access Expiration Days.
•
T - Temporary. The notes are associated with a customer invoice. You must use this option to access Invoice Number. When the invoice is paid, the note is removed from the customer’s collection history.
4
For Expiration Days, enter the number of days until the notes are removed from the customer’s credit collections history. The default is set in Additional Option Parameters. This field is only applicable if you chose G for Note Type.
5
If Note Type is T, enter the Invoice Number to reference the temporary notes. When the invoice is paid, the note is removed from the customer’s credit collections history.
6
Press F5-Notes to open the Credit Collections Customer Notes screen.
7
Enter any notes regarding the customer or the invoice.
8
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the notes information.
