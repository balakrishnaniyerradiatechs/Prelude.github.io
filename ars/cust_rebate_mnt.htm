Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance screen
Customer rebates are calculated according to the rebate program assigned to the customer and the date the customer paid the invoice (refer to Creating a Customer Rebate Program). If the customer paid an invoice after the date defined by their rebate program, you can use Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance to override the eligibility setting to allow them to receive the rebate anyway. You can also disallow the customer from receiving a rebate for a selected invoice. Refer to Editing Customer Invoice Rebates for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance
