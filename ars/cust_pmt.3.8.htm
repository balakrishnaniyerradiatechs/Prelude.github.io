Reconciling Partial Payments
If the amount entered in Cash Applied in the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen (refer to Applying Cash to Invoices) is less than the Invoice Amt, the following warning appears:
Warning, This is a partial payment!
1
Press Continue.
2
For M/C, choose from the following:
•
A - Adjust to Existing Invoices (refer to Adjusting Tax and Freight).
•
C - Charge Back Entry (refer to Issuing Charge Back Invoices).
•
M - Miscellaneous Entry (refer to Writing off the Unpaid Balance).
•
N - No Adjustment (default).
Invoice Detail Inquiry
To examine an invoice (e.g., a discrepancy exists between what the customer is paying and the open balance), from the Action Bar select Invoice Func > Examine Invoice to open the Invoice Detail Inquiry. You can examine the detail information on the invoice including freight, tax, and any pending RGAs.
 
Accounts Receivable Maintenance
To edit invoice due dates, discount dates, or other A/R information, select Action Bar > Invoice Func > Invoice Maintenance to open the Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen. Refer to Editing an A/R Invoice.
 
Cumulative Balance Inquiry
To view a cumulative balance of open invoices, press F7-Cumm Bal to open the Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen. The individual balance for each invoice displays along with the cumulative balance going down the list.
