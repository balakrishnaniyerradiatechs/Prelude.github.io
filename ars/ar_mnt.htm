Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen
You can use the Accounts Receivable Maintenance screen to edit the due date, invoice status, or other information on an open invoice. Refer to Editing an A/R Invoice for more information.
You can also access this screen to edit an invoice from the Credit Collections Schedule Entry screen and the Cash Receipts Invoice Detail Entry screen.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > A/R Invoice Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice Number
The invoice number to edit.
Customer
The customer assigned to the invoice.
Invoice Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Discount Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive a discount.
Invoice Status
The status of the invoice:
•
None.
•
Bankruptcy.
•
Collections.
•
Disputed. Include Disputed Invoices in Collections in Additional Option Parameters determines if disputed invoices are included in the Credit Collections Subsystem
•
Litigation.
•
Insufficient funds.
•
Rebate.
•
Write-off.
Repl Equipment
Indicates if the order is for replacement equipment:
•
Y - The invoice is for replacement equipment for material returned on an RGA
•
N - The invoice is not for replacement equipment.
Generate Replacement Equip S/O in Order Processing Parameters determines if you can generate a replacement order from an RGA.
Excluded
Indicates if the invoice is excluded from the credit limit test:
•
Y - Exclude this invoice from the normal customer open A/R balance. The invoice will be held in the excluded A/R balance so that it is not evaluated in the credit limit check for new sales orders. Refer to Excluding Sales Orders From the Credit Limit.
•
N - Include this invoice in the credit limit test.
Customer P/O#
The customer’s purchase order number.
Cust Release#
The customer’s release number.
F2-Save
Saves the edited A/R information.
F5-Notes
Opens the Invoice Notes screen to allow you to enter notes regarding the invoice. These notes do not print anywhere and are for internal reference only.
F6-Invoice Review
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry to allow you to view information on the open invoice.
Action Bar
Multi Level Discounts
Opens the Multi Level Discount screen to allow you to view or edit additional discount dates and discount amounts.
