Customers With On Account Balances Report
The Customers With On Account Balances report lists all customers, by credit manager, that have extra cash applied to their account. If funds are leftover in Cash Receipts Entry after a customer pays an invoice, you can apply the cash to their account to use for future orders. Refer to Applying Extra Cash On-Account.
You can choose to list each individual invoice to which cash was applied or to view only the total amount of cash on-account for each customer.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Cash Receipts Reports > Customer With On Account Balances
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Sell Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the order, regardless of the shipping warehouse.
•
Credit Manager. The credit manager responsible for the accounts.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer account number.
Description/Contract
Line 1: The customer description.
Line 2: The customer contact.
Phone
The customer contact’s phone number.
A/R Balance
The customer’s open accounts receivable balance. Sales orders invoiced but not yet paid.
LastPaid
The date that cash was last applied to this customer’s account.
LastSale
The last date that an order was entered for this customer.
Invoice#
The invoice number created for the cash on-account amount (refer to Applying Extra Cash On-Account).
Inv Date
The date the cash on-account invoice was created.
Balance Due
Amount of cash on-account for the customer.
Coa
A Y indicates that the customer has extra cash-on-account.
