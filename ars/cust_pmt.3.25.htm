Entering a Credit Card Payment Batch
To pay open customer invoices with a credit card:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > A/R Credit Card > A/R Credit Card Payment Entry to open the A/R Credit Card Payment Entry screen.
2
For Batch Number, press ENTER over NEW to assign the next system generated number.
3
For Transaction Date, accept the default of today’s date or enter the appropriate date to post the payment. The fiscal period displays to the right.
4
Enter the Bank Code to designate where the money is being deposited.
5
Enter the Customer that is making the payment. The customer’s open A/R Balance displays.
If the customer’s account is set up with default credit card information (Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F7-Credit Card), the information defaults for Credit Card Type, Credit Card Number, and Expiration Date.
6
For Credit Card Type, enter the code for the credit card company (e.g., M/C, Visa, AMEX). These are set up in Credit Bureau Maintenance in the Order Processing module.
7
Enter the customer’s Credit Card Number.
8
Enter the Expiration Date on the card.
9
For CVV number, if required, enter the 3-digit code on the back of the card. This number is used by some companies to prevent theft. This information is not stored in the system.
10
If you use the AVS (address verification service) enter the AVS Street Addr and the AVS Zip Code for the card user’s billing address. If you do not use address verification, leave these fields blank.
11
For Invoice List Option, choose from the following:
•
A - Lists all open invoices for the customer.
•
S - Allows you to enter select invoices for the customer.
The invoice date, invoice amount, open balance, and available discount display for each open invoice.
For each invoice line item, Cash Applied displays the balance minus the discount available. The customer earns a discount if they pay the invoice on time (refer to Using A/R Payment Terms).
12
Enter the payment amount to apply to the customer’s credit card for each invoice. Change the amount if necessary. The total Amount Applied displays at the bottom of the screen.
13
Press F2-Save after all invoices are applied. Once the credit card transaction is complete, the following message appears:
This Transaction has been approved
14
Press RETURN to continue with the next transaction.
