Creating Unearned Discount Reason Codes
If a customer pays an invoice past the discount date, normally they will not receive the cash discount earned for paying on time. But, you may have situations when you want to give the customer the discount anyway (e.g., good customer, the check was late due to slow mail, you are behind in applying cash). If the payment is late, the Disc Appl (Discount Applied) defaults to zero. If you override Disc Appl, you are required to enter an unearned discount code to justify the discount (refer to Overriding an Unearned Discount).
Related Report
Description
Unearned Cash Discounts Report
Cash discounts that were allowed although cash was applied to the invoice after the discount due date.
 
To create unearned discount reason codes:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Unearned Discount Reason Maintenance to open the Unearned Discount Reason Maintenance screen.
2
For Unearned Disc Reason, enter a 1 or 2 digit alphanumeric code to use if you override the discount amount when applying cash.
3
Enter the Description that explains why you are overriding the discount amount (e.g., good customer, mail delay).
4
Press F2-Save to save the unearned discount code.
