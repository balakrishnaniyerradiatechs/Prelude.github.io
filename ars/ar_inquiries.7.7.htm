A/R Terms Inquiry
The A/R Terms Inquiry displays payment information for each payment terms code set up in A/R Payment Terms Maintenance. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms for information on defining and setting up payment terms.
Viewing A/R Terms
To view information for a payment terms code:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > A/R Terms Inquiry to open the A/R Terms Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Payment Terms code to view.
Information for the terms code appears.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Description
The description of the payment terms code.
Terms Percent
The discount percentage for this terms code. For example, if Terms Percent is 2% and is applied to an invoice totaling $100.00, the discount is $2.00.
Due Date
The date invoices are due.
Net Days Allowed
The number of days after the invoice date when payment is due. For example, if the invoice date is 5/10 and Net Days Allowed is 15, payment is due by 5/25. If Net Days Allowed is P15 (Prox Terms), payment is due by 6/25.
Net Day-Of-Month
The day of the month when payment is due. Net Day-Of-Month works with Cutoff Day. For example, if Net Day-Of-Month is 25 and Cutoff Day is 15, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the month. If Net Day-Of-Month is P25 and Cutoff Day is 25, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the next month.
Cutoff Day
The last day on which invoices dated before Net Day-Of-Month are due. See Net Day-Of-Month for an example.
Net Day-Of-Month
If this terms code has 2 cutoff days, the second due date.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the second due date.
Net Day-Of-Month
If the terms code has 3 cutoff days, the third due date.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the third due date.
Discount Date, Disc. Days Allowed, or Disc. Day-Of-Month are only required if you offer a discount to customers who pay invoices early.
Discount Date
The date customers should pay by to receive a discount. This date overrides the calculated discount date.
Disc. Days Allowed
The number of days after the invoice date when a discount no longer applies. For example, if an invoice is dated 5/25, and the Discount Days Allowed is 15, the customer must pay before 6/09 to receive the discount.
Disc. Day-Of-Month
The day of the month on which payment is due for the customer to receive a discount. Disc. Day-Of-Month works with Cutoff Day. For example, if Disc. Day-Of-Month is 25 and Cutoff Day is 15, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the month to receive a discount. If Disc. Day-Of-Month is P25 and Cutoff Day is 25, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the next month to receive a discount.
Cutoff Day
The last day on which invoices dated before the Disc. Day-Of-Month are due. See Disc. Day-Of-Month for an example.
Disc. Day-Of-Month
If the terms code has 2 cutoff days, the second discount date.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the second discount date.
Disc. Day-Of-Month
If the terms code has 3 cutoff days, enter the third discount date.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the third discount date.
Cash Type
Indicates if the terms apply to cash sales:
•
Y - The sale is processed as a cash sale.
•
N - The sale is not a cash sale.
Credit Card Type
Indicates if the terms apply to credit card sales:
•
Y - The sale is processed as a credit card sale.
•
N - The sale is not a credit card sale.
C.O.D. Type
Indicates if the terms require cash on delivery (C.O.D.):
•
Y - The sales order requires C.O.D.
•
N - The sales order does not require C.O.D.
Cash Equivalent
If C.O.D is Y, indicate if the customer must provide cash or cash equivalent (such as a money order or cashier’s check) at the time of delivery:
•
Y - The customer must provide cash, money order, or cashier’s check. No personal checks.
•
N - The customer can pay by personal check or other means.
If you use PSS, this information is passed on to PSS.
Installments
Determines if multiple A/R invoices are created based on an installment payment schedule:
•
Y - Installment invoices are produced based on the payment schedule. A single invoice is created for each payment with a new calculated discount and due date. All invoices are created at the same time.
•
N - Installment type invoices are not used.
Pay In Advance
Indicates if a customer must pay in advance:
•
Y - The customer must pay in advance, even for backordered merchandise.
•
N - The customer does not have to pay in advance.
Lease Type
Indicates if the payment type is for leased material:
•
Y - The order is for leased material.
•
N - The order is not for leased material.
Deposit Required
Indicates if a deposit is required from customers:
•
Y - A deposit is required on sales orders, even if the material is backordered.
•
N - A deposit is not required.
Credit Card Entry Mode
Indicates this A/R terms code activates the credit card entry mode:
•
Y - The credit card information screen in the sales order header is suppressed and the Sales Order Completion screen automatically appears upon completion of the order. The customer’s credit card information defaults from Customer Maintenance.
•
N - The credit card entry mode is not activated.
F2-Save
Saves the A/R term information.
F4-Delete
Deletes the A/R terms.
F5-Multiple Discount Levels
Opens the Multiple Discount Levels screen to allow you to view any additional percentages set up for the terms code.
