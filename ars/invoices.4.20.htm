Accounts Receivable Parameters
To set system statement printing options:
1
Select Option Parameters > Accounts Receivable Parameters to open the Accounts Receivable Parameters screen.
2
For Min. Stmt Amount, enter the minimum amount a customer must owe to receive a statement. Statements will generate for customers that owe this amount or more (balance forward statements not included).
3
For Min. Stmt Credit Amount, enter the minimum amount of credit owed to a customer that will generate a statement for this customer. Statements will generate for customers that are due this amount or greater.
4
For Save M/E A/R Stmt Detail, determine if customer statements are saved to the customers’ A/R history during Month-End Customer A/R aging Save.
•
Y - Customer statement history is updated (necessary if you require reprints of customer statements).
•
N - Customer statement history is not updated.
This flag only applies to customers that have Print Statement set to Y in Customer Maintenance. If the customer has Print Statement set to B (balance forward), the statement updates when printed. Refer to Customer Maintenance).
