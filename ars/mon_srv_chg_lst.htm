Service Charge Journal
The Service Charge Journal lists service charge invoices created for a warehouse for a fiscal period. Service charges are service fees passed on to customers for past due invoices. You can set your system to automatically calculate the service charge invoice based on a percent defined in each customer’s record. Refer to Generating Service Charge Invoices for more information.
You can choose to list detail information for each service charge invoice for each customer or to only show the total amount of service charge invoices billed within a fiscal period.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Service Charge Journal
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse numbers to include.
•
Period. The fiscal period to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice#
The service charge invoice number. SC displays at the end of each service charge invoice.
Customer
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Due Date
The date the service charge invoice is due.
Period
The fiscal period in which the service charge was generated.
Srv Charge
The service charge percent set up in Customer Maintenance that determines the service charge percent to charge this customer for past due invoices.
Basis
The invoice base amount used to calculate the service charge.
Charge Amt
The calculated service charge amount.
