Customer Invoice Inquiry
The Customer Invoice Inquiry displays detail information for each invoice for a customer. Selection Criteria allows you to sort or limit the list by date or by invoice status.
Viewing Open Customer Invoices
To list customer invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Customer Invoice Inquiry to open the Customer Invoice Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
For Sort, choose from the following:
•
By Invoice Number.
•
By Invoice Date.
•
By Due Date.
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Descending Due Date.
4
For Filters, choose from the following:
•
Open Invoices.
•
Paid Invoices.
•
Both Open & Paid Invoices.
•
Yes. Opens the Customer Invoice Inquiry Filter Options screen to allow you to further limit the selection criteria by invoice status or invoice date.
Invoices for the customer appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice#
The customer’s invoice number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for the invoice.
Whse
The selling warehouse.
Inv Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Past
The number of days the invoice is past its due date, if applicable.
IS
The payment status of the invoice:
•
Blank - None.
•
B - Bankruptcy.
•
C - Collections.
•
D - Disputed.
•
L - Litigation.
•
N - Insufficient funds.
•
P - An unpaid invoice has an open RGA.
•
R - Rebate.
•
W - Write-off.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
C
Indicates if the sales order is set up for consolidated invoice printing:
•
Y - The sales order is set up for consolidated invoice printing (Consolidate Inv? set to Y in the sales order header).
•
Blank - The sales order is not set up for consolidated invoice printing.
Balance Due
The amount of the invoice that is still due.
Status
The status of the invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
P - Paid.
Excluded
Indicates if the order is excluded from the credit limit test:
•
Y - The value order is excluded from the credit limit check calculations.
•
N - The order value is included in the credit limit check.
Refer to Excluding Sales Orders From the Credit Limit.
F5-Review Invoice
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry to display detail information for the invoice including freight, tax, and discount amounts.
F7-Cumulative
Opens the Cumulative Balance Inquiry screen to allow you to view the running balance for all open invoices.
F8-Credit Comment
Opens the Credit Comments screen to display credit comments for the invoice.
Edit Credit Comments in Accounts Receivable Parameters indicates how notes are entered and viewed in Accounts Receivable Inquiry, Customer Invoice Inquiry, and Customer Maintenance.
•
Y - Enter free form credit comments.
•
N - Use the standard Notes Entry screen for credit comments.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Returns to the previous page if more than one page of transactions appear. The number of records that display depend on Records to Block in Company Maintenance > Action Bar > GUI Options.
sF6-Next Group
Forwards to the next page if more than one page of transactions appear. The number of records that display depend on Records to Block in Company Maintenance > Action Bar > GUI Options.
Action Bar
Images
If your company uses CoStar ® imaging, you can access images associated with this invoice and customer.
Review Consol Hist
If the invoice is a consolidated invoiced, the Consolidated Invoice Inquiry displays information on consolidated invoices. Refer to the Order Processing documentation for more information on consolidated invoicing.
