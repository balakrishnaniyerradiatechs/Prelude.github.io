Cash Deposit Report
The Cash Deposit report lists bank deposits made from cash sales. Refer to Recording Cash Deposited from Cash Sales for information on Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance.
You can choose to list deposits made at each branch or to only show total deposits per branch.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Cash Deposit Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that sold the material.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the material.
Description
The description of the warehouse.
Dep Date
The date the cash was deposited in the bank.
Bank
The code for the bank where the cash was deposited.
Description
The description of the bank.
Deposit Amt
The amount of cash deposited in the bank.
User
The user ID of the person that entered the deposit in Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance.
Date
The date the information was entered in Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance.
Time
The time the information was entered in Cash Sales Deposit Maintenance.
