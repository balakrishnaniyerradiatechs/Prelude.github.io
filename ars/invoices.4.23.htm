Purging Zero Balance A/R Records
After accounts receivable invoices are completely paid, you can purge the zero balance invoices.
To purge zero balance A/R invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Purge Zero Balance A/R records to open the Purge Zero Balance A/R Records screen.
2
For With Warehouse Number Equal To, enter the invoice selling warehouse where you want the records purged or leave blank to purge records for all warehouses.
3
For With Customer Number Equal To, enter the customer whose records you want to purge or leave blank to include all customers in the selected warehouses.
4
For With Zero Bal Date Less Than Or Equal To, enter the date to purge all zero balance invoices. Any invoices paid before this date are purged.
5
Press ENTER to purge the zero balance invoices.
