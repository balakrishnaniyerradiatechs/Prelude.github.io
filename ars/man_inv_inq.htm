Manual A/R Inquiry
The Manual A/R Inquiry displays information for an invoice that was entered in Manual Invoice Entry (refer to Manually Entering an Invoice).
Viewing a Manual Invoice
To view information on a manual invoice:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Manual A/R Inquiry to open the Manual A/R Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Invoice Number.
Detail information for the invoice appears.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice Number
The manual invoice number.
Customer
The customer billed on the manual invoice.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number.
Salesrep
The primary salesrep assigned to the customer in Salesrep One in Customer Maintenance.
Invoice Date
The date the invoice was entered.
Payment Terms
The payment terms used on this invoice to calculate the discount. Payment terms are assigned to a customer in Customer Maintenance but you can override this field in Manual Invoice Entry. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms for more information.
Discount Date
The date the customer must pay the invoice to receive the calculated discount.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Invoice Status
The payment status of the invoice:
•
Blank - None.
•
B - Bankruptcy.
•
C - Collections.
•
D - Disputed.
•
L - Litigation.
•
N - Insufficient funds.
•
P - An unpaid customer invoice has an open RGA.
•
R - Rebate.
•
W - Write-off.
Invoice Amount
The total amount billed to the customer.
Distribution G/L Account
Div
The division this customer/invoice is assigned to. The division is the highest possible grouping in your company.
Reg
The region this customer/invoice is assigned to. The region is a subset of Division - if Division is used.
Loc
The selling warehouse this customer/invoice is assigned to.
Dep
The department this customer/invoice is assigned to. The department is a subset of warehouse/location. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse.
Ccr
The cost center this customer/invoice is assigned to. A cost center is a subset of department - if department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department.
Account#
The general ledger account that this invoice updates.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
