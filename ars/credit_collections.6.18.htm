Credit Collections History Inquiry
The Credit Collections History Inquiry displays the credit collection notes entered in Credit Collections Notes Maintenance (refer to Entering Credit Collections Notes). The inquiry is updated when a scheduled collection activity is completed (refer to Entering Scheduled Credit Collection Activities) or a letter is sent to a customer (refer to Generating Collection Letters on Demand).
To view credit collection notes:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Inquiries > Credit Collections History Inquiry to open the Credit Collections History Inquiry screen.
2
For Customer Number Equal To, enter the customer for whom you would like to view credit collection notes.
3
Enter the beginning and ending dates to include.
4
For Start With Beginning or Ending Entries?, choose from the following:
•
B - View credit collection notes from beginning to end (oldest note first).
•
E - View credit collection notes from end to beginning (most recent note first).
5
Press ENTER and choose the method of output.
