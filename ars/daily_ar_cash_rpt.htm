Open Accounts Receivable Report
The Open Accounts Receivable report lists open invoices for customers including the discount amounts and due dates. You can choose to run the report to display only the total amount due per customer or to list each open invoice.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Open Accounts Receivable
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer Range. The beginning and ending customer numbers to include. Leave blank to include all customers.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The number for the customer with open invoices.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice#
The open invoice number.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Inv. Amount
The amount originally billed to the customer on the invoice.
Dsc Date
The date by which the customer must pay the invoice to receive the Disc Amount. Payment dates and discount amounts are determined by the customer’s payment terms code. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms.
Disc Amount
The discount amount the customer will receive if they pay their invoice by the Dsc Date.
Balance Due
The amount that the customer still owes on the invoice.
Check Number
The check number, if the customer already made a partial payment on the invoice.
Check Amount
The amount of the check, if the customer already made a partial payment on the invoice.
