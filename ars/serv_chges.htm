Auto Service Charge Entry screen
The Auto Service Charge Entry screen allows you to generate service charges for past due invoices. Normally, service charges are generated once a month. If you run Auto Service Charge Entry more than once a month, a warning message appears. Refer to Generating Service Charge Invoices for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Auto Service Charge Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Current Invoice Date
The date to appear on the service charge invoices.
Last S/C Run Date
Displays the last date the service charge process ran. Normally, service charges are generated once a month. If you run it more than once a month, a warning message appears.
Current Days Limit
The days past due an invoice must exceed before service charges are calculated. For example, if you enter 120, only invoices over 120 days past due are included in the service charge calculation. This value is overridden by Service Charge Days in Customer Maintenance.
Include Credits?
Determines if credit memos are included in service charge calculations:
•
Y - Deduct credit memos from invoices before calculating service charges.
For example, a customer is set up with a Service Charge % of 2.00. The customer’s invoices over 120 days past due total $1000.00. Credit memos for the customer total $100.00. The base value used to calculate the service charge is $900 (1000 - 100) and the service charge amount is $18 (900 x .02).
•
N - Do not use credit memos in service charge calculations.
For example, a customer is set up with a Service Charge % of 2.00. The customer’s invoices over 120 days past due total $1000.00. Credit memos for the customer total $100.00. The base value used to calculate the service charge is $1000 (credit memos are ignored) and the service charge amount is $20 (1000 x .02).
Skip Charge if < Minimum?
Determines if the calculated service charge is invoiced to the customer if it is below the Min. Service Charge set in Accounts Receivable Parameters:
•
Y - If the calculated service charge is below the Min. Service Charge amount, the customer is not billed a service charge for past due invoices.
•
N - If the calculated service charge amount is below the Min. Service Charge amount, the service charge is increased to meet the Min. Service Charge.
Include Prev Service Chrgs?
Determines if previously calculated service charges are included in the new service charge calculation:
•
Y - Previous service charges that meet the Current Days Limit are included in the base invoice amount used to calculate the current service charge.
•
N - Previous service charges are excluded from service charge calculations.
Create Invoice for each Whse?
Determines if you will create service charge invoices by customer or by warehouse by customer:
•
Y - Create service charge invoices by warehouse by customer (e.g., one service charge invoice is created for each customer/warehouse combination). Warehouse is determined by the sales order’s selling warehouse.
•
N - Create service charge invoices by customer (e.g., one service charge invoice is created for each customer). Warehouse is determined by the customer’s selling warehouse set in Customer Maintenance.
