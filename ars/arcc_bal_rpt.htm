ARCC Balancing Report
The ARCC Balancing Report list detail batch information for customer credit card payments for open A/R balances.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > A/R Credit Card > ARCC Balancing Report.
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Batch. The batch number from A/R Credit Card Payment Entry. Leave blank to include all batches.
•
Invoice Number. The invoice numbers to include in the report. Leave blank to include all invoice numbers.
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report. Leave blank to include all customers.
•
Batch Date Range. The beginning and ending batch entry dates to include in the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Batch
The batch number entered in A/R Credit Card Payment Entry (refer to Entering a Credit Card Payment Batch).
Batch Date
The date the batch was entered.
User
The user ID of the employee that entered the batch.
Cust
The customer that made the credit card payment.
Cust Desc
The customer description.
Cc Type
The credit card type (e.g., VISA, M/C AMEX).
Credit Card
The customer’s credit card number.
Exp
The date the credit card expires.
Auth#
The credit card post-authorization number from protobase.
Invoice
The customer invoice number that was paid by credit card.
Inv Amt
The amount of the customer’s invoice.
Cash Appl.
The amount that was applied to the customer’s credit card.
