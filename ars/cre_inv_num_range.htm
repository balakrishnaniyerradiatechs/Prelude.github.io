Invoice Number Range Entry screen
If you use the F6-Auto Pay feature in Cash Receipts Entry, you can use the Invoice Number Range Entry screen to enter a beginning and ending invoice number to apply cash to a range of invoices.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Cash Receipts Entry > F6-Auto Pay > Invoice Number Range
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Beginning Invoice Number
The beginning customer invoice number to apply cash to.
Ending Invoice Number
The ending customer invoice number to apply cash to.
