Converting Customer Rebate Information
If you edit rebate information in one of the following fields:
•
Customer Rebate# (Customer Maintenance > F7-Order)
•
Eligibility Type (Customer Rebate Maintenance)
•
Eligible Days to Pay (Customer Rebate Maintenance)
You must run the Rebate Change Conversion to update customer’s A/R history. The next time the rebate reports print, the customer’s rebate information will include the new rebate percent and dollars. If the customer rebate code is removed, no rebate is calculated for the customer.
To update customer aging calculations with edited rebate information:
1
Select Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer Rebate Reports > Rebate Change Conversion.
2
Enter the Customer Number to update or leave blank to update all customers.
3
Enter a beginning and ending Invoice Date to include for invoiced orders for these customers.
4
For Customer Rebate Code, enter the customer rebate number to reset. The A/R history is recalculated for all customers with this rebate number.
The Customer Rebate number is created in Customer Rebate Maintenance (refer to Creating a Customer Rebate Program) and then assigned to each customer in Customer Maintenance (refer to Assigning a Rebate Program to a Customer).
