Accounts Receivable Aging Report
The Accounts Receivable Aging report lists the current or past due payment status for each invoice for a customer.
Aging Information
Open A/R Aging Method in Accounts Receivable Parameters determines if open accounts receivable are aged by date or fiscal period.
Include Discount Aging in Accounts Receivable Parameters determines if net or gross amounts are displayed with aging information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > A/R Aging Reports > Aged Accounts Receivable
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer Range. The beginning and ending customer numbers to include. Leave blank to include all customers.
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse shipped the material.
•
Salesrep. The sales representative assigned to the customer.
•
Invoice Status. Include only invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off
Leave blank to include all invoices.
•
Invoice Status#. Exclude invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off
Leave blank to include all invoices.
•
Sort by Warehouse?.
-
Y - Sort by selling warehouse.
-
N - Sort by customer or salesrep.
•
Sort by Cust or Slmn?:
-
C - Sort by customer.
-
S - Sort by sales representative.
•
Age by Due Date or Inv Date.
-
D - Age by due date.
-
I - Age by invoice date.
•
Aging Period Balances. The aging period to include (e.g, enter 1 to include customers with at least one invoice in/over aging period 1).
•
Suppress Customer Detail?.
-
Y - Do not print the contact name, phone number, and A/R terms.
-
N - Print all customer contact and terms information.
•
Totals Only?.
-
Y - Only print the totals of each aging column.
-
N - List the aging information for each invoice for each customer.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice#
The customer’s invoice number.
Cust P/O#
The customer’s purchase order number.
St#
The customer’s ship-to address:
•
D - The customer’s default address from Customer Maintenance.
•
M - A manual ship-to address was entered for the sales order.
•
Any other alphanumeric ID that was set up as a shipping location for the customer in Customer Ship-To Maintenance.
Inv Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Fut/Current
The amount of invoices that are not past due.
1-30
The total amount of invoices that are 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
The total amount of invoices that are 31 to 60 days past due.
61-90
The total amount of invoices that are 61 to 90 days past due.
Over 90
The total amount of invoices that are over 90 days past due.
Contact Name (unlabeled field)
The customer contact name from Customer Maintenance.
Phone Number (unlabeled field)
The customer contact’s phone number.
A/R Terms (unlabeled field)
The customer’s payment terms. Refer to Using A/R Payment Terms.
Last Receipt Date
(unlabeled field)
The date the last time cash was received from this customer.
Last Receipt Amount
(unlabeled field)
The amount of the last cash receipt from this customer.
