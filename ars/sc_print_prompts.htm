Service Charge Invoice Printing screen
Once you generate service charges (refer to Automatically Assessing Service Charges and Manually Assessing or Editing a Service Charge), you can use the Service Charge Invoice Printing screen to print, fax, or email the service charge invoices.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Processes > Service Charge Processing > Service Charge Invoice Printing
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
S/C Invoice Number
The service charge invoice number. Leave blank to print all service charge invoices.
Warehouse Number
The warehouse for which you want to print service charges. Leave blank to print service charges for all warehouses.
Invoice Message
A message to appear at the bottom of the service charge invoices.
Printer Form Queue
The printer number where service charges invoices print.
F5-Reprint
Allows you to reprint any service charge invoices.
