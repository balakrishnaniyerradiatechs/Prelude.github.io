Credit Collections Notes Maintenance screen
You can use the Credit Collections Notes Maintenance screen to record conversations or notes regarding the customer’s past due balances. Once you enter notes, they appear in Credit Collections Historical Notes Maintenance and in the Credit Collections History Inquiry.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Collections Subsystem > Maintenance > Credit Collections Notes Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer for whom you are recording notes.
Note Type
The types of notes:
•
P - Permanent. The notes stay in the customer’s credit collections history indefinitely.
•
G - General. The notes have an expiration date. You must use this option to access Expiration Days.
•
T - Temporary. The notes are associated with a customer invoice. You must use this option to access Invoice Number. When the invoice is paid, the note is removed from the customer’s collection history.
Expiration Days
If Note Type is G, the number of days until the notes are removed from the customer’s credit collections history. The default is 45.
Invoice Number
If Note Type is T, the invoice number to reference the temporary notes. When the invoice is paid, the note is removed from the customer’s credit collections history.
F2-Save
Saves the notes for the customer.
F5-Notes
Opens the Credit Collections Customer Notes screen to allow you to enter any notes regarding the customer or the invoice.
