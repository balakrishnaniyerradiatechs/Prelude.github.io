Detailed Aged Accounts Receivable Report
The Detailed Aged Accounts Receivable report lists the current or past due payment status for each invoice for a customer for a month-end date.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > A/R Aging Reports > Aged Accounts Receivable - Month End (Original)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer Range. The beginning and ending customer numbers to include. Leave blank to include all customers.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse shipped the material.
•
Sort by Warehouse?.
-
Y - Sort by selling warehouse.
-
N - Sort by customer or salesrep (following option).
•
Sort by Cust or Slmn?:
-
C - Sort by customer.
-
S - Sort by sales representative.
•
Detail Or Summary?:
-
D - List each invoice for each customer.
-
S - Only list total amounts due for each customer.
•
Age by Due Date or Inv Date.
-
D - Age by due date.
-
I - Age by invoice date.
•
As of Date. The month end date to use for aging invoices.
•
Include Invoice Status. Include only invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off.
Leave blank to include all invoices.
•
Exclude Invoice Status. Exclude invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off
Leave blank to include all invoices.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
The customer number.
Customer Name
The customer description.
Invoice#
The customer’s invoice number.
ShipTo
The customer’s ship-to address:
•
D - The customer’s default address from Customer Maintenance.
•
M - A manual ship-to address was entered for the sales order.
•
Any other alphanumeric character that was set up as a shipping location for the customer in Customer Ship-To Maintenance.
Inv-Date
The date the sales order was invoiced. A Y after the date indicates that the invoice is marked as disputed.
Future
The total amount of invoices that will be due more than 30 days future.
Current
The total amount of invoices that are not past due and not a future.
1-30
The total amount of invoices that are 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
The total amount of invoices that are 31 to 60 days past due.
61&Over
The total amount of invoices that are over 61 days past due.
Tot-Amount
The total value of open invoices for the customer.
