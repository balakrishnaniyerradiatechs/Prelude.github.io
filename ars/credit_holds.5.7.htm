Rental Parameters
If you use the Rental Module:
1
Select Option Parameters > Rental Parameters to open the Rental Parameters screen.
2
For Cred Chk in Periodic Billing?, determine if periodic billing is included in the credit check process.
•
Y - Include periodic billing in the credit limit check.
•
N - Do not include periodic billing in the credit check.
3
Press F2-Save to save the credit check information.
