Purging a Batch
Once you match the reference numbers with open invoices for a batch, you should purge the batch so that the paid invoices do not appear the next time you match invoices. If a batch appears on the Fast Cash Receipts Exception Report, you will not be able to purge it. You can use High Volume Cash Receipts Maintenance (refer to Manually Matching and Editing Invoices), to edit the batch before you update it again.
To purge a batch:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > High Volume Cash Receipts > Batch Purge to open the High Volume Cash Purge screen.
2
Enter the Batch Number that you want to purge. Leave blank to purge all posted batches.
3
Press F2-Save. A message appears to indicate that the batch was deleted.
4
Press ENTER.
