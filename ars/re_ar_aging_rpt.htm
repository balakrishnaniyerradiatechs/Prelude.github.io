Detail Replacement Equipment Aged Accounts Receivable Report
The Detailed Replacement Equipment Aged Accounts Receivable report lists the current or past due payment status for replacement equipment invoices for a customer. A sales order for replacement equipment is designated by setting Replacement Equip (Sales Order Header > F5-Add Info - F5-Add Info) to Y.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > A/R Aging Reports > Replacement Equipment Aged A/R
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer Range. The beginning and ending customer numbers to include. Leave blank to include all customers.
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the material.
•
Sort by Warehouse?.
-
Y - Sort by selling warehouse.
-
N - Sort by customer or salesrep.
•
Sort by Customer or Salesrep?:
-
C - Sort by customer.
-
S - Sort by sales representative.
•
Include RE Invoices?.
-
Y - Include only replacement equipment invoices.
-
N - Do not include replacement equipment invoices.
-
B - Include both replacement equipment invoices and non replacement equipment invoices.
•
Detail Or Summary?:
-
D - List each invoice for each customer.
-
S - Only list total amounts due for each customer.
•
Age by Due Date or Inv Date.
-
D - Age by due date.
-
I - Age by invoice date.
•
Include Invoice Status. Include only invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off.
•
Exclude Invoice Status. Exclude invoices with the following status codes:
-
B - Bankruptcy.
-
C - Collections.
-
D - Disputed.
-
L - Litigation.
-
N - Insufficient funds.
-
R - Rebate.
-
W - Write-off
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust #
The customer number.
Customer Name
The customer description.
Invoice#
The open invoice number.
RE
Indicates if it is a replacement equipment order:
•
Y - The order is for replacement equipment. Replacement Equip (Sales Order Header > F5-Add Info - F5-Add Info) to Y.
•
N - The order is not for replacement equipment.
ShipTo
The customer’s ship-to address:
•
D - The customer’s default address from Customer Maintenance.
•
M - A manual ship-to address was entered for the sales order.
•
Any other alphanumeric character that was set up as a shipping location for the customer in Customer Ship-To Maintenance.
Inv-Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Future
The total amount of invoices that will be due in the future.
Current
The total amount of invoices that are not past due and not a future.
1-30
The total amount of invoices that are 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
The total amount of invoices that are 31 to 60 days past due.
61&Over
The total amount of invoices that are over 61 days past due.
Tot-Amount
The total value of open invoices for the customer.
