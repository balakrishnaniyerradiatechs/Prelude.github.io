Customer Maintenance
To set statement options for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Select F6-A/R > F5-Add Info to open the Additional A/R Information screen.
4
For Print Statement, determine the type of statements to print for this customer:
•
Y - Print open item statements. The customer’s history is not updated until you run the Month-End Customer A/R aging save (General Ledger > Processes > End of Month Closing). Refer to the General Ledger documentation for more information.
•
B - Print balance forward statements. A balance forward statement includes past due balances. When you print the statements, history is updated and the customer’s balance moves to the previous fiscal period.
•
N - Do not print statements for this customer.
5
For Statement Document Option, determine how statements are transmitted to the customer:
•
B - Mail and fax.
•
E - Email only.
•
M - Mail and email.
•
N - Mail only.
•
Y - Fax only.
6
For Print Credits/Stmts, determine if the customer statement includes credit memos:
•
Y - Include credit memos.
•
N - Do not include credit memos.
7
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the customer statement information.
