Accounts Receivable
Credit Holds/Releases
Releasing Credit Holds
When you place a sales order on credit hold by selecting Suspend, Prelude defaults to Suspend (instead of Pick Ticket) when the sales order is released from credit hold. When you place a sales order on credit hold by selecting Pick Ticket, Prelude defaults to Pick Ticket when the sales order is released from credit hold.
Tracking Administrative Releases
Enter Y for Admin Mgr. Release in File Update Parameters to track administrative hold releases. To view admin hold releases, go to Financial > Accounts Receivable > Reports > Admin Manager Release.
Entering Dollar Amounts
In Accounts Receivable Parameter, enter the maximum order amount above which all cash and COD orders go n credit hold regardless of the customer's credit standing. The reason code in Credit Release Inquiry is COD.
Invoices
Viewing Open Invoices
Enter A in the Invoice List Option field to view open invoices for the customer or S to select invoices numbers manually using the F3 key.
Searching by Invoice Number
Press F3 to search by invoice number in A/R Credit Card Payment Entry.
Setting Default Sort Options
You can define the default sort option for invoices in Cash Receipts Entry in Default Parameters Maintenance. Choose one of the following:
•
I - Invoice Date
•
D - Due Date (default).
Generating Credit Collections Letters
For Invoices to Include, select:
•
C (Complete Invoice List) to include $0.00 invoices and credit memos on the letter.
•
S (Select Invoices) to select the invoices that appear on the collection letter.
Editing Credit Comments
Use the Edit Credit Comments flag in A/R Parameters to allow users to edit credit comments. Enter Y to allow users to edit credit comments. Enter the date and time for credit comments in Notes Entry. If you enter N, Review Date does not appear because the standard Notes Entry screen appears. This new feature affects the following programs:
•
Systems > System Parameter > Maintenance > Option Parameters > A/R Parameters.
•
Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Customer Inquiry.
•
Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Customer Invoice Inquiry.
•
Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > A/R & Customer Credit Inquiry.
•
Financial > Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance.
Entering CVV Numbers
You can enter the 3 digit number on the back of the credit card (the CVV number) in the CVV Number field if your credit card company uses it to prevent fraud.
Excel Output Option
You can choose Excel to generate reports in the Accounts Receivable module.
Inquiries
Customer Invoice Inquiry
The Display Sequence field in the Customer Invoice Inquiry was renamed Sort. The Invoice Status field was renamed Filters. This field defaults to O (Open Invoices). Select Y to display the filters screen and limit the records that display on the inquiry screen.
A/R & Credit Comments Inquiry
Use the F6-Summary key to choose a report format and view a summary of the A/R & Credit Comments Inquiry statistics. Prelude now excludes cash terms sales from updated sales when you run an A/R & Credit Comments Inquiry. Prelude includes the total merchandise amount with sales tax on the inquiry. The following fields have changed in the A/R & Credit Comments Inquiry:
•
Master Account; renamed as Master.
•
Phone Number; renamed as Contact.
•
Fax.
•
DaysSO - new field that shows Days Sales Outstanding based on the Month-End Customer A/R Aging Save in the General Ledger module.
A/R Transaction Audit Inquiry
Use F5-Review for the CRE code to display invoices paid in Cash Receipts Entry.
