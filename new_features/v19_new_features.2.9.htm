Purchasing
Changes to the Buyer Queue Inquiry
Modified Queues
•
Product Alerts:
The Product Alert Parameters screen (Purchasing Controls > Purchasing Parameters > F7-Product Alerts) allows you to set a day of the month to evaluate a product’s usage and the percentage of the product’s Forecast that causes an Early Warning alert. If a product’s usage by that day exceeds the defined percent of its Forecast, an Early Warning product alert appears in the Buyer Queue.
The product’s Forecast is set up in Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance.
•
Recommended Purchase Orders:
The new sF5-Prv Group and sF6-Next Group function keys allow you to view the previous or the next group of records. The n umber lines per block is determined by Records to Block in Company Maintenance.
The new Cube field displays the cubic volume of a product from Product Vendor Maintenance. This can help determine the amount of merchandise required to fill a bin or a shipping container.
•
Purchase Order Expedite:
When you fax, print, or e-mail the expedite report to the vendor, the text This is not a new Purchase Order is at the top so that the vendor does not mistake it for a new order.
New Queue
•
Vendor Request for Quotes:
The Vendor Request for Quotes queue is updated when a Vendor RFQ release is generated to request pricing from a vendor. When the vendor replies with a price quote, you can use this work queue to enter the response. Once all vendors for a product respond with a quote, you can choose to send the responses to the Employee Queue Inquiry for the person that requested the quote.
Vendors
Calculate Vendor Rebates
The new Rebate Maintenance screen in Vendor Maintenance > F5 Action Bar > Rebates allows you to enter information to calculate vendor rebates. The Vendor Rebate Report provides detail or summary list of reconciled vendor invoices used to calculate rebates for a period of time.
Assigning a Buyer for Direct Ships/Emergencies by Vendor Line
The new DS/Emg Buyer field in Vendor Line Maintenance allows you to assign a buyer for direct ship and emergency purchase orders created for a vendor line.
Fax Overdue Purchase Orders to Vendor
A third option for the Fax Expedite Flag (A - Always) in Vendor Maintenance > F5 allows you to fax the vendor each night for overdue purchase orders. The purchase order lines appear on the expedite report.
Vendor Statistics Includes Returns
The Vendor Statistics screen in Vendor Maintenance > F8 now has a Returns column to show the total amount of vendor claims for each month.
Website Address Added to Vendor Maintenance
You can now enter a Website Address in Vendor Maintenance and Inquiry. The address is for reference only and does not access the web page.
New Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen
The Quote VRFQ Preselect flag in Additional Option Parameters determines if the Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen appears when you request a vendor quote from in Quote Order Entry. The screen defaults in any vendors assigned to the product in Product Vendor Maintenance and you must indicate which vendors to include on the RFQ.
Using Transfer Buyers in Vendor and Vendor Line Maintenance
The new Transfer Buyer fields allows you to assign a vendor responsible for transfers for this vendor’s products. Nightly Processing uses the following hierarchy to determine which buyer to send recommended transfers to:
•
Vendor Line Maintenance > Transfer Buyer (new field)
•
Vendor Maintenance > Transfer Buyer (new field)
•
Warehouse Maintenance > Buyer
•
Vendor Line Maintenance > Buyer
•
Vendor Maintenance > Buyer
To load the transfer buyer fields with the updated buyers, choose Processes > ADS Utilities > Purchasing Utilities > Load Transfer Buyers.
To separate recommended transfers by buyer set Rec. Transfer by Buyer in Inventory Parameters to Y.
Purchase Orders
Freight Tracking on Direct Ship Purchase Orders
The new Direct Ship Freight Tracking Screen in Purchase Order Entry allows you to enter a freight tracking number on direct ship purchase orders. You can also access the UPS Tracking website from this screen to check the status of the shipment. To access the screen go to:
•
Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > P/O Func > Enter Freight Tracking for Direct Ships
•
From Purchase Order Inquiry, Purchase Order History Inquiry, or Purchase Order Log Inquiry, choose F5-Review > Action Bar > Add Info > Enter Freight Tracking for Direct Ships
Reprinting Purchase Orders
When you re-print a purchase order, the warning message now warns you that regardless of how you transmitted the P/O the first time, it will automatically be sent to the printer. If you want to email or fax the order, you must go back into Purchase Order Entry to select the method of transmission.
Change Warehouse by Line Item for Multi-branch PO
If a purchase order is multi-branch, you can now change the warehouse at the line item level in Purchase Order Entry and Stock Receipts Entry.
Purchase Order Log Inquiry
Two new codes were added to the Purchase order Log Inquiry:
•
SRM - An alternate buyer was selected in the Stock Receipts Discrepancy Inquiry.
•
INS - A product was sent to the Quality Control Inspection Queue.
Reports
New Fill Rate Reports
The new fill rate reports allow you to view the percentage of products that were picked the first time the pick ticket printed:
•
Fill Rate Report By Product
•
Fill Rate Report By Product Line
•
Fill Rate Report By Vendor
•
Fill Rate By Customer
Warehouse Turns Report (modified)
New options are available for the Stocking Status selection criteria:
•
S - Stock products only.
•
N - Non-stock products only.
•
B - Both stock and non-stock products.
•
C - Consignment products only.
•
A - All products.
