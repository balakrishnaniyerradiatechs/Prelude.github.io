Service Agreement Entry
A new Floor Plan Customer field has recently been added to the Service Agreement Entry screen. This new field was created to allow a third party customer to be established for Service Agreements. If a customer has a Floor Plan Customer currently established in Customer Maintenance, you can select a Floor Plan Customer for third party billing for a service agreement. If in fact a Floor Plan Customer was established and the Service Agreement Entry screen contains a Floor Plan Customer, the message This Order Will Be A Floor Plan Order? will be displayed in Service Order Entry. If Yes is selected, the third party customer will be billed for the service order once it is invoiced. If No is selected, the original customer number that pertains to the service agreement will then be billed.
