Inventory
Product Maintenance
Part Option Added as a Product Type
The Product Type field in Product Maintenance has a new P option to designate the product as a part. You can use this option to group products by product type for custom reports.
Creating Base Master and Base Child Products
You can now designate a product as a base master (BM) or a base child (BC) in the Special Operations field in Product Maintenance. When the master product or any of the child products are entered on a sales order or quote, the product tagging screen includes the master product and all the child products. When you update or change the master product, all child products are updated also. The stocking unit of measure should be the same for both the master and child products. Nightly Processing does not recommend a purchase order for the child products.
Using Product Tagging for Kit Components
The new T option is available for S/O Modify in Kit Maintenance. When you enter the kit parent on a sales order, the Product Tagging screen opens and prompts for the kit components (Kit Type must be set to R to use this option).
Exploding Kit Parents on Purchase Orders
The new P/O field in Kit Maintenance determines if a kit parent explodes into its component parts when you enter it on a purchase order. If necessary, you can recalculate the cost so that the total cost of the kit line items equals the cost of the kit parent.
Adding Related Products
The new Type field (Product Maintenance > F6 > Related Products) determines how to add a related product when the original product is entered on a sales order. The related product(s) can be required or optional. You can also set a multiplier and a price to determine the quantity and price of the related product. The original (default) behavior is also available.
Adding Product Notes Globally
The Doc field (Product Maintenance> F6-Xref/Notes > Product Notes) has a new G (global text) option to indicate that the product notes should appear in all of the entry screens.
Printing Warehouse Product Notes
The Warehouse Product Notes screen in Product Warehouse Maintenance allows you to print the product notes on the Recommended Stock Transfer Report and Vendor Request for Quotes.
Viewing SRT in Product Tagging Entry
The Avail column in Product Tagging Entry now displays the SRT (Standard Repair Time) for service products with an SRT in PRoduct Maintenance. If the service product does not have an SRT, the Avail Column shows SERVICE.
Extending Warranty for Service Items
Set Service Item Flag in Product Maintenance to W to indicate that the product has an extended warranty. When you enter the product on a sales order either directly or as an accessory, the Warranty Period Unit in Product Maintenance > F5 > F5 calculates the warranty expiration date. You can view customer warranty information in:
•
Customer/Product and Product/Customer Warranty Inquiry
•
Serial Number History Inquiry > WtyExp/Mat & WtyExp /Lab
Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance
The new Purchase Parameters Additional Information screen (Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance > F7-Add Info) allows you to enter additional factors that may affect recommended purchase quantities. The Ann. Usage Override, W/O Mfg Production, Single Customer, and Distribution Ctr fields were moved from the Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance screen. The T/R Reserve Formula, Percentage and Quantity fields are new features.
Reserving Inventory for Sales Orders
The new T/R Reserve Formula field in Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance > F7 allows you to reserve inventory for sales orders for a warehouse so that it is not available for transfers. You can either use a sales forecast formula and percent to reserve inventory or a set quantity. Nightly Processing and End of Month Calendar Close check this information when recommending a purchase quantity.
Checking Gross Profit for Admin Holds
The new Bypass Low Level GP flag (Product Maintenance > F5) determines if a product is included in the manager low level gross profit % check for Admin Order and Admin Quote Hold.
Updating Multiple Catalog Page Numbers
The new Catalog Purge Utility Maintenance allows you to enter or edit the catalog page number for multiple products at once. The page number you enter here updates each product in Product Maintenance.
Disallowing Product Merges
You can no longer merge the following types of products in Universal Product Merge Entry.
•
A superceded and its superceding product.
•
A lot product and a non-lot product.
•
A serialized product and a non-serialized product.
•
Products with different units of measure.
Adding Options to Serialized Equipment
Two new programs, Equipment Option Code Maintenance and Equipment Option Group Maintenance, allow you to create and group option codes to define any optional features that may apply to serialized equipment.
You can assign an option group (e.g., lawn mower, tractor) to a piece of equipment in Product Maintenance > F5 so that when you purchase the product, you can add options (e.g., heater, alarm) in the product’s option group. These options are added in Purchase Order Entry as comments. When you receive the piece of equipment in Stock Receipts Entry, you can confirm the options that are accurate. Option code information appears in:
•
Serial Number History Inquiry > Action Bar > Opts
•
Serial Number History Inquiry by Serial Number > Action Bar > Opts
•
Open Purchase Order Detail Report
Lot Screen Available for Seed Control Items
When you enter S (seed control) for the Lot Flag (Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info > Lot Flag = S), the Lot Information screen now appears to allow you to enter lot information. Previously, only the Y setting opened the lot information screen. When the product is entered in inquiries or processes, you can now view or enter lot information.
Prompt for Lot Information in Sales Order Entry
To prompt for lot information on sales orders, you can set the new Lic Plate-SOE Lot Prompt flag in the Lot Information screen (Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info > Lot Number =Y) to Y.
Using New Parker Reporting Marketing Codes
A new Parker Marketing Maintenance program allows you to set up marketing divisions to assign to products. The following Parker maintenance programs now include the marketing codes.
•
Parker Division Maintenance
•
Parker Division/Marketing Maintenance
•
Parker Division/Marketing/Product Maintenance
•
Product Maintenance and Product Line Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Parker Information
Gasket/Hose Subsystem
The new Gasket/Hose Subsystem (/M:Gasket) has maintenance options for gasket, hose, and related product requirements (/PM > Special Operations = G, GC, HK, or HC), a physical inventory menu, reports, and inquiries.
Product Cost
Adjusting Cost for Vendor Consigned Inventory
The new Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry program allows you to change the cost on products that are on consignment from a vendor. Once the inventory is sold and available for payment in A/P, you can no longer adjust the cost. You can view the adjustments in Daily Cost Adjustment Journal - Vendor Consigned and Monthly Cost Adjustment Journal - Vendor Consigned.
Tracking Vendor Cost
The new Vendor Cost Audit flag in File Update Parameters determines if changes in unit cost, replacement cost, and direct ship cost are tracked in the system.You can also purge this data in Purge Utility Menu > Inventory > Vendor Cost Audit.
Rounding Replacement Cost by Product Lines
The new Replacement Cost field in Product Line Maintenance (Round Prices = Y) allows you to enter the number of decimal places to round replacement cost.
Product Usage
Moving Product Usage
When you move product usage to another product, 12 months of usage are included. If you add a usage product number in the Related Products screen in Product Maintenance, the usage from the original product moves to the usage product. If you remove the usage product, the usage goes back to the original product. The new PUA code in the Usage Audit Inquiry indicates that usage was moved or added.
Recording Consignment Order Usage
The Over Shp Whse Cons Ord? flag in Order Processing Parameters determines if usage on 08 consignment sales orders updates the shipping warehouse or the billing warehouse.
New Out of Stock and Unusual Usage Exception Flags
The new Out of Stock Exception and Unusual Usage Exception flags in Purchasing Parameters Maintenance determine if Product Usage Maintenance and End of Month Calendar Close override purchasing parameters for products with unusual usage or out of stock exceptions.
Viewing Company Wide Usage
The new Company Wide Usage screen allows you to view product usage and override information for the entire company. You can access from the Action bar in Product Usage Maintenance and Product Usage Inquiry.
Stock Receipts
Allocating Linked Freight to Direct Ship Purchase Orders
The Allocate Linked Frt to S/O flag in Purchasing Parameters now also applies to direct ship purchase orders in Direct Ship Receipt Entry.
Substituting and Recosting Received Superseded Product
When you receive a superseded product by line item and that product is linked to a sales order, you may enter the number of the superseding product. A comment line is added to the linked sales order to reference the original product. You can also recost the product based on the superseding product. Choose Yes and Recost to adjusts the cost and extended total.
Excluding Unusual Lead Times From Lead Time Calculations
When a product is received with a lead time that exceeds the warehouse’s Lead Time Exception Days in Purchasing Parameters Maintenance, it is excluded from the Average Lead Time calculated for the product in Product Vendor Maintenance.
Entering Manufacturer Information for License Plate Products
When you receive a license plate product and the License Plate Information screen appears, you can press the new F5-Description key to enter the Manufacturer Name, Manufacturer Lot Number, and Manufacturer Date information, if applicable.
Changing Warehouse by Line for Multi-Branch PO
If a purchase order is multi-branch, you can now change the warehouse by line item in Purchase Order Entry and Stock Receipts Entry.
Transfers
Link Key added to Transfers and Work Orders
The new F6-Link key in Transfer Entry and F7-Link key in Work Order Entry allow you to link the product on the order to a backorder on another transfer, work order, or sales order.
Package Quantity Checked for Recommended Transfers
The new Transfer Information option in Product Warehouse Maintenance allows you to enter a transfer package quantity that Nightly Processing checks to recommend stock transfer quantities if Round To Pkg Qty in Inventory Parameters > F5 is Y or U. This field overrides the transfer package quantity in Product Maintenance.
Transfer Checked for Completion in TSSCE/RTSSCE
When shipping a transfer in Transfer Serial Shipping Container Entry, the transfer is checked for completion before you can continue into Transfer Shipments Entry. Previously, if you only shipped partial quantities and then suspended the shipment, the full quantity would ship. New messages warn you about the errors and you can return to Transfer Shipments Entry.
Using Transfer Buyers in Vendor and Vendor Line Maintenance
To recommend transfers by buyer, Rec. Transfer by Buyer field in Inventory Parameters > F5 must be set to Y. The new Transfer Buyer fields in Vendor Maintenance and Vendor Line Maintenance allow you to assign a vendor to transfers for this vendor’s products. Nightly Processing evaluates the Vendor Line Maintenance and Vendor Maintenance Transfer Buyer before the Buyer in Warehouse Maintenance, Vendor Line Maintenance, and Vendor Maintenance. To make the Buyer for the vendor also the Transfer Buyer, run the following new program: Processes > ADS Utilities > Purchasing Utilities > Load Transfer Buyers (new option)
Update Buyer Queue Inquiry for To or From Warehouse
The new Transfers To Recv Whse BQI flag in Inventory Parameters > F5 determines if Nightly Processing recommends transfers to the buyer for the To or the From warehouse.
Partially Allocate Freight on Transfer Receipts
Set Prorate Transfer Rate in Inventory Parameters > F5 to partially allocate freight to only the items received on a partially received transfer. The percent of freight is allocated according to the freight class in Product Maintenance.
Vendor Claims & Warranty Inventory
Additional Reference Number in Vendor Claims
The new Ref# field in the Vendor Claim Detail Information screen allows you to enter any additional reference information. This information can help find a vendor claim number when you are reconciling the claim.
Warranty Tag Bin Added for Reference
The new Warranty Tag Bin Maintenance screen (Inventory > Vendor Claims) allows you to assign a bin for a warranty tag product. You must use this new process to add inventory in this warranty bin. Any quantity added does not affect on-hand inventory; it only displays for reference in the warranty tag inquiry screens. To view bin information, choose F5 from Warranty Tag by Tag Inquiry, Warranty Tag by Vendor Inquiry, Warranty Tag by Product Inquiry, or Warranty Tag by Customer Inquiry.
Product Tagging Available
The Product Tagging screen is now available in Vendor Claim Entry as a means to enter products/quantities on a vendor claim.
Vendor Claim Update Processing Checks for Claim Numbers
If you left the Beginning Claim Number and the Ending Claim Number blank, a message appears to warn you that you may unintentionally update some claims. You can continue, or to go back and enter claim numbers.
Cycle Counting/Physical Inventory
Adding Bins to the First Level Cycle Count
The new Bin 1st Level Cycle Count Request Entry screen allows you to select a range of bins to add to the first level cycle count queue. These products are in addition to the products selected by Cycle Count Nightly Processing.
Automatically Updating Products in First Level Cycle Count
The new Cycle Count Auto Update field in Warehouse Maintenance determines if products with a zero on-hand are automatically updated as zero at the first level and not sent to the second level count queue. Previously, you had to enter the zero balance items for the first level cycle count.
Counts Remain if RF Gun Loses Connection
If an RF gun loses connection during the 2nd level cycle count entry, you can do a restart without losing the product counts already entered for your user ID. You still need to recount the product being counted when the gun lost connection.
Bin Required in Physical Tag Entry
The Physical Inventory Tag Entry screen now requires a bin number.
Include Zone in Pick Ticket Printing
The Zone Type field in Zone Maintenance indicates if the zone is pickable. The Generate Release By flag (Warehouse Maintenance > F5) must be Z (zone) to use this feature.
Inquiries
Core Credit Eligibility Inquiry (new)
Displays core credit information for customer invoices (Inventory > Inquiries > Core Inquiries).
Customer Invoice Core Inquiry (new)
Displays core charge invoices for a customer, including due dates and status (Inventory > Inquiries > Core Inquiries).
Price Tape Update Log (new)
Provides an audit trail of the price tapes that updated your product prices. For each price tape, you can view which files were updated (Inventory > Processes > Price Cost Update Procedure > Price Tape Update).
Spike Exception Inquiry (new)
Displays products with high and low spike exceptions (Inventory > Processes > Usage Exception Menu). Spike exceptions occur when usage is greater or less than the forecast.
Inventory Adjustment Inquiry (modified)
If a transfer is over-received, but inventory is not adjusted because it would bring the balance at the shipping warehouse below zero, comments display in the Inventory Adjustment Inquiry to explain why inventory was not adjusted.
Inventory Inquiry (modified)
•
New Vend Cons Onhand flag (F7-Add Info)
•
Product Bin Inquiry now accessible (F5-Review on Non-Nettable)
•
New LP/Max field displays the line point and maximum quantity
Products Receipts History (modified)
The new F8-PO Log key displays the Purchase Order Log Inquiry for the highlighted product.
Serial Number History Inquiry by Serial Number (modified)
The Book Value screen is now accessible from this inquiry. The book value for a serial number is updated from stock receipts, depreciation, cost adjustments, equipment attachments, or capital repairs.
Vendor Claim Log Inquiry (modified)
When you press F5-Review for the APP code, you can now view the Vendor Claim History Inquiry. Previously, the AP/PO Receiver History screen appeared.
Bin Location Inquiry (modified)
The bin cycle count history inquiry is now available in Bin Location Inquiry and Bin Location Maintenance > F5-Cycle Count History.
User Form Modifications
The Freight field was increased to 8 characters in:
•
Product Maintenance/Product Inquiry
•
Product Line Maintenance/Product Line Listing
•
Freight Class/Freight Class Listing
Reports
Daily Cost Adjustment Journal - Vendor Consigned (new)
Lists adjustments to vendor consigned cost for a day. You can adjust vendor consigned cost in Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry.
Import Cost Report (new)
Displays actual and received costs for imported merchandise.
Monthly Cost Adjustment Journal - Vendor Consigned (new)
Lists adjustments to vendor consigned cost for a month or fiscal period. You can adjust vendor consigned cost in Vendor Consigned Cost Adjustment Entry.
Product Not Counted Report (new)
New report on both the Inventory Tag Procedure menu and the Handheld Tag Procedure menu lists products with a saved on-hand quantity that were not counted in the physical inventory.
Bin Count Statistics Report (modified)
The Product Cycle Count Request Entry now updates month-to-date counts and rejects, in addition to the year-to-date counts.
Current Inventory Value Report - FIFO (modified)
The new Fiscal Period selection criteria allows you to enter the fiscal period for which you want to include the month-end inventory balance. The End of Month Closing process now saves FIFO layers of cost.
Cycle Count First and Second Level Reports
The source code now appears to indicate how a product was added to the first and second level queues:
•
BCCRE - Bin First Level Cycle Count Request Entry.
•
CCRE - Cycle Count Request Entry.
•
ECCI - Emergency Cycle Count Request Entry.
Serial Number Recall Notification Report (modified)
The selection criteria now includes a Sold? option, to let you determine if you want to the report to consist of sold or not sold serialized products. If you choose N (not sold) you can limit the report to a warehouse.
Current Consigned (Prod Type) Value Report
Sub totals by warehouse were added to the report.
