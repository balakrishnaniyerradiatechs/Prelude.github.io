Service Charge Processing
The F-5 Reprint option in Service Charge Invoice Printing reprints an exact copy of the original regardless of any changes (e.g., payments, manual charges). Previously, the reprinted service charge includes payments and changes.
Automatic Credit Card Processing
Automatic Credit Card Processing is a new menu that was added, to allow the payments of A/R balances to be paid by credit card. You must use IC Verify with this option. Once regular invoice processing was completed, the payment of A/R balances via the credit card process can then be activated. In combination with the setup of the options in Automatic Credit Card Processing, additional setup is required in Credit Bureau Maintenance and Customer Maintenance. If utilizing this routine to pay off installment invoices, only those invoices with a due date of less than or equal to the date entered will be paid. Any future installment invoices are paid on the due date.
Automatic Credit Card Processing Menu
•
Credit Card Verification Process - review the credit card process types and determine which customers and invoices qualify to be paid by credit card, based on the customer’s account.
•
CC A/R Batch Audit Rpt-Approved - displays the invoices that are eligible to be paid by credit card for each customer. These are the invoices for each customer that had been approved during the Credit Card Verification Process.
•
CC A/R Batch Audit Rpt-Disapproved - displays the invoices that had qualified to be paid by credit card, but were declined during the Credit Card Verification Process.
•
Credit Card A/R Batch Close - completes the batch and settlement to IC Verify.
•
Credit Card A/R Application - uses the fast cash routine to apply the appropriate credit cards to pay off the open invoices for each customer.
•
A/R Batch Close Reprint - allows the reprints of the batches and settlements made to IC Verify in the Credit Card A/R Batch Close Process.
•
Customers on A/R Hold Report - the customer code and description of the customers who have the CC Hold Flag set to H in Customer Maintenance.
