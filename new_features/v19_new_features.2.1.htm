V19 New Features
Accounts Payable
Invoicing
Changing A/P Terms
Enter the A/P terms in the A/P Terms Change Process to update for the open invoices for the vendor number entered. A/P Terms Change Process does not update the A/P Transaction Audit Inquiry. If you update the open invoices using an installment terms code, Prelude creates installment invoices for each invoice updated with new A/P terms.
Paying A/P Floor Plan Invoices
When you choose batch type F in the Payment Selection/Print screen, Prelude pays A/P floor plan invoices without generating a preprinted check. You can partially pay an invoice by using F6-Manual Select.
Entering Tax Amounts
If you enter a value in the Tax Amount field of Manual Invoice Entry, Prelude will automatically update the A/P Tax Amount in the general ledger.
Entering Equipment Attachments
When you attach equipment to rental items, Prelude updates the equipment status to E for Equipment Attachment. You cannot enter attached equipment (equipment with E status) in G/L accounts. This new feature affects the following programs:
•
Rental > Equipment Subsystem > Equipment Attachment Entry.
•
Accounts Payable > Manual Invoice Entry.
Splitting Receivers
You can split receivers in Invoice Reconciliation Entry by partial line item quantities for lot-controlled items.
Entering Recurring Vendor Invoices
The following fields were added to Recurring Vendor Invoice Entry:
•
Maximum Payment.
•
Maximum Period.
You can only enter a value in one of these fields, not both. Once the number of payments for the invoice was met or the fiscal period has past, Prelude will not select this invoice.
Selecting Dates for Consigned Inventory
You can specify a range of dates to limit the selected documents to appear in the Accounts Payable Consigned Inventory screen.
Selecting Invoices to Pay
You can enter an open balance to include or exclude from the auto selection. When you set the Additional Selection flag to Y or E, the A/P Check Add Selection screen was updated to include open balances greater than, less than, or equal to the criteria specified.
Reports
Report Output
You can choose Excel to generate reports in the Accounts Payable module.
Report Field Definitions
The standard field descriptions were updated to exclude Number. For example, Customer Number is now Customer, Phone Number is now Phone, and Fax Number is now Fax.
A/P Floor Planned Inventory Report
The following columns have changed in the A/P Floor Planned Inventory Report:
•
Model Number - added to print in the Serial Number/Model Number column.
•
Book Value - added to print in the Serial Number/Model Number column.
•
Receiver# - renamed from Rec#.
•
Invoice# - renamed from Inv#.
•
Seq# - moved to Receiver#/Seq#/Invoice# column.
•
Ext Amount - renamed from Extension.
•
Pd Amount - renamed from Amt Paid.
•
Inv Date - added.
•
Due Date - added.
•
Amt Due - added.
Inquiries
Accounts Payable Inquiry
Prelude updates vendor notes in the Accounts Payable Inquiry with the user ID and the time and date the note was entered. Vendor currency type was added to the Accounts Payable inquiry.
Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry & Released Inquiry
When there is a discrepancy, the receiver in question appears on the Accounts Payable Released Inquiry of the user who entered the Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry.
Vendor Invoice Inquiry
You can use the Filters screen in Vendor Invoice Inquiry to enter a fiscal period to limit the invoice that will display. If you enter a fiscal period and invoice dates, Prelude will only display invoices that meet both criteria.
The Display Sequence field was renamed Sort. The Invoice Status field was renamed Filters. This field defaults to O (Open Invoices). Select Y to display the filters screen and limit the records that display on the inquiry screen.
Monthly A/P History
Monthly A/P History displays the month-end information updated with of Month End Vendor A/P Aging process.
