Sales Order Entry
F5-Hdr Scr> F7-Tax Jur> Type
A new Type flag was added to the Sales Tax Entry screen in Sales Order Entry. This new flag works with the Resale Exemption flag (listed on the Maintenance portion of the New Features list) located in the Customer Product Number Maintenance screen. Action> Auto Reverse
•
Previously, the function of the Auto Reverse feature was referred to throughout the system as Void Sale. In addition to this name change, the functionality of this process has also been modified to include a calculation of restocking charge. Up to V16, the restocking charge percent established for the credit reason code entered pulled from the company level was not calculated for the Void Sale performed in Sales Order Entry, Credit Memo Entry or RGA Entry when reversing the original sales order. The Restocking charge was only considered when entering a credit memo or RGA against the original sales order in Credit Memo Entry or RGA Entry. This has since been changed for V17 to now calculate the restocking charge percent that is established for the credit reason code entered or from the company level. The auto reverse process will now calculate the restocking charge percent the same as if you entered a credit memo or RGA against the original sales order in Credit Memo Entry or RGA Entry.
•
In addition to the process name change of this feature, the functionality of the Auto Reverse process has also been modified. Previously, this process would not allow positive lines to be reversed. This has since been updated to permit the reversal of both positive as well as negative lines. The positive lines on the original sales order will be reversed as negative lines in Auto Reverse. The negative lines will be reversed to positive lines. The F3-Lookup on a invoice number field lists the invoices for the customer number (Positive totals-no credit memo lines, Positive Totals-some credit memo lines, Negative totals-all credit memo lines).
•
A new Retain Additional Charges prompt was added to the functionality of the Auto Reverse feature. This message appears and you can add charges such as outbound freight, handling, restocking charges that were calculated on the original sales order should be retained for reversal. Choose Yes to keep and reverse the additional charges in the Sales Order Completion screen. Select N to not retain the additional charges, therefore clearing out any added amounts in the completion screen.

Completion Screen
•
The recalculation of sales taxes was corrected for Versions 15 and higher. Previously if a line item on a sales order is flagged as nontaxable and the tax jurisdiction code is changed on the sales order completion screen, the taxable/non taxable amounts as well as the sales tax amounts were recalculating incorrectly. This issue has since been resolved and therefore all amounts are recalculated correctly if tax jurisdictions are changed on the sales order completion screen.

Release Confirmation Entry
The functionality of the Release Confirmation Entry screen was updated to work in concurrence with the new Shipping Conf. flag in Warehouse Maintenance as well as the additional flag settings that were added to the Email Ship Notice flag in Customer maintenance. V17 have now been enhanced with the ability to print, fax or e-mail shipping confirmations using the continuous printing process rather than the Shipping Confirmations Printing method. The Release Confirmation Entry screen now contains logic that will print, e-mail or fax the shipping confirmations based on the Email Ship Notice flag setting established for this customer, and If the Shipping Conf. flag is set to Y. The shipping confirmations will be printed, e-mailed or faxed at the time the Update option is selected when the release is processed. If the flag is set to N, users continue to can use the Shipping Confirmations Printing process to generate the shipping confirmations.
Invoice Preview & Printing
The functionality of the Invoice Printing process was modified to perform inter company postings for consignment orders with different selling and shipping locations. Previously, the G/L posting updated the inventory consigned account for the shipping location entered on the sales order. For V17, the G/L posting will update the inventory consigned account for the selling location entered on the sales order. The inter company Sales A/R and Inter company Sales A/P accounts must be established in the General Ledger Table Maintenance for the G/L postings to be updated correctly when the selling and shipping warehouses are different on the sales order.
