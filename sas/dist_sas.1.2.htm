Sales Forecasting
This document offers an overview of the numerous Sales Forecast processes which are beneficial in providing managers with the capability of generating reports as well as inquiries using combinations of data to better forecast sales. For the reports to function in Prelude, the following procedures must be established.
•
The required matrices must be set up in Sales Forecast Maintenance.
•
Run the B Update (Order Processing > Processes) at the end of each month.
•
The Forecast Work File Rebuild process must be run to update the sales information for the current month.
Path: Sales Analysis > Sales Forecast
Sales Forecast Maintenance (/SRM)
Matrices can be created for customer numbers, product lines, sales reps, as well as major groups for sales forecasting reporting purposes.
•
Matrix Type: The matrix type will determine what combination will be used to calculate prices and or rebates. Enter the corresponding number for the appropriate sales forecasting information to be entered.
-
1 - Customer Number/Product Line
-
2 - Sales Rep/Major Group
-
3 - Customer Number
-
4 - Customer/Major Group
-
5 - Sales Rep1
-
6 - Customer / Sales Rep
•
Sales Forecasting Matrix screen: The fields that are prompted will be based on the matrix type chosen. For example, for matrix type 1, you can enter a customer number and product line. Once the criteria in the Matrix screen was entered, the Sales Forecasting Entry screen will then be displayed.
•
Select F6 - Annual Sales to display the following screen:
•
Current Year Projected: Enter the projected annual sales for the current year in whole dollars.
•
Next Year Projected: Enter the projected annual sales for next year in whole dollars.
•
Current Year Potential: Enter the potential annual sales for the current year in whole dollars.
•
Next Year Potential: Enter the potential annual sales for next year in whole dollars.
•

•
F2 - Save: This F2-Save function will save this price matrix information in the system.
Forecast Work File Rebuild (/BUILD.FRCST.WORK)
This rebuild process will update the sales information for the current month from the Process Invoices B Update procedure located in the (Order Processing > Processes menu). This will be used along with the sales forecasting information entered in the matrices in Sales Forecast Maintenance. If a new matrix is added in Sales Forecast Maintenance, this rebuild must be run before the corresponding sales forecast report can be printed.
Reports
•
Forecast & G/P by Customer / Product Line (/FRCST.CUST.PL.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 1 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales and GP for a customer and product line.
•
Forecast by Customer / Product Line (/FRCST.CUST.PL.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 1 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a customer and product line.
•
Forecast & G/P by Sales Rep1 / Major Group (/FRCST.SREP.MG.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 2 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. This report includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales and GP for a sales rep 1 and major group.
•
Forecast by Sales Rep1 / Major Group (/FRCST.SREP.MG.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 2 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It will include current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a sales rep 1 and major group.
•
Forecast & G/P by Customer (/FRCST.CUST.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 3 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. This report will include current year, previous year, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a customer as well as gross profit.
•
Forecast by Customer (/FRCST.CUST.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 3 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a customer.
•
Forecast & G/P by Customer / Major Group (/FRCST.CUST.MG.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 4 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales and GP for a customer and major group.
•
Forecast by Customer / Major Group (/FRCST.CUST.MG.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 4 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a customer and major group.
•
Forecast & G/P by Sales Rep1 (/FRCST.SREP.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 5 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. This report includes current year, previous year, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a sales rep 1 as well as gross profit.
•
Forecast by Sales Rep1 (/FRCST.SREP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 5 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a sales rep.
•
Forecast & GP By Customer / Sales Rep (/FRCST.CUST.SREP.GP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 6 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales and GP for a customer and sales rep.
•
Forecast By Customer / Sales Rep (/FRCST.CUST.SREP.RPT) - This report uses the Sales Forecast Matrix 6 in Sales Forecast Maintenance. It includes current, previous, and next year projected vs. actual sales for a customer and sales rep.
•
Sales Forecast Inquiry (/SFI) - This inquiry displays sales forecasting data from Sales Forecast Maintenance.
