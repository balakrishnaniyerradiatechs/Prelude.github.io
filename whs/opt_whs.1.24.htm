Warehouse Maintenance
Path: System Parameter > Maintenance > Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > F7-RTS Maint > F5-Warehousing (/WM > F7 > F7)
The Warehousing screen in Warehouse Maintenance contains two flags which must be established to use Wrist RF Picking for sales orders.
•
S/O Radio Frequency Picking: Enter Y to use radio frequency picking for sales orders.
•
Update Pick Label File: This flag automatically updates to Y when the S/O Radio Frequency Picking flag is set to Y. This flag is required when using Wrist RF Picking for sales orders.
