Sales Order Picking/Shipping
Radio Frequency Sales Order Picking is designed to allow for a wide variety of warehouse layouts. The implementation depends on the physical layout of your warehouse and the methods used for picking material. This allows you to customize a method of picking lines for sales orders. Some users select orders from a pre-assigned list of sales orders, others select the next available order to pick. Some pick an entire order at one time, while other users pick orders by zone to gang pick a single sales order. Orders may be picked with RF units or with a pick ticket. Radio Frequency Release Confirmation Packing allows you to select a release for confirmation and packing, then have the system prompt for the items to be packed for that release.
