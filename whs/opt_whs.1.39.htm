Warehousing Inquiries
A variety of inquiries track of bin and product quantities.
Bin Onhand Inquiry (/BOHI, /RBOHI, /WRBOHI, /TRBOHI)
Menu Path: Warehousing >Inquiries > Bin Onhand Inquiry (also on Hendheld RF, Wrist Mount RF, and Truck Mount RF menus)
Lists products for a bin. Each of these includes onhand and available quantities.
Product Bin Inquiry (/PBI, /RPBI, /WRPCI, /TRPBI)
Menu Path: Warehousing >Inquiries > Product Bin Inquiry (also on Hendheld RF, Wrist Mount RF, and Truck Mount RF menus)
Finds bin locations for a product including onhand and available quantities.
Product/lot Availability Inquiry (/PLAI)
Menu Path: Warehousing >Inquiries > Product/Lot Availablity Inquiry
Displays all lot numbers for a product, including bin and onhand, committed and available quantities.
Lot Bin Inquiry (/LBI)
Menu Path: Warehousing >Inquiries > Lot Bin Inquiry
Displays bins for a product and lot, including expiration, vendor, and onhand, committed and available quantity.
Bin Movement Inquiry (/BMI)
Menu Path: Warehousing > Inquiries > Bin Movement Inquiry
Displays information on bin moves including the User Id, date, products, quantities, and to and from bins.
Pick Label/picker Inquiry (/PLPI)
Menu Path: Warehousing > Inquiries > Pick Label/Picker Inquiry
Displays pick label information for a pick label and picker Id. Includes release number, staging order release, product, shipping warehouse, pick date, bin, quantity and lot.
Pick Label History Inquiry (/PLHI)
Menu Path: Warehousing > Inquiries > Pick Label History Inquiry
Displays pick label history for a pick label and picker Id with release, staging order release, product, shipping warehouse, pick date, bin, quantity and lot .
Summary Bin Queue Inquiry (/SBQI)
Menu Path: Warehousing > Inquiries > Summary Bin Queue Inquiry
Lists the pending sales orders, work orders, bin moves, and 1st/2nd level cycle counts for RF.
