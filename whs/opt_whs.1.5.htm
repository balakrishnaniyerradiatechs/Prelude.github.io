Receiving Options (F8)
•
Allow new product in RF SRE: Enter Y to allow products on a P/O to be received even if the product record does not exist for the receiving warehouse. In versions 18 and higher, P/Os received are checked for P/O type 04 (multi branch).
•
Display Count of Open B/Os in RFSRE: Set to Y to prompt for open backorder quantities in RF Stock Receipts.
