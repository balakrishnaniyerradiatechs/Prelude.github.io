Wrist RF Order Picking
Path: Warehousing > Wrist RF Menu > Order Picking Ent (/WROPE)
Order picking is also available with Wrist RF devices. Once pick tickets were printed, this process performs Sales Order Picking and Serial Shipping Container Code Entry on your wrist device for a batch number.
Once the Wrist RF Order Picking process was performed, the picked sales orders are updated with the RFP (Radio Frequency Picking) and & SSC (Serial Shipping Container) codes in the Order Log Inquiry screen.
For a more detailed explanation of Wrist picking, see the Wrist Picking - Batch Processing document on the website under Warehousing.
