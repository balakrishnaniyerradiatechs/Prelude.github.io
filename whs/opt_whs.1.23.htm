Wrist Picking - Batch Processing
Wrist Picking Radio Frequency devices used with the Prelude Warehousing module combine multiple steps to simplify the process of sales order picking and shipping confirmation. Batch processing is used to prepare orders to be picked. The wrist picking RF devices then combine the picking process and serial ship container entry steps into one process.
This document outlines the steps necessary to implement the wrist RF order picking and confirmation process.
