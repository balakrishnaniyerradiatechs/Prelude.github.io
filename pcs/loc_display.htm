Letter of Credit Display screen
The letter of credit display screen allows you to view information on the letter of credit that references this purchase order. A letter of credit is a promise from the bank to honor the debt on a pending transaction.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > P/O Func > Letter of Credit Info
