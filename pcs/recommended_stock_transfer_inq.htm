Recommended Stock Transfers
The Recommended Stock Transfer work queue holds recommended stock transfers created by Daily/Nightly Processing. You can view and/or edit each recommended transfer if necessary and convert it into an open transfer.
Recommended Quantities
Refer to “Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance” in the Inventory documentation for information on how recommended quantities are calculated.
Option Parameters
Transfers to Rec Whse BQI (Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info), determines where the Buyer Queue Inquiry is updated for transfers:
•
Y - Update the Buyer Queue Inquiry for the To warehouse when a stock transfer is generated.
•
N - Update the Buyer Queue Inquiry for the From warehouse when a stock transfer is generated.
Converting and Releasing Recommended Stock Transfers
To list and convert the recommended stock transfers:
1
Select one of the following to access the Recommended T/R Inquiry screen:
•
Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiry. Place the cursor in front of Recommended Stock Transfers and press F5-Review.
•
Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiries > Recommended Stock Transfers. Enter the Buyer.
Recommended Stock Transfers created by Daily/Nightly Processing appear.
2
Highlight the transfer to convert and select one of the following:
•
F7- Auto Convert - Converts the recommended transfer directly into an open transfer without the option to view or edit it. Go to step 6.
•
F5-Edit - Allows you to view and/or edit the recommended transfer before converting it. Go to step 3.
3
If this is the first time you enter the recommended transfer, the Transfer Header screen opens first to allow you to enter or edit the header information. Press F2-Save to complete the header and open the Transfer Conversion Entry screen.
4
Edit the From warehouse, To warehouse, or the quantities on the transfer as necessary and press F2-Save. The following message appears:.
Do you Wish To Convert This Temporary Transfer Into A:
5
Choose from the following:
•
Transfer. Converts the recommended transfer into an open transfer. Go to step 6.
•
Return. Allows you to return to the transfer to make necessary changes.
•
No Conversion. Does not convert the transfer. It remains in the Buyer Queue.
6
Once you choose to convert the recommended transfer to an open transfer, the following message appears:
Do you wish to exclude backordered items during conversion?
7
Choose from the following:
•
No - Backorders are included on the transfers. The message “Backorders Exist, Supply codes set to Appropriate Default” displays.
•
Yes - Any backordered items are removed from the transfer.
The transfer is converted to an open transfer and removed from the Buyer Queue
8
After reviewing and/or converting the transfers, press F2-Finish to close the screen.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/ Function
Description
T/R#
The stock transfer number.
From
The number of the warehouse shipping the material.
Description
The description of the warehouse shipping the material.
To
The number of the warehouse receiving the material.
Description
The description of the warehouse receiving the material.
Volume
The volume of each item on the transfer if volume is stored in the product record.
Weight
The weight of each item on the transfer if weight is stored in the product record.
T/R Amount
The extended dollar amount of each item on the transfer.
F2-Finish
Closes the Recommended T/R inquiry screen.
F5-Edit
Opens the Transfer Conversion Entry screen and allows you to edit the recommended transfer before converting it into an open transfer.
F6-Enter T/R
Allows you to enter a transfer number for direct access to the transfer.
F6-Auto Convert
Automatically converts the recommended transfer into an open transfer without the option to edit it.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), T (top).
Action Bar
Refresh
Refreshes the screen.
Alt Buyer
Opens the Alternate Buyer Assignment Screen to allow you to send the product to another buyer’s queue for review.
Output
Allows you to print, fax, email or view the contents of the screen.
