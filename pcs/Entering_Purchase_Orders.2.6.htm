Entering Products
Once you complete the header screen (refer to Entering Purchase Order Header Information), you can enter products in the Purchase Order Entry screen.
To be able to enter stock items on purchase orders, Enter P/O For Stock must be set to Y for your user ID.in User Identification Maintenance > F6-Add Info.

For Product, choose one of the following:
•
The exact product number. The P/O Detail Information screen appears.

Enter the quantities and press F2-Save. The items appear directly on the purchase order. To change the quantity or cost or other information, highlight the line item and press ENTER to open the P/O Detail Information screen.
-
Highlight the desired product number and press F2-Save to open the P/O Detail Information screen.

Enter or verify the Qty-Opn in the P/O Detail Information screen.

For Accept, choose one of the following:
•
Y - If everything else in this screen is correct. The detail information screen closes and you can enter the next line item on the P/O.
•
N - If you need to change any of the remaining fields:
-
Del-Date: Change the delivery date if necessary. The default is from the Purchase Order Header if one exists. The delivery date information is used for the Low Level Expedite Report and the High Level Expedite Report.
Changing the Delivery Date
If you change the Del-Date, choose one of the following status codes:
•
C - Date originally confirmed by vendor.
•
E - Date confirmed by vendor after expediting.
•
O - Date overridden by operator.
If the Suppress Freight Days Prompt in Purchasing Parameters is set to N, you must enter the estimated Number Of Freight Days it takes for the merchandise to ship to your warehouse.
-
Promised: Enter the date the merchandise is scheduled to arrive. This information is used to calculate vendor statistics.
-
Gross, Disc% or Net-Cost: Change as necessary. Refer to Costing Purchase Orders for more information on costing methods and discount tables.
-
Src: The source where the cost was pulled from. Refer to Identifying Purchase Order Source Codes for a list of source codes.
-
Vendor Product#: If applicable, enter a vendor product number to cross reference to your product number and to print on the P/O.
-
Whse. Only accessible if the P/O Type is 04 (Multi-Branch).
-
F5-Add Info: Opens the P/O Additional Info screen to change information regarding Inspection, Thaw Dates, Vendor Contracts, and Vendor Quotes.
-
F2 - Save: Saves the detail information and brings you back to the Purchase Order Entry screen to continue adding items.
When all line items are entered, you can complete the purchase order. Refer to Completing a Purchase Order.
Backordered Items
P/O Auto B/O Cancel Var% in Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F7-Variances allows you to set a percent variance that determines if a backorder is automatically cancelled. No warning message displays. The Purchase Order Log Inquiry displays the ACB code (automatic cancellation of backorder). This flag does not affect purchase orders items that are linked to sales orders.
Product Tagging
Product Tagging in Inventory Parameters determines how you can look up and enter part numbers when searching by keyword:
•
N - Product tagging is not activated. You must enter 1 item at a time on the order, then enter the quantity in the detail information screen.
•
Y - Activates the Product Tagging Entry screen for entry programs to allow you to enter quantities for multiple products to go directly on the order.
•
A - The same as Y, except that the Product Tagging Entry screen also displays for inquiries, but in the inquiry mode. The inquiry mode displays the available and on-order, but you can only select one product.at at time.
•
I - The Product Tagging Entry screen displays in the inquiry mode for both entry programs and inquiries. The inquiry mode displays the available and on-order, but you can only select one product.at at time.
