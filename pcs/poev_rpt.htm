P/O Extended Evaluation Report
The P/O Extended Evaluation Report is the report version of the Vendor Line Evaluation screen that you can access when you convert a recommended purchase order to an open order from the Buyer Queue (refer to Recommended Purchase Orders). The report includes information on products in the vendor line that are not included on the recommended purchase order because they have not met the vendor line target requirements, but they may need to be purchased. You can review the items and add them to a purchase order.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > P/O Reports > P/O Extended Evaluation Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where the material is stocked.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include.
•
Vendor Line. The vendor lines to include.
•
Days To Order Point <. Include products that are this many days until the order point.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product that may need to be purchased.
Description
The product description.
P
Indicates if the product exists on a purchase order for the warehouse:
•
Y - The product exists on a purchase order.
•
N - The product does not exist on a purchase order.
Cost
The product cost.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Avail
The quantity available plus the quantity on-order.
LP/Min
The line point minimum. When the available quantity falls below the order point, the entire line is reviewed for a possible line buy. The re-order point depends on the type of product:
•
Min/Max products. When the available product falls to the minimum quantity or below, order back up to the max. In the case of replenishment consideration, the minimum is the re-order point.
•
Non min/max products. When the available product falls to the line point or below, order the replenishment quantity. In the case of replenishment consideration, the line point is the re-order point.
Frcst
The forecasted quantity for the current month.
Reccmd
The recommended order quantity calculated from Nightly Processing.
Days
The number of days to the line point/minimum.
(Net Avail - LP/Min) / Forecast
Extension
The extended cost of the recommended quantity.
