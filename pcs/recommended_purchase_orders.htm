Recommended Purchase Orders
The Recommended Purchase Order work queue holds recommended purchase orders created by Daily/Nightly Processing. You can review and edit each order before converting it into an open purchase order. If you have purchasing targets set up for vendor lines, you can review and compare the target quantities and add additional products to the order if necessary.
Recommended Quantities
Refer to “Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance” in the Inventory documentation for information on how recommended quantities are calculated.
Converting a Recommended Purchase Order
To list and convert recommended purchase orders:
1
Select one of the following methods to access the Recommended P/O Inquiry screen:
•
Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiry. Place the cursor in front of Recommended Purchase Orders and press F5-Review.
•
Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiries > Recommended Purchase Orders. Enter the Buyer.
The recommended purchase orders created by Daily/Nightly Processing appear.
2
Highlight the purchase order to convert and select one of the following:
•
F7- Auto Convert - Converts the recommended purchase order directly into an open purchase order without the option to view or edit it. Go to step 8.
•
F5-Edit - Allows you to view and/or edit the recommended purchase order before converting it. Go to step 3.
3
If this is the first time you enter the recommended purchase order, the Purchase Order Header screen opens first to allow you to enter or edit the header information (refer to Entering Purchase Order Header Information). Press F2-Save to complete the header and open the Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen.
Choose from the following:
•
To convert the recommended order directly into an open purchase order without changing it, go to step 7.
•
To edit quantities or price, press ENTER on an item to open the P/O Detail Information screen. Make any necessary changes to the quantity or price information and press F2-Save to save the changes. Once the recommended purchase order is correct, go to 7.
•
To investigate detail purchasing information on any item, highlight the item and select F6-Rec Rev to open the Recommended P/O Inquiry screen. Once the recommended purchase order is correct, go to 7.
•
To review linked sales orders, work orders, or transfers for a product, highlight the item and select F8-Link to display the orders. Once the recommended purchase order is correct, go to 7.
•
To review vendor line targets set up for a vendor line and to find additional products within a vendor line to add to the purchase order, highlight the product and select F7-Targets to open the Target Information screen. Continue with step 4.
4
The Target Information screen allows you to compare the total of the purchase order to the vendor line targets set up in Vendor Line Maintenance. Refer to the Vendor Line Target Parameters Screen.
Choose one the following methods to add or change quantities on the recommended purchase order:
•
F6-Proportion Increase - Proportionally increases all items on the purchase order (by package quantity) to meet the target amount. If the quantities are acceptable, continue with step 7.
•
F5-Evaluate Line - Opens the P/O Vendor Line Evaluation screen.
You can view products not included on the recommended purchase order because they do not meet target requirements for the vendor line set up in the Vendor Line Target Parameters Screen.

P/O Extended Evaluation Report

5
Enter a Quantity for each line item to add to the recommended purchase order.
6
Press F2-Save twice to return to the Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen
7
Once the products and quantities are correct and you are ready to convert the recommended order into an open purchase order, press F2-Save from the Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen. The following prompt appears:
Do You Wish To Convert This Temporary P/O into A.....
8
Choose one of the following:
•
Purchase Order: Automatically converts the recommended order into a purchase order and allows you to choose the method to notify the vendor (e.g., print, email, or fax).
•
Vendor RFQ: Automatically creates a vendor request for quote with the line items defined on the recommended P/O. See Entering a Vendor RFQ.
•
Return: Allows you to return to the recommended purchase order to make changes.
•
No Conversion: The purchase order is not converted and remains in your queue.
9
After all the purchase orders are reviewed and/or converted, press F2-Finish to close the screen.
Field & Function Key Descriptions (Recommended P/O Inquiry screen)
Field / Function
Description
P/O #
The temporary purchase order number generated by Nightly/Daily Processing.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist on the purchase order.
Type
 
Purchase order type. See Purchase Order Types.
Vendor Number Description
The vendor number and description.
Emg
 
Indicates if any product on the recommended purchase order has open backorders or is marked as an emergency:
•
Y - Emergency order.
•
B - Backordered item.
•
N - Not an emergency or a backorder.
Cube
The cubic volume of the products on the purchase order. This value comes from Product Vendor Maintenance and can help you to determine the quantity of merchandise that will fit in a shipping container.
Weight
The total weight of the purchase order.
Units
The number of units on this purchase order.
P/O Amount
The total dollar amount of this recommended purchase order.
Target
 
Indicates if the recommended purchase order meets the target amount set up for the vendor line:
•
Y - The order meets the vendor line target amount.
•
N - The order does not meet the vendor line target amount.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen.
F5-Edit
Accesses the Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen to allow you to review and edit the order before converting it into an open purchase order. You can also change the vendor number by selecting P/O Func > Change Vendor Number from the Action Bar.
F6-Enter P/O
Prompts for a purchase order number and allows you direct access to a purchase order if there are several pages of orders to scroll through.
F7-Auto Convert
Automatically converts a temporary purchase order into an open purchase order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), or T (top).
sF5-Prev Group
Allows you to view the previous page of purchase orders if more than one page of purchase orders are listed.
sF6-Next Group
Allows you to view the next page of purchase orders if more than one page of purchase orders are listed.
Action Bar
Action > Sort Options
Resorts the listed purchase orders by one of the following:
•
Vendor number.
•
Vendor description.
•
Purchase order type.
•
Dollar amount.
Alt Buyer
Opens the Alternate Buyer Assignment Screen to allow you to send the product to another buyer’s queue for review.
