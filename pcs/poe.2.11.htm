Merging With a Suspended Purchase Order
If your system is set up to track suspended purchase orders, you can merge a suspended purchase order with another purchase order for the same vendor. Suspended purchase order appear in the Buyer Queue Inquiry. Refer to Suspended Purchase Orders.
 
 
Tracking Suspended Purchase Orders
To track suspended purchase orders, you must set Maintain Suspended P/O’s in Purchasing Parameters to Y.
 
For Set P/O Date as Output Date, determine the date to use for the purchase order date:
•
Y - The purchase order date is the date the purchase order is printed, emailed, or faxed, rather than the date it was suspended.
•
N - The P/O date is not updated when processed. The date originally entered applies.
When you suspend a purchase order (see Completing a Purchase Order), you are prompted to track the order. If you choose Yes, you can access the order later to merge it with another purchase order.
If the purchase order is still suspended the next time you enter a purchase order to the same vendor, the following question appears when you complete the order.
Suspended Purchase Orders on File! Do You Wish to Continue Or Return Back Into Purchase Order for Merging?
1
Select Return to go back into the purchase order so that you can merge it with any existing suspended purchase orders.
 
 
You don’t have to wait to complete the order to merge. From the Purchase Order Entry screen you can place the cursor on the item to merge and go straight to step 2 while in the Purchase Order Entry screen. You can merge one or all items on the P/O.
2
From the Action Bar, select Prod Func > Merge w/Suspended P/O. The Merge With Suspended P/O screen appears and lists the suspended purchase orders for the warehouse and vendor. Direct ship purchase orders are excluded from the selection.
3
Press F5-Review to open the Purchase Order Display screen to review each purchase order.
4
Press F6-Merge to merge the selected purchase order with the one you are working on. The following question appears:
Merge Individual Products Or All Products?
5
Choose one of the following:
•
Individual: Merges the product that was highlighted when you selected Merge w/Suspended P/O. For example, if the current purchase order contains two line items and the cursor is in the second line of the purchase order, the second line on the purchase order merges with the suspended purchase order selected on this screen.
•
All: Merges all products on the current purchase order with the suspended purchase order.
•
Return: Goes back to the Purchase Order Entry screen.
As you merge each line item on the current purchase order into the suspended purchase order, the lines no longer appear on the current purchase order.
The quantities on the suspended purchase order are updated with the additional quantities.
To edit the suspended purchase order, you must go back into Purchase Order Entry to change and complete the order.
 
 
 
Buyer Queue
Suspended purchase orders are also tracked in the buyer’s queue for review. Refer to Suspended Purchase Orders.
 
 
Inquiries
The Purchase Order Log Inquiry displays the RMV (remove) code for the purchase order whose lines items were merged into the suspended order. The RMV code references the line item that was removed and the purchase order it was moved to.
The MOV (move) code displays for the suspended purchase order that had items merged into it. The MOV code references the line item that was added from the other purchase order.
The Purchase Order Inquiry displays an S in the S (suspended) column to indicate that the purchase order is suspended.
