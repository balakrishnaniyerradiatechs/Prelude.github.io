Recommended P/O Report
The Recommended Purchase Order report lists system-recommended purchase orders.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Nightly Processing Reports > Recommended Purchase Order Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Central Warehouse. The warehouse responsible for buying the material.
•
Vendor. The vendor(s) for which you want view recommended purchase orders.
•
Buyer. The buyer(s) for which you want view recommended purchase orders.
Column Descriptions
Column
Description
P/O#
The number of the recommended purchase order.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Vendor Product Number
The vendor’s number for the item (if any).
Whse
The warehouse that requires the item.
Qty-Ord
The recommended order quantity.
U/M
The unit of measure from the product record.
Gross-Cost
The gross cost.
Disc%
The discount percentage.
Net-Cost
The net cost of the item (Gross Cost - Disc%).
Extension
The extended dollar value for the recommended quantity (Net-Cost * Qty-Ord).
