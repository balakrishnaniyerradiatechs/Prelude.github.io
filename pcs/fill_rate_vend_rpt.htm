Fill Rate Report By Vendor
The Fill Rate By Vendor report displays the percentage of stock products for a vendor that were available when sales orders were first entered, not when invoiced. This report allows you to see how well a vendor is performing. You can choose to print the report for each product within a vendor or to print just the total fill rate for each vendor.
Products That Will Not Appear on the Fill Rate Reports
If the following conditions exist for a product/warehouse, it will not update the fill rate:
•
The stocking status is other than S (stock).
•
Both the min and max quantities are set to zero.
•
It has an order point override of zero.
•
The calculated order point is zero and it does not have an override (other than zero).
Fill Rate in File Update Parameters must be set to Y to capture fill rate information:
 
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Fill Rates at Order Entry Reports > Fill Rate By Vendor
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouses to include on the report.
•
Order Date Range. The beginning and ending order entry dates to include.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include on the report.
•
Product Line. The product lines to include on the report.
•
Major Group. The major product groups to include on the report. Major groups are groups of products or product lines that combine products for sales analysis.
•
Totals Only. Indicates whether you want to show the fill rate information for each product within a vendor or just the total fill rate for each vendor:
-
Y - Display the total fill rate information for each vendor.
-
N - Display the fill rate information for each product from each vendor.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The products within each vendor that were entered on sales orders within the selected date range.
Description
The product description.
OrdQty
The quantity of each product ordered within the selected date range.
PckQty
The quantity available to pick the first time the pick ticket printed.
Fill%
The fill rate (Pck Qty/Ord Qty).
#Lines
The total number of sales order lines entered for the product.
Filled
The number of lines filled the first time the pick tickets printed.
Fill%
The fill rate compared to the number of lines on the sales orders (Filled/#Lines).
If a line is 100% shipped then the fill rate for that line is 100%. But if the line is less than 100% shipped, then the fill rate for that line is zero.
