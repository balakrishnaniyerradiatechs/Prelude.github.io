Entering a Blanket Purchase Order
A blanket purchase order is a record of guaranteed pricing with a vendor for products for a period of time. Pricing for an item may be honored at one location, multiple locations, or all locations. You can source from an expired blanket purchase order or from an order that is completely released (the total quantity has been purchased). If the blanket purchase order is completely released and you source a purchase order from it, the Remaining Quantity becomes negative.
To enter a blanket purchase order:
1
Select Purchasing > Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem > Blanket Purchase Order Entry to access the Blanket Purchase Order Entry screen.
2
To enter a P/O Number, choose one of the following:
•
To start a new blanket purchase order press ENTER to assign a new blanket P/O number.
•
To edit an existing order, enter the blanket P/O number or press F3 to search for the P/O by vendor or vendor order number. The Blanket Purchase Order Entry screen opens so that you can add or edit items. Go to step 10.
3
Enter the Vendor Number.
Depending on your system settings, the Vendor Verification screen may appear. The address from Vendor Maintenance is displayed. If this is the correct address, enter Y in for Is This The correct Vendor?. If not, enter N and select another vendor.v
The Blanket P/O Header screen appears after you select a vendor.
4
Enter the Vendor Quote#, which is the vendor’s reference number for this quote or contract.
5
Verify the Ship-From Number. Choices are:
•
Enter a D to use the default address from Vendor Maintenance.
•
Enter an M to manually enter a ship-from address in the Ship-From Address screen.
•
Press F3 to search for a list of valid ship-from numbers for this vendor.
6
Enter the Beginning Date, which is the default beginning date for all of the items on the blanket P/O. Nightly Processing checks this date to determine when to recommend a purchase order for the material. If you change this date in the header when open line items exist on the order, a message prompts you to update all existing lines with the new date.
7
Enter the Expiration Date, which is the default ending date for all of the items on the blanket P/O. If you change this date in the header when open line items exist, a message prompts you to update all existing lines with the new date.
8
Verify or enter any other required information for this blanket P/O.
9
Once the header screen information is correct, press F2-Save to save the header information and to open the Blanket Purchase Order Entry screen.
10
For Product/Description, enter the first product to include on the blanket purchase order. The Blanket P/O Detail Information screen appears.
11
For Blk-Qty enter or verify the quantity that the vendor’s blanket P/O cost is valid for.
12
Enter the Net-Cost, which is the cost quoted by the vendor. This cost is used when a purchase order is sourced from this blanket P/O.
13
Enter the Beg-Date, which is the date the quote goes into effect. The default is the date entered in the header screen. Nightly Processing ignores a blanket P/O whose beginning date has not arrived when determining the price for a recommended P/O. Purchase Order Entry warns you about the date but does not prevent you from using the blanket P/O.
14
Enter the Exp-Date, which is the date the quote expires. Purchase Order Entry allows you to enter an expired blanket P/O but warns you that it is expired.
Rel-Qty is the quantity released to purchase orders. Once the line item is sourced on a purchase order, this field is updated to reflect the total quantity released.
Rem-Qty is the quantity of the current line item still available for sourcing in Purchase Order Entry. This field is updated each time a purchase order sources a quantity from the blanket purchase order.
15
If applicable, enter any Comments pertaining to the line item. These comments print on reports and display in the sourcing and inquiry screens.
16
In the Accept (A) field, enter one of the following:
•
Y - If everything else in this screen is correct.
•
N - If you need to change any of the fields.
17
Repeat steps 10-16 to add the next item to the blanket P/O.
18
Press F2-Save when finished entering items.
Refer to Completing a Purchase Order for information on faxing, e-mailing, or printing a purchase order.
