Purchase Order Entry screen
The Purchase Order Entry screen allows you to enter or edit purchase orders.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The purchase order number.
Vendor
The vendor’s name or number.
Product
The product number or description.
Description
The description of the product.
Whse
The warehouse number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for this line item.
R
A Q displays if the purchase order was generated from a quote using a Vendor RFQ.
Linkage
The document that the product is linked to (if applicable). The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Quantity
The quantity entered in the P/O Detail Information screen or the Product Tagging Entry screen.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on this purchase order.
Net Cost
The net cost of the item.
Cube
The total cubic volume of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Weight
The weight of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Units
The number of units of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Amount
The dollar amount of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
F2-Save
Finishes the purchase order and gives you the opportunity to print, fax, or email the order.
F4-Del
Allows you to delete the purchase order and prompts you to enter a Deleted P/O Reason Code. Refer to Deleting A Purchase Order.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen.
F6-Link
Opens the Purchase Order Linkage screen to manually link a nonstock item from a sales order, work order, or transfer to the purchase order. Refer to Manually Linking an Item.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), T (top).
Action Bar
Examine
Delivery and receipt information for the highlighted line.
Review
Opens the Purchase Order Review screen to allow you to review the extended cost and delivery information for each line item on the purchase order.
Prod Func
Opens the Product Functions Menu to provide access to the following:
•
Add Comment Lines. Opens the Comment Description Entry screen to allow you to add a comment line after the item. Refer to Entering Comments.
•
Line Item Split. Opens the PO Split Lines screen to allow you to split delivery dates for the line item. Refer to Splitting Line Items for Delivery Dates.
•
Linkage Deletion. Opens the Purchase Order Linkage Deletion screen to allow you to delete the link to a sales order, work order or transfer.
•
Customer Service Rep Message. Opens the Notes Entry screen to enter notes to the customer service representative.
•
Merge With Suspended P/O. Opens the Merge With Suspended P/O screen to merge the line item with a suspended purchase order. Refer to Merging With a Suspended Purchase Order.
P/O Func
Opens the P/O Functions menu to provide access to:
•
Resort Purchase Order. You can resort the P/O by: D - Product description. P - Product number. V - Vendor product number.
•
Recost Purchase Order. Allows you to recost the purchase order after all the items are entered, to take advantage of vendor discounts that apply based on the total quantity ordered. Refer to Re-costing Purchase Orders.
•
Letter of Credit Info. Opens the Letter of Credit Display screen.
•
Duplicate Purchase Order. Opens the P/O Duplication Entry screen to allow you to duplicate an entire open purchase order from the same vendor or a different vendor. This function is only available when no line items exist on the current purchase order. Refer to “Duplicating a Purchase Order”.
•
Duplicate Purchase Order History. Opens the P/O History Duplication Entry screen to allow you to duplicate a received purchase order from the same vendor or for a different vendor. This prevents you from having to re-enter a list of part numbers and prices.
•
Enter Estimated Freight. Opens the Freight Closeout screen so you can enter the estimated freight to proportionally add freight cost to all line items on the purchase order.
•
Enter Freight Tracking for Direct Ships. Opens the Freight Tracking screen so that you can enter the tracking number. You can also access the UPS Freight Tracking web page to track the order once it ships. This is only available for direct ship orders.
Notes
Opens the P/O Notes menu to provide access to the following.
•
Header Notes. Opens the Notes Display screen to allow you to enter or edit notes on the purchase order header.
•
Line Notes. Opens the Notes Display screen to enter or edit notes to the line item.
•
Header Attachments. Opens the Prelude Attach Viewer, which allows you to attach a document to the purchase order. You can attach any document/picture that you can access on your computer.
•
Line Attachments. Opens the Prelude Attach viewer to allow you to attach a document/picture regarding this line item. You must be on an existing line or in the P/O Detail Information screen to attach or view documents for a line item.
