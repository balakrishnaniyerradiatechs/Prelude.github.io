Blanket Purchase Order Entry screen
The Blanket Purchase Order Entry screen allows you to record guaranteed pricing from a vendor for products for a period of time. Refer to Entering a Blanket Purchase Order for more information.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem > Blanket Purchase Order Entry
