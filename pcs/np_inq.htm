Nightly Processing Inquiry
The Nightly Processing Inquiry lists Nightly Processing information for a product, including reasons why the item was not recommended for purchase. You can access the Inventory Inquiry and Purchasing Parameters Inquiry in this screen to further investigate the product.
Viewing Nightly Processing Information
To view Nightly Processing information for a product:
1
Select Purchasing > Inquiries > Nightly Processing Inquiries > Nightly Processing Inquiry to open the Nightly Processing Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product number or F3 to search.
3
Enter the Warehouse number.
Information about the product’s Nightly Processing status appears.
