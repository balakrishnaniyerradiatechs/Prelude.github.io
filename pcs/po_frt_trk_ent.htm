Freight Tracking screen
The Freight Tracking screen allows you to enter a UPS tracking number for direct ship purchase orders. You can also access the UPS tracking website to track an order through UPS after it ships.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > P/O Func > Enter Freight Tracking for Direct Ships
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ship Via
The method of shipping used for the direct ship purchase order.
Tracking Numbers
The freight tracking number for direct ship purchase orders.
F2-Save
Saves the tracking information.
F5-Access Tracking
Opens the UPS Tracking web page (if you have internet access) so that you can track the order through UPS.
