Transfer Conversion Entry screen
The Transfer Conversion Entry screen allows you to view/edit a recommended transfer from the Buyer Queue Inquiry before converting it into an open transfer. Nightly Processes creates the recommended transfer for the Buyer Queue.
Refer to Recommended Stock Transfers for information on converting a recommended transfer.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiries > Recommended Stock Transfers > F7-Auto Convert
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Transfer Number
The transfer number recommended by Nightly Processing.
From
The warehouse that will be shipping the material to another warehouse.
To
The warehouse that will be receiving the material.
Product Number
The products included in the recommended transfer.
Description
The description of the product.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for this line item.
Linkage Destination
The document that the product is linked to (if applicable). The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Quantity
The quantity to transfer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Cube
The total cubic volume of the items on the stock transfer.
Weight
The total weight of all the items on the stock transfer.
Cost
The total cost of the items on the stock transfer.
F2-Save
Saves the information on the screen and allows you to convert it into an open stock transfer.
F4-Del
Deletes the recommended stock transfer.
F5-Header
Opens the Stock Transfer Header screen to allow you to view or edit header information.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
