Purchase Order Review screen
The purchase order review screen displays extended cost and delivery information for each line item on the purchase order.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The purchase order number being reviewed.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Product Number
The product numbers on the purchase order.
Description
The descriptions of the product numbers.
Whse
The warehouse to receive the material.
Linkage
The document linked to the product (if applicable). The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Promised
The date the merchandise is scheduled for delivery at the warehouse. This date is used with the date the material is received to calculate vendor performance and ranking. Refer to Tracking Vendor Performance.
Delivery
The date the purchase order is scheduled for delivery.
Qty-Ord
The quantity of the product on the purchase order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity not yet received on the purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure as entered on the purchase order.
Gross
The gross dollar amount of the product before the discount.
Disc%
The discount entered on the purchase order, or from the vendor or vendor line.
Extension
The cost of the product multiplied by the quantity ordered, less the discount percent.
Vendor Product
The vendor’s part number.
Src
The price source code that identifies where the price came from. Refer to Identifying Purchase Order Source Codes.
OK
Closes the Purchase Order Review screen.
