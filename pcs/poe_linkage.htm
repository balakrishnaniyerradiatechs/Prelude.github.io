Purchase Order Linkage screen
You can use the Purchase Order Linkage screen to link a backordered item from a sales order, transfer, or work order to a purchase order. When the item is received into inventory, it automatically fills the backorder on the linked order. Refer to Manually Linking an Item.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > F6-Link
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The backordered item to link to the purchase order.
Document
The sales order, transfer, or work order numbers containing this item as an unlinked, backordered item.
Type
Identifies the type of order:
•
Order.
•
Transfer.
•
Work order.
Customer/
Warehouse
The customer number if the backordered item is on a sales order or work order. The branch number if the backordered item is on a transfer.
B/O Qty
The quantity of the backordered item.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item as it is entered on the sales order.
Req-Date
The date the backordered item is required by the customer.
Line Level
An asterisk (*) displays if the Req-Date displayed was overridden at the line item level.
F2-Link to P/O
Links the highlighted item to the purchase order.
F5-Review
Allows you to review the linked line item on the sales order, transfer, or work order.
