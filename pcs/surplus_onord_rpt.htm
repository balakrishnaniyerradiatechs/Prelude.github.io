Surplus Report with On Order Merchandise
The Surplus Report With On Order Merchandise displays products that contain surplus inventory and a quantity on order. You can view the purchase order, work order, or transfer containing the on-order quantity.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Surplus Reports > Surplus Report with On Order Merchandise
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g,. Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used. (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where products are on order that have a surplus quantity in stock.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product Line. The product line for listing surplus inventory.
•
Sort by Product Line/Vendor. Determines how the report is sorted:
-
P - Sorts by product line.
-
V - Sorts by vendor.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the products are on order.
Product Number
The product number that is on-order and has a surplus quantity.
Description
Description of the product number.
Surplus
Surplus quantity that exists in stock.
Cost
Unit cost of the product.
Ext-Surplus
Extended dollar amount of the surplus quantity in stock.
Cl
Product class.
Ty
Document type that contains the on order quantity:
•
P - Purchase order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Document#
Document number that contains the on-order quantity.
Exp-Date
Expected receipt date of the merchandise on the order.
Quantity
Quantity on order for this document.
2nd Row: Product Line
The product line number for surplus items if the report is run by product line. The vendor number if the report is run by vendor.
