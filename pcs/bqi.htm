Buyer Queue Inquiry
Each buyer is responsible for issues regarding the products to which they are assigned in Vendor Maintenance or Vendor Line Maintenance. The Buyer Queue Inquiry displays a list of work queues for a buyer and shows which have open issues. These work queues include recommended purchase orders to convert, backorders to expedite, discrepancies to resolve, purchase orders to release, and emergencies to handle.
You can access each work queue from the Buyer Queue Inquiry or as an individual inquiry.
Using the Buyer Queue Inquiry
To view the Buyer Queue Inquiry:
1
Select Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiry to access the Buyer Queue Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Buyer or press F3 to search. The number of open items in each work queue for this buyer appears under Open.
3
To view or edit open activities, place the curser in front of the desired Source and press F5-Review to open the appropriate work queue. Refer to the Buyer Queue Source descriptions for more information on each work queue.
Buyer Queue Source Descriptions
Source
Description
Emergency/Direct Line Items
Unlinked emergency or direct line items that need immediate attention. This queue is updated immediately after the sales order is entered.
Recommended Purchase Orders
Recommended purchase orders created by Daily/Nightly Processing. You can edit and convert the recommended orders into open purchase orders.
Suspended Purchase Orders
Suspended purchase orders generated in Purchase Order Entry. You can edit and release the orders or merge the orders with other open purchase orders for the same vendor.
Purchase Order Expedite
Items that meet the expedite date and were not faxed to the vendor. Updated by Nightly Processing.
Recommended Work Orders
Recommended work orders created by Daily/Nightly Processing. You can edit and convert the orders into open work orders.
Recommended Stock Transfers
Recommended stock transfers created by Daily/Nightly Processing. You can edit and convert the orders into open transfers.
Vendor Claims
Vendor claims created in Vendor Claim Entry, DOE convert, or rules set up in Vendor Maintenance. You can review and edit the claims.
Purchase Requisitions
Purchase requisitions entered in Purchase Requisition Entry. You can edit and convert the orders into open purchase orders.
Vendor Request for Quotes
Vendor RFQ releases generated in Vendor RFQ Entry. You can enter the quote responses from the vendor.
Product Alerts
Products whose usage falls above or below the Minimum or Maximum levels set for the product by the percentage in the Spike Exception Min/Max settings in Purchase Parameters Maintenance.
Purchase Hold Release
Purchase orders on hold. You can edit and release the orders.
Purchase Parameters Skipped
Products skipped during the End of Month Calendar Close and whose Purchase Parameters were not re-calculated.
Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry
A/P invoices manually placed on hold because of discrepancies in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry.
Stock Receipts Discrepancy
Receivers placed on hold for discrepancies in Stock Receipts Entry. You must release these before the Vendor can be paid.
EDI Freight Invoice
Freight invoices converted though EDI. This is the first level in the EDI freight approval process. Once approved, the invoice goes to the second level manager to approve and set up for payment.
Linked P/O Changes
Linked sales orders that were changed in Sales Order Entry and may require a change to the purchase order.
