Scheduling Nightly Processing
This process works the same as Nightly Processing (refer to Running Nightly Processing), except that it uses the general report format to allow you to schedule the procedure to reoccur daily. To change the selection of reports to print, refer to Defining Nightly Purchasing Reports to Print.
To schedule Nightly Processing:
1
Select Purchasing > Processes > Nightly Processing - Scheduled to access the Nightly Purchasing Process screen.
2
For Weekend Execution, choose from the following:
•
Y - If it is scheduled for the last business day of the week, typically a Friday night. The Pending Override Cancellations Report is printed and the frozen control flags are removed for products with an expired thaw. See Pending Override Cancellation Report.
•
N - If it is not scheduled on the last business day of the week.
3
For Daytime Execution, choose from the following:
•
Y - Nightly Processing will run during the day. Committed inventory is not cleared and rebuilt.
•
N - Nightly Processing will not run during the day. All files are evaluated and on-order and commitment information is rebuilt. This is the recommended method.
4
For Run Daily Business Update?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Nightly Processing updates Daily Business Statistics.
•
N - Nightly Processing does not build daily business statistics. You must run the Daily Business Statistics Build separately.
5
Enter the Warehouse number.
6
Press F2-Save.
7
Choose Schedule to display the Scheduler Job Parameters screen.
8
Enter the appropriate information to schedule the process and press F2-Save.
