Allocating Inbound Freight to Average Cost
You can estimate inbound freight and allocate it to the cost of each item on a purchase order as it is received. If the actual freight on the vendor’s invoice is different than the estimated freight, you can adjust it in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry to compensate for the difference.
System flags in Company Maintenance allow you to allocate freight to the average cost of inventory items when transfers and purchase orders are received. For information on estimating and allocating inbound freight to average cost, refer to the following sections:
•
Estimating Average Cost for Freight Charges.
•
Applying Estimated Freight to P/Os and Receivers.
