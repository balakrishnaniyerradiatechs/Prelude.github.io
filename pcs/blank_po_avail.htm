Blanket P/O Availability screen
The Blanket P/O Availability screen allows you to source line items on a purchase order from a blanket purchase order to receive special pricing quoted by the vendor. Refer to the Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem for more information.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > Blanket Purchase Orders Exists?
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Blk Po #
The blanket P/O number that contains the item you are entering on the purchase order. Highlight the P/O number you want to source from and press ENTER.
Vendor
The vendor who quoted the blanket P/O.
Exp Date
The expiration date of the blanket P/O. If you source from an expired blanket P/O, a warning message appears. You can continue with the expired blanket P/O or return to the Blanket P/O Availability screen to select another blanket P/O.
Beg Date
The beginning date of the blanket P/O.
Blk Qty
The quantity the blanket P/O price is quoted for.
Blk Cost
The cost the vendor quoted for the blanket P/O.
Rem Qty
The quantity remaining on the blanket P/O. This amount is reduced every time a purchase order sources from the blanket P/O.
Quantity
The quantity to allocate to the purchase order. A warning displays if the quantity is greater than the quantity remaining on the blanket P/O, but you are not prevented from entering the quantity.
F2-Save
Closes the Blanket P/O Availability screen.
