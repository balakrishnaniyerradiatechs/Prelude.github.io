Entering Line Item Thaw Dates
When you freeze controls for a product, its purchasing parameters are not re-evaluated with end-of-month processing. The thaw date is the date the productâ€™s purchasing parameters are no longer frozen.
If you enter a thaw date for a product number on a purchase order, the quantity is removed from the on-order quantity of this item and excluded from the Nightly Processing recommended purchase quantity calculations. Once the thaw date is reached, the line item quantity is added back into the product on-order quantity and included in the Nightly Processing recommended purchase quantity calculations. Refer to the Frozen Control Thaw Date Report.
Example: If you have a blanket P/O for a quantity of an item that is to ship over the course of a year, you may want to exclude future quantities from the on-order quantity so that the purchasing formulas recommend the stock that is needed now.
To enter a thaw date for an item on a purchase order:
1
When you enter a line item (refer to Entering Products), from the P/O Detail Information screen press F5-Add Info to access the P/O Additional Info screen.
2
Enter the Thaw Date for the item.
3
Press F2-Save to save the thaw date information for this item.
Note: A thaw date is not allowed on type 09 Future purchase orders.
When all line items are entered, you can complete the purchase order. Refer to Completing a Purchase Order.
