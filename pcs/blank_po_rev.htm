Blanket Purchase Order Review screen
The Blanket Purchase Order Review screen allows you to view the original quantity, released quantity, and the cost of each item on the blanket P/O.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem > Blanket Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The blanket P/O number.
Vendor
The number and description of the vendor associated with the blanket P/O.
Ln#
References the line number on the blanket P/O.
Product/ Description/ Comments
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: Comments associated with the line on the blanket P/O.
Whse
The warehouse for the blanket P/O.
Blk-Qty
The original quantity of the item on the blanket P/O.
Rel-Qty
The quantity of the item released to purchase orders.
Rem-Qty
The quantity remaining on the blanket P/O, not released to purchase orders.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item from the blanket P/O.
Status
The status of the product:
•
S - Stock.
•
N - Nonstock.
•
C - Consigned.
Cost/Ext.
Line 1: The unit cost of the item.
Line 2: The extended cost of the original quantity of the item on the blanket P/O.
