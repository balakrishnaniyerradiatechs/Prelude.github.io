Freight
If you want to include inbound freight cost to the cost of inventory, you can maintain freight rates to calculate estimated freight when receiving stock. When you enter the actual freight to reconcile the vendor invoice, it overrides the estimated cost to apply to the cost of inventory.
You can maintain estimated freights at the product level, the vendor line level, or the product line level.
