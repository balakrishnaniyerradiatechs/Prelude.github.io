Fill Rate Summary Report by Buyer/Planner
The Fill Rate Summary Report by Buyer Planner shows the percent of stock items and sales dollars that were available when sales orders were first entered, not when invoiced. This report allows you to see how well a buyer is maintaining inventory levels.
Products That Will Not Appear on the Fill Rate Reports
If the following conditions exist for a product/warehouse, it will not update the fill rate:
•
The stocking status is other than S (stock).
•
Both the min and max quantities are set to zero.
•
It has an order point override of zero.
•
The calculated order point is zero and it does not have an override (other than zero).
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Fill Rates at Order Entry Reports > Fill Rate Summary Report by Buyer/Planner
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Buyer. Typically the buyer assigned to the primary vendor. The buyer number can be overridden in Purchase Order Entry.
•
Entry Date Range. The beginning and ending order entry dates to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Byr
The buyer code
Description
The description of the buyer.
Ent-Date
The date the sales order was entered.
Ln Rev
The total number of lines reviewed to determine fill rate (stock items only).
Ln Fill
The number of lines that were filled.
Fill Rate
The number of lines on the order that are 100% pickable, divided by the number of lines on the order. Does not include items added to the order after it was originally entered.
Sales Rev
The total amount of sales dollars reviewed to determine fill rate.
Sales Fill
The total sales dollar amount of the lines that were filled.
Fill Rate
The percent of sales dollars that were able to fill orders.
