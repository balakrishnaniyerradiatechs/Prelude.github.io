Vendor Product Types Screen
The Vendor Product Types screen allows you to enter vendor product type codes to use as keywords when you search for a vendor. Refer to Creating and Assigning Vendor Product Types for information on creating product types.
The product types are a summary of the types of products the vendor sells. This can help you locate another source to acquire a type of product when your normal source cannot fulfill your current delivery requirement.
