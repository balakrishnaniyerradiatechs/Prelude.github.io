Estimating Average Cost for Freight Charges
Since actual freight charges may not be known when stock is received, you can estimate the freight amount by product line to calculate average cost. The allocation method set in Product Line Maintenance applies to the freight rate (refer to Entering Freight Rates).
To determine the method to calculate estimated freight:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line to access the Product Line Maintenance screen.
2
Enter a Product Line.
3
Select one of the following for Freight Allocation:
•
$ - Allocate freight charges based on dollars (dollar amount of receipt).
•
# - Allocate freight charges based on pounds (weight set in Product Vendor Maintenance).
•
U - Allocate freight charges based on units (units from P/O).
•
V - Allocate freight based on volume (cubic volume set in Product Maintenance).
•
Leave blank to not allocate freight.
4
Press F2-Save to save the freight allocation information.
