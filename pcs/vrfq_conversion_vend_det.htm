Vendor Detail Information screen
The Vendor Detail Information screen allows you to select a vendor from a list of vendors who responded to an RFQ for a product.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Vendor RFQ Subsystem > Vendor RFQ Conversion > F6-Vendor Detail
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Qty
The quantity of the quoted item.
U/M
The unit of measure of the quoted item.
Weight
The weight of the item.
Vendor Number
The vendor number.
Description
The vendor description.
Cost
The cost quoted by the vendor.
T
Cost type.
Freight%
The freight rate percent calculated from the Vendor RFQ header Freight rate.
Freight Amount
The freight cost quoted by the vendor.
Landed Cost
The cost of the item including duty and freight.
Duty
The duty dollar amount calculated by the Tariff Rate.
Tariff Rate
The rate to calculate Duty.
Price/Lbs
The price per pound of the product including freight and duty.
Select
Enter a Y to select this vendor for purchasing the item.
F2-Save
Saves the vendor purchase information and returns to the Vendor RFQ Conversion screen to allow you to purchase additional items.
F5-Notes Inq
Allows you to view any notes entered regarding this RFQ.
