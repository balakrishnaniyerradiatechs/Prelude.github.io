Duplicating a Purchase Order
You can duplicate a purchase order for the same vendor or for a different vendor. Duplicating an order prevents you from re-entering a list of part numbers and costs on a new order. You can also choose whether to use the same cost as the original purchase order or to recost the new purchase order with the current system costs.
Depending on whether the purchase order is open or received, use one of the following options to duplicate a purchase order:
•
Duplicating Open Purchase Orders.
•
Duplicating Received Purchase Orders.
