Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen
The Purchase Order Conversion Entry screen allows you to review, edit, and release a purchase order recommended by Nightly Processing. Refer to Recommended Purchase Orders for information on converting a recommended purchase order to an open purchase order.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiry > Recommended Purchase Orders > F5-Review > F5-Edit
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
P/O Number
The purchase order number.
Vendor Number
The vendor’s number and description.
Product Number
The product number recommended by Nightly processing. To edit or review, highlight the desired product number and press ENTER to open the P/O Detail Information screen.
Description
The description of the product.
Whse
The warehouse number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for this line item.
R
A Q displays if the purchase order was generated from a quote using the Vendor RFQ process.
Linkage
The document that the product is linked to (if applicable). The prefix indicates the document type:
•
S - Sales order.
•
T - Transfer.
•
W - Work order.
B
An asterisk (*) indicates that backorders exist.
S
An S indicates that a surplus quantity of this product exists in another warehouse.
Quantity
The recommended quantity.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Net Cost
The net cost of the item.
Cube
The cubic volume of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Weight
The weight of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Units
The number of units of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Amount
The total dollar amount of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
F2-Save
Saves the purchase order conversion information and gives you the opportunity to convert the recommended purchase order into an open purchase order.
F4-Del
Deletes the recommended purchase order.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen to review or make any necessary changes.
F6-Rec Rev
Opens the Recommended P/O Inquiry screen to display recommended purchase order information generated by Nightly Processing. If you use the Forecasting Module and the product is flagged with the DRP purchase method, the Distribution Requirements Evaluation Inquiry screen displays.
F7-Target
Opens the Target Information screen to allow you to compare the total of this purchase order to the targets established in Vendor Line Maintenance.
F8-Link
If the product is linked to a sales order, brings you to the linked line item on the order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), T (top).
Action Bar
Examine
Displays delivery and receipt information for the highlighted line.
Review
Opens the Purchase Order Review screen to review the extended cost and delivery information for each line item on the purchase order.
Prod Func
Opens the Product Functions Menu. You can choose one of the following functions for the line item:
•
Add Comment Lines. Opens the Comment Description Entry screen to add a comment line after the item. Refer to “Entering Comments”.
•
Merge With Suspended P/O. Opens the Merge With Suspended P/O screen to merge the line item with a suspended purchase order. Refer to “Merging With a Suspended Purchase Order”.
P/O Func
Opens the P/O Functions menu. You can execute the following functions for the purchase order:
•
Resort Purchase Order. To resort the P/O by: D - Product description. P - Product number.V - Vendor product number.
•
Recost Purchase Order. Allows you to recost the purchase order after all the items are entered, to take advantage of vendor discounts that apply based on the total quantity ordered. Refer to Re-costing Purchase Orders.
•
Change Vendor Number. Opens the New Vendor Number screen to allow you to enter a new vendor number for this purchase order.
Notes
Opens the P/O Notes menu:
•
Header Notes. Opens the Notes Display screen to enter or edit notes on the purchase order header.
•
Line Notes. Opens the Notes Display screen to enter or edit notes to the line item.
