Blanket P/O Detail Information screen
The Blanket P/O Detail Information screen allows you to enter valid dates and costs for each item on a blanket P/O. Refer to Entering a Blanket Purchase Order for more information.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem > Blanket Purchase Order Entry > Product Number
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number on the blanket purchase order.
Blk-Qty
The quantity of the item that the vendor’s price is valid for.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as quoted by the vendor for this blanket P/O.
Accept
 
Determines if the information is correct:
•
Y - The detail information is correct.
•
N - The detail information is not correct. You can change the detail information.
Net-Cost
The cost per item as quoted by the vendor. This cost is used when a purchase order is sourced from this blanket P/O.
S
Status of the item:
•
B - Bill of material parent.
•
C - Consignment.
•
K - Kit parent.
•
N - Non-stock.
•
S - Stock
•
X - Obsolete.
•
Null - No warehouse record exists.
Beg-Date
The beginning date the quoted price for this item goes into effect. Nightly Processing ignores a blanket P/O whose beginning date has not arrived when determining the price for a recommended P/O. Purchase Order Entry warns you about the date but does not prevent you from using the blanket P/O.
Exp-Date
The date the quote expires. Purchase Order Entry allows you to enter an expired blanket P/O but warns you that it is expired.
Rel-Qty
The quantity released to purchase orders. Once the line item is sourced on a purchase order, this field is updated to reflect the total quantity released.
Rem-Qty
The quantity of the current line item still available for sourcing in Purchase Order Entry. This field is updated each time a purchase order sources a quantity from the blanket purchase order.
Comments
Any comments pertaining to the line item to print on reports and display in the sourcing and inquiry screens.
F2-Save
Saves the detail information and closes the screen.
