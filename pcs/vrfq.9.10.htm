Creating a Vendor RFQ From a Sales Quote
To generate a vendor RFQ directly from Quote Order Entry:
1
From the Quotation Order Entry screen, as you enter each item in Product #/Description, the Quote Entry Detail screen appears.
2
From the Action Bar, select Entries > Request for Quote Entry. The Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen appears to display all the vendors assigned to the product in Product Vendor Maintenance.
 
The Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen only appears if Quote VRFQ Preselect? is set to Y in Additional Option Parameters > F5-Addit Info. If not using this screen, got to step 3.
3
Enter Y under Incl? for each vendor that you want to include in the quote request.
4
Press F2-Save to open the Request for Quotation Entry screen. This screen lists the vendors that you chose to include on the quote in step 2.
5
If you want to change any quantities or other information, highlight the Vendor# to edit and press ENTER. The RFQ Detail Information screen appears.
6
Change the Quantity or other information if necessary.
7
Press F2-Save to close the RFQ Detail Information screen.
8
Once the vendors and quantities in the Request for Quotation Entry screen are correct, press F2-Save. The following question appears:
Do You Wish To Print this RFQ?
9
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - Prints the RFQ.
•
No - Do not print the RFQ.
10
Press F2-Save after everything in the Quote Entry Detail screen is correct.
11
Repeat steps 1-9 for each item to add to the quote.
12
When all of the items on the quote are entered, press F2-Save. The following question appears:
Do You Wish to ...
13
Select Suspend to put the quote on hold until you receive a response from the vendor. The following question appears:
Do you Wish To Convert This Quote RFQ To A Vendor RFQ?
 
 
You must set Online Quote RFQ Conversion to Vendor RFQ in Additional Option Parameters to Y to be able to be able to go directly into the Vendor RFQ.
14
Select Yes. The assigned Vendor RFQ number displays.
15
Press ENTER and choose one of the listed methods to transmit the RFQ to the vendor. Go to step 3 on Generating a Vendor Release for options on sending the quote to the vendor.
Once you choose the method of transmittal, the following question appears:
Do You Wish To Continue With Vendor RFQ Entry?
16
Choose from the following:
•
No - Brings you back to Quote Order Entry to enter another quote. You can access and edit the vendor RFQ later in Vendor RFQ Entry. See Entering a Vendor RFQ.
•
Yes - Brings you into the vendor RFQ that you have just created to add vendors and/or quantities and to generate a release to the vendor. Refer to ”Generating a Vendor Release” on page 7-6.
When the vendor responds with a price quote, you can convert the RFQ directly into a purchase order. See the procedures for Converting a Vendor RFQ to a Purchase Order.
