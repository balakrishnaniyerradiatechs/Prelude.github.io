Open RFQ Release Inquiry
The Open RFQ Release inquiry displays vendor RFQs that were released to a vendor but not converted into a purchase order.
Viewing Open RFQs for a Vendor
To view released but not converted RFQs:
1
Select Purchasing > Vendor RFQ Subsystem > Vendor RFQ Inquiries > Open RFQ Release Inquiry.
2
Enter a Vend# or press F3 to search.
The open vendor RFQs that were released for this vendor appear.
