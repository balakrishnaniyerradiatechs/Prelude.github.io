Lead Time Trends Report
The Lead Time Trends report displays lead time trend information for a receipt date, vendor number, and product number. This information can help to analyze vendor performance. Refer to Tracking Vendor Performance.
The report excludes lead times for products if:
•
The Unusual Delivery flag is set to Y on the P/O Header.
•
The Emergency P/O flag is set to Y on the P/O Header.
•
The Purchase Order Type is 03 and the lead time is less than 0.
•
The Lead Time is greater than the Cutoff Days for Lead Times in Purchasing Parameters.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Lead Time Trend Report.
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Receipt Date. The receipt cutoff date. All stock received on or after this date.
•
Vendor. The vendor corresponding to the product number.
•
Product. The product number for which you wish to display lead time trend information.
•
Date Sort. The sorting method for the report:
-
A - Ascending P/O receipt date.
-
D - Descending P/O receipt date.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The description of the product.
Line 3: The 2nd line of description of the product.
Whse
Warehouse assigned to receive the material.
P/O #
Purchase order and release number for the received item.
P/O Date
The date the purchase order was entered.
Rec-Date
The date the item was received into the warehouse.
LeadTime
The lead time calculated from Purchasing Parameters Maintenance.
Qty-Ord
Quantity of the item originally ordered on this purchase order.
Qty-Rec
Quantity of the item received on this purchase order.
Cost
Replacement cost of the product.
