Purchase Requisition Entry screen
The Purchase Requisition Entry screen allows you to enter a purchase requisition to submit to the buyer assigned to the vendor. The requisition immediately appears in the assigned buyer’s work queue so that he/she can determine whether or not to convert the requisition into a purchase order. Refer to Purchase Requisitions.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Processes > Purchase Requisition Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Req Number
The purchase requisition number.
Vendor
The vendor’s name or number.
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Whse
The warehouse number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for this line item.
Quantity
The quantity entered in the P/O Detail Information screen or the Product Tagging Entry screen.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product on this purchase requisition.
Net Cost
The net cost of the item.
Weight
The weight of the accumulative lines on the purchase order.
Units
The number of units of the accumulative lines on the purchase requisition.
Amount
The dollar amount of the accumulative lines on the purchase requisition.
F2-Save
Saves the information in the purchase requisition entry screen and allows you to convert the requisition into a purchase order.
F4-Del
Allows you to delete the purchase requisition.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Requisition Header screen.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are order number, page number, line number, B (bottom), T (top).
Action Bar
Examine
Displays delivery and receipt information for the highlighted line.
Review
Opens the Purchase Order Review screen to allow you to review the extended cost and delivery information for each line item on the purchase requisition.
Notes
Opens the Notes Display screen to enter or edit notes on the purchase requisition.
