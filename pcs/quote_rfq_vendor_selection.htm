Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen
The Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen lists the vendors assigned to a product in Product Vendor Maintenance. You can choose the vendors to include on the vendor RFQ by entering a Y under Incl.
The Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen only appears if Quote VRFQ Preselect? is set to Y in Additional Option Parameters > F5-Addit Info.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Quotation Entry > Quote Entry Detail Screen > Action Bar > Entries > Request for Quote Entry
