Setting the Warehouse Sequence for Displays
Before generating temporary purchase orders, Nightly Processing checks the purchasing sequence to determine the order in which warehouses and surplus inventories are displayed and checked.
To set the purchasing sequence for warehouses:
1
Select Inventory Controls > Warehouse Sequences Maintenance to access the Warehouse Sequence Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Whse then select F5-Purchasing Seq to open the Purchasing Warehouse Sequence Maintenance screen.
3
Enter the Whse numbers in the sequence you want for the Nightly Processing lists. Only the warehouses on this list are used. All others with a surplus are not included in the recommended purchase orders and transfers. If you do not enter anything here, the sequences from the Warehouse Sequence Maintenance screen take effect.
4
Press F2-Save twice.
