Entering Products
After you enter the header information, the Vendor Request For Quote screen appears to allow you to enter or edit part numbers.
1
For Product Number, enter one of the following:
•
The exact product number to bring up the VRFQ Detail Information screen.
•
Part of the product number or description and press ENTER to search for the part number. The Product Tagging Lookup screen appears. Highlight the desired product number and press F2-Save to open the VRFQ Detail Information screen.
2
In Qty-Opn, enter the quantity that you want the vendor to quote.
3
For A (Accept), choose one of the following:
•
Y - If everything else in this screen is correct. This brings you back to the Vendor Request for Quote screen to continue adding items.
•
N - If you want to change the Req-Date or the Qty-Opn. Press F2-Save to go back to the Vendor Request for Quote screen to continue adding items.
4
Press F5-Add’l Qty to open the Add’l Qty screen to obtain prices for an additional quantity of the product. After you enter any additional quantities, press F2-Save twice to get back to the Vendor Request for Quote screen. The additional quantities appear as new line items on the RFQ.
5
Press F6-Addit Info to open the VRFQ Additional Information screen. The vendors that you entered on the quote are listed. You can add or delete vendors. Press F2-Save twice to go back to the Vendor Request for Quote screen.
 
6
After you finish entering products, press F2-Save to generate a vendor release. See Generating a Vendor Release.
