Freight Invoice Detail Inquiry screen
The Freight Invoice Detail Inquiry screen displays shipping information for a freight invoice.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Inquiries > Buyer Queue Inquiry > EDI Freight Invoice > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Document Id
Freight document number.
Shipper Vendor
The name and address of the vendor shipping the material.
Consignee
The name and address of the customer receiving the merchandise.
BOL/Ship Id
The bill of lading shipper identification number.
Del-Date
The date the customer received the material.
Tot Lbs
The total weight of the shipment.
Total Chg
The total amount charged by the vendor.
Total Pcs
The total number of pieces shipped.
Pieces
The number of pieces in the pallet/box.
Lading Description
Description of what shipped from the bill of lading.
Weight
The individual weight of each item shipped.
Frt Rate
The rate to calculate the estimated freight amount.
Amount
The dollar amount of the calculated freight.
FrtCd
The freight code supplied by the carrier.
F2-Finish
Closes the Freight Invoice Detail screen.
