Vendor Performance Inquiry
The Vendor Performance Inquiry screen displays performance statistics and current merchandise levels for a vendor.
Viewing Vendor Performance
To view vendor performance statistics:
1
Choose Purchasing > Inquiries > Vendor Performance Inquiry.
2
Enter the Vendor or press F3 to search.
3
Enter the Warehouse.
Vendor performance statistics appear.
Field Descriptions
Field
Description
This Yr
Total Shipments
The total number of shipments received from the vendor year to date.
Late Shipments
The number of shipments received late from the vendor year to date. A shipment is considered late if it is received after the Promised Date on the Purchase Order Header screen.
Late Percentage
The percentage of shipments received late from the vendor this year.
Late Performance
A ranking of the vendor compared to other vendors with late shipments. All vendors with the same number of late shipments are ranked equally by the Rebuild Procedure. Refer to Rebuilding Vendor Performance Statistics.
Total Late Ranking
The total number of vendors assigned rankings in Late Performance.
Icpl Shipments
Incomplete shipments from the vendor this year.
Icpl Percentage
The percentage of incomplete shipments from the vendor this year.
Icpl Performance
The ranking for a vendor compared to other vendors with incomplete shipments. All vendors with the same number of incomplete shipments are ranked equally.
Total Icpl Ranking
The total number of vendors assigned rankings in the Icpl Performance field.
Last Yr
Total Shipments
The total number of shipments received from the vendor last year.
Late Shipments
The number of shipments received late from the vendor last year.
Late Percentage
The percentage of shipments received late from the vendor last year.
Icpl Shipments
Incomplete shipments from the vendor last year.
Icpl Percentage
The percentage of incomplete shipments from the vendor last year.
Items Stocked
Class 1 through Class 12
The number of items stocked for the vendor, broken down by classification. Refer to “Classifying Products” in the Inventory Module.
Dead Stock
The number of products classified as dead stock (sales are less than dead stock value in Purchasing Controls > Purchasing Parameters > F6-Class Settings).
No Class
The number of products that do not have enough history to qualify for a classification.
Nonstock
The number of nonstock products from this vendor.
Dollars Invested
Class 1 through Class 12
The dollar amount of the inventory invested for the vendor, broken down by classification.
Dead Stock
The dollar amount of inventory classified as dead stock (sales are less than dead stock value in Purchasing Controls > Purchasing Parameters > F6-Class Settings).
No Class
The dollar amount of the products that do not have enough history to qualify for a classification.
Nonstock
The dollar amount of nonstock items for the vendor.
Totals As Of
The number of items and total dollars invested as of today’s date.
OK
Clears the statistics information for the vendor.
To print vendor performance statistics for a warehouse or vendor, select Purchasing > Listings > Vendor Listings.> Performance Listing.
