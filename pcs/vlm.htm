Vendor Line Maintenance
If a vendor has defined buying requirements for different categories of products, you can use vendor lines to assign different buying frequencies and target quantities for each group of products. These could be targets for receiving, freight paid, or buying restrictions related to weight, dollars, units, or cubic dimensions. The settings are evaluated by Daily/Nightly Processing to recommend purchase order quantities.
The following screens are available in Vendor Line Maintenance:
•
Vendor Line Maintenance Screen.
•
Vendor Line Target Parameters Screen.
•
Vendor Line Targets - Additional Information Screen.
•
Vendor Line Statistics Screen.
The information in the Vendor Line Inquiry screen (Purchasing > Inquiries > Maintenance Inquiries > Vendor Line Inquiry) is the same as the information in Vendor Line Maintenance.
To view information established in Vendor Line Maintenance, select Purchasing > Listings > Vendor Line Listings and choose one of the following:
•
All Information.
•
Target Parameters.
•
Purchase History.
•
Vendor Line/Buyer.
