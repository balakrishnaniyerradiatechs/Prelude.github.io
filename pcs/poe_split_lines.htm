PO Split Lines screen
The PO Split Lines screen allows you to divide a line item quantity to accommodate two or more delivery dates. Refer to Splitting Line Items for Delivery Dates.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Purchase Order Entry > Action Bar > Prod Func > Line Item Split
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quantity
The quantity to ship on each of the subsequent delivery dates. (The Del Date in the P/O Detail Information screen is the delivery date for the remaining quantity to ship.)
Del Date
The delivery date for the above Quantity. You are prompted to choose from the following:
•
Confirmed: If the delivery date is the date originally confirmed by the vendor. This selection places a C by Del-Date.
•
Expedited: If the delivery date is the date confirmed by vendor after expediting. This selection places an E by Del-Date.
•
Overridden: If you are overriding the original date. This selection places an O by Del-Date.
•
Return To Date: Allows you to change the date entered.
Number of Freight Days
The estimated number of days it takes for the merchandise to ship to your warehouse for the ship via type selected.
F2-Save
Saves the delivery information.
