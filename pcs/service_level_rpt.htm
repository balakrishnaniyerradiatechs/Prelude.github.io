Service Level Measurement Report
The Service Level Measurement report displays the percent of stock line items, by product line, that shipped. This information can help you to determine the percent of customer requirements that were met. Historical statistics are also included to indicate if the percentage is improving.
The Service Level Measurement Report does not include the following types of line items:
•
Non-stock
•
Asterisk (*) products.
•
Direct ship products.
•
Supply coded products.
•
Indirect, linked products.
•
Tag & Hold orders.
•
Manufacturer rep orders.
•
Consigned products.
•
Kit products.
•
Rental products.
•
Backorders completely shipped for lines that were previously shipped complete.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Service Level Measurement
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the products.
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Product Line. The product line for listing surplus inventory.
•
Break By Warehouse?. Determines how you want the report to print:
-
Y - Separate by warehouse and include totals by warehouse.
-
N - Combine all warehouses and include a grand total.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Prod Line
The product line for grouping products.
Description
The description of the product line.
Incomplete
S/O Lines
The number of lines on sales orders containing this product line that were shipped incomplete.
Total S/O Lines
The total number of lines on sales orders for this product line.
Service Level
The percent of line items shipped for line items ordered for this product line for the current month.
Historical Months
The service level for this product line for the previous 6 months, prior to the current month.
