VRFQ Response Detail Information screen
The VRFQ Response Detail Information screen allows you to enter cost, quantity, and delivery information received from the vendor for the RFQ.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Vendor RFQ Subsystem > Vendor RFQ Response Entry > ENTER on a Product Number
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number from the Vendor RFQ Response Entry screen.
Qty-Opn
The quantity quoted by the vendor.
U/M
The unit of measure quoted by the vendor.
A
Accept. Indicates if the information is correct:
•
Y - Everything is correct.
•
N - Allows you to change any of the fields.
Cost
The cost quoted by the vendor.
Del-Date
The delivery date quoted by the vendor.
T
Cost type. For information only.
Tariff
The tariff code assigned to the product (from Tariff Commodity Code Maintenance).
Rate
The tariff rate used to calculate Duty.
Duty
The duty dollar amount calculated by the Tariff and Rate.
Frt-Cost
The freight cost calculated from the Frt-Rate on the Vendor RFQ header.
Landed Cost
The cost of the item including duty and freight.
Extension
The extended cost of the Qty-Opn multiplied by the Cost.
F2-Save
Saves the response detail information.
F5-Notes
Opens the Notes screen to enter any information this vendor response.
