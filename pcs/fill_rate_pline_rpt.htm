Fill Rate by Product Line Report
The Fill Rate by Product Line report displays, by product line, the percentage of stock products that were available when sales orders were first entered, not when invoiced. You can choose to print the report for each product within a product line or to only print the totals for each product.
Products That Will Not Appear on the Fill Rate Reports
If the following conditions exist for a product/warehouse, it will not update the fill rate:
•
The stocking status is other than S (stock).
•
Both the min and max quantities are set to zero.
•
It has an order point override of zero.
•
The calculated order point is zero and it does not have an override (other than zero).
Fill Rate in File Update Parameters must be set to Y to capture fill rate information.
 
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > Fill Rates at Order Entry Reports > Fill Rate by Product Line
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouses to include on the report.
•
Order Date Range. The beginning and ending order entry dates to include.
•
Suppress Product Detail?. Determines if you want to display the fill rate by product or just the summary fill rate information for each product line:
-
Y - Suppresses the product detail. The report only displays fill rate by product line and warehouse.
-
N - Displays the fill rate information for each product within each product line and warehouse.
•
Product Line. The product lines to include on the report.
•
Major Group. The major product groups to include on the report (optional). Major groups are groups of products or product lines that group products together for sales analysis.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number.
Pline
The product line number.
Description
The description of the product and the product line.
Whse
The shipping warehouse where the fill rate was recorded.
OrdQty
The quantity ordered of each product for the selected dates.
PckQty
The quantity available to pick the first time the pick ticket printed.
Fill%
The fill rate (Pck Qty/Ord Qty).
#Lines
The total number of lines entered on sales order for the product.
Filled
The number of lines filled the first time the pick tickets printed.
Fill%
The fill rate compared to the number of lines on the sales orders (Filled/#Lines).
If a line is 100% shipped then the fill rate for that line is 100%. But if the line is less than 100% shipped then the fill rate for that line is zero.
