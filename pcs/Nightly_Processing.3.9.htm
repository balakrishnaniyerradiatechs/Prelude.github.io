Assigning Printers For Nightly Processing
Printer assignments determine where the Nightly Processing reports print.
To assign printers for Nightly Processing:
1
Select Systems > System Parameter > Maintenance > Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance to access the Nightly Processing Printer Assignments screen.
2
Choose a Warehouse then select F6-Printing > F6-NP Printers.
3
Enter the Physical Printer location, the Stationery type to use, the printer assignment Options, and the number of Copies to print.
4
Press F2-Save.
