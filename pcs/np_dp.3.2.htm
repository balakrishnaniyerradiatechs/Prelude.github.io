Setup Requirements for Nightly Processing
Nightly Processing uses the settings in Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance to determine when to replenish a product and how much to purchase (refer to “Maintaining Product Purchasing Parameters” in the Inventory documentation for detailed information on purchasing parameters). Purchasing parameters are also defined at the company and warehouse level. The purchasing parameters defined at the product level override those settings.
Once you define purchase requirements for each product, you must also set Nightly Processing maintenance flags and setup parameters to determine which reports are run, where they are to print, and which calculation options to use. Refer to the following:
