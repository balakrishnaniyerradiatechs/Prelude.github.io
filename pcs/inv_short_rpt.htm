P/O Inventory Shortage Report
The P/O Inventory Shortage report lists stock products that may have a negative inventory balance before the scheduled replenishment material is received. The available inventory and forecasted usage are used to calculate the future available quantity.
The report excludes:
-
Products that won’t reach a negative balance within their lead time.
-
Products that have no forecast during that period.
-
Products whose forecast is covered by the current lead time.
-
Non-stock products.
Menu Path: Purchasing > Reports > P/O Reports > P/O Inventory Shortage Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where the material is stocked.
•
Product. The products to include.
•
Product Line. The product lines to include.
•
Primary Vendor. The primary vendor assigned to the product.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
The product number.
Description
The description of the product.
Whse
The warehouse with the predicted shortage.
Ldt
The product’s Average Lead Time (from Product Vendor Maintenance).
Avail
The quantity of the product currently available to sell.
Frcst
The expected forecast of the product before the first purchase order delivery date.
Short
The future negative amount based on the available and forecasted amounts.
P/O Number
The purchase orders that contain the product.
Quantity
The quantity of the product on-order.
Delivery
The date the product is scheduled for delivery. Calculated from the product’s lead time.
Promised
The delivery date used to track vendor performance.
