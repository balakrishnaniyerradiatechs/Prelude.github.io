Open Backorder Detail Report By Vendor
The Open Backorder Detail Report By Vendor lists all backordered products for a vendor.You can evaluate the vendor’s performance and expedite the backordered products. The backorders for each vendor are totaled using replacement cost.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Summary Reports > Open Backorder Report By Vendor
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Warehouse. The warehouse responsible for shipping the material.
•
Vendor. The vendors responsible for the open backorders.
•
Product. The products on backorder.
•
Order Type. The sales order types to include on the report. Choose one or more of the following:
-
01 - Regular.
-
02 - Ship complete.
-
03 - Tag & hold.
-
04 - Direct ship.
-
05 - Counter invoice.
-
06 - Confirmation.
-
07 - Regular consignment.
-
08 - Confirmation consignment.
-
09 - Return goods.
-
10 - Repair orders.
-
12 - Invoice when complete.
-
13 - Ship backorder complete.
-
14 - Ship line item complete.
-
15 - Show order.
-
16 - Order projection.
-
17 - Manufacturer’s representative.
-
18 - Crib replenishment.
•
With Invoiced B/O’s. Determines if you want to include backorders that are still open after the orders are invoiced at least once:
-
Y - Include backorders still open after invoicing.
-
N - Do not include backorders that are still open after invoicing.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Primary Vendor/Description
The primary vendor number and description assigned to the product in Product Vendor Maintenance.
Product
Description 2
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered for each occurrence of the product.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Repl-Cost
The replacement cost for the product/warehouse when the report printed.
Extension
The total cost of the extended quantity of the backordered product (Qty-B/O x Repl-Cost).
