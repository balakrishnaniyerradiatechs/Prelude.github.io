Inspection Results History Inquiry
The Inspection Results History Inquiry lists inspection results for a vendor and allows you to view detail inspection result information for each entry.
Viewing Inspection Results
To list inspection results for a vendor:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quality Inspection Subsystem > Inquiries > Inspection Results Inquiry to open the Inspection Results History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Vendor for whom you would like to view inspection results.
3
For Sort, choose from the following to determine the sort sequence of the entries:
•
D - Purchase order entry date.
•
DD - Descending purchase order entry date.
•
P - P/O Number.
•
PD - Descending P/O Number.
4
For Filters, determine if you want to enter selection criteria to limit the list of entries. Choose from the following:
•
N - List all entries for the vendor.
•
Y - Opens the Inspection Results History Inquiry Filter Options screen to allow you to limit the results by the following criteria:
-
Entry Date Range.
-
Document Number.
-
Product.
-
Inspection Type.
-
Agent.
5
Press ENTER to list the inspection results for the vendor.
6
To view the detail information for a product on an inspection, highlight the Inspect# and press F5-Review to open the Inspection Results Inquiry screen
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Inspect#
The inspection number created in Inspection Results Entry.
Ent Date
The date the inspection information was entered.
Agent Name
The quality agent assigned to the inspection.
Document#
The document number on which the product was received.
Product
The product that required inspection.
Critical
Indicates whether it failed or passed critical inspection:
•
F/ - Failed. A number after the / is the number of times it failed.
•
P/ - Passed.
Major
Indicates whether it failed or passed major inspection:
•
F/ - Failed. A number after the / is the number of times it failed.
•
P/ - Passed.
Minor
Indicates whether it failed or passed minor inspection:
•
F/ - Failed. A number after the / is the number of times it failed.
•
P/ - Passed.
Type
The inspection type that identifies how the inspection request was generated:
•
C - Customer return.
•
P - PO receipts.
•
V - Vendor Performed.
•
W - W/O Manufacturing.
