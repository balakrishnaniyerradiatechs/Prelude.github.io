Creating Floor Plan Numbers
If you use floor plan financing and the customer requires a floor plan number on sales orders, you can use Floor Plan Number Maintenance to create the number.
Requiring Floor Plan Numbers
A floor plan number is required if both Financial Institution Floor Plan and Plan Number/Approval Req are set to Y in Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F8-Floor Plan. Refer to Setting up a Customer to use Floor Plan Financing.
To create a floor plan number:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Misc Code Files > Floor Plan to open the Floor Plan Number Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Floor Plan Number that references the floor plan agreement. You are required to enter this number when you enter a sales order for the customer that uses floor plan financing. Refer to Entering a Floor Plan Sales Order.
3
For Customer Number, enter the floor plan customer who is set up as a financial institution for other customers and is responsible for payment, whose credit limit is checked, and whose A/R balance is updated when orders are entered for customers assigned to them.
4
Enter a Description of the floor plan.
5
Enter the Expiration Date of the floor plan.
6
Press F2-Save to save the floor plan information.
