Branch Hold for Transfers
To define branch hold options for transfers:
1
Select Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Inv. Parameters screen.
2
For Transfer Branch Hold choose from the following:
•
Y - The transfer goes on Branch Manager Hold when a transfer lowers the quantity of an item below the safety stock level if the shipping warehouse is not in the replenishment path.
•
M - The transfer goes on Branch Manager Hold when a transfer lowers the quantity of an item below the safety stock level regardless of the replenishment path.
•
N - The transfer does not go on Branch Manager Hold when a transfer lowers the stock level of a product below safety stock.
3
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the transfer hold information.
Refer to Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance in the Inventory documentation for information on safety stock levels.
