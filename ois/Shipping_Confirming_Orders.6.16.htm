Printing a Bill of Lading
If the freight company requires a bill of lading to send with a shipment, use the Bill Of Lading Print screen to print a bill of lading for a confirmed release.
To print a bill of lading:
1
Select Order Processing > Batch Ticket Printing > Bill of Lading Printing to open the Bill Of Lading Print screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse Number where the labels are to print.
3
Enter the Sales Order Number for which you want to print labels.
4
Enter the Number Of Labels to print for the shipment.
5
For Printer Form Queue, enter the printer number where the labels are to print.
