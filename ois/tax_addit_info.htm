Additional Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen
The Additional Tax Jurisdiction Information screen allows you to determine the tax status of freight and handling charges for a tax jurisdiction.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Sales Tax > Tax Jurisdictions > F5-Addit Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Tax Outbound Frt
A check indicates that outbound freight is taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax Inbound Frt
A check indicates that inbound freight is taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax Handling
A check indicates that handling charges are taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax Misc Charge
A check indicates that outbound miscellaneous charges are taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax MNP Charge
A check indicates that MNP charges are taxable for this jurisdiction. MNP charges are miscellaneous charges in Canada that are taxable at the federal level but not at the provincial level.
Tax Labor
A check indicates that labor is taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax Delivery Charges
A check indicates that delivery charges are taxable for this jurisdiction. This flag overrides the setting in Product Warehouse Maintenance for labor type service items.
Tax Pick Up Charges
A check indicates that pick up charges are taxable for this jurisdiction.
Tax Restocking Charges
A check indicates that restocking charges are taxable for this jurisdiction.
F2-Save
Saves the tax information and returns to the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance screen.
