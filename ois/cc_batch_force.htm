Force Duplicate Authorization screen
The Force Post Authorization screen allows you to you to force an order through post-authorization for IC Verify for the following reasons:
•
The pre-authorization expired which released the original amount.
•
The credit card expired. This could happen if you pre-authorize but do not invoice for a month.
•
The credit card is reported stolen or lost.
•
A customer places two orders in the same day and the dollar amount is exactly the same. This could cause a rejection if the bank assumes that it was a duplicate sent in error.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Force Duplicate Transaction
