Re-Printing One Invoice
To re-print an invoice:
1
Select Order Processing > Invoice Preview and Printing > Invoice Reprinting to open the Invoice Reprint screen.
2
Enter the Invoice Number to re-print. The Order History Number, Customer, and Warehouse, appear below. The Order History Number generally refers to the sales order that was invoiced. If you do not know the Invoice Number, you can enter the Order History Number to access the Invoice Number.
3
For Inv Document Option, determine how the invoice is to transmitted to the customer:
-
B - Both fax and print.
-
E - Email only.
-
M - Email and print.
-
N - Print only.
-
Y - Fax only.
4
If you are printing the invoice, enter the Printer Form Queue where it should print.
5
Verify or change the Invoice Message to appear at the bottom of the invoice.
6
Once you choose Execute. The following message appears:
## record(s) selected for Invoice Printing!
7
Choose one of the following:
•
Print Invoices - Print the invoice.
•
Mask Print Test - To test the alignment of the invoice forms in the printer. Not necessary for laser printers. After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Invoices.
•
Abort Printing - Cancel the invoice print process.
