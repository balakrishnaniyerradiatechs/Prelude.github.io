Allocate Backorders To Existing P/O’s screen
If you enter an unavailable stock product on a sales order, you can use the Allocate Backorders to Existing P/Os screen to see if the product is on a purchase order you can source from. If you choose to link the backorder to the purchase order (and the product is not already linked to another sales order, transfer, or work order) when the backorder arrives in your warehouse, it automatically allocates to your customer’s order. You must source the whole backorder quantity. Refer to Sourcing a Backorder to an Existing Purchase Order for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > Line Item Detail screen > F8-Source P/O
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product on backorder.
Backorder Quantity
The quantity of the product on backorder.
P/O Number
The numbers of the purchase orders that contain the product.
Vendor
The vendor assigned to each purchase order.
Avg LT
The average lead time of the product when purchased from this vendor. The time it takes for the product to arrive in your warehouse from the time it is ordered.
P/O Date
The date the purchase order was entered.
Del-Date
The expected delivery date for the backordered product.
Whse
The warehouse that will receive the material.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the purchase order.
OpnQty
The quantity open (not yet received) on the purchase order.
Linked
The quantity of the product that you want to link to from the purchase order to your sales order.
New P/O#
The new or existing purchase order if you want to split a quantity off of the original purchase order number:
•
To split a quantity onto an existing purchase order, enter the purchase order number.
•
To split a quantity onto a new purchase order, enter NEW for the next available purchase order number. Refer to Linking a Purchase Order to a Backorder.
•
Leave blank to keep the product assigned to the original purchase order.
F2-Save
Saves the purchase order information.
F5-Review
Opens the Purchase Order Display screen to allow you to view detail information on the purchase order.
F6-Invty Inq
Opens the Inventory Inquiry screen to allow you to view detail availability information for the product.
