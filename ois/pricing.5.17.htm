Copying all Price Matrices for a Product
If you want to copy price matrices for a product to another product, use the Price Matrix Mass Update. Only matrix types 1 (customer/product) and 6 (customer price group/product) are copied when you use this procedure.
To copy price matrices that are set up for a product to another product:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Price Matrix Maintenance > Price Matrix Mass Update to open the Price Matrix Mass Update screen.
2
Enter the Product with matrices to copy.
3
If you just want to copy matrices set up for the product in a single warehouse, enter the Warehouse number.
4
For Job/Customer P/O, enter the job or customer purchase order number if you just want to copy the matrices that apply to one job or purchase order.
5
For Contract, enter the contract number if you just want to copy the matrices that apply to one contract. Otherwise, all type 1 and 6 matrices are copied.
6
For Drop Ship, indicate if this matrix is limited to direct ship orders only:
•
Y - Copy only matrix types 1 and 6 that have Drop Ship set to Y.
•
N - Copy all type 1 and 6 matrices.
7
For Currency, if you use multiple currencies, enter a currency type if you only want to copy product matrices for a currency type.
8
For Copy Ctr#, indicate if you want to copy the contract number from the old price matrix to the new one:
•
Y - Copy the contract number to the new matrix.
•
N - Do not copy the contract number from the old matrix to the new one.
9
For Copy Exp Date, indicate if the expiration date from the old matrix should be copied to the new matrix:
•
Y - Copy the expiration date from the old contract.
•
N - Do not copy the expiration date. The new matrices are created with no expiration date.
10
For Override Price, indicate if the price for the product/matrix can be overridden on a sales order:
•
Y - The price can be overridden on a sales order.
•
N - The price cannot be overridden on a sales order.
•
H - Prices can be raised but not lowered below gross price.
11
Under Copy To:, enter the Product that is to receive the price matrix.
12
For Whse, enter a warehouse number if the price matrix only applies to one warehouse. Leave blank to copy the matrix to all warehouses.
13
For D/S, indicate if the new product matrix should be for drop ship orders only:
•
Y - The new matrix only applies to drop ship orders.
•
N - The new matrix applies to all orders.
14
For Job/Cust P/O, enter a job or customer purchase order number if the new matrix is only for a specific job or purchase order.
15
For Contract, enter a contract number if the new matrix is only for one contract.
16
Repeat steps 11-15 for all products that you want to copy the price matrix to.
17
Press F2-Save. The following message displays:
This Process Will Copy the “FROM” Price Matrix to All the “TO” Product Numbers.
Do You Wish to Continue?
18
Choose from the following:
•
Y - All prices matrices for the product are copied to the new product number.
•
N - Price matrices are not copied.
