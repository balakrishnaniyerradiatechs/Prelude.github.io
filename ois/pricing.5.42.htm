Creating a Promotion Price Matrix by Order Total
You can create a promotion matrix that gives a customer a discount based on the value of their order rather than a product. You can also set up multiple levels of discounts for different buy quantities.
To create a promotion matrix based on the order total:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance to open the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen.
2
For Plan #, press ENTER to assign the next available plan number for promotional price matrices.
3
For Matrix Type, select 2 (Order). The Promotion Matrix Maintenance Header screen appears.
4
Enter the Vendor that the promotion applies to, if applicable.
5
Enter the Start Date to indicate when the product promotion takes effect.
6
Enter the Expire Date for when the promotion ends. Leave blank if the promotion is always in effect.
7
If the promotion is associated with a marketing campaign, enter a Campaign Number. This number must be entered in Sales Order Entry for the promotion to take effect. Leave blank if not using campaign numbers or if all marketing campaigns use the promotion.
8
For Audit Sales, determine if sales using this promotion are tracked:
•
Y - Track promotion sales. Informational only for custom reporting.
•
N - Do not track promotion sales.
9
For Blanket Contract, enter a blanket P/O contract if one was set up in Blanket Contract Maintenance (refer to Blanket Contracts). If you enter a value, only sales orders with this blanket contract number in the Customer P/O# field will receive the promotional pricing.
10
For One Time Promo, indicate if this promotion is a one time offer. Choose from the following:
•
Y - This is a one time offer only for each customer.
•
N - The promotion is available for all orders up until the Expire Date.
11
For Buy Amt, enter the value a customer’s order must be to get a discount off of their purchase.
12
For Disc%, enter the discount percent the customer will receive if they purchase the Buy Amt. For example, if the customer buys $100 worth of merchandise, they will get an extra 10% off of their total order.
13
Repeat steps 11 & 12 for as many discount levels as you want to set up for this promotion.
14
Press F2-Save to save promotion matrix information.
Exclude/Include Customers/Products
•
To exclude customers from a promotion matrix, or to create a list of customers that should be the only ones to receive a promotion matrix, refer to Excluding/Including Customers in a Promotion Matrix.
•
To exclude product lines from a promotion matrix or to create a list of product lines that should be the only ones to receive a promotion matrix, refer to Excluding or Including Product Lines on a Matrix.
Entering a Promotional Product on a Sales Order
When you enter a sales order for a customer that qualifies for promotional products, after you exit the Sales Order Header screen, the Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry appears to list available promotions. Also, PROMO appears at the bottom of the Line Item Detail screen to let you know that promotional pricing is in effect. The free products are automatically added to the order.
