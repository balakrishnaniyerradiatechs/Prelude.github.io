Progress Billing Screen
If you use progress billing to invoice a customer job in increments as each section of the job is completed, you can enter the estimated price, cost, and gross profit information for the job in the Progress Billing screen. As each section of the job is billed, you can use the Progress Billing Inquiry or the Progress Billing Summary Report to view the estimated, invoiced, and remaining amounts on the job.
Refer to “Progress Billing” in the Work Order documentation for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > Action Bar > Add’l Opts > Progress Billing Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Progress Billing?
Indicates if this job will use progress billing:
•
Y - Progress billing will be used. You can bill the customer in increments as each section of the job is completed.
•
N - Progress billing will not be used.
Salesrep #1
The sales representative assigned to the job.
Selling Whse
The warehouse that receives credit for the order.
Estimated
The estimated sales amount of the job.
Estimated Cost
The estimated cost of the job.
Estimated GP%
The estimated gross profit earned on the job.
F2-Save
Saves the progress billing information.
F5-Detail
Opens the Progress Billing Inquiry to display the sales, cost, and gross profit amounts already invoiced and the amounts remaining.
