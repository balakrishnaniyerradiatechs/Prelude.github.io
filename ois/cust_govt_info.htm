Government Information Screen
If your customer is affiliated with the U.S. government, they may require a DD250 inspection and receiving report to print with sales orders. The Government Information screen allows you to enter default DD250 information. Refer to Printing DD250 Invoices.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > Action Bar > Add’l Options > Government Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Form DD250 Required Flag
The default flag for sales orders for this customer to indicate if a DD250 form is required:
•
Y - The customer requires a DD250 form.
•
N - The customer does not require a DD250 form.
The DD form 250 is a material inspection and receiving report that prints for government customers. If a sales order has the Print DD250 Flag set to Y, then it will print a DD250 invoice instead of the regular invoice. Refer to Printing DD250 Invoices for more information.
Government Customer Flag
Indicates if the customer is part of the U.S. government:
•
Y - This is a government customer.
•
N - This is not a government customer.
Source Inspection Required
The location where orders for this customer are inspected:
•
D - At the destination point.
•
S - At the source.
•
N - No inspection required.
Payment Made By Code
The code that prints in the Payment Will Be Made By Code block (12) on the DD250 form.
F2-Save
Saves the government information.
