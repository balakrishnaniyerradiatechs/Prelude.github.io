Setting an Auto Cancellation Date by Line Item
To enter an automatic order cancellation date for a product on a sales order:
1
From within the Sales Order Entry Line Item Detail screen, select F5-Add Info to open the Line Item Detail Additional Information screen.
2
For Auto Cancellation Date, enter the date the line item should be cancelled if the backordered product is not received by this date. This date overrides any auto cancellation date entered at the line item level (refer to Setting an Auto Cancellation Date at the Header Level).
3
Press F2-Save to return to the Line Item detail screen.
The Lost Sales Reason Code CD (refer to Creating Lost Sales Reason Codes), displays on the lost sales reports.
