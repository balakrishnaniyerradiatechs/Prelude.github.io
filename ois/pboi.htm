Product Backorder Inquiry
The Product Backorder Inquiry lists all backorders for a product. You can view the detail sales order/customer information for each backorder.
Viewing Backorders for a Product
To view backorders for a product:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Product Backorder Inquiry to open the Product Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product Number.
3
Enter the shipping Warehouse number.
Open sales orders that contain the backordered product appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
The sales order number that contains the backordered product.
Customer
The customer who ordered the product.
Description
The customer description.
Req-Date
The date the customer requires the product.
Qty-Ord
The quantity ordered by the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product, as entered on the sales order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity due to the customer, including the quantity being shipped and the quantity backordered.
Fut
An F in this column indicates that this is a future order.
Qty-Pck
The quantity allocated to ship.
Qty-Shp
The quantity of the product that already shipped.
Qty-Bo
The quantity backordered.
S
The supply code for this line item that indicates where the backorder is being sourced from:
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Kit
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
Linkage
The linked document number, if the product is linked to a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Sales Order Display screen to allow you to view detail information for the sales order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
