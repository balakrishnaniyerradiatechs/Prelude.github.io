Contract Price Ship-To Usage Report
To analyze the effectiveness of contract pricing for customers by ship-to location, use the Contract Price Ship-To Usage Report to view the quantity of products purchased by customer ship-to location for a contract.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Ship-To Usage Report.
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customer for whom you want to view ship-to contract product usage.
•
Contract. The contracts to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The number and description of the customer using the contract pricing.
Ship-To/Contract
The ship-to number and the contract number for that ship-to location.
Product / Description
Line 1: The number for the product using contract pricing.
Line 2: The product description.
Price
The contract price for the customer/product.
Avg Cost
The average cost of the product.
Shp Usg
The quantity of the product billed to date to this customer ship-to location using this contract price.
