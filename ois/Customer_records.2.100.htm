Merging/Replacing Customer Accounts
If you want to move or merge customer information from one customer account to another, you can use Universal Customer Merge Entry. You must run the Universal Customer Replacement /Merge Update to complete the merge/change.
To merge customer information:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Universal Customer Replacement/Merge > Universal Customer Change/Merge Entry to open the Universal Customer Merge Entry screen.
2
For From Customer, enter the number of the customer whose information you want to move.
3
For To Customer, enter the number of the customer who will be updated with the information from the From Customer.
•
If you enter a new customer number that does not currently exist in the system, a C appears for Merge Type to indicate that you will copy the customer information.
•
If you enter an existing customer number, the following message appears.
This number already exists in the Customer file. This will result in a merge of the ‘from customer’ data into the ‘to customer’ data. Is this what you want to do?
Choose from the following:
-
Yes - An M appears for Merge Type to indicate that you will merge the customer information.
-
No - Returns to the To Customer field so you can enter another customer number.
4
Press F2-Save to save the customer information.
