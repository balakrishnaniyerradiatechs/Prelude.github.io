Daily Sales Tax Adjustment Summary Report
The Daily Sales Tax Adjustment Detail report summarizes tax adjustment information for a tax jurisdiction for a day. To adjust tax, enter A in the M/C column in Cash Receipts Entry. Refer to Cash Receipts Entry in the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information on adjusting tax.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Sales Tax Reports > Sales Tax Adjustment Summary
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Posting Date. The date the sales orders were posted to the general ledger.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Tax Jurisdiction
The tax jurisdiction code. These codes are set up in the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen and then assigned to customers in the Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen and the Customer Job Sales Tax Maintenance Screen . You can override a tax jurisdiction in the Sales Order Header Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen.
Applicable
The total value of merchandise (Taxable + Nontaxable).
Taxable
The total taxable merchandise for the corresponding tax jurisdiction.
Nontaxable
The total amount of tax exempt merchandise for the tax jurisdiction.
Tax Amount
The total amount of tax charged.
