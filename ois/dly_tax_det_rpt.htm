Daily Sales Tax Detail Report
The Daily Sales Tax detail report displays sales tax information for sales orders for a day.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Sales Tax Reports > Sales Tax Detail
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Invoice Date. The date the sales orders were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Jur
The tax jurisdiction code. These codes are set up in the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen and then assigned to customers in the Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen (Customer Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F6-Sales Tax) and the Customer Job Sales Tax Maintenance Screen (Customer Job Numbers > F5-Add Info > F5-Tax Jurisdiction). You can override a tax jurisdiction in the Sales Order Header Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen.
Description
The description of the tax jurisdiction (e.g., state, city, country).
Inv#
The invoice number.
Exempt#
The customer’s tax exempt number, if applicable.
Cust#
The customer who purchased the merchandise.
Description
The customer description.
Applicable
The total value of merchandise (Taxable + Nontaxable).
Taxable
The value of the merchandise that is taxable.
Nontaxable
The value of the merchandise that is exempt from tax.
Tax Charged
The value for the amount of tax charged.
