Entering a Quick Quotation
If you need a quick method to quote a customer price and availability without having to enter the header information or generate a copy of the quote, you can use Quick Quotation Entry. After you quote the prices, you can turn the quick quote into a regular quote or a sales order.
To use Quick Quotation Entry:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Quick Quotation Entry to open the Quick Quotation Entry screen.
2
Enter the line items as you would on a regular quote or sales order (refer to Entering Sales Order Line Items).
3
When you press F2-Save to complete the quote, the following message appears:
Do You Wish To......
4
Choose from the following:
•
Quote - Opens the Quotation Header screen to allow you to enter the header information to save the information as a regular quote. You can edit and/or complete the quote as usual (refer to Entering a Quote).
•
Order - Opens the Quote Conversion screen to allow you to enter the header information and convert the quote to a sales order. Refer to Converting a Quote to a Sales Order.
•
Suspend - Suspends the quote. You can access it again later.
•
Return - Returns to the Quick Quotation Entry screen to allow you to verify or edit the quote.
