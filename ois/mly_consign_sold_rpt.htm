Monthly Consigned Sold Report (Product Type)
The Monthly Consignment Inventory Sold Report displays all vendor consigned inventory (status C) sold for a fiscal period. Vendor consigned inventory is inventory that you do not pay for until you sell it.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Consigned Sold Report (Product Type)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period in which sales orders containing vendor consigned inventory were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Prod Line
The product line.
Product Number
The product number.
Product Description
The description of the product.
Invoice#
Order#
Line 1: The invoice number for the sold vendor consigned inventory.
Line 2: The original order number for the vendor consigned inventory.
Customer Number/Name
The customer number and description.
Invoice Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Qty Sold
The quantity of the vendor consigned inventory sold.
Extension
The extended price of the total quantity sold.
