Monthly M/R Register
The Monthly M/R Register lists vendor, customer, and commission information on all manufacturer rep orders that were updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing (refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order) for a selling warehouse and fiscal period.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > Monthly M/R Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that received credit for the orders.
•
Period. The fiscal period in which the orders were updated.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order#
The manufacturer rep order number.
Proc.Dte
The date the order was updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing. Refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order.
Vendor
The vendor that paid you the commission for the order.
Description
The vendor description.
Cust#
The number for the customer that ordered the material from the vendor.
Description
The customer description.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number.
Rep
The salesrep assigned to the order.
Merch Amt
The total amount ordered by the customer.
Comm Amt
The total amount of commission paid to you by the vendor.
Gp%
The percent of the customer’s price that vendor paid to you as commission.
