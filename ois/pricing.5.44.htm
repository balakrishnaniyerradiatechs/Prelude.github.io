Price Labels
If your customer requires price labels on the products that you sell to them, you can design a price label format and then assign the label information to the customer and the products they buy. You can also charge the customer for the labels either by label or by a percentage of the sales amount. If you do charge the customer, you can either itemize the price to add to each product or add the total charge to the invoice. Labels print when pick tickets print for orders that require price labels.
Contact Prelude for help with setting up labels.
