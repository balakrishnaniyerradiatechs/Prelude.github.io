Viewing Product Complements
Complement information provides the salesperson with helpful information regarding a product. Once complements are set up for a product in Product Maintenance, any time you enter the product on a sales order, COMP displays on the Line Item Detail screen to let you know that complements exist for the product.
Assigning Complements
Product complements are assigned to a product in Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Complements (refer to “Complements” in the Inventory documentation for information on setting up product complements).
When you sell a product that has complements set up, the Product Complementary Inquiry screen appears after you exit the Line Item Detail screen.
Press OK after you view the complements, to return to the Sales Order Entry screen.
