Promotional Price Matrices
Price promotions allow you to give customers discounts or free products. You can give a discount based on an order total or give away free products based on a purchased product (e.g., buy one, get one free). You can also include or exclude customers from a promotion. You should review and update or delete promotions periodically to make sure that the correct promotion is used, otherwise, the system uses the first matrix that was set up. If you use the Marketing module, you can also use promotional pricing to work with marketing campaigns.
Promotion Exceptions
Promotional matrices are not included for confirmation sales orders, credit lines, kit components, or for products with a sales price that is equal to or less than the order line cost.
