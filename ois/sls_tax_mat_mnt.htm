Sales Tax Matrix Maintenance Screen
The Sales Tax Matrix Maintenance screen allows you to set up sales tax matrices to calculate tax rates based on customers, products, and tax group combinations. When you enter a sales order using the customer/product combination, the tax rate in the sales tax matrix overrides any other sales tax calculations.
Sales Tax Matrix Override
Sales Tax Matrix in Order Processing Parameters determines if you can override a Sales Tax Matrix:
•
Y - You can override the sales tax matrix.
•
N - You cannot override the sales tax matrix.
•
S - Tax determined by the ship-to state on the order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Sales Tax > Sales Tax Matrix
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Matrix Type
The sales tax matrix number that determines the type of information used to calculate tax rates:
•
1 - Customer/Product.
•
2 - Customer Tax Group/Product.
•
3 - Customer/Product Tax Group.
•
4 - Customer Tax Group/Product Tax Group.
•
5 - Customer Ship-To/Product.
•
6 - Customer Ship-To/Product Tax Group
Tax Jurisdiction/State
The tax jurisdiction code to use with this tax matrix, that overrides any existing tax jurisdiction set up for the customer or product.
Input Information: Fields vary depending on which matrix type you choose:
Customer
The customer eligible for this tax matrix.
Ship-To Number
The ship-to number for this customer that is eligible for the tax matrix.
Customer Tax Group
The customer tax group from Valid Response Maintenance.
Product
The product number that is eligible for the tax matrix.
Product Tax Group
The product tax group in Valid Response Maintenance.
Taxable
Determines if the matrix for this product/customer combination is taxable:
•
Y - Tax applies to sales orders using this matrix.
•
N - Tax does not apply to sales orders using this matrix.
•
Blank - Uses the tax setting from Customer Ship-To Maintenance.
Dollar Value
The dollar amount at which this tax matrix goes into effect. For example, if the Dollar Value is $100 and a the unit price of an item is $150, then the first $100 is taxed at the customer’s normal rate and $50 is taxed at the Tax Rate % in this matrix.
Tax Rate %
The tax rate percent to apply to the dollar amount that is in excess of the Dollar Value established in this matrix.
Tax Amt Per Unit
The tax dollars per unit to add to the calculated sales tax amount. For example, if the customer purchases 10 of the product, the Tax Amt Per Unit is multiplied by 10 to calculate the amount to add to the sales tax.
F2-Save
Saves the tax matrix information.
F4-Del
Deletes the tax matrix.
