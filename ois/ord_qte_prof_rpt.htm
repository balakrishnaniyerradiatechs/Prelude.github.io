Order/Quote Profitability Report
The Order/Quote Profitability report lists gross profit information for each line item on a quote or sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Order/Quote Profitability Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Order Or Quote?. Determines whether you want to view a sales order or a sales quote:
-
Q - Quote.
-
O - Order.
•
Order Number. The sales order or quote number (only one) for which you want to view gross profit information.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order
The sales order or quote number.
Ln#
The line number on the sales order or quote.
Product Number
The product number.
Ord Qty
The quantity ordered.
Ext Price
The total sales price for the extended quantity of the product (Ord Qty x Price).
Ext Cost
The total cost for the extended quantity of the product (Ord Qty x Cost).
Profit
The gross profit dollars earned for this product (Ext Price - Ext Cost).
Profit %
The gross profit percent earned for the total quantity sold or quoted for this product (Ext Cost/Ext Price).
