Adding Charges to an Invoice
To add freight or any other charges to a sales order either before or after it is invoiced, use Commission Charges Entry to correct the commission amount due to the salesrep.
Updating Commission
Update Commissions in Accounts Payable Parameters (refer to Setting System Parameters for Commission) determines how to update commission when cost is adjusted in Commission Charges Entry.
•
C - Create a commission record for the additional charge. The charge appears on the commission report as C followed by a 6-digit invoice number.
•
A - Delete the original commission record and re-create it to include the additional charge. The invoice date is updated to the date of the new invoice.
To add charges to an invoice:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Charges Entry to open the Commission Charges Entry screen.
2
Depending on whether or not the order is already invoiced, enter one of the following:
•
Order Number - If the order is not yet invoiced.
•
Order History Number - If the order is already invoiced.
The Customer and the Invoice Number Display.
3
Enter the Charge Type. Choose from the following:
•
F - Freight.
•
L - Labor.
•
M - Miscellaneous.
•
R - Remnant.
4
Enter the Charge Amount to add to the invoice.
5
Press F2-Update to adjust the commission due to the salesrep.
