Customer/Product History Inquiry
The Customer/Product History Inquiry displays sales order and pricing information for each time a customer purchased a product.
Viewing Customer Product Information
To view invoiced sales orders for a customer/product:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer/Product History Inquiry to open the Customer/Product History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Enter the Product.
Invoiced sales orders for the customer that contain the product number appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
The sales order number.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for the order.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Whse
The shipping warehouse assigned to the order.
Ord-Date
The date the customer ordered the material.
Qty-Ord
The quantity originally ordered by the customer.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped to the customer.
Shp-Date
The date the product shipped to the customer.
Class
The inventory classification code assigned to the product.
Net Price
The net price that the customer paid for the product.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to show detail product and shipping information for the entire sales order.
F7-Last Price Paid
Opens the Last Price Paid Inquiry so you can see the price paid each time the customer purchased the product, starting with the most recent order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Lists the previous group of orders, if applicable.
sF6-Next Group
Lists the next group of orders, if applicable.
