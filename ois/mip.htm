Printing Installment Invoice Memos
If you have customers that pay in installments, you can use Installment Memo Invoice Printing to generate a reminder notices to let them know when the next installment payment is due.
You can set up a customer to be billed on an installment plan by assigning a Lease Contract Payment Terms code to the customer. Payment terms codes are set up in A/R Payment Terms Maintenance in the Accounts Receivable module and assigned to customers in the Customer Maintenance Screen. You can override the payment terms for a sales order in the Sales Order Header Order Additional Information Screen.
To generate installment invoice reminder notices:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > Installment Memo Invoice Printing to open the Installment Memo Invoice Printing screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to print installment memos. Leave blank to include all warehouses.
3
Enter the Customer for whom you want to print installment memos. Leave blank to include all customers that are set up for installment invoices.
4
Enter the beginning and ending date range to limit the installment memos to an invoice date range.
5
Indicate the Printer Form Queue where you want the installment invoice memos to print.
6
Press F2-Save to print the invoice memos. The following message appears:
Load Invoice Forms In Printer & Align Visually
7
Choose one of the following:
•
Print Invoices - Print the installment invoice memos.
•
Mask Print Test - To test the alignment of the invoice memos in the printer. After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Invoices.
•
Abort Printing - Cancels the invoice memo print process.
If a customer has more than one invoice due for a time period, you can print individual memos for each installment invoice. Any installment payments appear on the invoice.
