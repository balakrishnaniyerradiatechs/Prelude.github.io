Previewing Invoices
The Invoice Preview report is an optional step that allows you to preview the invoices that are flagged to generate to verify that they are correct. If something is not correct (e.g., GP%) you can change it before you send the invoices.
To preview invoices:
1
Select Order Processing > Invoice Preview and Printing > Invoice Preview Report to open the Invoice Preview Report screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse(s) whose invoices you want to preview.
3
Select the Invoice Cycle to print. Each customer is assigned to one of the following:
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
•
W - Weekly.
•
D - Daily
4
For Print Source, indicate which types of invoice documents that you want to preview. Choose from the following:
•
I - Print invoices only.
•
C - Print credit memos only.
•
B - Print both invoices and credit memos.
•
M - Mail counter invoice.
5
For Based on Acct/Comm Cost, determine which cost to display. Choose from the following:
•
A - Accounting cost.
•
C - Commission cost.
6
For Detail Or Summary?, choose from the following:
•
D - Show the detail product information for each invoice.
•
S - Show the summary information for each invoice.
7
Once you choose Execute, you can print, email, or fax the report for review.
Refer to the sample report and the column descriptions on the following pages.
After you review the invoices and correct any errors, you can print the invoices. Refer to Printing Invoices.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Sell
The selling warehouse, which is the warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
Order#
The sales order number that will be invoiced.
Cust#
The customer number.
Customer Name
The customer description.
Disc/All
The total order discount amount allowed.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was available to ship.
Frt+Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges for the material that shipped. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
MiscChg
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Min+Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise sold.
Dep-Amt
The deposit balance currently available. This amount represents the initial deposit placed on a sales order or the remaining balance if some of the deposit was applied to an invoice.
Invoice-Amt
The total amount of the invoice, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost-Amt
The total cost of the merchandise.
Gp%
The gross profit percentage earned for the products sold.
