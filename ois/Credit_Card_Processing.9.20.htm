Removing Rejected Credit Card Transactions
If you have items on the Credit Card Post Authorization Rejection Report, you can still close the batch, but you must eventually handle the rejections.
As long as the Credit Card Post Authorization Approval Report matches the Protobase report (refer to Closing a Credit Card Batch), you can close the batch.
To remove rejection transactions you may need to:
•
Change the expiration date (refer to Changing an Expiration Date) and then re-run the post-authorization.
•
Delete the transaction and move it to history (refer to Deleting Rejected Transactions).
•
Re-run the post-authorization to catch any transactions that were approved since the first post-authorization.
•
Enter a negative invoice for the customer in Cash Receipts Entry to add it to their open A/R balance (refer to the Accounts Receivable documentation).
