Quote History Product Inquiry
The Quotation History Product Inquiry lists purged quotes for a product. Converted, expired, and cancelled quotes are purged when you run the Disposition Analysis reports (refer to the Quotation Disposition Detail Report and the Quotation Disposition Summary Report).
Once a quote is moved to history, you can not convert it to a sales order. However, you can duplicate it and then convert it into a sales order.
Viewing Purged Quotes by Product
To list purged quotes for a product:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Inquiries > Quotation History By Product Number to open the Quote History Product Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product.
3
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to view quotes.
Purged quotes for the product/warehouse display.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quote#
The quote number.
Customer#
The customer who requested the quote.
Qte Date
The date the quote was entered.
Exp Date
The date the quote expires. If the customer does not accept the quote by this date, the quoted price is no longer valid.
Qty Qte
The quantity quoted.
Net Price
The price quoted to the customer for the product.
Description
The customer description.
F2-Finish
Closes the Quote History Product Inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Quotation Display screen to allow you to view detail information for the quote.
F8-Log
Opens the Quote Log Inquiry to list every transaction that occurred for this quote.
F9-Goto Ln
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
