Customer Job Analysis Report
The Customer Job Analysis Report displays accounts receivable, merchandise, and gross profit information for a customer job. Refer to the Customer Job Maintenance Screen for information on setting up a customer job.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer Job Analysis Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customer whose job information you want to view.
•
Salesrep One. The salesreps to include.
•
GP% range. The beginning and ending gross profit amounts to include.
•
Status. The job status:
-
A - Active.
-
N - Inactive.
-
C - Closed.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer number.
Job_Number
The customer job number.
Description
The customer description.
Job Description
The job description.
S
The status of the job.
•
A - Active.
•
N - Inactive.
•
C - Closed.
Cls_Date
If Status is C, the date the job was closed.
Merch
The total amount of merchandise sold on the job
Cost
The total cost of the job.
Frt_Cost
The total freight cost associated with this job.
Gross Profit
The total gross profit amount earned on this job.
GP-Pct
The total gross profit percent earned on this job.
Mfg_Rep
The amount paid to the manufacturer’s rep for this job.
Tot_Profit
The amount of profit earned on this job.
