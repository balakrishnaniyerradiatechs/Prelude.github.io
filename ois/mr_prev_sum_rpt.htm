M/R Preview Report
The M/R Preview report lists information on manufacturer rep orders that shipped, for which commission was paid, and are ready to be processed in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > M/R Preview Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouses that will receive credit for the sale.
•
Vendor. The vendor that paid the commission.
•
Customer. The customer that purchased the product.
•
Salesrep. The sales representative that was assigned to the order.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order #
The manufacturer rep order number.
Ent-Date
The date the order was entered.
Vendor
The number for the vendor that will pay you commission for the sale.
Description
The vendor description.
Cust#
The number for the customer that purchased the product.
Description
The customer description.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number.
Rep1
The sales representative assigned to the order.
Merch-Amt
The total amount of merchandise the customer purchased on this order.
Comm-Amt
The total amount of commission that you will receive from the vendor for the sale of this order.
