Creating a Promotion Price Matrix by Product
You can use price promotions to give customers free products based on the following:
•
Buy a product, get another of the same product for free or at a discount.
•
Buy a product or combination of products to get a promotional product for free or at a discount.
•
Buy one or more of a product to get a promotional product or group of promotional products for free or at a discount.
To create a promotion price matrix based on a product:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance to open the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen.
2
For Plan #, press ENTER to assign the next available plan number for promotional price matrices.
3
For Matrix Type, select 1 (Product).
4
For Promotion Type, choose from the following to determine how to give free products:
•
1 - Same as. Buy one product get one free.
•
2 - Mix and match. Buy a product or combination of products to get a promotional product for free.
•
3 - Specific buy, specific get. Buy one or more of a promotional product to get a promotional product or group of promotional products for free.
The Promotion Matrix Maintenance Header screen appears.
5
Enter the Vendor that the promotion applies to.
6
Enter the Start Date to indicate when the product promotion takes effect.
7
Enter the Expire Date for when the promotion ends. Leave blank if the promotion is always in effect.
8
If the promotion is associated with a marketing campaign, enter a Campaign Number. This number must be entered on a sales order for the promotion to take effect. Leave blank if not using campaign numbers or if all marketing campaigns use the promotion.
9
For Audit Sales, determine if sales using this promotion are tracked:
•
Y - Track promotion sales. Informational only for custom reporting.
•
N - Do not track promotion sales.
10
For Blanket Contract, if applicable, enter a blanket P/O contract if one was set up for this product in Blanket Contract Maintenance (refer to Blanket Contracts). If you enter a value, only sales orders with this blanket contract number in the Customer P/O# field will receive the promotional pricing.
11
For One Time Promo, indicate if this promotion is a one time offer. Choose from the following:
•
Y - This is a one time offer only for each customer. Once the customer buys the product, they are excluded from the promotion.
•
N - The promotion is available for all orders up until the Expire Date.
12
For Buy Qty, enter the quantity that the customer must buy to qualify for the promotion. For example, enter 1 if the customer must buy 1 product to receive the free product.
13
For Get Qty, enter the free quantity of the product to receive if the Buy Qty is met.
14
Enter a Description of the promotion.
15
Press F2-Save to return to the Promotion Type Matrix Screen.
16
For Buy Product, enter the products the customer must buy to receive the free or discounted products.
17
For Get Product, enter the products the customer will get if they purchase the Buy Product. This does not apply for Promotion Type 1 (same as) because the customer automatically gets another of the same product for free.
Copy Buy Products
To copy the Buy Products to the Get Products without having to re-type each part number, press F6-Copy Buys. All Buy Products automatically appear under Get Product.
18
For Get Qty, enter the number of the Get Product that is to be given free or discounted to the customer.
19
For Price, determine if special pricing applies to the Get Product.
•
N - Includes the Get Product on the sales order with a $0.00 selling price.
•
Y - Opens the Promotional Product Pricing screen to allow you to enter special pricing for the Get product as a set price or a percentage off of a basis field.
20
Press F2-Save to save the promotion matrix information.
Exclude/Include Customers/Products
•
To exclude customers from a promotion matrix or to create a list of customers that should be the only ones to receive a promotion matrix, refer to Excluding/Including Customers in a Promotion Matrix.
•
To exclude product lines from a promotion matrix, or to create a list of product lines that should be the only ones to receive a promotion matrix, refer to Excluding or Including Product Lines on a Matrix.
Entering a Promotional Product on a Sales Order
When you enter a sales order for a customer that qualifies for promotional products, the Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry displays when you save the Sales Order Header screen to list promotions currently available. The Line Item Detail screen for each promotion product that you enter also displays the Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry. PROMO displays at the bottom of the Line Item Detail screen to let you know that promotional pricing is in effect. The free products are automatically added to the order.
