Contract Price Maintenance screen
You can use the Contract Price Maintenance screen to identify special pricing for a customer by product and date range. Contract prices override all other prices set up for a customer. Once the contract pricing is entered, you can track the quantity of each product sold that received a contract price by customer ship-to address.
You can either create a customer contract within Price Matrix Maintenance (refer to Creating a Price Matrix) or use Contract Price Maintenance to automatically create a type 1 Customer/Product price matrix (refer to Creating a Customer Price Contract).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer assigned to the contract.
Contract #
The contract number to assign to the customer.
Start Date
The date when the prices take effect.
Expiration Date
The date the prices are no longer valid.
Product
The product covered by the contract.
Customer Part #
The customer’s part number for the product. If you enter a customer part number that does not exist (refer to Setting Up Customer Product Numbers), it is automatically created as a Customer Part Number.
Purchase Order #
The customer’s purchase order number associated with the contract. This field is for information only and does not update the price matrix.
Line Number
The line number to cross-reference for old contract information if you are converting data from a previous system. If you enter a value, the Customer Part Number is updated.
Minimum Qty
The minimum quantity the customer must buy on a sales order line item to receive the contract price. This value becomes the MinQty in the Price Matrix Maintenance screen.
Maximum Qty
The maximum quantity that the customer can buy to receive the contract price. This value becomes the MaxQty in the Price Matrix Maintenance screen.
Price
The contract price for the product if the price matrix uses the $ method. If you enter a price, it becomes the Price in the Price Matrix Maintenance screen.
Unit of Measure
The unit of measure for the product on this contract. This value is becomes the U/M in the Price Matrix Maintenance screen. This field cannot be changed after you press F2.
Est Qty Shipped
The estimated quantity that you think you might ship to the customer. This field is for information only and allows you to judge the effectiveness of the contract once the Act Qty Shipped is updated by orders shipped to the customer.
Act Qty Shipped
The quantity of the product shipped so far to the customer for this contract.
Total Price Ext
The total extended price of all sales order line items containing this product that shipped to the customer for this contract.
Total Cost Ext
The total extended cost of all sales order line items containing this product that shipped to the customer for this contract.
Last Ship Date
The last date this product shipped to the customer for this contract.
Last Ship Qty
The quantity of the product on the last shipment to the customer for this contract.
# Of Orders
The number of orders that shipped to the customer that contain this product for this contract.
ShipTo
The customer’s ship-to locations that are valid for this contract. These addresses are updated when the order is processed through the B Update if the ship-to on the order is not already included.
Description
The description of the ship-to location.
Bin
The number for the customer’s bin for the ship-to address (information only).
Bin Qty
Defines the quantity of the product the customer’s bin will hold at the corresponding ship-to location (information only).
Min Qty
The minimum quantity the customer wants to maintain in their bin. The value from the Minimum Qty in the top part of the screen is the default but may be overridden (information only).
Max Qty
The maximum quantity the customer wants to maintain in their bin. The value from the Maximum Qty in the top part of the screen is the default but may be overridden.
Qty-Shp
The quantity of this product shipped for this contract to the customer ship-to location.
Total Ext
The total extended price of all sales order line items for this product shipped for this contract to the customer ship-to location.
F2-Save
A type 1 price matrix is created or updated.
F4-Del
Deletes the contract matrix.
