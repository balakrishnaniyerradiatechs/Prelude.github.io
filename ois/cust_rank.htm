Customer Ranking Procedure screen
The Customer Ranking Procedure ranks customers, sales, and profit by company, selling warehouse, and/or territory. You must run this procedure to update the data on the Customer Ranking Report.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Customer Ranking Procedure
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
RANK BY (P)REVIOUS, (C)URRENT YEAR OR (L)ast 12 MONTHS?
Determines how to rank the customers:
•
P - By the previous year usage.
•
C - By the current year usage.
•
L - By the last 12 months usage.
Build Option - (C)ompany, (W)hse, (T)erritory, or (A)ll
Determines how to build customer ranking:
•
C - Company.
•
W - Warehouse.
•
T - Territory.
•
A - All.
