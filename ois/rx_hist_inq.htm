Prescription History
After a sales order is entered for a prescription (refer to Entering Prescription Products) you can view the status of open and invoiced sales orders for a customer prescription.
To view prescription history:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Physician/RX Maintenance > Customer Prescription Maintenance to open the Customer Prescription Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer to receive the prescription.
3
Enter Prescription #:
4
Press F5-Products > F7-History to open the Prescription History screen.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The prescription product number.
Qty
The quantity of the product prescribed on the initial prescription.
Refills
The number of refills available for this prescription.
Labels
The number of labels printed for this prescription.
Available
The quantity of the product still needed to fill the remaining refills of the prescription.
Open Order
The open sales orders entered for the product, that are not yet invoiced.
WB
The user ID of the person that entered the sales order.
Open Quantity
The quantity of the product on the open sales order.
Order Hist#
The sales order numbers for any orders already invoiced for this product.
Invoice#
The invoice numbers for any orders already invoiced for this product.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
WB
The user ID of the person that entered the sales order.
Ship Qty
The quantity of the product that shipped on the sales order.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
If the cursor is on an Open Order, the Sales Order Display screen opens to allow you to view detail information on the order.
If the cursor is on an Order Hist#, the Order History Inquiry Display screen opens to allow you to view detail information for the invoice.
