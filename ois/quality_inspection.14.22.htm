Quality Corrective Action Entry
Once a complaint is entered (refer to Recording a Product Complaint), you can use Quality Corrective Action Entry to assign the person responsible for the solution and to record the resolution of the problem.
To enter a corrective action:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quality Inspection Subsystem > Quality Corrective Action Entry to open the Quality Corrective Action Entry screen.
2
For Action#, choose from the following:
•
Press ENTER over NEW to create a new corrective action number.
•
Enter an existing corrective action number.
•
Press F3 to search for previously created corrective actions.
3
For Status, choose from the following:
•
O - Open.
•
C - Closed.
•
I - In process.
•
U - Unassigned.
•
W - Waiting for response.
4
If an incident is already entered for this problem (refer to Quality Incident Entry) enter the Incident# that this corrective action is intended to resolve. Press F3 to search for open incidents. You can leave this blank if the incident is not yet created.
5
Enter the quality Agent responsible for the corrective action (refer to Creating Quality Agent Codes).
6
For Title, enter a brief summary title or description of the corrective action.
7
For Description, enter any additional information or further explanation of the corrective action taken to solve the problem.
8
Press F2-Save to save the corrective action information.
