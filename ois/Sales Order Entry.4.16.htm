Re-Pricing an Entire Sales Order
In addition to using price matrices and or contract pricing to price a customer’s orders (refer to the Product Pricing), you can change prices or gross profit amounts for an entire sales order.
To access the following re-price options, select Reprice Order Options from the Action Bar on the Sales Order Entry screen.
Customer’s Regular Price
Re-prices the entire order to the customer’s regular price that they would normally get before any additional discounts are applied. Refer to Product Pricing for information on how customer prices are set up.
Gross Margin Percentage
Allows you to enter a gross margin to re-price the entire order to earn a gross margin percent. If you want to make a 20% profit margin, enter 20 for Reprice Percent.
Less Discount
Allows you to take an additional discount off of each line item. Enter the discount amount in Reprice Percent, then for Reprice Percent Method, choose one of the following prices levels to apply the discount to:
•
S - Suggested retail.
•
C - Customer’s default price column.
•
N - Current sales order line net price.
Cost Plus
Reprices the entire order by adding a percentage to the cost to calculate the price. If you want to sell the products at cost plus 25%, enter 25 for Reprice Percent.
Minimum Gross Margin
Automatically re-prices each line item to the minimum gross margin allowed for its product line. This value is defined in Product Line Maintenance > Gross Profit Test? = Y > G/P Lower Percent (refer to Product Line Maintenance in the Inventory documentation). Only products with a G/P Lower Percent in Product Line Maintenance are re-priced.
Column Pricing
Re-prices the entire order to use a column price. Enter the Reprice Column. If the column that you choose is zero for an item, the price remains unchanged.
Sum & Reprice
Uses the sum & reprice method to reprice the order. Sum & reprice allows you to combine products in a defined group and give a discount if the total number of items purchased in the group meets a quantity break level. Separately these products may not meet the quantity break price but combined, they meet a discount break level. Refer to Sum & Reprice and Quantity Break Discounts.
Pricing By Square Footage
For rental orders only. Calculates a rental charge based on the Square Footage Factor and the Rental Retail Value from the Rental Module screen in Product Maintenance.
Increase/Decrease Percent
Re-prices the line items by a percent increase or a percent decrease. For Increase/Decrease, enter either I to increase the price or D to decrease the price. Then for Reprice Percent, enter the percent by which you want to increase or decrease the price of the products on the order.
