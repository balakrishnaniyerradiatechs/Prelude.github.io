Monthly Credit Card Register
The Monthly Credit Card Register lists credit card sales invoiced for a month or fiscal period. Refer to Credit Cards for information on credit cards.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Credit Card Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period for which you want to view credit card sales.
•
Sort By Credit Card Type. Determines how the report is sorted:
-
Y - Sort and subtotal the report by credit card type.
-
N - Sort by invoice date.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Order#
The sales order number.
Inv Date
The day the credit card sale was invoiced. Refer to Pre-Authorizing a Sales Order.
Customer
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Type
The credit card type (e.g., VISA, M/C, American Express). Refer to Assigning Credit Bureau Codes.
Credit Card Number
The credit card number; only the last 4 digits display depending on user ID settings (refer to Setting up Your System for Credit Cards).
Exp Date
The expiration date on the credit card.
Auth#
The credit authorization number.
Card Amount
The amount post authorized to the credit card.
