Change ‘All’ Sales Rep’s screen
The Change All Sales Reps screen allows you to change the salesrep who will receive commission on an order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Maintenance > F6-Chg All Rep’s
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
From Sales Rep
The salesrep currently assigned to the invoice and who will be paid commission.
To Sales Rep
The number for the salesrep that should received commission for the sale.
F2-Save
Saves the new salesrep information.
