Notice of Furnishing Report
The Notice of Furnishing report lists customer jobs for each state that furnished a notice to ensure that lien rights are maintained. The letter of furnishing is required by construction labor laws in some states.
Print Furnishing must be set to Y in Customer Job Maintenance > F6-Lien/Bond for this customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Lien / Bond Reports > Notice Of Furnishing Report
