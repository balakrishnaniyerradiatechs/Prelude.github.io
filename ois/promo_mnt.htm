Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen
The Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen allows you to set up price promotions to give customers discounts or free products. You can give a discount based on their order total or give away free products based on a product (e.g., buy one, get one free). You can also include or exclude customers from a promotion. You should review promotions periodically because if a product has more than one type of promotion, the first matrix that was set up is used. If you use the Marketing module, you can also use promotional pricing to work with Marketing Campaigns.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Plan #
The plan number for the promotional price matrix.
Matrix Type
Determines the type of promotion:
•
1 - Product. Refer to Creating a Promotion Price Matrix by Product.
•
2 - Order. Refer to Creating a Promotion Price Matrix by Order Total.
Promotion Type
If you chose 1 for Matrix Type, this field designates how the free products are given:
•
1 - Same as. Buy one product get one free.
•
2 - Mix and match. Buy a product or combination of products to get a promotional product for free.
•
3 - Specific buy, specific get. Buy one or more of a product to get a promotional product or group of products for free.
Buy Product
If you chose 1 for Matrix Type, enter the product the customer must by to earn the free product.
Get Product
The product the customer will receive if they purchase the Buy Product.
Get Qty
The quantity of the Get Product that the customer will receive for free.
Price
Determines if special pricing applies to the Get Product:
•
N - The Get Product is free.
•
Y - Opens the Promotional Product Pricing screen to allow you to enter special pricing for the Get product. You can either enter a price or a percentage off of a basis field.
F2-Save
Saves the promotion matrix information.
F4-Del
Deletes the promotion matrix.
F5-Hdr
Opens the Promotion Matrix Maintenance Header screen to allow you to enter additional information for the promotion.
F6-Copy Buys
Copies all of the products listed under Buy Product to display under Get Product.
F7-Cust Exc
Opens the Promotion Exception Maintenance screen to allow you to include or exclude a customer from a promotion matrix.
F8-Prd Exc
Opens the Promotion Product Exception Maintenance screen to allow you to include or exclude product lines from a promotion matrix.
F9-Goto
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
