Customer Backorder Inquiry
The Customer Backorder Inquiry lists open backorders for a customer.
Viewing Customer Backorders
To list backorders for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Backorder Inquiry to open the Customer Backorder Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number.
Open backorders for the customer appear.
