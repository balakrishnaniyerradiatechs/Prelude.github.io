Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen
When a vendor notifies you that a customer placed an order, you can enter the manufacturer’s rep order. Depending on when the vendor notifies you of the shipment and when the commission is paid, the order can be entered and completed all at once, or the whole process can be completed in three separate entries into the order. For example, when you enter the order you can:
•
Record the acknowledgement of the order.
•
Enter the shipped quantity information.
•
Record the commission paid to you by the vendor.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturers Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The number assigned to the manufacturer’s rep order.
Vendor
The vendor that will pay you the commission for the material sold to the customer.
Product
The products ordered by the customer.
Description
The description of the product.
Qty-Opn
The quantity ordered by the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Net Price
The price that the customer paid for the product.
Comm Dollars
The total amount of commission that you should receive for the products sold on this order.
S/O Dollars
The total amount that the customer owes the vendor for the order.
F2-Save
Saves the information and allows you to suspend, complete, or return to the order.
