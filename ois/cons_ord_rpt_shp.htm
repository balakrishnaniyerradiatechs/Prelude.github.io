Consignment Order Release Report By Ship Whse
The Consignment Order Release Report By Shipping Whse displays released consignment order information for a shipping warehouse. Consignment orders contain products that are on loan to the customer. If the customer sells the product, then the customer is invoiced for the products sold. If the customer does not sell the products, they can be returned back to stock. Refer to Using Consignment Sales Orders for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Consignment Reports > Consignment Report by Shipping Whse
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The shipping warehouse for the consignment orders.
•
Customer. The customer holding the consigned merchandise.
•
Order Date. The date the customer ordered the consigned merchandise.
•
(A)cct/(C)omm Cost. The type of cost to use:
-
A - Accounting cost.
-
C - Commission cost.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust #
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Slsm
The sales representative assigned to the sales order.
Ord Date
The date the consignment order was placed.
Order #
The sales order number.
#
The number of times the order went through the invoice cycle.
P
Indicates the pick ticket print status:
•
S - The order was suspended and no pick ticket printed.
•
P - The product is available and a pick ticket will print during the next print cycle or when the date to print is reached.
•
Blank - The pick ticket is not ready to print.
I
Indicates the order’s invoice status:
•
Y - The order is flagged for invoicing.
•
H - The order is on hold and cannot be invoiced until released. Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
•
Blank - The order is not yet flagged for invoicing.
Product #
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Curr
The currency indicator code assigned to the order, if you use multiple currency.
Open
The quantity of the product on the consignment order.
UOM
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Cost Ext
The extended cost value of the total quantity of the product (Open x unit cost).
Price Ext
The extended price value of the total quantity of the product (Open x unit Price).
