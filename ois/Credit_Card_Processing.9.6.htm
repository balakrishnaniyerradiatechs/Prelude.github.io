Assigning Credit Card Information to a Customer
To set up a customer to use default credit card information:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance Screen.
2
For Payment Terms you can enter a default payment terms code that applies to credit cards (refer to Setting up Credit Card Payment Terms). When you enter a sales order for this customer, you must enter the credit card information if it does not already exist.
You do not have to assign a credit card Payment Terms to a customer if they don’t always pay by credit card. You can enter any other payment terms for the customer, and then if they decide to pay for an order by credit card, you must choose Complete when you save the sales order. You can change the payment terms and then enter or use the default credit card information.
3
Select F6-A/R > F7-Credit Card to open the Credit Card Information Screen.
4
For Suppress CC Save Prompt, determine if you can update credit card information when you use a new credit card on a sales order:
•
Y - Do not allow new credit card information to update Customer Maintenance.
•
N - When you complete a sales order for this customer, a message appears asking if you want to update the customer’s record with the new credit card information.
5
For Card Type, enter the credit card company (e.g., Visa, AMEX). These are set in Credit Bureau Maintenance. Refer to Assigning Credit Bureau Codes.
6
Enter the customer’s credit Card Number.
7
For Valid From, enter the beginning month and year that the credit card is valid (e.g., 0107 for January 2007).
8
For Expiration, enter the month and year the credit card expires.
9
For Default, indicate if this is the default credit card to use for the customer:
•
Y - This is the default credit card.
•
N - This is not the default credit card.
You can only have one default card for a customer.
10
If you use AVS (Address Verification System) to verify the address of a credit card holder, press F5-Bill-To Info to open the Bill To Information Screen. You can enter the customer’s billing address for verification purposes.
If Default AVS For Credit Card in Additional Option Parameters is set to Y, this information defaults when you pre-authorize the sales order (refer to Pre-Authorizing a Sales Order).
11
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the customer credit card information.
