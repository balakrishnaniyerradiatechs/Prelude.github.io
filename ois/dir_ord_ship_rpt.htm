Direct Ship Lines to Invoice Report
The Direct Ship Lines To Invoice report lists direct ship orders that are ready to confirm for invoicing. These sales orders are set up when the vendor invoice is reconciled in Accounts Payable or received in Direct Ship Receipt Entry.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Direct Ship Lines To Invoice
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse. The selling warehouse is the warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse ships the merchandise.
•
Written By. The user ID of the person who entered the order.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/Name
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Rep
The number of the sales representative assigned to the sales order.
Ord-Date
The date the order was entered.
Order#
The sales order number.
I
Indicates the order’s invoice status:
•
Y - The order is flagged for invoicing.
•
H - The order is on hold and cannot be invoiced until released. Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
•
Blank - The order is not yet flagged for invoicing.
P/O#
The linked purchase order number.
Ln#
The line on the purchase order that is ready for invoicing.
Product Number/Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Qty-Opn
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Qty-Rec
The quantity that the vendor shipped to your customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Price/Ext.
Line 1: The unit price of line item.
Line 2: The extended price of the total quantity of the line item that was received (Qty-Rec x Price).
Cost/Ext.
Line 1: The unit cost of the line item.
Line 2: The extended cost of the total quantity of the line item that was received (Qty-Rec x Cost).
