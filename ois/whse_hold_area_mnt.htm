Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen
If you use warehouse hold areas for Tag & Hold sales orders, after you enter the order (refer to Entering a Tag & Hold Sales Order), you can use the Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen to assign a hold area. When the first Tag & Hold order prints, it instructs the warehouse personnel to pick the material and place it in the warehouse hold area. When the customer is ready for the material, the pick ticket that prints instructs the warehouse personnel that the material is in the hold area.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Hold Area Assignment
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Warehouse
The shipping warehouse for the order.
Hold Area Number
The hold area where you want the order to be held. Refer to Creating a Warehouse Hold Area.
Order#
The sales order number to assign to the hold area.
Customer
The customer assigned to the order.
Description
The customer name.
F2-Save
Saves the hold area assignment.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
