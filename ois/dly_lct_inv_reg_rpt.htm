Daily Lease Contract Invoice Register
The Daily Lease Contract Invoice Register lists all lease contract invoices for a warehouse and day, including the sales tax, payment, and invoice amounts. A lease contract order is a type of master invoice used to bill a customer based on an installment plan. The orders are designated by a Lease Contract Payment Terms Code in the Sales Order Header (Order Additional Information Screen). Refer to Printing Installment Invoice Memos for information on generating a reminder notice for installment invoices.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Lease Contract Invoice Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Invoice Date. The date the lease contract was invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice#
The number of the invoice billed to the customer.
Inv Date
The date the customer was invoiced.
Cust#
The customer on the lease contract.
Description
The customer description.
Tax Amt
The amount of tax included in the invoice.
Payment Amt
The amount due from the customer for this month, including interest and other charges.
Invoice Amt
The amount of the invoice for this month, including interest and other charges.
Currency
Currency code, if you use multiple currencies.
