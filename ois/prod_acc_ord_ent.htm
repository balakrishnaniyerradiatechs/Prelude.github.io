Accessory Product Order Selection screen
The Accessory Product Order Selection screen appears when you enter a product that has accessory products associated with it (Refer to “Accessory Products” in the Inventory documentation for information on setting up accessories). You can view the list of accessories and select the ones to add to the order. Refer to Adding Accessory Products to an Order for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > enter a product that has accessories
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Number
The original product on the sales order that has accessory products assigned to it.
Product Number
The accessory products that can be sold with the product entered on the sales order.
Description
The description of the accessory product.
Available
The quantity of the accessory product that is available for your sales order.
Surplus
The surplus quantity of the product that is available. The surplus quantity is the available amount of the product that is above the recommended quantity to keep in stock.
Quantity
The quantity of the accessory product to add to your sales order.
Script
Any comments or sales tips regarding the accessory product. This information is set up in Product Maintenance for the original product to help you sell the accessory product to the customer.
F2-Accept
Adds the selected quantities of accessory products to the sales order.
