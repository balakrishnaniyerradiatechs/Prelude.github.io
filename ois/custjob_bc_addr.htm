Bond Company Information Screen
If a bond company is connected with the job, you can enter the bond company address and phone number in the Bond Company Information screen.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > Action Bar > Add’l Opts > Bond Company Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Bond Company
The name of the company holding the bond.
Address Line
The address for the bond company.
City
The bond company’s city.
State
The bond company’s state.
Zip
The zip code.
Phone Number
The bond company’s phone number.
F2-Save
Saves the bond company information.
