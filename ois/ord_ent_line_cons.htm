Consignment Order Detail screen
You can use the Consignment Order Detail screen to manually bill a customer for a product on an 08 consignment order, or to return the product to stock. Each line item on a consignment order can be billed or returned to stock or a combination of both. Refer to Manually Billing Consignment Orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > 08 Consignment Order > Line Item
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number on the order.
Quantity
The quantity of the product originally sent to the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the sales order.
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the material.
A
Accept:
•
Y - Everything for the line is correct. No changes required.
•
N - Changes required for one or more of the following fields.
Qty-Shp
The quantity for which you want to invoice the customer.
Qty-Stk
The quantity to return to stock.
Gross Price
The gross price of the product.
Disc%
The discount percentage applied to the Gross-Price.
Net Price
The net price of the product (Gross-Price x Disc%).
Src
The source code that indicates where the price came from. Refer to Price Source Codes for a listing of codes.
Tx?
Indicates if the product is taxable:
•
Y - The product is taxable.
•
N - The product is tax exempt. A customer’s tax exempt status is set up in the Customer Maintenance Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen, the Customer Ship-To Maintenance Ship-To Tax Information Screen, or in Product Warehouse Maintenance.
Extension
The extended price for the total quantity shipped of the product (Qty-Shp x Net Price).
Cost
The cost of the product.
Gp%
The gross profit percentage earned on this line item.
Profit
The total gross profit amount earned for this product.
Available
The quantity of the item available to sell for this warehouse.
Onorder
The quantity of the product on order for this warehouse.
Stock Status
The stocking status of the product:
•
S - Stock.
•
N - Nonstock.
•
C - Consignment.
F2-Save
Saves the information in the Consignment Order Detail screen and returns to the Sales Order Entry screen.
F5-Add Info
Opens the Additional Information screen to allow you to view additional detail information for the product and customer.
F6-Substitutes
Opens the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen to allow you to view information on available substitutes for the product.
F9-Invty Inq
Opens the Inventory Inquiry screen to allow you to view stock status, pricing information, and linked order information for the product.
