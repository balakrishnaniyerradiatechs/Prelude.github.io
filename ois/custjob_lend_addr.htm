Lender Information Screen
If a lender is involved with the job, you can enter the lender address and phone number in the Lender Information screen.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > Action Bar > Add’l Opts > Lender Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Lender Name
The name of the company or person lending money for the job.
Address Line
The lender’s address.
City
The lender’s city.
State
The lender’s state.
Zip
The zip code.
Phone
The lender’s phone number.
F2-Save
Saves the lender information.
