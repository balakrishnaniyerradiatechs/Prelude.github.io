Selecting a Customer
When you enter a sales order, you must first identify the correct customer and ship-to address for the order.
To begin a sales order:
1
Select Order Processing > Sales Order Entry to open the Sales Order Entry screen.
Location
The Location for your user ID determines the warehouses for which you can enter sales orders. If you have access to more than one warehouse, the Location Retrieval screen appears first. Enter the appropriate Selling Warehouse, Department, and/or Cost Center.
2
For Order Number choose one of the following:
•
To start a new sales order, press ENTER. A new sales order number is assigned.
•
To retrieve the last order you entered in this session, press F8.
•
To edit an existing order, enter the sales order number or press F3 to search for the order by customer. The Sales Order Entry screen appears. Go to Entering Sales Order Line Items.
3
To select a Customer, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the customer number.
•
Enter a customer keyword, part of the customer’s name, or other search criteria defined by your company and press ENTER. If a list of names appears, select the correct name.
•
Enter CASH to open the Cash Customer Information screen. Enter the name and address for a cash customer or enter a phone number to search for an existing cash customer by phone number.
Depending on your system settings, the Customer Verification or the Customer Ship-To Verification screen may appear.
Customer Verification Screens
Verify Customer in SOE in Order Processing Parameters determines if the Customer Verification or the Customer Ship-To Verification screen appears:
•
N - By-pass the customer verification screens.
•
Y - Display the customer’s name and address from Customer Maintenance and require verification.
•
S - Display the customer ship-to name and address from Customer Ship-To Maintenance and require verification.
4
For Is This The Correct Ship-To (Y/N)?, choose from the following:
•
Y - If it is the correct ship-to address.
•
N - If it is not the correct ship-to address. Choose from the following:
-
Enter another ship-to address account number.
-
Enter D to duplicate the customer’s main address from Customer Maintenance.
-
Enter P if the customer is picking up the order.
-
Enter N to open the Customer Ship-To Maintenance Screen and to create a new ship-to account.
-
Enter M to open the Ship-To Address screen and enter a manual address for one time use.
-
Enter C to use an address from Contact Maintenance.
Once you select the correct customer/customer ship-to address, the Sales Order Header screen appears. Go to Entering Sales Order Header Information.
Credit Code in the Customer Maintenance A/R Information Screen determines if orders can be entered for this customer if they are on credit hold.
