Universal Customer Replacement Merge
The procedures on the Universal Customer Replacement/Merge Menu allow you to either change a customerâ€™s account number or to merge all the information from 2 customer accounts into 1 account.
All files that include the customer number fields are updated (custom files and field will require a custom modification) and sales information in the customer file is updated. Once the procedure is complete, the original customer account number is no longer a valid customer number in your system.
Use the following steps to merge/change customer information.
1
Use Universal Customer Change Merge/Entry to indicate the From Customer whose information will be moved and the To Customer that will receive the information (refer to Merging/Replacing Customer Accounts).
2
Preview the customer numbers that will be changed/merged (Run the Universal Customer Replacement /Merge Preview Report).
3
Run the update to move the customer information. This process is not reversible (refer to Universal Customer Replacement /Merge Update).
4
Review the information that was changed (refer to the Customer Replacement / Merge Audit Report).
5
Clear the cross reference file (refer to Clearing the Cross Reference File) so that new entries made be made in the Universal Change/Merge Entry.
