Printing Shipping Labels
If you attach shipping address labels to send shipments to your customer, you can use Shipping Label Printing to print labels for confirmed releases.
To print shipping labels:
1
Select Order Processing > Batch Ticket Printing > Shipping Label Printing to open the Shipping Label Printing screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse Number where the labels are to print.
3
Enter the Sales Order Number for which you want to print labels.
4
Enter the Number Of Labels to print for the shipment.
5
For Printer Form Queue, enter the printer number where the labels are to print. Shipping labels will print immediately to the designated printer.
