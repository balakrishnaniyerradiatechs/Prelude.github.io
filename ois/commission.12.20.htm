Splitting Commission Between Salesreps
If you want to split the total commission on a sales order between two or more sales reps, you can use the Commission Splits on the sales order header.
To split the salesrep commission on a sales order:
1
Select Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > F5-Hdr Scr > Action Bar > Add Info Comm Splits to open the Commission Splits screen (refer to Entering a Sales Order for more information on entering a sales order).
2
For Rep, enter the first salesrep to receive commission for this sales order.
3
For Rate, enter the percentage of the total commission that this salesrep should receive on this sales order.
4
Continue to enter the other salesreps that are to split the commission on this order. The total of the Rate column must equal 100 before you can exit the screen.
5
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the commission information and to return to the Sales Order Entry screen.
