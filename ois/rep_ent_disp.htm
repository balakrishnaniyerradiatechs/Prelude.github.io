M/R Display screen
The M/R Display screen or M/R History Display is accessible by pressing the F5-Review from any of the Manufacturer Rep Inquiries. You can view detail shipping and commission information for each item on a manufacturerâ€™s rep order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturers Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Inquiries > Manufacturer Rep Inquiry > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
M/R Order#
The manufacturer rep order number
Vendor Number
The number and description of the vendor that is paying commission for the product sold to the customer.
Product
The product purchased by the customer from the vendor.
Description
The product description.
Quantity
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped by the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Qty-B/O
The quantity of the product not yet shipped to the customer.
Net Price
The price the customer paid for the product.
Comm-%
The commission percent (of Net Price) that the vendor will pay you for the sale of this product to the customer.
Comm-Rate
Not applicable.
Qty-Paid
The quantity for which the vendor has paid commission to you.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the M/R Order Header screen to allow you to view customer information for the order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number or reference number to go directly to.
