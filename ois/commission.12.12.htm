Setting Commission Rates for a Product
You can use Product Maintenance to set up commission rates for a product that overrides commission rates set up for salesreps. This commission rate is overridden by any matrices set up in Commission Matrix Maintenance.
To set up commission rates for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product for which you want to enter commission overrides.
3
Press F6-Xref/Notes.
4
Enter Y in Commission to open the Commissions screen.
Regular Sales refers to orders shipped from your warehouse to the customer. Direct Sales refers to orders shipped from the vendor directly to the customer.
5
For SlsRep #1 Comm Type, enter the commission type for the Salesrep One on the sales order header. Choose from the following:
•
$ - The sales rep receives a dollar amount per unit sold.
•
* - The sales rep receives a percent of sales.
•
G - The sales rep receives a percent of gross profit.
6
For Commission $,%, enter either the dollar amount or the percent of sales or gross profit that determines the sales rep’s commission.
7
If applicable, enter the same information fields for Slsrep #2 Comm Type and Written By Comm Type. Written by is the user ID of the person who entered the order.
8
Repeat steps 5-7 for Direct Sales orders.
9
Press F2-Save to save the commission information.
