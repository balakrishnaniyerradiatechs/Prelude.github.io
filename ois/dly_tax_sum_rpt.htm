Daily Sales Tax Summary Report
The Daily Sales Tax Summary report displays the total amount of taxable and non taxable merchandise invoiced on sales orders for a day.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Sales Tax Reports > Sales Tax Summary
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Invoice Date. The date the sales orders were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Jur
The tax jurisdiction code. These codes are set up in the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen and then assigned to customers in the Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen and/or the Customer Job Sales Tax Maintenance Screen. You can override a tax jurisdiction in the Sales Order Header Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen.
Description
The description of the tax jurisdiction location.
Applicable
The total value of merchandise (Taxable + Nontaxable).
Taxable
The total taxable merchandise for the corresponding tax jurisdiction.
Nontaxable
The total amount of tax exempt merchandise for the tax jurisdiction.
Special Tax
%
An override percent for special handling charges, if a sales tax matrix is in effect.
Taxable
The total value of the merchandise taxable at the special tax rate.
Tax Charged
The total amount of tax charged for the tax jurisdiction.
