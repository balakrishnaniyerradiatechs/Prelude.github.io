Monthly Gross Profit Report by Sell Warehouse
The Monthly Gross Profit Report by Sell Warehouse displays the gross profit amount and percent for all sales orders invoiced for a selling warehouse for a month or fiscal period.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Gross Profit Reports > By Selling Warehouse
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period in which the sales orders were invoiced.
•
G/P Based On Acct/Comm Cost. Determines if the gross profit is calculated based on accounting or commission cost:
-
A - Accounting cost.
-
C - Commission cost.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice#
The customer’s invoice number.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise on the invoice.
Other Chgs
The total of charges other than merchandise on the invoice (e.g., freight, handling charges, tax).
Inv Amount
The total invoice amount (Mdse-Amt + Other Chgs).
Cost
The total cost of goods sold on the invoice.
Gross Profit
The total gross profit amount earned on the invoice.
G/P%
The total gross profit percent earned on the invoice.
