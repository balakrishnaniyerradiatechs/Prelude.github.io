Open Manufacture Rep Order Detail Report
The Open Manufacture Order Detail Report lists detail information for manufacturer’s rep orders that were entered but not yet updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > M/R Open Order Detail Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse that received credit for the sale.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include on the report.
•
Customer. The customers to include on the report.
•
Salesrep. The sales representatives to include on the report.
•
Entry Date range. The beginning and ending date range for which you would like to view open Manufacturer Rep orders.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The number for the vendor that will pay you commission.
Vendor Name
The vendor description.
Cust#
The customer that ordered the material from the vendor.
Cust-P/O#
The customer’s purchase order number.
Rep1
The sales representative that will receive credit for the order.
Ord-Num
The manufacture rep order number.
Ent-Date
The date the order was entered in your system.
Product Number
The product ordered by the customer.
Description
The description of the product.
Qty-Opn
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Net-Price
The price the customer paid for the product.
Ext-Amt
The total extended price of the product ordered by the customer (Qty-Opn x Net Price).
Rate
The percent of Net-Price that the vendor will pay you for the sale of this product.
Comm-Amt
The amount of commission that you should expect to receive from the vendor for the sale of this product (Ext-Amt x Rate/100).
