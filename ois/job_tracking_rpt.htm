Monthly Job Tracking Report
The Monthly Job Tracking report displays draw and waiver information for a customer job.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Monthly Job Tracking
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customer whose jobs you want to view.
•
Job Number. The job number if you only want to view one job.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust #
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Job Number
The customer’s job number that identifies a group of orders.
Initial Draw
The total amount that orders using this job number can accumulate to, before orders will go on credit hold for this customer.
Ent-Date
The date the job was created.
Current Draw
The dollars already used for this job.
Waiver Amt
Partial lien amount if you charge a damage waiver when renting to this customer.
Waiver
The date the partial lien was charged.
Notice Order Num
 
The sales order number if a notice of furnishing was sent for the job. Refer to the Notice of Furnishing Report.
Ship Dt
The date the notice of furnishing was sent.
