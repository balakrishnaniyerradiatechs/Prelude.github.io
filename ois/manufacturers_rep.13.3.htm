Completing a Manufacturers’s Rep Order
Once a vendor pays you all or part of the commission for shipped quantities on a Manufacturer’s Rep Order (refer to Entering a Manufacturer’s Rep Order), you can complete the order and record the amount of commission you received from the vendor.
To complete a manufacturer’s rep order:
1
For each line on the manufacturer’s rep order that you want to complete, press ENTER to open the M/R Detail Information screen.
If you previously recorded the shipping information, Qty-Shp, Net Price Comm%, Ship Date are already be filled in. If this is the first time you are entering the order and you are recording all the information at once, enter data in all of the fields.
2
For Paid Qty, enter the quantity for which the vendor is paying you commission. Act-Comm updates to the calculated amount that the vendor is paying you (Paid Qty x Net Price x Comm%/ 100). You can override the amount if necessary.
3
Press F2-Save to save the commission information and to return to the Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen.
4
Repeat steps 1-3 for each item for which you have received a commission payment.
5
After you update the paid quantities on the Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen, press F2-Save and choose Complete to open the M/R Completion screen.
The shipped Merchandise Amount, Expected Commission, and Actual Commission for the order displays.
6
Enter the Check Number from the manufacturer.
7
Enter the Check Date from the check.
8
Enter the Check Amount.
9
Press F2-Save.
10
If the Check Amount does not equal the Actual Commission, the following message appears:
The Check Amount Does Not Equal The Commission Amount!! Do You Wish To...
Choose from the following:
•
Return. Allows you to return to the M/R Completion screen to change either the check amount or return to the order to change the Act-Comm.
•
Allocate. Adjusts the commission amount and distributes the difference to each line on the order.
11
After you either change the amounts or allocate the difference, the following message appears:
Are You Ready To Process This Order?
Choose from the following.
•
Yes. Completes the order and sets it up for updating (refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order).
•
No. Suspends the order until you return to the order and choose to process it.
