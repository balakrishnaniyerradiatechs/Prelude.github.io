Manually Placing Orders on Administrative Order Hold
You can manually place a sales order on Administrative Order Hold either from within Sales Order Entry or from Manual Administrative Hold.
To manually place an order on Administrative Order Hold within a sales order:
1
From the Sales Order Entry screen Action Bar, select Order Func > Manual Administrative Order Hold.
2
Enter Y for Manual Administrative Hold.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the sales order.
4
After you save the order, it goes on hold and the pick ticket does not print until the order is released by the Administrative Order Manager. You have the option to enter a message to the manager or to enter notes regarding the order.
 
To manually place an order on hold using Manual Administrative Hold:
1
Select Order Processing > Processes > Manual Admin Hold Procedure to open the Manual Administrative Hold screen.
2
For Order Number, enter the sales order number to place on hold.
3
For Order or Invoice Hold, enter O to place the order on Administrative Order Hold.
4
Press F2-Update to complete the hold.
