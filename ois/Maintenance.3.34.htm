Creating a Customer Prescription
Before you can enter a prescription product on a sales order (refer to Entering Prescription Products), you must set up a prescription number that identifies the customer, physician, products, quantities, refills, and expiration date information.
To set up a prescription for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Physician/RX Maintenance > Customer Prescription Maintenance to open the Customer Prescription Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer to receive the prescription
3
For Prescription #, choose from the following:
•
Enter NEW to assign the next system generated number (refer to Setting Prescription and Facility Counter Numbers).
•
Enter any prescription number as determined by your company.
•
Enter an existing prescription number to view or to track refill information.
•
Press F3 to search for existing prescriptions for this customer.
4
Enter a Ship To account number if you want the prescription to be limited to just one ship-to account set up for the customer. The labels will print the address information.
5
For Issued, enter the date the physician issued the prescription.
6
For Expiration, enter the date the prescription expires. The default is the date set up for each state in Maximum RX Month Maintenance (refer to Identifying Maximum RX Months by State). If no default was set up for the state, the system default is 6 months
Status
The Status of the prescription depends on if today’s date is before or after the expiration date:
•
Open - The prescription is not yet expired.
•
Closed - The prescription is expired.
7
Enter any Comment to include with the prescription, if applicable.
8
For Contact, enter the name of the person who the prescription is for.
9
Enter the Renewal date of the prescription.
10
For Phoned, enter the date the physician was contacted for renewal.
11
For Phone Ok’d, enter Y if the prescription was phoned in and confirmed.
12
Enter the Species that the prescription is for. Only valid species may be entered (refer to Creating Valid Species Responses).
13
Enter the Physician that issued the prescription. The address and other information set up for the physician will default into the remaining fields (refer to Setting up Physician Information).
14
To enter the products included in the prescription, press F5-Products to open the Customer Prescription Product Maintenance screen.
15
Enter the Product number on the prescription. This number must be set up as a prescription product (refer to Identifying a Product as a Prescription).
16
For Qty, enter the quantity of the product to fill the prescription with.
17
Enter the number of Refills allowed for this prescription.
18
Enter the number of Labels to print for the prescription.
The Available quantity represents the quantity needed to fill the prescription (e.g., the original quantity plus the number of refill quantities.
Qty + (Qty x Refills).
19
The Notes Entry screen appears to display any instructions from the vet set up for the RX Group. You can add or edit the instructions. If you change the instructions here, it does not affect the instructions set up for the RX Group.
20
Press F2-Save to return to the Customer Prescription Product Maintenance screen. An asterisk (*) appears under Instr if instructions exist for this product line.
21
Press F2-Save to return to the Prescription Customer Maintenance screen.
22
Once everything is correct, press F2-Save to complete the prescription.
23
Choose from following:
•
To enter a sales order for the prescription, refer to Entering Prescription Products.
•
To view product quantities used and available, refer to Prescription Quantities.
•
To view the open and invoiced sales orders for this customer prescription, refer to Prescription History.
