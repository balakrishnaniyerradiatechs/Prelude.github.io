Monthly Cash Sales Register
The Monthly Cash Sales Register lists all 05 sales orders for the month for which the customer paid cash. You can view the amount of each invoice including the merchandise price and cost, extra charges, taxes, and gross profit amounts.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Cash Sales Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Fiscal Period. The fiscal period for which you want to view cash sales.
•
Include Credit Card Sales. Indicates if credit card sales are included:
-
Y - Include credit card sales.
-
N - Exclude credit card sales.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Inv/Order#
Line 1: The cash sale invoice number.
Line 2: The sales order number.
Inv Date
The date the cash sale was entered.
Cust#
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Disc-All
The total order discount amount allowed as shown in Disc/Allowance in the Sales Order Completion screen.
Misc-Chgs
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Refer to Entering a Miscellaneous Product.
Frt/Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that was sold.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was sold.
Min/Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise sold.
Dep-Amt
The current deposit balance. This amount represents the initial deposit placed on a sales order.
Cash-Amt
The total amount of the cash sale, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost
The total cost of the merchandise sold on the order.
G/P%
The gross profit percentage earned for the products sold.
