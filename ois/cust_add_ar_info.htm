Additional A/R Information Screen
The Additional A/R Information screen allows you to enter statement and other accounts receivable information for the customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F5-Add Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Print Statement
Determines the type of statements to print for this customer:
•
Y - Print open item statements.
•
B - Print balance forward statements that include past due balances.
•
N - Do not print statements.
If you change the statement type from B to Y, the balance forward statement is deleted.
If you change the statement type from N or Y to B, the balance forward statement is created when you press F2-Save if you use the Month End A/R Save.
Statement Document Option
Determines how statements are transmitted to the customer:
•
B - Mail and fax.
•
E - Email only.
•
M - Mail and email.
•
N - Mail only.
•
Y - Fax only.
Print Credits/Stmts
Determines if the customer statement includes credit memos:
•
Y - Include credit memos.
•
N - Do not include credit memos.
Detail A/R
Determines if the customer’s open invoices display in Cash Receipts Entry:
•
Y - A list of open invoices are maintained for inquiries and for cash receipts.
•
N - The list of open invoices does not display in Cash Receipts Entry. You must enter the invoice number when receiving cash, instead of choosing from a list of open invoices. You may want to choose this option if the customer has a large number of open invoices (e.g., over 500).
Unauth Cash Disc Taken
Indicates if this customer has taken unauthorized cash discounts:
•
Y - The customer took unauthorized cash discounts.
•
N - The customer did not take unauthorized cash discounts.
This flag is automatically updated from cash receipts but can be overridden.
Personal Guarantee
Indicates if this customer offers a personal guarantee that open balances will be paid:
•
Y - The customer offers a personal guarantee.
•
N - The customer does not offer a personal guarantee.
Checks Accepted
Indicates if you can accept checks from this customer for cash transactions:
•
Y - Checks are accepted for cash sale payments.
•
N - Checks are not accepted for cash sales.
Dep Req’d For B/O
Indicates if you should collect a full deposit for backordered items:
•
Y - The customer must pay the full price of backordered products before the sales order can be completed. You may want to use this if a customer has previously ordered nonstock merchandise and then cancelled the order.
•
N - You do not have to collect the full deposit for backordered items.
Credit Collections Type
The credit collection type that defines the customer’s payment habits. These types are established in Credit Collections Type Maintenance in the Accounts Receivable Credit Collections Subsystem to group customer for collection (e.g., slow paying, good customer).
Suppress Collections
Determines if the customer is included or excluded from credit collections, if you use the Credit Collections Subsystem:
•
N - Include the customer in credit collections.
•
Y - Exclude the customer from credit collections.
•
D - Exclude the customer from credit collections if their A/R balance is below the Min Cred Coll Amt value set in Account Receivable Parameters or Credit Collections Min $ in the next field.
Credit Collections Min $
If Suppress Collections is set to D, Credit Collections Nightly Processing skips this customer if their open A/R balance is below this amount. This value overrides the value entered in the Min Cred Coll Amt set in Accounts Receivable Parameters.
Collections Method
Determines how the letters generated by the Credit Collections Subsystem are transmitted to the customer:
•
B - Mailed/faxed.
•
E - Emailed.
•
F - Faxed.
•
M - Mailed.
•
S - Mailed/emailed.
E-Mail Address
The email address for credit collection letters and customer statements, if different than the email address on the Customer Maintenance screen.
Statement Fax
The fax number for statements. If left blank, then the customer’s main fax number is used.
Credit Card Process Type
If you use Automatic Credit Card Processing for customers that use a credit card to pay A/R balances, this field indicates how often the credit card is charged:
•
D - Daily.
•
M - Monthly.
For use with IC verify which is no longer supported by Prelude.
Credit Card Inv Process Type
If you use Automatic Credit Card Processing for customers that use a credit card to pay A/R balances, this field indicates if the credit card should be charged by individual invoice or for all invoices:
•
I - Individual invoices.
•
A - All invoices.
For use with IC verify which is no longer supported by Prelude.
Credit Card Hold Flag
Indicates if credit cards from this customer were declined by the Automatic Credit Card Application Process:
•
H - Credit cards were declined. If one credit card is declined, any credit cards used by this customer will cause an order to go on hold.
•
Blank - No credit cards from this customer were declined.
For use with IC verify which is no longer supported by Prelude.
F2-Save
Saves the additional A/R information.
