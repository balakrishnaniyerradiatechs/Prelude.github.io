Progress Billing Inquiry
Progress Billing allows you to bill a customer in increments as a job is completed. After a job is set up in the Progress Billing Screen (in Customer Job Maintenance), and the job is billed in the Progress Billing Entry screen, you can use the Progress Billing Inquiry to view the estimated, invoiced, and remaining amounts of the sales, cost, and gross profit. You can also find this information in the Progress Billing Summary Report.
Viewing Billing Information for Jobs
To display billing information for jobs in progress:

Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order #
The orders numbers already entered against this job number.
Estimated
The estimated Sales, Cost, and GP% set up for this customer job in Customer Job Maintenance in the Progress Billing Screen.
Invoiced
The Sales, Cost, and GP% amounts for the portion of the job that was billed so far.
Remaining
The remaining Sales, Cost, and GP% amounts for the portion of the job that is not yet invoiced.
WO Issues
The cost and gross profit percentage earned for any work order issues on the linked work order.
WO Est Cost
The estimated work order cost.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Invoices
Opens the Customer Invoice Inquiry to allow you to view the parts of this job that are already invoiced.
F6-Order
Opens the Open Order Inquiry to display detail information for the remaining sales orders that are not yet billed.
F7-History Orders
Opens the Customer Job History Inquiry to display the parts of the job that are already billed.
