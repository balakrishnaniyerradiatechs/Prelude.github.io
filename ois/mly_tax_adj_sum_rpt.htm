Monthly Sales Tax Adjustment Summary Report
The Monthly Tax Adjustment Detail Report displays a summary of sales tax adjustments for a fiscal period. A tax adjustment is recorded when you enter A in the M/C column in Cash Receipts Entry. Refer to Cash Receipts Entry in the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information on adjusting tax.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Sales Tax Reports > Sales Tax Adjustment Summary
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse affected by the adjusted tax.
•
Period. The month or fiscal period the sales tax was adjusted.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Jur
The tax jurisdiction code. Refer to the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen for information on setting up tax jurisdictions.
Description
The description of the tax jurisdiction location.
Applicable
The total value of merchandise (Taxable + Nontaxable).
Taxable
The total taxable merchandise for the corresponding tax jurisdiction.
Nontaxable
The total amount of tax exempt merchandise for the tax jurisdiction.
Tax Charged
The total amount of tax charged for the tax jurisdiction.
