Adjusting Commission After Invoicing
If you need to adjust a salesrep’s commission or change the salesrep on an order after it is updated in the B Update, you can use A/R Commission Maintenance to override the calculated commission.
To adjust a salesrep’s commission:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Maintenance to open the Commission Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Invoice Number to adjust. The line items from the invoice appear.
3
Depending on what you need to adjust, choose from the following:
•
Adjusting Commission for a Line Item.
•
Changing the Salesrep on an Invoice.
•
Adjusting the Commission Percent.
