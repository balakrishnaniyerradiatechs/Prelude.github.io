Customer Rebate Summary Report
The Customer Rebate Summary report lists the totals of rebate and eligibility amounts for each customer for a time period. The rebate programs are established in the Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen and then assigned to each customer in the Customer Maintenance Order Information Screen.
Rebate amounts are calculated in Cash Receipts entry when the customer pays their invoice (refer to “Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance” in the Accounts Receivable documentation).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer Rebate Reports > Customer Rebate Summary Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers eligible for rebates.
•
Customer Rebate Number. The rebate program number set up in Rebate Maintenance, if you want to limit the report to just one program.
•
Sort By Rebate# or Customer. The sort order:
-
C - Sort by customer number.
-
R - Sort by rebate number.
•
Rebate Period. The rebate period for this report. The report will only print for rebate programs with this Credit Report Freq:
-
A - Annually.
-
Q - Quarterly.
-
M - Monthly.
-
S - Semi-monthly.
•
Invoice Date Range: The beginning and ending invoice dates to include in the report.
•
Report Description. Additional information to define the report. Prints on the 4th line of the header.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice #
The invoice number on which the rebate products were billed.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Inv Amt
The total amount of the invoice.
Tot Purch
The total value of merchandise purchased by the customer.
Oth Chgs
Other charges included in the invoices (e.g., freight, handling, tax).
Elg Amount
The amount of the invoices that are eligible to include in the rebate calculation.
Pd Within Elg Days
The rebate amount that will be paid to the customer if they pay their invoices by the number of days set in Eligible Days to Pay in the Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen.
Paid After Elg Days
The rebate amount that will be paid to the customer if they pay their invoices after the number of days set in Eligible Days to Pay in the Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen.
Unpaid Balance
The amount of the invoices not yet paid by the customer.
Rebate Amt
The calculated rebate amount due the customer if they pay their invoices on time.
