Daily Bookings Report
The Daily Bookings report lists every sales order line item entered, changed, or deleted for a day or date range. You can sort the report by Salesrep One, Salesrep Two, or Written By.
File Update Parameters Maintenance.
Set Bookings Y to record bookings information for new orders as well as any deleted lines or price/cost changes. Bookings are the amount for which you obtained orders, but are not necessarily invoiced. This information is helpful for companies with long lead times to help view business trends. Enter Y or N for each of the following to indicate if you want to keep bookings information for:
•
Manufacture reps.
•
Quotations.
•
MITS (third party software).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Daily Bookings Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Sort By Rep1/Rep2/ or W/B?. Determines the sort sequence for the report. Choose from the following to sort by:
-
1 - Salesrep One assigned to the sales order.
-
2 - Salesrep Two assigned to the sales order.
-
W - The sales order’s Written By user ID.
•
Salesrep. The sales representative numbers to include in the report if you chose 1 or 2 for the sort option.
•
Written By. The user ID of the person that entered the order if you chose the W sort option.
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report.
•
Type. The document types to include:
-
O - Orders.
-
M - Manufacturer’s representative orders (refer to Using Manufacturer Rep Sales Orders).
-
Q - Quotes.
•
Update Date Range. The beginning and ending booking dates to include on the report. The booking date is the date the order was entered or updated.
•
Summary Report Only?. Indicates if you want to include detail bookings or summaries only:
-
Y - Show only total price, cost, and gross profit information for each salesperson or written by sales person.
-
N - Include detail information for each sales order (e.g., product, order number, price, cost).
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/
ShipTo
The ship-to customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Product Number/ Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Order Num
Status
Line 1: The sales order number.
Line 2: The status of the line item:
•
NEW - The line item was first entered on the sales order.
•
CHG - The line item was changed.
•
DEL - The line item was deleted.
Ord Date
Update
Line 1: The date the order was entered.
Line 2: The date the order was changed or deleted.
Customer P/O#
Quantity
Line 1: The customer’s purchase order number, if applicable.
Line 2: The quantity ordered/changed to/deleted.
Price/UOM
The product’s sales price and unit of measure.
Extended Price
The extended price for the total quantity of the item ordered, changed, or deleted.
Extended Cost
The extended cost of the total quantity of the item ordered, changed, or deleted.
Gross Profit $
The total gross profit dollars earned for the line item.
Gross Profit %
The total gross profit percentage earned for the line item.
