Creating a Gift Certificate in Sales Order Entry
To create a gift certificate in Sales Order Entry:
1
From the Sales Order Entry screen (refer to Entering Sales Order Line Items), depending on whether the gift certificate is taxable or not, enter one of the following:
•
M - Miscellaneous taxable.
•
MN - Miscellaneous non-taxable.
The Miscellaneous Amount Entry screen appears.
2
For Misc. Amount, enter the value of the gift certificate that the customer wants to purchase.
3
For Misc. Reason Code, enter the reason code for gift certificates (refer to Creating Reason Codes for Gift Certificates). The G/L account assigned to the reason code displays and cannot be edited.
4
Press F2- Save to open the Gift Certificate Maintenance screen.
5
For Certificate Number, choose from the following:
•
If you have your own gift certificate numbers, enter the number.
•
If you use system generated gift certificate numbers (refer to Setting System Options for Gift Certificates) press ENTER over NEW to assign a new gift certificate number.
6
For Recipient Name, enter the name of the person who will receive the gift certificate. You can use this name when searching for a gift certificate.
7
Enter the address information for the recipient, if known.
8
If applicable, enter a Message to print on the certificate.
9
For Expiration Date, enter the date the gift certificate will expire (optional). When the gift certificate is used, the expiration date is validated if one exists. If the gift certificate is expired, a warning appears but the gift certificate can still be used.
Issuing Customer defaults to the customer number on the sales order.
Certificate Amount and Remaining Balance default to the amount entered in the Misc. Amount on the Miscellaneous Amount Entry screen.
10
If you want to print the certificate, press F8-Print Certificate.
11
Press F2-Update. The Product Description screen appears.
12
You can enter a Description 1 and a Description 2 if you want additional information to appear on the sales order.
13
Press ENTER to return to the sales order.
14
Complete the sales order as usual (refer to Finishing the Sales Order).
The new gift certificate cannot be used until the sales order is invoiced (refer to Invoicing). The Customer Gift Certificates general ledger account is posted to when a gift certificate sales order is invoiced.
