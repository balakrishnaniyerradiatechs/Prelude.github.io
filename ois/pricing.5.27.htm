Manually Entering Contract Ship-To Information
To manually enter the customer ship-to information on a Customer Price Contract (refer to Creating a Customer Price Contract):
1
For ShipTo, enter the customer’s ship-to locations that are valid for this contract. When an order for this customer/product/contract combination is processed through the B Update, the system fills in this field if the ship-to on the order is not already listed. This field does not exclude a ship-to location from receiving the contract price.
The ship-to Description displays.
2
If applicable, enter the number of the customer’s Bin for the corresponding ship-to address.
3
For Bin Qty, define the quantity of the product the customer’s bin will hold at the corresponding ship-to location (informational only).
4
For Min Qty, enter the minimum quantity the customer wants to maintain in their bin. The value from the Minimum Qty in the top part of the screen is the default but may be overridden (informational only).
5
For Max Qty, enter the maximum quantity the customer wants to maintain in their bin. The value from the Maximum Qty in the top part of the screen is the default but may be overridden.
Qty-Shp displays the quantity of this product shipped for this contract to the customer ship-to location.
Total Ext displays the total extended price of all sales order line items for this product shipped for this contract to the customer ship-to location.
6
Press F2-Save. Depending on whether this is a new contract, one of the following occurs:
•
If you created a new contract, a type 1 price matrix is created for the customer/product.
•
If you edited or viewed an existing contract, the following message displays:
Contract Currently Exists in Price Matrix File -
Overwrite?
7
Choose from the following:
•
Yes - The contract information is updated for the price matrix.
•
No - The price matrix is not updated.
