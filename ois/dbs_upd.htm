Daily Business Update
Normally you should update daily business statistics as part of Nightly Processing, but if not, you can use the Daily Business Update to update the statistics that appear on the Daily Business Statistics Report.
For more information on updating business statistics during Nightly Processing, refer to the Nightly Processing Procedures in the Purchasing documentation.
To update daily business statistics as a stand-alone procedure:
1
Select Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Summary Reports > Daily Business Statistics > Daily Business Update to open the Daily Business Update screen.
2
For Update Type, indicate if you want to update the daily business statistics for the current day or the number of days in Days Back for History in File Update Parameters (refer to Daily Business Statistics). Choose from the following:
•
D - Current day only.
•
A - All dates in File Update Parameters.
3
For Warehouse Number, if you chose A for Update Type, you can designate the warehouses for which you want to rebuild sales order history and A/R historical data.
The daily business statistics are updated and will appear on the Daily Business Statistics Report.
