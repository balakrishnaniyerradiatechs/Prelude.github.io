Releasing Tag & Hold Sales Orders
When the customer is ready for part or all their merchandise entered on a Tag & Hold order (refer to Entering a Tag & Hold Sales Order), you can use Tag and Hold Release Entry to designate the products and quantities to release to the customer.
To release a Tag & Hold order:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > Tag & Hold Release Entry to open the Tag & Hold Release Entry screen.
2
Enter the Order Number to release. The following message displays:
Do You Want to Release The Tag and Hold Order By....
3
Choose from the following:
•
Line Item - Allows you to release one line item at a time.
•
Exception - Automatically enters the Opn-Qty into the Qty-Rel on every item, but allows you to change any quantities.
•
Abort - Exits the release without updating any information.
If you chose Line Item or Exception, the staged items on the order display.
4
Move the cursor to each item to release or edit and press ENTER to open the Line Item detail screen.
5
Enter the quantity to release to the customer in Qty-Rel.
6
Repeat steps 4 & 5 for each item to release.
7
Press F2-Save to release the products/quantities and complete the order. Refer to Finishing the Sales Order for more information.
8
Print the pick ticket in Pick Ticket Printing that includes the products released in Tag and Release Entry. The pick ticket displays the customer’s ship-to address. Refer to Printing Pick Tickets.
