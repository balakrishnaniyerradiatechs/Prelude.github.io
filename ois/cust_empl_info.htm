Customer Employees Screen
You can use the Customer Employees screen to enter the names of the customer’s employees to select from in the Contact field in Sales Order Entry. If you enter authorized purchasing agents for the customer in the Customer Authorized Purchasing Agents Screen, the names must also exist here.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F8-Contact > F5-Employees
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Employee Name
The names of the customer’s employees, to appear in the selection screen of the Contact field in Sales Order Entry.
F2-Save
Saves the customer’s employee information.
