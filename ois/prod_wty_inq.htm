Product/Customer Warranty Inquiry
The Product/Customer Warranty Inquiry displays all customers that purchased a warranty product. The sales order must be invoiced to appear in this inquiry.
Viewing Warranty Sales Information
To view customers that purchased a warranty product:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Product/Customer Warranty Inquiry to open the Product/Customer Warranty Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the warranty Product.
3
Enter the Serial Number, if applicable.
Sales orders containing the warranty product appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order
The sales order number.
Customer
The customer who bought the warranty product.
Description
The customer description.
Invoice
The invoice number on which the product was billed to the customer.
Inv-Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Ext
An (*) asterisk indicates an extended warranty.
Exp-Date
The date the warranty product expires.
Lab-Date
The date the warranty labor expires.
Wty-Hrs
The number of usage hours that the product is under warranty.
Quantity
The quantity of the product purchased by the customer.
MCv
Indicates warranty coverage:
•
Y - The materials are covered under warranty.
•
N - The materials are not covered under warranty.
LCv
Indicates labor coverage:
•
Y - Labor is covered under warranty.
•
N - Labor is not covered under warranty.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to allow you to view detail information on the invoiced order.
F6-Serial#s
Displays serial numbers for the product.
F7-Review Ext
Allows you to review the sales order that the warranty was sold on.
F8-Log
Opens the Order Log Inquiry which displays every transaction that affected the sales order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
