Quotation Completion screen
The Quotation Completion screen allows you to add freight and extra charges to a quote. You can print, email, or fax the quote to the customer (refer to Printing Quotes) but no order is generated. You can go back into Quote Order Entry later to change to or to turn it into a sales order. If the quote is not eventually turned into a sales order or deleted, it may be purged.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems Quotation Subsystem > Quotation Entry > Complete the quote
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Terms Code
The accounts receivable payment terms code that indicates when payment is due, payment conditions, and any applicable discounts. The default comes from the customer’s Payment Terms in the Customer Maintenance Screen.
Merchandise
The total value of the products on the quote.
Inbound Freight
Inbound freight charges you are passing on to your customer.
Outbound Freight
The cost to ship the material to the customer.
Handling
Any applicable handling charges. Automatically calculated according to the customer’s Freight Information Screen.
Delivery
Any delivery charges, if known.
Pick Up
Any extra pick up charges (primarily used with the Rental module).
Minimum Charge
A calculated charge may appear to increase the order amount to the minimum amount defined either by:
•
The Min Invoice Amount in the customer’s Order Information Screen.
•
The Min Order Amount for the ship via code (refer to the Ship Via Freight Info Screen).
•
The minimum order amount defined at the company level.
Disc/Allowance
A discount amount to apply to the entire order by using:
•
A dollar amount (e.g., 10.75).
•
A percentage of the Merchandise amount (e.g., P15 for a 15 percent discount).
In either case, you must enter a Discount Reason Code. Refer to Creating Discount Reason Codes.
Sales Tax
The calculated sales tax. If necessary, you can change or verify the tax calculations by pressing F5-Tax Jur to open the Sales Tax Jurisdictions screen.
Total Sale
The total amount to quote to the customer for the merchandise, including any extra freight charges and tax.
Pending Deposit
The amount of deposit put down for the quote.
F2-Complete
Competes the quote and allows you to transmit it to the customer.
F5-Tax Jur
Opens the Sales Tax Jurisdictions screen to allow you to verify or edit tax exempt information.
Action Bar
Deposit Recalc
Recalculates the amount of deposit required if handing charges were added.
Curr Conv
Converts to your native currency if you use multiple currency.
