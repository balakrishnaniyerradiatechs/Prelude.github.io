Deleted Orders Report
The Deleted Orders report displays detail information for orders that were deleted in Sales Order Entry. The orders are grouped by the cancellation reason code that explains why the order was cancelled. For more information on cancelled sales orders, refer to Creating Order Cancellation Reason Codes.
You can run the report for one or all cancellation reason codes and for one or more cancellation dates. After you run the report, you can choose to purge the product information so that it is not included the next time you select the report.
Track Deleted Sales Orders
Keep Deleted Orders? in File Update Parameters must be set to Y to require the salesperson to enter an order cancellation reason code.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Deleted Orders
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Sell Warehouse. The warehouse that receives the credit for the sale.
•
Cancellation Reason Code. The order deletion reason code set up in the Order Cancellation Reason Maintenance screen.
•
Cancellation Date Range. The beginning and ending dates for which you want to include deleted sales orders.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/Name
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer name.
Slsm
The sales representative assigned to the sales order.
Ord-Date
The date the order was entered.
Order#
The sales order number.
CR/Tp
The cancellation reason codes are set up in the Order Cancellation Reason Maintenance screen to track why orders are cancelled. These may include:
•
01 - Out of stock.
•
02 - Price too high.
•
03 - Data entry error.
•
04 - Customer request.
•
05 - Late delivery.
•
05 - Credit problem on the account.
The sales order types are:
•
01 - Regular.
•
02 - Ship complete.
•
03 - Tag & hold.
•
04 - Direct ship.
•
05 - Counter invoice.
•
06 - Confirmation.
•
07 - Regular consignment.
•
08 - Confirmation consignment.
•
09 - Return goods.
•
10 - Repair orders.
•
12 - Invoice when complete.
•
13 - Ship backorder complete.
•
14 - Ship line item complete.
•
15 - Show order.
•
16 - Order projection.
•
17 - Manufacturer’s representative.
•
18 - Crib replenishment.
Refer to Sales Order Types for a complete explanation of each order type.
Product Number
The products on the deleted order.
Description
The product description.
C
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
•
Blank - Not part of a kit.
S
The line item’s supply code (if one appears on the sales order):
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity of the product on the deleted order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Price/Ext
Line 1: The price of the product as entered on the sales order.
Line 2: The total value of the extended quantity of the product (Qty-Opn x Price).
