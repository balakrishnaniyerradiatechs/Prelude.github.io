Product Substitutes Inquiry screen
The Product Substitutes Inquiry screen displays substitute products available for a product. Refer to Substitute Products in the Inventory documentation for information on setting up substitute products.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > Line Item Detail screen > F6-Subs
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The product number that has substitutes.
Warehouse
The warehouse for which you are inquiring on substitute products.
Available
The quantity of the Product available in the selected Warehouse.
Substitute Product
The product that is set up as a substitute product for this product.
Description
The description of the Substitute Product.
Sugg Retail
The suggested retail price of the substitute product.
Cust Price
The customer’s price for the substitute product.
Available
The quantity available of the substitute product.
Surplus
The surplus quantity of the substitute product that is available in the selected Warehouse.
F2-Finish
Closes the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen.
F5-Accept Substitute
Places the Substitute Product on the sales order or inquiry screen.
F6-Whse Avail
Opens the Product Warehouse Availability Inquiry screen to display availability of the product in other warehouses.
F7-Equivalents
Opens the Equivalents screen to allow you to view products set up as equivalent products. Equivalent products are products that do not exist in the system that could be a stock/substitute product in the future.
