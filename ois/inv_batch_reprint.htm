Invoice Reprint Entry screen
The Invoice Reprint Entry screen allows you to re-print, re-email, or re-fax multiple invoices for a customer. If you fax or email, all invoices must be for the same customer since the system uses the fax or email number from the customer rather than the sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Invoice Preview and Printing > Invoice Batch Reprinting
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Invoice#
The invoice number to re-print.
Order HIst#
The sales order history number.
Whse
The selling warehouse number.
Customer
The number for the customer who purchased the merchandise.
Description
The customer description.
Inv Document Option
Indicates how the invoices are transmitted to the customer. If more than one customer is included in the invoice re-print, you can only choose the N (print) option:
•
B - Both fax and print.
•
E - Email only.
•
M - Email and print.
•
N - Print only.
•
Y - Fax only.
Printer Form Queue
Indicates where the invoices should print. if applicable.
Invoice Message
The message to print at the bottom of the invoice.
