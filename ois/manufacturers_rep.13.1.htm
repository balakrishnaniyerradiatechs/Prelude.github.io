Manufacturer’s Rep Subsystem
If a customer orders and receives material directly from the vendor but the vendor pays you commission for the sale, you can use a manufacturer’s rep sales order. The Manufacturer’s Rep Subsystem only tracks commission and sales analysis for these orders. No inventory is committed, the customer has no open accounts receivable balance, and the general ledger is not affected.
The following steps are involved in a manufacturer’s rep sales order:
1
The vendor notifies you when they receive an order from the customer.
2
You enter a manufacturer’s rep order to acknowledge the order.
3
The vendor notifies you when the material ships.
4
You enter the shipped quantities on the order to update your sales information.
5
The customer pays the vendor and the vendor pays you a commission.
6
You enter the amount of commission paid and complete the order.
7
You update the order to move the order to history and to update sales analysis.
