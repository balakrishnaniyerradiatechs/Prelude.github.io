Lien / Bond Information Screen
The Lien / Bond Information screen allows you to enter information regarding liens, bonds, letters of commencement, and letters of furnishing.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > F6-Lien/Bond Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Partial Lien Date
The date a partial lien was given.
Partial Lien Amt
The value of the partial lien.
Final Lien Date
The final lien date.
Lien Notice Date
The date the lien notice was sent.
Lien Expire Date
The date the lien expires.
Bond Expire Date
The date the bond expires.
Public Or Private
Indicates if the job is public or private:
•
1 - Public.
•
2 - Private.
Print Commencement?
Indicates if you want to print a notice that announces that work is about to commence on a project:
•
Y - Print a Notice of Commencement.
•
N - Do not print a Notice of Commencement.
Print Furnishing?
Indicates if you want to print a letter, required by construction labor laws, that must be furnished to insure that lien rights are maintained:
•
Y - Print a Notice of Furnishing.
•
N - Do not print a Notice of Furnishing.
First Ship Date
The first date material shipped for this job.
Last Ship Date
The last time a material was shipped for this job.
F2-Save
Saves the lien/bond information.
