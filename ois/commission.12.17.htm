Overriding Commission in Sales Order Entry
You can enter commission information in Sales Order Entry that overrides commission calculated in Commission Matrix Maintenance, Price Matrix Maintenance, Product Maintenance, or Sales Representative Maintenance. You can override the cost at the header level or at the line item level, or even split commission between 2 or more sales reps. Refer to the following:
•
Overriding Commission For a Salesrep.
•
Overriding Commission for One Line Item.
•
Splitting Commission Between Salesreps.
