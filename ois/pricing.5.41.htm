Excluding or Including Product Lines on a Matrix
To exclude product lines from a promotion matrix or to create a list of product lines to include on a matrix:
1
From the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen, select F8-Prd Exc to open the Promotion Product Exception Maintenance screen.
2
For Product Exception, enter L for product line.
3
For Exception Method, choose from the following:
•
I - Include the product lines listed under Exception List in this promotion. All other product lines will not receive the promotional pricing.
•
E - Exclude the product lines listed under Exception List in this promotion. All other product lines will receive the promotional pricing.
4
For Exception List, enter the product line numbers to include or exclude from the promotion depending on the Exception Method setting.
5
Press F2-Save to save the exception information and to return to the Promotion Type Maintenance screen.
