Creating a Commission Matrix
A commission matrix allows you to vary the commission rate and/or the salesrep by customer, customer price group, warehouse, product number, vendor number, product price group, product line, major group, or a combination of these criteria as set up in Commission Matrix Types & Sequence Maintenance (refer to Setting System Parameters for Commission). The commission calculated in a commission matrix overrides commissions calculated in Product Maintenance or Sales Representative Maintenance.
To create a commission matrix:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Matrix Maintenance to open the Commission Matrix Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Matrix Type for which you want to create a commission matrix. Press F3 to view the types set up for your company in Commission Matrix Types & Sequence Maintenance (refer to Setting System Parameters for Commission).
The next screen displayed depends on the Matrix Type entered. For example: if Matrix Type 1 (Customer/Product) is selected, The Customer Number / Product Number Matrix screen appears.
3
For Sales Type, indicate if this matrix applies to direct sales orders or orders shipped from your warehouse. Choose from the following:
•
WHS - Warehouse sales (default).
•
DIR - Direct sales.
4
Depending on the matrix type, fill in the remaining fields as required. For example, for Matrix Type 1, enter the Customer Number and the Product Number. For Matrix Type 2, enter the Customer Number and the Vendor Number.
The Commission Matrix Input screen appears.
5
For Override Order Sales Rep, determine if commission calculated for the salesreps in the matrix overrides all the salesreps on the sales order. Choose from the following:
•
Y - A commission rate is calculated and recorded for all salesreps defined in the matrix, regardless of the salesreps on the sales order. If a rep field is not defined, then the salesrep from the sales order is used (refer to Setting Commission Rates by Salesrep).
For example, if a Rep1 exists, then it overrides Salesrep One on the sales order. But if Rep2 is not set up then Salesrep2 from the sales order is used.
•
N - A commission rate is calculated and recorded only for the salesreps defined on the sales order. The rate used depends on the following:
-
If the salesrep on the sales order is also included in the matrix, then the commission rate from the matrix is used.
-
If the salesrep number is not included in the matrix, then the commission rate from Sales Representative Maintenance applies (refer to Setting Commission Rates by Salesrep).
6
For Begin Date, enter the date that this commission matrix becomes effective.
7
For Expire Date, enter the date that this commission matrix no longer applies or leave blank for no expiration date.
8
For Rep1, enter one or more Salesrep numbers as established in Sales Representative Maintenance. This would normally be an outside salesrep and is compared with Salesrep One on the sales order.
9
For Rep2, enter one or more Salesrep numbers as established in Sales Representative Maintenance. This would normally be an inside salesrep and is compared with Salesrep Two on the sales order.
10
To determine how commission is calculated for each corresponding salesrep, choose one of the following options for Mtd, Gross Pft Tbl, Percent, and Price:
•
If commission is paid as a dollar amount:
-
For Mtd, enter $.
-
For Price - enter the dollar amount the salesrep earns for each unit sold. For example, enter 500 if the salesperson is paid $5 for each unit sold.
•
If commission is based on a percent of sales:
-
For Mtd, enter *.
-
For Percent, enter the percentage of the total sale that the salesrep receives when this product is sold. For example, enter 6 if the salesrep is to receive 6% of total sales.
•
If commission is based on a percent of gross profit:
-
For Mtd, enter G.
-
For Percent, enter the percent of gross profit the salesrep receives when this product is sold. For example, enter 3 if the salesrep is to receive 3% of the gross profit amount.
•
If commission is based on a percent of gross profit using a Gross Profit Table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table):
-
For Mtd, enter T.
-
For Gross Pft Tbl, enter the Gross Profit Table which allows you to vary commission by the gross profit amount on the sales order.
11
If you want additional salesreps to receive commission for orders meeting the matrix criteria, select F5-Add Rep to open the Other Sales Rep Input screen.
12
For Add Rep #, enter any additional salesreps to pay commissions along with the salesreps entered on the main screen and/or the salesreps defined in the sales order (e.g., to pay a manager’s commission). Multiple salesreps may be defined along with the method to calculate the commission (refer to step 10).
13
Press F2-Save twice to save the commission matrix information.
