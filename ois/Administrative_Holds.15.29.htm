Manually Placing Quotes on Hold
You can manually place a Quote on Administrative Quote Hold from within Quote Order Entry.
To manually place an order on Administrative Quote Hold:
1
From the Quotation Order Entry screen Action Bar, select Quote Func > Manual Administrative Hold.
2
Enter Y for Manual Administrative Hold.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the quote.
4
After you save the order, it will go on hold and not print until it is released by the Administrative Quote Manager. You have the option to enter a message to the manager or to enter notes regarding the quote.
