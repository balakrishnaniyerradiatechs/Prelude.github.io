Blanket Contract Maintenance screen
The Blanket Contract Maintenance screen allows you to assign special pricing to products for a customer. When you enter the blanket contract number in the Customer P/O# field on a sales order for the customer, the blanket contract pricing applies to the order and updates the customer’s blanket P/O balance (refer to the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen). You can track the quantities sold by product number by customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Blanket Contract Maintenance > Blanket Contract Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer to receive a blanket contract.
Entry Date
The date the contract is entered.
Blanket Contract#
The blanket contract number that references the customer’s special pricing and terms.
Product
The product number to receive a blanket contract price for the customer.
Description
The description of the product.
CPN
The customer part number displays if one was set up for the customer for this part number (refer to Setting Up Customer Product Numbers).
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as quoted on the blanket contract.
Meth
The method to calculate the price, as set up in the Blanket Contract Detail screen:
•
$ - The price is a set price.
•
* - The price is calculated as a percent of a price or a multiplier over a cost.
•
A - A discount is added in addition to the discount set up for the customer in Add. Discount% in the Customer Maintenance Additional Order Information Screen.
•
M - The rebate amount is based on a net margin.
Refer to Creating a Blanket Contract.
Percent
The percent of the price or the multiplier used to calculate the price.
Basis
The price or cost column used to calculate the price.
Price
The customer’s price for the product if the method is $.
F5-Copy (only appears before you enter header information)
Opens the Blanket Contract Copy screen to allow you to copy a contract from another customer.
F2-Save
Saves the blanket contract information.
F4-Del
Deletes the blanket contract.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Header Information screen for the blanket contract.
F6-Hdr Notes
Opens the Notes Display screen to allow you to enter notes regarding the blanket contract.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
