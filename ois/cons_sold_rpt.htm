Monthly Consigned Sold Report (P/O Type)
The Consignment Sold report displays vendor consigned inventory received on a type 10 purchase order (or Vend Consign is set to Y in the Purchase Order Header screen) that was sold or adjusted out of inventory. In either case, the vendor must be paid for the merchandise. Vendor consigned inventory refers to inventory that you do not pay for until you sell it.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Consigned Sold Report (P/O Type)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Vendor Number. The vendor that shipped the consigned inventory to your warehouse.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Document #
The document number associated with the transaction. The prefix indicates the type of transaction:
•
IA - Inventory adjustment number.
•
SO - Sales order number.
Product Number / Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Date
The date the product was sold or adjusted.
Quantity
The quantity sold or adjusted.
Cost
The unit cost of the product.
Extension
The total cost of the extended quantity of the product (Quantity x Cost).
