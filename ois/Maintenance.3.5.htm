Manually Recording Lost Sales Orders
If you want to record a lost sale for a product without entering a sales order (e.g., if a customer asked for a product that you did not have), you can use Lost Sales Entry to record a lost sale for a customer/product/warehouse.
To enter a lost sale:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > Lost Sales Entry to open the Lost Sales Entry screen.
2
Enter the Customer for whom you want to record a lost sale.
3
Enter the Product for which you want to record a lost sale.
4
For Sell Warehouse, enter the warehouse that would have received credit for the sale.
5
For Salesrep #1, enter the salesperson that would have received credit for the sale.
6
Enter the Quantity of the product for which you lost a sale.
7
Enter the Reason Code that explains why the sale was lost and determines if product usage is updated. Lost sales reason codes are set up in Lost Sales Reason Code Maintenance (refer to Creating Lost Sales Reason Codes).
8
Press F2-Save to update the lost sale.
