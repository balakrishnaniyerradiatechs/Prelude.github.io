Quote Conversion screen
You can use the Quote Conversion screen to convert an entire quote or part of a quote to a sales order either from Quote Order Entry or Sales Order Entry.
•
To convert a quote from Quote Order Entry, upon completion of the quote, select Order to open the Quote Conversion screen.
•
To convert a quote from the Sales Order Entry screen, before you enter a Customer, press F5-Convert Quote to open the Quote Conversion screen.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quote Number
The customer’s quotation number.
Customer
The customer number and name associated with the quote.
Product
The product on the quote.
Description
The product description
Quantity
The quantity of the product quoted to the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as quoted to the customer.
Price
The price quoted to the customer.
Convert
Indicates if you want to convert the quote line item to the sales order:
•
Y - Convert the line item onto the sales order (default).
•
N - Do not convert the line item onto the sales order.
F2-Save
Converts the selected quote lines to the sales order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
