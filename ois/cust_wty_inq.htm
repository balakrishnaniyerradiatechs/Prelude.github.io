Customer/Product Warranty Inquiry
The Customer/Product Warranty Inquiry displays warranty products sold to a customer. The sales order must be invoiced to appear in this inquiry.
Warranty product information is set up in Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F5-Add Info. Refer to the Inventory documentation for more information on warranty products.
Viewing Warranty Products for a Customer
To view warranty products purchased by a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer/Product Warranty Inquiry to open the Customer/Product Warranty Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
The sales order number.
Product
The product number that is under warranty.
Description
The product description.
Invoice#
The invoice number on which the product was billed to the customer.
Inv-Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Wty-Hrs
The number of usage hours that the product is under warranty.
Exp-Date
The date the warranty product expires.
Lab-Date
The date the warranty labor expires.
Quantity
The quantity of the product purchased by the customer.
MCov
Indicates warranty coverage:
•
Y - The materials are covered under warranty.
•
N - The materials are not covered under warranty.
LCov
Indicates labor coverage:
•
Y - Labor is covered under warranty.
•
N - Labor is not covered under warranty.
Ext
An (*) asterisk indicates an extended warranty.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to allow you to view detail information on the invoiced order.
F6-Serial#s
Displays serial numbers for the product.
F7-Review Ext
Allows you to review the sales order on which the warranty was sold.
F8-Log
Opens the Order Log Inquiry, to list every transaction that affected the sales order.
F9-GoTo Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or order number to go directly to.
