Crib History Inquiry
The Crib History Inquiry lists invoiced orders that were received by a customer who is set up with a crib warehouse. A crib warehouse refers to a warehouse located within your customer’s facility. The products remain your property and the customer is not billed for the material until they take it out of the crib. Refer to Using Crib Warehouses for more information.
Crib Warehouse
A warehouse must be established as a crib warehouse in Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > Crib Information > Crib flag = Y.
The customer must use consolidated invoicing (refer to Printing Consolidated Invoices) and they must have a Ship-To number (refer to Customer Ship-To Maintenance).
Viewing Crib Invoices
To list invoiced crib orders for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Sales Order Inquiries > Crib History Inquiry to open the Crib History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number.
3
For Cust Po/Job#, choose from the following to limit the list by:
•
P - Purchase order.
•
J - Job.
•
Q - Release.
•
Blank - All invoiced crib orders.
Invoiced crib orders for the customer appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
The invoiced crib order number.
Notes
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist on the sales order.
Ord-Date
The date the order was entered.
Inv-Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number.
Release Number
The customer’s purchase order release number, if applicable.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Allows you to view detail information on the crib order.
F7-Releases
Opens the Release History Inquiry to allow you to view detail information for the release.
F8-Log
Opens the Order Log Inquiry to allow you to view each transaction that affected the order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
