Price Listing All Products - Excel
The Product Price List - Excel report lists product prices, weights, and UPC codes for a customer using an a Microsoft Excel price spreadsheet. You can limit the list by product type. Once you execute the report, Excel opens to list the products (if your user ID is set up to use Excel).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Pricing Reports > Standard Pricing Reports > Price Listing All Products - Excel
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse for the customer.
•
Customer. The customer whose prices you want to display.
•
Catalog Date. The customer’s prices as of this date. If you enter a future date, then that date is used to calculate prices.
•
Job Number. The customer’s job number, if applicable.
•
Product Type. The product types to include. Choose from the following:
-
F - Finished good.
-
R - Raw material.
-
B - Finished and raw material.
-
E - Expense Item.
-
N - Rental.
-
U - Used.
-
P - Part.
-
Blank - All product types.
