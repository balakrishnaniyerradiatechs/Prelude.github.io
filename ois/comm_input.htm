Commission Matrix Input screen
The commission Matrix Input screen allows you to vary the commission rate and/or the salesrep by customer, customer price group, warehouse, product number, vendor number, product price group, product line, major group, or a combination of these criteria as set up in Commission Matrix Types & Sequence Maintenance (refer to Setting System Parameters for Commission). The commission calculated in a commission matrix overrides commissions calculated in Product Maintenance or Sales Representative Maintenance. Refer to Creating a Commission Matrix for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Matrix Maintenance > Enter a Matrix Type
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Override Order Sales Reps
Determines if commission calculated for the salesreps in the matrix overrides all the salesreps on the sales order:
•
Y - A commission rate is calculated and recorded for all salesreps defined in the matrix, regardless of the salesreps on the sales order. If a rep field is not defined, then the salesrep from the sales order is used (refer to Setting Commission Rates by Salesrep).
For example, if a Rep1 exists, then it overrides Salesrep One on the sales order. But if Rep2 is not set up then Salesrep2 from the sales order is used.
•
N - A commission rate is calculated and recorded only for the salesreps defined on the sales order. If the salesrep on the sales order is also included in the matrix, then the commission rate from the matrix is used. If the salesrep number is not included in the matrix, then the commission rate from Sales Representative Maintenance applies (refer to Setting Commission Rates by Salesrep).
Begin Date
The date the commission matrix becomes effective.
Expire Date
The date the commission matrix expires.
Rep 1
The Salesrep numbers from Sales Representative Maintenance. This would normally be an outside salesrep and is compared with Salesrep One on the sales order.
Description
The description of the salesrep.
Mtd
The method to determine how commission is calculated:
•
$ - Commission is paid as a dollar amount. Enter the amount in Price.
•
* - Commission is paid as a percent of sales. Enter the Percent of the sale that determines commission.
•
G - Commission is paid as a percent of the gross profit of the order. Enter the Percent of gross profit to pay.
•
T - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit using a gross profit table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table). Enter the Gross Pft Tbl number.
Grs Profit Tbl
If Mtd is T, the gross profit table which allows you to vary commission by the gross profit amount on the sales order. Refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table.
