Sales Order Credit Card Authorization Entry screen
If your credit card system is down and you must pre-authorize a credit card sale, you can use the Sales Order Credit Card Authorization screen to manually enter the authorization number that you receive over the phone.
You cannot add credit card information in this screen, it must already exist in the sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Add Manual Auth Code to Order
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The sales order number that must be pre-authorized by the credit card company.
Customer
The customer associated with the sales order.
Status
Indicates if Print was selected upon completion of the sales order:
•
P - Print was selected upon completion of the sales order.
•
Blank - Suspend was selected upon completion of the sales order.
Address One
The first line of address for the customer’s address.
Address Two
The second line of address of the customer’s address.
Address Three
Third line of the customer’s address.
City
The customer’s city.
State
The customer’s state.
Zip
The customer’s zip code.
Pay
The method of payment, which is always be 03 for bank card.
Type
The type of credit card (e.g., M/C, VISA, AMEX).
Credit Card Number
The customer’s credit card number.
Expire
The expiration date of the credit card.
Payment Amt
The amount due from the customer.
Auth Num
The authorization number that you received over the phone.
F2-Save
Saves the credit card authorization information.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Sales Order Display screen to allow you to view detail information for the order.
