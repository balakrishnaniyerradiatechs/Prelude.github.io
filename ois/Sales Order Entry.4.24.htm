Using a Control Product
When you designate a product as a control product, the other products on the sales order will not ship until the control product is available to ship. You may want to use this if you sell a product that has accessories and the customer cannot use the accessories until the main product is available. You can only enter one control product per sales order.
To designate a product as a control product:
1
When you enter a line in Sales Order Entry (refer to Entering Stock Products), from the Line Item Detail screen select F5-Add Info to open the Line Item Detail Additional Information screen.
2
For Controlling Product, enter Y.
3
Press F2-Save twice to return to the Sales Order Entry screen and continue with the order.
The entire sales order will not ship until at least one of the control product is available to ship. Once the control product is pickable, the pick ticket prints with the control product and any other products that are available. Any remaining backordered products ship as normal.
