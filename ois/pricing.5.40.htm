Excluding/Including Customers in a Promotion Matrix
When you create a promotion matrix, all customers are automatically included. But, you can exclude customers from a promotion matrix or create a list of customers that are the only ones to receive the promotional pricing.
To include or exclude customers from a promotion matrix:
1
From the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen, select F7-Cust Exc to open the Promotion Exception Maintenance screen.
2
For Exception Type, enter C for customer number.
3
For Exception Method, choose from the following:
•
I - Include the customers listed under Exception List in this promotion. All other customers will not receive the promotional pricing.
•
E - Exclude the customers listed under Exception List in this promotion. All other customers will receive the promotional pricing.
4
For Exception List, enter the customer numbers to include or exclude from the promotion depending on the Exception Method setting.
5
Press F2-Save to save the exception information and to return to the Promotion Type Maintenance screen.
