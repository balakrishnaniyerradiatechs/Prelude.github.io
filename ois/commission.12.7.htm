Accounts Payable Parameters
To determine how commission is updated when cost is adjusted in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry:
1
In Option Parameters, select Accounts Payable Parameters to open the Accounts Payable Parameters screen.
2
For Update Commissions, determine how to update commission when cost is adjusted in Commission Charges Entry (refer to Adding Charges to an Invoice):
•
C - Create a commission record for the additional charge. The charge appears on the commission report as a C followed by a 6-digit invoice number.
•
A - Delete the original commission record and re-create it to include the additional charge. The invoice date is updated to the date of the new invoice.
If commission records are already purged, new commission records are created for the full commission amount resulting in overpayment of commissions.
3
Press F2-Save to save the commission information.
