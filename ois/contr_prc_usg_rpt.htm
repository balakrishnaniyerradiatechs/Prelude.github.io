Contract Price Usage Report
To analyze the effectiveness of contract pricing for customers, use the Contract Price Usage Report to view the quantity of products purchased by customer/contract.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Usage Report.
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customer for whom you want to view contract product usage.
•
Contract. The contracts to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The number and description of the customer using the contract pricing.
Contract
The contract number.
Product /Description
Line 1: The product using contract pricing.
Line 2: The product description.
Price
The negotiated contract price for the customer/product.
Avg Cost
The average cost of the product.
Est Usg
The estimated usage of the product as entered in Est Qty Shipped, in the Contract Price Maintenance screen when the contract was created. Refer to Creating a Customer Price Contract.
Act Usg
The quantity of the product billed to date to this customer using this contract price.
On Hand
The quantity of the product in on-hand inventory.
On Ord
The quantity of the product currently open on purchase orders.
