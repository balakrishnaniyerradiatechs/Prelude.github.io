Setting Maintenance Options for RGAs
The following maintenance options determine when, where, and how RGAs are transmitted to your customer.
Warehouse Maintenance
To set printing options for RGAs:
1
From Warehouse Maintenance, select F6-Continuous to open the Continuous Printing Setup screen.
2
For Return Goods, determine if RGAs print continuously or by batch:
•
Y - RGAs print upon completion of Return Goods Authorization Entry.
•
N - RGAs print by batch (Refer to Printing RGAs by Batch).
3
For Prt, enter the port number (created in Continuous Printer Port Maintenance) used to control continuous printing.
4
For Form Queue, enter the printer number where RGAs print.
5
Press F2-Save twice to save the printing information.
Customer Maintenance
To set printing options for a customer:
1
In Customer Maintenance, select F7-Order > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Order Information Screen.
2
For RGA Document Option, indicate how RGAs are transmitted to the customer. Choose from the following:
-
B - Print and fax.
-
E - Email only.
-
M - Email and print.
-
N - Print only.
-
Y - Fax only.
