Releasing Orders from Administrative Invoice Hold
The Administrative Invoice Inquiry contains sales order invoices put on hold for pricing or other ordering restrictions defined by your company (refer to Defining Options for Administrative Invoice Hold for information on how to place orders on hold). The order must be released from hold before an invoice can print. Your user ID must be set up as an Administrative Invoice Manager to release orders from the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
Tracking Released Sales Orders
To track orders released from Administrative Invoice Hold, enter Y for Admin Mgr. Release in File Update Parameters.
To review or release orders on Administrative Invoice Hold:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Receivable > Inquiries > Administrative Invoice Release Inquiry to open the Administrative Invoice Inquiry screen. The Administrative Invoice Manager ID will default if you are an Administrative Invoice Manager.
2
Press ENTER. Invoices on Administrative Invoice Hold appear.
3
Move the cursor to the order that you wish to review or release and choose from the following:
•
F5-Review - Opens the Sales Order Display screen to allow you to review the sales order.
•
F6-SOE - Opens the Sales Order Entry screen to allow you to modify the sales order. You can also release the order for invoicing and send a message to the queue of the employee who entered the order.
•
F7-Toggle - Allows you to move back and forth between the original list of orders on hold and the orders in the hold queue. To send an order to the hold queue, enter one of the following under Reviewed.
-
C - Customer review.
-
I - Internal review.
-
S - Salesrep review.
•
F8-Release - Allows you to release the order from hold and send a message to the queue of the employee that entered the order.
4
Repeat step 3 for each order that you want to review, edit, or release.
5
Press F2-Save to close the inquiry screen.

Order Amount
The total purchase price for the products on the sales order.
User
The user ID of the person that entered the sales order (Written By).
Hdr
The reason code to indicate why the order went on Administrative Invoice Hold (refer to Defining Options for Administrative Invoice Hold). Multiple codes may be displayed for each order. Codes in this column refer to the holds affecting the order header:
•
A - A credit card order was entered without an authorization code. Refer to Credit Cards.
•
C - Credit memo amount exceeded for this user. Refer to User Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options.
•
D - Sales order’s negative deposit exceeds user’s limit.
•
I - The customer is always on invoice hold. Refer to Customer Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options.
•
M - Manually placed on administrative hold. Refer to Manually Placing Orders on Administrative Invoice Hold.
•
P - The customer requires a P/O number. Refer to Customer Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options.
•
Q - The invoice amount is less than the Admin Invoice Amt in the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers screen, if Require P/O Number (Customer Maintenance > F7-Order) is set to I. Refer to Customer Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options.
•
S - Salesrep One or Salesrep Two was changed on the Sales Order Header screen.
•
T - No tax jurisdiction code exists in the sales order header Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen.
•
X - Exceeds the pre-authorized percent for Credit Card Orders (refer to System Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options).
•
0-9 - User defined codes.
Line
The reason code to indicate why the order went on Administrative Invoice Hold (refer to Defining Options for Administrative Invoice Hold). Multiple codes may be displayed for each order. Codes displayed in this column refer to the holds affecting line items on the order:
•
C -The price was overridden below cost.
•
G -The price was overridden below GP% established for your company or for a product line.
•
R -The price was overridden below the customer’s default price.
Message
Any notes entered to the administrative hold manager by the Written By sales person when the order went on hold.
Reviewed
Indicates if the order was reviewed within this inquiry:
•
Y - This order was reviewed.
•
N - This order was not reviewed.
If you want to move the order to a hold queue for review, enter one of the following:
•
C - Customer review.
•
I - Internal review.
•
S - Salesrep review.
Once moved, use F7-Toggle to see orders in the hold queue. If necessary, you can then release the order from the hold queue.

F8-Release
Allows you to release the order from hold, send a message to the employee’s queue, and set the order up for invoicing.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
