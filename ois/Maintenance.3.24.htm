Cross Referencing Customer Crib Orders
When you import sales orders for crib products using the Direct Order Entry module, you must designate the crib or bin information.
To enter cross reference information for crib products:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Customer Crib Maintenance > Crib To Warehouse Maintenance to open the Crib to Warehouse Cross Reference Maintenance screen.
2
For Crib Number, enter the crib warehouse number.
3
Enter the Warehouse that will be assigned to the sales order for this crib.
4
If you manage cribs by bin location, enter the Bin number assigned to this crib.
5
For Default Department, enter the customer billing department to use for sales orders.
6
Enter the Shrinkage Department if shrinkage is set up for a customer ship-to account.
7
For P/O Number, enter the customer’s main purchase order number to use for the crib.
8
For Shrink Acct Code1, enter the shrinkage accounting code to use if shrinkage is used for a customer and ship-to. This code is not general ledger related but can be used for crib reports to find lost inventory.
9
Enter the Crib Type. Choose from the following:
•
GM - General Motors.
•
D - Delphi.
•
F - Ford.
10
Press F2-Save to complete the crib cross reference information.
