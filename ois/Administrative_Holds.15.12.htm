Product Line Level Administrative Order Hold Options
You can set up products within a product line to go on Administrative Order Hold if the Gross Profit levels are too high or too low.
To set Administrative Order Hold options for a product line:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line to open the Product Line Maintenance screen.
2
For Profit Test?, enter Y to open the G/P Percent Limits screen.
3
For Mgr Low Level G/P%, enter the minimum acceptable gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you enter an item on a sales order with a gross profit less than this percent, the order goes on Administrative Order Hold. This value overrides Mgr Low Level Minimum GP% in the Administrative Order Hold Parameters screen (refer to System Level Administrative Order Hold Options).
4
For Mgr High Level G/P%, enter the maximum acceptable gross profit percent acceptable for products in this product line. If you enter an item on a sales order with a gross profit more than this percent, the order goes on Administrative Order Hold. This value overrides Mgr High Level GP% in the Administrative Order Hold Parameters screen (refer to System Level Administrative Order Hold Options).
5
Press F2-Save to save the gross profit limit information.
