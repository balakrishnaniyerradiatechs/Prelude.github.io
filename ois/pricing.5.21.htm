Setting System Options for Retail Price Matrices
You must set the following system parameters to use retail price matrices:
Order Processing Parameters
To set system price matrix flags:
1
In Order Processing Parameters, select F5-Add Info > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Options screen.
2
For Excl ‘*’ Items From MatPrc , determine if asterisk (*) products are excluded from price matrix calculations:
•
Y - Asterisk items are excluded from price matrix calculations. Pricing from the product or warehouse applies.
•
N - Asterisk items are included in price matrix calculations.
3
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the matrix information.
 
Retail Price Matrix & Sequence Maintenance
To define the price matrix types and their relative hierarchy:
1
Under Order Processing Controls, select Retail Price Matrix & Sequence to open the Retail Price Matrix Maintenance screen. Only the matrices listed are available for your company to use.
2
Enter the Retail Price Matrix Types in the sequence they should be evaluated. The first matrix in the sequence is used first unless Best Price is turned on or a customer has a contract price. Delete any matrices that you do not use. Available matrix types are:
•
1 - Customer/Product.
•
2 - Customer/Customer Product.
•
3 - Customer/Product Line.
•
4 - Customer/Vendor.
•
5 - Customer/Product Price Group.
•
6 - Customer Price Group/Product.
•
7 - Customer Price Group/Customer Product.
•
8 - Customer Price Group/Product Line.
•
9 - Customer Price Group/Vendor.
•
10 - Customer Price Group/Product Price Group.
•
13 - Customer Number.
•
21 - Customer/Product Range.
•
26 - Customer Price Group/Product Range.
 
Retail Price Matrix Purge
The Price Matrix Purge Procedure on the Purge Utility Menu allows you to delete each retail price matrix detail line by expiration date. If the Expiration date that you enter is after the current system date, you are warned that current and/or future records are about to be purged and allowed to exit the process without purging. Once all price matrix detail lines are deleted, the record is automatically deleted.
