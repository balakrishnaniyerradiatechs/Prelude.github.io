Shipping Split Quantities out of Other Warehouses
Depending on system settings, the Auto Order Split screen may appear if a product that is backordered in the shipping warehouse, is available in other warehouses.
System Level
Shipping Whse Split /Reassignment (Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters > F5 > F5) determines if the Auto Order Split screen appears:
•
Y - If the quantity is available in another warehouse, automatically prompt with the Auto Order Split screen in Sales Order Entry to let the user decide if they want to ship part of the quantity from other warehouses.
•
N - Do not allocate any inventory from another warehouse. The product will be backordered.
•
A - Automatically ship part of the line item from a warehouse where it is available.
Warehouse Level
Multiple Whse In SOE (Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > F5-Add Info) Determines if more than one shipping warehouse is allowed on the same sales order:
•
Y - You can select the warehouse for each line item.
•
A - Automatically display a list of warehouses and the available quantity of the product in each warehouse any time the quantity available is less than the quantity ordered. You can select a warehouse to ship from. A separate pick ticket prints in each warehouse.
•
N - All lines must use the same shipping warehouse.
The setting at the warehouse level overrides the setting at the system level.
The Auto Split Warehouse Sequences Maintenance screen (Inventory Controls > Warehouse Sequences Maintenance > F6-Auto Split Seq) allows you to override the list of warehouses for the Auto Order Split Screen.
Shipping Matrices
If a shipping matrix is set up for a product, you cannot select a different warehouse. Refer to Overriding Shipping Locations for Products.
To ship split quantities of a product out of another warehouse:
1
Enter a quantity of the product in the Line Item Detail screen (refer to Entering Stock Products).
2
If the product is backordered but exists in another warehouse, after you press F2-Save, the Auto Order Split screen appears.
3
To determine which warehouses to ship from, choose from the following:
•
To ship the available quantity of the product from the original warehouse assigned to the order, press F2-Save. The available quantity will ship from the original warehouse and the remaining quantity will go on backorder.
•
To ship an available quantity out of another warehouse, highlight the warehouse and press F5-Warehouse Select. The available quantity will ship out of the selected warehouse.
•
To let the system automatically select the available quantities from warehouses where it is available, press F6-Auto Allocation. The available quantity in each warehouse that is needed to fill the order will appear under ReqQty.
-
Change the quantities as necessary.
-
Press F2-Save to return to the Line Item Detail screen.
The Line Item Detail screen will display the Quantity and Qty-B/O that will ship out of the original warehouse.
The Sales Order Entry screen will create a separate line for each quantity that is shipping out of another warehouse.
