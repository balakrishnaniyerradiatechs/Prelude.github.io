Monthly Sales Tax Detail Report
The Monthly Sales Tax Detail report displays tax information for each customer tax jurisdiction for all sales orders invoiced in a month or fiscal period.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Sales Tax Reports > Sales Tax Detail
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period when the sales orders were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Jur
The tax jurisdiction code. Refer to the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen.
Description
The description of the tax jurisdiction (e.g., state, city, county).
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Exempt#
The customer’s tax exempt number, if applicable.
Cust#
The customer who purchased the merchandise.
Description
The customer description.
Applicable
The total value of merchandise (Taxable + Nontaxable).
Taxable
The value of the merchandise that is taxable.
Nontaxable
The value of the merchandise that is exempt from tax.
Tax Charged
The dollar value for the amount of tax charged.
