Open Sales Order Aging Report
The Open Sales Order Aging Report shows aging information for open orders for a customer or customer ship-to address. You can view up to 4 aging columns and a grand total for all warehouses.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Aged Open Order Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Ship-To. The ship-to addresses for the customer that you want to include in the report.
•
First Aging Date. The date to consider as the first aging date for open orders.
•
Second Aging Date. The date to consider as the second aging date for open orders.
•
Third Aging Date. The date to consider as the third aging date for open orders.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer with open orders.
Description
The description of the customer.
Order#
The sales order number of the order that is open.
Shipto
The ship-to number for the customer, if applicable.
Ord Date
The date the customer ordered the material.
Req Date
The date the sales order is required.
Rep1
The Salesrep One assigned to the sales order.
On or After Date
The value of open orders that have an Ord Date after the Third Aging Date.
On or After Date
The value of open orders that have an Ord Date between the Second Aging Date and the Third Aging Date.
On or After Date
The value of open orders that have an Ord Date between the First Aging Date and the Second Aging Date.
Before Date
The value of open orders that have an Ord Date on or before the First Aging Date.
