Monthly M/R Register By Sales Rep 1
The Monthly M/R Register By Salesrep 1 lists vendor, customer, and commission information on all manufacturer rep orders that were updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing (refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order), for a salesrep and fiscal period.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > Monthly M/R Register By Sales Rep1
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Salesrep. The salesrep assigned to the order.
•
Period. The fiscal period in which the order was updated.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order#
The manufacturer rep order number.
Proc.Dte
The date the order was updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing. Refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order.
Vendor
The vendor that paid you the commission for the order.
Description
The vendor description.
Cust#
The number for the customer that ordered the material from the vendor.
Description
The customer description.
Customer P/O
The customer’s purchase order number.
Merch Amt
The total amount ordered by the customer.
Comm Amt
The total amount of commission paid to you by the vendor.
Gp%
The percent of the Net Price that the vendor paid to you as commission for the sale of the product.
