Creating Gift Certificates
You can create a gift certificate either in Sales Order Entry or in Gift Certificate Maintenance. If you create the gift certificate in Sales Order Entry, the sales order must be invoiced before the gift certificate can be used.
Refer to the following for information creating a gift certificate:
•
Creating a Gift Certificate in Sales Order Entry.
•
Creating a Gift Certificate in Gift Certificate Maintenance.
Once a gift certificate is created, the customer can use it as a form of payment (refer to Applying a Gift Certificate to a Sales Order).
