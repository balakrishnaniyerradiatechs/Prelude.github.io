Re-Allocating Available Backorders
If a future sales order is reserving all of the on-hand quantity of a product and another customer requires the product immediately, you can re-allocate the material using the Backorder Reallocation Availability screen.
Re-allocating backorders is not advised if your product delivery dates are not accurate.
To re-allocate backordered material:
1
From the Sales Order Entry screen Action Bar, select Order Func > Allocated Inv. Review to open the Backorder Reallocation Availability screen.
This screen is only available if enough inventory of the backordered item will be received before the pick date of the item on the future sales order.
The backordered, on-hand, committed, on order, and available quantities of the product display.
2
For Qty, enter the quantity of the product that you want to re-allocate from the future sales order to the order you are entering.
If you want to view the order that you are re-allocating the material from, press F5-Invty Inq to open the Inventory Inquiry screen. You can view purchase orders, work orders, or transfers on which the product is expected to be received and you can view sales orders or transfers to which the product is allocated.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the Sales Order Entry screen and to continue with the order.
If you re-open the Line Item Detail screen for the product, the re-allocated quantity now appears under Qty-Pck and is no longer backordered. The same quantity is now backordered on the future order.
