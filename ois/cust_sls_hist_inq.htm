Customer Sales History Inquiry
The Customer Sales History Inquiry displays detail sales information for invoiced orders for a customer within a time frame. You can view the information by order, by ship-to, by product number.
Viewing Invoiced Orders
To view invoiced orders for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Sales History Inquiry to open the Customer Sales History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
For Beg Date and End Date, enter the date range for which you want to view the customer’s invoiced orders.
The customer’s invoiced orders appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
Invoice#
Line 1: The sales order number.
Line 2: The invoice number.
Ord-Date
Inv-Date
Line 1: The date the customer ordered the material.
Line 2: The date the order was invoiced.
Sell
Ship
Line 1: The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
Line 2: The warehouse that shipped the order.
Product
Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The description of the product.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Qty-Ord
Qty-Shp
Line 1: The quantity ordered by the customer.
Line 2: The quantity shipped.
Net Prc
Comm-Cost
Line 1: The net price for the product.
Line 2: The Cost For Sales set up for the product which is the cost used to calculate commission.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen. You can view detail product and price information for the entire order.
F6-By Product
Opens the Customer Sales Report by Product screen. You can search for a product by product number, product description, or product line and then print, fax, email, or view the product information for this customer.
F7-Ship To
Opens the Customer Sales History By Ship-To screen to display customer orders/invoices for a ship-to address for the customer.
F8-Vendor
Opens the Customer Sales By Vendor screen to allow you to only print a report showing the customer’s product sales history in order by primary vendor number. Products with no primary vendor are grouped at the beginning of the report.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Returns to the previous page if more than one page of sales orders are included in the inquiry.
sF6-Next Group
Proceeds to the next page if more than one page of sales orders are included in the inquiry.
