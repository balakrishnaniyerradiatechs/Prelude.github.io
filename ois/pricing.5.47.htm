Assigning Price Label Information to Products
Once you create a price label format (refer to Formatting Price Labels) and assign label information to customers (refer to Assigning Price Label Information to Customers), you can set up price label information for each product.
To set up products to print labels:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen
2
Enter the Product and press ENTER.
3
Select F5-Add Info > F7-Labels to open the Product Labels information screen.
4
For Print Price Label, determine if customer price labels print for this item when sold. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Price labels print.
•
N - Price labels do not print.
If a product is set up to print price labels when you sell it, labels only print for customers with Print Price Label (Customer Maintenance > Action Bar > Add’l Opt > Label Information) set to Y.
5
For Price Sticker Mult, enter the multiplier to calculate the number of price labels to print for the product. When the product is sold, this number is multiplied by the quantity to determine the number of labels. If the customer orders 10 and the Price Sticker Mult is 10, 100 labels print.
6
For Print Cust/Prod Lbl, determine if product labels print when the product is sold. Choose from the following:
•
Print product labels when sold.
•
Do not print product labels when sold.
7
For Case Pack, enter the quantity of the product packed in a shipping case.
8
For Pallet Pack, enter the quantity of the product packed in a shipping pallet.
9
For Qty/20’ Container, if applicable, enter the quantity of the product included in a 20 foot container.
10
For Qty/40’ Container, if applicable, enter the quantity of the product included in a 40 foot container.
11
For Product Label Size, enter the default label size if you print labels when the product is received. Choose from the following:
•
L - Large.
•
S - Small.
12
For Qty/Product Label, enter the default number of labels to print when the product is received.
13
For Print Barcode Label, choose from the following to indicate if the price labels print barcodes when this item is received:
•
Y - Barcode the product label regardless of the vendor barcode setting.
•
N - Do not barcode the product label regardless of the vendor barcode setting.
•
V - Barcodes print based on the vendor barcode setting.
•
R - Barcodes print only on reports or documents (requires a custom forms modification).
14
Press F2-Save to save the labels information.
