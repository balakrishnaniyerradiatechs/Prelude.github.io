Summary Invoice Printing screen
You can use the Summary Invoice Printing screen to print invoices all on one invoice every day, month, or week. When the sales orders go through Invoice Printing (refer to Printing Invoices), they are held until the customer’s summary invoice print cycle comes up and then combined and generated as one invoice and assigned one invoice number (refer to Printing Summary Invoices).
Customer Maintenance F7-Order > F5-Add Info
Invoice Print Cycle. Determines how often invoices print for this customer:
•
D - Daily.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
•
W - Weekly.
Summary Invoices. Determines whether a regular invoice or a summary invoice prints for a customer:
•
Blank - Use regular invoicing.
•
Y - Print summary invoices on a cycle basis (refer to Invoice Print Cycle).
•
R - Print summary invoices off-cycle for the customer. The summary invoice is printed at any given time rather than a scheduled time such as daily, weekly, or monthly.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Invoice Preview and Printing > Summary Invoice Printing
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer Number
The customer for whom you want to print a summary invoice. The customer must have Summary Invoices set to Y or R in Customer Maintenance. If you want to print a summary invoice for all customers that are set up for summary invoicing, leave this field blank. A separate summary invoice prints for each customer.
Warehouse
The warehouse number if you want to limit the summary invoice printing to customers who are assigned to this selling warehouse in Customer Maintenance or have sales orders invoiced for this warehouse.
Printer Form Queue
The printer where the invoices should print.
Print Source
Indicated which types of invoices to include. Choose from the following:
•
I - Print invoices only.
•
C - Print credit memos only.
•
B - Print both invoices and credit memos.
•
M - Mail copies of counter invoices to customers whose Mail Invoices flag is set to Y in the Customer Maintenance Figure 2.1.16.
Invoice Message
A message to print at the bottom of the invoice, if applicable.
F5-Summary Invoice Reprint
Opens the Summary Invoice Detail Reprint screen to allow you to re-print the invoice.
