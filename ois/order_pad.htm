Order Pad Inquiry
You can use the Order Pad Inquiry search for products purchased by a customer. For each product number, you can view the invoiced sales order information. If you access this inquiry from Sales Order Entry or Quote Order Entry, you can add the products to the sales order.
To activate the Order Pad Inquiry, you must set Keep Order Pad? in File Update Parameters to Y.
Viewing Products Sold to a Customer
To list of product information for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Order Pad Inquiry.
You can also access this inquiry in Sales Order Entry > F8-Order Pad and in Quote Order Entry > F8-Order Pad.
2
Enter the Customer number.
3
Press ENTER to open the Order Pad Inquiry screen.
4
To limit the search to products purchased from one ship-to location, enter the Ship-To number.
5
For Description to Search, you can enter part of a keyword to limit the search. Leave blank to review all products purchased by this customer.
6
For Puch Contact, you can enter the Purchasing Contact assigned to the customer in Customer Contact Management Maintenance if you want to limit the product list to purchases for a contact.
7
For Sales or Prod Sort, choose from the following to sort the list of products by:
•
D - Dollar sales.
•
P - Product number.
•
U - Unit sales.
8
Press ENTER to list product sales for the customer.
9
If you opened this screen from Sales Order Entry, enter the quantity of each product to add to the sales order.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The products that fall within the selection criteria.
Description
The product description.
Last Qty
The quantity sold the last time the customer purchased this product.
Last Date
The date the last time the product shipped to the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product from the sales order.
Avail
The quantity available.
Avg Sales
The average sales for the product in dollars or units. Calculated using a rolling average of the last 13 months, including this month:
•
If you chose to sort by product or units, the average number of units sold per month to this customer displays.
•
If you chose to sort by dollars, the average price of the product when sold to this customer.
Campaign
The campaign code if the sale is associated with a marketing campaign.
Quantity
The quantity to add to your order or quote, if applicable.
Price
The price to charge the customer, if you accessed this screen in Sales Order Entry. You can only change the price of the product if the Price Override Code flag is set to Y for your user ID.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen. If you are in Sales Order Entry and you entered a quantity, the product and quantity are added to the order.
F5-Cust/Prod Inq
Opens the Customer/Product History Inquiry to display sales order and pricing information for each time the customer purchased the highlighted product.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a page number, line number, or reference number to go directly to.
