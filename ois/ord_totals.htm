Order Totals screen
The Order Totals screen displays the accumulated totals of the quantity, weight, and gross profit of the products on the sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > F6-Totals
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quantity
The total quantity of products ordered.
Weight
The total weight of the products ordered on the sales order.
Profit
The total profit amount earned for the accumulated products on the order.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent earned for the accumulated products on the order.
Dollars.
The total customer price for the accumulated products on the order.
