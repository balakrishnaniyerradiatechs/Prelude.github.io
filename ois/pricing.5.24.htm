Rounding Prices for Retail Matrices
To round the prices calculated by a retail price matrix, use Retail Price Rounding Maintenance to determine how the last cent is rounded.
To designate how a price is rounded:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Retail Price Matrix Maintenance > Retail Price Rounding Maintenance to open the Retail Price Rounding Maintenance screen.
2
For Customer, choose from the following:
•
Enter a customer number if you want the rounding information to apply to one customer only.
•
Enter DEF to set the rounding default information for all customers.
3
For From and Thru, enter the beginning and ending price values to define rounding. For example, for 1.00 - 10.00, define a set of rounding rules; for 10.01 - 20.00, define a new set of rounding rules.
4
For N0, determine how to round a retail price if the calculated value ends in 0. Enter one of the following:
•
Ux - To round the price up to x. For example, enter U1 to round the decimal up to the nearest 1.
•
Dx - To round the price down to x. For example, enter D0 to round the decimal down to the nearest 1.
•
X - To leave the price as calculated.
5
Repeat step 4 for N1 through N9, for values ending in 1 through 9.
6
Press F2-Save to save the rounding information.
