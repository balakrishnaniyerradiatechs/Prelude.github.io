Customer Ranking Report
The Customer Ranking Report ranks customers either by total sales amount purchased or by gross profit. You can sort the list by company, warehouse, or territory.
Building Customer Performance Information
Before you can run the Customer Ranking report, you must build the customer performance records (Order Processing > Processes > Customer Ranking Procedure) to rank customers by sales and gross profit for a time period.
The ranking information also displays in the Customer Performance Summary Inquiry (Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Info Inquiries > Customer Performance Summary Inquiry).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer Ranking Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Rank By Company, Whse Or Terr. Determines if you want to rank customers by:
-
C - Company sales.
-
W - Warehouse sales.
-
T - Territory sales.
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Territory. The territories used to group customers (e.g., east, west). These codes are established in the Territory Maintenance Screen and then assigned to a customer or customer ship-to account.
•
Rank By Sales or G/P. Determines if you want to list customers by the total sales amount purchased or by the gross profit. Choose from the following to rank by:
-
S - Sales.
-
G - Gross profit.
•
# Of Customers To Present. Limits the number of customers to view if you only want to see the top customers.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Rep1
The sales representative assigned to the customer (Salesrep One in Customer Maintenance).
Whse
The selling warehouse assigned to the customer.
Terr
The territory number used to group customers for sales reports. These codes are set up in the Territory Maintenance Screen and assigned to customers in the Additional Customer Information Screen.
Sales GM or
Sales $
Sales gross margin (if you ran the report by Gross Profit): The total gross profit earned on purchases from this customer.
Sales (if you ran the report by total sales): The total amount purchased by this customer.
Ranking
The ranking of the customer. The list is sorted in order from the highest sales or gross profit earned on purchases from this customer.
