Entering a Tag & Hold Sales Order
If your customer wants material picked and held until they call back with shipping or other additional instructions, you can enter a type 03 tag & hold sales order. This type of order allows you to release only what the customer needs (e.g., customer is building a house and needs items in stages).
To enter and release a Tag & Hold sales order:
1
Enter an 03 Order Type in Sales Order Entry (refer to Entering Sales Order Header Information).
2
If you use warehouse hold areas to stage tag & hold orders, refer to Using Warehouse Hold Areas for Tag & Hold Orders. Otherwise, go to step 3.
3
Print the staging document in Pick Ticket Printing (refer to Printing Pick Tickets by Batch). As each item on the order becomes available, a staging document prints but no release is created. The staging document contains the customer's name and identifies it as a Tag & Hold/Staging Document instead of printing a ship-to address so that warehouse personnel cannot ship the order.
Backorders
To show backorders on the first staging document, set Print B/O Staging Docs (Order Processing Parameters > F5 > F5) to Y.
4
When the customer is ready for their merchandise, use Tag and Hold Release Entry to list the products and quantities to ship to the customer. Refer to Releasing Tag & Hold Sales Orders.
5
Use Pick Ticket Printing to print the pick ticket that includes the products released in Tag and Release Entry. The pick ticket displays the customerâ€™s ship-to address.
6
After the product ships, confirm and invoice the order as normal.
