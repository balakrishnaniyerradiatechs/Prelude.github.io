Customer On-Time Shipments/Quality Report
The Customer On-Time Shipments/Quality report displays on-time and late shipment information for sales orders. If the ship date is equal to or later than the required date, the sales order is considered late.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer On-Time Shipments/Quality Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Fiscal Period. The fiscal period for which you want to view late shipments.
•
Ship Date Range. The beginning and ending ship dates to include.
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the merchandise.
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report.
•
Cutoff Day 1. The first cutoff date for late orders. For example, enter 5 to have a column heading of 1-5 days late.
•
Cutoff Day 2. The second cutoff date for late orders. For example, enter 10 to have a column heading of 6-10 days late.
•
Detail or Summary. Indicates whether you want to view the detail information for each line item on a late shipment or the total late shipments per customer.
-
D - Detail. Displays late shipment information for each line item.
-
S - Summary. Only shows the customers and the number of shipments in each column.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer#
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
On Time
The number of on-time shipments sent to the customer. A shipment is considered on-time if the ship date is at least one day before the required date.
Late 1-5
The number of late shipments according to the first cutoff date.
Late 6-10
The number of late shipments according to the second cutoff date.
Late 11+
The number of late shipments after the second cutoff date.
Total Shipped
The total count of shipments to the customers for the time period.
%On Time
The percentage of shipments shipped on-time to this customer (On Time / Total Shipped).
Total Returns
The total count of orders returned by the customer.
If you chose the detail option, the following columns also display for returned items
Order#
The credit memo number.
Product#
The returned product.
Qty Ord
The quantity the customer authorized to return on the RGA.
Qty Ship
The quantity the customer returned.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the credit memo.
Reason
The reason why the product was returned. Refer to Creating Credit Reason Codes.
If you chose the detail option, the following columns also display for late items
Late xx Days
The detail information for sales order releases that shipped late.
Order#
The sales order release number that was shipped late.
Product#
The product that shipped late.
Qty-Ord
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Shp-Qty
The quantity shipped to the customer.
