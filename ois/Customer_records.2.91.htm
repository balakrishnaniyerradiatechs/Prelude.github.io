Customer Tax Codes and Jurisdictions
To calculate the correct sales tax for customers, you must consider state tax laws, tax jurisdictions, tax exempt codes, and tax rates. You can set up sales tax information in the following screens:
•
State Abbreviation Maintenance Screen.
•
Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen.
•
Geographic Code Maintenance Screen.
•
Sales Tax Matrix Maintenance Screen.
•
Sales Tax Exempt Code Maintenance Screen.
Once the tax information is set up, you can assign them to customer records in Customer Maintenance, Customer Ship-to Maintenance, and Customer Job Maintenance.
