Lien / Bond Reports
A lien is a charge or claim held against property until a debt is paid. You can record lien and bond information for customer jobs in the Lien / Bond Information Screen (Customer Maintenance > F6-Lien/Bond). The following reports provide lien/bond information for customer sales orders:
•
Pending Lien/Bond Report.
•
Notice of Commencement Report.
•
Notice of Furnishing Report.
