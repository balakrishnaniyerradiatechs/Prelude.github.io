Backorder Reallocation Availability screen
If a future sales order has all of an available quantity of a product reserved and another customer requires the product immediately, you can re-allocate the material using the Backorder Reallocation Availability screen. This option is only available if inventory of the backordered item will be received before the due date of the future sales order. Refer to Re-Allocating Available Backorders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > Action Bar > Order Func > Allocated Inv. Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product#
The product number that is backordered and on a future sales order.
B/O Qty
The quantity backordered for the customer that requires the material immediately.
Onhand
The quantity on-hand in your warehouse, both allocated and un-allocated.
CommQty
The quantity of the product committed to the future sales order.
ExpQty
The quantity on order that is expected to be received into your warehouse
Avail
The quantity available from future sales orders that can be re-allocated to your sales order.
Qty
The quantity that you want to allocate to your sales order.
F2-Save
Re-allocates the selected quantity and returns to the Sales Order Entry screen.
F5-Invty Inq
Opens the Inventory Inquiry screen to allow you to view sales orders, purchase orders and transfers that contain the item.
