Tag & Hold Report
The Tag & Hold report list type 03 (tag and hold) sales orders for which a pick ticket printed. Tag and hold orders print an initial document that contains the customer's name and identifies it as a Tag & Hold/Staging Document instead of a printing a ship-to address so that warehouse personnel cannot ship the order. When the customer calls back with shipping instructions, the order is released in Tag and Hold Release Entry and the released products and quantities print on a pick ticket so that they can be shipped to the customer. The order is confirmed and invoiced as normal. Refer to Entering a Tag & Hold Sales Order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Tag & Hold Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report.
•
Sell Warehouse. The selling warehouse, which is the warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
Line 1: The number for the customer who ordered the material.
Line 2: The customer description and contact phone number.
Rep1
The sales representative assigned to the order.
Order#
The sales order number.
Ord Date
The date the sales order was entered.
Req Date
The date the customer requires the order.
Product Number
The products on the sales order.
Description
The product description.
Qty-Opn
The quantity ordered and not yet shipped.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Net Price
The selling price as entered on the sales order.
Ext Price
The total value of the open quantity on the order (Qty-Opn x Net Price).
