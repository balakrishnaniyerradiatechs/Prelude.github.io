Prescription Quantities
After a prescription is entered (refer to Creating a Customer Prescription) you can view the quantities and dates for the initial prescription and refills. The refill dates are automatically calculated.
To view prescription quantities:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Physician/RX Maintenance > Customer Prescription Maintenance to open the Customer Prescription Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer to receive the prescription
3
Enter Prescription #:
4
Press F5-Products > F6-Quantities to open the Prescription Quantities screen.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The prescription product.
Qty
The quantity of the product prescribed.
Refills
The number of refills available for this prescription.
Labels
The number of labels printed for this prescription.
Available
The quantity of the product still needed to fill the remaining refills of the prescription.
End Date
The ending date of the original prescription and each refill.
Qty/Mth
The quantity of the prescription to be filled each month.
Qty Used
The quantity of the product already used for each month. This quantity is updated when a sales order is entered for the product.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen.
