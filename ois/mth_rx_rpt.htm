Monthly Prescription Report
The Monthly Prescription report lists invoiced prescription products for a physician.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Physician/RX Maintenance > Monthly Prescription Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the products.
•
Physician. The physician that prescribed the products.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Ship-To Number. The customer ship-to numbers to include.
•
Invoice Date Range. The beginning and ending invoice dates to include.
•
Period. The fiscal periods to include.
•
Printer. The printer to send the report to. If the physician is set up to email or fax reports, the report will automatically email or fax to the physician.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer
The customer number and description.
Rx #
The prescription number. Refer to Creating a Customer Prescription.
Ord-Date
The date the customer ordered the prescription. Refer to Entering Prescription Products.
Inv-Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Product Number
The prescription product number.
Scientific Name
The scientific name of the product.
Shp-Qty
The quantity of the product shipped on this release.
