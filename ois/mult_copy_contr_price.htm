Copy Contracts screen
The Copy Contacts screen allows you to copy a contract from one customer to another.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Copy
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Copy From Customer
The customer whose contract you want to copy.
Copy From Contract
The contract number that you want to copy.
Copy To Customer
The customer that should receive the contract information.
Copy To Contract
The new contract number for the Copy to Customer.
F2-Save
Copies the contract information to the new customer.
