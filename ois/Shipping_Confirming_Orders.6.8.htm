Printing Packing Lists
If you want to send a packing list with a shipment that lists the confirmed shipped quantities, you can print the packing list after the release is confirmed in Release Confirmation Entry (refer to Confirming Releases). You can print a packing list for just one release or for all shipments by customer and or warehouse. If you do not continuously print packing lists, you must print the packing lists by batch.
