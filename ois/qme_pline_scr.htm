Quick Matrix Entry/Product Line screen
The Quick Matrix/Product Line screen in Quick Price Matrix Entry allows you to modify multiple price matrices (type 3 or 8) for a customer/customer price group at the same time. You can modify the beginning and ending dates and the pricing method information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Price Matrix Maintenance > Quick Price Matrix Entry > Matrix types 3 or 8.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer whose prices are being updated.
PLine
The product lines included in the matrix.
Description
The description of the product line.
Beg-Date
The date the prices take effect.
Exp Date
The date the special pricing ends.
Mt
Determines the method to calculate the price:
•
$ - The product has a set price. Enter the Price.
•
* - The price is a percentage of a column price or cost. Enter the Basis and Pct.
•
T - A Quantity Discount Table calculates the price. Enter a discount break Table. Refer to Discount Break Tables for information on setting up a discount break table.
Table
The discount break table number to use.
Price
If you entered $ for Mt, enter the price.
Basis
If you entered * for Mt, indicate which price or cost column to use for the calculation. The discount calculated is a percentage over a price column if a price is referenced or a percentage over a cost if a cost is referenced.
Pct
If you entered a price column in Basis, enter the discount to give off of the price. This value appears in Disc% on the Line Item Detail screen on the sales order. If you entered cost column in Basis, enter a percentage over the cost to calculate the sales price. For example if you enter 20 for Pct, the cost is divided by .80 to determine the sales price. This value appears in Net Price on the Line Item Detail screen on the sales order.
F2-Save
Saves the price matrix information.
