Entering a Sales Order that Requires a DD250 Invoice
If a customer requires a DD250 invoice:
1
Make sure that the Print DD250 flag in the sales order header Order Additional Information Screen is set to Y. This defaults from the Customer Maintenance Government Information Screen.
2
As you complete the Line Item Detail screen for each product on the order, the Government Information screen appears.
3
For Mil Number, enter the military number to print on the DD250 Form (block 16) immediately following the product description.
4
Enter the Cage Code for the vendor. The default is from Vendor Maintenance (refer to Setting Maintenance Flags for DD250 Form Printing) and prints on the DD250 Form (block 16) immediately following the product number.
5
For FMS Contract Notes (Foreign Military Sales Notes), enter contract notes to print on the DD250 Form (block 16) immediately following the Cage Code.
6
Enter the Government Item Number to print on the DD250 Form (block 15). The first four positions are required and must be numeric. The last two positions are optional and may be alphanumeric (e.g., 1234YZ).
7
For Marked For Code, enter a code to print in the Marked For Code block on the DD250 Form (block 14). This code is not validated.
8
For Marked For Description, enter up to four lines of description to print in the Marked For block on the DD250 form (block 14).
9
Press F2-Save to save the government information.
10
Complete the sales order as usual (refer to Finishing the Sales Order).
When you confirm the release (refer to Confirming a Release that Requires a DD250 Invoice) you must enter the remaining DD250 information that prints on the DD250 invoice.
