Adding Accessory Products to an Order
Accessory products are products that you can sell to go with another product. When you sell an item that has accessory products, you can view the list of accessories and select the ones you want to add to the order. Once accessory products are assigned to a product, any time you enter the product on a transaction or in an inquiry, ACC displays to let you know that accessories are available. If accessory groups are set up, you can assign an accessory group so that an entire list of products display any time the original product is entered on a sales order.
Assigning Accessory Products
Accessory products are assigned to a product in Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Accessory products (refer to “Accessory Products” in the Inventory documentation for information on setting up accessories).
When you sell a product that has accessories set up, the following message displays after you exit the Line Item Detail screen.
Do You Wish To Continue With Accessory Items Entry?
1
Choose from the following:
•
No - Accessory products are not listed or added to the order.
•
Yes - The Accessory Product Order Selection screen appears and lists any accessories that can be sold with the product you entered.
2
If you want to add an accessory product to the order, enter the Quantity to sell on the line for each product. Any comments or sales tips regarding the product appear under Script at the bottom left side of the screen.
3
Press F2-Accept. Any products that you select appear on the Sales Order Entry screen.
