Identifying a Product as a Prescription
Before you can enter and track perception products on sales orders (refer to Entering Prescription Products) you must identify the product as a prescription and assign it to a prescription group (refer to Creating Prescription Groups).
To identify a prescription product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance to open the Product Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Product number.
Refer to “Product Maintenance” in the Inventory documentation for complete instruction on creating products.
3
For Special Operation, enter RX to open the RX Group Maintenance screen.
4
Enter the Rx Group that this product belongs to. RX groups are established in RX Group Maintenance to standardize instructions (refer to Creating Prescription Groups).
5
Press F2-Save twice to save the RX information.
When you enter this product on a sales order, you are required to enter a prescription number created for the customer (refer to Creating a Customer Prescription).
