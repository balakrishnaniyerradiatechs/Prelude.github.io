Consignment Order History Inquiry screen
The Consignment Order History Inquiry screen displays detail pricing and shipping information for a consignment order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Inquiries > Sales Order Inquiries > Consignment Order History Inquiry > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The consignment order number.
Cust#
The customer number and description.
Product#/
Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Tx?
Indicates if the product is taxable:
•
Y - The product is taxable.
•
N - The product is tax exempt. A customer’s tax exempt status is set up in the Customer Maintenance Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen or the Customer Ship-To Maintenance Ship-To Tax Information Screen.
Quantity
The quantity of the product on the order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Src
The source code that indicates where the product price came from. Refer to the Price Source Codes.
Qty-Pck
The quantity picked.
Gross-Price
The gross price of the product.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped.
Disc%
The discount percentage applied to the Gross-Price.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered.
Whse
The selling warehouse.
Net-Price
The net price of the product on the sales order (Gross-Price x Disc%).
Profit
The total gross profit amount earned on this order.
GP%
The gross profit percentage earned on this order.
S/O Amount
The total amount of the sales order.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Hdr Scr
Displays the Sales Order Header screen.
F6-Completion Scr
Displays the Sales Order Completion screen.
F9-Goto
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays detailed product, price, cost, serial number and shipping information for the sales order.
Notes
Allows access to following notes entry/display screens:
•
Header Notes.
•
Line Notes.
•
EDI Line Item Notes.
•
EDI Header Notes.
Doc Func
Allows access to the following reports, if you are authorized:
•
Summary G/L Recap.
•
Detail G/L Recap.
Images
Allows access to the following order images, if you use the imaging system:
•
Invoice.
•
Proof Of Delivery.
•
Customer P/Os.
•
DEA Certificates.
•
Manual/Faxed Orders.
•
Rental Check-In.
•
Rental Exchange.
•
Repair Delivery.
•
Repair Pickup.
•
Repair Field Service Documents.
•
Contract.
•
Quote.
•
Customer PN Docs.
•
Product Images.
•
Customer Images.
