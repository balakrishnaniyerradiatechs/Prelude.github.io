Monthly Gift Certificates Register
The Monthly Gift Certificates Register lists gift certificates that were applied to sales orders and invoiced during a month or fiscal period. For more information, refer to Gift Certificates.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Gift Certificates Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period when the gift certificates were applied and invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Inv/Order#
Line 1: The sales order number on which a gift certificate was used.
Line 2: The invoice number on which the gift certificate was billed.
Inv Date
The date the gift certificate was applied.
Cust#
The customer that purchased the gift certificate.
Disc/All
The total order discount amount allowed as shown in Disc/Allowance in the Sales Order Completion screen.
Misc-Chgs
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Refer to Entering a Miscellaneous Product.
Frt/Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that was invoiced.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was available to ship.
Min/Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise sold.
Dep-Amt
The current deposit balance available. This amount represents the initial deposit placed on a sales order.
Cert-Amount
The total amount of the invoice, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost
The total cost of the merchandise sold.
G/P%
The gross profit percentage earned for the products sold.
Currency
The currency code assigned to the customer, if you use multiple currency.
