Converting an RGA into a Credit
Once you issue an RGA (return goods authorization) to a customer, when the material is returned to your warehouse, you can convert the RGA into a credit without re-entering the information. If the material is returned to stock it becomes available in inventory.
If a customer returns material without an RGA, you can still issue a credit. Refer to Entering a Credit Memo.
To convert an RGA to a credit memo:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > Credit Memo Entry to open the Credit Memo Entry screen.
2
For Order Number, enter the RGA number. The line items from the RGA appear on the screen.
3
To change any quantities or prices, highlight the product to change and press ENTER to open the Line Item Credit Detail screen. Edit the information as necessary. For more information, refer to steps 3-11 in Entering a Credit Memo.
4
Once all the quantities and prices are correct, press F5-Hdr Scr to open the Sales Order Header Screen.
5
Change the Order Type to an 06 (Confirmation).
6
Verify or edit the header information as necessary.
7
Press F2-Save to return to the Credit Memo Entry screen.
To complete the credit to the customer, continue with step 12 in Entering a Credit Memo.
