Sending Acknowledgements by Batch
If you do not continuously print acknowledgements (refer to Setting Maintenance Options for Acknowledgements), the acknowledgments are held in a queue until you print, fax, or email them by batch in Acknowledgement Printing.
To transmit acknowledgements by batch:
1
Select Order Processing > Batch Ticket Printing > Acknowledgement Printing to open the Acknowledgement Printing screen.
2
Enter the selling Warehouse for which you want to transmit acknowledgments.
3
Enter the Sales Order Number to print a single acknowledgement or leave blank to transmit all acknowledgements in the queue.
4
Enter the Selling Warehouse for which you want to transmit acknowledgments.
5
For Ack Document Option, if you want to limit the acknowledgements to one transmission type, enter one of the following:
•
C - Print and fax.
•
E - Email only.
•
F - Fax only.
•
M - Email and print.
•
N - No acknowledgment.
•
T - Text email.
•
Y - Print only.
6
If you are printing the acknowledgments, enter the Printer Form Queue where you want them to print.
7
For Acknowledgement Date, enter the date to appear on the acknowledgments.
8
For Acknowledgement Message, enter any message that you would like to print in the body of the document.
9
Press ENTER.
10
Once you choose Execute the following message appears:
Load Acknowledgement Forms In Printer & Align Visually
11
Choose from the following:
•
Print - Prints the acknowledgements.
•
Mask Print Test - Tests the alignment of the acknowledgement forms in the printer. Not necessary if you use a laser printer. After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Acknowledgements.
•
Abort - Cancels the acknowledgements printing.
12
After you select Print, the following message appears:
Acknowledgement Printing Complete...
13
Choose from the following:
•
Process - If all acknowledgements printed successfully.
•
Reprint All - If none of the acknowledgements printed successfully. This option is also useful if only a couple of acknowledgements printed successfully from a large print job.
•
Single Reprint - To reprint acknowledgements individually. This option is useful if a couple of acknowledgements jammed in the printer.
•
Abort - Cancel the acknowledgement printing.
