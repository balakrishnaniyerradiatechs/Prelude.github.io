Quotation Display screen
The Quotation Display screen allows you to view detail line item and header information for a quote.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Inquiries > Quotation By Customer > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Quote Number
The quotation number.
Customer
The customer associated with the quote.
Ln#
The line number for the item on the quote.
Product Number
The product being quoted.
Description
The product description.
Customer Product#
The customer’s product number, if applicable (refer to Setting Up Customer Product Numbers).
Quantity
The quantity of the product being quoted.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the quote.
Gross Price
The gross price of the product before any applicable discounts.
Disc%
The customer’s discount for the product.
Net Price
The customer’s net price (Gross Price x Disc%).
Conv
C indicates that line item on the quote was converted to a sales order.
Whse
The shipping warehouse for the quote.
Tx
Indicates if the product is taxable:
•
Y - The product is taxable.
•
N - The product is tax exempt. A customer’s tax exempt status is set up in the Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen or the Ship-To Tax Information Screen.
AQH
The reason code to indicate why the order went on Administrative Quote Hold (refer to Defining Options for Administrative Quote Hold). Multiple codes may be displayed for each quote.
•
D - Override freight dollars.
•
F - Override freight terms.
•
I - Incompatible ship via.
•
J - Exceeded the quote limit for the Written By user ID that entered the quote.
•
L - All quotes for the customer go on Administrative Quote Hold (If Admin Hold Override is set to Q for the customer in the A/R Information Screen).
•
M - Manual hold.
•
I - Incompatible ship via.
•
N - Price overridden below minimum price.
•
V - Manager low level/high level limit GP%.
Src
The quote source that indicates how you obtained this quote. Common examples are:
•
C - Customer call in.
•
D - Direct order entry.
•
E - Electronic data interchange.
•
K - Special order-telemarketing.
•
L - Sales representative.
•
M - Mail in.
•
R - Remote order entry.
•
S - Sales force automation.
•
T - Telephone sales.
•
W - Will call.
•
X - Special order.
Refer to Defining Order Source Codes for information on creating source codes.
Profit
The amount of profit earned for each line item. The total profit amount displays at the bottom.
Gp%
The gross profit percent earned for each line item. The total gross profit for the order displays at the bottom of the screen.
Extension
The total of the extended quantity quoted (Quantity x Net Price).
Q/O Amount
The total amount of all the products on the quote.
F2-Finish
Closes the Quotation Display screen.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Quotation Header Display screen to allow you to view the header information for the quote.
F6-Comp Scr
Opens the Quotation Completion Display screen to allow you to view detail information on additional freight and handling charges for the quote. Refer to the Quotation Completion screen for an explanation of each field.
F8-Log
Opens the Quote Log Inquiry to allow you to view every transaction that affected the quote.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays detail price and gross profit information for the quote.
Notes
Allows you to view or edit the following:
•
Header Notes. Notes regarding the entire sales order.
•
Line Notes. Notes regarding the line item that your cursor is on.
•
Service Problem Notes. Problems notes on a service order.
•
Service Solution Notes. Solution notes on a service order.
•
Header Attachments. Attach a document or picture to the order.
•
Line Attachments. Attach a document or picture to the line item.
Job Info
Opens the Job Quote Confirmation screen to allow you to view customer contacts entered for the job/quote and to determine if and how the quote was transmitted to the customer. If the quote was emailed or faxed to more than one contact, the EML and FAX codes display for each contact on the Quote Log Inquiry.
