Setting System Options for Price Matrices
The following system options determine how price matrices are used:
Company Maintenance
To determine if prices matrices are used by your company:
1
In Company Maintenance, select F5-Add Info.
2
Set Price Matrix to Y.
3
Press F2-Save twice.
 
Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters
To set system price matrix flags:
1
In Order Processing Parameters, select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Drop Ship Price Matrix, determine if separate price matrices are kept for direct shipments:
•
Y - Separate matrices are maintained for direct ships. In this case you must maintain two matrices, one for stock sales and one for drop ship (direct) sales, even if they are the same. Price Matrix Maintenance prompts for a direct ship price.
•
N - The same matrix pricing applies to stock and direct orders.
3
Press F5-Add Info to open the Additional Options screen.
4
For Excl ‘*’ Items From MatPrc , determine if asterisk (*) products are excluded from price matrix calculations:
•
Y - Asterisk items are excluded from price matrix calculations. Pricing from the product or warehouse applies.
•
N - Asterisk items are included in price matrix calculations.
5
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the matrix information.

Price Matrix Exceptions
•
Price matrix types 53 & 54 can only be used if Product# Prefix is set to Y in Inventory Parameters and all of your product numbers begin with a valid prefix.
•
Price matrix types 21 Customer/Product Range, 26 Customer Price Group/Product Range, and 34 Product Range allow you to enter a range of products.
 
Price Matrix Purge
The Price Matrix Purge Procedure on the Purge Utility Menu allows you to delete each price matrix by expiration date. If the Expiration date that you enter is not before the current system date, you are warned that current and/or future prices are about to be purged and allowed to exit the process without purging. Once all price matrix detail lines are purged, the matrix is automatically deleted.
