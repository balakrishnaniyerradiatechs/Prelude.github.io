Rebate Change Conversion
The Rebate Change Conversion updates customers’ A/R history if you edit rebate information in one of the following fields:
•
Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > Customer Rebate#.
•
Customer Rebate Maintenance > Eligibility Type or Eligible Days to Pay.
To update A/R records with rebate information:
1
Select Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer Rebate Reports > Rebate Change Conversion.
2
Enter the Customer Number to update or leave blank to update all customers.
3
Enter a beginning and ending Invoice Date to include for invoiced orders for these customers.
4
For Customer Rebate Code, enter the customer rebate number to reset. The A/R history is recalculated for the customers with this rebate number.
The Customer Rebate number is created in Rebate Maintenance and then assigned to each customer in Customer Maintenance.
