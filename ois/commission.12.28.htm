Adjusting the Commission Percent
To change commission percent for a single salesrep or for all salesreps on an invoice:
1
From the A/R Commission Maintenance screen (refer to Adjusting Commission After Invoicing), select F7-Chg All %â€™s to open the Commission Percent Change screen.
2
For New Commission Percentage, enter the percent that you want to pay the salesrep(s). This new commission is calculated according the method set up for the product/customer in Commission Maintenance (refer to Creating a Commission Matrix).
3
For To Sales Rep, enter the salesrep that this new percent applies to. Leave blank to apply this commission percent to all salesreps included on this invoice.
4
Press F2-Save to save the salesrep commission percent information.
