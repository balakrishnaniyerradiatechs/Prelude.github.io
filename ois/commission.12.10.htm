Setting Commission Rates by Salesrep
You can set up commission rates to apply to all invoiced sales orders for a salesrep. This rate is overridden by commission rates set for a product in Product Maintenance or a commission matrix set up in Commission Matrix Maintenance. If you only want to calculate commissions using matrices, it is not necessary to set up commission rates in Sales Representative Maintenance.
To set up a commission rate for an individual salesrep:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Sales Representatives to open the Sales Representative Maintenance Screen.
2
For Commission Type, determine how commission is calculated for this salesrep on sales orders shipped out of your warehouse. Choose from the following:
•
$ - Commission is based on the dollar amount sold. Enter the amount in Rate. For example if you enter 100, the salesrep would get $1.00 for each unit sold.
•
* - Commission is based on a percentage of sales. Enter the percent in Rate. For example if the salesrep is to receive 6% of total sales, enter 6 for Rate.
•
G - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit. For example, if the salesrep is to receive 3 percent of the gross profit, enter 3 for Rate.
•
S - Commission is based on a percent of sales using a Gross Profit Table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table) which allows you to vary commission by the total sales amount of the order. Enter the table number in the next (unlabeled) field.
•
T - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit using a Gross Profit Table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table) which allows you to vary commission by the gross profit amount on the sales order. Enter the table number in the next (unlabeled) field.
3
For Direct Comm Type, determine how commission is calculated for this salesrep on direct sales orders. Choose from the following:
•
$ - Commission is based on a dollar amount. Enter the amount in Rate. For example if you enter 100, you would get $1.00 for each unit sold.
•
* - Commission is based on a percentage of sales. Enter the percent in Rate. For example if the salesrep is to receive 6% of total sales, enter 6 for Rate.
•
G - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit. For example, if the salesrep is to receive 3 percent of the gross profit, enter 3 for Rate.
•
S - Commission is based on a percent of sales using a Gross Profit Table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table) which allows you to vary commission by the total sales amount of the order. Enter the table number in the next (unlabeled) field.
•
T - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit using a Gross Profit Table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table) which allows you to vary commission by the gross profit amount on the sales order. Enter the table number in the next (unlabeled) field.
4
Press F2-Save to save the salesrep information.
