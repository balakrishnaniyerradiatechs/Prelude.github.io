Assigning Vendor Information to a Contract
If you enter vendor contract number or quote number information in the Vendor Information screen in Price Matrix Maintenance, the vendor information carries over to:
•
The Line Item Detail Additional Information screen on the sales order.
•
EDI transmissions for linked purchase orders.
•
The purchase order line item for sales orders linked to purchase orders.
 
To set up vendor contract information in a price matrix:
1
After you enter a line item in the Price Matrix Maintenance screen, (refer to Creating a Price Matrix) select Vend Info from the Action Bar to open the Vendor Information screen.
2
Enter the Vendor Contract# to assign to the line item.
3
If applicable, enter the Vendor Quote#.
4
If applicable, enter the Vendor Salesman#.
5
Press F2-Save to save the vendor contract information.
