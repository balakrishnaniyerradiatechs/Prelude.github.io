Sales Order Display screen
The Sales Order Display screen is accessible within many inquiry programs to display detail shipping and pricing information for a sales order.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The sales order number.
Customer
The customer number and description.
Product
The product sold to the customer.
Description
The product description.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes are attached to the sales order.
Whse
The selling warehouse.
Quantity
The quantity shipped.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Qty-Pck
The quantity picked.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped to the customer.
Qty-BO
The backordered quantity of the product.
Gross Price
The gross price of the product.
Disc%
The discount percent applied to the gross price on the sales order.
Net Price
The net price of the product (Grs Price x Disc%).
Extension
The value of the total quantity of the product sold to the customer. (Net Price x Qty Shp).
Quantity
The total quantity of products ordered by the customer.
Weight
The total weight of all the products ordered.
Profit
The total gross profit amount to earn on this sales order, when all the products are invoiced.
Gp%
The average gross profit percent of all the products ordered by the customer.
S/O Amount
The total value of the products ordered by the customer.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Sales Order Header screen.
F6-Comp screen
Opens the Sales Order Completion screen to display freight and closing costs for the sales order.
F7-Rev Link
Opens the Purchase Order Display screen if this sales order is linked to a purchase order.
F8-Log
Opens the Order Log Inquiry which lists every transaction that affected the sales order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays detailed product, price, cost, and shipping information for the sales order.
Notes
Allows access to following notes entry/display screens:
•
Header Notes.
•
Line Notes.
•
Conversion Notes.
•
EDI Line item notes.
•
EDI Header Notes.
•
Service problem notes for service orders.
•
Service solution notes for service orders.
Order Info
Allows access to the following screens:
•
Campaign Priority Codes.
•
Prefix Settings.
•
Summary G/L Recap Report.
Images
Allows access to the following order images, if you are using the imaging system:
•
Invoice.
•
Proof Of Delivery.
•
Customer P/Os.
•
DEA Certificates.
•
Manual/Faxed Orders.
•
Rental Check-In.
•
Rental Exchange.
•
Repair Delivery.
•
Repair Pickup.
•
Repair Field Service Documents.
•
Contract.
•
Quote.
•
Customer PN Docs.
•
Returned Goods.
•
Product Images.
•
Customer Images.
