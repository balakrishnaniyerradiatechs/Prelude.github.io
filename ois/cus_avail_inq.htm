Customer Price/Availability Inquiry
The Customer Price/Availability Inquiry displays pricing and availability information for a customer/warehouse/product.
Kit Parents
The Precost Kit From Rollup flag in Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters determines if the kit parent item defaults to the price/cost from the rollup of the components
Viewing Customer/Product Price and Availability
To view pricing information:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Price and Availability Inquiry to open the Customer Price/Availability Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Identify the Sell Warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
4
If applicable, enter a Job# or a CustP/O# that the customer will use for the sale.
5
For Product/Description, enter the part number.
6
If applicable, enter a Quantity and U/M to view pricing information for different quantities of the product.
Pricing information appears.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Whse
The selling warehouse.
Available
The quantity available in the corresponding warehouse.
Status
The stocking status of the product:
•
S - Stock.
•
N - Non-stock.
•
C - Consigned.
Prices
The list price and column pricing for the product, or matrix contract pricing set up for the customer.
Quantity
Allows you to enter a quantity to view pricing at different quantities.
U/M
The unit of measure assigned to the product in product maintenance.
Gross Price
The gross price for the product for this customer.
Disc%
The discount off of the gross price.
Net Price
The net price (Gross Price x Disc%) for the product for this customer.
Extension
The extended price for the total quantity of the product, if you entered a value in Quantity (Net Price x Quantity).
Src
The source of the product price (e.g., the price column, quantity break price matrix type, contract). Refer to Price Source Codes for a description of each source code.
Weight
The weight of the product from Product Maintenance.
Cost
The cost of the product and the gross profit percent earned for the net price for the Quantity entered.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Inv Inq
Opens the Inventory Inquiry screen to display inventory status information for the product.
F6-Whse Avail
Opens the Product Warehouse Availability Inquiry screen which lists inventory levels in other warehouses.
F7-Qty Brk
Displays pricing levels for different quantities of the product, if applicable.
F8-Prc Mat
Opens the Customer Price Group/Product Number Matrix Inquiry screen to display price matrix information for the product/customer. Refer to Price Matrices for more information.
F9-Ret Mat
Displays retail price matrix information for the product/customer. Refer to Retail Price Matrices for more information.
Action Bar: Inquiries
Alternates
Opens the Product Alternates Inquiry screen to list any alternates available for the product.
Substitutes
Opens the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen to list substitute product information for the product.
Accessories
Opens the Accessory Inquiry to list any accessory products set up for the product. Accessory products are products that you may want to sell to go along with another product (refer to Adding Accessory Products to an Order).
Complements
Opens the Product Complement Inquiry to list any complements set up for the product. Complement information provides the salesperson with helpful information regarding a product.
Additional Description
Opens the Additional Description screen to allow you to view any additional descriptions set up for the product.
Product Notes
Opens the Product Notes screen to allow you to enter any notes regarding this product.
Customer Discounts
Opens the Chain Discount Inquiry screen to display chain discount information set up for the product for the customer.
TPCx Price Availability Request
If your company is set up to use TPCx (Trading Partner Connect), you can check price and availability for the product from other trading partners.
