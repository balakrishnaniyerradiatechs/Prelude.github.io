Entering Upsell Products
An upsell product is a more expensive product or list of products that you may want to offer when a customer asks for an item. You can review the upsell products and replace the original product if the customer agrees to buy the more expensive product.
Upsell Products
Upsell products are assigned to a product in Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes > Upsell products (refer to “Upsell Products” in the inventory documentation for more information).
When you enter a product on a sales order that has upsell products assigned to it, the following message displays:
This product has upsell products. Do you wish to review?
1
Choose from the following:
•
No - Upsell products are not listed. You can continue with the Line Item detail screen for the original product.
•
Yes - The Upsell Entry screen appears to display the following:
-
Upsell products assigned to the original product.
-
The availability of the upsell products.
-
The price of the upsell products.
-
The difference in price between the original product and each upsell product.
-
Script regarding the upsell product. This may be information regarding the upsell product that might help you to encourage the customer to purchase the more expensive product.
2
Choose from the following:
•
F2-Save - Closes the Upsell Entry screen and returns to the Line Item Detail screen without replacing the original product entered on the sales order.
•
F5-Accept - Replaces the original product with the highlighted upsell product on the Sales Order Entry screen. The Line Item Detail screen appears and you can continue with the sales order.
