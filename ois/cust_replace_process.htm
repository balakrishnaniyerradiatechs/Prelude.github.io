Universal Customer Replacement /Merge Update
The Universal Customer Replacement /Merge Update selects all customer changes that you entered (refer to Merging/Replacing Customer Accounts) and either copies the customer information to a new customer number or merges the existing customer into another existing customer. Only standard files and fields are updated (custom files and field will require a custom modification). Sales information in the customer file is updated.
WARNING Make sure that no records are locked and that no users are on the system before you run this process. This is a data conversion and cannot be reversed.
To merge/change the customer information:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Universal Customer Replacement/Merge > Universal Customer Replacement/Merge Update to open the Customer Replacement/Merge Process screen.
2
Once you choose Execute, a warning message appears to let you know that all the customer information will be copied/merged to the new customer number. At the end of the warning, the following question appears.
Do you wish to proceed?
3
Choose from the following:
•
No - Cancels the update procedure.
•
Yes - Begins the update. All information is copied to the new customer numbers.
