Overriding Commission Cost using a Price Matrix
You can use Price Matrix Maintenance to override a commission cost for a product on a sales order.
To override a commission cost using a price matrix:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Price Matrix Maintenance to open the Price Matrix Maintenance screen.
2
Follow the procedures for Creating a Price Matrix to fill in the appropriate pricing information.
3
Press F6-Commission Cost to open the Commission Cost Entry screen.
4
For Cost Method, choose from the following to determine how the commission cost is calculated for line items that meet this matrix criteria:
•
* - Use a percentage of a price/cost field. Enter the following:
-
For Basis, enter the price or cost column (from Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing) to use as the base amount to calculate the discount or markup.
-
For Percent, if you entered a price column in Basis, enter the percent to take off of the price. If you entered cost column in Basis, enter a percent over the cost to calculate commission cost.
•
$ - Price/Cost. Use only if the matrix is for a specific product. The cursor moves to the Cost field to allow you to enter the cost.
•
T - Use a discount break table (refer to “Discount Break Tables” in the Order Processing documentation). The system determines a cost based upon the discount level. Enter the discount break Table number to use. The system determines a purchase cost based upon the discount level.
5
Press F2-Save to save the commission cost information.
