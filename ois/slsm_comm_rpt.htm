Salesman Commission Report
The Salesman Commission report lists salesrep commission earnings for invoices updated during the B Update.
Commissions in File Update Parameters must be set to Y to use this report.
To run the Salesman Commission report:
1
Select Order Processing Additional Subsystems Commission Subsystem > Salesman Commission Report.
2
Enter the beginning and ending invoice dates for which you want to include salesrep commission.
3
After you choose to Execute the report, the following message appears:
Ok to purge Commissions records to HIstory File for report just printed?
4
Choose from the following:
•
No - Keeps the commission records open. Be careful not to re-pay the salesreps the next time the report prints.
•
Yes - Moves the commission records to Commission History.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
The customer number.
Inv#
The invoice number updated.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Product Number
The product sold to the customer.
Quantity
The quantity of the product sold.
Ext Price
The extended price of the total quantity sold.
Ext Cost
The extended cost of the total quantity sold.
GP%
The gross profit percent generated from the sale.
%-Rate
The commission percent to pay this salesrep if commission is calculated as a percent of the total sale (Mtd set to * in the Commission Matrix). Refer to Creating a Commission Matrix.
$-Rate
The commission to pay this salesrep if commission is a set amount for the product (Mtd set to $ in Commission Matrix Maintenance). Refer to Creating a Commission Matrix.
C-Rate
The commission percent to pay this salesrep if commission is calculated as a percent of the gross profit earned on the sale (Mtd set to G in Commission Matrix Maintenance). Refer to Creating a Commission Matrix.
Commission
Depending on the commission method, the commission amount due to the salesrep.
