Credit Information Screen
The Credit Information screen displays the credit limit and A/R balance for the customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > F5-Hdr Scr > F9-Credit Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Credit Limit
The customer’s credit limit.
A/R Balance
The total amount of the customer’s unpaid invoices.
Order Balance
The total amount of open orders for the customer.
Credit Balance
The credit limit (A/R Balance and Order Balance).
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
