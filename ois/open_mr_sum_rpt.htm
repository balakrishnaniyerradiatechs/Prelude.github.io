Open M/R Summary Report
The Open M/R Summary Report lists summary information for manufacturer’s rep orders that were entered but not updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > M/R Open Order Summary Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse that received credit for the sale.
•
Vendor. The vendors to include on the report.
•
Customer. The customers to include on the report.
•
Salesrep. The sales representatives to include on the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order#
The manufacturer rep order number.
Ent-Date
The date the order was entered.
Vendor
The vendor that will pay you commission.
Description
The vendor description.
Cust#
The number for the customer that purchased the product from the vendor.
Description
The customer description.
Customer P/O Merc
The customer’s purchase order number for the merchandise.
Rep1
The sales representative that will receive credit for the sale.
Merch-Amt
The total amount of merchandise the customer ordered from the vendor.
Comm-Amt
The amount of commission that you should expect to receive from the vendor for this order.
