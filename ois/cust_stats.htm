Customer Statistics Screen
The Customer Statistics screen displays sales, profit, and payment information for a customer, broken down by month.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F9-Statistics
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Life Sales
The total value of products sold to this customer since their account opened.
Life Returns
The total value of products returned by this customer.
Month
The breakdown by month for sales, returns, profit, receipts, discounts and freight.
Sales
The total value of products sold to the customer for the corresponding month (including credits given to the customer).
Returns
The total value of products returned by the customer for the corresponding month.
Profit
The total profit earned from this customer for the corresponding month.
Receipts
The total amount of cash received from this customer for the corresponding month.
Discounts
The total value of discounts given to this customer for the corresponding month.
Freight
The total amount of freight billed to the customer for the corresponding month.
OK
Closes the screen.
F5-Add Stats
Opens the Additional Statistics screen to allow you to view additional order and year-to-date sales for the customer.
F6-Summary
Allows you to print or transmit the customer’s statistics information.
Action Bar
Graphs
Allows you to print or view the customer’s statistics in a graph. You can view graphs for the following:
•
Current year sales.
•
Current year returns.
•
Current year gross profit.
•
Current year cash receipts.
•
Current year discounts.
•
Current year freight.
Performance
Opens the Customer Performance Summary Inquiry to display sales and ranking information for the customer.
