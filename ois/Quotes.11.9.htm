Converting a Quote to a Sales Order
You can convert an entire quote or part of a quote to a sales order either from Quote Order Entry or Sales Order Entry:
•
To convert a quote from Quote Order Entry, upon completion of the quote, select Order to open the Quote Conversion screen.
•
To convert a quote from the Sales Order Entry screen, before you enter a Customer, press F5-Convert Quote to open the Quote Conversion screen.
1
If you accessed this screen from Sales Order Entry, you must enter the Quote Number. If you entered the screen from Quote Order Entry the Quote Number already appears. The lines from the quote display.
2
For each line item, choose one the following for Convert:
•
Y - Convert the line item onto the sales order (default).
•
N - Do not convert the line item onto the sales order.
3
Press F2-Save to convert the selected quote lines to the sales order.
The Sales Order Header screen appears and you can continue the sales order as normal (refer to Entering Sales Order Header Information).
Supply Code
If a supply code was entered for a line item on the quote, the supply code remains the same when the quote is converted to a sales order.
Commission Code Override
If a cost was changed on the quote, the cost is carried over to the sales order when the quote is converted and is considered a commission cost override.
