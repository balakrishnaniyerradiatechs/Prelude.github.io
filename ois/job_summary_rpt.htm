Customer Job Summary Report
The Customer Job Summary report list lien information on open customer jobs.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Summary Reports > Customer Job Summary
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Job Number. The job numbers to view.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust # Description
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Job Number
The job number assigned to the customer.
Job Description
The description of the job.
Current Draw
Outstanding amount.
Waiver Date
The date the partial lien was put against the job.
Waiver Amount
Partial lien amount applied to the job.
Lien Notice
The date the lien notice was sent.
Entry Date
The date the job was entered.
Terms Date
The due date of the open amount is due, according to the payment terms set up for the order.
