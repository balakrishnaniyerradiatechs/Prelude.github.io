Daily Credit Card Sales Register
The Daily Credit Card Sales Register lists all credit card transactions for the day, according to what your general ledger says you pre-authorized. The total on this report should match the combined total of the Credit Card Post Authorization Approval Report and the Credit Card Post Authorization Rejection Report that generate when you post-authorize the batch. Refer to Post-Authorizing Credit Card Transactions for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Credit Card Sales Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Invoice Date. The date the credit cards sales were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice#
The invoice order number.
Order#
The sales order number.
Inv Date
The day the credit card sale was invoiced.
Customer
The customer number.
Description.
The customer description.
Type
The credit card type (e.g., VISA, Master Card, American Express).
Credit Card Number
The credit card number. Depending on how View Credit Card# is set for your User ID in User Identification Maintenance, either the entire number or only the last 4 digits display.
Exp Date
The expiration date on the credit card.
Auth#
The credit authorization number.
Card Amount
The amount billed to the credit card.
