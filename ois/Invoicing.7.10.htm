Printing DD250 Invoices
The DD250 form is a Material Inspection and Receiving Report printed for government customers. You must use DD250 Invoice Printing to print any sales orders that are set up to print on this type of form.
You can create DD250 codes and set maintenance flags for each warehouse, vendor, and customer that uses the DD250 form. When you enter and confirm sales orders set up for DD250 invoicing, you must enter the required information to print on the form.
