Setting up a Customer to use Floor Plan Financing
To set up a customer to use another customer as a financial institution:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F8-Floor Plan to open the Floor Planning Maintenance Screen.
2
For Financial Institution Floor Plan, indicate if this customer uses floor plan financing:
•
Y - This customer uses financial floor planning. Any time you enter a sales order for this customer, you are asked if this is a floor plan order. Refer to Entering a Floor Plan Sales Order.
•
N - This customer does not use floor planning.
3
For Floor Plan Customer, enter the customer who is responsible for payment, whose credit limit is checked, and whose A/R balance is updated when an order is entered for this customer. If you do not enter a floor plan customer number, then any customer can be used as the floor plan customer on the sales order header. Refer to Entering a Floor Plan Sales Order.
4
For Plan Number/Approval Req, determine if an approval and a floor plan number are required when this customer places an order:
•
Y - The Floor Plan Approval/Plan Number screen appears when you enter a sales order for this customer. Refer to Entering a Floor Plan Sales Order.
•
N - A plan number and approval are not required in Sales Order Entry.
5
Press F2-Save to save the floor plan information.
