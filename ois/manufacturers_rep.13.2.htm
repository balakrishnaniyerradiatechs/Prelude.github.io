Entering a Manufacturer’s Rep Order
When a vendor notifies you that a customer placed an order, you can enter the manufacturer’s rep order. Depending on when the vendor notifies you of the shipment and when the commission is paid, the order can be entered and completed all at once, or the whole process can be completed in three separate entries into the order. For example, when you enter the order you can:
•
Record the acknowledgement of the order.
•
Enter the shipped quantity information.
•
Record the commission paid to you by the vendor.
Regardless of how many steps it takes to enter all the information from the vendor, once the order is shipped and the commission check is received, you must update the order (refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order) to complete the process and to update sales statistics.
To enter the manufacturer’s rep order:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Entry to open the Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen.
2
For Order Number choose one of the following:
•
To start a new order, press ENTER. A new sales order number is assigned.
•
To edit an existing order, enter the order number or press F3 to search for the order by customer.
3
Enter the Vendor that will pay you commission.
The M/R Order Header screen appears.
4
For Customer Number, enter the customer that purchased the material from the vendor. This customer must already be set up in your system.
5
Enter the appropriate Ship-To Number or press F3 to search for ship-to addresses for this customer. To view or change the ship-to address, press F5-Ship-To to open the M/R Ship-To Address screen.
6
Enter the Sell Warehouse that will receive commission for this order.
7
For Customer P/O#, enter the customer’s purchase order number if available.
8
For Salesrep #1, either accept the default Salesrep One assigned to the customer or change it to the appropriate sales representative that will receive commission for this order.
9
Accept or change the Written By if necessary (defaults to the user ID of the person logged onto the terminal).
10
For Entry Date, enter the date the vendor notified you of the order.
11
Press F2-Save to return to the Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen.
12
Enter the first Product on the order. The M/R Detail Information screen opens.
13
Enter the Quantity ordered by the customer.
14
Depending on when the vendor notifies you of the order, choose one of the following methods to complete the shipping information:
•
If the vendor is just sending an acknowledgment of an order but nothing has shipped, enter the Qty-B/O.
•
If the vendor is notifying you that the material shipped to the customer, enter the Qty-Shp.
•
If the vendor is notifying you that the customer has paid, after you enter the Qty-Shp, enter the Paid Qty. Refer to Completing a Manufacturers’s Rep Order for information on how to record the commission paid.
15
For Net Price, enter the customer’s price for the product.
16
To enter the amount of commission that you will receive from the vendor for this sale, choose one of the following methods:
•
Commission percent. For Comm%, enter the percent of the Net Price that you will receive as commission from the vendor for this sale. The total amount of commission that you should expect to receive for this line item will appear in Exp-Comm.
•
Commission amount. For Comm-Rate, enter the actual amount that you will receive as commission from the vendor for this sale.
17
For Ack-Date, enter the date the vendor first notified you of the order from the customer.
18
If the vendor is notifying you that the material shipped, enter the Ship Date.
19
If the manufacturer’s acknowledgement references a number, enter the number in Manufacturer Ref#.
20
If you filled in a quantity in Paid Qty, the value in Act-Comm updates automatically to the amount of commission you will receive for the material shipped.
21
Press F2-Save to save the line item information and to return to the order entry screen.
22
Repeat steps 12-21 for each line item to add to the order.
23
Press F2-Save to complete the order. The following message appears:
Do You Wish To.....
24
Choose from the following:
•
Suspend. Use this option if you are only entering an acknowledgement or shipped quantities but have not yet been paid.
•
Complete. Use this option if you entered a Paid Qty on the order and want to update that a quantity has shipped and the vendor is paying you the commission. Refer to Completing a Manufacturers’s Rep Order.
•
Return. Allows you to return to the order to review or edit.
