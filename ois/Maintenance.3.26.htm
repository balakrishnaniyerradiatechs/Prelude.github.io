Setting up and Tracking Prescriptions
If you sell products prescribed by doctors, you can set up a product as a prescription so that when a salesperson enters the product on a sales order, they are required to enter the prescription number. You can also set up and track the number of refills, expiration dates, and special instructions to include with each prescription.
