General Contractor Information Screen
If a general contractor is working on the job, you can enter the address in the General Contractor Information screen.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > Action Bar > Add’l Opts > General Contractor Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
General Contractor
The name of the general contractor responsible for the job.
Address One
The first line of the contractor’s address.
Address Two
The second line of address.
City
The contractor’s city.
State
The contractor’s state.
Zip
The zip code.
Phone
The contractor’s phone number.
F2-Save
Saves the general contractor information.
