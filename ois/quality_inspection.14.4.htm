Assigning Inspection Procedures for Products
To define the type of inspection procedure required for a product.
1
In the Inventory module, select Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
Set Qual Insp Required to Y to open the Inspection Procedures screen.
If you use Quality Control Inspections for receiving in the Inventory module (Quality Inspection- AQL to N in Inventory Parameters) the Inspection Level screen opens to allow you to enter the inspection level for the product. Refer to ”Requiring Quality Control Inspections in Receiving” in the Inventory documentation for more information.
3
For Proc#, enter the inspection procedure to use as the default for this product. Inspection procedures are set up in Quality Inspection Procedure Maintenance (refer to Setting Acceptable Quality Levels for Inspection Procedures).
The Description, Version, Version Date and Inspection Type display for reference.
4
Enter any Instructions to display in Inspection Results Entry > F6-Instructions (refer to Inspection Results Entry).
5
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the inspection procedure information.
 
When the product is received in Stock Receipts Entry, it will be held in the Quality Control Inspection Inquiry for review and release. Refer to Quality Control Inspection Inquiry.
