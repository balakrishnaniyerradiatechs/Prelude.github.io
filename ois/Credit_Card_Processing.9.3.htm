Activating Protobase for a Warehouse
To activate Protobase for a warehouse:
1
In Warehouse Maintenance, select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Credit Bureau Override, select N so that Credit Bureau codes are validated by the system.
3
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the credit bureau information.
If a credit card verification service is not integrated with Prelude, select Y for Credit Bureau Override so that you can enter Credit Bureau codes that are not validated by the system.
