Creating a Gross Profit Table
Gross profit tables allow you to set up the commission percent earned for varying levels of gross profit. When you set up commission rates for a salesrep in Sales Representative Maintenance or Commission Matrix Maintenance (refer to Creating a Commission Matrix), you can assign a gross profit table instead of a constant percent.
To set up a gross profit table:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Gross Profit Range Maintenance to open the Gross Profit Range Maintenance screen.
2
For Table Number, enter an alphanumeric code to represent a gross profit table.
3
Enter a Description of the table.
4
For Min Grs Prof%, define the minimum gross profit percentage that will earn the salesrep the corresponding Commission %.
For example, if you enter 10, 20, and 30 under Min Grs Prof % and 4, 5, and 6 under Commission %; any line items that generate a gross profit amount between 10 and 19.99 percent, will earn the salesrep a commission of 4 percent. Any line items that generate a gross profit percent amount between 20 and 29.99 percent will earn the sales representative a commission of 5 percent. Any line items that generate a gross profit percent of 30 or higher, will earn the salesrep a commission of 6 percent.
5
Press F2-Save to save the gross profit table information.
Gross Profit Range Listing
To view a list of existing gross profit range tables, select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Gross Profit Range Listing.
