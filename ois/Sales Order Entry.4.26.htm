Entering Prescription Products
If you sell products prescribed by doctors, you can set up a product as a prescription so that when a salesperson enters the product on a sales order, they are required to enter the prescription number. You can also set up and track the number of refills, expiration dates, and special instructions to include with each prescription. Refer to Setting up and Tracking Prescriptions for detailed instructions on how to set up prescription and physician information.
Administrative Order Hold for Prescription Products
If a product is set up to require a prescription, you can set up and Administrative Order Hold option (Type X) to make sure that the order goes on Administrative Order Hold if a prescription number is not entered for the product. Refer to System Level Administrative Order Hold Options.
When you enter a prescription product on a sales order, the Prescription Entry screen appears.
1
Enter the Prescription# that is set up for this customer for this product. Press F3 to search for existing prescription numbers. The following information displays:
•
Physician. The number and the name of the physician that prescribed the product for this customer. Refer to Setting up Physician Information.
•
Comment. Any comments entered for this prescription. Refer to Creating a Customer Prescription.
•
Quantity. The quantity prescribed by the physician to fill the prescription.
•
Refills. The number of refills available for this prescription for this customer.
•
Remaining. The total quantity needed to fill the remaining amount on the prescription, including refills. For example, if the original quantity prescribed was 30, with 2 refills, the total quantity remaining would be 90.
•
Renewal. The date the prescription is available for renewal.
•
Phoned. The date the physician was contacted for renewal.
•
Phone Ok’d. A Y indicates that the prescription was phoned in and confirmed.
If the customer has already exceeded their quantity of the prescription available for the month, a warning message appears, but you are still allowed to enter the product.
2
Press F2-Save to open the Line Item Detail screen. Refer to Entering Sales Order Line Items for detailed instructions on entering line items.
 
Complete sales order as usual (refer to Finishing the Sales Order).
