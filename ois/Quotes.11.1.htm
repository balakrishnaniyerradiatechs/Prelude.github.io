Quotation Subsystem
A quote allows you to give a customer price and availability information on on products without affecting or reserving inventory. Once the customer approves the quote, you can convert the entire quote or individual line items into a regular sales order. Another option is a standing quote, which is entered once and then repeatedly converted into a sales order every given number of days. When necessary, you can purge converted, expired, or deleted quotes from the system.
