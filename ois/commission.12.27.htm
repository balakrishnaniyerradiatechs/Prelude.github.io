Changing the Salesrep on an Invoice
If you want to change the salesrep who will receive commission for all line items on an invoice:
1
From the A/R Commission Maintenance screen (refer to Adjusting Commission After Invoicing), select F6-Chg All Rep’s to open the Change ‘All’ Sales Rep’s screen.
From Sales Rep displays the salesrep number currently assigned to the invoice.
2
For To Sales Rep, enter the number for the salesrep that should receive commission for this order.
3
Press F2-Save to save the salesrep commission information and to return to the A/R Commission Line Adjustment screen.
