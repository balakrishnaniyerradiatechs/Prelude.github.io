Gross Profit Range Maintenance screen
The Gross Profit Range Maintenance screen allows you to set up a table to determine commission percentages earned for varying levels of gross profit. When you set up commission rates for a salesrep in Sales Representative Maintenance or Commission Matrix Maintenance (refer to Creating a Commission Matrix), you can assign a gross profit table instead of a constant percent.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Gross Profit Range Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Table Number
An alpha-numeric code to represent the table.
Description
A description of the table.
Min Grs Prof%
The minimum gross profit percentage that will earn the salesrep the corresponding Commission %.
For example, if you enter 10, 20, and 30 under Min Grs Prof % and 4, 5, and 6 under Commission %; any line items that generate a gross profit amount between 10 and 19.99 percent, will earn the salesrep a commission of 4 percent. Any line items that generate a gross profit percent amount between 20 and 29.99, will earn the sales representative a commission of 5 percent. Any line items that generate a gross profit percent of 30 or higher, will earn the salesrep a commission of 6 percent.
Commission%
The commission percent the salesrep will earn for the corresponding gross profit percent.
F2-Save
Saves the gross profit table information.
F4-Del
Deletes the gross profit table.
