Assigning a Warehouse Hold Area to a Sales Order
If you use warehouse hold areas for Tag & Hold sales orders, after you enter the order (refer to Entering a Tag & Hold Sales Order), you can use the Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen to assign a hold area. When the first Tag & Hold order prints, it instructs the warehouse personnel to pick the material and place it in the warehouse hold area. When the customer is ready for the material, the pick ticket that prints instructs the warehouse personnel that the material is in the hold area.
To assign a warehouse hold area to a type 03 Tag & Hold sales order:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Hold Area Assignment to open the Warehouse Hold Area Maintenance screen.
2
For Warehouse, enter the shipping warehouse for the order.
3
For Hold Area Number, enter the hold area where you want the order to be held. Refer to Creating a Warehouse Hold Area.
4
Enter the Order# that you want to assign to the hold area.
5
Press F2-Save to complete the hold area assignment.
