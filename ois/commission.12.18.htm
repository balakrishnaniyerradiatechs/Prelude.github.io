Overriding Commission For a Salesrep
To override the salesrep or the commission cost for sales orders at the header level:
1
Select Order Processing > Sales Order Entry to open the Sales Order Entry screen.
2
Enter the Order Number for which you want to override commission.
3
Select F5-Hdr Scr > Action Bar > Add Info Comm Override to open the Commission Header Override screen.
4
For Rep, enter the salesrep to receive commission for this sales order.
5
For Meth, enter method to calculate commission. Choose from the following:
•
* - Calculate commission as a percentage of the total sale.
•
G - Calculate commission as a percentage of the gross profit.
6
For Rate, enter the percentage of the sale or gross profit to use to calculate commission. For example; if Meth is G and Rate is 3, the salesrep will receive 3% of the gross profit of the sale.
7
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the commission information and to return to the Sales Order Entry screen.
