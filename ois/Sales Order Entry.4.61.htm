Manually Billing Consignment Orders
If you are not using the Auto Consignment Billing Update to bill the customer for an 08 consignment order (if you need to change quantities) or if you are returning the material to stock, you must manually set up the consignment order for invoicing. Each line item can be billed or returned to stock or a combination of both. The whole order does not have to be billed at once.
To bill a customer or return products to stock for an 08 consignment order:
1
In Sales Order Entry (refer to Entering a Sales Order), enter the 08 consignment invoice number.
2
Press ENTER for each line item to open the Consignment Order Detail screen.
3
For A (Accept) enter N.
4
In Qty-Shp, enter the quantity for which you want to invoice the customer.
5
In QtyStk, enter the quantity to return to stock.
6
Press F2-Save.
7
Repeat steps 2-6 for each line item to bill or return to stock.
8
Press F2-Save.
9
Choose Complete to set the order up for invoicing.
When the order is invoiced, the quantities are deducted from the consigned inventory balances.
