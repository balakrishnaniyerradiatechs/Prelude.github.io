Monthly Credit Memo Register
The Monthly Credit Memo Register lists all credit memos issued for each warehouse for a month or fiscal period. For more information on credit memos, refer to Entering a Credit Memo.
This report excludes cash sales and type 07 & 08 consignment orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Credit Memo Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period for which you want to view credit memos.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Inv/Order#
Line 1: The credit memo or invoice number.
Line 2: The order number.
Inv Date
The date the credit was issued.
Cust#
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Disc/All
The total order discount amount allowed as shown in Disc/Allowance in the Sales Order Completion screen.
Misc-Chgs
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Refer to Entering a Miscellaneous Product.
Frt/Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that was credited.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was credited.
Min/Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that was credited. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise credited.
Dep-Amt
The current deposit balance.
Inv Amount
The total amount of the credit, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost
The total cost of the merchandise on the order.
G/P%
The gross profit percent calculated.
