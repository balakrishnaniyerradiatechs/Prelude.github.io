Other Sales Rep Input screen
The Other Salesrep Input screen allows you to add additional salesreps to receive commission for orders meeting matrix criteria (refer to Creating a Commission Matrix for more information).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Matrix Maintenance > F5-Add Rep
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Add Rep#
Any additional salesreps to whom you will pay commissions along with the salesreps entered on the Commission Matrix Input screen and/or the salesreps defined in the sales order (e.g., to pay a manager’s commission).
Description
The description of the salesrep.
Method
The method to determine how commission is calculated:
•
$ - Commission is paid as a dollar amount. Enter the amount in Price.
•
* - Commission is paid as a percent of sales. Enter the Percent of the sale that determines commission.
•
G - Commission is paid as a percent of the gross profit of the order. Enter the Percent of gross profit to pay.
•
T - Commission is based on a percent of gross profit using a gross profit table (refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table). Enter the Gross Pft Tbl number.
Grs Profit Tbl
If Mtd is T, the gross profit table which allows you to vary commission by the gross profit amount on the sales order. Refer to Creating a Gross Profit Table.
Percent
If Mtd is *, the percent of the sale that determines commission.
If Mtd is G, the percent of the gross profit that determines commission.
Price
If Mtd is $, the amount of commission to pay the salesrep.
F2-Save
Saves the salesrep commission information.
