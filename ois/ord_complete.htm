Sales Order Completion screen
When you finish a sales order and choose Complete (refer to Finishing the Sales Order), the Sales Order Completion screen appears to allow you to add freight, delivery, or handling charges or to take payment for the order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > F2-Save
