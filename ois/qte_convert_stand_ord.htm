Converting Standing Quotes
If you created a type 04 Standing Quote in Quote Order Entry (refer to Entering a Quote), you must use Standing Quote Conversion to convert the quote into a sales order. The quotes are converted based on the Frequency specified in the Standing Order Maintenance screen in Quote Order Entry.
To convert standing quotes into sales orders:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Processes > Standing Quote Conversion to open the Standing Quote Conversion screen.
2
Enter the Selling Warehouse for which you want to convert standing quotes.
3
Enter the beginning Conversion Date. Any standing quote whose Required Date is after this date, is converted.
4
Enter the ending Conversion Date. Any standing quote whose Required Date is on or before this date, is converted. For example, if you enter today’s date, any standing quote whose required date is today or previous to today is converted to a sales order.
5
Once you choose Execute, the standing quotes are converted into sales orders.
Once the quotes are converted, the Required Date for each quote is increased by the number of Frequency days that you entered in the Standing Order Maintenance screen in Quote Order Entry.
Frequency in the Standing Order Maintenance screen is determined using one of the following methods:
•
To convert the quote every set number of days, enter the number of days (e.g., enter 45 to convert the quote every 45 days).
•
To convert the quote once a month, enter a D in front of the day of the month (e.g., enter D15 to convert the quote on the 15th of every month).
•
To convert the quote bi-monthly (every other month) enter a B in front of the day of the month (e.g., enter B15 to convert the quote on the 15th of every other month).
•
To convert the quote every quarter (every 3 months) enter a Q in front of the day of the month (e.g., enter Q15 to convert the quote on the 15th of every third month).
•
To convert the quote semi-annually (every 6 months) enter an S in front of the day of the month (e.g., enter S15 to convert the quote on the 15th every 6 months).
