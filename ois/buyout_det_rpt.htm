Nonstock (Buyout) Report
The Nonstock (Buyout) report displays nonstock sales for a ship date to allow you to track miscellaneous (asterisk), nonstock products that do not have part numbers. The report is sorted by the vendor product number as entered on the sales order.
After you run the report, you can choose to purge the information so that it is not included the next time you run the report.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Nonstock (Buyout)

Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vend#
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Vendor Part Number
The vendor part number as entered on the sales order.
Order#
The sales order number.
Cust#
The customer number.
Shp-Date
The date the product shipped out of the warehouse.
Shp-Qty
The quantity shipped.
Net Cost
The cost of the product from the sales order.
Net Price
The price billed to the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
YTD Qty
The quantity bought by this customer year-to-date.
