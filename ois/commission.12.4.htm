Company Maintenance
To determine if commission matrices are used to calculate commission:
1
In Company Maintenance, select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Commission Matrix, determine if the system checks for commission matrices to calculate commission:
•
Y - Use commission matrices from Commission Matrix Maintenance and the commission rates from Sales Representative Maintenance.
•
N - Do not use commission matrices to calculate commission.
Commission matrices are used to vary the commission rate or salesrep for customers and/or products (refer to Creating a Commission Matrix).
3
Press F2-Save to save the commission matrix information.
