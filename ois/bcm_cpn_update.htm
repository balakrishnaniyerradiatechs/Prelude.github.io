Copying a Blanket Contract
If you want to use the pricing and terms information from a customer’s blanket contract for another customer, you can copy the contract and then change any information as necessary.
To copy a contract to one or more customers:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Blanket Contract Maintenance > Blanket Contract Maintenance to open the Blanket Contract Maintenance screen.
2
Press F5-Copy to open the Blanket Contract Copy screen.
3
Under Source, enter the Customer Number whose blanket contract you are copying.
4
Enter the Blanket Contract# that you will copy.
5
For Destination, enter the Customer Number that is to receive the blanket contract information. You can enter multiple customers.
6
Enter the Contract# to identify the contract for the destination customer.
7
For Copy CPN?, determine if you want the customer part numbers of the source customer to copy to the destination customer’s blanket contract. Choose from the following:
•
N - Do not copy the customer part numbers to the destination customer.
•
Y - Copy the customer part numbers to the destination customer.
If you select Y for Copy CPN?, you must run the Blanket Contract CPN Mass Update (Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Blanket Contract Maintenance > Blanket Contract CPN Mass Update) to copy the customer part numbers from the source blanket contract to the destination blanket contract.
8
For Copy Comm Mgmt, determine if the commodity management fields are copied from the source blanket contract to the destination blanket contract. The commodity management fields are the custom items from the contract (BOH Min, BOH Beg, NOQ, Target Price, Use Code, and Type). Choose from the following:
•
N - Do not copy the commodity management fields to the destination customer.
•
Y - Copy the commodity management fields to the destination customer. You must run the Blanket Contract CPN Mass Update to update the customer part numbers.
9
Choose the appropriate Option to copy the blanket contract:
•
1 - Clear and replace the blanket contract if one exists for the destination customer number entered. All header information is copied to the new blanket contract. Manually change the header information if needed.
•
2 - Append new lines to the destination contracts. Use this option if you added new line items to the source contract and want to update the destination contracts. The destination customers must have existing contracts.
•
3 - Update and append to the destination contracts. Use this option if you want to update all price and rebate values for all lines on the blanket contract. The destination customers must have existing contracts.
10
Press F2-Save. The Blanket Contact Maintenance screen appears with the contract information for the new customer.
11
Modify the blanket contract if necessary.
12
Press F2-Save to save the contract information.
