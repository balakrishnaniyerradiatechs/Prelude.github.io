Using Serial Shipping Container Codes
A Serial Shipping Container Code (SSCC) is a unique identification system used to identify a carton or pallet. SSCCs provide quality assurance by assigning codes and printing labels to verify shipped products and quantities. If your customer requires an SSCC for every shipment they receive, you can use Serial Shipping Container Entry to confirm the releases and print the detail label information.
