Upsell Entry screen
If you enter a product on a sales order that has an upsell product assigned to it, a dialog box appears asking if you want to review the upsell products. If you answer Yes, the Upsell Entry screen appears. You can review the products and replace the original product if necessary. Refer to Entering Upsell Products for more information on adding upsell products to a sales order.
Upsell Products
An upsell product is a more expensive product or list of products that you may want to offer when a customer asks for an item. Upsell products are assigned to a product in the Upsell Product Maintenance screen (Product Maintenance > F6-Xref/Notes). Refer to “Upsell Products” in the Inventory documentation for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > enter a product that has upsell products assigned to it
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Number
The product that was originally entered on the sales order that has upsell product assigned to it.
Price
The price of the original product.
Product Number
The product that can be offered as an upsell product when the customer asks for the original product. This might be a more expensive or newer product that could be sold instead of the product that the customer asks for.
Description
The description of the upsell product.
Available
The quantity of the upsell product that is available.
Price
The customer’s price for the upsell product.
Difference
The difference in price between the original product and the upsell product.
Script
An asterisk (*) indicates that a script is available to help you sell the upsell product. The script appears below.
Script (bottom of screen)
Notes regarding the upsell product that might help you to encourage the customer to purchase the more expensive product.
F2-Save
Closes the screen but does not replace the original product with the upsell product.
F5-Accept
Replaces the original product on the sales order with the upsell product.
