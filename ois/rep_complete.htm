M/R Completion screen
The M/R Completion screen allows you to record the check information from a vendor when they pay you the commission for a manufacturer’s rep order. For more information, refer to Completing a Manufacturers’s Rep Order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturers Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Entry > F2-Save
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Merchandise Amount
The total value of merchandise ordered by the customer.
Expected commission
The total amount of commission from the vendor that you are expected to earn from this customer’s order.
Actual Commission
The actual amount of commission paid from the vendor for the quantity shipped.
Check Number
The vendor’s check number.
Check Date
The date of the vendor’s check.
Check Amount
The amount of the vendor’s check.
F2-Save
Saves the information and asks if you are ready to process the order (refer to Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order).
