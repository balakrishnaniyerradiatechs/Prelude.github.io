Order History Inquiry Display screen
The Order History Inquiry Display screen displays detail shipping and pricing information for an invoiced sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Inquiries > Sales Order Inquiries > Sales Order History Inquiry > F5-Review
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The sales order number.
Customer
The customer number and description.
Product
The product sold to the customer.
Description
The product description.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates if notes are attached to the sales order.
Whse
The shipping warehouse.
Quantity
The quantity shipped.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Qty-Pck
The quantity the system shows as picked.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped to the customer.
Qty-BO
The backordered quantity of the product.
Grs Price
The gross price of the product.
Disc%
The discount percent applied to the gross price on the sales order.
Net Price
The net price of the product (Grs Price x Disc%).
Extension
The value of the total quantity of the product sold to the customer (Net Price x Qty Shp).
Cust Product#
The customer’s product number for the product.
Weight
The total weight of all the products sold.
Cost
The total cost for sales for all products sold.
Profit
The total gross profit amount earned on this sales order.
Gp%
The average gross profit percent of all the products sold.
S/O Amount
The total value of the products sold to the customer.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Sales Order Header screen.
F6-Completion screen
Opens the Sales Order Completion screen to display freight and closing costs for the sales order.
F7-Log
Opens the Order Log Inquiry which lists every transaction that affected the sales order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
Action Bar
Examine
Displays detailed product, price, cost, and shipping information for the sales order.
Notes

•

•
Service Solution Notes.
Prod Func
Allows access to Campaign Priority Maintenance.
Order Func
Allows access to the following:
•
Campaign priority codes.
•
Campaign Priority Maintenance.
•
Prefix settings.
•
Summary G/L Recap Report.
•
Detail G/L Recap Report.
•
Invoice reprints.
Images

Invoice.

Contract.
•

Customer PN Docs.

Returned Goods.
•
Product Images.

Customer Images.
