Notice of Commencement Report
The Notice of Commencement report lists customer jobs for each state that had a notice printed to announce that work is about to commence on a project.
Print Commencement must be set to Y in Customer Job Maintenance > F6-Lien/Bond for this customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Lien / Bond Reports > Notice Of Commencement Report
Selection Criteria
