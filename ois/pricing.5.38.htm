Setting System Options for Promotional Price Matrices
The following systems options determine how promotional price matrices are applied:
Order Processing Parameters
To activate your system for promotional price matrices:
1
In Order Processing Parameters, select F5-Add Info > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Options screen.
2
For Promotional Pricing, determine if promotional price matrices are allowed:
•
Y - You can create promotional price matrices.
•
N - Promotional pricing is not allowed.
3
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the promotional pricing information.
 
Marketing Parameters
If you use the Marketing module, you can set the following flags to enable marketing campaigns to work with the promotional price matrices:
1
In Marketing Parameters, for Campaign Priority Code, determine if priority codes are required:
•
Y - A campaign code is required for every sales order.
•
N - Campaign codes are not required for sales orders, you cannot enter a code.
•
O - Campaign codes are optional. You may enter a code.
2
For Utilize Campaign Pricing, determine if customers on campaign lists receive campaign pricing:
•
Y - Customers on campaign list receive campaign pricing for sales orders and quotes.
•
N - Do not use campaign pricing.
3
Press F2-Save to save the marketing information.
