Change Credit Card Exp Date screen
If an order fails post-authorization due to an expired credit card, you can manually change the expiration date and then re-run the post-authorization. The change also updates the credit card information on the sales order. Refer to Changing an Expiration Date.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Change Expiration Date
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order Number
The sales order number that failed the post-authorization.
Type
The type of credit card (e.g., VISA, M/C).
Card Number
The credit card number.
Exp
The correct expiration date for the credit card.
Amount
The amount of the sales order.
Approval
The amount approved.
Message
Any message entered during post-authorization, that might explain why the authorization failed.
F2-Force Authorize
Completes the update.
