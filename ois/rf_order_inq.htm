RF Scanned Orders Inquiry
The RF Scanned Orders Inquiry lists sales orders that were suspended in one of the Radio Frequency Order Entry submenus in the Warehousing Module.
Viewing Suspended RF Sales Orders
To list suspended orders:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Sales Order Inquiries > RF Scanned Order Inquiry to open the RF Scanned Orders Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the shipping Warehouse Number.
3
Enter the Sort sequence for the list. Choose from the following:
•
O - Order number.
•
D - Order date.
•
R - Required date.
•
T - Order type.
Suspended orders display.
