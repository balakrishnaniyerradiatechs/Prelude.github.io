Customer P/O Percentage Report
The Customer P/O Percentage report displays blanket purchase order information for a customer. You can determine how close the customer is to reaching the blanket purchase order limit. Refer to Blanket Contracts for information on setting up and tracking blanket purchase orders for a customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer P/O Percentage Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report.
•
Percentage Amount >. Include all blanket purchase orders for which the customer has purchased more than this percent of the total purchase order.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer#
The customer number.
Description
The customer description.
P/O#
The customer’s blanket purchase order number. Refer to Creating a Blanket Contract.
P/O Limit
The blanket purchase order amount limit.
P/O Balance
The amount remaining on the blanket purchase order. Refer to Tracking Blanket P/O Information for a Customer.
% of Limit
The percentage of the purchase order balance that remains compared to the purchase order limit (P/O Balance / P/O Limit).
P/O Expire
The date the blanket purchase order expires.
