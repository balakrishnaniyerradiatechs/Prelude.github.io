Overriding Commission for One Line Item
If a salesrep should receive credit for only one item on a sales order, you can override the commission for just one line.
You cannot override commission at the line item level if commission for the order is already split between more than one salesperson (refer to Splitting Commission Between Salesreps).
To override salesrep commission for one line item on a sales order:
1
In the sales order Line Item detail screen (refer to Entering a Sales Order for more information on entering a sales order), enter C for Accept to open the Commission Override screen.
2
For Rep, enter the number for the salesrep that should receive commission for this line item.
3
For Type, choose from the following to determine how to calculate commission for this line item:
•
* - Calculate commission as a percentage of the total sale. Enter the percentage the salesrep will receive in Rate.
•
G - Calculate commission as a percentage of the gross profit. Enter the percentage the salesrep will receive in Rate.
•
$ - The salesrep will receive a fixed dollar amount for the sale of this product. Enter the dollar amount in Rate.
4
Press F2-Save to return to the Line Item Detail screen.
