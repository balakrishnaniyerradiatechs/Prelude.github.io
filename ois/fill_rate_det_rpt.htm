Fill Rate Detail Report
The Fill Rate Detail Report lists the percentage of products that were available when sales orders shipped. This information can help to determine how well you are servicing your customers.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Fill Rate Detail
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The shipping warehouse where the pick tickets printed.
•
Customer. The customers to include.
•
Ship Date. The date the products shipped.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Order
The sales order number.
Invoice
The invoice number.
Customer/
Description
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The description of the customer.
Product/Description
Line 1: The product sold to the customer.
Line 2: The description of the product.
Ordered
The quantity of the product ordered by the customer.
Shipped
The quantity of the product that shipped to the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Percent
The fill rate percent (e.g., 100.00 indicates that the line item shipped complete, 0.00 indicates that the line item did not ship complete).
