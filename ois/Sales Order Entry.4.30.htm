Taking Payment for an Order
To take payment for an 05 Order Type or a sales order with cash terms:
1
Press F2-Complete on the Sales Order Completion screen (refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen) to open the Cash Application screen.
2
For Pay Type, choose from the following:
•
01 - Cash.
•
02 - Check. The Additional Check Information screen appears to allow you to enter the check number, driver’s license number and other personal information for the customer to authorize the check.
A driver’s license number is required if the Driver Lic Req/Checks flag in Option Parameters > Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add info is set to Y.
•
03 - Bank Card. Opens the Additional Credit Card Information screen to allow you to enter the credit card type, number, and expiration date information. Refer to Credit Cards.
•
11 - Gift Certificate. Opens the Additional Gift Certificate Information screen to allow you to enter the gift certificate number. Refer to Gift Certificates.
Pay types are set up in Order Processing Controls > Payment Methods.
3
For Payment Amount, enter the amount paid by the customer.
4
Press F2-Save. The following message appears:
Print Invoice?
5
Choose from the following:
•
Yes. If you use continuous printing for invoices, the invoice prints immediately. Refer to Invoicing.
•
No. No invoice prints.
