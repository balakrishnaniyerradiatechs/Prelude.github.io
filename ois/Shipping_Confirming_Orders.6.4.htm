Sending Shipping Confirmations
If your customer would like to receive verification that an order shipped, you can send a shipping confirmation that lists the products and quantities shipped on an order. You can set maintenance options by warehouse and by customer to determine whether confirmations print, fax, or email as soon the order is released for invoicing in Release Confirmation Entry or if they are held in a queue and printed by batch.
