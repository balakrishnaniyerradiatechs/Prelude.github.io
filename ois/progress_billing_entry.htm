Progress Billing Entry screen
The Progress Billing Entry screen allows you to bill a customer for a part or a percent of a sales order for a customer job. You can also view the estimated and actual sales, costs, and gross profit percents billed to date. Once you save the Progress Billing Entry screen the following occurs:
•
The order is set up for invoicing.
•
The service product on the sales order is duplicated to control this portion of the billing. The Bill Amount becomes the sales price and the Bill Cost becomes the cost.
•
Any Billing Comments entered in Progress Billing Entry are added as comments on the sales order.
•
The sales price of the original line item is reduced to show the remaining value.
Refer to “Progress Billing” in the Work Order Documentation for more information on using progress billing for bill of material jobs on work orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Optional Entry & Printing > Customer Job Progress Billing Entry
