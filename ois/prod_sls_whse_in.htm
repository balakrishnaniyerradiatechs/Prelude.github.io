Product Sales History Inq-Shipping Warehouse
The Product Sales History Inquiry by Shipping Warehouse lists invoiced sales orders for a product for a shipping warehouse. You can view the detail order information for each invoice.
The Product Sales History Xref Rebuild updates this inquiry.
Viewing Product Sales by Warehouse
To list sales orders for a product/shipping warehouse:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Product Sales Hist Inq-Ship Whse to open the Product Sales History Inquiry - Shipping Warehouse screen.
2
Enter the Product Number.
3
Enter the shipping Warehouse number.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Order#
The sales order number.
Customer
The number for the customer that purchased the product.
Description
The customer description.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that notes exist for the order.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Ord-Date
The date the customer ordered the material.
Qty-Ord
The quantity ordered by the customer.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the sales order.
Qty-Shp
The quantity of the product that was available to ship.
Shp-Date
The date the product shipped.
C
The inventory classification code assigned to the product.
Net Price
The net price that the customer paid for the product.
Linkage
The linked order number if the product was linked to a purchase order or transfer.
R
Displays an R if the product was used for a type 10 repair order.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen which displays detail product and pricing information for the entire order.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
sF5-Prev Group
Returns to the previous page if more than one page of sales orders are included in the inquiry.
sF6-Next Group
Proceeds to the next page if more than one page of sales orders are included in the inquiry.
