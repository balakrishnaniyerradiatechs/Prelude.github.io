Text Emailing an Individual Shipping Confirmation
You can text email a shipping confirmation to a customer for a sales order after it ships by just entering a release number and an email address. The customer does not have to be set up to automatically receive text email shipping confirmations.
Text Email Documents
Email Document Text Maintenance (Miscellaneous Controls) must be set up with the ORDSHP document code to allow you to text email a shipping confirmation.
To text email a shipping confirmation to a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry & Printing > Stand Alone Email Shipping Notice to open the Stand-Alone Email Shipping Notice screen.
2
Enter the Release Number on which the material shipped. The Customer number and name display.
3
Verify or enter the Email Address. The email address from the sales order appears, but you can override it.
4
Press F2-Send Email to text email the confirmation.
