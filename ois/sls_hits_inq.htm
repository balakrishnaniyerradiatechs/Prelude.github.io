Sales Hits Inquiry
The Sales Hits Inquiry displays the number of times a product was invoiced for a shipping warehouse. The inquiry contains the number of hits per month for this year, last year, and the year before last.
One sales hit is recorded each time the product is sold - regardless of the quantity on the order. Backorders and credits are excluded from sales hit calculations.
Viewing Sales Order Hits
To display sales hits for a product/warehouse:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Sales Hits Inquiry to open the Sales Hits Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Product. The product description appears.
3
Enter the Warehouse for which you want to view sales hits.
Sales hit history for a product appears.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Primary Bin
The primary bin number if one is assigned to the product.
CY Hits
Current year sales hits. The number of times the product was invoiced for each month of the current year.
PY Hits
Previous year sales hits. The number of times the product was invoiced for each month of the previous year.
PY2 Hits
Second previous year sales hits. The number of times the product was invoiced for each month of the year before last.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Bin Inquiry
Opens the Multiple Bin Inquiry screen to list all bins where the product may exist.
