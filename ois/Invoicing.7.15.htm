Confirming a Release that Requires a DD250 Invoice
If a sales order is set up to print a DD250 invoice (refer to Entering a Sales Order that Requires a DD250 Invoice), when you confirm the shipment, the DD250 screen appears to allow you to enter government information.
Additional Option Parameters
Order Completion Inquiry from RCE must be set to A so that the Sales Order Completion screen appears in Release Confirmation Entry after you confirm the shipped quantities.
To enter DD250 information in Release Confirmation Entry:
1
After you save the Sales Order Completion screen (refer to Confirming Releases), the DD250 Information screen appears.
2
For Acceptance Point, indicate where the products will be inspected. Defaults from the Customer Maintenance but you can override it. This information prints in block 8 of the DD250 Form. Choose from the following:
•
S - At the source.
•
D - At the destination.
•
N - Neither.
3
Enter the FOB Point for freight to print in block 11 of the DD250 Form. Choose from the following:
•
S - Source.
•
D - Destination.
4
If applicable, enter a Shipment Number (for information only) to print in block 2 on the DD250 form.
5
For Bill Of Lading, accept the default from the sales order or enter a new one to appear in block 4 on the DD250 form.
6
For Trans. Control Number, enter the transaction control number (for information only) to print in block 4 (TCN) on the DD250 Form.
7
Enter the Govt. Ship Via Number (for information only) to print in block 4 on the DD250 form.
8
Enter the Payment By Code (refer to Creating Payment-By Codes) to print in block 12 on the DD250 form.
9
Enter the Administered By code (refer to Creating Administered-By Codes) or enter M to enter a manual Administered By code, name, and address, to print in block 10 on the DD250 Form.
10
Enter the Ship From Code, name, and address to print in block 11 on the DD250 form (block 11). If you leave this field blank, then “Same as block 9” prints in its place.
11
For Quality Assurance Rep, enter the name of the quality assurance representative to print in block 21 on the DD250 form next to the TYPED NAME field.
12
Enter any Packaging Information to print in block 16 on the DD250 form immediately below the FREIGHT TOTAL field.
13
Enter up to 8 lines of Contractor Notes to print in block 23 on the DD250 form.
14
Press ENTER to complete the release confirmation and to set the order up for invoicing. Refer to Printing Invoices Using the DD250 Form.
