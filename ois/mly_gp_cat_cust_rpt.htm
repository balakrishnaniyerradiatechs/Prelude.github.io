Monthly Gross Profit Report by Catalog/Customer
If you use catalogs to sell your products, the Monthly Gross Profit Report by Catalog/Customer displays the gross profit amount and percent by catalog and customer for a fiscal period. Once you create a catalog number to identify a catalog (refer to Creating a Catalog ID Code), you can assign it to part numbers in Product Maintenance > F6-Xref-Notes > Alternates > Catalog Id.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Gross Profit Reports > By Catalog Code/Customer
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Period Range. The fiscal period range to include.
•
Catalog Id Code. The catalog ID code (set up in the Catalog ID Code Maintenance screen) for which you want to view gross profit and percent.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the material.
•
Customer. The customer for which you want to view the monthly gross profit and sales.
•
Product Line. The product lines to include.
•
Product. The products to include.
•
G/P Based On Acct/Comm Cost. Determines if the gross profit is calculated based on accounting or commission cost:
-
A - Accounting cost.
-
C - Commission cost.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Period
The fiscal period.
Cat ID
The catalog number (refer to Creating a Catalog ID Code).
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the products.
Cust#/Ship To Name
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The description of the customer’s ship-to address.
First Sale
The first date the product sold.
Last Sale
The last date the product sold.
Pline
The product line number.
Product/Description/Alternate
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The alternate product number, if applicable.
Qty-Opn
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Profit
The gross profit amount earned on sales of the product.
GP%
The gross profit percentage earned on sales of the product.
