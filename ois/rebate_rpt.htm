Rebate Report
The Rebate report lists rebate amounts calculated when sales orders containing rebate price matrices are invoiced. Refer to Creating a Price Matrix for information on rebate prices.
After you run the report, you can choose to purge the product information so that it is not included the next time you run the report.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Rebate Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Vendor. The vendor associated with the rebates.
•
Customer. The customer to receive the rebate amounts.
•
Invoice Date Range. The beginning and ending dates for which you want to view rebates.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The selling warehouse number.
Customer
The number for the customer who earned the rebate.
Description
The customer description.
Ord/Inv
Line 1: The sales order number that contains the rebate items.
Line 2: The invoice number from which the rebate was calculated.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
T
The rebate calculation method assigned in Price Matrix Maintenance:
•
$ - The rebate is calculated using a specific rebate cost.
•
* - The rebate is calculated using a percent of Basis.
•
T - The rebate is calculated using a quantity discount table.
B
Rebate basis field. The price or cost that is the basis for the rebate:
•
0 - Suggested retail.
•
1 - Price1.
•
2 - Price2.
•
3 - Price3.
•
4 - Price4.
•
5 - Price5.
•
A - Average cost.
•
B - Product quantity break.
•
C - Customer default column.
•
O - Cost for sales.
•
R - Replacement cost.
•
S - Standard cost.
Reb Amt/Pcnt
The value of the rebate if a price/cost was entered. The percent of the rebate if basis was used.
Qty-Shp
The quantity of the rebate product on this invoice.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Actual Amt
The extended rebate amount due for this order.
