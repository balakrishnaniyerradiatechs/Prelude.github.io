Quality Incident Entry
A Quality Incident is the collection of all feedback entries (refer to Quality Feedback Entry and corrective action entries (refer to Quality Corrective Action Entry) made for a complaint. You can use this screen to tie together all parts of the Quality Inspection Subsystem to maintain quality control on products from manufacturers and to improve service to customers.
When you create a Quality incident, not all of the information may be available to fill in all of the fields. It is normally held open for awhile to allow you to add accumulative feedback and corrective action information as it is received.
You can also access this screen in Quality Feedback Entry > F5-New Incident.
To enter a quality incident:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quality Inspection Subsystem > Quality Incident Entry to open the Quality Incident Entry screen.
2
For Incident#, choose from the following:
•
Press ENTER over NEW to create a new incident number.
•
Enter an existing incident number.
•
Press F3 to search for previously created incidents.
3
For Status, choose from the following:
•
O - Open.
•
C - Closed. You cannot close the incident until all corrective actions have been completed.
•
I - In process.
•
U - Unassigned.
•
W - Waiting for response.
4
Enter a Description of the problem. This description may include a combination of complaints all regarding one product or employee.
5
For Incident Type, enter the type of incident such as customer return or complaint.
6
Under Sources, for Type, enter the source type of the incident. Choose from the following:
•
F - Feedback entry. For Document, enter the feedback number created in Quality Feedback Entry. The Description of the complaint, the quality Agent, and the Inspection Type display.
•
I - Inspection result entry. For Document, enter the inspection result number (refer to Inspection Results Entry).
•
R - Return sales order. For Document, enter the credit memo number.
7
If you have already entered corrective actions for the complaint (refer to Quality Corrective Action Entry), you can add them to the incident under Corrective Actions. The status and agent information display.
To review detail information for an incident type entry or corrective action, place the cursor either on the Source Type or the Action# and press F5-Review.
8
Press F2-Save to save the incident information.
You can access the incident later to view or add information or to change the incident status. Once all issues are resolved, you can change the status to C (closed).
