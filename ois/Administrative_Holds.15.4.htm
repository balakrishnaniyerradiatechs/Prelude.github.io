Branch Hold for Sales Orders
To define branch hold options for sales orders:
1
Select Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Interbranch/Surplus Only, choose from the following:
•
Y - Sales orders shipped out of another warehouse will go on Branch Hold if the entire quantity cannot be filled from surplus inventory at the shipping warehouse.
•
N - Sales orders will not go on branch hold when shipping out of another warehouse.
3
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the interbranch information.
Refer to Product Purchasing Parameters Maintenance in the Inventory documentation for information on safety stock levels and surplus inventory
