Customer Job Maintenance Screen
The Customer Job Maintenance screen allows you to set up job information for a customer. Customer jobs are used when a portion of the customerâ€™s purchases are segregated for pricing and/or credit purposes. You can use the job number in Customer Maintenance, Customer Ship-To Maintenance, Quotation Entry, or Sales Order Entry for the customer.
Require Job Number in the Order Information Screen determines customer job number requirements.
Customer Job in File Update Parameters determines if customer job information is maintained whenever a job record is created and referenced in Sales Order Entry.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers
