Assigning Price Label Information to Customers
Once you set up the price label format (refer to Formatting Price Labels), you can assign price label format code to a customer in Customer Maintenance to determine if price labels print and how much to charge the customer. Labels only print for products set up to print labels (refer to Assigning Price Label Information to Products).
To assign price label information to a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Customer and press ENTER.
3
From the Action Bar select Add’l Opts > Labels Information to open the Customer Labels Information Screen.
4
Set Print Price Label to Y to open the Price Label Information screen.
5
To determine how much to charge a customer for each price label, choose one of the following methods, but not both:
•
Charge per Label: Enter the amount to charge the customer for each label printed.
•
Label Charge Percent: Enter the percent of the sales amount to charge for each label.
6
For Itemize Prc Lbl Chg, indicate if price label charges are itemized on the invoice:
•
Y - The price label charge is added to the order when it is released for invoicing and prints under Misc Chg. You can view the label charge in the Sales Order Inquiry and in the Sales Order History Inquiry.
•
N - The price label charge is added to the price of the product by line item on a sales order.
7
For Apply Price Label, determine if price labels are attached to products before they ship:
•
Y - Attach the price labels on products before shipping.
•
N - Send the labels with the shipment but do not attach them.
8
For Price Label Format Code, enter the Format Code from the Price Label Format Maintenance screen that identifies the design of the printed label. Refer to Formatting Price Labels.
9
For Prc Sticker Name Abbr, enter an abbreviation of the customer’s name to print on the price labels.
10
Press F2-Save to save the price label information.
