M/R Detail Information screen
The M/R Detail Information screen allows you to record the shipping and commission information for each product on a manufacturer’s rep order. For more information, refer to Entering a Manufacturer’s Rep Order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturers Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Entry > Enter a Product
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number of the product on the order.
Quantity
The quantity that the customer ordered.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Qty-Shp
The quantity that shipped, according to the vendor.
Qty-B/O
The quantity not yet shipped.
Paid Qty
The quantity for which the vendor is paying you commission.
Net Price
The price the customer paid for the product.
Comm%
The percent of the Net Price that you will receive as commission from the vendor for this sale.
Comm-Rate
No longer applicable.
Exp-Comm
The total amount of commission that you should expect to receive for this line item once it ships.
Ack-Date
The date the vendor first sent you an acknowledgement of the customer’s order.
Ship Date
The date the vendor shipped the products to the customer.
Manufacturer Ref#
The reference number from the manufacturer’s order, if applicable.
Act-Comm
The actual amount of commission paid to you by the vendor (Paid Qty x Net Price x Comm%/100). This is automatically updated when you enter a Paid Qty.
F2-Save
Saves the information and returns to the Manufacturer Rep Order Entry screen.
