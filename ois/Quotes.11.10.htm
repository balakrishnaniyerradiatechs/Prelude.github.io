Converting Line Items from Quotes
If you are in Sales Order Entry and you enter a product for which a quote already exists for the customer, depending on system flags (refer to Quote Conversion Flags), you can convert the quote directly onto the sales order.
To convert a line item from quote:
1
If the customer has an existing open quote for a product that you enter on a sales order for the same customer, the following message appears:
Customer Has Open Quotations For This Product! Do You Wish To Convert A Quote Line?
Choose from the following:
•
No - The Line Item Detail screen appears and you can enter the order as usual.
•
Yes - Opens the Quote Line Item Conversion screen to display the prices quoted to the customer for the product.
2
For Conv, indicate if you want to convert the quote line item onto the sales order:
•
Y - Convert the quote line item to the sales order. The price and quantity are pulled from the quote. If the quote is on Administrative Quote Hold, it must be released before it can be converted.
•
N - Do not use this quote for the sales order.
3
Press F2-Save:
•
If you chose Y for Conv, the line item appears on the Sales Order Entry screen with the quantity and price from the quote.
•
If you chose N for Conv, the Line Item Detail screen appears to allow you to enter quantity and price information.
