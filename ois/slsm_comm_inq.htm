Salesrep Commission Inquiry
The Salesrep Commission Inquiry displays sales, gross profit, and commission information for all invoiced sales orders for a sales representative.
User Identification Maintenance
Salesrep in User Identification Maintenance > F5-Administrative determines if a user can enter salesrep numbers in the Salesrep Commission Inquiry.
Viewing Salesrep Commission
To commission information for a sales representative:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Salesman Commission Inquiry to open the Salesrep Commission Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the number for the Sale Rep whose commission you want to view.
3
Enter a Begin Date and an End Date for the invoiced sales orders that you want to view.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Sales Rep
The number for the salesrep whose commission you want to view.
Begin Date
The beginning invoice date for the sales orders that you want to view.
End Date
The ending invoice date for the sales orders that you want to view.
Invoice
The invoice number.
Order
The sales order number.
Inv Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Sales
Total sales amount invoiced on the corresponding sales order.
Gp
The total gross profit amount earned on the sales order.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent earned on the sales order.
Comm$
The total commission amount earned by the salesrep for this order.
F2-Save
Close the inquiry screen
