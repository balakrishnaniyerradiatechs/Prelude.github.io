Copying a Customer Contract
To copy a contract from one customer to another:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Copy to open the Copy Contracts screen.
2
For Copy From Customer, enter the customer whose contract you want to copy.
3
For Copy From Contract, enter the contract number that you want to copy.
4
For Copy To Customer, enter the customer that should receive the contract information.
5
For Copy To Contract, enter the new contract number for the Copy to Customer.
6
Press F2-Copy to copy the contract information (excluding ship-to information).
