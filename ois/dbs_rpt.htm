Daily Business Statistics Report
The Daily Business Statistics Report displays daily sales, bookings, inventory value, A/P, and A/R information by warehouse. Refer to the Daily Business Update for information on generating daily business statistics.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Summary Reports > Daily Business Statistics > Daily Business Statistics Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse for which you want to view daily statistics. The selling warehouse is the warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse ships the merchandise.
•
Report Statistics For Date. The date for which you want to view business statistics.
•
Calendar or Fiscal Dates. Determines whether the totals reflect the calendar year or the fiscal year:
-
C - Calendar year.
-
F - Fiscal year.
•
Company Totals Only. Determines whether to print the full report or totals only:
-
Y - Print company totals only.
-
N - Print the full report.
Column Descriptions
Heading
Description
Warehouse Sales
The total value of sales orders invoiced for products (stock, non-linked) shipped out of the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
G/M Whse Sales
The total gross margin value earned on sales orders invoiced for products shipped out of the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Direct Sales
The total value of sales orders invoiced for direct ship products for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
G/M Dir Sales
The total gross margin value earned on direct ship sales orders invoiced for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Indirect Sales
The total value of indirect (linked, but not direct shipped) sales for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
G/M Indirect Sales
The total gross margin earned on indirect (linked, but not direct shipped) sales invoiced for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Total Mdse Sales
The total value of merchandise invoiced for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date. This amount consists of the totals from the Invoice Register, Credit Memo Register, Cash Sales Register and the Consignment Invoice Register.
Cash Sales
The total value of cash sales for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Credit Memos
The total value of credit memos issued for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Gross Margin
The total gross margin value earned for all merchandise invoiced for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Mnfctr Rep Sales
The total value of manufacturer’s rep sales orders invoiced for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
GM%
The total gross margin percent earned on all merchandise invoiced for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Common Carrier
The total value of invoiced sales orders that were shipped by common carrier for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Delivered
The total value of invoiced sales orders that were delivered by your truck for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Will Call
The total value of invoiced sales orders that were picked up in will call for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Total Invoices
The total value of merchandise invoiced for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
Bookings (Upd Dte)
The total accumulated bookings and bookings charges for the orders entered or edited on this day.
Gross Margin
The gross margin earned for the day’s bookings.
GM%
The total gross margin percent earned for the day’s bookings.
Ord Val/Count/Ave
Part 1: The total amount of the open orders (excluding consignment) for the day. This amount comes from the grand total on the Open Sales Order Summary Report.
Part 2: The total count of open orders for the day.
Part 3: The value of the average open order for the day (Ord Val / Count).
Consgn Val/Cnt/Ave
Part 1: The total amount of open consignment orders for the day. This amount comes from the grand total on the Open Consignment Detail Report.
Part 2: The total count of consignment orders for the day.
Part 3: The value of the average open order (Consgn Val / Cnt).
Stock Inventory
The total value of stock products in on-hand inventory.
Consists of the total value from the Current Inventory Value Report - Average (Inventory Module).
Non-Stock/Linked
The total value of non-stock or linked inventory on-hand.
Consigned/On S/O
The total value merchandise designated to consigned inventory.
Consists of the total value from the Current Inventory Value Report - Average (Inventory Module). Stocking Status Code = C.
Warranty Rtn/Scr
The total value of warranty (both return and scrapped) inventory.
Damaged
The total value of damaged inventory.
In-Transit (Shp Whse)
The total value of merchandise in-transit to another warehouse.
 
Total (unlabeled)
The total value of all products in inventory.
 
Inventory Adjmnts
The total value of inventory adjusted for the warehouse for today’s date, month-to-date, and year-to-date.
A/R Aging Amount
Future: The total receivables not yet due.
Current: The total receivables due now.
1-30 Days: The total receivables 1-30 days late.
31-60 Days: The total receivables 31-60 days late.
61 Days & Over: The total receivables over 61 days late.
Total: The total of open receivables.
% To Total
The percentage each aging column represents, compared to the total receivables.
Cash Received
The amount of cash received. This amount should balance to the Daily Cash Sales Register.
A/R Write-Off
The amount of cash written off.
Earned Cash Disc
The total value of cash discounts earned.
Unearned Cash Disc
The total value of cash discounts taken but not earned.
Ave Days to Pay
The average number of days it takes for customers to pay their invoices. Invoice values are weighted so that invoices of higher amounts calculate a higher average days to pay. Disputed invoices are excluded from the calculation. Refer to “Calculating Average Days to Pay” in the Accounts Receivable documentation.
Customer Count
The total number of customers that have this warehouse as their default selling warehouse.
New Customers
The total number new customers that have this warehouse as their default selling warehouse.
A/P Payment Schedule Amount
On-hold: The total payables on hold that are due to vendors.
For 1-14 Days: The total payables due to vendors within 14 days.
For 15-28 Days: The total payables due to vendors within 15-28 days.
For 29-42 Days: The total payables due to vendors within 29-42 days.
Future: The total payables due for any future dates more than 42 days.
Total: The total amount of outstanding payables due to vendors.
% To Total
The percentage each payment column represents compared to the total payments due.
Regular Checks/Count
The total value of regular (type A) checks processed for the day/ the total count of checks processed for the day.
The total value of regular checks processed month to date/ the total count of checks processed month to date.
The total value of regular checks processed year to date/ the total count of checks processed year to date.
Hand Checks/Count
The total value of manual checks (type M) processed for the day/ the total count of manual checks processed for the day.
The total value of manual checks processed month to date/ the total count of manual checks processed month to date.
The total value of manual checks processed year to date/ the total count of manual checks processed year to date.
Totals
The total number of checks processed for the day.
Vendor Count
The number of vendor records.
P/O Val/Count/Ave
Part 1: The total amount of the open purchase orders for the day. This amount comes from the grand total on the Open Sales Order Summary Report.
Part 2: The total count of purchase orders for the day.
Part 3: The value of the average purchase order for the day.
Cash Onhand In Bank
The value of cash in the bank that is available to pay vendors.
