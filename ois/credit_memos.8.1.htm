Credit Memos & RGAs
You can use a credit memo to give credit to customers for returned items or pricing errors. If an RGA (return goods authorization) was issued to the customer to authorize return for a product, you can enter the RGA number for the credit memo and the product information will already be filled in. If no RGA was previously issued, you can still enter a credit memo to credit the customer.
