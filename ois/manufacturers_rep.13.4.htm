Updating a Manufacturer Rep Order
After you enter the shipped quantities on a manufacturer’s rep order and record the check information for commission paid by the vendor, you must update the order. This process moves the order to history and updates sales analysis. Inventory and accounts receivable accounts are not affected.
To update a manufacturer’s rep order:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing to open the M/R Process Routine screen.
2
For Process Date, enter the date that you want to record the commission. This date determines which fiscal period is updated for sales analysis statistics.
3
Enter the Warehouse Number to update with the commission information or leave blank to update all warehouses for which Manufacturer’s rep orders were entered.
4
For M/R Order Number, enter the manufacturer’s rep order number to update or leave blank to update all orders for the selected warehouses.
The order is moved to history and sales analysis statistics are updated.
