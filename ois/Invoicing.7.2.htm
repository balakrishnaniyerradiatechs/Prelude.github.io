Setting Maintenance Options for Invoices
You can print, email, or fax invoices to customers depending on how flags are set for each warehouse and customer.
Warehouse Maintenance
To determine when invoices print, email, or fax:
1
From Warehouse Maintenance, select F6-Printing to open the Continuous Printing Setup screen.
2
For Invoice, determine if cash, COD, and will call invoices print, fax, or email continuously or by batch.
•
Y - Print, fax, or email the invoices to the customer upon completion of the sales order.
•
N - Do not print, fax, or email the invoice upon completion of the sales order. Printed by batch (refer to Printing Invoices).
•
D - Prompt for the printer queue from the Sales Order Entry screen.
3
For Prt, enter the port number (created in Continuous Printer Port Maintenance) to control continuous printing.
4
For Form Queue, enter the printer number where the invoices print.
5
Press F2-Save twice to save the printer information.
Order Processing Parameters
To determine if an order is invoiced after the first shipment if multiple releases exist for an order:
1
From Order Processing Parameters, select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Partial Shpmnt Invoicing, choose from the following:
•
Y - An invoice prints after the first release of a sales order.
•
N - All releases must be confirmed before an invoice prints.
3
Press F2-Save twice to save the invoicing information.
Customer Maintenance
To set up a customer’s invoicing options:
1
From the Customer Maintenance Screen, select F6-A/R to open the A/R Information Screen.
2
For Master Account Flag, determine if this customer is a master account:
•
Y - This account is a master account and one or more sub-accounts can be linked to this account. It cannot be changed to N unless the sub-accounts are unlinked.
•
N - Not a master account.
3
Enter the Master Account number, if this account is a sub-account of a master account.
4
If this customer is a sub-account and a Master Account is assigned, Master Account Invoice determines which account is updated with accounts receivable information and which address prints on invoices:
•
N - The sub-account is updated with A/R information and receives the invoices.
•
Y - The master account is updated with A/R information and receives the invoices.
•
A - The master account is updated with A/R information, but the invoices are mailed to the sub-account.
5
From the Customer Maintenance Screen, select F7-Order to open the Order Information Screen.
6
For # Invoice Sets, enter the number of copies of each invoice this customer should receive. If this field is set to zero, the customer will not receive any invoices.
7
Select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Order Information Screen.
8
For Invoice Print Cycle, determine how often invoices generate for this customer:
•
D - Daily.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
•
W - Weekly.
9
For Inv Document Option, determine how invoices transmit to the customer. Choose from the following:
•
B - Both fax and print.
•
E - Email only.
•
M - Email and print.
•
N - Print only.
•
Y - Fax only.
10
For Invoice To Ship-To, indicate if Invoice Printing uses the Contact, E-mail Address, or Fax information from the customer’s ship-to information. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Send the invoice to the address, email, or fax in Customer Ship-To Maintenance for the customer, if it is used on an order.
•
N - Send the invoice to the address, email, or fax set up in Customer Maintenance.
11
Press F2-Save 3 time to save the customer’s invoicing information.
