Tax Exempt Expiration Report - Customer
The Tax Exempt Expiration Report-Customer displays upcoming expiration dates for customer sales tax exemption certificates.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Tax Exempt Expiration Report - Customer
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Expiration Date. The date by which the tax exempt certificates will expire.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/ Description
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Tx Jurisdiction
The tax jurisdiction code. These codes are set up in the Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance Screen and then assigned to customers in the Customer Sales Tax Entry Screen and the Customer Job Sales Tax Maintenance Screen. You can override a tax jurisdiction in the Sales Order Header Tax Jurisdiction Information Screen.
Exempt Number
The sales tax exempt certificate number.
Exp Date
The date the tax exempt certificate expires.
Purchasing Contact/Phone/Fax
The purchasing contact name, phone, and fax number from the Customer Contacts Screen (Customer Maintenance > F8-Contact).
