Printing Backorder Notices
If a customer account should receive notices for products on backorder, use Backorder Notice Printing to generate the notices.
If you want backorders for a customer to be included in the backorder notice printing, you must set Print B/O Notice in Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > F5-Add Info to Y for the customer.
You must contact Activant to have backorder notice printing activated.
To print backorder notices:
1
Select Order Processing > Batch Ticket Printing > Backorder Notice Printing to open the Print B/O Notice screen.
2
For B/O Notice Eff Date, enter the date up to which you want to include products still on backorder. The system subtracts 30 from the day you enter. For example, if you enter todayâ€™s date, all backordered line items that have a pick date earlier than 30 days ago, are included.
3
For Printer Form Queue, enter the printer where you want the backorder notices to print.
4
Press F2-Save to generate notices for backordered items for all customers that have Print B/O Notice set to Y in the Customer Maintenance Additional Order Information Screen.
