Customer Level Administrative Invoice Hold Options
You can set up restrictions for a customer to determine when their orders should go on Administrative Invoice Hold.
To set Administrative Invoice Hold options for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number.
3
Select F6-A/R to open the A/R Information Screen.
4
For Admin Hold Override, choose from the following:
•
B - Always place sales orders for this customer on both Administrative Invoice and Administrative Order Hold.
•
I - Always place sales orders for this customer on Administrative Invoice Hold.
•
N - Never place an order for this customer on hold. You can override this option using Manual Administrative Invoice Hold while in Sales Order Entry. Refer to Manually Placing Orders on Administrative Invoice Hold.
•
O - Always place sales orders and quotes for this customer on Administrative Order Hold.
•
Q - Always place quotes for this customer on Administrative Quote hold.
5
For Admin Invoice Mgr I.D., enter a manager ID if you want to override the manager ID set up at the warehouse level. All invoices for this customer will go to this manager’s work queue.
6
Press F2-Save to save any changes and to return to the Customer Maintenance screen.
7
Select F7-Order to open the Order Information screen.
8
For Require P/O Number, choose from the following:
•
Y - The customer requires a purchase order. Opens the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen. You can enter blanket purchase order information for this customer. You can also establish purchase order patterns that must match the pattern on sales orders for the customer.
•
N - Purchase orders are not required for this customer.
•
O - Places an order with no purchase order on Administrative Order Hold.
•
I - Opens the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen. You can enter an Admin Invoice Amt so that sales orders are placed on Administrative Invoice Hold if the order is below this minimum and more than the P/O Required Amt.
9
Press F2-Save to save any changes and to return to the Customer Maintenance screen.
10
Press F2-Save again to save all the information.
