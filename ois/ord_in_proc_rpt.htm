Orders in Process (Picking) Report
The Orders in Process (Picking) report lists the status of open sales orders for which pick tickets printed, but are not yet invoiced. This information is similar to the Open Order Release Inquiry.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Orders in Process (Picking)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Warehouse. The shipping warehouse where the pick ticket printed.
•
Customer. The customer numbers to include.
•
Salesrep #1. The Salesrep #1 assigned to the sales order.
•
Ship Via Class. The ship via classes to include.
•
Print Date Range. The beginning and ending pick ticket print dates to include.
•
Processed Flag. Indicates if you want to include releases processed in Release Confirmation Entry that are not yet invoiced or on hold:
-
Y - Include confirmed releases.
-
N - Do not include confirmed releases.
-
H - Include only orders on hold.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Release#
The sales order release number.
I
Indicates the order’s invoice status:
•
Y - The order was confirmed in Release Confirmation Entry and is flagged for invoicing.
•
H - The order is on hold and cannot be invoiced until released. Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
•
Blank - The order is not yet flagged for invoicing.
Ord-Req
The date the order is required by the customer.
Cust#/Name/PO#
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Line 3: The customer purchase order number, if applicable.
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The product description.
C
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
•
Blank - The item is not part of a kit.
S
Line 1: The line item’s supply code (if one appears on the sales order):
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Line 2: The linked document number, if the product is linked to a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
Dt/Tm
Line 1: The date the order was entered.
Line 2: The time the order was entered.
Qty-Pck
The quantity available to pick.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Price/Ext.
Line 1: The price of the product.
Line 2: The extended price of the total quantity of the picked product (Qty-Pck x Price).
