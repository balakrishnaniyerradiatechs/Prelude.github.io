Additional Keywords Screen
You can use the Additional Keywords screen to create the following additional methods to look up a customer in customer name searches:
•
If you want to look up a customer based on their Contact name, you can enter C for Keyword
•
If the customer changed their name, you may want your sales people to still be able to find the customer under the old name.
•
If acronyms or symbols exist in a customer’s name, you may need several ways to lookup the customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F5-Add Info > F5-Keywords
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Keywords
Additional means to search for a customer:
•
Enter any additional names or keywords to make it easier to search for this customer.
•
Enter C to be able to search for customers based on the Contact name from Customer Maintenance.
F2-Save
Saves the customer keyword information.
