Setting up Facility Information
If more than one physician works at a hospital or doctor’s office, you can set up a facility number to group the physicians. When you create a physician number in RX Physician Maintenance (refer to Setting up Physician Information) you can enter the facility number to automatically fill in the physician’s address and phone number information.
To set up a facility number:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Physician/RX Maintenance > Facility Maintenance to open the Facility Maintenance screen.
2
For Facility, choose from the following:
•
Enter up to 8 alphanumeric characters to identify the facility.
•
Enter NEW to create a system generated number (refer to Setting Prescription and Facility Counter Numbers)
•
Press F3 to view existing facilities.
3
Enter the Facility Name.
4
Enter the address, phone, fax, and email information for the facility.
5
Enter the Manager for the facility, if applicable.
6
Press F2-Save to save the facility information.
