Monthly Credit Reason Report
The Monthly Credit Reason report lists the total credit memos issued for each product line for a month. The report is sorted by credit reason code (refer to Creating Credit Reason Codes).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Monthly Credit Reasons
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Month Number. Choose from the following:
-
The month for which you want to view credit memos.
-
C - Use the current system date. The date that prints at the top of the report is the month for generated information.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Pline
The product line assigned to the product.
Description
The product line description.
Credit $
The total credit given for the month for each product line for the reason code.
Sales
The total sales for the product line for the month.
Cr %
The credit percent for Credit $ vs. Sales.
YTD Cred $
The total amount of credit issued for the product line so far this year.
YTD Sales $
The total sales dollars generated for the product line so far this year.
Cr %
The credit percent for YTD Cred $ vs. YTD Sales $.
