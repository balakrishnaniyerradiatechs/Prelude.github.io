Monthly Bookings Report
The Monthly Bookings Report lists every sales order line item entered, changed, or deleted for a fiscal period. You can choose to sort the report by the Salesrep One, the Salesrep Two or the Written By assigned in the Sales Order Header screen.
File Update Parameters Maintenance.
Set Bookings to Y to record bookings information for new orders as well as any deleted lines or price/cost changes. Bookings are the amount for which you obtained orders, but are not necessarily invoiced. This information is helpful for companies with long lead times to help view business trends. Enter Y or N for each of the following to indicate if you want to keep bookings information for:
•
Manufacture reps.
•
Quotations.
•
MITS (third party software).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Monthly Bookings Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Period Range. The fiscal periods to include.
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Sort By Rep1/Rep2/ or W/B?. Determines the sort sequence for the report:
-
1 - Sort by the Salesrep One assigned to the sales order.
-
2 - Sort by the Salesrep Two assigned to the sales order.
-
W - Sort by the sales order’s Written By user ID.
•
Salesrep. The sales representative numbers to include in the report if you chose 1 or 2 for the sort option.
•
Written By. The user ID of the person that entered the order if you chose the W sort option.
•
Customer. The customers to include in the report.
•
Document Type. The document types to include:
-
O - Orders.
-
M - Manufacturer’s representative orders.
-
Q - Quotes.
•
Summary Report Only?. Indicates if you want to include detail bookings or summaries only:
-
Y - Summaries only (extended values only).
-
N - Include detail information (e.g., order numbers, products, prices).
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/
ShipTo
The ship-to customer number.
Description
The customer description.
Product Number/ Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Order Num
Status
Line 1: The sales order number.
Line 2: The status of the line item:
•
NEW - The line item was entered on the sales order.
•
CHG - The line item was changed.
•
DEL - The line item was deleted.
Ord Date
Update
Line 1: The date the order was entered.
Line 2: The date the order was changed or deleted.
Quantity
The quantity ordered/changed/deleted.
Price/UOM
The product’s sales price and unit of measure.
Extended Price
The extended price for the total quantity of the item ordered, changed, or deleted.
Extended Cost
The extended cost of the total quantity of the item ordered, changed, or deleted.
Gross Profit$
The total gross profit dollars earned for the line item.
Gross Profit%
The total gross profit percentage earned for the line item.
