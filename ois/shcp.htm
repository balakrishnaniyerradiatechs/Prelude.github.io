Transmitting Shipping Confirmations by Batch
If your warehouse does not continuously generate shipping confirmations (refer to Setting Shipping Confirmation Maintenance Options), the confirmations are held in a queue until you print, fax, or email them by batch in Shipping Confirmation Printing.
To batch generate shipping confirmations:
1
Select Order Processing > Batch Ticket Printing > Shipping Confirmation Printing to open the Shipping Confirmation Printing screen.
2
Enter the pick ticket Release Number that shipped to the customer.
3
If the confirmations are being printed, enter the Printer Form Queue where you want the tickets to print.
4
Choose Execute to print, email, or fax the shipping confirmations to the designated printer, email address, or fax number.
