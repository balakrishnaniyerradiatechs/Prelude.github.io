Open Order Buyout Not Linked Report
The Open Order Buyout Not Linked report includes open sales orders with supply codes (except D) that are not linked to a purchase order, work order, or transfer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Open Order Buyout Not Linked Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse. The selling warehouse is the warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse ships the merchandise.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number / Description
Line 1: The un-linked product number.
Line 2: The description of the product.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Order #
The sales order number.
Ln#
The line number on the sales order.
S
Line 1: The line item’s supply code if the product is on backorder and varies depending on the product’s replenishment path and product settings. Commonly used options are
•
:B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Line 2: The linked document number, if the product is linked to a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
C
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
•
Blank - The item is not part of a kit.
Customer / Description
The customer number and description.
Qty-Opn
The quantity of the un-linked item on the sales order.
Cost
The unit cost of the un-linked item.
Ext-Cost
The extended cost of the total quantity of the product (Qty-Opn x Cost).
H
Indicates if the order is on hold:
•
CR - Credit hold.
•
CK - Check received with no authorization.
•
CC - Credit card number received with no authorization.
•
PIA - Wire transfer with no transfer number or a personal check.
•
Blank - Not on hold.
