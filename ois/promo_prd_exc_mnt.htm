Promotion Product Exception Maintenance screen
The Promotion Product Exception Maintenance screen allow you to include or exclude product lines from a promotion matrix.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance > F8-Prd Exc
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product Exception
L for Product Line is the only exception method available at this time.
Exception Method
Indicates whether you will include or exclude product lines from a promotion:
•
I - Include the product lines listed under the Exception List in this promotion. All other product lines will not receive the promotional pricing.
•
E - Exclude the product lines listed under the Exception List in this promotion. All other product lines will receive the promotional pricing.
Exception List
The product line numbers to include or exclude from the promotion depending on the Exception Method setting.
F2-Save
Saves the exception information and returns to the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen.
