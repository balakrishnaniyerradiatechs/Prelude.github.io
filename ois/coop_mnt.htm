Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen
If you offer rebates to customers for products purchased, you can define rebate percents and eligibility requirements in Customer Rebate Maintenance. You can also exclude products by part number, product price group, product line, or vendor. The rebates are calculated in Cash Receipts Entry when the customer pays their invoice (refer to “Generating Customer Invoice Rebates” in the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information on Customer rebates).
Once you set up a rebate, you can assign each rebate number and start date to one or more customers in the Customer Maintenance Order Information Screen.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Control Files > Customer Rebates
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer Rebate
The rebate number that identifies this rebate program. You can assign this number to a customer in the Customer Maintenance Order Information Screen.
Rebate Description
A description of the rebate program.
Rebate Credit Percent
The percent that determines the rebate amount due to the customer for each invoice. Rebate Amount = (amount invoiced during the eligibility period) x (Rebate Credit Percent).
Credit Report Freq
Determines how often customer rebates are calculated:
•
A - Annually.
•
Q - Quarterly.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
Rebate Percent
The percentage used to calculate customer rebates. This field is for information only and may be used for custom reports.
Rebate Check Freq
Determines how often customer rebates are paid:
•
A - Annually.
•
Q - Quarterly.
•
M - Monthly.
•
S - Semi-monthly.
Eligibility Type
Determines how the Eligible Days to Pay is calculated:
•
I - Based on the invoice date.
•
D - Based on the due date.
Eligible Days to Pay
The number of days after the invoice date by which the invoice must be paid to be eligible for this rebate. The rebate is calculated when the invoice is paid.
Exclude/Include Method
Determines whether you will include or exclude types (in Exclude/Include Type) or lists (Exclude/Include List):
•
E - Exclude types/lists for the customer rebate.
•
I - Include types/lists for the customer rebate.
Exclude/Include Type
The code that defines the product group to exclude/include from the rebate program:
•
G - Product price group.
•
L - Product line.
•
P - Product.
•
V - Vendor.
This code is used with the Exclude/Include List to determine which products are not part of rebate calculations.
Exclude/Include List
The products, product lines, product price groups, or vendors to exclude or include from rebate calculations, depending on the Exclude/Include Type.
F2-Save
Saves the rebate information.
F4-Del
Deletes the rebate program.
F5-Rebate Table
Opens the Rebate Table Maintenance Screen. You can set up different rebate percentages to apply based on the purchase amount.
