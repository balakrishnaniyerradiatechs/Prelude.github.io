Manual Credit Hold Status Procedure screen
If you want to put a sales order on credit hold that did not automatically go on credit hold, you can use the Manual Credit Hold Status Procedure screen. Refer to the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information on Credit Holds.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Manual Credit Hold Procedure.
