Product Pricing
If the pricing that you give to your customers varies by customer, product, date range, contract, blanket purchase order, product promotions, or any other criteria, Prelude’s pricing methods allow you to develop a unique pricing structure for each situation. When you enter a sales order (including quotes, work orders, rental orders, or shop repair orders), the customer’s prices display for each product. The source code indicates where the price comes from (e.g., price matrices, contracts, column pricing, quantity breaks, sum and reprice discounts, or lot pricing). At the basic level, you can maintain prices by warehouse or by company, depending on how you sell your products.
