Point of Sale
The Point of Sale Subsystem is used for cash sale orders on point-of-sale equipment such as cash drawers, pole displays, and receipt printers. To view point of sale information for a fiscal period, you must re-build point-of-sale information before you can run the Point of Sale Report. Refer to the following:
•
Point of Sale Report Build.
•
Point of Sale Report.
