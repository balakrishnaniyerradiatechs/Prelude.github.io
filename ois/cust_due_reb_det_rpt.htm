Customer Rebate Detail Report (Table)
If you defined your customer rebate program using the Rebate Table Maintenance Screen, you can use the The Customer Rebate Detail report for tables to view rebate percentages based on the total amount of products purchased. The rebate programs are assigned to each customer in the Customer Maintenance Order Information Screen. The rebate amounts are calculated in Cash Receipts entry when the customer pays their invoice (refer to Customer Invoice Rebate Maintenance in the Accounts Receivable documentation).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Customer/Ship-To Reports > Customer Rebate Reports > Customer Rebate Detail Report (Table)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customers eligible for rebates.
•
Customer Rebate. The rebate program number set up in Rebate Maintenance if you want to limit the report to just one program.
•
Rebate Period. The rebate period for this report. The report will only print for rebate programs with this Credit Report Freq:
-
A - Annually.
-
Q - Quarterly.
-
M - Monthly.
-
S - Semi monthly.
•
Invoice Date Range: The beginning and ending invoice dates to include in the report.
•
Report Description. Defines what the report is used for. Prints on the 4th line of the header.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice #
The sales invoice number.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Inv Amt
The total amount charged to the customer.
Tot Purch
The total value of merchandise purchased by the customer.
Oth Chgs
Other charges included in the invoice (e.g., freight, handling, tax).
Elg Amount
The amount of the invoice that is eligible to include in the rebate calculation.
Date Pd
The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive the rebate.
Pd Within Elg Days
The amount of rebate that will be paid to the customer if they pay their invoice by the number of days set in Eligible Days to Pay in the Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen.
Paid After Elg Days
The amount of rebate that will be paid to the customer if they pay their invoice after the number of days set in Eligible Days to Pay in the Customer Rebate Maintenance Screen.
Unpaid Balance
The amount of the invoice not yet paid by the customer.
Rebate Amt
The calculated rebate amount due the customer if they pay their invoice on time.
