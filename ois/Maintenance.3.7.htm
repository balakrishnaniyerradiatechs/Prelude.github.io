Using Floor Plan Financing
Floor plan financing is used when a customer is financially responsible for orders for another customer. The floor plan customer is the customer who is responsible for payment, whose credit limit is checked, and whose A/R balance is updated when an order is entered for another customer. Once a customer is set up to use floor plan financing, each time you enter an order for the customer, a message appears to ask if this is a floor plan order.
