Salesman A/R Commission Report
The Salesman A/R Commission report list salesrep commission earnings for invoices updated during Cash Receipts Entry.
A/R Commissions in File Update Parameters must be set to Y to use this report.
To run the Salesman A/R Commission report:
1
Select Order Processing Additional Subsystems Commission Subsystem > Salesman A/R Commission Report.
2
Enter the beginning and ending invoice dates for which you want to include salesrep commission.
3
After you choose to Execute the report, the following message appears:
Ok to purge Commissions records to History File for report just printed?
4
Choose from the following:
•
No - Keeps the commission records open. Be careful not to re-pay the salesreps the next time the report prints.
•
Yes - Moves the commission records to Commission History.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
The customer number.
Customer Name
The customer description.
Inv#
The invoice number updated.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Inv Amt
The total amount invoiced.
Inv Margin
The amount of profit generated from the sale.
Amt Received
The amount paid by the customer.
Disc Amt
The customer’s discount for the order. Applicable if the customer receives a discount if the invoice is paid early.
Comm Amt
The amount of commission earned by the salesrep for this order.
Comm Pay
The amount of commission paid to the salesrep for this order.
