Daily Business Statistics
Daily business statistics include sales, invoiced amounts, inventory value, accounts receivable, and accounts payable information by warehouse. System options determine what is included and how to update business statistics. The Daily Business Statistics report displays the results. Refer to the following for information on daily business statistics:
•
Daily Business Update.
•
Daily Business Statistics Report.
File Update Parameters
The following flags in File Update Parameters Maintenance determine how the system updates the daily business statistics.
Bookings. Set to Y to record bookings information for new orders as well as any deleted lines or price/cost changes. Bookings are the amount for which you obtained orders, but are not necessarily invoiced. This information is helpful for companies with long lead times to help view business trends. Enter Y or N for each of the following to indicate if you want to keep bookings information for:
•
Manufacture reps.
•
Quotations.
•
MITS (third party software).
DBS Statistics. Set to Y to update daily business statistics for the report. Enter the following information:
•
A/P Aging Days Interval: The number of days for aging accounts payable (the length of the A/P period).
•
A/R Aging Days Interval: The number of days for aging accounts receivable (the length of the A/R period).
•
A/R Write-Off G/L Acct: The general ledger account to record miscellaneous adjustments in Cash Receipts Entry for bad debt write-offs. The account description displays for reference.
•
Days Back for History: The number of previous days to select for order history to update merchandise and gross profit amounts on the Daily Business Statistics Report.
•
Bank Account G/L Acct: The main bank general ledger account number. The balance of the account prints on the Daily Business Statistics report below the total amount of Accounts Payable so you can compare liabilities to cash on-hand.
