Authorized List of SalesRep #2 Screen
The Authorized List of SalesRep #2 screen allows you to assign sales representatives to a customer. Once you assign salesrep numbers in this screen, only these salesreps are accepted when you enter an order or a quote for this customer.
Utilize SalesRep#2 in Company Maintenance > F5-Add Info determines if Salesrep Two is accessible in Customer Maintenance:
•
Y - You can enter a Salesrep Two in Customer Maintenance.
•
N - You cannot enter a Salesrep Two in Customer Maintenance.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > Action Bar > Add’l Opts > Valid Salesrep 2
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Rep
The sales representative numbers to assign to Salesrep Two (Sales Order Header screen > F5-Add Info) for this customer. Any number entered here is the only representative that can be used in Salesrep Two on sales orders for this customer. If left blank, any sales representative can be assigned to sales orders for this customer.
Description
The sales representative’s name.
F2-Save
Saves the sales representative information.
