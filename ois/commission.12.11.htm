Assigning Salesreps to a Customer
Once you have salesreps set up, you can assign a primary and secondary salesrep to a customer in Customer Maintenance. These salesreps default for all orders for the customer and any customer ship-to addresses, but can be changed in order entry.
To assign salesreps to a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance Screen.
2
For Salesrep One, enter the number for the primary salesrep for this customer.
3
For Salesrep Two, enter a secondary salesrep for the customer (e.g., inside salesrep).
4
Press F2-Save to save the salesrep information.
Utilize Salesrep#2 in Company Maintenance > F5-Add Info determines if a secondary salesrep can be entered in Customer Maintenance.
