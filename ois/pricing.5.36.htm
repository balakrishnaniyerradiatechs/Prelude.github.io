Tracking Blanket P/O Information for a Customer
If a blanket purchase order is set up for a customer in Blanket Contract Maintenance (refer to Creating a Blanket Contract), the blanket purchase order balance is automatically updated for the customer in Customer Maintenance. As sales orders are entered for the customer using the blanket purchase order number, the P/O Balance in the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen is updated to reflect the balance remaining on the purchase order.
Administrative Order Hold
If you want to put a sales order on Admin Order Hold if the customer exceeds their blanket P/O amount, enter an AOH Type of B (Blanket P/O Amount Exceeded) in the Administrative Order Parameters screen (Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add Info > Administrative Order Hold = Y). Refer to System Level Administrative Order Hold Options.
To view or edit blanket purchase order information:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance screen.
2

3
For Require P/O Number, enter Y to open the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen.
4

For Customer P/O#, if a blanket purchase order contract was set up for the customer (refer to Creating a Blanket Contract) the blanket purchase order appears here. As sales orders are entered for the customer using the blanket purchase order number, the P/O Balance is updated to reflect the amount remaining on the blanket purchase order.
6
Press F2-Save 3 times to save any changes and to close the screen.
