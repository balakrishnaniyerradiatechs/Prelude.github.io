Adjusting Commission for a Line Item
For each line item on an invoiced order, you can adjust the unit price, unit cost, the salesrep, and/or the commission calculation.
To adjust the commission for a line item:
1
From the A/R Commission Maintenance screen (refer to Adjusting Commission After Invoicing), select F5-Line Item Adj to open the A/R Commission Line Adjustment screen.
2
Depending on what you want to change, choose from the following:
•
To change the selling price of the product, enter the price for the extended quantity in Price/Ext. This value is divided by the Quantity to calculate the unit price.
•
To change the cost of the product, enter the cost for the extended quantity in Cost/Ext. This value is divided by the Quantity to calculate the unit cost.
•
To change the salesrep who will receive commission, either override the salesrep number in Rep or add an additional salesrep as another line under Rep.
•
To change the commission amount to pay the salesrep for the product, choose one of the following methods:
-
To calculate commission as a percent of the selling price of the line item, enter * for Type and the percent of the sales price under Rate.
-
To calculate commission as a percent of the gross profit of the line item, enter G for Type and the percent of the gross profit for Rate.
-
To enter the exact commission amount that you want to pay the salesrep for the line item, enter $ for Type and the amount to pay under Comm$.
Comm Amt displays the new commission calculation for the line item.
3
Press F2-Save to save the commission information and to return to the A/R Commission Line Adjustment screen.
