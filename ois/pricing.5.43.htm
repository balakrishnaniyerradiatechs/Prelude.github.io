Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry
The Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry displays all available promotional type matrices for a customer/product.
Viewing Customer Promotion Matrices
To list promotion matrices set up for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry to open the Customer Promotion Matrix Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer whose promotion matrices you want to view.
3
For Product, enter the product for which you want to view promotions for this customer, or leave blank to view all product promotions that exist for this customer.
Product promotions for the customer appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer number and description.
Product
The product for which you want to view promotions.
Promo#
The promotion matrix number (the Plan # from the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen).
M
The Matrix Type from the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen:
•
1 - Product.
•
2 - Order.
Promotion Type
The Promotion Type from the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen:
•
1 - Same as. Buy one product get one free or at a discount.
•
2 - Mix and match. Buy a product or combination of products to get a promotional product for free or at a discount.
•
3 - Specific buy, specific get. Buy one or more of a product to get another promotional product or group of promotional products for free or at a discount.
Beg Date
The date the promotion begins.
Exp Date
The date the promotion expires.
Buy Qty
The quantity the customer must buy to get the free or discounted product, if applicable for this type of promotion.
Get Qty
The quantity the customer gets if they purchase the Buy Qty.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Promotion Type Matrix Inquiry screen to allow you to view detail information on the promotion matrix.
