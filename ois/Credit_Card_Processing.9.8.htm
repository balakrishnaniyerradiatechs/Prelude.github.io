Using Administrative Invoice Hold for Credit Cards
Credit card sales orders should not be invoiced without an authorization code. If an order is invoiced without the authorization code, you cannot balance your credit card batch at the end of the day and you might not ever get paid.
To activate Administrative Invoice Hold for credit card sales orders:
1
In Order Processing Parameters, select F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Administrative Order Hold, enter Y to open the Administrative Invoice Hold Parameters screen.
3
For AIH Types, enter A (Credit Card Order With No Authorization Code) so that credit card sales orders cannot be released for invoicing if they do not have an authorization code. The order must be released from hold before the invoice generates.
Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry for an explanation of all the invoice hold options and releasing invoices from hold.
4
Press F2-Save twice to save Administrative Invoice Hold Parameters.
