File Update Parameters
To determine when and how a salesperson is paid commission:
1
In Options Parameters, select File Update Parameters to open the File Update Parameters Maintenance screen.
2
For Commission, determine if commission information is captured when a sales order goes through the B Update process:
•
Y - Update commission information when the sales order is updated in the B Update (refer to Updating Sales Statistics (‘B’ Update)).
•
N - Do not update commission information during the B Update.
3
For A/R Commissions, indicate if commission information is updated when the customer pays their invoice:
•
Y - Update commission information when the customer pays their invoice.
•
N - Do not update commission information when the customer pays their invoice.
Refer to Updating and Paying Commission.
4
To determine if sales analysis is maintained for salesreps and commissions, press F5-Sales Analysis to open the Sales Analysis Maintenance screen.
5
For Sales Rep1, indicate if sales analysis is kept for the Salesrep One assigned to the sales order:
•
Y - Maintain sales analysis for the Salesrep One.
•
N - Do not maintain sales analysis for Salesrep One.
6
For Sales Rep2, indicate if sales analysis is kept for the Salesrep Two assigned to the sales order:
•
Y - Maintain sales analysis for the Salesrep Two.
•
N - Do not maintain sales analysis for Salesrep Two.
7
For Commissions, indicate if commission information is kept in the sales analysis file:
•
Y - Capture commission information by salesrep in the sales analysis file.
•
N - Do not capture commission information by salesrep in the sales analysis file.
8
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the commission information.
