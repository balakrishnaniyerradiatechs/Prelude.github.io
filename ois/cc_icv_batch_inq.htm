IC Verify Batch Inquiry screen
The IC Verify Batch Inquiry allows you to connect to your service provider to display or print old batch information, including credit card authorization data.The batch balance that displays is what the bank thinks is approved. This number should match the balance on the Credit Card Post Authorization Rejection Report. If not, then the approval/rejection reports should be run again to locate the discrepancy. The purge clears out the information but IC Verify or Protobase keeps a copy at the UNIX level for 9 years.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > IC Verify Batch Inquiry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Start Date
The date of batch that you want to verify.
End Date
The end of the date range for the batch that you want to verify.
Credit Card Number
The credit card number to verify. Leave blank to verity all credit card numbers.
Amount Settled
The amount approved.
Customer Name
The name of the customer to verify.
Selling Warehouse
The warehouse that sold the material.
Credit Card Type
The credit card company if multiple merchants are associated with a card type.
A/R Settlement?
Determines if A/R balances are applied to a credit card:
•
Y - Apply A/R balances to a credit card.
•
N - Do not apply A/R balances to a credit card.
F2-Inquire
Dials up the service provider to verify the information.
