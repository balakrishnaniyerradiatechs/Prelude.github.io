Sum & Reprice and Quantity Break Discounts
Sum & Reprice allows you to combine products in a defined group and give a discount if the total number of items purchased in the group meets a quantity break level. Separately these products may not meet the quantity break price, but combined, they meet a discount break level. For example, if a customer buys 6 cans of paint, they get a discount whether they buy 6 of the same color or 6 different colors.
The following flags work independently of each other and determine how quantity break discounts apply to your customers and products.
Customer Maintenance
To set pricing flags for a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance > F7-Order > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Order Information Screen.
2
For Sum & Reprice Flag, indicate if this customer is eligible for Sum & Reprice quantity break discounts. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Sum & Reprice applies to this customer when enough products within the same product group are purchased in the amount that meets a quantity break discount.
•
N - Sum & Reprice discounts do not apply to this customer.
3
For Allow Qty Disc, determine if quantity discounts are allowed for this customer:
•
Y - The customer receives quantity discounts if enough of a single item is purchase to get the quantity discount.
•
N - The customer does not receive quantity discounts.
•
H - The customer receives the highest quantity discount regardless of the quantity purchased on each sales order.
4
Press F2-Save time to save the sum & reprice information.
Product Maintenance
To set pricing flags for a product:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance Product Maintenance > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Sum & Reprice Flag, determine if quantity break levels apply to a group of items:
•
Y - Sum & reprice groups apply for this item. When the product is sold, the system totals the quantity sold of all items in the same Sum & Reprice group. The price quantity break that applies to the total quantity of the group is used for each item on the sales order.
•
N - Sum & reprice groups do not apply to this item.
3
For Grp, if the Sum & Reprice Flag is set to Y, enter the Sum & Reprice group to which you want to assign the product.
4
For Tbl, enter the quantity discount table code to use when products are grouped (refer to Discount Break Tables). If this field is blank, the product’s price break table is used.
5
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the sum & reprice information.
Price Matrix Maintenance
To set a price matrix to use sum and reprice:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Price Matrix Maintenance > Price Matrix Maintenance > enter the header information to open the Price Matrix Input screen.
2
For SR, indicate if Sum & Reprice applies for products affected by this price matrix:
•
Y - If multiple products on a sales order have the same matrix type, they are combined for a better quantity break discount. If the customer has Best Price turned off, then the system uses the first matrix it finds to calculate the customer's price. If the customer has Best Price turned on, the system reviews all matrices to determine the lowest price.
•
N - Sum & Reprice does not apply for this price matrix.
