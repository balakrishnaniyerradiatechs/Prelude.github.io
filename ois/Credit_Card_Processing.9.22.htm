Deleting Rejected Transactions
If you have items that appear on the Credit Card Post Authorization Rejection Report when you post-authorize a batch, you can use Credit Card Rejection Maintenance to manually remove rejected items and post them to history. You would only use this method if you know the reason for rejection (e.g., the customer paid by other means) and it cannot be handled by other methods (refer to Removing Rejected Credit Card Transactions).
To edit rejected credit card transactions:
1
Select Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Credit Card Rejection Maintenance to open the Credit Card Authorization Rejection Maintenance screen.
2
For Reference Key, enter the sales order number to edit. The Customer, Order and credit card information appear.
3
For Move To Hist?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Moves the transaction to history and updates the status as deleted.
•
N - Leave the transaction as is.
4
Press F2-Save to update the information. If you chose Y for Move To Hist?, a message appears to remind you to use Cash Receipts Entry to re-open and update the A/R invoice.
