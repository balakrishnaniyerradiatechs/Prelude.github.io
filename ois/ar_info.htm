A/R Information Screen
The A/R Information screen displays credit limit, credit balance, and invoice information for a customer job.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers > F7-A/R Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Credit Limit
The customer’s credit limit for this job.
Order Balance
The value of open orders for this job.
Total Invoiced
The total of orders invoiced for this job.
Cash Received
The amount paid by the customer so far on this job.
Invoice Balance
The unpaid invoices for the job.
Credit Left
The amount of credit left for this job.
Merchandise
The total amount of merchandise sold on this job.
Cost
The total cost of this job.
Freight Cost
The total freight cost associated with this job.
Gross Profit
The total gross profit amount earned on this job.
G/P Percent
The total gross profit percent earned on this job.
Mfg Rep
The amount paid to the manufacturer’s rep for this job.
Total Profit
The amount of profit earned on this job.
F5-Open Orders
Displays open sales orders for this job.
F6-History Orders
Displays invoiced sales orders for this job.
F7-Open Quotes
Displays open quotes for this job.
F8- Open Invoices
Opens the Customer Invoice Inquiry to display open invoices for this job.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
Action Bar
CJob Rpt
Opens the Customer Job Analysis Report.
