Customer Job History Inquiry Screen
The Customer Job History Inquiry displays invoices billed to the customer job.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers Action Bar > Job Bill Inq > Job History Inquiry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer
The customer number and description.
Job Number
The number of the job you are viewing.
Invoice#
The invoice number billed.
Inv-Date
The date the invoice was billed.
Inv Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
F2-Finish
Closes the screen.
F5-Review
Displays detail information for products billed for the job.
F8-Log
Opens the Customer Job Log Inquiry to display every transaction that occurred for the job so far. Refer to Order Log Inquiry for a description of each transaction code.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
