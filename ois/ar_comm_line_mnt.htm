Order Processing Field Definitions
A/R Commission Line Adjustment screen
The A/R Commission Line Adjustment screen allows you to adjust the unit price, unit cost, or the commission calculation for a line on an invoiced order. Refer to Adjusting Commission for a Line Item.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Commission Subsystem > Commission Maintenance > F5-Line Item Adj
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
Line 1: The highlighted item from the Commission Maintenance screen for which you want to adjust commission.
Line 2: The product description.
Quantity
The quantity of the product on the order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Price/Ext.
Line 1: The unit price of the product.
Line 2: The extended price of the total quantity of the product on the invoice.
Cost/Est.
Line 1: The unit cost of the product.
Line 2: The extended cost of the total quantity of the product on the sales order.
Rep
The salesrep assigned to the order. You can change the rep or add another one on the next line.
Description
The salesrep description.
Type
The method to calculate commission:
•
* - Calculates commission as a percent of the selling price.Enter the percent under Rate.
•
G - Calculates commission as a percent of the gross profit. Enter the percent under Rate.
•
$ - Allows you to enter the amount you want to pay the salesrep. Enter the amount under Comm$.
Comm$
The amount of commission to pay the salesrep for the line item. Only accessible if you entered $ for Type.
Rate
The percent of the selling price or the gross profit used to calculate commission.
Comm Amt
The amount of commission calculated for the line item and the salesrep.
F2-Save
Saves the commission information.
