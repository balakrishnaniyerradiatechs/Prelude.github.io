Applying a Gift Certificate to a Sales Order
Once a gift certificate is created (refer to Creating Gift Certificates), the customer can use it to pay for merchandise and services.
To use a gift certificate to pay for an order:
1
After you enter all the line items on the sales order (refer to Entering a Sales Order), choose Complete to open the Sales Order Completion screen.
2
Make sure the Terms Code is for cash sales.
3
From the Action Bar, select CC Auth to open the Cash Application screen.
4
For Pay Type, enter 11 for Gift Certificate.
The Additional Gift Certificate Information screen appears.
5
Enter the Gift Certificate# to use for payment.
If the gift certificate was created in Sales Order Entry but not invoiced, a warning message appears and you cannot use the gift certificate until it is invoiced.
The Certificate Balance and the Expiration Date display. If the balance is zero, the certificate is not valid. If the gift certificate is expired, a warning message appears but you can still use the certificate.
6
Press F2-Save to return to the Cash Application screen. The Payment Amt defaults to either the amount owed or the remaining gift certificate balance, whichever is smaller.
7
Press F2-Save to complete the gift certificate application. The gift certificate is updated with a new remaining balance.
