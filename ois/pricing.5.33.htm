Blanket Contracts
A blanket contract allows you to arrange special pricing, ship via methods, and freight terms for a group of products for a customer or group of customers. When you enter a sales order for the customer using the blanket contract in the Customer P/O# field, the pricing from the blanket contract applies to the order and updates the customer’s account (refer to the Customer Blanket P/O Numbers Screen). You can track the quantities sold by product number by customer.
