Last Price Paid Inquiry
The Last Price Paid Inquiry displays sales order and pricing information for a customer/product, starting with the most recent order.
Viewing Sales Order Pricing
To view the most recent sales orders for a customer/product:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Last Price Paid Inquiry to open the Last Price Paid Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Enter the Product for which you want to view the most recent price paid.
Sales order and pricing information for the customer/product appear.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Family
The product family, if applicable.
Order#
The sales order numbers that contain the product.
N
An asterisk (*) indicates that line notes exist for this item on the order.
ST
The ship-to number for the customer.
Product
The product ordered by the customer.
Ord-Date
The date the customer ordered the material.
Qty-Ord
The quantity ordered.
Net Price
The net price (Gross Price - Disc %) for the product.
GP%
The gross profit percent earned by the sale of this product to this customer.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Review Order
Opens the Order History Inquiry Display screen to allow you to access product and pricing information for the highlighted sales order.
F6-Line Notes
Opens the Notes Display screen which allows you to view or enter any notes for the product/sales order.
F8-IDI
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry to allow you to view detail information on the customer’s invoice.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
