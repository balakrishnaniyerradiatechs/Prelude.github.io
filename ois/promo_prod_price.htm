Promotional Product Pricing screen
The Promotional Product Pricing screen allows you to set up a method to calculate the price for a product on a pricing promotion.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Promotional Pricing > Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance > Price = Y
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Mtd
Determines the method to calculate the price:
•
$ - The product has a set price. The cursor moves to Price to allow you to enter the price.
•
* - The price is a percentage of a column price or cost. Enter the Basis and Pct.
•
T - A Quantity Discount Table calculates the price. The cursor moves to Table to allow you to enter a discount break table. Refer to Discount Break Tables for information on setting up a discount break table.
Price
If you entered $ for Mtd, enter the price.
Basis
If you entered * for Mtd, indicate which price or cost column (from Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing) to use for the calculation. The discount calculated is a percentage over a price column if a price is referenced or a percentage over a cost if a cost is referenced.
Pct
If you entered a price column in Basis, enter the discount to give off of the price. This value appears in Disc% on the Line Item Detail screen on the sales order. If you entered cost column in Basis, enter a percentage over the cost to calculate the sales price. For example if you enter 20 for Pct, the cost is divided by .80 to determine the sales price. This value appears in Net Price on the Line Item Detail screen on the sales order.
F2-Save
Saves the pricing information and returns to the Promotion Type Matrix Maintenance screen.
