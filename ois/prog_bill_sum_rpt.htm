Progress Billing Summary Report
If you use progress billing to invoice customers in increments for a large job, you can use the Progress Billing Summary report to view the estimated, invoiced, and remaining amounts for the customer job. The estimated sales, cost, and gross profit information for a customer’s job is from the Customer Job Maintenance Progress Billing Screen. You can also find this information in the Progress Billing Inquiry.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Progress Billing Summary Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Customer. The customer whose billing information you want to view.
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Salesrep #1. The sales representative assigned to the order.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Customer #/Description
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Job#/Description
Line 1: The customer’s job number.
Line 2: The description of the job, if applicable.
Estimated: This information is entered for a customer job number in the Progress Billing Screen.
Sale
The estimated sales price for the job when it is finished.
Cost
Your estimated cost for the job.
GP%
The estimated gross profit that you will earn for this job.
Invoiced
Sale
The sales invoiced on this job so far.
Cost
The cost invoiced on this job so far.
GP%
The gross profit earned on this job so far.
Remaining
Sale
The sales not yet billed on this customer job.
(Estimated Sale - Invoiced Sale).
Cost
The cost not yet billed on this customer job.
(Estimated Cost - Invoiced Cost).
GP%
The gross profit not yet earned on this customer job.
(Estimated GP% - Invoiced GP%).
