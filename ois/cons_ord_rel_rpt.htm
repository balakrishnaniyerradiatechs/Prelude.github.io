Consignment Order Release Report
The Consignment Order Release report displays information for released type 08 consignment sales orders. Consignment orders contain products that are on loan to the customer. If the customer sells the product, the customer is invoiced for the products sold. If the customer does sell the products, they may be returned back into inventory. Refer to Using Consignment Sales Orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Order Detail Reports > Consignment Reports > Consignment Order Release Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Sell Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse ships the merchandise.
•
Customer. The customer holding the consigned merchandise.
•
Order Date. The date the customer ordered the consigned merchandise.
•
(A)cct/(C)omm Cost. The type of cost to use:
-
A - Accounting cost.
-
C - Commission cost.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust #
The customer number.
Slsm
The sales representative assigned to the customer.
Ord Date
The date the order was placed.
Order #
The sales order number.
#
The number of times the order went through the invoice cycle.
P
Indicates the pick ticket print status:
•
S - The order was suspended and no pick ticket printed.
•
P - The product is available and a pick ticket will print during the next print cycle or when the date to print is reached.
•
Blank - The pick ticket is not ready to print.
I
Indicates the order’s invoice status:
•
Y - The order is flagged for invoicing.
•
H - The order is on hold and cannot be invoiced until released. Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
•
Blank - The order is not yet flagged for invoicing.
Product #
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Curr
The currency indicator code assigned on the order, if you use multiple currency.
Open
The quantity open on the sales order.
UOM
The unit of measure as entered on the sales order.
Cost Ext
The extended cost of the total quantity of the line item (Open x product cost).
Price Ext
The extended price of the total quantity of the line item (Open x product price).
