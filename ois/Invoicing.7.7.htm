Re-Printing Multiple Invoices
Invoice Batch Re-printing allows you to re-print, re-email, or re-fax multiple invoices for a customer. If you fax or email, all invoices must be for the same customer.
To re-transmit multiple invoices:
1
Select Order Processing > Invoice Preview and Printing > Invoice Batch Reprinting to open the Invoice Reprint Entry screen.
2
Enter the Invoice# to re-print. The sales Order Hist#, Whse, Customer, and Description display.
3
Continue to enter as many additional invoices that you need to re-print. You can re-print invoices for multiple warehouses and customers.
4
For Inv Document Option, indicate how the invoices are transmitted to the customer. If more than one customer is included in the invoice re-print, you can only choose the N (print) option.
-
B - Both fax and print.
-
E - Email only.
-
M - Email and print.
-
N - Print only.
-
Y - Fax only.
5
If you are printing the invoice, enter the Printer Form Queue.
6
Verify or change the Invoice Message to print at the bottom of the invoice.
7
Press ENTER. The following message appears:
## record(s) selected for Invoice Printing!
8
Choose one of the following:
•
Print Invoices - Print the invoice.
•
Mask Print Test - Tests the alignment of the invoice forms in the printer. After the forms are correctly aligned, select Print Invoices.
•
Abort Printing - Cancel the invoice print process.
