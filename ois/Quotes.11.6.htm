Setting Maintenance Options for Printing Quotes
The following options determine how quotes are transmitted to the customer:
Customer Maintenance
To set quote printing options for a customer:
1
In Customer Maintenance, select F7-Order > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Order Information screen.
2
For Qte Document Option, determine how quotes transmit to the customer. Choose from the following:
•
B - Fax and print.
•
E - Email only.
•
M - Email and print.
•
N - Print only.
•
Y - Fax only.
3
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the quote information.
Warehouse Maintenance
To set quotation print information for a warehouse:
1
In Warehouse Maintenance, press F6-Printing to open the Continuous Printing Setup screen.
2
For Quotation, determine if quotes print continuously or by batch. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Quotes print upon completion of the quote.
•
N - Quotes print by batch (refer to Printing Quotes by Batch).
3
For Prt, enter the port number (created in Continuous Printer Port Maintenance) used to control continuous printing.
4
For Form Queue, enter the printer number where quotes print.
5
Press F2-Save twice to save the printer information.
