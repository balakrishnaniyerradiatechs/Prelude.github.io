Monthly Invoice & C/M Sequence Register
The Monthly Invoice and C/M Sequence Register displays invoices and credit memos processed for a fiscal period.
This report excludes cash sales and type 07 & 08 consignment orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Monthly Accounting Reports > Invoice & C/M Sequence Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Period. The fiscal period for which you want to view invoices and credit memos.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Inv/Order#
Line 1: The invoice number.
Line 2: The order or credit memo number.
Inv Date
The date the credit was issued or the order was invoiced.
Customer
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Disc-All
The total order discount amount allowed as shown in Disc/Allowance in the Sales Order Completion screen.
Misc-Chgs
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Refer to Entering a Miscellaneous Product.
Frt/Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was invoiced.
Min/Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that was invoiced. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise sold.
Dep-Amt
The current deposit balance. This amount represents the initial deposit placed on a sales order or the remaining balance if some of the deposit was applied to an invoice.
Inv Amount
The total amount of the credit or invoice, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost
The total cost of the merchandise on the order.
G/P%
The gross profit percent calculated from the products sold or credited.
