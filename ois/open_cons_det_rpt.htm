Open Consignment Detail Report
The Daily Open Consignment Detail Report lists open type 08 confirmation consignment orders for a day for a customer. Refer to Using Consignment Sales Orders for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Open Consignment Detail Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Customer. The customers for which you want to view open consignment orders.
•
(A)cct/(C)omm Cost. (A)cct/(C)omm Cost. The type of cost to use:
-
A - Accounting cost.
-
C - Commissions cost.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#/Name
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer name.
Slsm
The salesperson assigned to the customer.
Ord-Date
The date the order was entered.
Order#
The sales order number.
#
The number of times the order went through the invoice cycle.
P
Indicates the pick ticket print status:
•
S - The order was suspended and no pick ticket printed.
•
P - The product is available and a pick ticket will print during the next print cycle or when the date to print is reached.
•
Blank - The pick ticket is not ready to print.
I
Indicates the order’s invoice status:
•
Y - The order is flagged for invoicing.
•
H - The order is on hold and cannot be invoiced until released. Refer to the Administrative Invoice Inquiry.
•
Blank - The order is not yet flagged for invoicing.
Product Number/ Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
C
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
•
Blank - Not part of a kit.
S
Line 1: The line item’s supply code (if one appears on the sales order):
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Line 2: The linked document number, if the product is linked to a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
Qty-Opn
The quantity open on the sales order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the sales order.
Cost/Ext
Line 1: The unit cost of the product.
Line 2: The total cost of the extended quantity of the product (Qty-Opn x Cost).
Price/Ext
Line 1: The unit price for the product.
Line 2: The total price for the extended quantity of the product (Qty-Opn x Price).
