Setting up Credit Card Payment Terms
You can set up a terms code that applies to credit card orders so that credit card information is required when you use this terms code on a sales order. If you have a customer that usually pays with a credit card, you can then assign this payment terms codes to the customer so that their credit card information is defaulted to their sales orders (refer to Assigning Credit Card Information to a Customer).
To set up a Payment Terms code for credit cards:
1
On the Accounts Receivable Menu, select A/R Payment Terms Maintenance to open the A/R Payment Terms Maintenance screen.
2
Set Credit Card Type to Y to indicate that this is a credit card payment type.
3
For Credit Card Entry Mode, choose from the following:
•
Y - (recommended). The cash application screen automatically appears when you complete a sales order so that you can pre-authorize the credit card transaction. If the customer has credit card information set up in Customer Maintenance, it is automatically defaulted so that you are not required to enter it in the Sales Order Header.
•
N - When you enter a sales order for the customer, the Credit Card Information screen appears after you complete the header so that you can enter or verify the credit card information. When you finish entering the line items and press F2-Save, you must choose Complete to access the Sales Order Completion screen and to pre-authorize the credit card transaction.
4
Press F2-Save to save the payment terms information.
