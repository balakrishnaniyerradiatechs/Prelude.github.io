Cash Application screen
The Cash Application screen allows you to enter the method of payment and payment amount for a cash sales order.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry > Complete > F2-Complete
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Pay Type
The method of payment:
•
01 - Cash.
•
02 - Check. The Additional Check Information screen appears to allow you to enter the check number and other personal information for the customer.
•
03 - Bank Card. Opens the Additional Credit Card Information screen to allow you to enter the credit card type, number, and expiration date information. Refer to Credit Cards.
•
11 - Gift Certificate. Opens the Additional Gift Certificate Information screen to allow you to enter the gift certificate number. Refer to Gift Certificates.
Pay types are set up in Order Processing Controls > Payment methods.
Payment Amt
The amount being paid by the customer for the corresponding Pay Type.
Amount Tendered
The total amount received from the customer for all the pay types used for the order.
Change Due
The amount due back to the customer if the Amount Tendered is more that the Total Due on the Sales Order Completion screen.
F2-Save
Saves the payment information and returns to the Sales Order Completion screen.
F5-Additional Info
Depending on the Pay Type, opens one of the following screens:
•
02 - Check. Opens the Additional Check Information screen to allow you to enter the check number and other personal information for the customer.
•
03 - Bank Card. Opens the Additional Credit Card Information screen to allow you to enter the credit card type, number, and expiration date information.
•
11 - Gift Certificate. Opens the Additional Gift Certificate Information screen.
