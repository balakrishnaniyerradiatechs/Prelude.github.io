Splitting Line Items for Delivery Dates
If you need to ship the total quantity of a product on a sales order into separate shipments to meet your customerâ€™s schedule, you can split the quantity to accommodate two or more delivery dates. Nightly Processing uses the delivery dates to calculate replenishment quantities.
When you enter a quantity on the Line Item Detail screen (refer to Entering Sales Order Line Items), the Req-Date and Pck Date that you enter become the required date and the pick ticket date for one part of the quantity to ship. To split the remaining quantity into separate delivery dates, refer to the following:
1
On the Sales Order Entry screen, highlight the item to split. From the Action Bar, select Prod Func > Line Item Split. The Order Line Split screen appears.
2
Enter the Quantity that you want to ship on the second delivery date.
3
For Req Date, enter the date when the customer requires the second shipment of the product.
4
Change the Pck Date if the calculated date is not correct.
5
Continue to enter quantities and required dates for as many split quantities as necessary. The overall quantity in the Order Line Split screen must not be equal to or greater than the original line item quantity on the order. The quantity remaining for the line item uses the original required date from the Line Item Detail Information screen.
6
Press F2-Save. The split line items display on the sales order with the respective new quantities and delivery dates. All other line item information is the same as the original line item. The original quantity is modified to equal the original quantity minus the new split quantities.
Splitting Line Item Quantities Linked to Purchase Orders
Multiple P/O Sourcing in Purchasing Parameters must be set to Y to allow you to split a quantity linked to a purchase order.
