Using Crib Warehouses
A crib warehouse refers to a warehouse located within your customer’s facility. For example, you might set up a tool crib in the customer’s warehouse. The tools remain your property until the customer is billed at the end of the month for all tools taken out of the crib. The customer must be set up for consolidated invoicing (Consolidate Invoices set to Y in the Additional Order Information Screen) to use a crib warehouse.
Product Crib Information
The Product Whse Crib Information screen allows you to enter crib product information if the warehouse is a tool crib at a customer’s facility (Product Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Optns > Crib Information).
