Administrative Order Inquiry
The Administrative Order Inquiry contains sales orders put on hold for pricing or other ordering restrictions defined by your company. The order must be released from hold before a pick ticket can print. Refer to the following sections for information on setting up administrative hold options and releasing orders from hold:
