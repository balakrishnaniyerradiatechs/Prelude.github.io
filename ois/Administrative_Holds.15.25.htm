System Level Administrative Quote Hold Options
You can set option parameters for your company to define the restrictions that place quotes on Administrative Quote Hold.
To define Administrative Quote Hold parameters:
1
Select Order Processing Parameters > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For Administrative Quote Hold, enter Y to open the Administrative Quote Hold Types screen.
3
Under AQH Types, enter one or more of the following codes to determine what places a quote on Administrative Quote Hold:
•
F - Override Freight Terms: The Freight Terms code is changed in the Quotation Additional Information screen on the quote header.
•
I - Incompatible Ship Via: The Ship Via Code is incompatible with the customer or the product. Refer to Product Level Administrative Quote Hold Options.
•
P - Price Violations: A price is changed to a value that is lower than the default price, lower than the cost, or below the acceptable gross profit percent. This option must be selected as quote hold type to activate the N price violation type.
•
N - Minimum Price Violations: A line item’s selling price is below the product’s Minimum Price (Product Maintenance > F7-Pricing or Product Warehouse Maintenance > F7-Price/Cost).
•
V - Manager High/Low Level Limit GP%: A line item’s gross profit percent is below the Mgr Low Level Minimum GP% or more than the Mgr High Level GP% (on this screen). Contract pricing is excluded.
•
W - Web Quotes. Quotes placed through VIA.
4
For Mgr Low Level Minimum GP%, define the minimum gross profit percentage allowed for a line item before it is placed on hold. This value can be overridden if a Mgr Low Level GP% exists for the product line.
5
For Mgr High Level GP%, define the maximum gross profit percentage that a line item can be before it is placed on hold. This value can be overridden if a Mgr High Level GP% exists for the product line.
6
Press F2-Save to return to the Additional Information screen.
7
Press F5-Add Info to open the Additional Options screen.
8
For Suspend Orders From Activities, determine if suspended sales orders can go on credit hold or administrative hold. Choose from the following:
•
Y - Do not place a suspended order or quote on hold, even if it meets the criteria for credit hold or administrative hold.
•
N - If a sales order or quote meets the criteria for credit hold or administrative hold, it will go on hold even if it is suspended.
9
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the Order Processing Parameter Information.
