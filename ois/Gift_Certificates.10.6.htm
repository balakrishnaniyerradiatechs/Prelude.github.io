Creating a Gift Certificate in Gift Certificate Maintenance
If you want to create a gift certificate without entering a sales order, you can use Gift Certificate Maintenance. The gift certificate is immediately available for use as a form of payment. You can also use Gift Certificate Maintenance to edit any gift certificates created in Sales Order Entry (refer to Creating a Gift Certificate in Sales Order Entry).
To create a gift certificate:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Order Entry Control Files > Gift Certificate Maintenance to open the Gift Certificate Maintenance screen.
2
For Certificate Number, choose from the following:
•
If you have your own gift certificate numbers, enter the number.
•
If you use system generated gift certificate numbers (refer to Setting System Options for Gift Certificates) press ENTER over NEW to assign a new gift certificate number.
3
For Recipient Name, enter the name of the person who will receive the gift certificate. You can use this name when searching for a gift certificate.
4
Enter the address information for the recipient, if known.
5
You can enter a Message to print on the certificate.
6
For Expiration Date, enter the date the gift certificate will expire (optional). When the gift certificate is used, the expiration date is validated if one exists. If the gift certificate is expired, a warning displays but the gift certificate can still be used.
7
For Issuing Customer, enter the account number of the customer that is purchasing the gift certificate.
8
For Certificate Amount, enter the value of the gift certificate. The Remaining Balance defaults to the Certificate Amount. This balance is reduced each time the gift certificate number is used.
9
If you want to print a copy of the certificate, press F8-Print Certificate.
10
Press F2-Update to create the gift certificate.
The gift certificate is available for immediate use (refer to Applying a Gift Certificate to a Sales Order).
