Price Listing Warehouse Specific
The Price Listing Warehouse Specific displays all products/prices in one warehouse for a customer.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Miscellaneous Reports > Pricing Reports > Standard Pricing Reports > Price Listing Warehouse Specific
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse for the customer.
•
Customer. The customer whose prices you want to display.
•
Catalog Date. The customer’s prices as of this date. If you enter a future date, then the system uses that date to calculate prices.
•
Job Number. The customer’s job number, if applicable.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product Number
Line 1: The part number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product, if applicable.
Customer Prod Number
The customer’s part number, if applicable. Refer to Setting Up Customer Product Numbers.
Brk
The quantity break table assigned to the product/customer.
Quantity
The quantities the customer must buy to get the corresponding price.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product for this customer.
Customer Prc
The customer’s price for each quantity break.
