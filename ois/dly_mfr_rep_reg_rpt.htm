Daily Manufacturer’s Rep Invoice Register
The Daily Manufacturer’s Rep Invoice Register lists all manufacturer representative’s (type 17) orders invoiced in a day. Refer to Using Manufacturer Rep Sales Orders for more information.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Manufacturers Rep Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwestern Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
•
Currency. The currency indicator code assigned to the manufacturer’s rep.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Order#
The sales order number.
Inv Date
The date the order was invoiced.
Cust#/Name
The customer number and description.
Mdse-Amt
The total amount invoiced for merchandise.
Inv Amount
The total invoice amount, including taxes and extra charges.
Cost
The total cost of the products on the order.
G/P%
The gross profit percent earned on the order.
Currency
The currency code, if you use multiple currencies.
