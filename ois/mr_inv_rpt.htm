M/R Invoice/Shipped Report
The M/R Invoice/Shipped report includes information on manufacturer rep orders that shipped to the customer, but are not updated in Manufacturer Rep Invoice Processing.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Manufacturer Rep Subsystem > Manufacturer Rep Reports > M/R Invoice Shipped Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that received credit for the sale.
•
Ship Date Range. The beginning and ending dates that the material shipped to the customer.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Mf Ref
The manufacturer’s reference number assigned in the manufacturer’s rep order.
Cust#
The customer number.
Customer P/O#
The customer’s purchase order number.
Order #
The manufacturer’s rep order number.
Ent-Date
The date the order was entered.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 2: The second line of the description for the product.
Shp-Date
The date the product shipped to the customer.
Ship-Qty
The quantity of the product that shipped on this date.
Net-Price
The price the customer paid for the product.
Ext-Amt
The total price for the quantity shipped to the customer (Ship-Qty x Net-Price).
Comm%
The percent of the Net-Price that you will receive from the vendor for the product sold.
Comm-Rate
Not applicable
Comm-Amt
The actual amount of commission that you will receive (Ext-Amt x Comm%/100).
