Setting up Crib Information for a Product/Warehouse
The Product Whse Crib Information screen allows you to enter crib information for a product for the crib warehouse. The product is considered your property until it is billed to the customer.
To enter crib information for a product/warehouse:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Optns > Crib Information to open the Product Whse Crib Information screen.
2
Enter the crib accounting code to display when you sell the product. If left blank, the crib accounting code from Customer Department Maintenance is used (refer to Maintaining Customer Department File Information).
3
Enter the Crib Product Type. Choose from the following:
•
C - Consigned.
•
M - Managed.
This field is for information only. Can be used for custom reports.
4
Press F2-Save to save the product warehouse crib information.
