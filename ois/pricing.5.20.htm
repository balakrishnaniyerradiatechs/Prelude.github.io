Retail Price Matrices
Retail price matrices are used to give special prices beyond the column and/or quantity break prices. Just like regular prices matrices, you can set up different combinations for customers and products, for a single location or all locations. Refer to the following sections for information on retail price matrices:

Retail Price Recalc Utility
If you change a price in Retail Price Matrix Maintenance, you can use the Retail Price Recalculation Utility to update all open orders that contain the customer/product combination.
Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Retail Price Matrix Maintenance > Retail Price Recalc Utility
