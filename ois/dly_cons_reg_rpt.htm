Daily Consignment Register
The Daily Consignment Register lists all type 08 consignment orders invoiced for a day. A type 08 consignment order is created when a type 07 consignment order is invoiced. You can use the 07 consignment order to remove material from on-hand inventory when it is sent to the customer, but do not want to bill them until later (possibly when they sell it). For more information on consignment orders, refer to Using Consignment Sales Orders.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Reports > Daily Accounting Reports > Consignment Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse that entered the consignment orders.
•
Invoice Date. The day the consignment orders were invoiced.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Inv/Order#
Line 1: The consignment invoice number.
Line 2: The order number.
Inv Date
The date the consignment order was invoiced.
Cust#
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer description.
Disc-Allw
The total order discount amount allowed as shown in Disc/Allowance in the Sales Order Completion screen.
Misc-Chgs
The total charges from M (taxable), MN (non-taxable), and MNP (Canadian taxable) products, plus the delivery and pickup charges from the Sales Order Completion screen.
Refer to Entering a Miscellaneous Product.
Frt/Hand
The total of Inbound Freight, Outbound Freight, and Handling charges (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped.
Tax-Amt
The total amount of sales tax charged for the material that was available to ship.
Min/Rstk
The total of Minimum Charge and Restocking Charge (Sales Order Completion screen) for the material that shipped. Refer to Using the Sales Order Completion Screen.
Mdse-Amt
The total value of merchandise.
Dep-Amt
The deposit balance which represents the initial deposit placed on the consignment order or the remaining balance if some of the deposit was applied to an invoice.
Inv Amount
The total amount of the invoice, including any extra charges and tax.
Cost
The total cost of the merchandise sold, as entered on the sales order.
G/P%
The gross profit percentage earned for the products sold.
