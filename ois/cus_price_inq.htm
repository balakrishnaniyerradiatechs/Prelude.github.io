Customer Price Inquiry
The Customer Price Inquiry displays pricing information for a customer/warehouse/product combination.
Viewing Customer Prices
To view a customer price:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Price Inquiry to open the Customer Price Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Identify the Sell Warehouse that receives credit for the sale.
4
If you use multiple currencies, the Currency Indicator screen appears. Enter the Currency.
5
If applicable, enter a Job# or a CustP/O# associated with the customer/product price.
6
For Product/Description, enter the part number.
7
If applicable, you can enter a Quantity to see pricing information for different quantities of the product.
Pricing information appears.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Price Group
The price group assigned to the customer.
Def Prc Col
The price column set up for this customer (e.g., 0 - Suggested retail, 1 - Price 1, etc).
Tx?
Indicates if the product is tax exempt.
Price Grp
The price group assigned to the product.
Product Line
The product line number assigned to the product.
Vendor
The primary vendor assigned to the product.
U/M
The unit of measure assigned to the product.
Gross Price
The gross price assigned to the product/warehouse.
Disc%
The discount percent off of the gross price for the product.
Net Price
The net price (Gross Price x Disc%) for the product/warehouse.
Extension
The extended price of the product (Quantity x Net Price).
Src
The source of the product price (e.g., the price column or price matrix type). Refer to Price Source Codes.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Whse Avail
Opens the Product Warehouse Availability screen to display available quantity information for each warehouse that stocks the product.
F6-Qty Break
Displays the price assigned to each quantity break set up for the product.
F7-Price Matrix
Opens the Customer Price Group/Product Number Matrix Inquiry screen to display price matrix information for the product/customer. Refer to Price Matrices.
Action Bar: Inquiries
Alternates
Opens the Product Alternates Inquiry screen to list any alternates available for the product.
Substitutes
Opens the Product Substitutes Inquiry screen to list substitute product information for the product.
Accessories
Opens the Accessory Inquiry to list any accessory products set up for the product. Accessory products are products that you may want to sell to go along with another product (refer to Adding Accessory Products to an Order).
Complements
Opens the Product Complement Inquiry to list any complements set up for the product. Complement information provides the salesperson with helpful information regarding a product.
Additional Description
Opens the Additional Description screen to allow you to view any additional descriptions set up for the product.
Customer Discounts
Opens the Chain Discount Inquiry screen to display chain discount information set up for the product for the customer.
TPCx Price Availability Request
If your company is set up to use TPCx (Trading Partner Connect), you can check price and availability for the product from other trading partners.
