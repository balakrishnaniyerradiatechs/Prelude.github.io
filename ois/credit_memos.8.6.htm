Printing RGAs by Batch
If you don’t continuously print RGAs (refer to Setting Maintenance Options for RGAs), you can print the RGAs by batch.
To print RGAs:
1
Select Order Processing > Optional Entry and Printing > RGA Printing to open the RGA Ticket Printing screen.
2
Enter the Warehouse Number for which you want to print RGAs.
3
For Returned Goods Number, enter the RGA to print or leave blank to print all RGAs for the warehouse.
4
If you entered a Returned Goods Number, choose one of the following for RGA Document Option:
•
E - Email RGA.
•
F - Fax RGA.
•
B - Print and fax RGA.
•
M - Print and email RGA.
•
P - Print RGA.
5
Enter the Printer Form Queue where the RGAs are to print. The following message appears:
Load Pick Ticket Forms in Printer & Align Visually
6
Choose from the following:
•
Print RGA Tickets. Prints, faxes, or emails the RGAs.
•
Mask Print Test: Prints a mask to make sure paper is aligned properly.
•
Abort Printing. Cancels the print process.
Once you choose Print RGA Tickets, the following prompt displays:
RGA Printing complete...
7
Choose from the following:
•
Process. Updates the RGAs as printed.
•
Reprint All. Reprints all RGAs.
•
Single Reprint. Allows you to reprint a single RGA.
•
Abort. Cancels the print process.
Once you choose Process, the following question appears:
Are You Sure You Want to Process?
8
Enter Y to complete the RGA.
