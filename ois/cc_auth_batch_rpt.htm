Credit Card Authorization Batch Report
The Credit Card Authorization Batch Report includes sales orders that are pre-authorized but not yet invoiced or post-authorized. Refer to Post-Authorizing Credit Card Transactions.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Credit Card Authorization Batch
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse where credit card transactions took place.
•
Invoice Date. Not applicable.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the material.
Order#
The sales order number.
Inv Date
Not applicable.
Trans Date
Not applicable.
Cc Type
The credit card type. Refer to Assigning Credit Bureau Codes.
Pay Amt
The amount charged to the customer.
Batch Status
Not applicable.
Pre Auth
The pre-authorization number (refer to Pre-Authorizing a Sales Order.
Post Auth
Not applicable.
