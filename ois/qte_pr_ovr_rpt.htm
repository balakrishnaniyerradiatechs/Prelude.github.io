Quote Price Override Audit Report
The Quote Price Override Audit Report lists quotes on which a price was changed from the original price assigned to the customer.
After you run the report, a dialog box appears to allow you to purge the product information so that it is not included the next time you run the report.
Menu Path: Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Quote Price Override Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Division. The highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
Region. A subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
Department. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
Cost Center. A subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse that receives credit for the sale, regardless of which warehouse ships the merchandise.
•
Override Date <. Include quotes that had a price changed before this date.
•
Customer. The customer to include if you want to limit the report to a customer.
•
Product Type. The product type if you want to limit the report to only quotes with one or more of the following product types:
-
F - Finished goods.
-
R - Raw materials.
-
B - Finished and raw materials.
-
E - Expense items.
-
N - Rental products.
-
P - Parts.
-
U - Used materials.
Refer to Product Maintenance in the Inventory documentation.
•
Product Line. The product line numbers if you want to limit the report to one or more product lines.
•
Product. The product numbers if you want to limit the report to one or more product numbers.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Cust#
Line 1: The customer number.
Line 2: The customer name.
/Rsn Code
The price override reason code, if one was entered. Refer to Creating Reason Codes for Price Overrides.
Slm/QTE#
Line 1: The number for the sales representative assigned in the sales order header screen.
Line 2: The quote number.
P
Indicates the print/fax/email status of the quote:
•
P - Printed.
•
E - Emailed.
•
M - Printed/emailed.
•
B - Printed/faxed.
•
F - Faxed.
Product Number
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Line 3: The second line of description for the product.
Ov Date
The date the quote was overridden.
C
Indicates if the line item is part of a kit:
•
K - Kit parent.
•
C - Kit component.
•
Blank - Not a kit.
S
Line 1: The line item’s supply code (if one appears on the quote):
•
B - Purchase order.
•
E - Emergency purchase order.
•
D - Direct ship purchase order.
•
R - Emergency stock transfer.
•
T - Stock transfer.
•
W - Work order.
Line 2: The linked document number if the product is linked to a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
Pine
The product line number
#
The quantity of the product on the quote.
Typ
Line 1: The unit of measure for the line item:
Line 2: The product type:
•
F - Finished goods.
•
R - Raw materials.
•
E - Expense items.
•
N - Rental products.
Orig/Ext.
Line 1: The original price that appeared on the quote for the customer.
Line 2: The extended price (quantity x price).
Price/Ext
Line 1: The new price.
Line 2: The extended new price (quantity x price).
Cost/Ext
Line 1: The cost of the product.
Line 2: The extended cost of the product (quantity x price).
GP%/ID
Line 1: The gross profit percent earned on the quote.
Line 2: The user ID of the person that entered the quote.
