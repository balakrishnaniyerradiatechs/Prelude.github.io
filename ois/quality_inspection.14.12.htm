Tightened Inspection Table
The tightened inspection table shows the acceptance and rejection levels for each sample size of a product considered a tightened inspection. Tightened inspection is used when the results from a normal inspection have non-acceptable results in 2 out of the last 5 batches. These values are determined by ANSI and are already set in your system by Prelude but can be changed if necessary.
To view or edit the tightened acceptable quality levels:
1
Select Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quality Inspection Subsystem > Maintenance > Single Sampling - Tightened Inspection Table to open the Acceptable Quality Levels screen.
2
Press F2-Save to save any changes and to close the screen.
