Copying a Customer Contract by Product
To copy contract information for a customer/product to another customer/product:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Pricing Structures > Contract Price Maintenance > Contract Price Maintenance to open the Contract Price Maintenance screen.
2
From the Action Bar, select Copy Contract to open the Copy Contract Price screen.
3
For Copy From Customer #, enter the customer whose contract you want to copy.
4
For Copy From Contract #, enter the contract to copy.
5
For Copy From Product #, enter the product whose contract price you want to copy.
6
For Copy To Customer #, enter the customer that should receive the contract pricing.
7
For Copy To Contract #, enter the contract number for the customer receiving the contract information.
8
For Copy To Product #, enter the product that is to receive the contract price.
9
Press F2-Copy to copy the contract information (excluding ship-to information).
