Master Account Inquiry
The Master Account Inquiry lists all sub-accounts assigned to a master customer. Master accounts and sub-accounts are assigned in the Customer Maintenance A/R Information Screen.
Viewing Master Sub-Accounts
To list all customer sub-accounts assigned to a master account:
1
Select Order Processing > Inquiries > Customer Info Inquiries > Master Account Inquiry to open the Master Account Inquiry screen.
2
For Customer, enter the master account number (Master Account Flag set to Y in the Customer Maintenance A/R Information Screen).
All customers assigned to the master account appear.
