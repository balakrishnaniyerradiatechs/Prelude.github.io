Activating the Quality Inspection Subsystem
To activate the Quality Inspection Subsystem:
1
Select Option Parameters > Inventory Parameters > F5-Add Info to open the Inventory Add. Parameters screen.
2
Set Quality Inspection - AQL to Y to control the following:
•
The setup of the product in Product Maintenance (refer to Assigning Inspection Procedures for Products).
•
The required information in the Quality Control Inspection Inquiry).
3
Press F2-Save 2 times to save the quality inspection information.
If you use Quality Control Inspections for receiving in the Inventory module, set Quality Inspection- AQL to N. Refer to ”Requiring Quality Control Inspections in Receiving” in the Inventory documentation for more information.
