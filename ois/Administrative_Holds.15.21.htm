Manually Placing Orders on Administrative Invoice Hold
You can manually place a sales order on Administrative Invoice Hold either from within Sales Order Entry or from Manual Administrative Hold.
To manually place an order on Administrative Invoice Hold within a sales order:
1
From the Sales Order Entry screen Action Bar, select Order Func > Manual Administrative Invoice Hold.
2
Enter Y for Manual Administrative Hold.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the sales order.
4
After you save the order, it goes on hold and will not be invoiced until the order is released by the Administrative Invoice Manager. You can choose to enter a message to the manager or to enter notes regarding the order.
 
To manually place an order on hold using Manual Administrative Hold:
1
Select Order Processing > Processes > Manual Admin Hold Procedure to open the Manual Administrative Hold screen.
2
For Order Number, enter the sales order number to place on hold.
3
For Order or Invoice Hold, enter I to place the order on Administrative Invoice Hold.
4
Press F2-Update to complete the hold.
