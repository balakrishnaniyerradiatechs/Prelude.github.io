Credit Card Authorization Rejection Maintenance screen
If you have items that appear on the Credit Card Post Authorization Rejection Report when you post-authorize a batch, you can use Credit Card Rejection Maintenance to manually remove rejected items and post them to history. You would only use this method if you know the reason for rejection (e.g., the customer paid by other means) and it cannot be handled by other methods (refer to Removing Rejected Credit Card Transactions).
Menu Path: Order Processing > Processes > Credit Card Processes > Additional Batch Functions > Credit Card Rejection Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Reference Key
The sales order number to edit.
Move To Hist?
Determines if you want to move the transaction to history to show as deleted:
•
Y - Moves the transaction to history and updates the status as deleted.
•
N - Leave the transaction as is.
Customer
The customer associated with the sales order.
Order
The sales order number.
Inv Num
The invoice number assigned to the order.
Type
The type of credit card (e.g., VISA, M/C).
Credit Card
The credit card number.
Exp Date
The expiration date of the credit card.
Amount
The amount billed to the customer.
F2-Save
Moves the transaction to history.
