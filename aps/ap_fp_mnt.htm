A/P Floor Planning Maintenance screen
The A/P Floor Planning Maintenance allows you to create and edit sequence codes to assign to floor plan products. Floor plan items are paid for when the item is sold or on a designated date (e.g., 90 days from invoice date). Floor plan items are typically large, expensive items (e.g., farm tractors). To track the cost of floor plan items, you can assign a code to each product received. If you do not assign the code when the product is received, use A/P Floor Planning Maintenance to set up the code. You can use your own number or let the system automatically assign the next sequential number.
Refer to Tracking Floor Plan Inventory for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance> A/P Floor Plan Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Sequence Number
The sequence number for this A/P Floor Plan record.
Product
The product number of the item created for this floor plan.
Product Description (not labeled)
The product description.
Warehouse
The warehouse number for this floor plan.
Serial Number
The serial number for this item if one exists.
Status
The status of this floor plan:
•
O - Open. The item was not shipped or paid for.
•
S - Shipped. The item was fully or partially shipped.
•
P - Paid The item is fully paid for.
Quantity
The product quantity for the floor plan.
Cost
The cost per unit for this item.
Extension
The extended amount for the total quantity of this item.
Vendor
The vendor number. Displays the vendor description.
Invoice Number
The invoice number to assign to the floor plan item.
Invoice Date
The invoice date from the vendor’s invoice.
Due Date
The due date from the vendor’s invoice.
Amount Paid
The amount paid for the floor plan items purchased.
F2-Save
Saves the floor planning information and closes the screen.
F4-Del
Deletes the floor planning information.
F5-Shipped Info
Opens the Floor Plan Shipped Information screen so you can enter shipping information such as invoice number, date, and quantity for the floor plan item.
F6-Check History
Opens the Check History screen so you can enter the check number, date and amount for the floor plan item.
