Detail Payable Aging Inquiry
The Detail Payable Aging Inquiry displays aging and balance information for open invoices for a vendor.
Viewing Detailed A/P Aging
To view aged payables:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Detail Payable Aging Inquiry to open the Detail Payable Aging Inquiry screen.
2
Enter a number or keyword for the Vendor or press F3 to search.
