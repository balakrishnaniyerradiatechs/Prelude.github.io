Multi Discounts screen
The Multi Discounts screen allows you to view discount date and discount terms percentage for the invoice if paid by the discount date. The discount terms percentage is based on the Dsc Date and Term% you define for the invoice.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Inquiries > Vendor Invoice History Inquiry > Action Bar > Mlt Lvl Dsc
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln# (unlabeled)
Line count of discounts.
Dsc Date
The date by which you must pay the vendor to receive the discount. The system calculates this date using the invoice date and accounts payable terms.
Term%
The discount percentage to apply if you pay the vendor by the discount date.
OK
Closes the screen.
