Correcting a Vendor Number or Invoice Number
If you enter a invoice number incorrectly or enter an invoice for the wrong vendor, you must correct the invoice and/or vendor in Vendor Invoice Number Change Maintenance.
To correct a vendor invoice number:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Processes > Vendor Invoice Number Change Maintenance to access the Invoice Number Change Maintenance screen.
2
For Vendor, enter the number or description of the vendor or press F3 to search for the incorrect invoice.
3
Enter the incorrect Invoice Number or press F3 to search. The Invoice Date and Invoice Amount appear.
4
For New Vendor, choose one of the following:
•
If the invoice was entered for the correct vendor, enter the same vendor as in Vendor.
•
If the invoice was not entered for the correct vendor, enter the number or description of the correct vendor or press F3 to search.
5
For New Invoice Number, choose one of the following:
•
If the invoice number was entered correctly, enter the same number as Invoice Number.
•
If the invoice number was not entered correctly, enter the correct number.
6
Press F2-Save.
