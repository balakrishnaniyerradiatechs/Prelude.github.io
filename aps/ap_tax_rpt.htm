Accounts Payable Tax Report
The Accounts Payable Tax report shows vendor invoices with a tax amount.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > A/P Monthly Reports > A/P Tax Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Posted Period. The financial period associated with the A/P tax information.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
VAT Registration #
The value added tax registration number assigned to this vendor in Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P.
Invoice Number
The number of the vendor invoice.
Inv Date
The date of the vendor invoice.
Inv. Amount
The amount of the vendor invoice.
Tax Amt
The amount entered as tax.
