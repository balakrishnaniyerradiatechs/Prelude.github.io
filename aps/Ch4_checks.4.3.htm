Starting a Batch
To enter batch information:
1
In Check Batch, enter the number of the check batch (e.g., your initials and the date) or press F3 to search for a batch.
2
For Bank Code, enter the bank code or press F3 to search. The Bank Code represents the bank from which you will pay the vendors.
3
For Check Date, enter the date to print on the payment. The fiscal period appears.
 
You can press F5-Auto Sel, F6-Manual Sel, and F8-Print Chks after choosing a fiscal period.
4
For Batch Type, indicate which type of invoices you want to pay based on their status when they were reconciled (refer to Reconciling Inventory Invoices). Choose one of the following:
•
R - Regular: Prints paper checks.
•
A - A/P floor plan: Selects only floor plan invoices if the inventory was sold or if payment is due according to the payment terms.
•
F - A/P floor plan (automatic bank deduction): Selects only floor plan invoices but does not print a check.
•
B - Automatic bank deduction: Selects invoices that will be paid via automatic bank deduction.
•
E - Electronic funds transfer: Selects invoices that will be paid via electronic funds transfer.
•
C - Credit Card: Selects invoices that will be paid by credit card.
5
For Due Date, enter the date you want to use to select which invoices to pay. Invoices that are due before this date are selected. Leave this field blank to select invoices regardless of their due date.
6
For Discount Date, enter the date you want to use to select invoices. Invoices that must be paid by this date to get a discount are selected. Leave this field blank to select invoices regardless of discount date.
 
For A/P floor planning, the Discount Date is the ship date or the sell date.
7
For Take Lost Discounts?, choose one of the following:
•
Y - Take discounts regardless of the discount date.
•
N - Do not take discounts past their discount date.
 
If Unearned Disc Days is specified in Vendor Maintenance, it overrides your choice for Take Lost Discounts? For example, if the vendor’s Unearned Disc Days is 10, the discount will be taken for 10 days past the discount day. Therefore, if the discount date on the invoice is 10/01/04, the discount will be taken until 10/11/04 even if you enter N for Take Lost Discounts?. If you print checks after 10/11/04, the discount will not be taken even if you enter Y for Take Lost Discounts?.
8
For Additional Selection, choose one of the following:
•
N - Do not specify any additional criteria to select invoices to pay. Skip to step 10.
•
Y - Specify additional criteria to select invoices to pay. The A/P Check Add Selection screen appears.
•
E - Specify additional criteria to exclude invoices to pay. The A/P Check Add Selection screen appears.
9
For the following fields, enter the appropriate data or press F3 to search:
•
For Vendor Payable Type, enter the Payable Type in Vendor Maintenance for this vendor. For more information on Payable Types, refer to the Inventory documentation.
•
Enter the Division Number, which represents the highest possible grouping in your company, if applicable.
•
Enter the Region Number, which is a subset of Division, if Division is used.
•
Enter the Location Number, which is the physical location or warehouse.
•
Enter the Department Number, which is a subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a Warehouse.
•
Enter the Cost Center Number, which is a subset of Department if Department is used.
•
Enter a lower and/or upper open balance limit.
10
For Sort By Option?, choose how to sort the invoices on the check stub and the selection report:
•
D - Due date.
•
I - Invoice number.
•
N - Invoice date.
After you enter the batch information, you must identify which invoices to pay (refer to Selecting Invoices to Pay).
