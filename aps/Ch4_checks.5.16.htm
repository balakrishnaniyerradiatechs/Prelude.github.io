Printing Debit Memos
When you create a debit memo in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry or Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry, you can manually generate a debit memo if you want to provide the vendor with a copy.
Based on your system settings, a standard GRIM screen may appear. The GRIM allows you to change selection criteria and schedule debit memos to email, fax, or print. For more information, please see your System Administrator.
To generate debit memos:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Debit Memo Printing to access the Debit Memo Printing screen.
2
For Vendor, choose one of the following:
•
To print debit memos for only one vendor, enter the vendor’s number.
•
To print debit memos for all vendors, leave Vendor blank.
To reprint a debit memo, select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Debit Memo Reprinting.
3
For Debit Memo Number, choose one of the following:
•
To print one debit memo, enter the debit memo number.
•
To print all debit memos, leave Debit Memo Number blank.
4
For Document Option, choose one of the following:
•
P - Print (default).
•
F - Fax.
•
E - E-mail.
5
For Printer Form Queue, enter the designated printer you want to send the debit memo(s) to if you are printing.
6
Press F2-Save.
