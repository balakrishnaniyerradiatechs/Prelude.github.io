Additional Flags screen
The Additional Flags screen allows you to enter replacement equipment information. You can reverse the invoice with negative distribution amounts. The invoice number is the same, appended with an A.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Manual Invoice Entry > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Additional Flags
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Replacement Equipment Invoice
Determines if the invoice is a Replacement Equipment Invoice; choose Yes or No.
Advance Payment Collection Date
The advance payment date and fiscal period.
F2-Save
Saves the advance payment collection information.
