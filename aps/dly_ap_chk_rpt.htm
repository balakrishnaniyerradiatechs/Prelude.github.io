Daily A/P Check Register
The Daily A/P Check Register report includes all vendor payments processed in Payment Selection/Print for a date range. Refer to Selecting Vendors/Invoices to Pay.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Check Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Check Date range. The beginning and ending dates on which the payment was processed.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check#
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date on the payment.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The invoice number(s) that were paid on the payment.
Inv-Date
The date on the invoice.
Gross Paid
The gross amount due for the invoice.
Disc Taken
The discount taken from the invoice.
Net Amount
The amount paid (Gross Paid - Disc Taken).
