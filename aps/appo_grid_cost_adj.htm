AP/PO Cost Adjustments screen
The A/P-P/O Cost Adjustments screen allows you to select and recost items on the vendor invoice.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Invoice Reconciliation Entry > F7-Recost
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln# (not labeled)
The line count of the items on the receiver.
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The description.
Notes
An asterisk (*) if line notes exists. Press F5-Ln Notes to view the notes.
Whse
The shipping warehouse.
Qty Rec
The quantity of the product received.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Disc
The discount percentage applied to the product (if any).
Rev Cost
The revised cost of the product per unit.
Rev Dsc
The revised discount percentage (if any).
Extension
The extended dollar amount of the item (Qty Rec X Rev Cost - Rev Dsc (if any).
Linkage
The order number the item is linked to if the item is linked. Linkages can exist for sales orders, transfers, and work orders.
Vendor P/N
The product number assigned to the product by the vendor.
Revised Merchandise Total
The revised total for the invoice. To determine the final total, the system calculates all adjusted quantities, costs, and discount percent for the products on the invoice.
F2-Save
Saves the cost adjustment information.
F5-Ln Notes
Opens the Notes screen, which allows you to enter, edit, or view notes for this transaction. Only available from Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry.
F6-Hdr Review
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen. Only available from Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry.
F7-Buyer Notes
Displays notes between A/P and Purchasing. Only available from Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are line number, page number, reference number, B (bottom), or T (top).
Action Bar
Examine
Not applicable to cost adjustments.
Sort
Determines the order of the items on the invoice:
•
D - By product description.
•
P - By product number.
•
V - By vendor product number.
Misc Entry
Not applicable to cost adjustments.
