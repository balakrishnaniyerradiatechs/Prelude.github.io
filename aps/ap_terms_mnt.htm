A/P Terms Maintenance screen
The A/P Terms Maintenance screen allows you to create and maintain accounts payable terms. An accounts payable term is a code you can assign for different due dates, discount percentages, net and discount days of month, installments and floor planning. Terms codes are assigned to each vendor in Vendor Maintenance.
Refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance > A/P Payment Terms Maintenance.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Payment Terms
The terms code.
Description
The description of the A/P terms code.
Terms Percent
The discount percent for this terms code. This percentage is multiplied by the merchandise amount. For example, if Terms Percent is 2% and is applied to a merchandise total of $100.00, the discount is $2.00.
You must choose only one of Due Date, Net Days Allowed, or Net Day of Month. You cannot use any combination of these options.
About Prox Terms
For Net Days Allowed and Net Day of Month, you can use prox terms. Prox terms indicate that payment is due the following month. To use prox terms, enter a P before the date.
Due Date
The due date for invoices with this terms code. This date overrides the calculated due date.
Net Days Allowed
The number of days after the invoice date that a payment is due. For example, if the invoice date is 4/10 and Net Days Allowed is 15, payment is due by 4/25. If Net Days Allowed is P15 (prox terms), payment is due by 5/25.
Net Day of Month
The day of the month on which payment is due. Use prox terms if payment is due the following month. Net Day of Month works with Cutoff Day. For example, if Net Day Of Month is 25 and Cutoff Day is 15, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the month. If Net Day Of Month is P25 and Cutoff Day is 25, invoiced dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the next month.
Cutoff Day
The last day on which invoices dated before the Net Day of Month are due. See Net Day of Month for an example.
Net Day Of Month
If the vendor has 2 cutoff days, enter the second due date. Refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the second due date.
Net Day Of Month
If the vendor has 3 cutoff days, enter the third due date. Refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the third due date.
Discount Date, Disc. Days Allowed, or Disc. Day of Month are only required if you receive a discount for paying an invoice early.
Discount Date
The discount date for invoices with this terms code. This date overrides the calculated discount date.
Disc. Days Allowed
The number of days after the invoice date that a payment is due to receive a discount. For example, if the invoice date is 4/10 and Disc. Days Allowed is 15, payment is due by 4/25 to receive a discount. If Net Days Allowed is P15 (prox terms), payment is due by 5/25 to receive a discount.
Disc. Day of Month
The day of the month on which payment is due to receive a discount. Use prox terms if payment is due the following month. Disc. Day of Month works with Cutoff Day. For example, if Disc. Day Of Month is 25 and Cutoff Day is 15, invoices dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the month to receive a discount. If Disc. Day Of Month is P25 and Cutoff Day is 25, invoiced dated before the 16th are due on the 25th of the next month to receive a discount.
Cutoff Day
The last day on which invoices dated before the Disc. Day of Month are due. See Disc. Day of Month for an example.
Disc. Day of Month
If the vendor has 2 cutoff days, enter the second discount date. Refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the second discount date.
Disc. Day of Month
If the vendor has 3 Cutoff Days, enter the third discount date. Refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms.
Cutoff Day
The cutoff day for the third discount date.
Installments (Y/N)
Determines if you pay in single or multiple payments:
•
N - Create a single payment for invoices with these terms.
•
Y - Create multiple payments for invoices with these terms. Installment terms produce multiple payments based on the schedule; each has a new calculated discount and due date. Opens the A/P Installment Terms screen.
Floor Planning (Y/N)
Determines if material on a purchase order is received as floor plan inventory when received through stock receipts:
•
N - Material is not received as floor plan inventory.
•
Y - Material is received as floor plan inventory.
F2-Save
Saves the A/P terms information.
F4-Del
Deletes the A/P terms information.
F5-Multiple Discount Levels
Opens the Multiple Discount Levels screen.
