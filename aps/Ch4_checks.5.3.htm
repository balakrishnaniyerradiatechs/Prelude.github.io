Automatically Selecting Invoices to Pay
If you want to select all invoices that are ready to pay using the selection criteria entered in the header:
1
From the A/P Payment Selection Header screen, select F5-Auto Sel.
2
Enter the printer number where you want to print the Accounts Payable Payment Selection Register so that you can review the invoices that match your criteria. You can also print the report by selecting F7-Sel Rpt.
If you already manually or automatically selected invoices, a warning message appears to let you know that a selection was already made. Choose from the following:
•
Return to Header - does not automatically select any invoices. Any previous manual and automatic selections remain in the batch. The batch is not updated.
•
Continue - automatically selects invoices based on the selection criteria entered. The previous manual and automatic selections are discarded.
3
The Number of Vendors, Gross Pay Amount, Discount Amount, and Net Amount are updated on the A/P Payment Selection screen.
4
Choose from the following
•
To add or delete invoices from the list, refer to Manually Selecting Invoices After Auto Selecting.
•
To generate payment for the selected invoices by check, electronic funds transfer, automatic bank deduction or by credit card, refer to Paying the Vendor.
Negative Balances
Auto Sel Cred Bal Vend in Accounts Payable Parameters determines if vendors with a negative balance are included when you use F5-Auto Sel (from the A/P Payment Selection Header screen) when printing checks:
•
Y - Include vendors with a negative balance. After you use the Auto Select option, you must use F6-Manual Sel to add invoices to get the total to zero or greater. You cannot print checks if a vendor has a negative check total in the batch.
•
N - Exclude vendors with a negative balance.
