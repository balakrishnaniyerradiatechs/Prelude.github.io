Placing an Invoice on Buyer Hold
If you want the buyer to review and/or recost the P/O, you can put the invoice on buyer hold. After the buyer releases the receiver from the Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry (refer to the Purchasing documentation), you can release the receiver for payment (refer to the Accounts Payable Released Inquiry).
To place the invoice on buyer hold:
1
On the Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry screen, press F8-Buyer Hold. The Buyer Hold Notes screen appears.
2
Edit the Buyer if necessary (defaults to the buyer on the P/O).
3
Press ENTER.
4
To enter notes, press F5-Text Entry. The Buyer Hold Notes screen appears.
The Buyer Hold Notes screen displays notes from Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry, Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry, and from the buyer in the Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry.
5
Enter notes and press F2-Save.
6
On the Buyer Hold screen, choose OK.
The vendor invoice will appear in the Buyer Queue Inquiry.
