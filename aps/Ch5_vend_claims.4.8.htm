Entering a Credit or Debit Memo
If the Merch Amount and Received Total do not match and you want to create a credit or debit memo, refer to Creating an Additional Debit or Credit Memo.
You can create a debit or credit memo in addition to the vendor claim if the Merch Amount and Received Total match. To create a credit or debit memo:
1
On the Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry screen, press F6-Credit Memo. The AP/PO Credit Memo Entry screen appears.
2
In Comments, enter the reason for this credit or debit memo.
3
Enter each G/L account you want to credit or debit:
•
Div #, (Division), which represents the highest possible grouping in your company, if applicable.
•
Reg# (Region), which is a subset of Division if Division is used.
•
Loc# (Location), which is the physical location or warehouse.
•
Dep# (Department), which is a subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a Warehouse.
•
Ccr# (Cost Center), which is a subset of Department if Department is used.
•
Account # to use for the credit or debit memo. The description of the account appears.
4
Enter the Credit Amt. Enter a negative number to create a debit memo.
5
Press F2-Save.
The number of the additional credit or debit memo is the original credit memo number appended with an A. For example, if the original credit memo was 12345, the additional credit or debit memo number is 12345A.
