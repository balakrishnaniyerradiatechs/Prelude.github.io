Open Letter of Credit Report
This report displays all open letters of credit.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > Open Letter of Credit
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Vendor. The vendor who was paid with the letters of credit.
•
Document Number. The bank’s document number for the letter of credit.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
L/C Date
The date the letter of credit was issued.
Exp Date
The date the letter of credit expires.
Document Number
The document number assigned by the bank.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
P/O#
The purchase order paid with the letter of credit.
P/O Amount
The amount of the purchase order.
