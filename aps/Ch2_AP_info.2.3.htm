Using Accounts Payable Terms
Before you can create a vendor account (refer the Purchasing documentation for detailed information on Vendor Maintenance), you must create accounts payable payment terms to define payment rules and discount dates and amounts for each vendor. You can set up as many payment terms codes as necessary to accommodate any variation of due dates, payment dates, and payment methods.
