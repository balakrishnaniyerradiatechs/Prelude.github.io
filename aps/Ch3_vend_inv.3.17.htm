Reconciling High Volume Vendor Invoices
After you enter invoices and receive stock, you must reconcile the invoices and receivers so the invoices can be paid. To reconcile high volume invoices you must use High Volume AP/PO Matching Reconciliation, which searches for receivers that match invoices based on:
•
Vendor number.
•
Purchase order number.
•
Merchandise/invoice amount (within tolerance).
If an invoice and receiver match, the invoice is automatically set to pay. If they do not match, the invoices are not reconciled and they appear on the High Volume AP/PO Matching Not Processed report (refer to Correcting Open High-Volume Vendor Invoices).
To reconcile receivers with invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > High Volume AP/PO Matching > High Volume AP/PO Matching Reconciliation. The following dialog box appears.
This process will select all entries from High Volume AP/PO Entry and attempt to reconcile the invoice information entered with purchase orders on file for the associated vendors. Do You Wish To Continue?
2
Select one of the following:
•
No - Cancels the reconciliation.
•
Yes - The A/P Account Number screen appears.
3
Enter each G/L account you want to credit or debit:
•
Div (Division), which represents the highest possible grouping in your company, if applicable.
•
Reg (Region), which is a subset of Division if Division is used.
•
Loc (Location), which is the physical location or warehouse.
•
Dep (Department), which is a subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a Warehouse.
•
Ccr (Cost Center), which is a subset of Department if Department is used.
•
Account (G/L Account Number) to use for the vendor invoice. The description of the account appears.
4
Press F2-Save. The High Volume Select screen appears.
5
To reconcile invoices for a vendor, enter the Vendor Number. Leave blank to select all vendors.
6
To reconcile invoices for a user, enter the User Id of the person that entered the invoice. Leave blank to select all users.
7
Press ENTER.
8
To view invoices not reconciled, generate the High Volume AP/PO Matching Not Processed report. Refer to Correcting Open High-Volume Vendor Invoices.
