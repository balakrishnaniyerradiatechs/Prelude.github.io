Manually Selecting Invoices to Pay
You can manually enter invoices that you want to pay and edit the discount and gross pay amounts for each invoice. To manually select invoices:
1
From the A/P Payment Selection Header screen, select F6-Manual Sel to open the A/P Payment Manual Selection screen.
2
To add a vendor, enter the vendor number or description or press F3 to search. The A/P Payment Manual Selection Audit screen appears.
3
To add an invoice, enter the invoice number or press F3 to search.
4
To remove an invoice, highlight the Invoice Number you want to remove and type \ + ENTER.
5
Edit Dsc Amt and Gross Pay as necessary.
6
Repeat steps 2-5 as necessary.
7
When the list of invoices is correct, press F2-Save.
The Number of Vendors,. Gross Pay Amount, Discount Amount, and Net Amount are updated on the A/P Payment Selection screen.
8
To generate payment for the selected invoices by check, electronic funds transfer, automatic bank deduction or by credit card, refer to Paying the Vendor.
