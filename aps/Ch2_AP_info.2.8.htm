Tracking Floor Plan Inventory
Floor plan items are paid for when the item is sold or on a designated date (e.g., 90 days from invoice date). Floor plan items are typically large, expensive items (e.g., farm tractors). To track the cost of floor plan items, you can assign a code to each product received. If you do not assign the code when the product is received, use A/P Floor Planning Maintenance to set up the code. You can use your own number or automatically assign the next sequential number available. For more information on floor planning, refer to the Inventory documentation.
To track a floor plan item:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance> A/P Floor Plan Maintenance to access the A/P Floor Planning Maintenance screen.
2
To enter a Sequence Number, choose from the following
•
Enter your own sequence number.
•
Press ENTER for the next system generated sequential number,
•
Press F3 to search for the sequence number. You can search by Product Number, Serial Number and/or Vendor Number.
3
Enter the Product number or keyword.
4
Enter Warehouse number or keyword.
5
Enter the Status of the floor planned item. Status determines if the item was partially shipped or shipped complete. It also identifies if the invoice was partially or fully paid. Choose one of the following:
•
O - Open. Floor plan items are not shipped or paid.
•
S - Shipped. Items were partially shipped or shipped in full.
•
P - Paid. Floor plan items are paid in full.
6
Enter or search for the Serial Number.
7
Enter the Quantity of the A/P floor plan item.
8
Enter the Cost per unit for the product. The Extension amount displays the extended amount of the item(s) (Quantity X Cost).
9
Enter the Vendor.
10
To enter an Invoice Number, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the vendor invoice number.
•
Press F3 to search for the invoice number.
•
Leave blank if you do not want to assign the item to an invoice.
The Invoice Date and Due Date default from the invoice.
11
Enter the amount that was already paid (if any) in Amount Paid.
12
Press F2-Save.
