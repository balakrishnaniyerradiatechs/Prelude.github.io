A/P Payment Schedule Report
The A/P Payment Schedule report displays all open accounts payable invoices by A/P account or vendor.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Aged Payment Schedule
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information and determine how it sorts:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse number on the invoice.
•
First Payment Date. The first scheduled payment date.
•
Second Payment Date. The second scheduled payment date.
•
Third Payment Date. The third scheduled payment date.
•
Beginning Vendor. The vendor number to begin the report with.
•
Ending Vendor. The vendor number to end the report with.
•
Detail Or Summary Report. Determines if detail or summary information prints on the report:
-
D - Show detailed information including discount, invoice, and due dates as well as the invoice number.
-
S - Show summary information for each vendor.
•
Sort By A/P Acct Or Vendor. Determines how the report is sorted:
-
A - A/P account number.
-
V - Vendor number.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Vendor Description
The name of the vendor.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to deduct the discount.
Inv Date
The invoice date of the open payable.
Due Date
The due date of the open payable.
Invoice
The invoice number of the open payable.
Discount
The discount amount eligible for the invoice if paid by the Dsc Date.
1st Payment Date
The date of the first scheduled payment from the selection criteria.
2nd Payment Date
The date of the second scheduled payment from the selection criteria.
3rd Payment Date
The date of the third scheduled payment from the selection criteria.
HoldTotal
The total invoice amount on hold (Gross Amt - Gross Paid to Date).
Future Total
The balance that is due more than 30 days in the future.
