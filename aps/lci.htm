Letter of Credit Inquiry
The Letter of Credit Inquiry displays information for a letter of credit. For more information refer to Creating Letters of Credit.
Viewing Letters of Credit
To view letters of credit:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Letter of Credit Inquiry to open the Letter Of Credit Inquiry screen.
2
For Vendor, enter the number or keyword or press F3 to search.
3
For Document Number, enter the number or press F3 to search.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The bank’s vendor number.
Document Number
The document number from the bank for the letter of credit.
L/C Type
The letter of credit type:
•
R - Regular.
•
B - From the Bank Credit Line.
L/C Date
The date on which the letter of credit was issued.
Expiration Date
The date on which the letter of credit expires.
L/C Amount
The letter of credit amount.
Received Balance
The total dollar amount of the purchase orders on the Letter of Credit. Updated when a purchase order on the Letter of Credit is received.
Posted Balance
The total posted dollar amounts of the purchase orders on the Letter of Credit. Updated when a receiver is reconciled with the invoice.
P/O#
The purchase order(s) for which the letter of credit was issued.
P/O Date
The date of the purchase order.
P/O Dollars
The dollar amount of the purchase order.
L/C Amount
The dollar amount of the letter of credit.
Posted
P indicates that the invoice was reconciled.
Total Amount Distributed
The total amount to distribute to the G/L account.
OK
Closes the Letter of Credit Inquiry screen.
