Multiple Discount Levels screen
The Multiple Discount Levels screen allows you to setup multiple discount levels for the vendor. For example:
•
Ln# 1, Term% is 5.00, Dsc-Date is 01/15/05.
•
Ln# 2, Term% is 2.00, Dsc-Date is 01/30/05.
Vendor invoices paid by 1/15 qualify for a 5% discount. Invoices paid by 1/30 only qualify for a 2% discount.
You can also setup discount percentages for a number of days. For example, if Term% is 3.00, days is 10, invoices paid within 10 days of the invoice date qualify for a 3% discount.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance > A/P Terms Maintenance > F5-Multiple Discount Levels
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln# (unlabeled)
The line count for accounting distribution.
Term%
The early pay discount calculated with these payment terms.
Dsc-Date
The date by which you must pay the vendor to receive the discount based on the invoice date and payable terms.
Days
The number of days past the invoice date after which the discount is not available
F2-Save
Saves the multiple discount levels information.
