Multiple Discounts screen
The Multiple Discounts screen allows you to enter the discount date and discount terms percentage for the invoice if paid by the discount date. The discount terms percentage is based on the Dsc Date and Term% you define for the invoice. You can setup multiple discounts for an invoice. For example,
•
Dsc-Date is 01/15/05, Term% is 5.00
•
Dsc-Date is 01/30/05, Term% is 2.00.
Vendor invoices paid by 1/15 qualify for a 5% discount. Invoices paid by 1/30 only qualify for a 2% discount.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Manual Invoice Entry > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Multiple Discount
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Dsc Date
The date by which you must pay the vendor to receive the discount.
Term%
The early pay discount percent calculated for these payables terms.
F2-Save
Saves the multiple discount information.
