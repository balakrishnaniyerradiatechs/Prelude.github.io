Recording Manual Checks
If you write a check that is not processed through Prelude, you must record information about the check. You can reconcile the check with an existing invoice, create a new invoice, or record the check without an invoice.
To record a manual check:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Check Processing > Manual Check Entry to access the Manual Check Entry screen.
2
Enter the Bank Code.
3
Enter the number of the manual check in Check Number.
4
Enter the Vendor number, Check Date and Check Amount.
5
In Invoice Number, choose one of the following:
•
Press F5-Distribute to G/L to open the Miscellaneous G/L Entry screen to post the miscellaneous charges. Skip to step 7.
•
Enter an existing invoice number or press F3 to search for an invoice number. Skip to step 7.
•
Enter a new invoice number. The following dialog box appears.
This Invoice Does Not Exist On File!
6
Choose Create Invoice. For more information, see Posting Manual Invoices.
7
Edit the Gross Paid and Disc Taken if necessary.
8
Repeat steps 5-6 for each invoice that was paid with this check.
9
Press F2-Save.
