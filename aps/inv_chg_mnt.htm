Invoice Number Change Maintenance screen
If you enter an invoice number incorrectly or enter an invoice for the wrong vendor, you can correct the invoice and/or vendor in Vendor Invoice Number Change Maintenance.
Refer to Correcting a Vendor Number or Invoice Number for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Processes > Vendor Invoice Number Change Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number. Use M to create a Miscellaneous vendor when payments to the vendor do not justify creating a vendor record.
Invoice Number
The vendor’s invoice number.
Invoice Date
The vendor’s invoice date.
Invoice Amount
The vendor’s invoice amount.
New Vendor
The new vendor number to which you will assign the invoice.
New Invoice Number
The new invoice number.
F2-Save
Saves the updated invoice information.
