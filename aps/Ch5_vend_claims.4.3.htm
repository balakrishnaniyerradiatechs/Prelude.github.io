Adjusting Discrepancies
If a discrepancy exists between the Merch Amt and Received Total, use one of the following options to resolve the discrepancy:
•
Recost the claim. Refer to Recosting a Vendor Claim.
•
Choose a G/L account. Refer to Posting a Discrepancy to a G/L Account.
•
Create an additional invoice. Use if Merch Amt and Received Total don’t match. Refer to Creating an Additional Debit or Credit Memo.
•
Put the claim on buyer hold. Refer to Placing the Vendor Claim on Buyer Hold.
•
Enter a credit or debit memo. Use if Merch Amt and Received Total do match. Refer to Entering a Credit or Debit Memo.
