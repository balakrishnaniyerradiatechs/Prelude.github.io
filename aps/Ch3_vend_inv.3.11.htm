Merging Receivers
If a vendor invoice reflects 2 or more receivers, you must merge the receivers. To merge the receivers:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Invoice Reconciliation Entry > F6- Merge to access the Vendor Invoice Merge Routine screen.
2
Enter a Receiver# or press F3 to search for the receiver that you want to merge with the other receivers.
3
For Receive#, enter each receiver you want to merge
4
Press F2-Save. All receivers are combined and can be paid as one invoice.
5
Continue reconciling the claim as usual. Refer to Reconciling Vendor Invoices.
