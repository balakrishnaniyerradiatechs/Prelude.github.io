Manually Selecting Invoices After Auto Selecting
You can edit the list of invoices that were automatically selected. You can also edit the discount and gross pay amounts for each invoice. To manually select invoices after automatically selecting:
1
After entering header information and selection criteria and automatically selecting invoices, press F6-Manual Sel. The A/P Payment Manual Selection screen appears.
2
To remove all invoices for a vendor, highlight the vendor and type \ + ENTER.
3
Choose one of the following:
•
To add a vendor, enter the vendor number or description or press F3 to search. The A/P Payment Manual Selection Audit screen appears.
•
To edit invoices, highlight the vendor and press F5-Maintain Invoices. The A/P Payment Manual Selection Audit screen appears.
4
To add an invoice, enter the invoice number or press F3 to search.
5
To remove an invoice, highlight the invoice and type \ + ENTER.
6
Edit Dsc Amt and Gross Pay as necessary.
7
Repeat steps 2-6 as necessary.
8
When the invoices are correct, press F2-Save.
