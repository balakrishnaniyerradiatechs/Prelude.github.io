Month End A/P Aging screen
The Monthly Payment History screen allows you to view payment history for a vendor including past due amounts. You can select the period from which payment was made, the number of checks sent to the vendor, and the check number of the first check paid.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Inquiries > Accounts Payable Inquiry > Inquiries > Month End A/P Aging
