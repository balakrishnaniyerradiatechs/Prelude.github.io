Accounts Payable Inquiry
The Accounts Payable Inquiry displays payment and aging information, purchase orders and purchase amounts, and other statistics and information for a vendor.
Viewing Accounts Payable Information
To view the Accounts Payable Inquiry:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Inquiries > Accounts Payable Inquiry to open the Accounts Payable Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Vendor or press F3 to search and press ENTER.
Balance, terms, and aging for the vendor appear.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Start Date
The date the vendor was added to Prelude.
Contact
The contact name from Vendor Maintenance.
Currency (not labelled)
The currency for the vendor from Vendor Maintenance (if you use multiple currencies).
Phone
The phone number of the contact from Vendor Maintenance.
Fax
The fax number for the contact.
Pay To Vendor
The vendor that you should remit to (if different). Set up in Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P.
CC Vendor
If this vendor’s invoices should be paid by credit card, the number and name of the credit card vendor from Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P.
Payable Type
The vendor’s classification for payment. Refer to Creating Payable Types.
Payment Terms
The terms code and description assigned in Vendor Maintenance.
Payable Method
Indicates how the vendor is paid:
•
R - Regular (print checks).
•
B - Automatic bank deduction.
•
E - Electronic funds transfer.
•
C - Credit card.
Status (T/H/I)
Any restrictions on the vendor:
•
T - Trade. The vendor is not restricted.
•
H - Hold. You can issue a P/O, but the vendor cannot be paid.
•
I - Inactive. You can use the vendor only for inquiries and reports.
YTD Purchases
The total amount you purchased from this vendor this year.
YTD Payments
The total amount you paid to this vendor this year.
A/P Balance
The accounts payable balance for the vendor.
Last Pay Amount
The dollar amount last paid to the vendor.
Last Pay Date
The last date you paid the vendor.
Last Check#
The number of the last check issued to the vendor.
Oldest Invoice
The date of the oldest open invoice.
Future
Any open balance that is due more than 30 days in the future.
Current
The open balance that is due in the next 30 days.
1-30
Any open balance that is 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
Any open balance that is 31 to 60 days past due.
Over 60
Any open balance that is more than 60 days past due.
OK
Clears the data on the screen to allow you to enter a different vendor to view.
F5-Invoices
Opens the Vendor Invoice Inquiry.
F6-Open P/O’s
Opens the Purchase Order Inquiry screen to display detailed information for purchase orders.
F7-Stats
Opens the Vendor Statistics screen, which shows the amount purchased from, returned to, and paid to the vendor for the past three years.
F8-Detail Aging
Opens the Detail Payable Aging Inquiry to display balance and aging information for a vendor’s invoices.
F9-Notes
Opens the Vend Notes Mnt screen to allow you to enter notes.
Action Bar
Address
Displays the Vendor Address Information screen, which shows the address from Vendor Maintenance.
Inquiries
Provides access to the following:
•
Month End A/P Aging screen which shows payment history for a vendor including past due amounts.
•
Monthly Payment History screen which shows payment history for a vendor including the check numbers and number of checks.
