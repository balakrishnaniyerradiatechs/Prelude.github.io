Printing 1099 Forms
1
The Form 1099 Printing option allows you to print 1099s for services paid by your company. To print 1099 forms:
2
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Processes > Form 1099 Printing > Form 1099 Printing to access the Form 1099 Printing screen.
3
For Vendor, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the vendor number.
•
Press F3 to search for the vendor.
•
Leave blank to select all vendors.
4
Choose which year’s figures to use in Use (C)urrent /(P)revious Year Figures?:
•
C - Current year.
•
P - Previous year.
5
Select the form type in Enter 1099 Form Type For This Run:
•
D - 1099 Dividend.
•
I - 1099 Interest.
•
M - 1099 Miscellaneous.
6
Enter the Printer Form Queue to which you want the 1099 forms to print.
7
Press ENTER. After the 1099 forms print, the 1099 dollar amounts are deleted.
