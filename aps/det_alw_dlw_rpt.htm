Detail Claims Allowed/Disallowed Report
The Detail Claims Allowed/Disallowed report shows detailed information of all reconciled or replaced claims and how many items were allowed and disallowed.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Vendor Claim Reconciliation > Summary Claims Allowed/Disallowed Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse Number. Warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor Number. The vendor number(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor name.
Product
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The description of the product.
P/O #
The number of the vendor claim on which items were disallowed.
Clm-Date (unlabeled)
The date of the vendor claim.
Customer
The customer who returned the item (if appropriate).
Tag Number
The tag number if the product was in warranty inventory.
Gross
The revised discount amount.
Qty-Alw
The quantity allowed by the vendor.
Alw Amount
The extended cost of the items (Qty-Alw x Cost).
Dlw Qty
The quantity disallowed by the vendor.
Dlw Amount
The extended cost of the items (Dlw Qty x Cost).
