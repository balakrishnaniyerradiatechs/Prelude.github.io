Monthly A/P Void Check Report
The Monthly A/P Void Check report lists all voided payments for a fiscal period. For more information, refer to Voiding Payment.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable> Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > Voided Check Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Period. The fiscal period associated with the voided payments.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check#
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date of the payment.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The invoice number(s) that were paid.
Gross Paid
The gross amount due for the invoice.
Disc Taken
The discount taken from the invoice.
Net Amount
The amount paid (Gross Paid - Disc Taken).
G/L Distribution#
The override G/L account (if any).Description
Debit Amt
The debit amount (if any).
