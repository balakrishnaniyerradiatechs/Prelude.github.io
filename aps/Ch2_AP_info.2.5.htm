Creating A/P Terms
To create A/P terms codes to assign to customers:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance > A/P Terms Maintenance to access the A/P Terms Maintenance screen.
2
For Payment Terms, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the terms code.
•
Press F3 and enter search criteria.
3
Enter the Description of the terms code.
4
If you receive a discount from the vendor, for Terms Percent, enter the discount percentage. For example, if a Terms Percent of 2% is applied to a merchandise total of $100.00, the discount amount is $2.00.
5
To indicate when payment is due, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the Due Date on which invoices are due. This date overrides any calculated due date.
•
Enter the number of days after the invoice date that the payment is due for Net Days Allowed. You can use prox terms to indicate that payment is due the following month.
•
For Net Day of Month, enter the day of the month on which payment is due. Use prox terms to indicate that payment is due the following month. Also enter each of the vendor’s Cutoff Dates.
6
If you receive a discount for paying early, choose one of the following:
•
For Discount Date, enter the date on which invoices with this terms code are eligible for an early pay discount. This overrides any calculated discount date.
•
For Disc. Days Allowed, enter the number of days past the invoice date after which the invoice is not eligible for a discount. You can use prox terms to indicate that payment is due the following month.
•
For Disc. Day of Month, enter the day of the month after which discounts are no longer available for the invoice. Use prox terms to indicate that payment is due the following month. Also enter each of the vendor’s Cutoff Dates.
7
Select Y for Installments if this code is for installment invoices. Installment terms produce multiple invoices based on the payment schedule defined. Prelude creates a single invoice for each payment with a new calculated discount and due date for each invoice.
8
Select Y for Floor Planning if you will pay for inventory received with this A/P terms code when it is sold or by the due date, whichever is sooner.
9
Press F2-Save.
