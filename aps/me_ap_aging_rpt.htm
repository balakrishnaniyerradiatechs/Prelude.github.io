Month End A/P Aging Report
The Month End A/P Aging report lists open accounts payable balances based on the value at the end of a fiscal period and date.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > A/P Monthly Reports > Month End A/P Aging Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Posted Period. The fiscal period associated with the A/P balances.
•
Month End Cutoff Date. The last date to include. You can select the last invoice date to include for the fiscal period. Invoices dated after the cutoff date appear.
•
Sort By?. Determines how the report will be sorted:
-
D - By vendor description.
-
N - By vendor number.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor. If the vendor is on hold “Vendor on Hold” appears under the vendor description.
Invoice Number
The number of the invoice.
Inv Date
The date of the invoice.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Future
The A/P balance not yet due.
Current
The current amount due.
1-30
The balance that is 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
The balance that is 31 to 60 days past due.
61&Over
The balance that is more than 60 days past due.
