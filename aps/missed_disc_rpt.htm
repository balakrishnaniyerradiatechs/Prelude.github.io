Lost A/P Discounts Report
The Lost A/P Discounts report displays discounts that were lost because the payment was issued after the discount date.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > A/P Monthly Reports > Lost A/P Discounts Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Invoice Date range. The beginning and ending invoice dates to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The vendor invoice number.
Dsc Date
The date you should have paid the invoice to qualify for the discount.
Chk-Date
The date on which the payment was issued.
Check #
The payment number issued for the vendor invoice.
Disc. Lost
The discount amount lost due to paying after the discount date.
