Month End Multi Currency A/P Aging Report
The Month End Multi Currency A/P Aging report lists month-end multiple currency A/P transactions.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > A/P Monthly Reports > Month End Multi Currency A/P Aging Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Posted Period. The fiscal period associated with the multi currency transactions.
•
Effective Date. The currency exchange rate in effect on this date.
•
Currency Type. The currency type(s) to include.
•
Vendor Currency Type. The vendor currency type(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description/Inv Num
Line 1: The name of the vendor. If the vendor is placed on hold, “Vendor on Hold” displays under the vendor description.
Line 2: The invoice number.
Inv Date
The date of the invoice.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Future
The A/P balance not yet due.
Current
The current amount due.
1-30
The balance that is 1 to 30 days past due.
31-60
The balance that is 31 to 60 days past due.
61 & Over
The balance that is more than 60 days past due.
Inv Balance
The total balance of the invoice.
Report Curr Inv Amt
The invoice amount due.
