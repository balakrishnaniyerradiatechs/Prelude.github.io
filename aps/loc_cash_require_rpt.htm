L/C Acceptance Cash Requirements Report
This report lists any pending letter of credit withdrawals by your bank. These receivers were reconciled and will be paid by automatic bank deduction.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > L/C Acceptance Cash Requirements Rpt

Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Rcvr Num
The number of the receiver.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Bal Due
The balance due.
Ship/ETA
Line 1: The tracking number entered at stock receipts.
Line 2: The expected arrival date.
Doc/Exp Date
Line 1: The document number from the letter of credit.
Line 2: The expiration date from the letter of credit.
Terms
The terms code for the letter of credit.
30/60
The balance that is due in 30 to 60 days.
90/120
The balance that is due in 90 to 120 days.
150/180
The balance that is due in 150 to 180 days.
Future
The balance that is due after 180 days.
