Open Vendor Claim-by Warehouse Report
The Open Vendor Claim by Warehouse report lists unreconciled lines on vendor claims. For more information, refer to Reconciling a Vendor Claim with a Credit Memo.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Claim Reconciliation > Open Vendor Claim Report By Warehouse
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse. Warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor Claim Information
Claim #
The open vendor claim number.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Invoice Number
The claim number.
Inv Date
The date of the claim.
Due Date
The due date of the claim.
Period
The fiscal period associated with the claim.
Ship Via/Description
The ship via code and description of the ship via.
Status
The status of the vendor claim:
•
H - Claim is on hold.
•
T - Claim was released from hold.
Freight
The freight amount of the vendor claim.
Product Information
Ln#
The line number of the product.
Product Number
The product number of the item.
Description
The product description of the item.
Vendor Product#
The product number assigned by the vendor.
Whse
The warehouse number associated with this vendor claim.
Qty
The quantity of items on the vendor claim.
U/M
The unit of measure for each item.
Ext Gross
The extended gross amount of the item.
Ext Revised
The extended revised amount of the item.
