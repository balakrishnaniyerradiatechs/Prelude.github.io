Monthly Vendor Invoice Report
The Monthly Vendor Invoice report displays posting information for vendor invoices entered for a fiscal period.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > Vendor Invoice Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Period. The fiscal period of the vendor invoices.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Vendor Type. The vendor type(s) to include. Refer to Creating Payable Types.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The invoice number for this transaction.
Inv Date
The invoice date of this transaction.
Due Date
The date the vendor invoice payment is due.
S
The status of the vendor invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
H - Hold.
•
P - Paid.
Disc. Amt
The discount amount taken from the vendor invoice.
Inv. Amount
The amount of the vendor invoice.
Dist. Prefix
The distribution prefix associated with the vendor invoice.
Account#
THe G/L account number posted for this invoice.
Dist. Amount
The dollar amount distributed to the G/L account.
