A/P Installment Terms screen
The A/P Installment Terms screen displays the number, frequency, and percent of each payment.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Maintenance > A/P Payment Terms Maintenance > Installments = Y
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Number of Payments
The number of installment payments.
Frequency (M/Q/A)
Indicates when installment invoices are created:
•
M - Monthly.
•
Q - Quarterly.
•
A - Annually.
Initial Payment%
The percentage of the total sale due for the first payment.
Final Payment%
The percentage of the total sale due for the last payment.
Periodic Payment%
The amount due for the balance of the payments. For example, if Number Of Payments is 12, Initial Payment% is 10, and Final Payment % is 10, Periodic Payment % is calculated to 8.00. The amount due each month for the remaining 10 payments is 8% of the total sale.
F2-Save
Saves the installment terms information.
