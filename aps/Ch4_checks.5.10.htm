Paying by Credit Card
If the Batch Type is C, only invoices that should be paid by credit card are selected. When you press F8, the following occurs:
•
The invoices are marked as paid with sequentially assigned check numbers with a C prefix (e.g., C00075).
•
A new invoice is created for the credit card vendor (designated by CC Payment Vendor in Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P).
•
The invoice number displays as C/C.PAID|<check#>. You can then pay the credit card vendor (e.g., VISA, MasterCard).
Setting up a Vendor for Credit Card Payment
To set up a vendor to be paid by credit card set Payment Method to C in Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P. You must then enter the Payment Vendor who will receive the payment.
