Daily Letter of Credit Register
The Daily Letter Of Credit Register lists all letter of credit transactions entered for a date range.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Letter Of Credit Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Posting Date range. The beginning and ending dates for which you want to view letters of credit.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Document Number
The document number assigned by the bank.
L/C Date
The date the letter of credit was issued.
L/C Amount
The amount of the letter of credit.
P/O#
The purchase order paid with the letter of credit.
L/C Amount
The amount applied to the letter of credit.
