Multi Discounts screen
The Multi Discounts screen allows you to enter the discount date and discount terms percentage for the invoice if paid by the discount date. The discount terms percentage is based on the Dsc Date and Term% you define for the invoice. You can setup multiple discounts for an invoice. For example:
•
Dsc-Date is 01/15/05, Term% is 5.00.
•
Dsc-Date is 01/30/05, Term% is 2.00.
Vendor invoices paid by 1/15 qualify for a 5% discount. Invoices paid by 1/30 only qualify for a 2% discount.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Invoice Reconciliation Entry > Action Bar > Multi Disc
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Dsc Date
The date by which you must pay the vendor to receive the discount. The system calculates this date using the invoice date and accounts payable terms.
Term%
The discount percentage to apply if you pay the vendor by the discount date.
F2-Save
Saves the multiple discount information.
