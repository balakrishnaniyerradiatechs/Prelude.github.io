Paying the Vendor
You can pay the vendor by:
•
Electronic funds transfer (EFT).
•
Automatic bank deduction (ABD).
•
Credit card.
•
Check.
 
The Single Chk Per Inv flag in Vendor Maintenance determines how the invoices are paid:
•
Y - Generates one payment per invoice.
•
N - Generates one payment for all invoices.
