A/P Invoice Batch Information screen
The A/P Batch Information screen allows you to enter the batch number, date, and amounts for Manual Invoice Entry and Recurring Vendor Invoices. If A/P Batch Control is set to Y in Accounts Payable Parameters the batch information screen displays.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Manual Invoice Entry
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Recurring Vendor Invoices > Recurring Vendor Invoice Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Batch Number
The batch number.
Batch Date
The entry date for this batch.
Batch Amount
The total amount of all the invoices included in the batch.
# Of Invoices
The number of invoices in the batch.
Applied Total
The total dollar amount applied to the batch.
F2-Save
Saves the invoice batch information.
F4-Reset to Zero
Resets the Batch Amount and Applied Total to zero.
