Deleted Receiver/Claims Report
The Deleted Receiver Claims report shows all deleted receivers and vendor claims.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Deleted Receiver / Claim Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Fiscal Month. The fiscal period of the deleted receivers.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor/Name
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The name of the vendor.
P.O. #
The purchase order or vendor claim number.
Rec-Date
The receipt date of the deleted receivers.
Product Number
The products on the receiver.
Description
The description of the product.
Whse
The warehouse where the item was received.
Qty-Rec
The quantity of the product receiver.
U/M
The unit of measure for each item.
Gr-Price
The gross price of the item.
Extension
The extended price of the item (Gr-Price X Qty-Rec).
