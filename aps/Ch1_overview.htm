Overview
 
The Accounts Payable module in Activant Prelude provides a wide range of features and options to help you control accounts payable information and cash disbursement. Accounts Payable allows you to:
•
Reconcile open receivers with vendor invoices and resolve cost discrepancies.
•
Automatically distribute funds to G/L accounts for non-merchandise or recurring invoices without a receiving document (e.g., phone or electric bill).
•
View A/P data in detailed and summarized reports and inquiries that are designed to help maximize financial control.
This documentation provides information on the following A/P procedures:
•
Setting Up Accounts Payable Information.
•
Processing Vendor Invoices.
•
Paying Vendors.
•
Reconciling Vendor Claims.
•
Using Letters of Credit.
•
Generating 1099 Forms.
•
Accounts Payable Reports.
