Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry screen
Field and Function Key Descriptions
.
Field/Function Key
Description
Receiver#
The receiver number to reconcile, which is the P/O number followed by a dash (e.g., 10000621-1).
Vendor
The vendor number pulled from the original purchase order.
Invoice Number
The vendor’s invoice number. You can only use an invoice number once per vendor.
Invoice Date
The vendor’s invoice date.
Fiscal Period (not labeled)
The fiscal period associated with the invoice date entered.
Status
The status of the vendor’s invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
H - On Hold.
•
B - Automatic Bank Deduction. Select B if a bank credit line letter of credit is attached to the receiver. Defaults based on Vendor Maintenance.
•
E - Electronic Funds Transfer.
•
C - Credit Cards.
Payment Terms
The A/P terms code.
Discount Date
The discount date associated with the terms code.
Due Date
The due date of the invoice calculated from the A/P terms code.
Merch Amount
The merchandise amount of the invoice, which should match the Received Total. If the totals don’t match, you can recost the invoice, place the invoice on buyer hold, split, or merge the invoice, or create an additional invoice.
Freight Amount
The freight amount of the invoice (if any).
Include?
Determines if the Freight Amount is included in the payment to the vendor:
•
Checked - Includes the freight amount.
•
Not Checked - does not include the freight amount.
Duty Amount
The duty amount for the invoice (if any).
Include?
Determines if the Duty Amount is included in the payment to the vendor:
•
Checked - Includes the duty amount.
•
Not Checked - Does not include the duty amount.
Brokerage Amount
The brokerage amount for the invoice (if any).
Include?
Determines if the Brokerage Amt is included in the payment to the vendor:
•
Checked - Includes the brokerage amount.
•
Not Checked - Does not include the brokerage amount.
Tax Amountt
The tax amount for this invoice (if any). The tax amount is based on the landed cost of the merchandise.
Include?
Determines if the Tax Amt is included in the payment to the vendor.
•
Checked - Includes the tax amount.
•
Not Checked - Does not include the tax amount.
Misc Amount
Any miscellaneous charges the vendor may debit or credit that are not already included in this entry (e.g., charges other than merchandise and freight). You can choose the G/L account to post to.
Discount Amount
The discount amount associated with the terms code.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the invoice.
Received Total
The total amount of the receiver.
Revised Total
The revised total if you change the cost of the receiver (e.g., adjust cost).
Warranty Total
The warranty total if any.
Receipt Date
The date the product was received.
Ship Via
The fiscal period associated with the receipt date.
Emergency P/O
The ship via codes and description from the purchase order.
Freight Flag
Indicates if this purchase order was generated as an Emergency purchase order.
•
Y - Yes.
•
N - No.
Buyer
The name and number of the buyer. The number of the buyer who should review the vendor invoice.
Before you enter a Receiver#
F5-Split
Opens the Vendor Claims Split screen to exclude items on a receiver.
F6-Merge
Opens the Vendor Invoice Merge Routine screen, to combine multipe receivers.
After you enter a Receiver#
F2-Save
Reconciles the invoice and receiver and sets
F4-Del
Deletes the invoice.
F5-Hdr Scr
Opens the Purchase Order Header screen. This allows you to view the original purchase order header for the vendor invoice.
F6-Credit Memo
Opens the AP/PO Credit Memo Entry screen. This allows you to create a credit memo while in vendor invoice reconciliation entry.
F7-Recost
Opens AP/PO Cost Adjustment Reasons screen.
F8-Buyer Hold
Opens the Buyer Hold Notes screen to place the receiver on hold. The buyer can resolves a discrepancy between the merchandise and received totals. The buyer on the original P/O defaults. You can send notes to the buyer.
Action Bar
User Fields
See your System Administrator.
Notes
Opens the notes screen, which allows you to enter, edit, or view notes.
Multi Dsc
Opens the Multi Discounts screen to enter multiple discounts for the receiver.
Remit Comm
Opens the Remittance Check Comment screen to enter comments for payment.
Import Cost
Displays the APPO Import Costs screen to view importing cost information.
