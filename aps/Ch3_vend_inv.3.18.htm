Correcting Open High-Volume Vendor Invoices
High-volume vendor invoices are not reconciled if the vendor number or P/O number do not match or if the difference between merchandise and invoice amounts is greater than the Invoice Margin set in Accounts Payable Parameters. If a vendor invoice cannot be reconciled, it appears on the High Volume AP/PO Matching Not Processed Report.
To correct a receiver:
•
Correct the Merch Amt in High Volume AP/PO Entry and run High Volume AP/PO Reconciliation again.
•
Recost the receiver in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry, See Reconciling a Vendor Invoice to a Receiver.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > High Volume Invoice AP/PO > High Volume APPO Matching Not Processed Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Vendor Number. The vendor with unreconciled invoices.
•
Invoice Number. The number of the unreconciled invoice.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Entry#
The entry number assigned to this transaction.
Vend#
The vendor number which was paid on this invoice.
Description
The vendor’s description.
Invoice
The invoice number assigned to transaction.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to qualify for the discount.
P/O#
The purchase order paid for this invoice.
Merch Amount
The merchandise total for the invoice.
Misc Charges
The total amount of any other charges such as Freight, Duty, Brokerage, and Misc charges.
Total Amount
The total amount of the invoice (Merch Amount + Misc charges).
