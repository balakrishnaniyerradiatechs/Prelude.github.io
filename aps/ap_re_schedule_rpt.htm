A/P Replacement Equipment Schedule Report
The A/P Replacement Equipment Schedule report lists information for all replacement equipment transactions by the date entered.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Replacement Equipment Aged Schedule
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to limit the information:
•
First Payment Date. The first scheduled payment date.
•
Second Payment Date. The second scheduled payment date.
•
Third Payment Date. The third scheduled payment date.
•
Beginning Vendor. The vendor number to begin the report with.
•
Ending Vendor. The vendor number to end the report with.
•
Include RE Invoices?. Determines which replacement invoices print:
-
Y - Only replacement equipment invoices.
-
N - Only regular invoices.
-
B - Replacement and regular invoices.
•
Detail Or Summary Report. Determines if detail or summary information prints:
-
D - Show detailed information including discount, invoice, and due dates as well as the invoice number.
-
S - Show summary information for each vendor.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Vendor Description
The name of the vendor.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to deduct the discount.
Inv Date
The invoice date of the open payable.
Due Date
The due date of the open payable.
Invoice
The invoice number of the open payable.
RE Discount
The discount amount applied if paid by the Dsc Date.
1st Payment Date
The date of the first scheduled payment.
2nd Payment Date
The date of the second scheduled payment.
3rd Payment Date
The date of the third scheduled payment.
Hold Total
The dollar amount placed on hold (if any).
Future Total
The balance that is due more than 30 days in the future
