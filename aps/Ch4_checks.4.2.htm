Creating a Batch of Invoices to Pay
To process and pay open A/P invoices by batch, you must:
•
Start the check batch (refer to Starting a Batch).
•
Select invoices (refer to Selecting Invoices to Pay).
•
Pay the vendor (refer to Paying the Vendor).
To select invoices and pay vendors, select Financial > Accounts Payable > Check Processing > Payment Selection/Print to access the A/P Payment Selection Header screen.
