A/P Floor Plan Inquiry
The Accounts Payable Floor Plan Inquiry shows the product number, warehouse number, costs, shipment information, serial numbers, and payment history for floor plan items.
Viewing the Accounts Payable Floor Plan Inquiry
To view floor plan products:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Accounts Payable Floor Plan Inquiry to open the A/P Floor Plan Inquiry screen.
2
For Sequence Number, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the sequence number.
•
Press F3 and enter search criteria for Product, Serial Number, or Vendor.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field / Function
Description
Sequence Number
The sequence number assigned to the floor plan item.
Product
The product number and description.
Warehouse
The warehouse number and description in which the floor plan product was received.
Serial Number
The serial number of the product (if any).
Status
The status of the floor plan product:
•
O - Not shipped or not paid.
•
S - Shipped (fully or partially shipped).
•
P - Paid.
Quantity
The quantity of floor plan items.
Cost
The cost of the floor plan item.
Extension
The extended cost of the item (Quantity x Cost).
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Invoice Number
The invoice number associated with the floor plan product.
Invoice Date
The invoice date of the floor plan product.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Amount Paid
The total amount paid.
F5-Shipped Info
Opens the Floor Plan Shipped Information screen to display the invoice number, shipment date, and shipped quantity.
F6-Check History
Opens the Check History screen, which displays the check number, check date, and check amount for this product.
