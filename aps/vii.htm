Vendor Invoice Inquiry
The Vendor Invoice Inquiry lists detailed information for both open and/or paid invoices for a vendor.
Viewing Vendor Invoices
To view the vendor invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Vendor Invoice Inquiry to open the Vendor Invoice Inquiry screen.
2
For Vendor, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the Vendor or press F3 to search.
•
Press F6-Enter Inv#. Enter or search for a vendor and invoice number. The Vendor Invoice History Inquiry screen appears. For more information, see Vendor Invoice History Inquiry.
•
Press F7-Enter P/O#. Enter a P/O number to view related invoices.
3
For Sort, indicate the order in which you want to view the invoices:
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Due Date.
•
By Invoice Date.
•
By Invoice Number.
•
By Descending Due Date.
4
For Filters, choose which invoices you want to view:
•
Open Invoices.
•
Paid Invoices.
•
Both Open & Paid.
•
Yes - filter invoices based on Actual Status, Period, or Invoice Date.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description

The order in which you want to list the invoices:
•
By Descending Invoice Date.
•
By Due Date.
•
By Invoice Date.

By Descending Due Date.
Filters
The status of invoices you want to see:
•
Both Open & Paid.
•
Open Invoices.
•
Paid Invoices.
•
Yes - Allows you to use a filter to select Period, Actual Status, and Invoice Date range.
Open Balance
The current open balance.
Phone Number (Not Labeled)
The phone number of the vendor from Vendor Maintenance.
Invoice Number
The vendor invoice number.
N
An asterisk (*) displays if notes are on the invoice.
Inv Date
The date the purchase order was invoiced.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Invoice Amt
The total amount of the invoice.
Balance Due
The balance due for the invoice.
Last Check#
The last check number(s) written for payment of invoices.
S
The status of the vendor invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
P - Paid.
•
E - Paid by electronic funds transfer (refer to Transferring Funds Electronically (EFT)).
•
B - Paid by Automatic Bank Deduction (refer to Paying by Automatic Bank Deduction (ABD)).
•
D - Deleted.
F2-Finish
Closes the Vendor Invoice Inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Opens the Vendor Invoice History Inquiry to display detailed information about a single invoice.
F6-Enter Inv#
Allows you to enter the vendor number and invoice number to display. Use before selecting a vendor.
F7-Enter P/O#
Allows you to enter the purchase order number to determine which invoice to display. Use before selecting a vendor.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are line number, page number, B (bottom), or T (top).
sF5-Prev Group
Displays the next block of vendor invoices. The number of invoices in a block is determined in Company Maintenance.
sF6-Next Group
Displays the previous block of vendor invoices. The number of invoices in a block is determined in Company Maintenance.
Action Bar
Vend Ord#
Opens the Vendor/Order Prompt screen to select the vendor and vendor sales order number to determine which invoices will display.
Check Hist
View the history information for the checks against the vendor invoice(s).
AP Info
Displays the A/P Information screen, which allows you to view payment information for this vendor.
Vend Notes
Displays the Vendor Notes screen to allow you to view notes that exist for this vendor. Notes are from Vendor Maintenance.
