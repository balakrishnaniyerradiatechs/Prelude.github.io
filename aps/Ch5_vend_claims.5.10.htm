Placing the Vendor Claim on Buyer Hold
If you want the buyer to review and/or recost the claim, put the claim on buyer hold. To put the claim on buyer hold:
1
On the Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry screen, press F8-Buyer Hold. The Buyer Hold Notes screen appears.
2
Edit the Buyer if necessary (defaults to the buyer on the vendor claim).
3
Press ENTER. The Buyer Hold screen appears.
4
To enter notes, press F5-Text Entry. The Buyer Hold Notes screen appears.
5
Enter notes and press F2-Save.
6
On the Buyer Hold screen, choose OK.
The buyer reviews the claim in Accounts Payable Discrepancy Inquiry (refer to the Purchasing documentation) and releases it back to A/P. You can then release it (refer to Releasing Receivers from Buyer Hold).
