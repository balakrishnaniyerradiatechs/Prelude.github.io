Vendor Claims Allowed/Disallowed Summary Report
The Vendor Claims Allowed/Disallowed Summary lists summary information of all reconciled or replaced claims and how many items were allowed or disallowed.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Vendor Claim Reconciliation > Summary Claims Allowed/Disallowed Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse. Warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vend# / Vendor Name
Line 1: The vendor number.
Line 2: The vendor name.
Product / Description
Line 1: The product number.
Line 2: The description of the product.
Qty-Alw
The quantity allowed by the vendor.
Ext-Alw
The extended cost of the items (Qty-Alw x Cost).
Qty-Dlw
The quantity disallowed by the vendor.
Ext-Dlw
The extended cost of the items (Qty-Dlw x Cost).
