Monthly A/P Distribution Register
The Monthly A/P Distribution Register displays all vendor invoices by G/L account number.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > A/P Monthly Reports > A/P Distribution Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
G/L Warehouse. The selling warehouse(s) to include on this report.
•
Period. The fiscal period of the vendor invoices.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Dist. Prefix
The distribution prefix number that the vendor invoice posted to.
G/L #
The G/L number that the vendor invoice posted to.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The vendor’s invoice number for this transaction.
Inv Date
The date of the vendor invoice.
Due Date
The date the invoice payment is due.
P/O Number
The purchase order number of this invoice.
Dist Amt
The dollar amount distributed to the G/L account.
