Creating an Additional Debit or Credit Memo
If the Merch Total is different than the Received Total, you can create an additional credit or debit memo to make up the difference.
To create an additional credit or debit memo:
1
In Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry, choose Add Inv. from the Discrepancy Warning dialog box. The Add. Invoice Information screen appears. The entire discrepancy appears in Discrepancy Amt.
2
Change the Discount Amount, if necessary.
3
Enter Comments about the claim.
4
Press F2-Save.
The number of the additional claim is the vendorâ€™s original invoice number appended with an A. For example, if the original invoice was 12345, the additional invoice is 12345A.
