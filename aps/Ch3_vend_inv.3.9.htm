Entering a Credit or Debit Memo
You can create a debit or credit memo if the Merch Amount and Received Total match. To create a credit or debit memo:
1
On the Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry screen, press F6-Credit Memo. The AP/PO Credit Memo Entry screen appears.
2
In Comments, enter the reason you are creating this credit or debit memo.
3
Enter each G/L account you want to credit or debit:
•
Div (Division), which represents the highest possible grouping in your company, if applicable.
•
Reg (Region), which is a subset of Division if Division is used.
•
Loc (Location), which is the physical location or warehouse.
•
Dep (Department), which is a subset of Warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a Warehouse.
•
Ccr (Cost Center), which is a subset of Department if Department is used.
•
Account (G/L Account Number) to use for the vendor invoice. The description of the account appears.
4
For Credit Amount, enter the amount to apply to this vendor invoice. You can enter a negative number to create a debit memo. Refer to Printing Debit Memos for more information.
5
Press F2-Save.
