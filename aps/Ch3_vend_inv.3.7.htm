Creating an Additional Invoice
If the Merch Total is different than the Received Total, you can create an additional invoice to bill back the difference.
To create an additional invoice:
1
From the Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry screen, choose Addl Inv. from the Warning message. The Add. Invoice Information screen appears. The entire discrepancy appears in Discrepancy Amt.
2
Change the amount, if necessary. The Discount Amount displays. The amount that defaults is based on the Discrepancy Amt and Terms Code. For example, if Terms Code is 2% 10th (discount applicable if invoice is paid by the tenth of the month) and Discrepancy Amt is $500.00 (difference between Merch Amount and Received Total), $10.00 is the default for Discount Amount ($500 X 2%).
3
Enter Comments about the invoice.
4
Press F2-Save.
