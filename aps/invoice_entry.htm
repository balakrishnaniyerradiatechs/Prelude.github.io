Accounts Payable Manual Invoice Entry screen
The Accounts Payable Manual Entry screen allows you to create invoices for non-inventory items (e.g., phone bill, electric bill).
Refer to Manually Entering Expense Invoices for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Manual Invoice Entry
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number. Use M for a Miscellaneous vendor number to pay vendors that do not have or need a vendor. The name and address are printed on the checks.
Invoice Number
The invoice number to pay for the vendor.
Invoice Date
The invoice date.
Fiscal Period (not labeled)
The fiscal period of the invoice date.
Payment Terms
The accounts payable terms code from Vendor Maintenance. This determines the payment terms for this invoice. The codes are set up in A/P Terms Maintenance.
Discount Date
The payment date if a discount is taken. The system calculates a date using the Invoice Date and Payment Terms.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
P/O Number
The purchase order number.
Status
The status of the invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
H - On Hold.
•
B - Automatic Bank Deduction.
•
E - Electronic Funds Transfer.
•
C - Credit Cards.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the invoice, including Merch Amount and the Freight, Duty, Brokerage, and Miscellaneous charges, but not the Discount Amount.
Merch Amount
The total value of the merchandise.
Tax Amt
The tax amount for this invoice (if any) for reporting purposes only. This is typically a type of Value Added Tax.
Disc. Amount
The discount amount for this invoice (if any) using the merchandise amount and payment terms.
Ln#
The line count for accounting distribution.
Div
Division number. If your company uses divisions, this represents the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
Region number. A Subset of Division if Division is used (e.g. Southwestern Region, Northeastern Region).
Loc
Location number. The warehouse location that represents where the general ledger posting will post.
Dep
Department number. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
Cost Center number. A subset of Department if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department. (e.g. shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account#
The general ledger account number to post the payment to.
Description
The account number description.
Dist Amt
The dollar amount distributed to the account for this invoice.
Comment
Comments for the invoice. Comments display in Vendor Invoice History Inquiry:
•
Y - Enter comments in the Distribution Comments screen.
•
N - Do not enter comments.
Posted
Indicates that each distribution line posted to the general ledger. The P appears when the invoice is saved.
F2-Save
Saves the manual invoice entry information.
F4-Del
Deletes the manual invoice entry information.
F7-Remit Comment
Opens the Remittance Check Comment screen to allow you to enter comments for the invoice.
F8-A/P Account
The G/L Prefix information for the manual invoice being created.
F9-Batch Info
Opens A/P Vendor Invoice Batch Information screen to allow you to view the batch information for this invoice.
Action Bar
User Fields
See your Systems Administrator.
Paid Vendor
Opens the Paid For Vendor Information screen to allow you to select a different vendor to pay for this invoice.
Notes
Opens the Notes Display screen to allow you to enter notes for the invoice.
Consigned
Opens the Consigned Inventory screen to display consigned items for a consignment invoice. If you create a new manual invoice, this screen is blank. Press F7-Toggle to limit the lines based on a date range.
Add’l Options
Opens the Additional Options screen to select additional options for this invoice:
•
Multiple Discounts - Opens the Multiple Discounts screen to allow you to enter Discount Date and Term % information for the invoice.
•
Additional Flags - Opens the Additional Flags screen. You can set Replacement Equipment Invoice and Advance Payment Collection Date for the invoice.
•
Carrier Freight - Opens the Carrier Freight Charge Entry screen to allow you to enter a Carrier Freight Invoice for a receiver
•
1099 Category - Opens the 1099 Category Entry screen to allow you to enter the 1099 category for the invoice.
