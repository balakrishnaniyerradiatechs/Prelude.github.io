A/P-P/O Vendor Claim Cost Adjustments screen
The A/P-P/O Vendor Claim Cost Adjustments screen allows you to recost items and change quantities on the vendor claim.
Refer to Recosting a Vendor Claim for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Claim Reconciliation > Claim Reconciliation Entry > F7-Recost
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln# (not labeled)
The line number on the vendor claim.
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The description of the product.
Whse
The selling warehouse number associated with the product.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure (e.g., EA, CS).
Qty Clm
The quantity of the product on the claim.
Qty Alw
The quantity allowed on the claim.
Cost Clm
The product cost on the claim.
Disc% Clm
The discount amount (if any) applied to the claim.
Cost Alw
The product cost allowed.
Disc% Alw
The discount amount (if any) allowed for the vendor claim.
Rev Total
The revised total for the product.
OK?
Updates the line item.
R/S
Indicates if the highlighted line item is a return or scrapped item:
•
R - return.
•
S - scrapped.
Vendor Product Number
The product number assigned by the vendor.
Total Revised Claim
The revised total of the vendor claim.
F2-Save
Saves the cost and quantity adjustments.
F5-Notes
Opens the Notes Display screen to allow you to enter, edit or view notes.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are line number, page number, reference number, B (bottom), or T (top).
Action Bar
Examine
Displays delivery and receipt information for the highlighted item.
Sort
Determines the order of the items on the invoice:
•
D - By product description.
•
P - By product number.
•
V - By vendor product number.
Misc Entry
Not applicable to vendor claims.
