Open Vendor Claim Report
The Open Vendor Claim report lists all items entered on a vendor claim that are not reconciled or replaced. For more information, refer to Reconciling a Vendor Claim with a Credit Memo.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Claim Reconciliation > Open Vendor Claim Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse. Warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Receipt Date range. The beginning and ending receipt date range to include.
•
Due Date. The date the payment is due. The due date is calculated using invoice date and accounts payable terms.
•
P/O Type. The purchase order type(s) to include.
•
Status. Determines which claims are displayed on the report.
-
A - all claims.
-
H - claims that are on hold.
-
R - claims that are not on hold or suspended.
-
S - claims that are suspended.
-
T - claims were released from hold.
•
Receiver Amount. The dollar amount of the receiver.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor Claim Information
Claim#
The vendor claim number.
Clm-Date
The date of the vendor claim.
Inv-Date
The date of the open claim.
Due Date
The date the claim is due.
P/O Type
Desc
The description of the vendor claim type:
•
01- Regular.
•
01 - Vendor Show.
•
03 - Vendor Consigned Inventory.
•
04 - Non Inventory Expense.
Vendor#
The vendor number.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Brokerage
Not applicable to vendor claims.
Fiscal
The fiscal period associated with the claim.
Freight
The freight amount entered on the vendor claim.
Duty
Not applicable to vendor claims.
Ship Via
The ship via code and description associated with the invoice.
Status
The status of the open vendor claims.
•
H - Claim is on hold.
•
S - Claim was suspended.
•
T - Claim was released from hold.
Ln#
The line number of the item.
Product Number
The product number of the item.
Description
The description of the product.
Vendor Part#
The part number assigned to the item by the vendor.
Whse
The warehouse number associated with the products.
Qty-Clm
Qty-Alw
Line 1: Quantity on the vendor claim.
Line 2: Quantity allowed by the vendor.
U/M
The unit of measure for each product.
Price Clm
Ext. Clm
Line 1: Cost on vendor claim.
Line 2: Extended cost on vendor claim.
Price Alw
Ext. Alw
Line 1: The amount of the vendor’s credit for each unit.
Line 2: The total credit from the vendor.
