Setting Up Recurring Expense Invoices
You can use recurring vendor invoices for non-inventory expenses that are always paid on the same day of the month and are always the same amount. For example, if you have a rent payment of $1500.00 due at the first of each month, you can set up a recurring invoice for that payment. To use recurring invoices:
•
Enter a batch of recurring invoices (refer to Entering a Batch of Recurring Invoices).
•
Generate the Recurring Vendor Invoice Report (refer to Recurring Vendor Invoice Report).
•
Post recurring invoices (refer to Posting Recurring Vendor Invoices).
