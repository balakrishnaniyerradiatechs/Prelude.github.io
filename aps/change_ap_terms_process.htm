Changing Payment Terms for Open Invoices
If multiple open invoices have incorrect payment terms (refer to Using Accounts Payable Terms), you can change all of them at the same time. To change payment terms on open invoices:
1
Choose Financial > Accounts Payable > Processes > A/P Terms Change Process. The Change AP Terms Process screen appears.
2
Enter the following criteria to identify which open invoices you want to change:
•
Vendor - The vendor(s) for which you want to change payment terms on open invoices.
•
Payment Terms - The existing, incorrect payment terms on the open invoices.
•
Invoice Numbers - The numbers of the invoice(s) you want to change.
•
New Payment Terms - The correct terms code that you want to apply to the open invoices.
3
Press F2-Save. The open invoices are updated with the new payment terms.
When you change payment terms using the A/P Terms Change Process, the A/P Transaction Audit Inquiry is not updated.
