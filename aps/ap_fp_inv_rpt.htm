A/P Floor Planned Inventory Report
The A/P Floor Planned Inventory report displays the status of floor planned inventory items. Refer to Tracking Floor Plan Inventory for more information.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > A/P Floor Planned Inventory Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Vendor Number. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Product Number. The product number(s) to include.
•
Floor Plan Status. Determines the status of the floor plan:
-
O - Open. This item has not been shipped or paid.
-
S - Shipped. The item was fully or partially shipped.
-
P - Paid. The item was paid in full.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor/Description
Line one: The vendor number.
Line two: The vendor description.
Product Number/Description
Line one: Product number.
Line two: Product description.
Serial Number
The serial number of the product if one exists.
Model Number
The model number of the product if one exists
Book Value (label reflects the name of the Book)
The asset’s current book value.
Receiver#/Seq#
Receiver on which the items were received/sequence number of the floor planned items.
Invoice#
The vendor’s invoice number.
Inv Date
The date of the vendor’s invoice
Due Date
The date payment is due
S
The status of the floor planned inventory items:
•
O - Open.
•
S - Shipped.
•
P - Paid.
Ext Amount
The extended amount of the floor plan items.
Pd Amount
Amount already paid towards the floor planned items.
Amt Due
The remaining amount to be paid.
