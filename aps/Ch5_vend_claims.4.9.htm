Splitting a Single Claim
If the credit memo reflects part of your claim, you must split the claim. When you split a claim, the system creates a new claim with the line items that are not on the vendor credit memo. The new receiver automatically appends -50 to the first split claim (e.g., 123456-50) so that you can easily identify which claims were split. Additional split claims increment by one (e.g., 123456-51, 123456-52).
To split a claim:
1
On the Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry screen, press F5-Split. The Vendor Claims Split screen appears.
2
Enter the Claim# or press F3 to search. The open lines on the claim appear.
3
If the line item is on the claim, enter Y in Inc?.
4
In Qty Inc, enter the quantity that is on the credit memo.
5
Repeat steps 3-4 for each line item on the credit memo.
6
Press F2-Save. A notification dialog box with the new number appears.
The New Receiver Is 10001220-50
7
Choose Continue.
The items that were not included for this vendor invoice are now on the new claim number.
8
Continue reconciling the claim as usual. Refer to Reconciling a Vendor Claim with a Credit Memo.
Lines cannot be split for:
•
Lines with multiple sources.
The following conditions apply for partial split quantities:
•
The Qty-Inc must be equal to or less than the Qty-Rec of the line item.
•
Serialized items - Delete the serial numbers that are not on the invoice.
•
Freight and Duty/Brokerage amounts are split according to total percentage for all products on the receiver.
