Splitting a Single Receiver
If the vendor invoice reflects part of a receiver, you must split the receiver to create a new receiver with the line items that are not on the vendor invoice. The new receiver is automatically appended with -50, so that you can easily identify which receivers were split. Additional split receivers increment by one (e.g.,-51).
To split a receiver:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Invoice Reconciliation Entry > F5- Split to access the Vendor Invoice Split Routine screen.
2
Enter a Receiver# or press F3 to search for the receiver.
3
For each open line that is on the invoice, enter Y in Inc?
4
In Qty Inc, enter the quantity that you want to receive for the invoice. You can split receivers by partial line items. For example, if Qty Rec is 10 and you want to split the receiver to only pay for 5 now, enter 5 in Qty Inc.
5
Repeat steps 3-4 for each line item on the invoice.
6
Press F2-Save. A dialog box with the new receiver number appears.
The New Receiver Is 10000873-50
7
Choose Continue.
The items that were not included for this vendor invoice are now on the new receiver number.
8
Continue reconciling the invoice as usual. Refer to Reconciling a Vendor Invoice to a Receiver.
