Open Vendor Invoice Report
The Open Vendor Invoice report lists all vendor invoices that are not yet paid.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Open Vendor Invoice Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse. Receiving warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Receipt Date range. The beginning and ending receipt date range to include.
•
Due Date. The date payment is due, which is calculated using invoice date and A/P terms.
•
P/O Type. The purchase order type(s) to include.
•
Status. Determines which receivers appear on the report:
-
A - All receivers.
-
H - Receivers that are on hold.
-
R - Receivers that are not on hold or suspended.
-
S - Receivers that are suspended.
-
T - Receivers that were on hold but are released.
•
Receiver Amount. The dollar amount of the receiver.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Receiver Information
Receive#
The receiver number.
Rec-Date
The date the invoice was received.
Inv-Date
The date of the invoice.
Due-Date
The date payment is due.
PO Type
Desc
The description of the purchase order type:
•
01 - Regular.
•
02 - Direct Ship.
•
03 - Will Call.
•
04 - Multi Branch.
•
05 - Special.
•
07 - Capital Assets.
•
08 - Non Inventory Expenses.
•
09 - Future Purchase Order.
•
10 - Consigned.
•
12 - OTW-Stock.
•
13 - OTW - Direct.
Vendor#
The vendor number.
Invoice#
The invoice number.
Brokerage
The brokerage fee from On The Water Maintenance or calculated by the system.
Fiscal
The fiscal period associated with the invoice.
Freight
The freight amount from On The Water Maintenance or calculated by the system.
Duty
The duty from On The Water Maintenance or calculated by the system.
Ship Via
The ship via code and description associated with the invoice.
Status
The status of the open vendor invoice:
•
H - Receiver is on hold.
•
S - Receiver was suspended.
•
T - Receiver was taken off hold but not yet updated.
Product Information
Ln#
The line number of the item.
Product Number
The product number.
Description
The description of the product.
Vendor Part#
The part number assigned to the item by the vendor.
Whse
The warehouse number associated with the product.
Qty-Rec
The quantity of items received for the product.
U/M
The unit of measure for each item.
Gross Price/Ext.
Line 1: The gross price of each item.
Line 2: The extended gross price of each item (Gross Price x Qty-Rec).
Revised Price/Ext.
Line 1: The revised price of each item if cost was received in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry.
Line 2: The extended revised price of each item (Revised Price x Qty-Rec).
