Monthly A/P Check Register
The Monthly A/P check Register includes all payments processed for a fiscal period. For more information, refer to Selecting Vendors/Invoices to Pay.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > Check Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Period. The fiscal period associated with the payments.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check#
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date of the payment.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The invoice number(s) that were paid.
Inv-Date
The date of the invoice.
Gross Paid
The gross amount due for the invoice.
Disc Taken
The discount taken from the invoice.
Net Amount
The amount paid (Gross Paid - Disc Taken).
