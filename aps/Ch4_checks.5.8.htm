Transferring Funds Electronically (EFT)
If the Batch Type is E, only invoices that should be paid by electronic transfer (are selected. When you press F8, a file is created that is transmitted to your bank. The invoices are marked as paid with sequentially assigned check numbers with an E prefix (e.g., E00050).
Setting up a Vendor for Electronic Payment
To set up a vendor to be paid by electronic funds transfer, set Payment Method to E in Vendor Maintenance > F6-A/P.
