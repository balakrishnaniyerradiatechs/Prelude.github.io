Open Receiver Invoice Report
The Open Receiver Invoice report lists material received but not yet matched to a vendor invoice for payment in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Open Receiver Invoice Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Warehouse. The selling warehouse(s) to include.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Receipt Date range. The beginning and ending receipt date to include.
•
Due Date. The date the invoice is due, which is calculated using invoice date and terms.
•
P/O Type. The purchase order type(s) to include.
•
Not Suspended?. Determines if suspended receivers are included on the report:
-
Y - Include suspended receivers.
-
N - Do not include suspended receivers.
•
Currency. The currencies for which you want to see open receivers.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Receiver Information
Receiver #
The receiver number.
Vendor #
The vendor number.
Invoice #
The invoice number.
Rec-Date
The date the material was received.
Inv Date
The date of the invoice.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Period
The fiscal period associated with this invoice.
Ship Via/Description
Line 1:# The ship via code assigned to the receiver.
Line 2:# The description of the ship via code.
Status
The status of the open vendor invoice:
•
H - Receiver is on hold.
•
S - Receiver was suspended.
•
T - Receiver was taken off hold but not yet updated.
Product Information
Ln#
The line number of the product.
Product Number
The product number of the item on the receiver.
Description
The description of the product.
Vendor Product#
The vendor’s number for this product.
Whse
The warehouse number on the vendor invoice.
Qty
The quantity received of this item.
U/M
The unit of measure of the item.
Ext Gross
The extended gross amount of the item (cost x quantity received).
Ext Revised
The extended revised dollar amount of the item.
