Open Invoices On Hold Report
The Open Invoices on Hold report displays open vendor invoices that are on hold. You can manually put an invoice on hold if you want the buyer to review an invoice before you pay it. Invoices can also go on hold if the reconciliation limits were exceeded for the user that tried to reconcile the vendor invoice. You can release the receivers in the Accounts Payable Released Inquiry.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Vendor Invoices On Hold Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Posting Date range. The beginning and ending dates for which you want to view posted invoices.
•
Discount Date. The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive a discount. This date is based on the invoice date and A/P terms.
•
Vendor. The vendor number(s) to include.
•
Vendor Type. The vendor type(s) to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The number of the vendor’s invoice.
Inv Date
The date of the vendor’s invoice.
Due Date
The date on which payment is due to the vendor.
S
The status of the vendor invoice:
•
I - Invoice on hold.
•
V - Vendor on hold.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to deduct the discount.
Disc. Amt.
The amount of the discount on the invoice.
Inv. Amount
The invoice amount.
Paid to Date
The amount paid to date for this invoice.
Bal. Remain
The total amount due to the vendor for this invoice.
