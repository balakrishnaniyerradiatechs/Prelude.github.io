Viewing Invoices Selected to Pay
This report displays invoices selected for payment.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Check Processing > Payment Selection/Print > F7-Sel Rpt
Selection Criteria
Choose an output method:
•
S - Screen.
•
P - Print.
•
E - Email.
•
F - Fax.
Column Descriptions
 
Column Heading
Description
Vendor
The vendor of the invoice that was selected with this check batch.
Description
The vendor description.
Invoice Number
The invoice selected to pay with this check batch.
Inv-Date
The date of the invoice selected.
Dsc-Date
The discount date the invoice must be paid by to qualify for the discount.
Due-Date
The due date of the invoice. This is calculated from the A/P terms code.
Invoice
Bal Due
The total balance due for the invoice selected.
Disc Amt
The discount amount to deduct from the invoice (if any).
Selected to Pay
Invoice Amt
The amount that will be paid for the invoice.
Disc Amt
The discount amount that will be deducted from the invoice.
Net Amount
The net amount paid for the invoice (Invoice Amt - Disc Amt).
