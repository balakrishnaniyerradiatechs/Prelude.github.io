Paying by Credit Card
If the Batch Type is C, only invoices that should be paid by credit card are selected. When you press F8, each invoice is marked as paid. The invoices are marked as paid with sequentially assigned check numbers with a C prefix (e.g., C00075). A new invoice is created for the CC Payment Vendor from Vendor Maintenance with an invoice number of C/C.PAID|<check#>. You can then pay the credit card vendor (e.g., VISA, MasterCard).
