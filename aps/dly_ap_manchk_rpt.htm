Daily A/P Manual Check Report
The Daily A/P Manual Check report shows payments that were entered manually for a date range. For more information, refer to Recording Manual Checks.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Daily A/P Reports > Manual Check Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Check Date range. The beginning and ending dates on which the payments were entered.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check#
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date of the payment.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Description
The name of the vendor.
Invoice Number
The invoice number(s) that were paid.
Gross Paid
The gross amount due for the invoice.
Disc Taken
The discount taken from the invoice.
Net Amount
The amount paid (Gross Paid - Disc Taken).
G/L Distribution#
The override G/L account (if any).
Debit Amt
The debit amount (if any).
