Vendor Invoice Reconciliation
Since not every vendor invoices you in the same manner, the Prelude Accounts Payable module provides flexible options to allow you to reconcile receivers with various types of vendor invoices. You can split and merge receivers, adjust amount discrepancies, set up recurring invoices, pay invoices manually, or use batch reconciliation for EDI vendors.
