Vendor Invoice History Inquiry
The Vendor Invoice History Inquiry shows detailed information for a paid invoice.
Viewing a Vendor Invoice
To view open or paid vendor invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Vendor Invoice History Inquiry to open the Vendor Invoice History Inquiry screen.
2
Enter a number or keyword for the Vendor or press F3 to search.
3
Enter an Invoice Number or press F3 to search.
Field & Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number.
Invoice Number
The vendor invoice number.
Invoice Date
The date the purchase order was invoiced.
Fiscal Period (not labeled)
The fiscal period.
Payment Terms
The terms code and description associated with the invoice.
Discount Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to receive a discount based on the invoice date and A/P terms.
Due Date
The date on which payment is due to the vendor.
P/O Number
The purchase order number for this vendor invoice.
Status
The status of the vendor invoice:
•
O - Open.
•
P - Paid.
•
E - Paid by electronic funds transfer (refer to Transferring Funds Electronically (EFT)).
•
B - Paid by Automatic Bank Deduction (refer to Paying by Automatic Bank Deduction (ABD)).
•
D - Deleted.
Invoice Amount
The total amount of the vendor invoice.
Merch Amount
The total merchandise amount. Not including any additional charges such as freight, tax, duty and brokerage fees.
Tax Amt
The tax amount applied to this vendor invoice.
Discount Amount
The discount amount applied to the invoice (if any).
G/L Prefix information
Ln#
The line count for accounting distribution.
Div
Division number. If your company uses divisions, this represents the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
Region number. A Subset of Division if Division is used (e.g. Southwestern Region, Northeastern Region).
Loc
Location number. The warehouse location that represents where the general ledger posting will post.
Dep
Department number. A subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
Cost Center number. A subset of Department if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department. (e.g. shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The G/L account number for distributing the cost of the P/O.
Dist Amount
The dollar amount distributed to the G/L account.
Comments
The reason for the posting.
Posted
A P indicates that the distribution line posted to the general ledger.
Post Date
The date the amount posted to the G/L account.
User
The user ID of the person who processed the checks.
Currency (not labelled)
The vendor’s currency, if you use multiple currencies.
Total Distribution
Total amount distributed to the G/L accounts.
OK
Closes the Vendor Invoice History Inquiry screen.
F5-Review Receiver
Displays the AP/PO Receiver History screen, which lists history information for the receiver.
F6-Check History
Opens the Check History Inquiry screen to allow you to view the history of checks written against the vendor invoice(s).
F7-Remittance Comment
Displays the Remittance Check Comment screen, which allows you to view any remittance comments entered for this invoice.
Action Bar
Notes
Opens the notes screen to allow you to view and enter notes for this invoice.
Mlt Lvl Dsc
Opens the Multi Discounts screen to allow you to view multiple discount dates and percentages if multiple discount dates were set up for the invoice.
Misc Vend
If you are using a miscellaneous vendor, the Miscellaneous Vendor Information screen displays, which allows you to view contact name, phone number, and address information for the vendor.
Curr Info
Displays the currency for this vendor in the Currency Info screen.
Image
Allows you to view images associated with this invoice:
•
Invoices.
•
Letters of Credit.
•
Shipping Documents.
•
P/O Documents.
•
Other.
