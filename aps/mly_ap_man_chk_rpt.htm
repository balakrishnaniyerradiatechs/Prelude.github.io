Monthly A/P Manual Check Report
The Monthly A/P Manual Check report lists payments generated manually for a fiscal period. Refer to Recording Manual Checks.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > Reports > Monthly A/P Reports > Manual Check Register
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
Period. The fiscal period associated with the payments.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check#
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date of the payment.
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Invoice Number
The invoice number(s) that were paid.
Gross Paid
The gross amount due for the invoice.
Disc Taken
The discount taken from the invoice.
Net Amount
The amount paid (Gross-Disc Taken).
G/L Distribution#
The override G/L account (if any).
Debit Amt
The debit amount (if any).
