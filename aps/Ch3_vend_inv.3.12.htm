Pre-Paying a Vendor
If a vendor requires a pre-payment for a partial amount of an order in advance of a shipment, you can issue a manual check and then when the invoice is reconciled, you can post the pre-paid amount to the Pre-Paid Inventory G/L account.
Use the following steps to pre-pay a vendor:
1
Enter a manual check to the vendor (refer to Recording Manual Checks) and distribute the charges to the PrePaid Inventory G/L account.
2
When the merchandise arrives, receive the material as normal in Stock Receipts Entry.
3
When you reconcile the invoice, for Merch Amount, enter the amount that you still owe the vendor.
4
When you press F2-Save to complete the payment, the discrepancy message appears:
•
Choose the G/L Acct option.
•
Post the difference to the PrePaid Inventory G/L account.
