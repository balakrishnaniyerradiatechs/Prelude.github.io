Merging Vendor Claims
If a credit memo reflects two or more claims, you must merge the claims. To merge the claims:
1
On the Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry screen, press F6-Merge. The Vendor Claim Merge Routine screen appears.
2
Enter the Receiver# or press F3 to search for the claim that you want to merge.
3
Enter the Receive# or press F3 to search for the other claims you want to merge with the claim in Receiver Number.
4
Press F2-Save. All receivers are combined and can be paid as one invoice.
5
Continue reconciling the claim as usual. Refer to Reconciling a Vendor Claim with a Credit Memo.
