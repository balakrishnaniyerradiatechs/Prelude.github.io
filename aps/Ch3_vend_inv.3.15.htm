Entering High Volume Vendor Invoices
When you receive the vendor’s invoice, you can enter it in Prelude even if you have not received stock yet.
EDI Re-Cost
A/P Auto EDI Recost in Accounts Payable Parameters determines if the line item cost on a purchase order is automatically re-costed if the incoming EDI vendor invoice has a lower cost for the line item:
•
Y - Automatically recost the purchase order if the vendor invoice is lower for a line item.
•
N - Do not recost the purchase order when the vendor invoice is lower.
To enter invoices to pay:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > High Volume AP/PO Matching > High Volume AP/PO Matching Entry to access the High Volume AP/PO Matching Entry screen.
2
For Entry #, press ENTER to get a new number or press F3 to search for an existing number.
3
Enter the purchase order number to pay in P/O Number. The system automatically checks for the extensions (e.g., -1, -2, -3). The Vendor number and name appears.
4
For Invoice, enter the vendor’s invoice number.
5
For Invoice Date, enter the vendor’s invoice date.
6
Change the Terms Code, Discount Date and Due Date if necessary.
7
Enter the total value of the merchandise amount in Merch Amount.
8
Enter the following fees if they are listed on the invoice (the charges are allocated to the cost of the items according to your company settings):
•
Freight Amount.
•
Duty Amount.
•
Brokerage Amt.
•
Tax Amt.
9
If any of the fees in step 8 apply, check Include? to pay the vendor for the charge. Leave blank if you will pay another vendor.
10
Enter other charges in Misc Amt (e.g., credits or debits for miscellaneous charges to the vendor).
11
Press F2-Save.
