Paying on Receipt
Some vendors do not send an invoice, but require payment on receipt of the merchandise.
Vendor Requirements for Payment on Receipt
Pay On Receipt in Vendor Maintenance > F6-AP determines if a vendor requires payment on receipt:
•
N - Do not pay on receipt. Invoices are reconciled as usual.
•
Y - Pay on receipt. You must enter the invoice number, invoice date, and freight amount when you receive stock. The receiver and invoice are automatically reconciled and ready for payment.
•
A - Pay on receipt with auditing. You must enter the invoice number, invoice date, and freight amount when you receive stock. To reconcile the receiver and invoice, you can use either Vendor Invoice Reconciliation (refer to Reconciling a Vendor Invoice to a Receiver) or you can reconcile all of the pay-on-receipt receivers and invoices at once.
To reconcile all of the pay-on-receipt receivers and invoices at once:
1
Choose Financial > Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > Vendor Invoice Reconciliation > Pay On Receipt Vendor APPO Mass Update. The Pay On Receipt Vendor APPO Mass Update screen appears.
2
For Vendor, enter the vendor number or description or press F3 to search. Leave blank to include all vendors.
3
Enter a beginning and ending Receipt Date to limit the receivers that are reconciled.
4
Press F2-Save. The receivers and invoices are reconciled and are ready for payment.
