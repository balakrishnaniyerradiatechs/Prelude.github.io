Purchase Order Invoice Inquiry
The Purchase Order Invoice Inquiry lists all vendor invoices billed against a purchase order.
Viewing the Purchase Order Invoice Inquiry
To view purchase order invoices:
1
Select Financial > Accounts Payable> Inquiries > Purchase Order Invoice Inquiry to open the Purchase Order Invoice Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Purchase Order Number.
