Posting Expense Invoices
To pay a non-inventory expense (e.g., office supplies, utilities), you can enter it manually or set up recurring invoice payments. Refer to the following:
•
Setting Up Recurring Expense Invoices.
•
Manually Entering Expense Invoices.
WARNING Use manual and recurring invoices for expense items only. If you need to pay an inventory invoice that does not have a corresponding receiver, create one in Stock Receipts Entry (refer to the Inventory documentation).
