Previewing Reconciled High Volume Vendor Invoices
The High Volume AP/PO Matching Preview Report lists invoices for high volume AP/PO Matching transactions. This report only shows invoices that were reconciled since High Volume AP/PO Matching Reconciliation was last run.
Menu Path: Accounts Payable > AP/PO Subsystem > High Volume AP/PO Matching > High Volume AP/PO Matching Preview Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print:
•
User Id. The user ID for the transactions.
•
Entry Date. The date the transaction was processed. Leave blank to display all transactions.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Entry#
The entry number assigned to this transaction.
Vend#
The vendor number which was paid on this invoice.
Description
The vendor’s description.
Invoice
The invoice number assigned to transaction.
Due Date
The date the invoice is due.
Dsc Date
The date by which the invoice must be paid to qualify for the discount.
P/O#
The purchase order paid for this invoice.
Merch Amount
The merchandise total for the invoice.
Misc Charges
The total amount of any other charges such as Freight, Duty, Brokerage, and Misc charges.
Total Amount
The total amount of the invoice (Merch Amount + Misc charges).
