Monthly Payment Check Inquiry screen
The Monthly Payment Check Inquiry screen displays detailed payment history and check information for a check paid to a vendor.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Inquiries > Accounts Payable Inquiry > Inquiries > Monthly A/P History > F5-Review Checks
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln# (unlabeled)
The line count for payments sent to the vendor.
Check Number
The payment number.
Chk-Date
The date the payment was issued to the vendor.
Check Amount
The amount of the payment.
F2-Finish
Closes the Monthly Payment Check Inquiry screen.
F5-Review Check
Opens Accounts Payable Check Inquiry to view detailed payment information.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are line number, page number, reference number, B (bottom), or T (top).
