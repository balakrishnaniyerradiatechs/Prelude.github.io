A/P Payment Manual Selection Audit screen
The A/P Manual Selection Audit screen allows you to add vendors and /or invoices to select for payment.
Refer to Manually Selecting Invoices to Pay for more information.
Menu Path: Financial > Accounts Payable > Check Processing > Payment Selection/Print > F6-Manual Sel > F5-Maintain Invoices
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Vendor
The vendor number and description.
Ln# (not labeled)
The line count for accounting distribution.
Invoice Number
The invoice being paid.
Inv Date
The invoice date.
Disc Date
The payment date if a discount is taken. The system calculates a date using the invoice date and accounts payable terms.
Disc Avail
The discount amount available if the invoice is paid prior to the Dsc Date.
Disc Amt
The discount amount taken (if any).
Balance Due
The balance due for the invoice.
Gross Pay
The amount to pay to the vendor. You can override this amount to pay less than the total invoice amount.
Total
Displays the total for each column.
F2-Save
Saves the selection information and closes the screen.
F4-Del
Deletes the selection information.
F5-Floor Plan Info
Opens the Floor Plan Maintenance screen.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a reference number for quick access. Valid codes are line number, page number, reference number, B (bottom), or T (top).
