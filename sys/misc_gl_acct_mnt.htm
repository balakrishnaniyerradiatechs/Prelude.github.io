Misc. G/L Account Maintenance screen
The Miscellaneous Charges G/L Numbers screen allows you to set up G/L accounts to use for miscellaneous charges in Sales Order Entry.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Order Processing Controls > Misc Charges G/L Numbers
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Enter Accounts OK for Misc G/L Entry
Ln#
The line number for the G/L prefix and account.
Div
The division assigned to the account. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern, Western).
Reg
The region assigned to the account. A region is a subset of Division - if Division is used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The warehouse assigned the account.
Dep
The department assigned to the account. A department is a subset of warehouse. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center assigned to the account. A cost center is a subset of Department - if Department is used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The general ledger account number.
Description
The account description.
F2-Save
Saves the general ledger information.
F4-Del
Deletes general ledger account information.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number to go directly to.
