Retail Price Matrix Maintenance screen
Retail matrix types are set up by Prelude and are used to define special prices beyond column and/or quantity break prices. The Retail Price Matrix Maintenance screen allows you to define valid retail matrices for your company and the order in which they should be used.
Refer to “Retail Price Matrices” in the Order Processing documentation for more information.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Order Processing Controls > Retail Price Matrix & Sequence
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Retail Price Matrix Types
The retail matrix types that are valid for your company. The first matrix in the sequence is used first unless Best Price is turned on or a customer has a contract price.The available types in Prelude are:
•
1 - Customer/Product.
•
2 - Customer/Customer Product.
•
3 - Customer/Product Line.
•
4 - Customer/Vendor.
•
5 - Customer/Major Line.
•
6 - Customer Price Group/Product.
•
7 - Customer Price Group/Customer Product.
•
8 - Customer Price Group/Product Line.
•
9 - Customer Price Group/Vendor.
•
10 - Customer Price Group/Major Line.
•
13 - Customer Number.
•
21 - Customer/Product Range.
•
26 - Customer Price Group/Product Range.
F2-Save
Saves the retail price matrix types information.
F4-Del
Deletes the retail price matrices.
