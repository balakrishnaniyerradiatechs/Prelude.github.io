Modules screen
The Modules screen controls what modules are used by a company. Some of the modules are standard with Prelude and others must be purchased separately. Contact Activant Professional Services for licensing information.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Company Maintenance > F6- Modules

Contact Management
Determines if the company is licensed to use the Contact Relationship Management (CRM) module:
•
Y - Activates the CRM module.
•
N - Disables the CRM module.
The CRM module is generally used with the Marketing module to determine who to contact for promotions. If CRM is activated, literature is sent to the contact address from Customer Maintenance > Customer Contacts. If CRM is not activated, literature is sent to the prospect name in Prospect Maintenance.

•
Y - Activates Prelude’s UPS module for the manifest subsystem.
