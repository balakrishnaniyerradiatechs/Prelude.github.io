General Ledger Controls
Hierarchy Level Maintenance screen
Before you can set up your general ledger chart of accounts, you must use the Hierarchy Level Maintenance screen to establish the general ledger levels for your company. These levels define valid prefixes to use with general ledger numbers for divisions, regions, locations, departments, and cost centers, where applicable. Refer to “Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy” in the General Ledger documentation.
Once you set up your general ledger hierarchy, you can run Build Hierarchy List (on the General Ledger Controls menu) to build a list of all the possible G/L account prefixes.
WARNING Do not change your hierarchy levels without contacting Activant Prelude Software Support.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > General Ledger Controls > Hierarchy Maintenance
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Divisions
Determines if division-level accounting is used by your company:
•
Y - Activate Divisions.
•
N - Disable Divisions.
A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division). To view or add divisions, press F5-Div List to open the Division Master Maintenance screen.
Regions
Determines if regional-level accounting is used by your company:
•
Y - Activate Regions.
•
N - Disable Regions.
Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region). To view or add regions, press F6-Reg List to open the Region Master Maintenance screen.
Locations
Accept Y to activate locations (required). A location could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to. To view or add locations, press F7-Loc List to open theLocation Master Maintenance screen.
Departments
Determines if department-level accounting is used by your company:
•
Y - Activate Departments.
•
N - Disable Departments.
Departments are a subset of Locations, used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse). To view or add departments, press F8-Dep List to open the Department Master Maintenance screen.
Cost Centers
Determines if cost center-level accounting is used by your company:
•
Y - Activate Cost Centers.
•
N - Disable Cost Centers.
Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales). To view or add divisions, press F9-CCr List to open the Cost Center Master Maintenance screen.
F2-Save
Saves the general ledger Hierarchy information.
F5-Div List
Opens the Division Master Maintenance screen.
F5-Reg List
Opens the Region Master Maintenance screen.
F7-Loc List
Opens the Location Master Maintenance screen.
F8-Dep List
Opens the Department Master Maintenance screen.
F9-Ccr List
Opens the Cost Center Master Maintenance screen.
