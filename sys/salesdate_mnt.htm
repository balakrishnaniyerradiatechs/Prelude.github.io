Sales & Inventory Dates
Sales & Inventory Dates screen
The Sales & Inventory dates screen allows you to control the dates to use in the Sales Analysis module. You can choose to maintain the dates on a calendar year basis or on a fiscal basis to match the general ledger.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Sales & Inventory Dates
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Sales Analysis Start Date
The day of the month that is the first day of the corresponding period. For example, January 1st can be entered as 0101, 1.1, or 01 Jan
Calendar Usage Month
The month number that this period represents. This is the month that is updated with usage, sales, and purchases.
Season Index
The index between 0.00 and 12.00 that is used to enter an annual sales forecast in Sales Forecast Maintenance.
F2-Save
Saves the sales analysis information.
F4-Del
Deletes the sales analysis information.
