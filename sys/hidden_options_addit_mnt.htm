Additional Information screen
The Additional Information screen in the Additional Option Parameters allows access to more system option flags.
Menu Path: Additional Options Menu > Additional Option Parameters > F5-Addit Info
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Retain Lot Allocation
Determines if the lot information is retained for backordered line items:
•
Y - Retain the lot information when a lot product is backordered.
•
N - Do not retain the lot information when a lot product is backordered.
Expiration Days for General Comments
The default number of days before a general comment expires in the Credit Collection Subsystem. You can override this value for each credit collections comment that you enter for a customer.
When the number of Expiration Days in Credit Collections Schedule Entry or in Credit Collections Notes Entry is reached, the comment is removed from collection history.
Refer to the “Credit Collections Subsystem” in the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information.
Include Disputed Invoices In Collections
Indicates if disputed invoices are included in credit collections:
•
Y - Include disputed invoices in the collection queue.
•
N - Do not include disputed invoices in the collection queue.
You can mark the status of an invoice as disputed in Manual Invoice Entry (refer to ”Manually Entering an Invoice” on page 4-2) or in Accounts Receivable Maintenance (refer to ”Editing an A/R Invoice” on page 4-5).
Refer to the “Credit Collections Subsystem” in the Accounts Receivable documentation for more information.
Online Quote RFQ Conversion To Vendor RFQ
Determines if you can create a Vendor RFQ (request for quote) from a sales quote:
•
Y - You can create a vendor RFQ from within Quotation Order Entry.
•
N - Vendor RFQs are not allowed from within Quotation Order Entry.
Refer to “Creating a Vendor RFQ from a Sales Quote” in the Vendor RFQ section of the Purchasing documentation.
Quote VRFQ Preselect?
Determines if the Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen appears when you convert a sales quote to a vendor RFQ. This screen displays all the vendors assigned to the product in Product Vendor Maintenance:
•
Y - Displays the Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen to allow you to view all the vendors assigned to the product.
•
N - The Quote RFQ Vendor Selection screen does not appear.
Refer to “Creating a Vendor RFQ from a Sales Quote” in the Vendor RFQ section of the Purchasing documentation.
Include Credits In Collections
Determine if credits are included in the past due balance when Credit Collections Nightly Processing generates credit collection letters:
•
Y - Credit Collections Nightly Processing includes credits to determine if a collection letter should be generated.
•
N - Credit Collections Nightly Processing excludes credits to determine if a collection letter should be generated.
Refer to ”Generating Scheduled Collection Letters” in the Credit Collections section of the Accounts Receivable documentation.
Inventory Balancing Control Flag
Disallows procedures that can cause linked inventory to get out of balance. If this flag is set to Y, the following restrictions apply:
•
You cannot enter asterisk (*) part numbers in Vendor Claim Entry without a warranty tag.
•
You cannot link a purchase order to sales orders, work orders, or transfers if there is a pick quantity.
•
When you link a sales order to a purchase order, the quantity of an item on the sales orders must equal the quantity of the item on the purchase order.
•
You cannot receive a quantity of a linked item that is greater than the quantity on the purchase order.
•
You cannot enter asterisk (*) part numbers on a credit memo to return to stock. They must go into warranty, damaged, or scrapped inventory.
•
You cannot change the open quantity of a linked item on a sales order, work order, or transfer.
•
You cannot change the pick quantity for asterisk (*) items on a sales order, work order, or transfer.
•
You cannot increase the pick quantity of a linked stock item on a sales order (decrease only).
•
The ship quantity must equal the pick quantity for asterisk (*) part numbers in Release Confirmation Entry.
•
You cannot increase the quantity of linked stock items in release confirmation entry or Transfer Shipments Entry.
•
If an asterisk (*) part number is received, you cannot delete the item on the sales order until you enter a negative cash receipt.
Convert Price To Contract U/M?
Determines if contract prices use the contract unit of measure or the unit of measure from Product Maintenance:
•
Y - The contract price in a price matrix or blanket contract converts to the contract unit of measure.
•
N - The contract prices uses the unit of measure from Product Maintenance.
Negative Line Items On Pick Tickets
Determines if negative line items print on pick tickets:
•
Y - Print negative line items on pick tickets.
•
N - Do not show negative line items on pick tickets.
Convert Quote As A New Line
Determines if a line item that is converted from a quote appears as a new item on the sales order:
•
Y - A line converted from a quote appears as a new item on the sales order, only the product number and quantity are retained.
•
N - When a line item is converted from a quote to a sales order, the pricing information is retained.
Default AVS For Credit Card
Determines if AVS (Address Verification Service) information defaults from the customer’s account (Customer Maintenance > F6-A/R > F7-Credit Card > F5-Bill-To) when you pre-authorize the sales order:
•
Y - If an address exists for a customer, it defaults on the Additional Credit Card Information screen when you pre-authorize a sales order.
•
N - Address information does not default to the Additional Credit Card Information screen when you pre-authorize a sales order. You must manually enter the address each time.
Refer to “Credit Cards” in the Order Processing documentation for more information.
F2-Save
Saves the option parameter information.
