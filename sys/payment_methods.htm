Payment Methods screen
Payment methods are set up by Prelude and are used to determine how you will accept payment upon completion of a sales order. The Payment Methods screen allows you to determine the payment methods that are allowed for your company.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Order Processing Controls > Payment Methods
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Valid Payment Methods
The payment methods that are valid for your company. The available types in Prelude are:
•
01 - Cash.
•
02 - Check.
•
03 - Bank Card.
•
04 - A/R Invoice.
•
05 - Wire Transfer.
•
06 - A/P Refund.
•
07 - Apply To Invoice.
•
08 - Apply To Order.
•
09 - Sending Personal Check.
•
10 - Certificate A/R.
•
11 - Gift Certificate.
F2-Save
Saves the payment method information.
F4-Del
Deletes the valid payment methods.
F5-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number to go directly to.
