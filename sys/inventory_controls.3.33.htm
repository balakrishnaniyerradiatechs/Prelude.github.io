TPCx Information screen
If you use Trading Partner Connect, you can use the TPCx Information screen to set up this warehouse as a TPCx warehouse. Trading Partner Connect offers a private internet trading network designed to streamline the commerce process among distributors, manufacturers/suppliers, and customers. You can streamline your business practices by making transactions with other members of the trading network in real time. Instead of mailing printed forms or making phone calls, transactions through Trading Partner Connect are immediately transmitted to trading partners, allowing for faster responses.
Menu Path: Systems > System Parameters > Maintenance > Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > Action Bar > Addl Opts > TPCx Information
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
TPCx Warehouse
Determines if this warehouse is included in TPCx to share price and availability information:
•
Y - This warehouse is included in TPCx.
•
N - This warehouse is not included in TPCx.
F2-Save
Saves the TPCx information.
