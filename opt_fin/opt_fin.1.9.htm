Sales Order Entry & Quotation Order Entry (/SOE & /QOE)
Menu Path: Order Processing > Sales Order Entry
Order Processing > Additional Subsystems > Quotation Subsystem > Quotation Entry
Once the Vertex module is activated, the GEOCODES for sales orders and quotes default from the master records. The system defines the tax authorities based on those GEOCODES.
If you use a manual ship-to, the GEOCODES default when you enter the city, state, and zip code. Note: In V18+, the city and state are verified against the city and state in Customer Maintenance. If they do not match, you must update the tax exempt status.
If the city, state, and zip cross multiple county lines, you must choose the proper GEOCODE. These tax authorities are analyzed more specifically at invoicing when each product and additional charge is evaluated. For example, if an order has 3 line items plus freight and handling charges on a sales order, 5 register records are created to manage reports and returns.
