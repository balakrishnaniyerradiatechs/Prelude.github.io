Listings
All listings are in Financial > Fixed Assets > Listings.
•
Book listing (/BOOK.LST) - Codes and descriptions from Book Maintenance.
•
Class Listing (/CLASS.LST) - Class codes from Class Maintenance.
•
Assets Listing (/ASSETS.LST) - Asset master record data from Asset Maintenance.
•
Assets By Asset Number (ASSETS.ASSET.LST) - Asset master data by asset number.
•
Assets By Vendor (/ASSETS.VENDOR.LST) - Asset information by vendor.
•
Assets By Location (/ASSETS.LOC.LST) - Asset information by warehouse.
•
Assets By Class (/ASSETS.CLASS.LST) - Asset information by class code.
•
Assets By Depreciation Method (/ASSETS.METHOD.LST) - Asset information by depreciation method.
•
Equipment Listing (/EQUIP.LST) - Equipment with the assets and their profitability statistics. Equipment information is set up in Equipment Maintenance.
•
Equipment Leasing (/LEASE.LST) - Equipment assets that are leased. Equipment leasing information is set up in Equipment Maintenance - F5 - Lease Info.
•
Depreciation Listing (/DEPR.LST) - Equations used to calculate each method of depreciation. Depreciation methods are set up in Depreciation Maintenance.
•
Disposal Code Listing (/DISPOSAL.LST) - Disposal codes and descriptions that identify the retirement or disposal of assets from Disposal Maintenance.
•
Investment Credit Listing (/ITC.LST) - Displays the investment tax credit table. The investment tax credit table can be maintained in Investment Credit Maintenance.
