Reports
•
Job Activity Date Listing (/JOB.ACT.DATE.LIST) - Products for a customer and job. Includes the quantities of the items, the sales order date and purchasing information.
•
Unbilled Cost Report (/UNBILL.COST.RPT) - Estimated and actual costs of the items for the customer and job. This report is updated after Job Billing Entry is completed.
•
Job Cost Analysis Worksheet (/JOB.COST.ANALYSIS.RPT) - Products by customer or job.
•
Job Cost Analysis Listing (/JOB.COST.ANALYSIS.LIST) - Products by customer or job for selected customers and jobs.
•
AIA Invoice Report (/AIA.INVOICE.RPT) - AIA invoicing jobs for a customer. The scheduled value, work completed, percentage completed, balance, and retainage amount appear. This report matches the AIA G702 Document for Application and Certificate for Payment.
•
Custjob Monthly Sales Tax Detail Report (/CUSTJOB.MLY.TAX.DET.RPT) - Sales tax detail for a division, selling warehouse, department, and fiscal period.
•
Custjob Monthly Sales Tax Summary Report (/CUSTJOB.MLY.EAX.SUM.RPT) - Sales tax summary for a division, selling warehouse, department, and fiscal period sorted by selling warehouse or tax jurisdiction.
