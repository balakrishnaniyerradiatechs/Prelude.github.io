Job Accounting (V17)
The Job Accounting Module tracks estimated and actual jobs costs during a contract period. It handles billing differently than the standard Prelude method. You can use AIA (American Institute of Architects) invoicing and you can invoice the product before it ships. You can also use change order control to edit an order before the pick ticket prints and the invoice processes.
Licensing Note: You must license this module through your Activant sales representative.
Note: Unless other wise noted, all features are available from the Job Accounting menu.
