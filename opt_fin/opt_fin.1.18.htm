Chart of Accounts Maintenance (/COAM)
Path: General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts
Create two G/L accounts for job accounting:
•
Customer Job Work In Process - used in internal billing for inventory relief. Inventory is credited and Customer Job Work In Process is debited.
•
Customer Job Sales - used in the customer job billing process. A cost of goods sold account may track cost of goods sold for job billing.
Note: If the customer uses tax jurisdictions for jobs, create Customer Job Use Tax Payable and Customer Job Use Tax Cost accounts here and in General Ledger Table Maintenance.
Product Line Maintenance (/PLM > F7)
Path: Inventory > Maintenance > Product Control Files > Product Line > F7-G/L Accounts
For V18+, a G/L account override called Job Sales exists for use with job accounting sales by product line. This account is posted to during Job Invoice Printing and Processing.
Customer Maintenance (/CM > F7)
•
Require Job Number: Determines if a job number is required on the customer’s orders:
-
Y - A job number is required, but not validated. You can create a new job number.
-
S - A job number is optional and validated. You cannot create a new job number.
-
R - A job number is required, but not validated. You cannot create a new job number.
-
N - A job number is not required.
-
V - A job number is required and validated. You can create a new job.
-
J - A job number is required and validated. You cannot create a new job.
This flag must be set to Y, S or V to use Change Order Control in Customer Job Maintenance.
•
Customer Job Number: The valid default job number for this customer’s orders.
•
F5 > Inv Document Option: Determines how a customer’s job invoices are transmitted:
-
B - Fax and print.
-
E - E-mail.
-
M - E-mail and print.
-
N - Print.
-
Y - Fax.
Email addresses and fax numbers come from Customer Maintenance or Customer Ship-To Maintenance.
Note: If Mail Invoices, Summary Invoices, or Consolidate Invoices is Y, you cannot use Change Order Control for a job.
Customer Job Maintenance (/CJM)
Customer Job Maintenance defines pricing or credit terms for a job. Jobs can be assigned as a default in Customer Maintenance and Customer Ship-To Maintenance or added to an order.
•
Customer Number: The customer number for this job.
•
Job Number: The job number to create or edit.
•
Description: A description of the job.
•
Description Two: If necessary, an extended description for the job.
•
Status:
-
A - Active.
-
N - Inactive. You cannot use an inactive job on an order if Require Job Number is Y or V and Keep Customer Jobs in File Update Parameters is Y.
•
Initial Draw Amt: The initial draw amount. When this amount is exceeded, orders for this job go on credit hold.
•
Current Draw Amt: The available draw amount on this job.
•
A/R Terms Number: The accounts receivable terms code to use for this job.
•
Terms Exp. Date: The date on which the terms expire.
•
Credit Limit: The credit limit for orders against this job.
•
Customer Job Type: The customer job type to group the customer for sales and reporting.
•
Currency Indicator: The currency code for this job or leave blank to use native currency.
•
Delivery/Pick-Up Charge:
-
B - Charge for delivery and pickup.
-
D - Charge for delivery only.
-
N - Do not charge for delivery or pickup.
-
P - Charge for pickup only.
•
Delivery Inst. Enter any instructions to print on pick tickets for this job.
•
F5-Add Info: Additional controls are available for Job Information.
•
Change Order Control: Determines if the job is subject to the change order routine in Sales Order Entry to allow the original sales order to be changed. Only available if Require Job Number in Customer Maintenance is Y, S ,or V.
-
Y - Change control is used.
-
N - Change control is not used.
•
Retainage Percentage: The retainage percentage of each job invoice that the customer retains until the job is complete.
•
Retainage Due Date: The estimated cutoff date the customer waits before paying the retainage amount.
•
AIA Invoice: Determines if the job requires an AIA report:
-
Y - The job is included in AIA printing.
-
N - The job is not included in AIA printing.
•
Job Line Item Tax Flag Default: The default tax setting for the customer job line items:
-
Y - taxable.
-
N - non-taxable
-
U - use tax. Use tax payable and cost accounts are used in G/L postings.
•
Salesrep#1: The primary sales representative for the job.
•
Salesrep#2: The secondary sales representative for the job.
•
F5-Tax Jurisdictions.: Sales Tax Jurisdictions can be established in this screen:
•
Tax Jur: A valid tax jurisdiction from Tax Jurisdiction Maintenance.
•
Tax?: Determines if tax should be calculated for this jurisdiction:
-
Y - Calculate tax.
-
N - Do not calculate tax.
•
Exempt Number: If Tax? is N, the sales tax exempt number from the certificate issued by the taxing authority.
•
Exp Date: The expiration date of the tax exemption certificate.
