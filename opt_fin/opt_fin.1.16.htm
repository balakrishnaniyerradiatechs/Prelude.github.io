Reports
All reports are in Financial > Fixed Assets > Reports.
•
Optimum Depreciation (/OPT.DEPR.RPT) - This report compares any three specified depreciation methods for selected assets. By entering the starting and ending asset IDs and tax percentage rates, an analysis is produced showing the yearly depreciation amounts and present value of the tax reduction for the selected depreciation methods. This helps to determine which method to use.
•
Repairs / Scheduled Maintenance (/OPT.DEPR.RPT) - Provides information on equipment established as a repair / scheduled maintenance asset.
•
Equipment Profit And Loss (/PROFIT.LOSS.RPT) - Generates a report of equipment and profitability from the assets file. Shows the installation cost, cumulative depreciation, and the difference between installation cost and cumulative depreciation (net value). The procedure subtracts repairs and maintenance expenses and adds revenue from jobs and leases to produce a profit and loss report for the equipment.
•
Investment Tax Credit (/ITC.RPT) - Report of assets codes for investment tax credit.
•
Acquisitions (/AQUISTION.RPT) - Report of assets acquired before a date.
•
Disposals (/DISPOSAL.RPT) - Report of all assets disposed of before a date.
•
Manual Depreciation (/MDU.RPT) - Report of manual depreciation for end-of-period processing. It does not update any files.
•
Units Of Usage Depreciation (/UUDU.RPT) - Prints a report for end-of period processing of all assets with a depreciation method of U. It does not update any files.
•
Depreciation By Method (/DMU.RPT) - Prints a report of automatically calculated depreciation based on a user-specified number of periods.
•
Accumulated Depreciation (/LTD.DEPR.RPT) - Fixed assets showing accumulated depreciation for a book.
