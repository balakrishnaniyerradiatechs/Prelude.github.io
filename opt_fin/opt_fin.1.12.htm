Fixed Assets (V17)
The Fixed Assets module allows you to maintain records for fixed assets and depreciation books for your company. You can choose the depreciation calculation method for end-of-period or end-of-year taxes and reporting. You can track maintenance schedules for items and billing records for leased items.
