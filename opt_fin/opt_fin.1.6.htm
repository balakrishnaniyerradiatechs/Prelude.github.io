Rebuild ABC Data
This process performs a rebuild on the files that pertain to Activity Based Costing.
Path: ABC Accounting > Rebuild Menu > Rebuild ABC Data
Files used for ABC Module:
•
CONTROL - Where Cost Driver records are stored.in one of these formats:
-
CO#!ABC.COST.DRIVERS – Company Cost Drivers Record.
-
CO#!ABC.COST.DRIVERS!WHSE# - Warehouse Cost Driver Records.
•
CUST.STAT.ABC – Customer P&L Information is stored. The key is CO#!CUST#!FISC.YR
•
PROD.STAT.ABC - Product P&L Information. The key is CO#!PROD#!FISC.YR.
•
VEND.STAT.ABC - Vendor P&L Information. The key to this file is CO#!VEND#!FISC.YR.
•
PROD.LINE.STAT.ABC – Product Line P&L Information.The key is CO#!PLINE!FISC.YR.
•
VEND.LINE.STAT.ABC – Vendor Line P&L Information. The key is CO#!VEND#-VLINE!FISC.YR.
The four main programs for updating the files are:
•
ABC.UPDATE
•
POST.SR.ABC
•
APPO.ABC.UPDATE
•
ABC.REBUILD
The ABC.UPDATE routine is called from several places:
•
Marketing Update – This will track any phone call activity that occurs.
•
Contact Diary Update – Tracks contacts made through this module.
•
Commission Update through B-Update – Updates the commission costs from every order invoiced.
•
Stock Receipts Update – This will capture over receipts, partials, rejections, etc.
•
B-Update – This will track invoice lines, sales, cost of goods sold, gross margin, etc.
•
APPO Update – This will track cost discrepancies.
•
Cash Receipts Update – This will track discounts taken.
•
Month End A/R Aging Update – This will capture the month end A/R balance, which is then used for Capital Cost calculations.
•
Month End Inventory Balance Update – This will capture the month end inventory balance which is then used for Capital Cost calculations.
You MUST run the Monthend A/R Aging and Monthend Inventory Balance Update to capture information for capital costs.
