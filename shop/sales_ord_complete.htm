Sales Order Completion screen
The Sales Order Completion screen displays the total price of the merchandise from an order and allows you to add fees (e.g., freight, handling). You can also adjust tax jurisdictions and exemptions.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Shop Repair Order Entry > F2-Save > F2-Save > Complete
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Terms Code
The A/R terms for the order.
Merchandise
The total price for merchandise.
Inbound Freight
The inbound freight to charge the customer.
Outbound Freight
The outbound freight to charge the customer.
Handling
The handling fee to charge the customer.
Delivery
The delivery fee to charge the customer.
Pick Up
The pickup fee to charge the customer.
Restocking Charge
Any restocking fee to charge the customer.
Minimum Charge
The minimum order charge (if any).
Disc/Allowance
Any discounts for the order.
Sales Tax
The total sales tax for the order.
Total Sale
The total for the order, including merchandise, fees, and taxes, less any discount.
Deposit Required
If a deposit is required, the amount of the deposit.
Remain Deposit Bal.
If a deposit is required or was paid, the amount of the remaining balance.
Total Due
The total price still due on the order.
F2-Complete
Saves the information.
F5-Tax Jur
Opens the Sales Tax Jurisdictions screen.
F6-Dates
Opens the Order Dates screen.
Action Bar
Freight Cost
The cost of freight for this order. Used to compare to the freight charged.
CC Auth
If cash terms were used, opens the Cash Application screen to allow you to authorize a credit card.
Addtl Opts - Deposit Info
Opens the Deposit Information Inquiry screen, which lists all deposits made for this order.
Addtl Opts - Deposit Recalc
Recalculates the deposit amount.
Addtl Opts - Currency
If you use multiple currencies, changes the currency exchange rate.
Addtl Opts - Currency Conversion
If you use multiple currencies, allows you to enter charges in native currency and recalculate the currency on the order.
Service Info
Opens the Additional Information screen to record hourly meter information.
Notes - Header Notes
Allows you to view or edit header notes.
Notes - Conversion Notes
Not applicable to shop repair orders.
Notes - EDI Header Notes
Not applicable to shop repair orders.
