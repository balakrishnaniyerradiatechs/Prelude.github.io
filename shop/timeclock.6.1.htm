Time Clock Entry & Billing
Depending on how you record the time a technician worked on a job, and how you bill your customers, you can use one of the following time clock methods:
Shop TimeClock Entry allows technicians to clock into jobs or other tasks (e.g., cleaning the shop, break time) multiple times as they work during the day. You can use this method to pay technicians for hours worked. Refer to Using the Shop TimeClock.
Technician Job Billing Entry allows the technician or a supervisor to enter all time worked on a job or jobs for a single day. Because all the time worked during the day is entered at once, you cannot invoice orders until Technician Job Billing Entry is complete for all technicians. Refer to Billing Jobs for Technicians.
