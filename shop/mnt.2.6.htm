Overriding G/L Accounts by Pay Type/Product Line
By default, all accounts are at the company-level in the G/L table. You can override those accounts by pay type (refer to Creating Pay Types) or by pay type/product line combination.
To override a GL account for a pay type and product line:
1
Select Shop Repair > Maintenance > Pay Type/Product Line Maintenance to open the Pay Type/Product Line Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Pay Type (refer to Creating Pay Types for information pay types).
3
Enter the Product Line for which you want to override G/L accounts. The available GL accounts display under G/L Table Headings.
4
Enter override account numbers as necessary.
5
Press F2-Save.
