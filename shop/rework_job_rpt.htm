Shop Rework Job Report
The Shop Rework Job Report lists invoiced jobs that are the result of a safety rework.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Reports > Shop rework Job Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Selling Warehouse. The warehouse where the rework is being performed.
•
Invoice Date range. The beginning and ending dates that the shop repair orders were invoiced.
•
Technician. The ID of the technician that performed the rework.
