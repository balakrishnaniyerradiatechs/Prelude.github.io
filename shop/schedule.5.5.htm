Scheduling Appointments by Day
If you choose the D option for View in the Calendar Header screen (refer to Scheduling Resources for Shop Repair Jobs) the Repair Detail screen opens to allow you to view detail schedule information for each day and to enter or edit appointments for the resources available.
1
Choose from the following:
•
To edit a previously scheduled appointment, highlight the line item and press F6-Edit Appt.
•
To schedule a new appointment, move the cursor to the next blank line and select F5-New Appt.
The Shop/Repair Appointment Entry screen appears.
2
Accept or enter the Resource (e.g., technician, truck) for the appointment.
3
The Date and Hours default from the Calendar Header screen. Change if necessary.
4
Enter the Start Time and End Time of the appointment.
5
For Schedule Type, enter the type of work you are scheduling. Choose from the following:
•
A - Appointment.
•
P - Personal time.
•
S - Sick.
•
T - Training.
•
V - Vacation.
6
If applicable, enter any Comments regarding the appointment.
7
Enter the Status of the appointment. Choose from the following:
•
O - Open.
•
C - Complete.
If you are scheduling the resource from a shop repair order, the remaining fields will be filled in from the order.
8
If you are scheduling the resource for a specific job on an order, enter the Order Number. If you accessed this screen from a shop repair order, the order number and the Required Date, Last Contact and Model number from the shop repair order appear.
9
Enter the Job number from the shop repair order. The Description, Pay Type and Status of the job display.
10
When the appointment information is complete, press F2-Save. If the number of hours that you scheduled is less than the number of hours entered in the Calendar Header screen, the following message appears:
Scheduled hours [2] is fewer than original hours [3] to be scheduled. Do you wish to generate another appointment for the remaining hours?
11
Choose from the following:
•
Y - Returns to the Shop/Repair Appointment Entry screen to open a new appointment for the remaining hours.
•
N - Returns to the Repair Detail screen.
