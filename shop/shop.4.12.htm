Entering Accessories
If an item on the order has accessories and you want to view and/or them to the order:
1
Press F2-Save on the Line Item Detail screen (refer to Adding or Editing Line Items). A dialog box with the following message appears (only after the first time through the screen):
Do You Wish To Continue WIth Accessory Items Entry?
2
If you select Yes, the Accessory Product Order Selection screen appears.
3
For each item that you want to enter on the order, enter Y for Add To Order? and enter a Quantity.
4
Press F2-Accept. The additional items appear on the shop repair order. Continue entering line items (refer to Adding or Editing Line Items) and jobs (refer to Creating the Job) as necessary.
If you use the Product Tagging Entry screen to enter quantities, the products appear as line items on the order. To access the Line Item Detail screen, put your cursor on the item and press ENTER. If you put your cursor on an item without entering a quantity and press F2-Save on the Product Tagging Entry screen, the Line Item Detail screen appears.
