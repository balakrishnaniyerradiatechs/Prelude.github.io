Maintaining Customer Equipment Information
When a customer buys a serialized product, the serial number is assigned to that customer to help track return and repair information. If the product is sold or for any reason becomes the property of another customer, you can use Customer Equipment Maintenance to assign the product and serial number to the new customer.
To re-assign a model/serial number to another customer:
1
Select Shop Repair > Maintenance > Customer Equipment Maintenance to open the Customer Equipment Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number of the customer originally assigned to the serial number(s).
3
For Default Warehouse, enter the warehouse where the product was sold.
4
All serialized products, including the equipment identification number, for this customer/warehouse combination appear.
5
If you want to re-assign a product/serial number to another customer, highlight the appropriate Model Number and press F5-Change Customer. The Change Customer screen appears.
6
Enter the New Customer Number.
7
Press F2-Save to complete the change.
