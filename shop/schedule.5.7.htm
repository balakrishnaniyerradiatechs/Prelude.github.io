Scheduling Appointments by Month
If you choose the M option for View in the Calendar Header screen (refer to Scheduling Resources for Shop Repair Jobs), the Scheduling Calendar screen opens to allow you to view the available resource hours for each day of the month.
The number under each date shows the total amount of hours available depending on how many technicians you have. For example, 40 could means that you have 5 technicians who can each work 8 hours. Once work is scheduled for a day and allotted to jobs, the number decreases by the number of hours scheduled.
1
To view scheduling information for your available resources, press F5-Review to open the Repair Detail screen.
2
Refer to Scheduling Appointments by Day to add or edit appointments.
