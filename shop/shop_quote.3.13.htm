Changing the Totals
After you add all necessary jobs and related products and labor, you can change the profit, gross profit percent, or the total value, if necessary.
To change the totals for a shop repair quote:
1
On the Quote Repair Order Entry screen (refer to Starting or Editing a Shop Repair Quote), press F6-Totals to open the Order Totals screen.
2
Change Profit, Gp%, or Dollars as necessary. When one field is updated, the others automatically adjust.
3
Press F2. A dialog box appears with the following message:
Should These Adjustments Be Expressed As Lot Billing?
4
Choose one of the following:
•
Yes - Provide one total and do not show line item prices on the invoice and acknowledgement. Src for each line item changes to LT (lot billing).
•
No - Adjust the line item prices and show the new calculated line item totals.
Adjusted prices appear on Quote Repair Order Entry screen.
