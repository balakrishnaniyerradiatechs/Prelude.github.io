Quotation Completion screen
The Quotation Completion screen displays the total price of the merchandise from a shop repair quote and allows you to add fees (e.g., freight, handling). You can also adjust tax jurisdictions and exemptions. After completing this screen, you can print, email, or fax the quote to your customer.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Shop Repair Quote Subsystem > Shop Repair Quote Entry > F2-Save > F2-Save > Complete
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Terms Code
The A/R terms for the order.
Merchandise
The total price for merchandise.
Inbound Freight
The inbound freight to charge the customer.
Outbound Freight
The outbound freight to charge the customer.
Handling
The handling fee to charge the customer.
Delivery
The delivery fee to charge the customer.
Pick Up
The pickup fee to charge the customer.
Minimum Charge
The minimum order charge (if any).
Disc/Allowance
Any discounts for the order.
Sales Tax
The total sales tax for the order.
Total Sale
The total for the order, including merchandise, fees, and taxes, less any discount.
Pending Deposit
If a deposit is required, the amount of the deposit.
F2-Complete
Saves the information.
F5-Tax Jur
Opens the Sales Tax Jurisdictions screen.
Action Bar
Deposit Recalc
Recalculates the deposit amount.
Currency Conv
If you are using multiple currencies, allows you to enter charges in native currency and recalculate the currency on the order.
