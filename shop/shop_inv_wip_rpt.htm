Shop Inventory Work In Process Report
The Shop Inventory Work in Process Report lists inventory allocated to the Work in Process (WIP) inventory account during Release Confirmation Entry for shop repair orders. The Work in Process account allows you to remove the products on a shop repair pick ticket from inventory. When the order is invoiced, the Work in Process inventory balance is relieved, rather than the on-hand inventory balance.
Shop Repair Option Parameters
You must set Process Inventory as WIP, to Y to process inventory as work-in-process (WIP). Inventory is relieved from the on-hand balance during Release Confirmation Entry and put into the WIP balance. When the order in invoiced, the quantities move out WIP.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Reports > Shop Inventory WIP Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse Number. The warehouse where the repair work is being performed and the inventory is allocated.
•
Period. The month or fiscal period to include.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Release#
The shop repair order release number that identifies the repair work being done.
Proc Dte
The date the repair order was entered.
Product
The products included on the order.
Job
The job number on the order that contains the repair work being done.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the order.
Qty
The quantity of the product allocated to the repair.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Extension
The extended cost of the total quantity of the product required.
