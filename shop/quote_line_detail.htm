Quote Entry Detail screen
The Quote Entry Detail screen allows you to enter data about a product entered on an order. You can enter quantity, warehouse, supply code, and pricing information. You may also see cost and GP%, depending on your user settings.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Shop Repair Quote Subsystem > Shop Repair Quote Entry > enter a part number
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line number.
Quantity
The quantity of the product required to complete the job.
U/M
The product’s unit of measure (e.g., EA, DZ).
Whse
The selling warehouse.
A
Indicates if you accept the remaining information on the screen:
•
Y - The data is correct.
•
N - The data is not correct and needs to be edited.
Sc
The source code for the product if the product needs to be replenished:
•
P - Purchase.
•
T - Transfer.
•
M - Manufacture.
•
D - Direct.
•
O - Outplant.
•
E - Emergency purchase.
•
R - Emergency transfer.
Gross-Price
The gross price for the product.
Disc%
The discount percentage.
Net Price
The price of one unit of measure of the product, less the discount.
Scr
The source of the price:
•
P<#> - Price column.
•
C<#> - Contract.
•
M<#> - Price matrix.
•
LT - Lot billing.
•
OV - Manual override.
•
VDS - Volume discount.
•
SP - Sum and reprice.
•
CB - Blanket contract.
Tx?
Indicates if the product is taxable:
•
Y - Product is taxable.
•
N - Product is not taxable.
Extension
The total price for the product.
Cost
The cost of the product.
Gp%
The percent gross profit for the product.
Profit
The amount of profit of the product.
Req-Date
The date the product is required. If blank, defaults to the Req-Date on the Sales Order Header screen.
Pck-Date
The date the product should be picked. If blank, defaults to the Pck-Date on the Sales Order Header screen.
Promised
The date the product was promised to be delivered to the customer.
Available
The quantity of the product that is available.
Onorder
The quantity of the product that is on order through a purchase order, transfer, or work order.
Stock Status
The stocking status of the product:
•
N - Non stock.
•
S - Stock.
Pricing (not labelled)
Lists prices for the product. Matrix, contract, quantity breaks, or price column prices may appear.
F2-Save
Saves the data.
F5-Add Info
Opens the Additional Information screen to allow you to enter or view customer or vendor information regarding the part number.
F6-Substitutes
Opens the Substitutes screen, if substitutes are available.
F9-Invty Inq
Opens the Inventory Inquiry screen.
Action Bar
Inquiries
Lists available inquires for this product.
Entries
Allows you to go to Work Order Entry or Quick Quote Entry.
Retail Price
Shows retail price if calculated with a retail price matrix or allows you to enter a retail price.
Notes
Allows you to view or enter notes regarding the quote.
