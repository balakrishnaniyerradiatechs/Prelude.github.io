Entering a Job
After you open a shop repair order (refer to Starting or Editing a Shop Repair Order), you can enter one or multiple jobs. Each job refers to a task to complete for the model and is assigned a number (e.g., A, B, C, D). You can enter problem information and/or warranty information for each job, indicate which parts are required to complete the job, and schedule technicians to work on the repair.
