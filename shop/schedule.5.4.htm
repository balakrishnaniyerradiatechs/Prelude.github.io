Scheduling Resources for Shop Repair Jobs
You can use the scheduling calendar to schedule appointments and assign resources to jobs.
To assign your available resources to shop repair jobs:
1
You can access the schedule calendar from either:
•
A shop repair order: Shop Repair Order Entry > Shop Repair Recap Information screen > F8-Schedule.
•
The Calendar Scheduling menu: Shop Repair > Processes > Calendar Scheduling > Scheduling Calendar Entry.
The Calendar Header screen appears.
2
Depending on if you accessed this screen from the Calendar Scheduling menu or from within a shop repair order, you may need to enter the following information:
•
The Date to schedule.
•
The Whse (warehouse) to schedule.
•
The total number of Hours to schedule.
3
Enter the Type of calendar that you are scheduling/reviewing.
•
D - Delivery.
•
R - Repair.
•
W - Work Orders.
4
For View, indicate the type of calendar you want to schedule/view:
•
D - Detail Information. Opens the Repair Detail screen so you can enter information for the job and schedule appointments. Refer to Scheduling Appointments by Day.
•
W - Weekly calendar. Opens the Shop/Repair Week View screen so you can schedule resources for a week. Refer to Scheduling Appointments by Week.
•
M - Monthly calendar. Opens the Scheduling Calendar screen to allow you to view/enter detail repair information for a day of the month. Refer to Scheduling Appointments by Month.
