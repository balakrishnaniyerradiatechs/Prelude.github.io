Adding or Editing Line Items
You can enter and edit parts for a job either while entering a job (refer to Entering Parts for a Job), or as a regular line item on the Quote Repair Order Entry screen.
To add or edit a line item from the Quote Repair Order Entry screen:
1
Enter the Product to add to the quote. If more than one job exists on the quote, you must choose which job to add the product to. The Quote Entry Detail screen appears.
Cost, Gp%, and/or Profit may not appear based on your user ID.
2
Enter the Quantity, U/M, and Whse.
3
If you need to change any other data for the line item, enter N for A (Accept) and edit the following fields as necessary:
•

Gross Price, Disc%, or GP%. If you change any one of these, the other two fields update accordingly. If you enter a price, discount, or gross profit that exceeds the limits set for the product line or company, you may receive a warning message and/or the quote may go on Administrative Quote Hold.
•

-

•
Req-Date and Pck-Date. You can override the dates on the Sales Order Header screen for this line only.
•
Under the inventory information (Available, Onorder, Stock Status), a list of options may appear for the product. Valid options are:
-
ALT - Alternate product numbers exist (e.g., UPC, UCC).
-
SUB - Substitutes are available. To view the substitutes, press F6-Subs. The Product Substitutes Inquiry screen appears. Press F5 to substitute the highlighted product. Press F2-Save to close this window without using a substitute.
-
COMP - Complements about the product are available. They appear after you press F2-Save on the Quote Entry Detail screen. Refer to Adding or Editing Line Items.
-
KIT<#> - Product is a kit parent. The number indicates how many kits can be assembled from existing stock. If the kit is flagged as an exploded kit, items in the kit can be broken into separate line items in the quote. Refer to Entering a Kit Parent for more information on entering kit parents.
-
BOM<#> - Product is a BOM parent. The number indicates how many finished goods can be made from existing stock
4
When the line item information is correct, press F2-Save to continue with the quote.
