Entering Standard Repair and Setup Time for Products
If you sell products that require setup work, you can enter SRT (standard repair time) and SST (standard setup time) information published by the manufacturer of the product. This information determines default labor charges for setup and repair operations for each product.
To set up standard repair information:
1
Select Inventory > Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Product Maintenance > Action Bar > Addtl Opts > Shop Repair Information to open the Shop Repair Information screen.
2
For SRT Product Table#, enter the Standard Repair Time table number that applies to this product. This number is published by the manufacturer and refers to a model number or a group of similar models.
3
For SRT Repair Group, enter the repair group code for this type of labor (e.g., maintenance, diagnostics, attachments). The codes are set up in Repair Group Maintenance (refer to Identifying Repair Groups).
4
For SRT Time, enter the standard repair time (in hours) for this product as published by the manufacturer. When you enter products on a shop repair order, the Product Tagging Entry screen displays the SRT time for each product to help you to determine how long a repair job will take.
5
For SST Time, enter the standard time needed to set up this product. You can use this information to estimate set-up time when you enter a shop repair order.
6
For Setup Required, indicate if the product requires a shop repair set-up order when sold. Choose from the following:
•
Y - The product requires set-up. A shop repair order generates when this item sold.
•
N - No set-up is required for this product.
7
Press F2-Save to save the SRT and SST information.
