Shop Job Recap Report
The Shop Job Recap Report compares actual and quoted labor, parts, and miscellaneous fees for an order.
Menu Path: Add. Modules > Shop Repair > Shop Repair Order Entry > Shop Repair ReCap Information screen > Action Bar > Recap Rpt
Column Descriptions
 
Column Heading
Description
Job
The job number. Refer to Creating the Job.
Paytyp
The pay type for the job. Refer to Creating Pay Types.
Type
Indicates where the charges came from:
•
Actual - Charges are from a shop repair order.
•
Quoted - Charges are from a shop repair quote.
Labor
Price
The total price of labor on the order or quote.
Cost
The total cost of labor on the order or quote.
Profit
The total profit of labor on the order or quote.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent of labor for the total or quote.
Parts
Price
The total price of parts on the order or quote.
Cost
The total cost of parts on the order or quote.
Profit
The total profit of parts on the order or quote.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent of parts for the total or quote.
Misc
Price
The total price of miscellaneous fees on the order or quote.
Cost
The total cost of miscellaneous fees on the order or quote.
Profit
The total profit of miscellaneous fees on the order or quote.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent of miscellaneous fees for the total or quote.
Over All
Tot-Profit
The total profit for labor, parts, and miscellaneous fees for the order or quote.
Gp%
The total gross profit percent for labor, parts, and miscellaneous fees for the order or quote.
