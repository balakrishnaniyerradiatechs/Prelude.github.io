Customer Equipment Inquiry
The Customer Equipment Inquiry lists all serialized products sold to, repaired for, or rented to a customer. You can view detail pricing, invoicing, and repair information for each order.
Viewing Serialized Products for a Customer
To list serialized product information for a customer:
1
Select Shop Repair > Inquiries > Customer Equipment Inquiry to open the Customer Equipment Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the Customer Number.
Serialized Model numbers assigned to the customer appear.
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer Number
The customer who purchased the serialized products.
Model Number
The serialized models (product numbers) sold to this customer.
Description
The product description.
Serial Number
The serial number assigned to the product.
EIN
The equipment ID number assigned to this serial number, if applicable.
Invoice Date
The date the sales order was invoiced.
Invoice#
The invoice number on the sales order.
YTD Service
The total year-to-date amount paid by this customer for service on this model number. Updated when the order is invoiced.
LTD Service
The total life-to-date amount paid by this customer for service on this model number. Updated when the order is invoiced.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-Serial Hist
Opens the Serial Number History Inquiry screen to display detail activity for the highlighted serial number.
F6-Invoice
Opens the Invoice Detail Inquiry screen to list accounts receivable information for the highlighted invoice.
F7-Repair Hist
Opens the Model/Serial Repair Inquiry to show detail repair information for the highlighted model/serial number.
