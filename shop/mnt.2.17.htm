Recording Model/Serial Number Information
Model Information Maintenance allows you to record engine and transmission information for a model by serial number.
To record model/serial number information:
1
Select Shop Repair > Maintenance > Model Information Maintenance to open the Model Information Maintenance screen.
2
For Model, enter the part number to maintain.
3
Enter the Serial number associated with the model number. The Engine/Transmission Information screen appears.
4
To edit the information, press F5-Edit.
5
For Engine Info, Transmission Info, or Other Info, enter any of the following:
•
Make.
•
Model.
•
Spec.
•
Serial.
6
If the model/serial number combination has any attachments, enter the following information for each attachment:
•
Attachment Model - The part number of the attachment.
•
Serial Number - The serial number for the model that was attached.
•
Quantity - The quantity of the attachments.
7
Press F2-Save.
