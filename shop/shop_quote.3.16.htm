Printing Quote Tickets
Upon completion of a shop repair quote, you can generate pickup and/or delivery tickets. For example, when you create the quote, you may want to print a pickup ticket. If the customer does not accept the quote, you may want to print a delivery ticket so you can return the repaired item.
•
Pickup Ticket - Shows the customer’s contact information, the product(s) to pick up, and any pick up instructions. Refer to Printing Pickup Tickets.
•
Delivery Ticket - Shows the customer’s contact information, the product(s) to deliver, and any delivery instructions. Refer to Printing Delivery Tickets.
Continuous Printing
The following flags in Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > F6-Printing determine whether to continuously print or batch print shop repair documents:
•
Shop Repair.
•
Shop Pickup.
•
Shop Delivery.
•
Invoice Preview.
•
Shop Inspection
The following flags in Order Processing Controls > Startup Parameter Maintenance > F5-Printers allow you to override the continuous printing options for a location within a warehouse:
•
Shop Repair Quote Queue.
•
Shop Repair Queue.
•
Shop Repair Pickup Queue.
•
Shop Repair Delvry Que.
•
Shop Repair Inspection.
