Changing the Totals
After you add all necessary jobs, products, and labor, you can change the profit, gross profit %, or the total value of the order, if necessary.
To change the totals for a shop repair order:
1
On the Shop Repair Order Entry screen (refer to Starting or Editing a Shop Repair Order), press F6-Totals. The Order Totals screen appears.
2
Change Profit, Gp%, or Dollars as necessary. if you update any one field, the others automatically adjust as well.
3
Press F2-Save. A dialog box appears with the following message:
Should These Adjustments Be Expressed As Lot Billing?
4
Choose one of the following:
•
Yes - Provide one total and do not show line item prices on the invoice and acknowledgement. Src for each line item changes to LT (lot billing).
•
No - Adjust the line item prices and show the new calculated line item totals.
Adjusted prices appear on Shop Repair Order Entry screen.
