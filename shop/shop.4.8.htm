Importing a Job From a Quote
While in Shop Repair Order Entry, you can import a job that was previously entered on a shop repair quote. You may want to use this if you have a repair that requires additional work and you need to provide an estimate to the customer. You can enter the shop repair quote; then once approved, import the quoted job onto the existing order.
To import a quote onto a job:
1
From the Shop Repair Order Entry screen Action Bar, select JobFunc > Import Job From Quote to open the Import Shop Quote Job screen.
2
Enter the previously entered Quote Number to import. The Customer number and description display.
3
For Move Job Number, enter the job number that you want to import from the pre-existing quote.
4
Press F2-Save to add the job to your shop repair order.
