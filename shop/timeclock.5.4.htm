Clocking In and Out of Jobs/Tasks
As you begin each task or job, you can enter or scan your time in Shop Repair TimeClock Entry. To clock out of a code or job, just clock in to the next code or job.
To clock into a job:
1
Select Add. Modules > Shop Repair > Shop Repair Time Clock > Shop Time Clock Entry to open the Shop Repair TimeClock Entry screen.
 
You may scan barcodes to enter your technician number and/or job code.
2
For Technician, enter your technician number.
3
For Job/Code, choose one of the following:
•
To clock into a code (e.g., SW - start work, BT - break time), enter the code.
•
To clock into a job, enter the order number and job number in the pattern <order>!<job>. For example, to clock into job B for order 98650, enter 98650!B.
Description, Repair, and Job Number default based on your entry for Job/Code. Start Time displays the current system time, which is the time that you clock into this job or code. You cannot edit the Start Time.
4
Enter a Comment if necessary.
5
Press F2-Save.
6
Press Esc to exit timeclock entry.
