Entering Parts for a Job
You can add and edit parts for a job either as a regular line item on the quote (refer to Adding or Editing Line Items) or from the Shop Repair Job Entry screen.
To enter parts while entering job:
1
On the Shop Repair Job Entry screen, from the Action Bar choose Parts Entry to open the Parts Entry screen.
If the job is based on a job code, the products entered in Job Code Maintenance appear. For more information, refer to Setting Up Job Codes.
2
In Product, enter the part required to complete the job. If you are entering the product for a job code, the following message appears:
Do you wish to update the job code record with the product?
3
Choose one of the following:
•
No - To add the product for this instance of the job only.
•
Yes -To add the product to this job code permanently. The Additional Parts Information screen appears. In Cd, indicate if the product is required (R) or optional (O) and enter the quantity and price of the product to use on the job. Press F2-Save.
4
For Quantity, enter the quantity of this product that is required to complete the job.
5
Repeat steps 2-4 for each part to add.
6
Press F2-Save. The parts appear as line items when you save the job.
