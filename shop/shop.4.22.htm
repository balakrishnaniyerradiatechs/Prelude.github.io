Printing Shop Repair Tickets
When you save a shop repair order, you can generate several types of tickets but not necessarily all at once. For example, when you create the order, you may want to print a repair ticket. After evaluating the repair and adding necessary products to the order, you may want to print a pick ticket. After the job is complete and the item is ready to deliver to the customer, you may flag the delivery ticket and invoice preview to print.
You can print each of the types of shop repair documents by batch or set up continuous printing. To print the documents by batch, refer to the following sections:
•
Printing Repair Tickets.
•
Printing Pickup Tickets.
•
Printing Delivery Tickets.
•
Printing Inspection Tickets.
•
Printing Pick Tickets.
Continuous Printing
The following flags in Inventory Controls > Warehouse Maintenance > F6-Printing determine whether to continuously print or batch print shop repair documents:
•
Shop Repair.
•
Shop Pickup.
•
Shop Delivery.
•
Invoice Preview.
•
Shop Inspection.
The following flags in Order Processing Controls > Startup Parameter Maintenance > F5-Printers allow you to override continuous printing for a location within a warehouse:
•
Shop Repair Quote Queue.
•
Shop Repair Queue.
•
Shop Repair Pickup Queue.
•
Shop Repair Delvry Que.
•
Shop Repair Inspection.
