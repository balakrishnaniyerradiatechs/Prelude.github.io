Entering Warranty Information for a Job
If the pay type selected for a job is identified as a warranty pay type (refer to Creating Pay Types) you can enter warranty information for the job from the job entry screen.
To enter warranty information:
1
On the Shop Repair Job Entry screen, press F7-Warranty to open the Warranty Information screen. Some information on this screen may be defaulted based on the pay type.
2
If the warranty claim will be submitted electronically, enter Y in Submit Electronic.
3
For Warranty Vendor, enter the vendor number for the warranty claim.
4
For Ownership Flag, indicate the status of the product. Choose one of the following:
•
C - Customer owned.
•
D - Demo unit.
•
P - Parts previously installed.
•
S - Stock unit.
5
For Main SRT Code, enter the SRT code. This field may be required depending on the pay type. For more information on SRT codes, refer to Entering Standard Repair and Setup Time for Products.
6
For Failed Product#, enter the product number of the part that failed. This field may be required depending on the pay type. Refer to Creating Pay Types.
7
For Damage Code, enter the code (e.g., 01, 02) for the type of damage to the item being repaired. Refer to Setting Up Damage Codes for more information.
8
If you have a warranty claim reference number from the vendor, enter it in Warranty Claim. If you do not enter anything, the order number defaults when you complete the job.
9
If the warranty claim is being submitted after the warranty expiration date or if the repair exceeds the allowed SRT time, enter a Policy Approval# to indicate that the vendor has approved the overage.
10
If the main model/serial number combination for the repair is an attachment to a primary part, enter the primary part’s Model Override and Serial Override.
11
Press F2-Save to save the information and close the Warranty Information screen.
