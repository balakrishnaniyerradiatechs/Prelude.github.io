Display Kit Parent
A display type kit parent (Type D in Kit Maintenance) refers to a group of products sold as one unit. The inventory is kept at the parent level. When you enter the kit parent on an order (refer to Entering a Kit Parent), the parent prints on the pick ticket and the parent and the components print on the invoice. When you choose a display kit parent type, the quantity defaults to 1 and is automatically entered as a line item.
