Setting up Sub-Serial Numbers
You can assign sub-serial numbers to parts that make up a main serialized product. For example, the main product might be a car and the sub-products are the parts being repaired. When you use the Override Serial Number function in Shop Repair Job Entry to update the sub-serial number (refer to Creating the Job), the system updates the sub-serial number along with the main serial number for the order.
To set up sub-serial numbers:
1
Select Shop Repair > Maintenance > Sub-Serial Number Maintenance to open the Sub-Serial Number Maintenance Screen.
2
Enter the Product of the main product for the serialized item to repair.
3
Enter the Serial Number of the main product number to repair.
4
Enter the Warehouse where you are repairing the product.
5
For Customer, enter the number for the customer set up to use the main product number.
6
Enter the Ship-To Number for the customer.
7
For Product, enter the serialized item considered a sub-item of the main product number.
8
Enter the Serial Number of the sub-serialized item.
9
For Reference#, enter information to reference the serialized item.
10
Press F5-Inspections to open the Inspections screen to enter any inspection codes required for the product. Refer to Setting Up Inspection Codes.
11
Press F2-Save to save the sub-serial number information.
