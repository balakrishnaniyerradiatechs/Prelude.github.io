Adding a Service Bulletin to an Order
When a vendor issues a service bulletin for a product that should be repaired, you can add the information to products in your system (refer to Entering Service Bulletins). When you enter a model/serial number combination on a shop repair order that has a service bulletin assigned to it, the following message displays:
Service Bulletin Levels “2” Exist For This
Model/ Serial Number! Do you Wish to Review?
1
Choose from the following:
•
Return - Goes back to the Shop Repair Information screen so that you can continue entering data. Refer to Starting or Editing a Shop Repair Order.
•
No - Goes to the Shop Repair Order Entry screen without creating jobs for the service bulletins. The Shop Repair Job Entry screen appears. Refer to Entering a Job.
•
Yes - Opens the Shop Repair Service Bulletin Entry screen so that you can review the bulletins (press F5-Review for detailed information) and add them to the order if necessary.
2
For Accept, choose from the following:
•
B - The repair indicated in the bulletin was already completed.
•
N - Do not add the bulletin to this order. A warning appears when you save the data in the Shop Repair Information screen.
•
Y - Add the bulletin as a job to the order. Refer to Entering a Job.
3
Press F2-Save to return to the Shop Repair Order Entry screen.
