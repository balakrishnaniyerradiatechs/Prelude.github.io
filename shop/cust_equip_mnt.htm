Customer Equipment Maintenance screen
The Customer Equipment Maintenance screen allow you to reassign a product/serial number to a different customer. When a customer buys a serialized product, the serial number is assigned to that customer to help track return and repair information. If the product is sold or for any reason becomes the property of another customer, you can use Customer Equipment Maintenance to assign the product and serial number to the new customer.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Maintenance > Customer Equipment Maintenance
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Customer Number
The customer that the product serial numbers are originally assigned to.
Default Warehouse
The warehouse where the equipment was sold.
Model Number
The model number of the product sold to the customer.
Description
The description of the product/model.
Serial Number
The serial number of the model that this customer bought.
Ein Num
The equipment identification number
F-Finish
Closes the screen and saves any edited information.
F5-Change Customer
Opens the Change Customer screen to allow you to reassign the product/serial number to another customer.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to identify a reference number on the screen that you want to jump to. Valid codes are page number, line number, B (bottom), or T (top).
