Productivity By Technician Report
The Productivity By Technician Report uses time clock entries to compare a technician’s time clocked less lunch and breaks and time clocked working on a repair. For more information on technicians, refer to Setting up Technicians.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Reports > Productivity By Technician Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Technician#. The technician(s) for which you want to view productivity. Refer to Setting up Technicians.
•
Entry Date range. A range of time clock entry dates. These dates are entered automatically when a technician clocks in and out of a job, but may be changed by a manager.
You can generate this report with totals only or with data for each day worked.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Tech
The technician’s number.
Tech Name
The technician’s name.
Date
The entry date for the clocked hours.
Payroll Hours
The numbers of hours the technician was clocked in, less lunch.
Avail Hours
The number of payroll hours less breaks.
Actual Hours
The number of hours that the technician was clocked in as working on a job.
Productivity
The percent of available hours that the technician was working on a job ([Actual Hours/Avail Hours] x 100).
