Technician Job Billing Inquiry
The Technician Job Billing Inquiry list the shop repair orders that a technician worked on in a day.
Viewing a Technicianâ€™s Time on Shop Repair Orders
To view technician job billing information:
1
Select Shop Repair > Processes > Shop Time Clock > Technician Job Billing Inquiry to open the Technician Job Billing Inquiry screen.
2
In Technician#, enter the code for the technician for whom you want to view timeclock entries.
3
For Date, choose the date for which you want to view timeclock entries.
Timeclock entries for that technician and date appear.
