Completing Jobs
To complete the jobs:
1
Open the order and enter additional products or labor (refer to Adding or Editing Line Items) and jobs (refer to Creating the Job).
2
On the Shop Repair Order Entry screen, press F2-Save. The Shop Repair Recap Information screen appears.
3
For each job you want to complete, put your cursor on the job and press F7-Pre-Scan Edit to see what information is missing from the job. The following information is required:
•
Solution text. To enter solution text, put your cursor on the job for which you want to enter solution text and press F5-Edit. The Shop Repair Job Entry screen appears. Press F6-Solution and enter solution text. If this job is based on a job code, the solution text may already be entered.
•
Warranty information. If the Pay Type for the job is a warranty type the Warranty Information screen must be completed. This information may include a Main SRT Code, Failed Product#, and Damage Code. Refer to Entering Warranty Information for a Job.
4
When the information for the job is complete, press F6-Complete Job. If the job is based on a job code that has shop charges (refer to Setting Up Job Codes), a dialog box with the following message appears:
Additional Shop Charges Calculated. Do You Wish To Generate Job For Charges?
If Auto Shop Charges in Shop Repair Options is set to Y (refer to Setting Shop Repair Parameters), the above message does not appear. The shop charge is automatically created for each job on the order and is determined by the pay type assigned to the job. Refer to Creating Pay Types.
5
Choose one of the following:
•
Return - Goes back to the Shop Repair Recap Information screen.
•
Yes - Creates another job with the shop charges.
•
No - Does not create another job for the shop charges.
6
If you choose Yes, the Shop Repair Job Entry screen appears. Refer to Creating the Job for more information.
7
When you are finished entering the job, press F2-Save.
8
Repeat steps 3-7 for each job that is complete. Continue to Adding Fees and Taxes.
If the job’s pay type is set to submit claims electronically, the following message appears:
Are You Ready To Submit This Job Electronically For Claims?
If you choose Yes, the job’s status is changed to Pending Wty Submittal. When the job is submitted, the status is changed to Pending Wty Response. When you receive a response from the vendor, the job’s status is changed to Completed.
