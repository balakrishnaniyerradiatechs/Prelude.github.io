Printing Delivery Tickets
Delivery tickets show the customer’s contact information, the products to deliver, and any delivery instructions.
If you are continuously printing delivery tickets, do not use this process. They print immediately after you flag them to print and save the order. Refer to Saving the Shop Repair Order.
To batch print delivery tickets:
1
Select Shop Repair > Batch Printing > Shop Delivery Ticket Printing to open the Shop Repair Delivery Ticket Printing screen.
2
Enter the following selection criteria as necessary:
•
Shop Order Number - The number of the order.
•
Division - The division number for the order(s).
•
Region - The region number for the order(s).
•
Selling Warehouse - The warehouse that will receive credit for the repair.
•
Department -The department number for the order(s).
•
Cost Center - The cost center number for the order(s).
•
Customer - The customer number(s) for the orders.
•
Printer Form Queue - The printer that the repair tickets should print to.
3
Press F2-Save and choose Execute. A dialog box with a message similar to the following appears:
1 Records Selected For Shop Repair Delivery Ticket Printing! Load Shop Ticket Forms In Printer & Align Visually.
4
Choose one of the following:
•
Print Tickets - Continue with printing.
•
Abort Print - Stop printing. No delivery tickets print.
5
After you select Print Tickets, the Print Confirmation dialog box appears. Choose one of the following:
•
Process - All delivery tickets printed successfully.
•
Reprint All - None of the delivery tickets printed successfully. This option is also useful if only a couple of delivery tickets printed successfully from a large print job.
•
Single Reprint - Reprints delivery tickets individually. This option is useful if a couple of delivery tickets jammed in the printer.
•
Abort - Cancels all delivery printing.
6
If you selected Process, the Process Verification dialog box appears. Choose one of the following:
•
Reprompt - Keep this dialog box open.
•
Yes - Complete delivery ticket processing.
•
No - Return to step 5
