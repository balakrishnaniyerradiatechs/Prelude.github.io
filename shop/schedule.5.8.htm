Scheduling Individual Appointments for Employees
If you just want to schedule personal or vacation time for an employee without going through the whole Scheduling Calendar, you can use the Scheduling Calendar Appointment Entry to schedule time.
To enter a standalone appointment for an employee:
1
Select Shop Repair > Processes > Calendar Scheduling > Scheduling Calendar Appointment Entry to open the Scheduling Calendar Appointment Entry screen.
2
Enter the Date to schedule.
3
Enter the Whse that the employee is assigned to.
4
Enter the Type of calendar that you are scheduling.
5
For Sched Type, enter the type of time that you are scheduling:
•
A - Appointment
•
P - Personal time.
•
S - Sick time.
•
T - Training.
•
V - Vacation.
6
Press ENTER to open the Shop/Repair Appointment Entry screen.
7
For Resource enter the technician for which you would like to schedule time.
8
Enter the Date for the scheduled time.
9
Enter the number of Hours that the employee will be gone.
10
Enter the Start Time and End Time of the appointment.
11
For Schedule Type, enter the type of time you are scheduling. Choose from the following:
•
A - Appointment.
•
P - Personal time.
•
S - Sick.
•
T - Training.
•
V - Vacation.
12
Press F2-Save to schedule the employee’s time.
