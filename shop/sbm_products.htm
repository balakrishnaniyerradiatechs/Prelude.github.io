Service Bulletin Product Coverage screen
When you enter a service bulletin, you must indicate which product(s) and serial number(s) are affected by the service bulletin. The Service Bulletin Product Coverage screen allows you to enter one or multiple product/serial number combinations and ranges. When you enter a matching product/serial number combination a shop repair order, the service bulletin automatically appears for to you select as a job.
Menu Path: Shop Repair > Maintenance > Service Bulletin Maintenance > F5-Products
Field/Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Product
The number of the product that is affected by the service bulletin.
Beginning Serial#
The first affected serial number.
Ending Serial#
The last affected serial number.
F2-Save
Saves the product information.
