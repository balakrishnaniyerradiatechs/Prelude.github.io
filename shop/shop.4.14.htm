Exploded Kit Parent
If a kit parent is an explosion type (Type E in Kit Maintenance), you can choose to explode the kit components on an order (refer to Entering a Kit Parent):
1
Press F2-Save on the Line Item Detail screen for the kit parent part number. A dialog box with the following message appears:
Do You Wish To Explode The Kit Components On To The Sales Order?
2
Choose one of the following:
•
No - The item behaves like a regular (type R) kit parent.
•
Yes - The Multiplier screen appears. Enter the quantity of kits that are being ordered and press F2-Save. The components are multiplied by the value you entered to calculate the open quantity for the order. The components appear as individual line items on the shop repair order. Comment lines indicate the beginning and end of the component parts.
3
Indicate which job the kit applies to.
