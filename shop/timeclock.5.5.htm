Billing Jobs for Technicians
If you want to enter billable time for multiple jobs worked on during the day, you can use Technician Job Billing Entry. Since all the time worked during the day is entered at once, you cannot invoice orders until Technician Job Billing Entry is completed for all technicians.
If you use this method to bill customers and pay technicians, you must first set up labor types and labor rates so that labor charges can be added to the order when Technician Job Billing Entry is complete. Refer to the following sections for information on Technician Job Billing:
•
Identifying Shop Labor Types.
•
Setting up Technicians.
•
Entering Billable Time for Technicians.
