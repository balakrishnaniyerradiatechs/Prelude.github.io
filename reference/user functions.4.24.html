Deleting a Line Item
To delete a line item:
1
Put the cursor on the line you wish to delete. It is automatically highlighted.
2
Type \ and press ENTER.
 
Linked Items
You cannot delete an item on a sales order that is linked to a purchase order, work order, or transfer. If an item is linked, a warning message appears and you cannot proceed. You must first delete the linked purchase order, work order, or transfer.
Lost Sales
If your company tracks lost sales for deleted items on sales orders, refer to “Tracking Lost Sales” in the Order Processing documentation.
Pick Tickets
Warning messages appear if a pick ticket exists. Find the pick ticket before continuing.
