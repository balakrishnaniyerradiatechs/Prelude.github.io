Printing Pick Tickets and Acknowledgements
You can choose to print pick tickets continuously upon completion of a sales order or to hold the tickets in a queue and print them by batch. The Pick Ticket Date in the Sales Order Header screen indicates when a pick ticket prints but can vary by line. You can set maintenance options by customer, customer ship-to, warehouse, or company to calculate pick ticket days, set minimum/ maximum dollar levels, determine information to print on pick tickets, and establish shipping routes.
After you enter a sales order, you can send an acknowledgement to the customer to let them know that the order was received and entered. Maintenance options determine if acknowledgments are printed, faxed, or emailed continuously or held in a queue and transmitted by batch.
