Sales Analysis
The Sales Analysis module provides a variety of reports to help managers combine and analyze sales data from customers, vendors, products, and salesreps. You can use the information to forecast sales based on current year and previous year sales statistics. You can set up forecast matrices to help you to determine what combination of customers, product lines, sales reps, and major groups are used to calculate prices and/or rebates.
