Rental
If you rent equipment to customers, you can use Prelude’s Rental module to track how long a customer has an item, the amount of fuel used, scheduled maintenance, and billing. Features in the rental module allow you to:
•
Maintain Equipment ID Numbers: The Equipment Subsystem maintains equipment ID numbers (EIN) along with any associated products, part’s lists and scheduled maintenance. The EIN also tracks statistics for rental products even after they are no longer a part of your fleet.
•
Enter Quotes. The Rental Quotation Subsystem allows you to enter and print a quote for a customer to review before converting it into an order.
•
Use Periodic Billing: If you bill customers regularly for a repeat rental or a long term contract, you can use periodic billing to automatically bill a customer in each billing cycle.
