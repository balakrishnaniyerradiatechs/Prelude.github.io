Setting up Vendors
Vendor Maintenance stores information for each vendor including multiple addresses and contacts, purchasing options, accounts payable information, warranty information, and other vital pieces of data regarding how the vendor operates. If a vendor has buying requirements for different categories of products, you can use vendor lines to assign different buying frequencies and target quantities for each group of products. These could be targets for receiving, freight paid, or buying restrictions related to weight, dollars, units, or cubic dimensions. The settings are evaluated by Daily/Nightly Processing to recommend purchase order quantities.
