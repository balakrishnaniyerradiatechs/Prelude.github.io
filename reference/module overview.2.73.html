Shop Repair
If your company performs maintenance or repairs on equipment for your customers, you can use the Shop Repair module to professionally and efficiently manage service and repair operations. A shop repair order automatically maintains:
•
Multiple repair or setup jobs.
•
Additional parts.
•
Technician repair time.
•
Extra repair costs.
•
Service requirements.
•
Repair cost and time estimates.
•
Problem and solution descriptions.
•
Job status.
•
Technician productivity.
•
Scheduling requirements.
As you enter an order, you can access warranty information and repair history for the part being repaired. The order remains open while technicians add parts, repair times, and labor charges to the order. Once the repair is complete, you can set up the order for invoicing or invoice it immediately.
Features available in the Shop Repair module allow you to:
•
Enter Quotes: Shop quotes allow you to quote repair costs to a customer for a product model and serial number without having to create a shop repair order.
•
Use a Time Clock: Depending on how you record the time a technician worked on a job, and how you bill your customers, you can use a time clock to pay technicians for hours worked and/or to bill customers for time spent on a job.
•
Schedule Technician Time: You can use the scheduling calendar to schedule which technicians will work on the job, when they will start, and how much time it will take.
