Issuing RGAs & Credit Memos
You can use a credit memo to give credit to customers for returned items or pricing errors. If an RGA (return goods authorization) was issued to the customer to authorize return for a product, you can enter the RGA number for the credit memo to use the RGA data. If an RGA was not issued, you can still enter a credit memo to credit the customer.
When a customer returns an item for which you will receive a credit or a replacement from the vendor, you can create a warranty tag to designate the item as warranty inventory. A warranty tag tracks the inventory as it is returned to the vendor until credit is received.
