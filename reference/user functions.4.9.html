Using Menus
The menus that appear on your screen may be different from the examples in this document. Also, you may not see some of the standard or optional menus based on your group or user setup. For example, you may be set up as part of a sales group that does not have access to the Financial menu. You may have access to the Order Processing menu, but not the Commissions Subsystem submenu. If you need to access additional options that are currently not available to you, see your Systems Administrator.
Each menu may have a submenu as shown on the following screen.
If an item on a menu has ellipses (...) or an arrow, select that item to see its submenu. If the item does not have ellipses or an arrow, select that item to launch a process (e.g., display a screen).
You can use the arrow keys to move around the system. Use the left and right arrow keys to move back and forth between main menu items and to view submenus. Use the up and down arrows to move within a menu. Once you highlight the process you want to run, press ENTER.
