Handling Backorders
When products are backordered on a sales order, the pick ticket automatically releases when the stock is received and allocated to open backorders. If you want to reserve a stock or non-stock product on a purchase order for your sales order, you can link the backorder to the purchase order so that it is automatically allocated for your customer when it is received. Refer to the following for information on linking backorders to sales orders:
•
Linking stock backorders to a purchase order: If you enter an unavailable stock product on a sales order, you can check to see if the product already exists on a purchase order. If you choose to link the backorder to the purchase order (and the product is not already linked to another sales order, transfer, or work order) when the backorder is received, it automatically allocates to your customer’s order.
•
Linking non-stock products to a purchase order: Any non-stock product entered on a sales order must be linked to a purchase order or transfer. When the sales order is complete, you can go directly into the purchase order, transfer, or work order, or choose to let the item appear in the buyer’s work queue for the buyer to order.
