CRM
The CRM (customer relationship management) module allows you to identify each person that you work with from a customer or prospect as a contact. You can then track marketing activities, sales orders, and quotes by contact rather than by customer.
You can use Customer Contact Management Maintenance to:
•
Enter detail information about the contact.
•
Set up keywords for a contact.
•
View sales statistics for the contact.
You can use other features in CRM to:
•
Move contacts to a different customer.
•
Merge contact information.
•
Set up views to limit the information by major groups or product lines.
