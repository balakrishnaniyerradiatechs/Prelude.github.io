Commission Subsystem
If you pay commission to your salesreps, you can automatically calculate sales commission based on a percent of sales, a percent of gross profit, or an amount per sale. The calculated commission appears on sales orders but you can override the amount if necessary. Once sales orders are invoiced and commissions are calculated, you may adjust the calculated amount due to each salesrep.
