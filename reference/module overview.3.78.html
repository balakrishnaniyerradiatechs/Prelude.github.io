Job Accounting
If you bill customers according to individual jobs, you can use the Job Accounting module to track estimated and actual costs incurred during a contract period. The billing system allows you to handle lot billings, and if necessary, to bill a customer before shipping the products.
You can maintain individual credit limits, tax jurisdictions, financing information, and contract names for each job. You can set up your system so that when a salesperson enters a job number on a sales order, they are required to enter job information. If products are to be released to the job site on a controlled basis rather than when available, you can use the Tag and Hold order type to release a partial or complete quantity of an item.
