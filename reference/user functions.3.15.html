Moving Through Screens
Use the following to navigate through or out of a screen:
•
ENTER to move from one field to the next.
•
Arrow keys (up and down) to move between fields.
•
/<#> to jump to the field on the screen that corresponds to #. For example, PO Number is the tenth field on the Sales Order Header screen . To jump straight to PO Number from anywhere on the screen, type /10 and press ENTER.
