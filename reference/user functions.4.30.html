Prelude Notify
Prelude Notify provides instant message notification for work queue issues. You can download Prelude Notify from the Activant Customer Website and set your Options so that either an animated character or a pop-up window appears on your screen anytime a new entry is sent to your work queue.
To activate Prelude Notify for your user ID:
1
In User Identification Maintenance, select F5-Administrative to open the Administrative Settings screen.
2
For Machine Name, enter your Computer name from the Information screen on Prelude Notify.
3
For Notification Method, select A for Prelude Notify.
4
Press F2-Save twice to save the notification information for your user ID.
