Transferring Stock
A stock transfer moves material from one warehouse to another within your company. To keep inventory and general ledger balances accurate, you must record the movement of transferred products and quantities and adjust for any shipping discrepancies that occur.
The following process effectively updates and tracks inventory in both warehouses:
1
Enter a transfer to allocate inventory.
2
Print the transfer to provide a pick ticket.
3
Confirm the transfer shipment to relieve inventory in the shipping warehouse.
4
Receive the transfer to update inventory in the receiving warehouse.
