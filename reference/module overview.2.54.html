Accounts Payable
The Accounts Payable module provides methods to reconcile open receivers with vendor invoices and to resolve cost discrepancies. You can also pay recurring invoices or non-inventory (e.g., expense) invoices. Reports and inquiries are available to help you maximize financial control.
