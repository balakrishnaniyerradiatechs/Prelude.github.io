Toolbar Alerts and Inquiries
The toolbar on the main Prelude screen allows you to quickly access inquiries and work queues. Items with an asterisk (*) turn red to alert you that something requires your attention. These work queues/inquiries can be accessed either by clicking on the appropriate icon on the status bar, by typing in the slash command, or by using the menus.
