Recording Product Usage
Incorrect inventory levels can cause your company to carry costly overstock or risk lost sales due to backorders. To maintain profitable inventory levels, Prelude provides a series of checks to determine when and where to record product usage. You can record usage for each product in each warehouse to help determine future replenishment quantities. You can also calculate weekly usage for seasonal products that generate high usage for only a few months of the year.
