Confirming and Invoicing Sales Orders
Once a sales order release is packed to ship out of your warehouse, you can confirm the shipped products and quantities and set the order up for invoicing. Depending on your warehouse procedures and customer requirements, you can also generate shipping confirmations, packing lists, and shipping labels.
After the sales orders are confirmed, you can generate the customer invoices. Depending on customer requirements, you can accommodate how often they receive an invoice, what type of invoice prints (summary invoices, consolidated invoices, DD250 invoices) and how the invoice is transmitted (e.g., faxed, emailed, printed).
