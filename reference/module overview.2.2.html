Order Processing
In the Prelude Order Processing Module you can enter, confirm, and invoice sales orders and credit memos. You can set up customer accounts to accommodate shipping, tax, and pricing information for sales orders. You can also determine ordering restrictions for each customer and warehouse so that unauthorized orders are not shipped. Depending on the types of products that you sell, you can set system options to require or display information as sales orders are entered.
