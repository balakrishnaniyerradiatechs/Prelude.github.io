Fixed Assets
 
The Fixed Assets module allows you to maintain records for all fixed assets and depreciation books for your company. You can choose the depreciation calculation method for end-of-period or end-of-year taxes and reporting. You can also track maintenance schedules for items and billing records for leased items. This module is usually used with the Rental module to track depreciation for rental equipment.
