E-Commerce
Prelude E-Commerce functions include:
•
VIA: VIA links your customers to your website so they can browse, purchase or research your products. You can track customer names/email addresses, catalog requests, and sales history. You can also set up advertising campaigns and default web pages for each customer.
•
TPCX: Trading Partner Connect offers a private Internet trading network designed to streamline the commerce process among distributors, manufacturers/suppliers, and customers. You can streamline your business practices by making transactions with other members of the trading network in real time. Instead of mailing printed forms or making phone calls, transactions through Trading Partner Connect are immediately transmitted to trading partners, allowing for faster responses.
•
DOE: Direct Order Entry allows you to convert data (e.g., customers, vendors, entry programs) from outside sources (e.g., spreadsheets) into Prelude. The information is cross-referenced and stored.
•
EDI: Electronic Data Interchange allows you to send and receive files from external sources. Files are converted to the appropriate database format to move in/out of Prelude. You can use EDI for freight invoices to automate the approval process and eliminate manual freight invoices.
