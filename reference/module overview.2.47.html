Receiving Customer Payments
When you receive payment from a customer, you can use Cash Receipts Entry to apply the amount to one or more of the customerâ€™s invoices. You can accumulate checks from one or more customers to create a cash receipts batch. The batch amount represents the total of cash and checks included in a bank deposit. If the payment does not match the invoice, you can choose from several options to adjust for over or under payments and post to the correct general ledger account.
