Introduction
The first part of this reference manual gives an overview of the fundamental concepts of each module in Prelude. The second part of the manual describes some of the basic conventions and general procedures used in all of the Prelude modules (e.g., logging in and out, printing, faxing, shortcuts, naming conventions, security, navigating through the system). Your company’s procedures may differ from those described here depending on flag settings, modifications, or use of optional modules.
