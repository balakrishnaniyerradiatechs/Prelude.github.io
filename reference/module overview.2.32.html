Issuing Blanket Purchase Orders
The Blanket Purchase Order Subsystem provides a method to record guaranteed costs with your vendors. The blanket orders are not actual purchase orders, but a record of a quote or contract price with a vendor. When you enter a quoted product on a purchase order in Purchase Order Entry, a message appears to let you know that a blanket purchase order exists for the item. You can then source the item from the blanket P/O using the quoted price. The quantity on the blanket P/O is reduced by the amount entered on the purchase order. Expired or exceeded blanket P/Os can still be used and will remain in the system until you delete them.
Nightly Processing checks for existing blanket purchase orders when recommending a purchase order. The lowest blanket price on file is retrieved for the recommended purchase order. Blanket purchase orders that have not reached their beginning date are excluded. The Recommended Purchase Order Report displays all blanket purchase orders for a product so you can choose if you want to release material off of a different blanket purchase order than the one recommended.
