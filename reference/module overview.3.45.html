Accounts Receivable
The Prelude Accounts Receivable module provides flexible methods to apply customer payments to open invoices, hold sales orders for customers that are over their credit limit, and schedule activities to collect on past due invoices. Inquiries and reports are also available to allow you to monitor customer payments and aging information. Refer to the following:
•
Setting up Payment Terms.
•
Receiving Customer Payments.
•
Issuing Customer Rebates.
•
Generating Service Charge Invoices.
•
Printing Customer Statements.
•
Collecting on Past Due Invoices.
•
Using Credit Holds.
•
Accounts Receivable Inquiries & Reports.
