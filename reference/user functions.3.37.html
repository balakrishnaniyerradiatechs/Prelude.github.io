Scheduling Reports
You can schedule a report to run once or on a repeat basis. When you schedule the report, it runs as a phantom process that does not tie up computer time. You can print, e-mail, or fax scheduled reports.
Scheduled reports appear in the Scheduler Job Inquiry and Scheduler Job Maintenance. You can edit or delete scheduled reports in Scheduler Job Maintenance.
To schedule a report:
1
After you enter the report selection criteria (refer to Entering Selection Criteria), the following dialog box appears.
2
Select Schedule. The Scheduler Job Parameters screen appears.
3
Select a Start Time for the report to begin.
4
For Print/Email/Fax, choose from the following:
•
P - Print the report.
•
E - Email the report. The E-Mail Information screen appears. Refer to E-Mailing Documents.
•
F - Fax the report. The Fax Information screen appears. Refer to Faxing Documents.
5
If you want the report to run on a regular basis, set Recurring to Y.
6
For Recurring Pattern, choose from the following:
•
D - Daily. Enter the recurring information in Daily Parameters.
•
W - Weekly. Enter the recurring information in Weekly Parameters.
•
M - Monthly. Enter the recurring information in Monthly Parameters.
•
Y - Yearly. Enter the recurring information in Yearly Parameters.
•
F - Fiscal. Enter the recurring information in Fiscal Parameters.
7
Press F2 to schedule the report.
 
 
Maintaining Scheduled Reports
Scheduler Maintenance on the Systems menu allows your System Administrator to view, edit, or delete scheduled reports.
