Reconciled Vendor Claims - Cost Difference Report
This report lists vendor claims that were reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry, that have a difference in cost between the vendor’s replacement cost and the accounting cost on the vendor claim.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Reconciled Vendor Claims > Cost Difference
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Period Range. The beginning and ending fiscal periods to include.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the vendor claim.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that updated the vendor claim.
Vendor Clm#
The vendor claim number.
Inv Date
The date the vendor claim was reconciled.
Product
The product entered on the vendor claim.
Cost Diff
The difference between the accounting cost and the replacement cost on the vendor claim.
