Open Vendor Claims Accrual - Tag Type S Report
This report lists scrapped warranty inventory that was entered on a vendor claim and updated in Vendor Claim Update Processing but not yet reconciled with the vendor’s credit in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry or Warranty Replacement Entry.
You can view the detail open tag information for each product or the total value of open warranty tag (scrapped) products for each warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Open Vendor Claims Accrual - Type S
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse with the open vendor claims.
Vendor Clm#
The vendor claim number.
Po Type
The vendor claim type:
•
01 - Regular vendor claim. For regular or warranty inventory.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory. Products in stock that you do not pay for until sold.
•
04 - Non-Inventory Expense. An expense item that is not part of on-hand inventory.
Product Number
The product on the vendor claim.
Wty Tag #
The warranty tag number for the product.
Scr VC Qty
The scrapped quantity of the product on the vendor claim.
VC Acct Cost
The accounting cost of the product on the vendor claim.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the vendor claim.
VC Acct Ext
The total extended value of the open warranty tags (Ret VC Qty x VC Acct-Cost).
