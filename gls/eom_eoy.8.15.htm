Fixed Assets Year-End
If you use the Fixed Assets module, you must close fixed assets for a fiscal year:
1
Finish closing the last regular period (Financial > Fixed Assets > Processes > End of Period).
2
Delete any asset records if applicable (depending on company policy).
3
Prepare a list of all Fixed Assets (Financial > Fixed Assets > Assets Listings). Make extra copies for auditors and insurance files.
4
Prepare a Depreciation Schedule for the entire year ????. Make extra copies for an audit trail and archive one for tax purposes.
5
Prepare a Asset Disposal report (Financial > Fixed Assets > Reports > Disposals). Make extra copies for auditors.
6
Backup the data files.
7
Run End of Year Processing (Financial > Fixed Assets > Processes > End of Year).
