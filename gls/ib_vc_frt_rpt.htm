Vendor Claim Freight Report
The Vendor Claim Freight report lists freight charges on vendor claims that were updated in Vendor Claims Update Processing but not yet reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry or Warranty Replacement Entry.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Open Vendor Claim Freight
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the material to the vendor.
Vendor Clm#
The vendor claim number.
Vendor
The vendor number.
Vendor Name
The vendor description.
Rec-Date
The date the vendor claim was updated.
Freight Amt
The amount of freight incurred on the vendor claim.
