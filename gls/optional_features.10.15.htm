Assigning Currency Information to Vendors
A vendor that does not use your native currency can be set up with a currency type and override exchange rate.
To assign a currency type or exchange rate to a vendor:
1
Select Purchasing > Maintenance > Vendor Maintenance to open the Vendor Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Vendor.
3
Press F5-Add Info > F8-Currency to open the Vendor Currency screen.
4
Enter the Currency type to use for this vendor if they do not use your native currency.
5
For Override Exch Rate, enter the currency override exchange rate negotiated for this vendor if the Currency for the vendor is other than native. This rate overrides the exchange rate set for the currency type in Currency Type Maintenance (refer to Creating Currency Types) or in Currency Exchange Rate Maintenance (refer to Maintaining Currency Exchange Rates) for purchase orders, stock receipts, and accounts payable transactions.
