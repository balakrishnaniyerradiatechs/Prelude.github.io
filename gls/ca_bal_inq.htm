Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry
The Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry lists balances for a fiscal period for each G/L prefix combination associated with a general ledger account number. From this inquiry screen you can also access the Chart of Account Inquiry and the Chart Detail Display for detail information on the account. The information in this inquiry is also printed on the Chart of Accounts Balance Report.
If you rebuild the chart of account prefixes (refer to Rebuilding Chart of Account Prefixes) after accounts are created or edited, the Chart of Account Balance Inquiry lists the G/L prefixes of an account number in order by division, region, location, department, and cost center.
Viewing a General Ledger Chart of Account Balance
To display the balance information for a general ledger account:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Inquiries > Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry to open the Chart of Account Balance Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the general ledger Account number to view.
3
Enter the fiscal period to view in For Period Ending.
The balance of each general ledger prefix combination displays.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Account
The general ledger account number to view.
For Period Ending
The ending fiscal period to include.
Ln#
The line number to reference the general ledger prefix combination.
Div
The division number if your company uses divisions. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number, if your company uses regions. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The location number, which could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or a corporate location where transactions may post to.
Dep
The department number if your company uses departments. Departments are a subset of Locations, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center number if your company uses cost centers. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Balance
The total balance for each general ledger prefix combination for this account.
Totals
The total balance for all locations for this account.
F2-Save
Closes the inquiry screen.
F5-COA Inquiry
Opens the Chart of Account Inquiry to display detail information including the account status and account type for the general ledger account.
F6-Detail G/L Inquiry
Opens the Chart Detail Display to display detail debit and credit transaction postings per day for the highlighted account and general ledger prefix.
