A/P Voided Checks (AV)
If you void an Accounts Payable check (refer to “Voiding a Payment” in the Accounts Payable documentation) the postings are reversed from when the check printed:
•
Accounts Payable is credited for the entire invoice amount.
•
Cash and Purchase Discount are debited.
•
The corresponding invoice is re-opened.
For voided manual checks, the postings from Manual Check are reversed.
