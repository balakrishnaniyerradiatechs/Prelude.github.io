Duplicating a Chart of Accounts
If you want to copy your general ledger chart of accounts from one entity to another (e.g., new location), you can use Duplicate Existing COA to copy the accounts without manually re-entering the information.
This procedure only copies from one entire prefix to another. You cannot copy from whse 20 to whse 30 without the other entities being defined. For example, if you use Division, Location, and Department, then you must enter a value for these three fields. Only the accounts with the same Division, Location, and Department are copied.
WARNING Make sure that your existing accounts are valid before you copy them. If you re-copy, only new accounts are added, nothing is removed or changed.
To copy a chart of accounts from one location to another:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts Duplication to open the Duplicate Existing COA screen.
2
For Existing Account Prefix, depending on what is required by your company (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy), enter the Div, Reg, Loc, Dep, and CCr that you want to copy the chart of accounts from.
3
For New Account Prefix, enter the Div, Reg, Loc, Dep, and CCr that you want to copy the chart of accounts to.
4
For Suffix to Add to New Descriptions, you can add a suffix that will appear at the end of each general ledger account description. For example, if you want to identify all accounts for New York, you can enter NY so that NY appears after each new account number for that location.
5
Press F2-Update to create the new chart of account.
