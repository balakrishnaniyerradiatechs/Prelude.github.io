Duplicate Existing COA screen
To copy your general ledger chart of accounts from one entity to another (e.g. new location), use Duplicate Existing COA to copy the accounts without re-entering the information. Refer to Duplicating a Chart of Accounts.
This procedure only copies from one entire prefix to another. You cannot copy from whse 20 to whse 30 without the other entities being defined. If Division, Location, and Department were the only ones turned on, then these three would have to have an input value, and only the accounts with the same Division, Location, and Department are copied.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts Duplication
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
G/L Table Headings
Existing Account Prefix
Depending on what is required by your company (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy), the Div, Reg, Loc, Dep, and CCr that you want to copy the chart of accounts from.
New Account Prefix
The Div, Reg, Loc, Dep, and CCr that you want to copy the chart of accounts to.
Suffix to Add to New Descriptions
If applicable, the suffix that should appear at the end of each general ledger account description. For example, if you want to identify all accounts for New York, you can enter NY so that NY appears after each new account number for that location.
F2-Update
Creates the new Chart of Account Prefix.
