G/L Redistribution Proportion screen
The G/L Redistribution Proportion screen allows you to redistribute a general ledger account balance from one account to one or more other accounts based on the proportions of other accounts (refer to Redistributing a G/L Balance by Proportion).
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Processes > Redistribution Processes > Redistribution Maintenance > Distribution Type = P > F5-Distribute
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Distribute To
Ln#
The line of prefix information that the G/L balance will be redistributed to.
Div#
The division number that the G/L balance will be redistributed to, if your company uses divisions.
Reg#
The region number that the G/L balance will be redistributed to, if your company uses regions.
Loc#
The location number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to.
Dep#
The department number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to, if your company uses departments.
Ccr#
The cost center number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to, if your company uses cost centers.
Account#
The general ledger account number that will receive a proportion of the redistributed balance.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
Proportioned From
Div#
The division number that the G/L balance will be proportioned from, if your company uses divisions.
Reg#
The region number that the G/L balance will be proportioned from, if your company uses regions.
Loc#
The location number that the G/L balances will be proportioned from.
Dep#
The department number that the G/L balances will be proportioned from, if your company uses departments.
Ccr#
The cost center number that the G/L balances will be proportioned from, if your company uses cost centers.
Account#
The general ledger account number that has its balance proportioned from.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
F2-Save
Saves the redistribution information and returns to the G/L Redistribution Maintenance screen. Refer to Redistributing a General Ledger Balance.
