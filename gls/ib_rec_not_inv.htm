Inventory Received Not Invoiced Report
This report lists, by warehouse, products on purchase orders that were received from the vendor but are not yet reconciled in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry. You can choose to list each receiver or the total amount for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Stock Inventory > Stock Inventory Accrual
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The receiving warehouse that has the unpaid receiver.
P/O #
The receiver number that is not yet paid.
Product Number
The product on the unpaid receiver.
Rec-Date
The date the material was received into the warehouse.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received into the warehouse.
Net Cost
The net cost of the product received.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product received.
Tot Frt
The total amount of freight for the received product.
Tot Rec Ext
The total value of the extended quantity (Qty-Rec x Net Cost).
