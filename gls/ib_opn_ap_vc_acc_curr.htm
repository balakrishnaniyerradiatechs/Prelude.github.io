Accounts Payables Accrual - Vendor Claims - Currency Report
This report lists vendor claims updated in Vendor Claims Update Processing but not yet reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry. The report also includes the currency rate and the extended value in native currency. Refer to Using Multiple Currency for more information on currency.
You can view detail information for each product or the total amounts for each warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Currency Reports > Accounts Payables Accrual - Vendor Claims - Currency
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that shipped the vendor claim.
Vendor Clm #
The vendor claim number.
Po Type
The vendor claim type:
•
01 - Regular vendor claim. For regular or warranty inventory.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory. Products in stock that you do not pay for until sold.
•
04 - Non-Inventory Expense. An expense item that is not part of on-hand inventory.
Product Number
The product returned on the vendor claim.
Wty Tag #
The warranty tag number assigned to the product on the vendor claim.
Ret VC Qty
The quantity returned to the vendor on the vendor claim.
Net Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the vendor claim.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
AP Rcv Ext
The extended value of the open accounts payable amount (Ret VC Qty x Net Cost).
CRate
The currency exchange rate.
US AP-RcvExt
The extended value of the non-reconciled amount, in your native currency.
