Delete a Fiscal Year screen
The Delete a Fiscal Year screen allows you to delete the entire fiscal year from the fiscal period table when you are ready to close a fiscal year so that no more entries may be made. This process cannot be stopped once it begins. The system will then roll any future documents for this period into the next available open period. Refer to Constructing the Fiscal Period Table.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Processes > End of Month Closing > Fiscal Period Table > F4-Delete Year
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Enter YES and ‘F2’ to begin
Enter Y to remove the indicated fiscal period from the fiscal period table.
F2-Save
Begins the deletion process.
