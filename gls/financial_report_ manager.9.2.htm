Creating Financial Report Groups
Financial groups allow you to group general ledger accounts for reporting purposes (e.g., assets, liabilities, income, expenses). You can create the groups in Financial Report Group Maintenance and then assign general ledger accounts to the group. You can also assign a financial group to an account in Fin Rpt Group in Chart of Accounts Maintenance (refer to Chart of Accounts Maintenance).
To create financial report groups:
1
Select General Ledger > Financial Report Manager > Financial Report Group Maintenance to access the Financial Report Group Maintenance screen.
2
To select a group, choose one of the following:
•
Enter the Group Name or press F3 to search.
•
To create a new group, enter a Group Name and Description.
3
To determine how debits and credits appear on the financial reports, choose one of the following for Reverse Sign?:
•
Yes - Credits are reversed to print as positive and the debits print as negative.
•
No - The signs of the credits and debits are not reversed.
4
Enter each general ledger Account# to assign to the group.
5
Press F2-Save to save the financial report group information.
To view a list of existing financial report groups, select, Financial > General Ledger > Financial Report Manager > Financial Report Groups Listing.
