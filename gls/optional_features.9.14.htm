Assigning Currency Information to Customers
You can assign a currency type and an override exchange rate to customers and customer jobs.
To assign a currency type and exchange rate to a customer:
1
Select Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Maintenance to open the Customer Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Customer.
3
Press F6-A/R to open the A/R Information screen.
Customer Jobs
You can also assign a currency type to a customer job in Order Processing > Maintenance > Customer Job Numbers.
4
For Currency, enter the currency type (refer to Creating Currency Types) to assign to the customer. Leave blank if they use your native currency.
5
If you negotiated an exchange rate for this customer, enter it in Override Exch Rate. This rate overrides the daily exchange rate (refer to Maintaining Currency Exchange Rates) or currency rates set in Currency Type Maintenance (refer to Creating Currency Types).
6
Press F2-Save twice to save the currency information.
