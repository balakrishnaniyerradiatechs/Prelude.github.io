Linkage Adjustments (LA)
Linked inventory refers to a product that is linked to a customer’s sales order. When it is received, it is automatically allocated to the order. If the customer decides they don’t want the product after you receive it but before you invoice it, you must adjust the linkage to move the value of the product from linked inventory to stock inventory.
If you delete a sales order with linked lines, delete a linked product from a sales order before the material is shipped to your customer, or short-ship a linked line, the linked value must be transferred from linked to stock inventory. Average cost is updated when you delete an item from an order, delete an entire order, or short-ship a line with linkage history.
