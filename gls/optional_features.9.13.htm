Creating G/L Accounts for Multiple Currencies
To use multiple currencies, your Chart of Accounts must contain the following general ledger accounts for each currency type:
•
Accounts Payable-Native.
•
Accounts Payable-Foreign Currency.
•
Currency Clearing Account.
•
Currency Re-value Clearing Account.
•
Currency Re-value Gain/Loss Account.
To set up a general ledger account for multiple currencies:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts Master to open the Chart of Accounts Maintenance screen.
2
Enter the Account number. Refer to Chart of Accounts Maintenance for detailed information on setting up a general ledger account in Chart of Accounts Maintenance.
3
For Currency Type, enter the currency to use for this account. Defaults to the currency type defined in Location Address Maintenance (refer to Assigning Default G/L Currency Types). If this field is left blank, native currency is used. You cannot change the currency for an account once transactions post to it.
4
Complete the account information as necessary and press F2-Save.
