Warranty Replacements (WR)
When a vendor replaces an item on a vendor claim, use Warranty Replacement Entry to reconcile the returned product with the replacement product (refer to “Entering Warranty Replacements” in the Inventory documentation). Once you receive the items into the warehouse, the on-hand quantities and the G/L postings are created as follows:
•
The accrual postings from the vendor claim update are reversed.
•
Warranty Return, Warranty Scrapped, or Inventory is credited for the original products on the vendor claim.
•
Inventory is debited for the new replacing material.
•
If the replacing material has a different cost than the returned material, a Cost Difference account is debited or credited.
