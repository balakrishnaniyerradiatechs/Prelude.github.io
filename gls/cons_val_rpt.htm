Current Consigned (P/O Type) Value Report
The Consigned Inventory Valuation Report by P/O displays the value of all products in on-hand inventory received from a type 10 (consigned) purchase order. Consigned inventory is inventory that you have in stock but do not pay for until it is sold.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Consigned Inventory - Vendor > Current Value - Consigned (P/O Type)
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Warehouse. The warehouse containing the consigned inventory.
•
Vendor. The vendor that shipped the consigned inventory.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Product
Line 1: The consigned product number.
Line 2: The product description.
Whse
The warehouse containing the consigned inventory.
Vendor
Line 1: The number for the vendor that shipped the consigned inventory.
Line 2: The vendor description.
Orig Qty
The original quantity ordered on the consigned purchase order.
Unit Value
The unit cost of the consigned product.
Orig Value
The original extended value of the product when it was received.
Remain Qty
The remaining quantity still in inventory.
Remain Val
The extended value of the quantity of the product remaining in inventory.
