Closing Accounts Payable for Year-End
To close out accounts payable at year-end:
1
Review the outstanding checks list and void any if necessary.
2
Finish closing the last regular monthly activities (refer to End-of-Month Closing Procedures).
3
Run the Month End A/P Aging Report, which lists all unpaid vendors. You may need copies for auditors or taxes.
4
Prepare a Form 1099 for each vendor that is not a corporation and fits the IRS requirement for payments within a calendar year (Accounts Payable > Processes > Form 1099 Printing).
5
Delete inactive vendors (the amount of time a vendor must be inactive before it is deleted is determined by your company).
