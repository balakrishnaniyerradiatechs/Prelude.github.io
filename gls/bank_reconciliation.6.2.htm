Reconciling Checks and Deposits
Reconciliation Entry lists outstanding payments and deposits for a bank so that you can balance your checks and deposits to a bank statement.
To reconcile your checks and deposits with the bank:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Bank Reconciliation > Reconciliation Entry to open the Bank Reconciliation Entry screen.
2
Enter the Bank Code for the bank whose statement you are reconciling. The bank code becomes the prefix for check numbers to make sure they are unique. Bank codes are set up in Bank Code Maintenance on the Systems menu.
3
Enter the Cutoff Date for which you want to include payments and deposits to reconcile. All checks and deposits entered on or before this date appear.
4
For 'Clr' Column Default, determine the default for the Cleared column:
•
Y - Set the default for Cleared to Y for all payments and deposits for all transactions before the cutoff date.
•
N - Set the default for Cleared to N for all payments and deposits for all transactions before the cutoff date. You must manually mark the payments and deposits as cleared.
5
For Beg. Balance, enter the beginning balance from your bank statement.
6
For Other Charges, enter any additional charges that the bank issued since the last statement. For example:
•
Service charges - Enter as a negative number.
•
Interest charges - Enter as a positive number.
7
For each line, determine if the deposit or check cleared the bank. Enter or accept one of the following for Cleared:
•
N - The deposit or payment did not clear the bank.
•
Y - The deposit or payment cleared the bank.
-
If the line item is a deposit, the Deposits total at the bottom of the screen is updated.
-
If the line item is a payment, the Checks total at the bottom of the screen is updated.
End. Balance keeps the running total of the Beg. Balance plus Deposits minus Checks.
8
After you enter Y for all payments/deposits that cleared the bank, press F2-Save. The following message appears:
This Procedure Will Update All Transactions That Have A “Y” in The Cleared Column. Do You Wish To Continue With Update Process?
9
Choose from the following:
•
Y - Update the cleared deposits and checks to reconcile the bank statement with your general ledger. Each line that is updated no longer appears in the Bank Reconciliation Entry screen.
•
N - Do not update any of the deposits or checks.
Be sure to thoroughly review the Bank Reconciliation Edit Report (F5-Edit Report) before pressing Y. Once the bank, accounts payable, and cash receipt records are updated.
The A/P Reconciliation Outstanding Checks Report lists checks still outstanding after reconciliation.
The Bank Reconciliation Outstanding Deposits Report lists deposits still outstanding after reconciliation.
Cleared Reconciled Checks
