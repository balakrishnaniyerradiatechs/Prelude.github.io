Journal Entry
To adjust a general ledger account:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Journal Entries > Journal Entry to open the Journal Entry Routine screen.
2
For Journal Number, press ENTER over NEW to generate a new journal number.
3
Enter the Transaction Date for the journal entry to post to the general ledger. The fiscal period appears.
The Open? column in Fiscal Period Table Maintenance must be set to Y, A, or P for the current fiscal period, or the next available fiscal period will display. Refer to Constructing the Fiscal Period Table.
4
For Reversal Date, if this is a reversal to the G/L, enter the date the journal entry should be reversed. This date must be later than the Transaction Date and must be within a period that is open for posting. You can reverse a journal entry if you want to accrue an expense for one month and then back out the transaction the next month. When you enter a reversal, two entries are posted to the G/L. All debits are posted as credits and credits are posted as debits.
5
Enter the Description of the journal entry to display on general ledger reports and inquiries. You can use this description as part of the selection criteria when running the Journal Entry Proof Report.
6
Enter the Division, Region, Location, Department, and Cost Center as required by you company (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy).
Status
As you enter the adjustment, the Status displays as Open. After you post the journal entry, the Status changes to Posted and you cannot edit the journal entry.
7
Enter the general ledger Account number to post to.
8
To debit the account, enter the Debit Amount.
9
To credit the account, enter the Credit Amount.
10
For C, indicate if you want to enter a comment regarding this journal entry:
•
Y - Opens the Journal Comments screen to allow you to enter a comment explaining this entry. After the comments are entered, you can use F5-Comment Display to review them.
•
N - No comments are necessary for this journal entry (default).
11
Once the debits and credits are equal to each other, press F2-Save. The following message appears:
Do you want to post this journal?
12
Choose from the following:
•
Y - Posts the entry to the general ledger. Once posted, you can still access and view it in Journal Entry, but you cannot edit the entry.
•
N - Suspends the entry. You can access, edit, or delete it later.
To view journal entries, refer to the Journal Entry Proof Report or the Journal Entry Inquiry.
