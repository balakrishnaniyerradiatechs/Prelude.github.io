Redistributing a G/L Balance by Percent
If you chose to redistribute a general ledger account balance by percent (refer to G/L Redistribution Maintenance), the G/L Redistribution Percentages screen appears after you press F5-Distribute.
1
Enter the prefixes and the Account# for the first general ledger account that you want to distribute the general ledger balance to.
2
Enter the Percent of the original general ledger balance that you want to redistribute to this general ledger account.
3
Repeat steps 1-2 for each account that you want to distribute the balance to. You can enter as many accounts as necessary, but the Total Percent must equal 100.
4
Press F2-Save to save the distribution information and to return to the G/L Distribution Maintenance screen.
5
Press F2-Save again to complete the distribution information.
Refer to the G/L Redistribution Update Process to complete the redistribution.
To view the distribution information before you complete the update, select Financial > General Ledger > Processes > Redistribution Process > Redistribution Listing to list the entries made in G/L Redistribution Maintenance.
