Payroll Year-End
To close payroll for a fiscal year:
1
Finish processing the last regular payroll.
2
Balance payroll totals to General Ledger accounts, insurance files, and government reports, Form 941, etc.
3
Backup the data files prior to closing.
4
Run the year-end processes.
5
Verify the accuracy of employee total wage reports.
6
Print Employee W2 Forms.
7
Delete former employee records.
