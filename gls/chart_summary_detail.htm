Summary of Chart Posting screen
The Summary of Chart Posting screen display the debits, credits, and net actual amounts for each fiscal period for the general ledger account in Chart of Accounts Maintenance.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts Master > F5-Summary Posting
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Period
The fiscal period posted to.
Debit
The debit amount posted in this period to this general ledger account.
Credit
The credit amount posted in this period to this general ledger account.
Net Actual
The net actual amount posted to the general ledger.
Cumulative Bal
The running total of the net amount up through the corresponding Period.
F2-Save
Closes the screen.
F5-Show Detail
Opens the Chart Detail Display to display detail posting information for the general ledger entry.
