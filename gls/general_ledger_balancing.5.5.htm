A/P Manual Checks (AM)
If you write a check to pay for inventory, you must still reconcile the receiver in Manual Check Entry to update the general ledger (refer to “Recording Manual Checks” in the Accounts Payable documentation). When you enter the vendor’s invoice number, you must enter the general ledger account number. The following postings occur when you complete the transaction:
•
Cash for the designated bank code is credited.
•
The account on the vendor invoice is debited.
