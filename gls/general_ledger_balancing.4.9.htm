Crib Replenishment (CB)
A crib warehouse refers to a warehouse located within your customer’s facility. For example, you might set up a tool crib in the customer’s warehouse. The tools remain your property until the customer is billed at the end of the month for all tools taken out of the crib (refer to “Using Crib Warehouses” in the Order Processing documentation). When a crib is replenished, the value of the merchandise is moved from one warehouse to another and the following postings occur:
•
Inventory at the From warehouse is credited.
•
Once the merchandise is received at the crib warehouse, Inventory is debited.
