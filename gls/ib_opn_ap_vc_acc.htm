Accounts Payables Accrual - Vendor Claims Report
This report lists vendor claims that were updated in Vendor Claim Update Processing but are not yet reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry or Warranty Replacement Entry.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Accounts Payables Accrual - Vendor Claims
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
whse
The warehouse with the updated vendor claims.
Vendor Clm #
The vendor claim number.
Po Type
The vendor claim type:
•
01 - Regular vendor claim. For regular or warranty inventory.
•
02 - Vendor show claim.
•
03 - Vendor consigned inventory. Products in stock that you do not pay for until sold.
•
04 - Non-Inventory Expense. An expense item that is not part of on-hand inventory.
Product Number
The product on the vendor claim.
Wty Tag #
The warranty tag number for the product, if applicable.
VC Qty
The quantity returned on the vendor claim.
Net Cost
The unit cost of the product.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product as entered on the vendor claim.
AP Rcv Ext
The total cost of the extended quantity returned on the vendor claim (VC Qty x Net Cost).
