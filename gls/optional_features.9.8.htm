Assigning Exchange Rates for Monthly G/L Reports
The fiscal period table contains all valid periods necessary for posting accounting data and allows you to maintain accounting records by date without requiring a month-end cutoff. You can use the table to determine which exchange rates to use on monthly general ledger reports.
Refer to Constructing the Fiscal Period Table for a complete explanation of the fiscal period table.
To determine which currency rates to use:
1
Select General Ledger Controls > Fiscal Period Table Maintenance to open the Fiscal Period Maintenance screen.
2
For each fiscal Period, under Open?, when you change the Y to N to close a fiscal period, the following message appears:
Do You Wish To Update Month End Currency Exchange Rates?
3
Choose from the following:
•
No - Currency rates are not changed.
•
Yes - The Month End Currency Rate Update screen appears to allow you to re-evaluate the posted transaction.
4
The rates from Currency Type Maintenance appear. You can change this if you want a different rate to apply in the monthly G/L reports. These changes do not change the rate in Currency Type Maintenance or Currency Exchange Type Maintenance.
5
Press F2-Save twice to save the currency information.
