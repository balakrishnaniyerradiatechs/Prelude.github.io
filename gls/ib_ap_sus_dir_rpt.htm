A/P Suspended Direct Ship Receivers Report
The A/P Suspended Direct Ship Receivers report lists, by warehouse, direct ship inventory receivers that were suspended in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry.
You can use this report to help balance direct ship inventory. If a direct ship receiver is suspended, there is no debit to direct ship inventory. However, since the product is received, the sales order is allowed to invoice and post a credit to direct ship inventory. This report must be subtracted from the Direct Ship Inventory Report and then balanced against direct ship inventory.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Direct Ship Inventory > A/P Suspended Direct Ship Receivers
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that sold the material.
P/O #
The purchase order number.
Product Number
The product that was shipped direct.
Description
The product description.
Rec-Date
The date the product was received by the customer.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received by the customer.
Net Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Tot Frt
The cost of freight for the product.
Tot Rec Ext
The extended value of the total quantity shipped (Qty-Rec x Net Cost).
