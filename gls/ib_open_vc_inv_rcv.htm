Open Vendor Claim Receivers - Regular Inventory Report
This report lists updated vendor claims for regular inventory that shipped out of the warehouse and were updated in Vendor Claim Update Processing. When the vendor claim is updated, the quantities are removed from inventory. The claim remains open until the vendor sends a replacement (refer to “Entering Warranty Replacements” in the Inventory documentation) or a credit (refer to “Reconciling a Vendor Claim with a Credit Memo” in the Accounts Payable documentation).
You can also view the Vendor Claim History Inquiry for detail information on each claim. You can choose to list each product on each vendor claim or to only print the total amount for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing Stock Inventory > Open Vendor Claim Receiver
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse number for the updated claim.
Vendor Clm#
The vendor claim number.
Product Number
The product for which a vendor claim was entered and updated.
Description
The product description.
Rec-Date
The date the product was updated in Vendor Claim Update Processing.
VC Qty
The quantity of the product entered on a vendor claim.
VC Acct Cost
The accounting cost of the product per unit.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
VC Acct Ext
The total value of the extended shipped quantity of the product (VC Qty x Acct Cost).
