A/R Manual Invoice (MI)
You can use Manual Invoice Entry to bill a customer for something that is not in the system and does not affect inventory, history, or sales analysis (refer to “Manually Entering an Invoice in the Accounts Receivable documentation). When you enter a manual invoice, you must enter the offsetting general ledger account that applies to customer billing. When the invoice is posted, the following occurs:
•
The selected accounts are credited.
•
Accounts Receivable is debited.
