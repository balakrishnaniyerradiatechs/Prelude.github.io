Closing Fixed Assets for Year-End
If you use the Fixed Assets module, you must close fixed assets for a fiscal year:
1
Close the last regular period (Financial > Fixed Assets > Processes > End of Period).
2
Delete any asset records if applicable (depending on company policy).
3
Generate a list of all Fixed Assets (Financial > Fixed Assets > Assets Listings) and keep extra copies for auditors and insurance.
4
Prepare a Depreciation Schedule for the upcoming year. Keep copies for auditor and taxes.
5
Prepare a Asset Disposal report (Financial > Fixed Assets > Reports > Disposals).
6
Backup the data files.
7
Run End of Year Processing (Financial > Fixed Assets > Processes > End of Year).
