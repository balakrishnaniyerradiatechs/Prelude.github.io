Products With Negative Average Cost Report
The Products With Negative Average Cost report lists products that have a negative average cost.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Inventory Balancing Reports - Misc > Products With Negative Average Cost
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the product exists.
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Onhand
The quantity of the product on-hand in this warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Avg Cost
The negative average cost for this product.
