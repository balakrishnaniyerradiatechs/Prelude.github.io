Rebuilding Chart of Account Prefixes
After you create general ledger accounts in Chart of Accounts Maintenance (refer to Chart of Accounts Maintenance) or duplicate a chart of accounts (refer to Duplicating a Chart of Accounts), you must rebuild the chart of account prefixes. Once the prefixes are rebuilt, the Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry displays the general ledger prefixes of an account number in order by division, region, location, department, and cost center.
To rebuild the chart of account prefixes:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Processes > Rebuild CA Description Prefixes to open the Chart of Account Description Prefix Rebuild screen.
2
Press Execute to begin the rebuild.
