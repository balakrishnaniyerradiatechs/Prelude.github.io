Direct Ship Inventory Report
The Direct Ship Inventory Report lists, by warehouse, inventory shipped directly to the customer for which the customer is not yet invoiced. You can choose to list each product on each sales order or the total amount for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Direct Ship Inventory > Direct Ship Inventory
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The selling warehouse.
Order#
The sales order number.
Product
The product number.
Description
The product description.
Qty-Pck
The quantity ordered by the customer.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped to the customer.
Link Cost
The cost of the product on the purchase order.
Link Hist
The linked purchase order number.
Cost Ext
The extended cost of the total quantity shipped (Qty-Shp x Link Cost).
