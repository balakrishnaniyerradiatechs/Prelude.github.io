Journal Entry Inquiry
The Journal Entry Inquiry displays the account, debit, and credit information for a general ledger adjustment.
Viewing a Journal Entry
To display a journal entry:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Journal Entries > Journal Entry Inquiry to open the Inquire Only JE screen.
2
Enter or search for the Journal Number.
Debit and credit information for the general ledger account adjustment appears.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Journal Number
The journal entry number.
User
The user ID of the person that entered the adjustment.
Transaction Date
The date and fiscal period of the journal entry.
Status
The status of the journal entry:
•
O - Open.
•
P - Posted.
Reversal Date
The date and fiscal period of the reverse journal entry.
Description
The reason for the general ledger adjustment.
Ln#
The line number to reference the general ledger adjustment.
Div
The division number. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number, if your company uses regions. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The location number which, could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to.
Dep
The department number if your company uses departments. Departments are a subset of Locations, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Cc
The cost center number if your company uses cost centers. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The general ledger account that was adjusted.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
Debit Amount
The amount debited to the general ledger.
Credit Amount
The amount credited to the general ledger.
C
Indicates if comments exist for this journal entry:
•
Y - Comments exist for this journal entry.
•
N - No comments exist for this journal entry.
Comment
Any comments associated with the journal entry.
Net Balance
Any difference between the debit and credit amounts.
Totals
The totals of the debit and credit amounts.
OK
Closes the inquiry screen.
F4-Del
Not applicable.
F5-Comment Entry
Opens the Journal Comments screen to allow you to view any comments regarding journal entry or distribution.
F9-Goto Line
Allows you to enter a line number, page number, or reference number to go directly to.
