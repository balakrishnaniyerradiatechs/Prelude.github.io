G/L Redistribution Percentages screen
The G/L Redistribution Percentages screen allows you to redistribute a general ledger account balance from an account to one or more other accounts by percent (refer to Redistributing a G/L Balance by Percent).
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Processes > Redistribution Processes > Redistribution Maintenance > Distribution Type = % > F5-Distribute
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Ln#
The line of prefix information that the G/L balance will be redistributed to.
Div#
The division number that the G/L balance will be redistributed to, if your company uses divisions.
Reg#
The region number that the G/L balance will be redistributed to, if your company uses regions.
Loc#
The location number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to.
Dep#
The department number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to, if your company uses departments.
Ccr#
The cost center number that the G/L balances will be redistributed to, if your company uses cost centers.
Account#
The general ledger account number that will receive a percentage of the redistributed balance.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
Percent
The percent of the G/L balance (from the account entered in the G/L Redistribution Maintenance screen) that will be redistributed to this account.
Total Percent
The total of all the percent balances which must equal 100.
F2-Save
Saves the redistribution information and returns to the G/L Redistribution Maintenance screen. Refer to Redistributing a General Ledger Balance.
