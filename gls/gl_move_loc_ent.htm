General Ledger Hierarchy Move screen
The General Ledger Hierarchy Move screen allows you to move general ledger account balances from one G/L prefix to another. Account balances are set to zero at the From location and moved to the To location. You must post all transactions before running this program (refer to Posting to the General Ledger).
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Processes > G/L Location Hierarchy Movement
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Enter Fiscal Period
The fiscal period from which you want to move the account balances.
From:
Div
The division from which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses divisions.
Reg
The region from which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses regions.
Loc
The location from which you want to move the account balance.
Dep
The department from which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses departments.
Ccr
The cost center from which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses cost centers.
To:
Div
The division to which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses divisions.
Reg
The region to which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses regions.
Loc
The location to which you want to move the account balance.
Dep
The department to which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses departments.
Ccr
The cost center to which you want to move the account balance, if your company uses cost centers.
F2-Save
Moves the account balances to the new general ledger prefix.
