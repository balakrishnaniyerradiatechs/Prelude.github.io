Products With Negative On Hands Report
The Products With Negative On Hands report list products that have a negative on-hand balance (e.g, a quantity on an pick ticket is changed to a quantity of more than what is on-hand in the warehouse).
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Inventory Balancing Reports - Misc > Products With Negative On Hand
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the product has a negative on-hand balance.
Product
The product that has a negative on-hand balance.
Description
The description of the product.
Onhand
The on-hand balance of the product in the warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure for the product.
Avg Cost
The average accounting cost for the product.
