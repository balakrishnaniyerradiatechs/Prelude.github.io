Warranty Stock Transfer In-Transit Report
The Warranty Stock Transfer In-Transit Report displays open warranty (type 03) stock transfers that shipped out of the From warehouse but are not yet received at the To warehouse. You can sort transfers either for the shipping or the receiving warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > In Transit Inventory > Open Wty Transfer In-Transit
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Shipping Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the warranty transfers that are not yet received at the receiving warehouse. Leave blank to include all warehouses.
•
Receiving Warehouse. The warehouse to receive the warranty transfers in-transit from the shipping warehouse. Leave blank to include all warehouses.
•
Shipment Date. The date the transfer shipped. Leave blank to view all shipped transfers for the warehouse combination.
•
Shipping/Receiving Whse. Determines how you want to sort the transfer:
-
S - Sort open transfers by the shipping warehouse.
-
R - Sort open transfers for the receiving warehouse.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Trans#/To
Line 1: The transfer number that shipped out of the from warehouse.
Line 2: The receiving warehouse.
Date-Shp
The date the transfer shipped.
Product Number
The shipped product.
Description
The product description.
S
The source of the product request, if applicable:
•
S - Sales order.
•
W - Work order.
Qty-Req
The quantity requested for the receiving warehouse.
Qty-Opn
The quantity not yet received at the receiving warehouse.
Qty-Shp
The quantity shipped from the shipping warehouse.
Qty-B/O
The quantity backordered at the shipping warehouse.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product as entered on the transfer.
Cost/Ext
The extended cost of the shipped quantity (Cost x Qty-Shp).
