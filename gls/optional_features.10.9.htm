Assigning Native Currency
You must identify the native currency used by your company before you can set up G/L accounts.
To assign a native currency for all general ledger transactions:
1
Select General Ledger Controls > Currency Maintenance > Native Currency Maintenance to open the Native Currency Maint screen.
2
Enter the Native Currency to use for your company on general ledger transactions.
3
Press F2-Save to save the currency information.
