Processed Vendor Claims - Cost Difference Accrual Report
This report lists the cost differences for vendor claims that were updated in Vendor Claims Update Processing but not yet reconciled in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry or Warranty Replacement Entry.
The Vendor Claim Cost Difference Accrual account is debited or credited when the vendor claim is updated in Vendor Claim Update Processing. The amount is the difference between the accounting cost and the vendorâ€™s replacement cost on the vendor claim. This entry is reversed when the vendor credit memo is received and entered in Vendor Claim Reconciliation Entry or Warranty Replacement Entry.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Processed Vendor Claims - Cost Difference Accrual
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description

Vendor Clm#

Rec-Date

Product Number
The product entered on the vendor claim.
Cost Diff
The difference between the accounting cost and the requested cost on the vendor claim.
