Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy
You must identify the grouping levels for your company before you can create the general ledger chart of accounts. These levels define valid prefixes to use with general ledger numbers for divisions, regions, locations, departments, and cost centers, where applicable.
To set up the general ledger hierarchy levels:
1
Select System Parameters > Maintenance > General Ledger Controls > Hierarchy Maintenance to open the Hierarchy Level Maintenance screen.
2
For Divisions, enter Y if your company uses division-level accounting. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
•
To view or add divisions, press F5-Div List to open the Division Master Maintenance screen.
•
Enter the Div number and Description for each valid division (e.g., 001, Western Division, 002, Central Division).
Use Division Address Maintenance (under General Ledger Controls) to add or edit address and manager information for the division.
3
For Regions, enter Y if your company uses regional-level accounting. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
•
To view or add regions, press F6-Reg List to open the Region Master Maintenance screen.
•
Enter the Reg number and Description for each valid region (e.g., 12, Southeast Region, 14, Southwest Region).
Use Region Maintenance (under General Ledger Controls) to add or edit address, division, and manager information for the region.
4
For Locations, enter Y to activate locations (required). A location could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to.
•
To view or add locations, press F7-Loc List to open the Location Master Maintenance screen.
•
Enter the Loc number and Description for each valid location (e.g., 10 Philadelphia, 20 Atlanta).
Use Location Maintenance (under General Ledger Controls) to add or edit address, division, region, and manager information for the location.
5
For Departments, enter Y if your company uses department level accounting. Departments are a subset of Locations, used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
•
To view or add departments, press F8-Dep List to open the Department Master Maintenance screen.
•
Enter the Dep number and Description for each valid department (e.g., 31, Accounting, 32, Sales).
Use Department Maintenance (under General Ledger Controls) to add or edit address, division, region, location, and manager information for the department.
6
For Cost Centers, enter Y if your company uses cost center level accounting. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
•
To view or add divisions, press F9-CCr List to open the Cost Center Master Maintenance screen.
•
Enter the Ccr number and Description for each valid cost center (e.g., 01, shipping, 01, receiving).
Use Cost Center Maintenance (under General Ledger Controls) to add or edit address, division, region, location, department, and manager information for the Cost Center.
7
Press F2-Save to save the general ledger hierarchy information.
