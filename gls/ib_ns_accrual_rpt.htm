Linked Inventory Received Not Invoiced Report
The Linked Inventory Received Not Invoiced report lists, by warehouse, products on purchase orders linked to sales orders that were received from the vendor but are not yet reconciled in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry. You can select to print the linked product information for each purchase order or to print only the total value of linked products for each warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Linked Inventory > Linked Inventory Accrual
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that received the material.
P/O #
The purchase order number.
Product Number
The product on the purchase order linked to a sales order.
Description
The product description.
Rec-Date
The date the linked product was received into your warehouse.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received into your warehouse.
Net Cost
The cost of the product as entered on the purchase order.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Tot Frt
The cost of freight for the product.
Tol Rec Ext
The extended value of the total quantity received (Qty-Rec x Net Cost).
