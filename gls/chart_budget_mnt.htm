Chart Budget Maintenance screen
The Chart Budget Maintenance screen in Chart of Accounts Maintenance allows you to enter up to four budget amounts for each fiscal period for this account. If you set up budgets, you can set up reports in Financial Report Manager to compare what is posted to what you have budgeted for each account.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Maintenance > Chart of Accounts Master > F7-Budgets
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Period
The fiscal period assigned to the budget.
Budget 1
The first budget for the fiscal period.
Budget 2
The second budget for the fiscal period.
Budget 3
The third budget for the fiscal period.
Budget 4
The fourth budget for the fiscal period.
F2-Save
Saves the budget information.
F5-Annual Amount
Opens the Maintain Annual Budget Distribution screen to allow you to split a budget amount to all fiscal periods within a fiscal year. Any existing budget amounts for the fiscal year are overridden.
F6-Repeat Amounts
Opens the Maintain Repeatings Budget Distribution screen to allow you to enter a budget amount and then use the amount for all periods in a fiscal year. This amount overrides existing amounts for the year.
