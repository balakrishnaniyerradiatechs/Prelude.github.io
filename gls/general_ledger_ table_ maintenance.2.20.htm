Chart of Account Inquiry
The Chart of Account Inquiry displays the general ledger prefixes and balances for a general ledger account.
Displaying a General Ledger Account
To view a general ledger account:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Inquiries > Chart of Accounts Master to open the Chart of Account Inquiry screen.
2
Enter the general ledger Account that you want to view.
The general ledger prefix, description, and balance information for the account display.
