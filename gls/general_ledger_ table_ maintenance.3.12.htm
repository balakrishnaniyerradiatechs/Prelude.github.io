Moving a G/L Balance to a new G/L Prefix
If you need to move general ledger account balances from one general ledger prefix to another you can use the General Ledger Hierarchy Move. All of the account balances are set to zero at the From location and moved to the To location. You must post all transactions before running this program (refer to Posting to the General Ledger).
To move general ledger account balances from one prefix to another:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Processes > G/L Location Hierarchy Movement to open the General Ledger Hierarchy Move screen.
2
For Enter Fiscal Period, enter the fiscal period from which the balances should be moved.
3
Under From:, based on your G/L hierarchy (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy) enter the division, region, location, department, and cost center that the balances should be removed from.
4
Under To:, enter the division, region, location, department, and cost center that the general ledger balances should be moved to.
5
Press F2-Save to move the account balances to the new general ledger prefixes.
