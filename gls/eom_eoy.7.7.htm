Revaluating Multiple Currency for Month-End
If you use multiple currency (refer to Using Multiple Currency), you can use the Month End Currency Revaluation to re-assign a date to open A/R and A/P transactions to account for changes in the currency rate since the transaction was entered.
6
Select Financial > General Ledger > Processes > End of Month End Closing > Month End Currency Revaluation to open the Currency Roll Forward Posting screen.
7
For Enter Fiscal Period To Revalue, enter the fiscal period to post to.
8
For Enter the Valuation Date, enter the date to use to re-price open currency transactions. The date must be within the fiscal period indicated.
9
Press F2-Save to re-price the currency.
The Currency Revaluation Clearing A/R and the Currency Revaluation A/P accounts are posted to.
