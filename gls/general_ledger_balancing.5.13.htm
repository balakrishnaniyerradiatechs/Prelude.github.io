Job Accounting (JA)
If you use the Job Accounting module, you can set up separate billing, shipping, and pricing information to track individual projects. You can also maintain different credit limits, tax jurisdictions, financing information, and contact addresses for each job. During Job Invoice Printing, the following postings occur:
•
A zero invoice generates for internal billing. Inventory is credited and Work In Progress is debited.
•
Job Sales is credited for the job order sales amount.
