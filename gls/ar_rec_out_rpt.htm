Bank Reconciliation Outstanding Deposits Report
Run the Bank Reconciliation Outstanding Deposits Report after reconciling and updating the bank statement with your records (refer to Reconciling Checks and Deposits) to list deposits not included on the bank statement. You can use this report to balance the bank statement to the general ledger.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Bank Reconciliation > Outstanding Deposits Report
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Deposit Date. The date the payments/cash were deposited.
•
Bank Code. The bank code assigned to the bank. You can include more than one for the report.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Check Amount
The total amount of checks cleared for the corresponding batch.
Total For Batch Number
The number of the cash receipts batch.
Total For Deposit Date
The deposit date.
