Physical Inventory (PH)
When you take a physical inventory (refer to “Physical Inventory” in the Inventory documentation), you count all your inventory items at one time (possibly over a span of 2-3 days). Before starting the physical inventory, you must make sure all pick tickets and transfers are pulled, released, and invoiced, and that all stock is received and put away so that you have a clean starting point before counting.
After the final count, on-hand and counted quantities are compared. After discrepancies are re-counted, you may need to adjust inventory:
•
Inventory is debited for positive adjustments or additions to your inventory.
•
Inventory is credited for negative adjustments or decreases in inventory.
•
The offsetting entries post to Physical Inventory Adjustments.
