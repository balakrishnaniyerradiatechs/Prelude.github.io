General Ledger Account Balance Inquiry
The General Ledger Account Balance Inquiry produces a report that lists the balance for a general ledger account number for the current time and date.
Viewing a General Ledger Account Balance
To produce the General Ledger Account Balance Report for one account:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Inquiries > General Ledger Account Balance Inquiry to open the Acct Number Prompt screen.
2
Enter the Account# for which you want to view a balance.
3
Choose the method of output for the report.

Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Div
The division number if your company uses divisions. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number, if your company uses regions. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The location number, which could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to.
Dep
The department number if your company uses departments. Departments are a subset of Locations, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center number if your company uses cost centers. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Balance
The total balance for each general ledger prefix combination for the account.
Total
The total balance for all locations for this account.
