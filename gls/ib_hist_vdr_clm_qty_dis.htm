Reconciled Vendor Claims - Quantity Disallowed Report
This report lists reconciled vendor claims on which the vendor did not give credit or a replacement for all of the products on the claim. The Vendor Claim Quantity Disallowed general ledger account is debited when the vendor credit memo is reconciled.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Reconciled Vendor Claims Quantity Disallowed
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to appear on the report:
•
Period Range. The beginning and ending fiscal periods to include.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the vendor claim.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse that updated the vendor claim.
Vendor Clm #
The vendor claim number.
Inv Dte
The date the vendor claim was reconciled.
Product
The product entered on the vendor claim.
Disallowed Qty
The quantity disallowed by the vendor on the vendor claim (i.e., the vendor did not give credit for this quantity).
VC Acct Cst
The accounting cost entered on the vendor claim.
VC Dis Ext
The cost extension of the quantity disallowed by the vendor.
