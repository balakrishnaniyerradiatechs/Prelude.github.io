Stock Receipts (SR)
When material is received, the following accrual accounts are posted to:
•
Inventory Received-Not Invoiced is debited for the total value of the material received. If inventory general ledger accounts are broken down by product line, the appropriate general ledger account is updated.
•
Accounts Payable Not Invoiced-Inventory is credited.
When direct ship products are received in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry, the following accounts are posted directly to (no accrual accounts are used):
•
Inventory-Direct is debited.
•
Accounts Payable is credited.
If Direct Ship P/O types are received through Direct Ship Receipt Entry, then they follow a similar G/L posting process as stock inventory.
