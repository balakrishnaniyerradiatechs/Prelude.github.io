Expanded Trial Balance Inquiry
The Expanded Trial Balance Inquiry provides an interactive trial balance sheet that allows you to view detail information for each account and each posted transaction. This information is the same as the information on the Trial Balance Report.
Viewing Trial Balances
To list trial balance information:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Inquiries > Expanded Trial Balance Inquiry to open the Trial Balance GRIM screen.
2
For Period to Report, enter the fiscal period that you want to view.
3
For Print Only This Period?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Print the trial balance for only the fiscal period in Period to Report.
•
N - Print the year-to-date trial balance, up to the fiscal period in Period to Report.
4
Enter the Division, Region, Location, Department, and Cost Center as required by your company. Leave these fields blank to include all.
5
For Sort and Break By Location?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Sort the balances by Location.
•
N - Do not sort the balances by Location.
6
For Suppress Zero Balances?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Exclude listings with a zero balance.
•
N - Include listing with a zero balance.
7
For Include Memo Accounts?, choose from the following:
•
Y - Include interim/memo accounts in the listing (only applicable if you use the Financial Report Generator).
•
N - Exclude interim/memo accounts in the listing.
8
For Native, Non-Native, All, Choose from the following:
•
N - Print listings with native currency only.
•
F - Print listings with non-native currency only. Enter the Currency type that you want to see.
•
Blank - Print all listings, regardless of the currency type.
9
Press F2-Save to save the selection criteria.
Once you choose Execute, the Expanded Trial Balance Inquiry displays.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Currency
The currency code for the transaction. Refer to Using Multiple Currency.
Div
The division number if your company uses divisions. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number, if your company uses regions. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The location number, which could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to.
Dep
The department number if your company uses departments. Departments are a subset of Locations, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center number if your company uses cost centers. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The general ledger account number.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
B/S Debit
The total debits on the balance sheet.
B/S Credit
The total credits on the balance sheet.
P&L Debit
The total debit amount from profit and loss accounts.
P&L Credit
The total credit amount from profit and loss accounts.
F2-Finish
Closes the inquiry screen
F5-Review
Opens the Expanded G/L Detail Inquiry screen to display each document number, general ledger module, post date, transaction date and period, and debit and credit amount for the highlighted account. From here you can access detail information on each transaction.
