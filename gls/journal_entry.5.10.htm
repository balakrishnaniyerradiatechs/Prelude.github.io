Posting Recurring Entries
Once recurring journal entries are set up for a fiscal period (refer to Recurring Journal Entry), you must post the entries to the general ledger. Generally, you would run this at the end of the month to include all entries for the month.
To post recurring journal entries:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Journal Entries > Recurring Journal Post to open the Posting of Recurring Journals screen.
2
For Post Period, enter the fiscal period for which you want to post the recurring journal entries.
3
To select the journal entries to post, choose from the following:
•
If your recurring journal entries are set up to post by batch, enter the Batch Number to post all recurring journal entries with the same batch number.
•
To post individual entries for the period, leave Batch Number blank and enter the recurring journal entry numbers on the lines below.
4
Press F2-Save to post the journal entries to the general ledger and to generate the Journal Entry Proof Report. The Journal Entry Proof report lists the journal entry debit, credit, and account information for the posted transactions.
Reversal Dates
Once you post the recurring entries, any existing reversal dates are cleared for the recurring journal entry. If the recurring journal entry is setup for multiple fiscal periods and requires a reversal entry for each month, you must bring up the journal entry in Recurring Journal Entry after each month's posting to update the reversal date before next month's posting.
