A/P Suspended Direct Ship Receivers - Currency (Daily) Report
This report lists direct ship inventory receivers that were suspended in Vendor Invoice Reconciliation Entry. The report also includes the currency rate and the extended value in native currency. You can use this report to help balance direct ship inventory. If a direct ship receiver is suspended, there is no debit to direct ship inventory. However, since the product is received, the sales order is allowed to invoice, which posts a credit to direct ship inventory. Refer to Using Multiple Currency for more information on currency.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Currency Reports > A/P Suspended Direct Ship Receivers - Currency (Daily)
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the inventory was received.
P/O #
The purchase order receiver number on which the inventory was received.
Product Number
The product number.
Rec-Date
The date the product was received.
Qty-Rec
The quantity received.
Net Cost
The cost of the received product.
U/M
The unit of measure of the product.
Tot Frt
The total amount of freight for the received product.
Tot Rec Ext
The extended value of the received quantity (Qty-Rec x Net Cost).
CRate
The currency exchange rate.
US-Rec Ext
The extended value of the received merchandise in your native currency.
