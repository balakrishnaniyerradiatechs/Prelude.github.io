Open Journal Entry Inquiry
The Open Journal Entry Inquiry displays open journal entries that are not posted for review.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Journal Entries > Open Journal Entry Inquiry.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
JE Number
The journal entry number.
TranDate
The date of the journal entry.
Period
The fiscal period of the journal entry.
Description
The reason for the general ledger adjustment.
F2-Save
Close the inquiry screen.
F5-Review
Open the Journal Entry Routine screen to allow you to review information journal entries.
