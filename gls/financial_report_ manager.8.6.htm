Choosing Basis Accounts
Financial Report Basis Maintenance allows you select accounts to total and to compare data. For example, you can use sales accounts as a basis to calculate overhead items as a percent of total sales. For the column %MTH, every detail line and subsequent accumulated fields show the total as a percentage of sales activity for the current month. For the column %YTH, detail lines are a percent of the sales for the YTD balances.
To choose basis accounts for a report:
1
From the Financial Report Master Maintenance screen (refer to Designing Financial Reports), press F7-Basis to open the Financial Report Basis Maintenance screen.
2
Depending on how your general ledger is set up (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy), enter the Division, Region, Location, Department, and Cost Center as required.
3
Enter the Account to use for the basis for this report line.
4
Choose how to calculate the lines: (+) to add or (-) to subtract the lines.
5
Repeat steps 2-4 for each account.
6
Press F2-Save.
