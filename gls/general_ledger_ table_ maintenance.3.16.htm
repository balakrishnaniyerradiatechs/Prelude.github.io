Redistributing a G/L Balance by Proportion
If you chose to redistribute a general ledger account balance based on the proportion of another account (refer to G/L Redistribution Maintenance), the G/L Redistribution Proportion screen appears after you press F5-Distribute.
1
Under Distribute To, enter the general ledger prefixes and the Account# for the first general ledger account that you want to distribute the general ledger balance to.
2
Under Proportioned From, enter the general ledger prefixes and the Account# for the general ledger account number that is used to determine the proportion that goes to the Distribute To account. You can enter as many lines as necessary to complete the distribution.
3
Press F2-Save to save the distribution information and to return to the G/L Distribution Maintenance screen.
4
Press F2-Save again to complete the distribution information.
Refer to the G/L Redistribution Update Process to complete the redistribution.
If you want to view the distribution information before you complete the update, select Financial > General Ledger > Processes > Redistribution Process > Redistribution Listing to list the entries made in G/L Redistribution Maintenance.
