Add a Fiscal Period screen
The Add a Fiscal Period screen allows you to add the next year to the fiscal period table. The number of fiscal periods per year is determined in Company Maintenance > F5-Add Info.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Processes > End of Month Closing > Fiscal Period Table > F5-Create New Year
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
New Year
The new year that you are adding. For example, enter 08 for 2008.
New Year Begin Date
The date the new year begins.
F2-Save
Saves the fiscal year information.
