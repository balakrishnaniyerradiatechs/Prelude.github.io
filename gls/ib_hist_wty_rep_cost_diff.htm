Warranty Replacements - Cost Difference Report
This report lists cost differences for vendor claim products reconciled through Warranty Replacement Entry. Cost differences occur when the vendor’s replacement cost is different than the warranty replacement accounting cost.
You can view the detail vendor claim information for each product or only the total value of vendor claims for a warehouse.
Menu Path: Financial > General Ledger > Reports > Inventory Balancing > Vendor Claim/Warranty Inventory > Warranty Replacements - Cost Difference
Selection Criteria
Use the following criteria to limit the information to print on the report:
•
Period Range. The beginning and ending fiscal periods to include.
•
Warehouse. The warehouse that shipped the vendor claim.
Column Descriptions
Column Heading
Description
Whse
The warehouse where the product was replaced.
Vendor Clm#
The vendor claim number associated with the warranty replacement.
WTR Date
The date the product was replaced in Warranty Replacement Entry.
Replaced Value
The value of the merchandise returned on the vendor claim.
Replacing Value
The value of the products sent by the vendor to replace the returned products.
Cost Diff
The difference between the replaced products and the replacing products (i.e., the difference between the cost on the vendor claim and the amount of credit received from the vendor).
