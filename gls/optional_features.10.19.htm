Setting Your Company to use Standard Cost
To set your company to use Standard Cost for your accounting cost:
1
Select Company Maintenance > F5-Add Info to open the Additional Information screen.
2
For G/L Cost, enter S for Standard Cost. The Standard Cost Info screen appears.
3
For Standard Cost Level, determine if standard cost is maintained at the product header level or the product warehouse level:
•
H - Cost is maintained at the product header level (Product Maintenance).
•
W - Cost is maintained at the product warehouse level (Product Warehouse Maintenance).
4
For Allow Std Cost Change In Maint?, determine if standard cost can be changed in Product Maintenance or Product Warehouse Maintenance:
•
N - Standard Cost is not accessible in Product Maintenance or Product Warehouse Maintenance. You can only change standard cost in Cost Adjustment Entry.
•
Y - Standard Cost is accessible in Product Maintenance or Product Warehouse Maintenance.
5
For Linkage Cost Method, determine if standard cost or the linked cost is used for a linked line item in sales orders, work orders, and transfers:
•
S - Standard cost is used for linked items.
•
L - Actual cost is used for linked items.
6
Press F2-Save 3 times to save the standard cost information.
