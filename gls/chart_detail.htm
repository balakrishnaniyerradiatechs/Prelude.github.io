Chart Detail Display
The Chart Detail Display screen lists the posting date and debit or credit amount for each transaction posted during a date or fiscal period range.
Viewing Account Transactions
To list the transactions for a general ledger account:
1
Select Financial > General Ledger > Inquiries > Detail General Ledger to open the Chart Detail Display screen.
This screen is also accessible from Chart of Accounts Maintenance > F6-Detail and Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry > F6-Detail.
2
Enter the Division, Region, Location, Department, and Cost Center as required by your company (refer to Setting up the General Ledger Hierarchy). If you accessed this screen from Chart of Accounts Maintenance or Chart of Accounts Balance Inquiry, this information is already filled in.
3
Enter the general ledger Account that you want to view.
4
Press ENTER.
5
Choose from the following to determine whether to display entries for a fiscal period range or a date range:
•
P - Show Period Range. Allows you to enter the Beginning Period and the Ending Period for which you want to include postings.
•
D - Show Date Range. Allows you to enter the Beginning Date and the Ending Date for which you want to include postings.
The posting date for each debit or credit to this account displays.
Field and Function Key Descriptions
Field/Function Key
Description
Div
The division number if your company uses divisions. A division is the highest possible grouping in your company (e.g., Eastern Division, Western Division).
Reg
The region number, if your company uses regions. Regions are a subset of Division - if Divisions are used (e.g., Southeast Region, Northwest Region).
Loc
The location number, which could be a warehouse location (e.g., Denver, Dallas) where inventory is kept or it could be a corporate location where transactions may post to.
Dep
The department number if your company uses departments. Departments are a subset of Locations, typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse (e.g., accounting, sales, warehouse).
Ccr
The cost center number if your company uses cost centers. Cost centers are a subset of Departments - if Departments are used. Typically used to separate expenses within a warehouse or department (e.g., shipping, receiving, outside sales, inside sales).
Account
The general ledger account number.
Description
The description of the general ledger account.
Balance
The running balance of the account.
Per’d
The fiscal period posted to.
Module
The general ledger module the transaction posted to. Refer to General Ledger Module Code Definitions.
PostDate
The date the transaction posted.
Debit
The amount debited to this account on this date.
Credit
The amount credited to this account on this date.
Totals
The totals of the Debit column and the Credit column.
F2-Exit
Closes the screen.
F5-Posting Review
Opens the G/L Summary Transaction Inquiry screen for the highlighted line to list documents, dates, and amounts related to the transactions.
