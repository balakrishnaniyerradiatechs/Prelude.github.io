General Ledger Balancing
The integrity of your general ledger depends on:
•
When to Collect Data. The data that you collect from reports and inquiries (refer to Using Reports/Inquiries to Balance the General Ledger) is the most crucial part of balancing. To make sure that the data is accurate, schedule the reports to run at the same time every day, after posting to the general ledger and when no transactions that affect balancing might be entered (e.g., stock receipts, invoicing). If you run the reports or inquiries at different times of the day or when transactions are processed, you cannot get an accurate picture of the perpetual figures or the general ledger.
•
How Often to Balance. You must balance frequently so that inventory is accurately represented. Activant recommends that you balance daily until you are comfortable with figures, reports, and balances, then you can balance weekly. The more frequently you balance, the easier it is to research discrepancies.
Prelude provides a balancing spreadsheet to help you compare the data generated from the general ledger and perpetual balance reports. Contact Activant Prelude Support for more information.
